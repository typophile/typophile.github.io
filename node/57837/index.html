FF Din: Right and Left Square Brackets

May 9, 2009 • leod

Hello all,

I’ve noticed that with all of the weights of FF Din, there seems to be a slight difference in weight between the right and left square brackets? (the right looks slightly heavier) I’m not sure if there is a reason for this (perhaps hinting)? It appears so on screen and in print! Strange.

Hi Leonski,

of course there is no reason for this. It is just that kind of mistakes that sneak their way through all quality tests. FSI and I recently prepared an update of FF DIN by extending the cyrillic character set and adding Cyrillics to the italic and condensed weight. In this process we also fixed this bug in FF DIN light, regular, medium and bold. In the black weight and in the italic and condensed weight, the brackets have always been right. The updated version is now being mastered, so it will be available soon.
