Okay, so most of you professional types already know about this site, but for those of us who might not…check out www.crank.com. Almost all my graphic/web design is for non-profs for free and most of them are for kids. Crank has some really fun/wacky fonts. I know they aren't up to Adobe standard but in the right situation can be just the right thing. I'm going to purchase several for a website I'm currently designing. So if you're looking for something really off the beaten type path, check this site out. As a hockey mom, I think the Candian fonts really got to me! They actually have some fairly decent fonts as well. Enjoy! (Some of the type descriptions are worth visiting on their own)
