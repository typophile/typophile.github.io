I don’t know enough about Islam to comment on your choice of symbols/characters and I would think that those are of primary importance for a logo of this kind. Therefore I can only comment on its aesthetics and exectution.

I like it, but…

I think the three elements (frame / three characters / star) need harmonizing. They each look like they are in a different style and would be better if they were all drawn with the same ‘brush’ with the same stroke thickness and sharpeness or roundness.

The frame is inconsistent in itself. The seven arches are rounded at their base and apex (and a bit wobbly) whereas the bottom point is sharp, and if it’s a book spine it needs some width.

The overall dimensions are rather square. It may look more elegant if it was taller, maybe with the dimensions of an archway.

The left and right characters (?) are a bit too close to frame. Maybe have those two less angled so the three of them form a more slender vertical shape.

On the frames bottom left and right corners the inside curve needs a smaller radius so the line doesn’t appear to get thicker there.

Hope these comments are of some help.
