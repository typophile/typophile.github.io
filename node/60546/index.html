Not in Door county, but not too far away: The Kohler Art Center in Sheboygan is great. Nothing to do with type, but worthwhile if you like “outsider” art. Every time we’ve been there there’s something interesting. Be sure to check out the washrooms. M. Schettl in Oshkosh is pretty cool, too. Again, not necessarily type-related.
