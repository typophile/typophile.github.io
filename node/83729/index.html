Alternative to Gloriola

July 13, 2011 • rubenDmarkes

I have this job for which I thought [[http://typographica.org/2008/typeface-reviews/gloriola/|Gloriola]] was perfect. I needed something which was forward looking – futuristic in a present kind of way, if that makes sense – but down to earth; elegant, not too youthful but not too classical; a bit of an optimistic young adult in typeface form (again, if that makes any sense). It needed to have a “techno-logical” edge to it, but still not fall too far away from the humanist tree. And of course, it had to have small caps. Anyway, I thought it was perfect in all the right ways.

But then I discover it’s not available for licensing until 2013. =(

So I start looking for an alternative. There’s [[http://www.fontshop.com/fonts/downloads/dstype/leitura_sans_complete/|Leitura]], which is great and cheap (and from a fellow portuguese), but I find it’s not perfectly designed (the width of the stroke seems irregular, at least in the small caps, which is my main concern, here); [[http://www.typography.com/fonts/font_overview.php?productLineID=100026&path=head|Whitney]], which I can’t get as single weight files; [[http://www.fontshop.com/fonts/singles/process_type_foundry/seravek_medium/|Seravek]], which is a bit too cute and frankly expensive; [[http://www.fontshop.com/fonts/downloads/just_another_foundry/facit_ot/|Facit]], which also has the right price but just doesn’t cut it for various reasons; [[http://www.typotheque.com/fonts/fedra_sans_alt|Fedra Sans Alt]], which is too expensive and has too high of an x-height; and I also considered [[http://www.fontshop.com/fonts/downloads/process_type_foundry/klavika_ot/|Klavika]], which lacks warmth, and [[http://www.fontshop.com/fonts/downloads/underware/auto_complete_family_ot/|Auto]], which has too much of a personality for the job.

So that should give you a quite clear idea of what I’m looking for; any suggestions?

Have you seen [[http://www.boldmonday.com/en/flex|Flex]]? Another alternative might be [[http://www.emtype.net/relato_sans_01.php|Relato Sans]].
