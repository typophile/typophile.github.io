Bryant 2 & Pro

January 25, 2008 • AndrewSipe

Current URL: http://www.processtypefoundry.com/typefaces/bryant2/index.html Bryant 2 & Pro Designed by [[Eric Olson]] 2002–2005 Bryant 2 and Bryant 2 Pro are the completely re-drawn and updated versions of our original [[Bryant]] series released in 2002. Initially a simple family of four fonts modeled around the popular Wrico lettering kits used by draftsmen and amateur sign makers in the 1960s and ’70s, Bryant 2 & Pro is now an expansive family of fonts for anything from worldwide branding projects to simple one-off posters.
