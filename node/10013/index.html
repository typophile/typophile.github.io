"has the accessibility symbol"

That's not an accessibility symbol. It's a 'handicapped' symbol or 'wheelchair accessible' symbol.

accessibility > physical disability. I'd seriously consider trying to come up with your own if you can. Check out what Apple use's for their icon.

I co-opted it for a quick icon on our site:
http://www.courts.state.mn.us/

Now, if you're trying to design a sign for handicapped parking, then it makes a lot of sense, but for accessibility in general, not so much.
