Hi Chris, Welcome to the Type Identification Board. As a newcomer, we don't want to chase you away, but it might be a good idea for you to read some of the FAQ information. That would help you with posting images (which didn't work this time, probably due to not using a .gif or .jpg file), and possibly about asking for free versions of commercial fonts.

You see, as a type-loving community, including many font designers, we try not to promote 'free versions', as they are usually rip-offs of someone else's design work. We prefer to support the work of legitimate font designers, and those who are reviving old designs that have not been previously digitized. There are also designers of original fonts who choose to make their fonts available for free, and those are fine too.

It's just the unauthorized, re-named copies of commercial fonts that make us get a little hostile. It's illegal, unethical and font piracy, plain and simple.

I should say, that this is my attempt to say what I believe most of us think about this, since I do not speak for Typophile.com.

We are here to help you identify fonts. If you have that sort of question, come back as often as you need our help.
