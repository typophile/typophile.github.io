2014-10-07 21:00:55 • Té Rowan

typography is more interesting than sports, celebrity news, the Prime Minister and the weather. As the “nuts and bolts” of written communication and therefore of all acquired human knowledge capable of being written down, plus the technology to do so, it is also more useful than some of those things. Doing it well makes life better. Doing it badly makes it worse. Perhaps some people don’t care about that, but if that’s the case, why would they waste time here?

Personally, I’m not offended by fonts used for subtitles. I’m offended when people who are paid way more than me make obviously lazy, lackadaisical and unconsidered font choices… That I could be making.
