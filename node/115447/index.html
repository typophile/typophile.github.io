Hi Theo, here are some chunky handwriting substitutes: Stentor by Veronika Elsner, Günther Flake based on the design of Stentor Heinz Schumann http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/ef/stentor/ http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/urw/stentor/ Rosalia by Ingo Preuss based on Stentor http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/preusstype/rosalia/ Fox by Ralph M. Unger based on a design by W. Rebhuhn for Genzsch & Heyse http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/urw/fox/ Blizzard based on Bison by Julius Kirn for Weber http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/urw/blizzard/ Brody based on a design by Harold Brodersen At Linotype, EF, URW etc. Also Filmotype Hemlock, Horizon, Havana. Dont neglect the comic book fonts, such as Danger Girl http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/comicraft/danger-girl/ Zoinks http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/comicraft/zoinks/ Hookey http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/comicraft/hooky/ None are as close as Signal BQ, but at least they give you some options.
