Your best chance is probably directly asking [[http://www.rinker.co/f2tegyjq4mkp7hswwyivm3n5lvey0r|Alex Rinker]] and if he tends to answer, drop the answer here. But I’d be not surprised it’s almost everything hand-drawn… Anyway, for /C and /B initials, I like [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/letterheadfonts/lhf-billhead-1890|Billhead LHF]] and [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/coffee-bin-fonts/letterhead|Letterhead]]. For the rest, I’d probably start with [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/flat-it/sheepman|Sheepman]] or [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/flat-it/house-of-cards|House of Cards]] and customize it. [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/hold-fast-foundry/gin|Gin]] might also be interesting. For a alternative but close design approach to LHF, [[http://decadetypefoundry.bigcartel.com|Decade Type Foundry]] is worth of interest.

I agree with Ryuk’s suggestion of House of Cards for IGAR OX. If you add Tuscan style indents at the top and bottom you are pretty close. Also his good finds for B. As for C almost any sans of the right weight and proportion would do if you add the Tuscan style ends. Don
2014-02-25 04:43:34 • donshottype
House of Cards is a revision of the old woodtype font Teniers. If you prefer to use something that is closer to the original, try: Teniers by Wooden Type Fonts http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/woodentypefonts/teniers/ Solotype’s Goodfellow http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/solotype/goodfellow/ William Page by Wooden Type Fonts 506 http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/woodentypefonts/william-page-506/ Don
