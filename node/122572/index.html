Agree with Don, custom lettering. 2 options for me:

  • Don’s approach: mash-up fonts, a basic sans and a script; for instance, the sans could be [[http://www.dafont.com/buttacup.font|Buttacup]] and the script [[http://www.myfonts.com/search/elan|Elan]] (Don’s suggestion), [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/itc/lightnin|Lightnin]], [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/canadatype/fontella|Fontella]], [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/itc/gillies-gothic|Gillies Gothic]], [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/bitstream/kaufmann|Kaufmann]], [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/jnlevine/penmanshift|Penmanshift]] (nice this one), [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/marksimonson/coquette|Coquette]], [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/ef/futura-script|Futura Script]], [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/flat-it/ht-maison|Maison]], [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/itc/varga|Varga]] (nice too), [[http://www.dafont.com/airstream.font|Airstream]]
  • choose an appropriate sans ([[http://www.dafont.com/buttacup.font|Buttacup]]) and customize letters. I like and even if it’s different (flare serif) [[http://youngjerks.com/#/highway-typeface|Highway]] by Dan Cassaro. Some ready-to-go options: [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/latinotype/selaive|Selaive]], [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/latinotype/merced|Merced]], [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/sudtipos/brownstone-sans|Brownstone Sans]], [[https://www.behance.net/gallery/6367355/Myra-Caps-(Typeface)|Myra Caps]], [[https://www.behance.net/gallery/2721389/Kylie-(Typeface)|Kylie]], [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/font-fabric/solomon|Solomon]], [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/yellow-design/gist|Gist]] (slab and inline… ouch… but same vein), [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/justanotherfoundry/peacock|Peacock]] Maybe a dead-end but you could have a look to [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/font-fabric/nexa-rust|Nexa Rust]] and try to mix Nexa Sans with its Script… Not sure it works though…
