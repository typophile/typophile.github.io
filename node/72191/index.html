While I have no solid answer for you, I think the term Southern really is too open ended to have any real depth or sophistication for typography, unless you want a caricature. In that case, gritty with idiosyncracy and hand-done works. Roadside sign letter might work. You could probably get away with some “western” tropes.

Unless you’re looking for contemporary southern typography, in which case you might want to pick up a regional design annual or two.
