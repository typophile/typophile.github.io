The Good Old Days

June 19, 2008 • BB

I was speaking to a work colleague of mine recently who was telling me of the good old days in the newspaper industry. - in the days before the humble Mac or PC, back in the days of hand and machine setting type from hot lead. He then proceeded to produce from a shelf in his office an old GALLEY which he had kept from a past life containing hot metal type. As a designer who has grown up with computer technology, I was fascinated by this piece of history so I had to ask if I could take some shots of it... Here are a some of them...enjoy.
