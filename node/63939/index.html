To give you an idea about what I mean, there are at least 995 serif fonts (per my [[http://bowfinprintworks.com/SerifGuide/serifsearch.php|Serif Guide]]) with an R with the leg coming from the bowl like this one. A certain percentage of them have a half serif on the leg. (There were five fonts with a similar R between Aalborg and Agora (14 listed fonts to that point), to give specifics. At that rate there could be 200 fonts or more with a similar R.)

Especially without other letters, and some assurance that these weren’t made-up letters, it seems problematic to search for an ID.
