REVISED to remove excess detail. Carisma has similar terminal structure to Venus but has less contrast. Consider O. The rhs of the O in Venus is monoline with the stroke about as thick as the top and bottom stroke of Carisma Light. The lhs stoke is almost as thick as Carisma Regular. The rhs curved strokes of B, D, P, and R as well as the rhs stroke for U are similarly monoline. Note differences in G, R, M, J, W, and Q which has a tail similar to Retro Super Skinny.
I wonder which of the many other fonts suggested come as close as Clarisma or closer than Clarisma. Don
