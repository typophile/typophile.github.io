guess the designer of this typeface

August 11, 2005 • george

Recently in a type ID request, one has brought up the issue of CANON corporate typeface. Their new marketing campaign started back in 2003 features a new typeface. It can be best seen in the PDF brochures they provide for download on the website. PDF indicates the typeface to be Denda New. Same information appears in a brand guidelines manual for another CANON Company.

Judging by the overall look and feel of the new typeface, can you guess (or maybe somebody out there knows) the designer of the typeface and the foundry releasing it.

To me, the italics ressemble JFPorchez’s LeMonde Sans Italic and the regular version has a Formata and Stella fell, but even Kievit wouldn’t be too far away.

There is a copyright notice in the type1 fonts. “Copyright © 2001 Fsm plus.”

If you ask google: -“Fsm plus” fonts- it will come up with two outdated links and a name. www.fsm-plus.nl & [[Fred Smeijers]]
