Given that it’s only two lines of type and there are more variations than can be accounted for by stylistic alternates, I think Don is correct–this was most likely hand-lettered by a calligrapher.

Alana achieves the same feel, and Laura Worthington has other fonts in the same style. Here’s a link to a site that also lists similar fonts:
