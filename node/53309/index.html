Hello all,

We have installed a new forum at: http://forum.fontlab.com/

For starters, the forum will host discussions about FontLab and Photofont products as well as about products from Microsoft Typography (Font Validator, VOLT, WEFT).

But we have also added a few other forums focused on specific issues with font development (e.g. hinting, OpenType feature development) and with type design.

It is not our plan to hijack Typophile of course, just offer a place for discussions that is specifically geared towards font editing applications and workflows.

Regards, Adam
