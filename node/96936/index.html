I’m sorry, I don’t know any font with such a /O but I can’t state it’s custom made. May be it is as simple as “generic extended sans like Helvetica/Akzidenz Grotesk with a custom /O”. What I can propose though:

  • the logo itself, freely available to download as vector: http://www.brandsoftheworld.com/logo/only-denim
  • some similars: [[http://www.foundrytypes.co.uk/the-foundry-typefaces/sans/params/foundry-monoline-12|Foundry Monoline]], [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/northernblock/neo-gram/|NeoGram]], [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/typodermic/sui-generis/|Sui Generis]] (which comes very close, for the /NLY part at least), [[http://www.typography.com/fonts/font_overview.php?productLineID=100041|Forza]]
  • a possible track to follow: have a closer look to the /® (register mark), the external circle and /O look like to share the same design. /R reminds me once again of [[http://www.foundrytypes.co.uk/the-foundry-typefaces/sans/params/foundry-monoline-12|Foundry Monoline]]. So my last call: custom job based on Monoline.
