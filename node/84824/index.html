Fábio: ‘Frank Blokland designs this beautiful cover. […] But this typeface was design by him?’

The letters on the cover (also on the back) are mine indeed, but actually Gerrit Noordzij designed (and edited) this Lecturis edition on type design by Dutch graphic design students. The letters I made as a student were placed on the cover at the original drawing size (by hand at that time, of course). The backside of the cover shows a very large lower case g (roughly ten inches), also from my hand. The types on right page of the last spread show type designs I made as a student too (thirty years ago). I ﬁnished some of these, but I never published them, because I did not consider them to be good enough.
