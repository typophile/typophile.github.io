My first-ever post in Typophile! Woo-hoo.

I'm a bit of a font dilettante, so please bear with me. My question has to do with the Gotham family: although I don't actually have the font, I've been seeing it everywhere and I love it. I understand it's a polished distillation of all the postwar typography (signage etc.) that's still visible all over New York. Well, what I'm looking for is a similarly polished distillation of something else entirely: the postwar typography that can still be seen here and there in cities like Prague and Budapest.

I'm not after some sort of nearly-illegible constructivist Soviet-looking face; rather, what I want to find is a slick-looking cousin of Gotham that echoes the slightly more daring (and sloppy) geometry of letterforms that appeared behind the Iron Curtain.
