Help with complementary fonts for Hawaiian Aloha

June 24, 2009 • katy_bug

I am a novice that is designing an activity guide for my friends upcoming beach wedding. I like [[http://new.myfonts.com/fonts/btn/hawaiian-aloha-btn/|Hawaiian Aloha]] and I need a couple of complementary fonts for sub-headings and text. Any suggestions?

Since you’re leaning towards one typeface by type designer Stuart Sandler, why not go with two of his other creations?

Cocktail Shaker is a fun retro script that could work for subheads and Backtalk Sans might work for your text.
