For a corporate logo i am looking for a font that combines sharp and round angels. If possible in every character.

You should not restrict yourself to “fonts” in seeking a solution to your “corporate logo”. You seem to have a good grasp in what you seek visually. The characters in a logo function completely different than those in “fonts” and have far less restriction.

Start with the characteristics you like, then hire someone to produce the logo. When I designed “word marks” it was very important what the individual characters were and the inter-relationship of the letterforms, not just the “look” of the “font”. Open up your possibilities, not limit them to finding and existing font.

Just my humble opinion.
