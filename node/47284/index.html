That's a hard one to search for. Its a Spencerian font as in Platt Rogers Spencer of the 19th century. Unfortunately, most type searches will lump copperplate scripts in with Spencerian because they are all formal pointed pen scripts. A excellent example of a Spencerian style font is: http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/ihof/p22-zaner/ or the thinner more casual: http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/ihof/p22-allyson/ Both are from Paul Hunt.
