Anyway, they felt that when a excalm- or questiondown was followed by a capital letter, the exclam- questiondown should ride on the baseline, otherwise, it should not.

We wrote a simple calt feature to take care of this Where (F) is foundry, and (1) and (2) show the added calt feature.
