2004-01-15 02:43:43 • markatos
They are both bad. With missing screen fonts, you cannot use the font at all. With missing PostScript fonts you cannot print or display the font on-screen except in whatever bitmap sizes are available (usually only a few). The font will also look very distorted and chunky except at those sizes. This will be especially noticeable at large sizes. You may not be able to use the font at all with some programs. You will also not see any anti-aliasing on-screen.
