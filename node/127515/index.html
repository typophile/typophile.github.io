Hi, I really love the branding of Cacao70 (www.cacao70.com). They use a great pairing of a powerful word mark and a script font. Could you please help me identifying them?

Thanks!

The script face is Garris: https://creativemarket.com/artone/302564-Garris-(Intro-40-OFF)

CACAO 70 appears to custom, but likely based on Beirut Poster (which appears to predate the larger Beirut type family and is no longer available): https://fontsinuse.com/typefaces/39875/beirut https://luzi-type.ch/beirut

The sans serif appears to be Maax: http://www.205.tf/maax/

About the branding: https://mindsparklemag.com/design/cacao-70-brand-identity/
