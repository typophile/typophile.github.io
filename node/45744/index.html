Three Gutenberg Bibles on display at the Morgan (NYC)

May 27, 2008 • Ricardo Cordoba

If you are in New York City between now and the end of September, do not miss this small but wonderful exhibition at The Morgan Library and Museum. Two of the bibles on display are made up of two volumes each, so you get to see eight spreads in all. (Read some more about each book here.) The exhibition is rounded out with an indulgence printed by Gutenberg and two Psalters printed by Gutenberg’s successors, Fust and Schoeffer. These psalters were the first printed books to carry a colophon.

Also worth checking out is the exhibition upstairs, Illuminating the Medieval Hunt. But that one ends August 10.
