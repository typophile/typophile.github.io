My experience is that Nike has a practice of creating its own lettering for apparel. I agree that Consort RR Extra Bold Condensed is a good substitute. Consort was a revival by Stephenson Blake in 1956 of the Clarendon of 1845 in regular and condensed widths with additional weights. Steve Jackaman’s digital version is well made. As far as I can determine, other retail Clarendons do not have a combination of width and weight that is as close as Consort RR Extra Bold Condensed. Don
