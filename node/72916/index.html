2010-08-05 10:43:03 • Andreas Stötzner
To me DIN and Gotham both feel even more friendly than Helvetica. Maybe try a gruff, masculine alternative like Folio, Venus, Bureau Grotesque, FF Dagny, Neuzeit, FF Bau, Monotype Grotesque or squarisch sans like Hydra, Eurostile, Gridnik or the new Forza.
2010-08-05 11:45:47 • Indra Kupferschmid
Those are very stylish, vigorous scripts, and don't strike me as being particularly femme. I don't think you need a butch sans for balance (just sufficient weight), but I do think that faces with a small x-height would match well; faces with a large x-height might appear clumsy in comparison.
