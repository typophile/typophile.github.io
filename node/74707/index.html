Newspaper Blackletter ID

September 29, 2010 • markmont

This question may have already been answered, but can someone please ID some good blackletter faces? Specifically New York Times, Chicago Tribune and/or Boston Globe? Thanks much.

–Markmont.

Those are all custom designs, made specially to suit the newspaper and the particular letters. The Tribune nameplate is by Jim Parkinson, as are many other blackletter nameplates.

For a classic textura (the variety of blackletter most of these nameplates are), my favorite is Goudy Text.
