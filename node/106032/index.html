Very nice lettering. A simple edit of the title to remove distractions: This is a nice pastiche of some circa 1900 lettering styles. Note the big bowl on R similar to University Roman; the high waist E and H similar to Eccentric; the tilde shapes (a.k.a) cyma or flames from the A [a feature discussed in a previous thread: http://typophile.com/node/103815]; and the U shape at the center of the crossbars of A and H. Disney animators were perfectly capable of making their own titles based on historic features or samples with consistent execution of matching letters. But perhaps this has in fact been done as a font. Anyone? Don
