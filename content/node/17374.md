body: "<strong>[[Indices]] : [[Designers]] : Vincent Connare</strong>\r\n\r\nVincent
  Connare has been working professionally in the software industry producing font
  projects for corporations for over fifteen years. While working at Agfa/Compugraphic
  from 1987 till 1993 he was a member of the [[Ikarus]], [[Intellifont]] and [[TrueType]]
  font production teams. At Agfa he was one of the first type designers to learn TrueType
  [[hinting]]. He finalized the hinting of four TrueType fonts to Apple's first TrueType
  font pack.\r\n\r\nIn 1993 he joined [[Microsoft]] Corporation as a Typographic Engineer
  and worked on custom type projects for Microsoft products. At Microsoft he designed
  and produced two of the most popular [[typeface|typefaces]] of the 1990's, [[Comic
  Sans]] and [[Trebuchet]]. There he also wrote documents relating to TrueType hinting
  and font production. \r\n\r\nToday, Vincent is on the staff of <a href=\"http://www.daltonmaag.com\">Dalton
  Maag Ltd.</a> A London based type design agency which specializes in corporate font
  solutions, branding, and type design.\r\n\r\nVincent is also an accomplished photographer
  and photo technician. He has worked as a newspaper photographer and darkroom technician
  and currently photographs TV presenters, actors and comedians. He has been published
  in advertisements and articles such as:\r\n<a href=\"http://www.closermag.co.uk/\"
