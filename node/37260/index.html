September 19, 2007 • wojtekmejor

Hi all,

I’m trying to identify the font that had once been used in this establishment: http://typotrip.wordpress.com/2007/09/16/type-archeology/ This is a tricky one since the letters themselves are not there anymore - just the marks they left on the wall.

Stephen from Typographica.org suggested it is something related to Volta Bold. That’s the closest so far but I have overlaid Volta Bold over the photograph and it seems the thick stem should be even bolder and the whole thing more extended.

Any ideas?

Quite likely it’s not a “font”. Until recently, signs on buildings like this one were usually made by hand by sign painters or sign makers, and each sign is unique (and in some cases each letter). This one looks like it may have had dimensional letters attached to the building.
