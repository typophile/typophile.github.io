EDIT: You are right, guys. It’s a similar variant of the “Coca-Cola” font. On the “kthomps5” links you can find Coca-Cola font is a combination of “American Pop One” and “American Pop Two”. In my case, Cacaolat is only American Pop One, corresponding at the “Coca” from “Coca-Cola”, so the font has been found. Thank you all, and thank you kthomps5 for the links!

Most likely custom calligraphy/type design, based on the historical labels: http://cacaolat.es/en/historia/

The style is often referred to these days as baseball script, and some of the modern typefaces in that style include “tail” versions of letters: http://www.myfonts.com/search/baseball+script/fonts/#item_65

Here are a few typefaces from the list that seem most similar to me: http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/fontmesa/american-pop/ http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/flat-it/machiarge/machiarge/glyphs.html http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/fontmesa/brewmaster/
