I went to see the Rick Griffin exhibit at the Laguna Art Museum this weekend; it was amazing to see the texture and detail of the originals. And how does he get that light in the water? The [[http://www.lagunaartmuseum.org/current_exhibits.html|exhibit]] was supposed to close Sunday, September 30, but the catalog and a limited edition poster are still available from the [[http://www.lagunaartmuseum.org/cart/|museum shop]]. The museum shop also has a variety of Griffin posters that aren’t shown on the website.
Wish I coulda gone… Have been a big fan of his artwork since purchasing this book when I was at University…
