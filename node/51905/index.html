2008-11-20 23:38:13 • gerald
Unfortunately “LAY” and “TY” are tough letter combinations to get right. I think your best option may be to letterspace them out a bit, especially if you don’t want the letters to touch. Or, you could try using a more condensed typeface, Trade Gothic Bold Condensed, or Univers Condensed.
2008-11-21 03:29:32 • Chris Keegan

Try making the angled lines the same angles an in the specific typeface, as it is, more or less on the second logo (picture8). I think a bolder typeface works better for this. Try aligning letters more, you can modify the letters to fit what you want. I agree with both gerald and chris, space out the letters a little.
