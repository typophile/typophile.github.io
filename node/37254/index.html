Overall I think it feels rather dated and also a bit childish, with the bright rainbow colors and the bold lines. First I would try to find a sharper and contemporary font, probably sans serif but not too bold and not too rounded. It’s probably good too use the rainbow, but I would make it less frivolous, and the lines more straight. It would indeed also be better to use black instead of the purple that is now dominant. Maybe it seems ironic but I would go for a slightly less ‘gay’ look overall… you don’t want to overstereotype, and I guess people could be put off if it’s too over the top.
