Clarification: the General family of 15 fonts — 5 weights x 3 widths — was released through Thirstype in July of 2003, and unreleased within 6 months.

General had an alternate schoolbook form of ‘a’, accessed via the contextual alternates OpenType feature. (In retrospect, not the best choice, but at the time one of the only features one could trust.)
