Number 4 is the best because you aren't fighting the typefaces. But "TREND" will be hard to read if it's small, far away, or the viewer is in motion (driving). I'm with Rez: re-think what the purpose of the logo is and what qualities it is meant to show. Szépség is a serif face that's interesting but not too special, so your logo will want to be subtle and sophisticated. If you want to be playful, find type that works together at being playful. Good luck.
