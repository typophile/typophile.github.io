Agency Gothic was uppercase only. David Berlow added a lowercase for Font Bureau Agency http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/fontbureau/fb-agency/ and added additional weights and widths. Nick Curtis has combined it with the lowercase from a 19th century font by James Conner’s Sons called Square Gothic to make Warp Three NF http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/nicksfonts/warp-three-nf/ Don
