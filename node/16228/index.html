“Simeon Out West” is the foundry name for fonts by Brett T. Johnson.

Brett T. Johnson began with Latin alphabet fonts based on letter forms from hand-lettered Eastern Orthodox manuscripts. Additional fonts are based on Egyptian Coptic lettering. Most, if not all, also contain a full set of Cyrillic letters.

Simeon Out West sells fonts through myfonts.com.

Available fonts include: Svati Nikolai Muscovite Manuscript Pravoslavnie Kniga Molitva Suzdal Vladimir Thebes Alexandria Scetis
