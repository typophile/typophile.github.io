It's possible that someone used a typeface for the "ocean club" logo, but the constructs are so easy to draw. (They are actually simplified parallel and concentric outlines, not what most typefaces are about.) Essentially only three character -- o, e and n. The c just is cut from the o. The e was flipped and rotated for the a and b. The n was flip for the u, and bisected to create the l.
