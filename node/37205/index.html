A quick comparison of Cronos Pro Light reveals the same price ($35) at every retailer I know:

MyFonts FontShop FontHaus Fonts.com Phil’s Fonts

Veer doesn’t sell single weights. Their family price is the same as Adobe’s: $199.

This is the case with most fonts of a particular foundry – the prices really don’t vary much from shop to shop. Rather than bargain hunting, your friend’s best bet is to go with the company she feels most confident in. Think about support and service after the purchase.
