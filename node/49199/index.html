hello typophile! I'm working on a comic book logo, wanting it to reference the gorgeous old auto-shop signs that you see rusting all over the US. I am aware that a lot of these were hand crafted by the sign shops that were commissioned for the them, but I was hoping someone can give me a handful of suggestions of typefaces I could start from when illustrating the logo. examples... Powell Garage is in a style that was very common in the 1930s and '40s. There are a few typefaces inspired by it: Futura Display LTC Tourist Gothic FF Moderne Gothics Refrigerator MVB Solano Gothic Gothic Medium Condensed Deluxe Mayo Corpus Gothic I also whipped something up in FontStruct that emulates the style, but because it's strictly modular it's not nearly as refined as most of the above. Please let me know if you end up using it.
