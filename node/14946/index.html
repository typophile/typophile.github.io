Boston Area Typophiles, Unite! TypeCon2006 Wants You...

September 1, 2005 • tamye

[[SOTA]] is making plans for its eighth conference, TypeCon2006, which will be held in Boston next July. Anyone in Boston/New England interested in volunteering and helping plan the conference should get in touch now. It’s fun, relatively painless, and you can have a say in making TC06 the best [[TypeCon]] ever. We’ll be coming to Beantown in October for planning meetings, but in the meantime, we’re starting on programming, workshops, venues, design, tours, and much more.

To get involved, post here and/or email me privately at tamye /at/ typesociety.org, and I’ll be in touch.

Non-Bostonians are also very welcome and encouraged to volunteer and help create a wonderful event! We have such an amazing group of volunteers each year - I can’t wait to work with old friends and new faces.
