Hi people, have a couple of short writings about typefaces. a short and sweet look at the Neutral typeface project and a more in-depth one from Kris Sowersby on Galaxie Polaris. Cheers Duncan http://www.experimenta.co.nz/Neutral.html http://www.experimenta.co.nz/Galaxie.html
This is a really interesting topic. At this point there are so many choices with such subtle differences within the classification of san serifs, that the one you choose to use is going to reflect some sort of intention. But even information that is desired to be neutral, is never desired to be boring, or at least un-noticed. So when a designer wants something to be "neutral" what are they really trying to say? Or perhaps the idea of neutrality changes. You have to change in order to remain neutral.
