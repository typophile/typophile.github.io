Your cleaned up version certainly seems to indicate a grunge font was used, and not something messed up afterwards. I'm not sure how to account for the apparent variation in the I's, but they are basically altered in similar ways, with that large chunk out of the lower left edge. I know you checked Dafont, but of course they can't have every grunge font. I would guess making a custom grunge font isn't too hard (judging from how many of them there are). There are plenty in the commercial font world too -- have you checked some of the large collections that let you search with keywords? I just did that at MyFonts and what do you know? [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/pizzadude/damaged-guts/|Damaged Guts]] from Pizza Dude matches it exactly, if you squoosh™ it. - Mike Yanega
