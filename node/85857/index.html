http://www.allfreefonts.com/font-539-galapogos_brk.html

More specifically, I am using the word “know” in all lowercase:

http://www.allfreefonts.com/custom-thumb.html?d=539&fontfilename=galapog…

The features that appeal to me are:

  1. Sans-serif

  2. No flags on letters like “n” (or whatever that straight part extending on the top left of the letter is called)

  3. All letters follow the “n” form. So, the “w” is like a double upside down “n” and the bottom of the “k” is “n” as well. The “o” is basically 2 “n” against one another, although I am more lenient on the “o” not following suit perfectly (as long as it flows).

  4. This goes along with #2 but basically 99% of all fonts have the “k” and “w” as sharp. Instead of the “k” looking like an “h” they have 2 straight lines making them up. The “w” ends up looking like double “v” rather than a double “u” and it kills it.

  5. Something bold please. It’ll be used for a logo

  6. If it were free for commercial use… that’d be great. If not, at least inexpensive to buy with a license that allows me to use the lettering for a logo.

Any and all help would be greatly appreciated. Thanks!
