2009-02-15 01:31:30 • ben_archer
Some observations at a glance:
  1. The metaphor hand-as-a-dove has been profusely used; it's not necessarily a weakness, but I guess I've seen that precise image before (try these bad samples: [[http://www.alohapeaceproject.com/images/index/Logo.jpg|Aloha]], [[http://www.coalitionforkids.ca/images/coalition-for-kids-dove-logo.png|CFKI]] or [[http://scarletowls.urfcomics.com/images/AboutBITHTileLogo.gif|BITH]]).
  2. The compass doesn't seem that to me; I see a quirky 16-hours-clock, instead; probably you should quit some elements (it is quite busy), and enlarge orthogonal beams in the rose; better: you could try tilting the vertical ones.
  3. Perhaps the disconnection you notice is in the font itself –I wonder who's its designer–, because it looks oddly geometric –and I appreciate Geometry a lot–, really ugly as sin (or that's what my displeased perception feels).
Nevertheless, I think your concept has potential. Just need more sensitive work. MarS
