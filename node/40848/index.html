Luke.

I don’t know that you will find a font so recognized as being associated with “booze” that it will work for you. An homage to a particular label would be more likely to work visually.

What I don’t get, in general, is defining a group in relation to something they don’t participate in. By the time you’ve named the group, and then strapped the members with a mark that recalls some alcohol product it would seem that you’ve put an almost obsessive emphasis on something you don’t have in common and maybe don’t want to emphasize.

There is a Pabst font that you might consider.
