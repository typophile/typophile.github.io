It must be a free “pixel font” named [[http://www.dafont.com/c-c-red-alert-inet.font?text=Spieloptionen+%2F+Grafikoptionen+%2F+Euer+Name|C&C Red Alert]] by N3tRunn3r

If you zoom in your image and take a look at the pixels, it looks like an exact match ;)

I found it using Find my Font - http://www.findmyfont.com
