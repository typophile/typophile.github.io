I don’t know when I’ll be in Worms again, but I can say this about the images you linked to (since I took them in the first place ;-) )

The grave markers are in the floor of the cathedral’s western apse, that’s the smaller apse down on ground level at the back end of the church.

Whoever looks at the markers should mark their dates (not visible in my old photos :-( ), but also remember that the dates of said persons' deaths do not necessarily equal the date that the grave markers were made. Bodies get moved a lot, especially those of important people.
