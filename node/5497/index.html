From my priceless Emigre magazine issue 13 (1989) "Redesigning Stereotypes", the contribution by Erik Spiekermann contains some information on DIN. DIN is the German Industrial Norm. This norm quantizes loads of different items, which each get their DIN number, probably to allow to identify and catalogue them. Both the popular industrial typeface DIN Mittelschrift and the Isonorm typeface for technical drawings seem to carry number 1451.
