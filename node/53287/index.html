Arial Black is the font that I chose to represent Martin Luther King Jr. I felt the characteristics of the font displayed King’s attributes well; for example, King was a bold individual and when he spoke he was assertive and stood strong as a leader. Arial Black is a strong, bold typeface that gives me a sense of assertiveness and communicates an intensity like King communicated people’s rights.
