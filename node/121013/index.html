EDIT: Trebuchet has been used for “light,” but the italic is faux—it’s an artificially slanted version of the roman, which is why the g isn’t a match to the true italic version of Trebuchet. At first, I thought that “Neuro” may be Brown by Lineto, but then I realized that it, too, was just a faux italic of Trebuchet Bold. Your first instincts were right, Darren. And the MS version of Trebuchet appears to be a good match.
