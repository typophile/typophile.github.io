2011-07-03 09:28:59 • Vaatika
Something that should have been asked at the very beginning: What is it about? Nothing like Bree for a long document, please! In fact the only sans I would personally recommend for long text is Legato (which however probably does not harmonize with Bree). Karmina: Note the very large x-height - suitable for ~9 point. Is that a size that works here? Unless you're trying to economize space, for a long document it seems too harsh in terms of readability. Excelsior: Are you looking for an old newspaper look? Otherwise I'd stay away from that class of type. Also, there's the same issue with the large x-height. BTW, I'm not sure you need something narrow - and certainly that can't be the only thing holding the two fonts together. But you might still look at the work of Sumner Stone, who seems to have a penchant for narrow type. hhp
