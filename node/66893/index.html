I’ve tangled with identifying this font before but at that time there was no Typophile to help. Hopefully this mystery can be cracked once and for all. This time its on a decal I’m remaking. Note the G is rounded on the outside corners but has straight corners inside. It also looks to have flat sides on all sides unlike the Eurostyle G. Very clean straight font. I’ll post two examples. One is the original italic look and the other is a version I straightened in case it’s a modified font.

It’s very likely that this is custom lettering for the logo. You might like FF Oxide or Stratum 2.
