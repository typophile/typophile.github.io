Remember you can specify multiple options for fonts in CSS. The browser will use the first if it’s installed, then move on to the second, then third, etc.

So you can specify an ideal-world font, then more common near matches, and finally just by sans or serif. eg.

font-family: Avant-Garde, Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; font-family: Adobe Garamond, Garamond, Times New Roman, serif;

But given that Apples are less and less likely to have Microsoft products on, not sure you can rely on their fonts being present any more.
