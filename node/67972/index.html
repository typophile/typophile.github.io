March 2, 2010 • Bald Condensed

I recently received the list of TDC2 winners, to help me prepare my FontFeed posts in time. Don't ask me anything, I am bound by professional secrecy. Although there is some really great stuff in there, on the whole I am not entirely happy with the results. This made me wonder if this simply was a somewhat leaner year, or if maybe the jury overlooked some worthy submissions (in my not-so-humble opinion). Of course I can only tell if I also know which submissions didn't receive a Certificate of Excellence in Type Design. So if you entered this year's TDC2 type design contest please e-mail me, and if there is no sample of your typeface available online please include a PDF or image or whatever. Thank you all for your co-operation. I hope I can turn this into an interesting post.
BTW I will not divulge any of the type designs that didn't win.
