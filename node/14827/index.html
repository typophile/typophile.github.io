That would be Berthold Garamond, which Adobe no longer sells. You can still get it from Berthold.

Garamond No. 2 SB and URW Garamond are also close matches and you don’t have to buy the whole family. Be aware, though, that since they are knock-offs of Berthold Garamond, Günter Gerhard Lange, the designer, would receive no royalties, which (presumably) he does when you buy from Berthold.
