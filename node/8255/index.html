This post is just to try and help with the identification process. I am not an expert at this stuff, so there's probably someone else with better advice. Picture 1 Picture 2 Picture 3 You asked for 3 fonts, so I'm assuming it's the "JMC" in the shield logo from picture 2, the blocky "JMC" from picture 1 and the "racing" script from picture 1. Based on the above, I would say: 1. The shield logo is most likely hand drawn. Similar to Copperplate Gothic Bold Condensed. 2. "JMC" seems to be in the same font as "California(?) Racing Designs" on the package in picture 3. Sneakers is pretty close, but there may be a better match. 3. The "racing" script may also be hand-drawn. Debonair JF comes close, but it will need a lot of tweaking. Maybe someone else knows the right one. Note, the fonts used in the headline of the website are not the same as any of the fonts that appear in the images. - Lex
