It hurt the eyes...

April 20, 2005 • rezmason

I was sitting in a presentation the other day- so I know that the font I'm about to discuss is included with either Macs or Microsoft Office. I couldn't help wincing at the projector screen. I mean, this font was awful. Serifs were inconsistently proportioned, which gave it a jagged and flawed feeling. I left the conference room with a big headache. The most notable trait of this font was that the Q was simply an O with a squiggle beneath it- there was no connection between the ellipse and the bottom stroke. My morbid curiosity made me wonder what font it was. Remember, I saw it in a Powerpoint presentation, so I doubt that it's uncommon. Apart from the Q, the key feature of the font is the serifs that wound the reader. Does anyone know what this font may be?
I'm going to guess Cochin. Am I right?
