[[http://prowrestling.wikia.com/wiki/Ox_Baker|Douglas “Ox” Baker]] won the NWA Texas Heavyweight Championship in 1977, which is probably what the T-shirts pictured here refer to. In the 7os, custom messages on T-shirts were most often produced with iron-on letters designed and made for that purpose. They weren’t copied from any specific font designed for typesetting, although they often resembled workhorse bold sans-serifs like Franklin Gothic.
