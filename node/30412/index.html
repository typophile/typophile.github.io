In Paul Felton’s Ten Commandments he uses Stempel Garamond. He uses alot of ligatures (ct & st -similar to caslon’s ligatures) and I was wondering if anyone knew which Stempel Garamond he used? I have linotypes and the opentype version however they only have two ligatures (ff & fl)

Cheers

Rick

There is only one digital Stempel Garamond commercially available. Stempel Garamond is the Garamond cut by the D. Stempel AG, which became part of Linotype in 1985.

Sabon Next, which is Jean François Porchez’s revival, redesign, and extension of Sabon, has more ligatures. Sabon was produced by Stempel in the 1960s, and bears many similarities to Stempel Garamond. http://www.linotype.com/53159/sabonnext-family.html
