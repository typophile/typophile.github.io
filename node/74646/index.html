Actually, Bernhard Modern is incorrect. This is [[http://www.fonthaus.com/fonts/vintagetype/pabst_oldstyle/vt10054|Pabst Oldstyle Roman]] by Vintage Type and sold by FontHaus. Compare its letterforms, and you’ll find that Pabst Oldstyle has the same antiqued letterforms as your posted example. This version of Pabst Oldstyle has slight differences from the more popular [[http://new.myfonts.com/fonts/lanston/ltc-pabst-oldstyle/|LTC Pabst Oldstyle]] by Lanston. Bernhard Modern is too refined, and the letterforms aren’t the same shapes. (Compare the capital R.)
