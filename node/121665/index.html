Custom font by Nico Schweizer....close match?

November 30, 2014 • jilljill

Hello Everyone,

I am in love with a custom typeface by Nico Schweizer created for Phaidon’s publication of Area. It is called Typ 1441. Lineto sells Schweitzer’s Typ 1451 (http://lineto.com/1.0/content_5.html) but it is too wide for my purposes (a design c.v.). I was wondering if anyone could recommend a close match?

Thanks much.

The whole point of a custom typeface is that it’s unique; the following suggestions are decent substitutes based on overall form and proportions, but may miss on some of the details (DIN comes closest on both the numerals and the letters).

ITC Conduit: http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/itc/conduit/

Modus: http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/garagefonts/modus/

DIN Text Condensed, by Parachute: http://www.parachute.gr/typefaces/allfonts/din-text-condensed-pro
