The concave style in _MAY THE BIRD..._ may not have made it into the digital era. The closest digital for the bottom three quarters could be Fredericksburg NF, an interpretation of the 19th century design called "Teutonic" [img:sites/default/files/old-images/FredericksburgNFAsMaySM_5548.jpg] http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/nicksfonts/fredericksburg-wbw/ The top quarter is a "wanted poster" reverse contrast style as shown in many "wild west fonts" such as Buffalo Western [img:sites/default/files/old-images/BuffaloWesternasMAY_4327.jpg] http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/kustomtype/buffalo-western/ Don

'MAY THE BIRD...': completely new to me this one; a cross-genre French Clarendon/Italienne x Tuscan Concave; couldn't find anything similar; best option would be what Don said: start with 'Teutonic' (another version [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/woodentypefonts/teutonic|here]]) and add large fat top serifs; you could also do it the other way round and starting from a Tuscan Concave Slab like [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/woodentypefonts/number-154|Number 154]] reworking all bottoms; 'LITTLE JIMMY DICKENS': interesting digital alternative with Sergiy Tkachenko's [[http://www.behance.net/gallery/3373761/Sports-World-(Typeface)|Sports World]] of 4th February;
