2007-10-01 19:26:27 • umlautthoni

Mark, Yes, I was in Nam in 1967-68. I was in MCB6. Our home base was in Chu Lai in the I core. We worked on highway 1 from Quang Nai to Quang Tri but I spent most of my time in Quang Tin. The SeaBees were very involved in Viet Nam, mostly supporting the Marines. During my last few months in Nam, including the Tet offensive of 1968, the Marines 3rd MAF moved further north to Que San and were replaced by the Army’s Americal Division. The Mi Lai massacre happened nearby during my tour.
