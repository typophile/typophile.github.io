Chris – Rich was modestly responding only to Dana’s specific requests. Each link goes to one of the three specific families she mentioned.

Dana – Be aware that the Poynter Gothic series is not available for individual retail license. It is part of Font Bureau’s Studio library, which is only licensed by contacting someone in the FB sales office directly. The Studio library fonts are geared towards larger publishers and require a minimum of 11 seats.
