DeSoto just released at Veer

June 3, 2009 • Stephen Rapp

DeSoto is a family of 4 faces based on a few letters from a 1958 DeSoto magazine ad. It's an extended width titling face with a luxurious yet warm feel. Each face is fully loaded with features including old style numbers, case-sensitive punctuation, alternates and ligatures, fractions, swashes and ornaments, and Central European language support. Joe Newton at Veer created the butterflies with ornaments and swashes. [img:sites/default/files/old-images/DeSoto-butterflies_5206.gif] [img:sites/default/files/old-images/CTT0000238_P_5708.gif]
