FontArk, the browser based font creator continue evolving.

We’ve just equipped FontArk’s system with fresh new Outline editing tools, leaving the “mono-linear” days behind. FontArk’s synchronized Skeleton infrastructure was built to enable this. You can now control the Outline Nodes freely in relation to the Skeleton Nodes. Although not covering all the possibilities out there (yet), it covers pretty much and enable super fast and flexible prototyping and experimenting.

This video gives a taste of the new functionality: http://youtu.be/jjgo7boQnxo

Full upgrade details + video tutorial here: http://fontark.net/farkwp/december-2014-upgrade-advance-outline-editing-is-here/

All the features are freely available to test and use at www.fontark.net Comments and experience with it are most welcome
