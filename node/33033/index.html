The whole layout is from the first (1934) American edition of “Ulysses”, designed by Ernst Reichel — according to Ned Drew & Paul Sternberger’s “By Its Cover—Modern American Book Cover Design” (Princeton Arcitectural Press, 2005). The initials look indeed very much like Truesdell but I think they must be hand-lettered. The text face may be Baskerville.
