2008-11-18 19:48:25 • hrant

The list of services on the back side of the card now appear to be off-center towards the right. Suggust you move the two columns to the left so that the left margin is the same as the left margin on the front of the card.

The bullets look a little weak. How about using a plus sign instead of the dot? That would tie into the plus signs in the main display line.

The type for italic blurb on the back side is a little light given the arwork behind it. Hopefully the font you’re using has a medium weight that falls between the light and bold weights you’re using on the card now.

Unlike the earlier post, I like the idea of repeating the contact info on the back. That way no matter which side of the card you pickup, you have the phone number available.
