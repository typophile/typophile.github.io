Typophile Sponsorships

December 16, 2009 • Zara Evens

About Typophile

Typophile launched in April 2000. It aims to further typography as a craft as well as a profession by providing an open, collaborative environment where users contribute and access content for free.

Typophile is produced, designed and maintained by Punchcut, a San Francisco based interface design company. Typophile Film Fest sponsors, advertisers and paying members provide the necessary financial support to keep Typophile thriving. Typophile has been featured in numerous online venues and in world class print publications like Metropolis, Applied Arts Magazine, STEP, Tupigraphia and How.

About the Audience

Typophile users are professional graphic designers, design educators and type designers. They thrive on collaboration – sharing information, discussing industry trends, and making product recommendations.

Aspiring and professional type designers post their work for peer critique. Currently the Type Critique Section holds over 1,000 fonts for review by designers from around the world. Graphic designers come to Typophile to be inspired, see what new fonts are available and get technical insights from peers.

Site Traffic

Registered users: 51,500+

Total page views per month: 850,000 (avg for 2009)

Homepage views per month: 80,000 (avg for 2009)

Approximately 60% of visitors are in North America, 30% Europe, and the rest South America, India, Asia, etc. The majority of registered users identify themselves as graphic designers. Type designers and design educators are also well represented.

a. Banner Advertising

Opportunity: Serve advertisements and/or text links in specific content areas, enabling sponsors to target specific sections like book reviews, type identification forums or technical discussions.

Duration: Month to month, with an initial 3 month commitment. (Not required for past advertisers.)

Cost per thousand (CPM): $3 per 1000 impressions

Example campaign A: 100,000 impressions per month = $300/mo Example campaign B: 300,000 impressions per month = $900/mo

b. Featured Face Sponsor

Opportunity: This unique offering allows any typeface to be displayed prominently as the large text on the Typophile homepage and throughout all forum and thread headers. The unique homepage is an up-to-the-minute, user-posted content set in the sponsor’s 70 point type. The sponsors’ fonts provide a great opportunity to debut a font or showcase a classic. Users are able to interact (as shown in the screen below) and see more of the typeface. Sponsors will also receive banner space identifying the typeface and a link to the sponsor web site.

Duration: Week to week, 1 font exclusive per week.

Cost: $500/wk
