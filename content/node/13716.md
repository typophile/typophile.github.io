body: "<strong>[[Indices]] : [[Libraries]] : St. Bride Printing Library</strong>\r\n\r\nThe
  St Bride Printing Library is located in [[London]], [[England]]. It is one of the
  largest libraries of printing history in the world. It\u2019s collection particularly
  surveys British printing and typographic works from the 19th and 20th centuries.
  Its reading room is open to the general public. \r\n\r\nThe [[Friends of St Bride
  Printing Library|Friends of St Bride]] has been organizing annual conferences in
  the library every autumn since 2002. These have all had a typographic focus. Past
  conference themes have included Hidden Typography, Bad Type and Temporary Type.
  2005\u2019s conference will be organized by Typevents on behalf of the Friends and
  the Library."
