Typophile + iPhone

October 3, 2007 • Joe Pemberton

I wanted to post a quick tip for iPhone users (browser stats suggest there are a growing number of you)…

Simply create a bookmark on your iPhone of the Typophile Tracker, http://www.typophile.com/tracker

This gives you a view of all the current and recent posts (wiki, forums, blogs) listed chronologically. No this isn’t new, but it should be useful until Apple supports the Flash player in Safari Mobile.

Posted from my phone… Cheers.
