As a start, you might consider Elzevir (or, in this case, Romana, which claims to be based on Elzevir), or a simple classic Garamond. A copy of L'Art et Les Artistes from 1925 which I am looking at right now uses Garamond throughout for body copy, and Curtis Post for subheads.
