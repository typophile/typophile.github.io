PI, the brand dates from 1960, and it is quite likely that the script was hand-lettered. As such, it was never a typeface that was commercially available.

And while Font Bureau’s Titling Gothic is a decent match for the other type, it’s more likely that it is the inspiration for Titling Gothic (which was designed in 2005), a typeface called ATF Railroad Gothic.
