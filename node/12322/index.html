[[Indices]] : [[Designers]] : Kevin Kegler Kevin Kegler is a Professor of Graphic Design at [[Daemen College]] in Amherst, New York. As well, he is a practicing designer and sculptor. Type design has evolved as a natural progression from his broad based approach to visual communication. Kegler has been the principal designer at First Hand Learning, Inc, a company he cofounded, and currently is the owner/director/principal at worknotwork in Buffalo, NY.
