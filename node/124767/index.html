I’m assuming from the name of the file and the style of lettering that this is from a fire engine.

This derives from actual hand-painted signage. Even today, when the lettering is a decal (rather than hand-painted), most are derived from hand-painted alphabets/templates, and not all have equivalents in the digital type world.

Closest typeface I could find is Flange: http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/berthold/flange-bq/

Similar, though the legs of the |R|s are straight, not curved, and the serifs are more pronounced: http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/ortizlopez/ol-signpainter-titling/ http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/parkinson/modesto/
