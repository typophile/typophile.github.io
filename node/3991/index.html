Dante - I like your magazine. I hope you don't mind a few
small criticisms.

- This type of medium (double page clickthru mag) is mostly
wonderful. I like that it allows the viewer to focus on each piece
without distraction. But what's common among all of the mags
of this type is that it's not easy to get at the artist credits. For
example, I'm looking at the work and I've clicked by the author
intro page and then I hit something I love. I have to page back
to see who it is. Would it be too intrusive to add a small note
below the magazine "frame" with the current artist info?

- Revol and Contribute both share unfortunately messy tables of
contents. If the artists are the most important part of the
publication, why not make their names and info as readable
as possible?

- Do you provide links to contributors' personal sites? If there
are, I missed them.

Overall, nice work. (I like the logo, I'm a sucker for antlers.)
