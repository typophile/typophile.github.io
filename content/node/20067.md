body: "<strong>[[Indices]] : [[Designers]] : Fiona Ross</strong> \r\n\r\nFiona Ross
  specializes in non-Latin [[type design]] and [[typography]]. From 1978 to 1989 she
  worked for [[Linotype]] Limited (UK) and was responsible for the design of Linotype\u2019s
  non-Latin fonts and typesetting schemes. Since 1989 she has worked as a consultant,
  type designer and visiting lecturer, and in 2003 joined the <a href=\"http://www.rdg.ac.uk/Typography/home.html\">Department
  of Typography & Graphic Communication</a> at the [[University of Reading]], teaching
  non-Latin typeface design on the [[MA in Typeface Design]] programme, and academic
  writing skills for all postgraduates in the Department.\r\nFiona Ross holds a BA
  in German, a PgDip in Sanskrit and Pali, and a PhD in Indian Palaeography from <a
  href=\"http://www.soas.ac.uk/\">SOAS</a> (School of Oriental and African Studies:
  University of London). She is a member of the [[ATypI]] (Association Typographique
  International) and a Fellow of the Royal Society of Arts.\r\n\r\n<strong>Additional
  Articles:</strong>\r\n<a href=\"http://www.itcfonts.com/Ulc/2611/BookRevBengaliChar.htm\">U&lc:
