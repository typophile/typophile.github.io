Doyens of Speculative Type Design

December 12, 2011 • hrant

I would like to compile a list of living designers who have exhibited high insight into "speculative" type design: works that push the boundaries with no firm hope of success. These don't have to be finished fonts, they just have to exhibit unorthodox thought, hopefully causing others to expand their design horizons. If you list anybody it would be nice to mention specifically why. This list might include graphic designers who have not made type themselves but have exhibited a strong appreciation for this "genre". I'll start off with the obvious: Neville Brody (Fuse). hhp
Two you already mentioned elsewhere: - Craig Eliason (Ambicase Modern) - Cuttlefish (Antherton Cloister) One that I'm not sure has an appeal outside the specific habitat (Fontstruct), but has the guts of trying: - Neurone Error (http://new.myfonts.com/foundry/Abneurone_Trauma_Types/)
