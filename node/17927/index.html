Not entirely sure that it is the weight of the P icon. I would say that the "a" and the "d" of Avant Garde possess less contrast which helps them work with the icon. Second, the four counters in the Avant Garde (o,a,o,d) have the optical effect of mimicking part of the angular P structure. Proxima Nova is effectively rather beautiful, but seems to me to be thrown by the proximity of such a geometric form. Am inclined to agree with Miss Tiffany. mc
