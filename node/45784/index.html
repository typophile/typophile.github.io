I know it’s a very subjective thing to suggest alternative fonts, not knowing exactly what you are looking for, but in terms of general feel Mark Jamra’s [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/itc/jamille/|ITC Jamille]] is a bit darker, but it is also a transitional style and does have SC and OSF.

You can also use the [[http://www.bowfinprintworks.com/SerifGuide/intro.php|Serif Font ID Guide]] to find other serifs with characteristics you like from Slimbach. It’s not only an ID tool. You can compare text samples of many of the fonts to see if the color and openness are what you are looking for, and see the italics at the same time.
