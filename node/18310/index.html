HI I am french, that’s maybe why I NEVER manage to use hints properly. if i generate PS1 automatic hints: when i print samples with xpress or illustator, some letters like U don’t lay on the blue zone, some others, like E, just jump to the top blue zone. So it’s impossible to judge if my drawnings are correct or not! is there something i should know about that? please help!
I don’t have a very technical understanding of this one, but my experience has been that these problems are usually caused by inprecise placement of points. Sometimes in the font editor it looks like the points are aligned correctly, but if you zoom way in you will see that they are off by one or two units. If the points at the top of your U are even one unit below the blue zone, it will render incorrectly. This is also true for overshoots that extend above the blue zone.
