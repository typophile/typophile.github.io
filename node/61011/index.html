CakeType news: Aniek typeface released

August 15, 2009 • Pieter van Rosmalen

Dear Typophilers,

CakeType is proud to announce the release of Aniek.

Aniek is based on a real non-connected handwriting, with an angle to the left. The typeface is designed for optimal readability in small sizes but also works well in larger sizes. The family includes seven weights from Light to Black with an extended (Latin) character set. OpenType features include slashed zero and alternative shapes for the f and fi ligature.
