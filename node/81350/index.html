Oak Knoll Press is having a 50% off sale

April 18, 2011 • blank

I don’t meant to spam, but I think this is of legitimate interest to Typophiles. Oak Knoll is selling many Oak Knoll press titles at 50% off right now. The sale includes books about Tschichold, Goudy, early specimens at Plantin-Moretus, and brings the price of Oak Knoll’s reprint of Updike’s Printing Types to $25.00. It’s a good chance to fill out a library without paying high prices for what is often not the best printing.
