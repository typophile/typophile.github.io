2008-04-25 01:57:34 • i cant delete my username

First, neither of these are typefaces. This wonderful mark was hand lettered.

I strongly advise against using a typefaces here — especially for the script. I love type and I could recommend a few fonts that we sell, but this sort of logo was meant to be drawn by hand on a circle and that’s nearly impossible to achieve well with a font. Get a [[lettering]] artist like Mark Simonson to assist you. Your client will appreciate a unique, handcrafted mark with letters that no one else can replicate.
