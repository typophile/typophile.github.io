Hello folks, Tried everything I know but can’t get an exact match for this script font. Nearest I got was Englische Schreibschrift medium. Also tried putting just the ‘L’ through MyFonts.com but that only threw up Gwendolyn ROB. Any ideas…?

Hi Paul. I think this is a handrawn capital L repositioned to sit in front of a slightly condensed and redrawn Shelley Script - either the Allegro or Andante. My guess is that the ‘L’ in all three variants of the Shelley weren’t quite readable enough… also the connection between the ‘o’ and ‘n’ at the end has been jazzed up with a superfluous ball terminal.

I agree with the idea that something has been customized, but my guess at the font used is Englische Schreibschrift (Kuenstler was close). I think the tail on the L has been redrawn, because the only L I found that looked like that ‘out of the box’ was from York Script, but other letters were not very close. This link shows English 157, which is Bitstream’s version of the script. I think the ’s' and ‘o’ characters are closer than Kuenstler.

Not sure if Test drives show connections well, because they are not kerned properly, but I suspect that the connectors were also drawn in for the Lanson logo.
