Type workshop The Hague

October 17, 2010 • Jean Paul Beumer

From 25 October until 29 November VIDE organises six type workhops. Each workhop will be held on Mondays from 5 pm - 9 pm, meal included.

Workshop #1 Type Anatomy Monday 25 October - #1.1 Letter construction Monday 1 November - #1.2 Painting and sketching letters

Workshop #2 Type Production Monday 8 November - #2.1 Drawing and designing letters Monday 15 November - #2.2 Digitising letters

Workshop #3 Type Experimentals Monday 22 November - #3.1 Re-Use Type Monday 29 November - #3.2 Redesign Type

Prices € 65 (individual workshop) - € 300 (all six workshops)

For more information, surf to http://www.viavide.com, or contact me for the e-newsletter (Dutch) at jeanpaulbeumer@gmail.com
