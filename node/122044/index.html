Hi there, I’ve having trouble figuring this one out (the sans, I know the italic is Cooper Black Italic). It’s not Helvetica, or Akzidenz, or Univers. The t, a, y don’t match. I think it might be a PLINC face, but the catalog I have doesn’t show the whole alphabet for comparison. Then again it’s a little stretched-out looking. Any ideas? Thanks. [img:sites/default/files/old-images/10869493_10153741422929368_3354803671837947311_o_4769.jpg]

Seems to be New Wide Nine, by the less-than-reputable OPTI Castcraft company (now out of business): http://opti.netii.net/fonts-i/index.htm

If you want a substitute with better provenance, Vonness by Font Bureau is a decent option (you’ll have to edit the |y|): http://www.fontbureau.com/fonts/Vonness/

The font used on the album is a Photo-Lettering Inc. font called Improvenu Wide 9. I have not seen any digital version other than the one by OPTI, so the OPTI font seems to be an original digital creation. But there are legal issues if any OPTI font is used for commercial purposes because OPTI is defunct and its fonts are orphans without known legal owners. Don
