MR & MRS gameshow typeface

September 24, 2009 • alfrost

I’m trying to identify the font used in the MR & MRS logo from the shoddy 1970’s gameshow. It looks strangely familiar but I can’t put my finger on it. Any sggestions?

The lettering is certainly post-70’s, since there were no DVD’s then, but I can’t find an exact match for the MR & MRS lettering. [[http://new.myfonts.com/fonts/bagraphics/metro-gothic/|Metro Gothic]] seems closest for the M and R; The ‘&" looks a lot like the one in [[http://new.myfonts.com/fonts/bomparte/sidewalk-cafe/|Sidewalk Cafe]]; and the S is similar to [[http://new.myfonts.com/fonts/urw/neuland/|Neuland]], for one suggestion. Other Art Deco fonts may have been involved, and some customization might be needed in any case, but I think these would help you get close unless someone knows the identical font.
