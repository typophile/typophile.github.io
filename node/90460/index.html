Where do you see the difficulties in 10pt? Print of web? Interstate isn’t all too bad in print, e.g. the normal light should work alright. http://www.fontbureau.com/media/pdf/Interstate.pdf

Combining two sans serif typefaces is always difficult. Could look a little itchy if you paired Interstate with a humanist sans, but maybe Frutiger works, try it. Those are supposed to be the the most readable. If you need a typeface which works well on the web, too, try Benton Sans. There is a special version called Benton Sans RE especially designed for small sizes. http://www.webtype.com/catalog/?tssans=693&wssmall=829&wsmedium=866&foundryfb=35
