I don’t know if anyone else noticed but today saw a phenomenal release of type news and marketing. In one day, I received an email from FontShop promoting the gorgeous new [[http://www.fontshop.com/features/newsletters/nov2007_a/ | FF Meta Serif]] (congratulations to Erik Spiekermann, Christian Schwartz, and Kris Sowersby), an email from House Industries noting that its new [[http://www.houseind.com/index.php?page=showfont&id=619 | Neutraface Complete]] is on sale until December 1, an email from Veer for Alejandro Paul’s new [[http://www.veer.com/ideas/feelscript/| Feel Script]], an email from ITC for its (questionably useful) [[http://www.itcfonts.com/Fonts/NewFonts/2007/1107_Intro.htm?osadcampaign=IMP | ITC Intro]], as well as an email about the release of [[http://www.fontlab.com/font-converter/scanfont/ | ScanFont 5.0]] for Windows.

It either means that Wednesdays are good days to release type news or that font serendipity is a real thing. Or, perhaps, I’ve subscribed to too many type email newsletters and haven’t noticed the confluence before.

Mostly, I wonder, what do folks think of FF Meta Serif? To me, it is lovingly crafted and will be a strong addition to my serif collection.

There has been a lot of activity on FF Meta Serif in this thread.
