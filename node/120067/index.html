Closest I could find to Black is Americain (which would still require some editing):

http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/hanoded/americain/ http://www.hanodedfonts.com/?download=americain

Invert the K, lower the crossbar on the A, and add a wing to the B, then faux italicize the works, and you’re pretty close.

Fairview could be edited to work: http://www.losttype.com/font/?name=fairview

These free fonts could also be reworked to achieve the look of the sample: http://www.dafont.com/search.php?q=Monotone&text=BLACK http://www.dafont.com/gotham-nights.font?text=BLACK http://www.dafont.com/kenyan-coffee.font?text=BLACK
