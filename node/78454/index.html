A few suggestions: Typographic Posters of the 20th century http://www.teien-art-museum.ne.jp/exhibition/typograph/index.html Great exhibition! The venue is neat too, the museum is located in the former mansion of a Japanese prince. Nice garden for strolling around after the exhibit. The Printing Museum near Iidabashi station is interesting and they offer printing workshops: http://www.printing-museum.org/en/index.html The GGG gallery run by Dai-Nippon Printing in Ginza has good exhibits: http://www.dnp.co.jp/gallery/ggg_e/index.html You can find lots of art listings here: http://www.tokyoartbeat.com/
