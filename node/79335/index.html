I believe this is custom. It’s Art Deco-ish, but I cannot find anything that is even very close. If I was trying to reproduce it I think I’d start with Gill Sans Condensed (Humanist 521 is Bitstream version). I’d tweak the R proportions, then use the 8 to make the E, and borrow the S from a font like Casablanca Condensed.
