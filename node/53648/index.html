[img:sites/default/files/old-images/sniper_6673.jpg] All though this is an audio book cover and not an actual book, I think this is a perfect example of type being used with an image. I love how the main part of the title, "Shooter", is in the scope of a sniper rifle. It relates perfectly to what the story is about instead of a random image that could be misleading like some covers out there. Overall, I think it's just really cool and well put together. After I found the audio book, I decided to look online to see if there was also a regular book. Below are the images I found to go with it: [img:sites/default/files/old-images/20060401-shooter_5576.jpg] [img:sites/default/files/old-images/354275_4102.jpg] I think both images are good book covers, but I like the first one better.
