I’m trying to ID the font, (numbers), in order to make a matching face for another gauge.

![](/images/old-images/clock numbers_5354.jpg)

Hard to see details on your sample, which would make a definitive ID simpler.

The issue here is the 1–which normally has at least a top serif, even in this style (gothic sans) of typeface.

Trump Gothic Condensed gets the general shape right, and if you substitute a capital I for the numeral 1, you’ve got a decent match. http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/canadatype/trump-gothic-pro/

Block Gothic Extra Condensed isn’t bad either: http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/redrooster/block-gothic-rr/

Or Vonness: http://www.fontbureau.com/fonts/Vonness/
