Blender does exist in various versions and variations - as the name says: it has been blended, obviously - and some may now look like they are owing to Crouwel. But all of these are originating from a design by Norm, and that is not hearsay or rumour or interpretation, it is just a little-known hard fact. T-Star Mono is a different case, though - a conceptually lazy piece of work, relying on someone else's aesthetic and using their approach as a formula for quick success. Whether outlines have been copied or not is not the question here, and I wasn't saying that. I was talking about the blatant "me too" mentality of all things Gestalten that bores me out of my brain.
