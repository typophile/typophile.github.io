[[Indices]] : [[Foundries]] : OurType OurType is a [[Belgium|Belgian]] type foundry launched by [[Fred Smeijers]] and [[Rudy Geeraerts]] in 2002. Since 2003 it is managed by Fred, Rudy, and [[Corina Cotorobai]].
