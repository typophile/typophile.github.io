Hello from Trek,

As previously asserted here on Typophile, the Trek wordmark is custom typography. It was designed by Joe Sutter, formerly of Milwaukee’s Hanson Dodge + Sutter, who found inspiration in the classic Honda logotype. Having worked on the identity for our new world cup mountain bike team, I can confirm that WORLD RACING is indeed set in Herb Lubalin’s Avant Garde Gothic—ITC Avant Garde Gothic Std Book to be more specific.

Ken
