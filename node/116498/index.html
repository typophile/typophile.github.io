Thanks Hrant,

The ‘experiment’ here was mixing a fairly geometric regular, with a more calligraphic italic. To make them different enough have an interesting typographic color, but blending in well.

Many font foundries don’t do real italics anymore, that way they can commercialize the font sooner.

So, as I wanted the typeface to ‘stand out’, I spend more time working on the italics than on the regular versions, which was a first for me! :)
