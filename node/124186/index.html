Lost Logo Font

March 29, 2015 • dottored

[img:sites/default/files/old-images/Logo Font_6186.gif]

Our logo was created in 2003 by a designer we can no longer locate. This image is the only text in the logo and we have no other samples. We would like to use the font in a sister site we are creating. Any help you can provide will be much appreciated.

Appears to be Bitstream’s take on Albertus, called Flareserif 821, which is one of the few versions of the typeface with a bold weight: http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/bitstream/flareserif-821/

But I’m not sure if bold is heavy enough to match your sample. Oddly, none of the “official” versions of the typeface appear to be available in an even heavier weight. You may have to employ another clone, Adelon Serial, to match it: http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/softmaker/adelon-serial/
