Suppose there existed a program that allowed me to manage and sort my typefaces on a PC by publishing date and/or some kind of regional influence. What would that program be called?
by publishing date and/or some kind of regional influence Are you talking about Font Explorer (FE)? Not to be confused with the rather new FontExplorer X (FEX)! The former can [f]ind fonts fitting each historical era and [s]elect a mix of emotions and impressions and find the fonts that suit them all. Yet, I don’t think this app is suited for managing fonts. For that, the latter is the right thing – however the Windows version of that is currently not available due to revision. There’s a FAQ on the Best Font Management. Though there are tags like ‘designer’ or ‘description’ in FEX, you have to do the sorting yourself, I’m afraid.
