Do you mean “Eastern” where you write “Western”? I will assume so; otherwise, I have difficulty in making sense of your questions.

Each weight of a font is a separate font. Times Bold is a separate font from Times Roman. Many East Asian typefaces have two, four, or even eight different weights. However, these are mostly higher end typefaces sold for professional publishing, not so much with system fonts.

Kozuka Gothic and Kozuka Mincho are both Adobe Japanese fonts that come in a range of weights, some at least are probably bundled with your apps.
