Infant means the "a" and "g" are monocular (and sometimes the "y" has a "u" top), matching what a child would write by hand, hence presumably read with more ease. News means the vertical proportions are conducive for setting the font in small sizes with no (or even negative) leading and short line measures, on low-quality paper. Cyrillic means it contains the characters supporting typesetting in the Cyrillic alphabet, which typically means in the Russian language. hhp
