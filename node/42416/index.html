You are referring to the “villain’s theme” from the days of silent film. To the best of my knowledge, it’s one of those things with no certain credited origin. It appears to be available in a pure form on this album by Al Webber.

A version I can recommend is “The Musical Sacrifice” by P.D.Q. Bach (Peter Schickele), which uses the theme to parody J.S. Bach’s “A Musical Offering.”
