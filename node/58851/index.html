It is so crooked I think someone did his own type-design maybe adapted a typeface like Bauhaus. It’s definitely not a good typeface/logo. If you need anything like it search for geometric sans serif faces from the Bauhaus tradition. (check the curves of the “a” where it meets the stem they are nasty, the horizontal parts of the circles in the “a” are fatter then the vertical parts which should be the other way around for optical correction and don’t get me started on the “n”). Believe me this type is painful to look at. (no offense to the designer it’s just no good)
