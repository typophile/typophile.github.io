Mike mentions “drawn.” AFAIK this was indeed the method used to make quality business cards until relatively recently. The drawing was in the form of engraving. This may be explained in part by the fact that very thin typefaces were subject to damage during the metal-type era. BTW, I agree with Mike that Monotype’s Classic Roman is a good choice for a substitute. If you want a closer approximation, you could copy the sweeping leg on R to K. Don
