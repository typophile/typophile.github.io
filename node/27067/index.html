Hi y'all…I feel a bit chagrinned at not knowing this one, it's been everywhere last few years. I think this font (or one like it) goes back to the 40s or even earlier. What I note about it is the distinctive vertical 'O' (it has no curve on the sides of the bowl) and the leg of the 'R' is straight, no inflections. I always see this font in it's UC version it seems, and it is often altered to look degenrated as if from an old press where all the ink doesn't get transferred…anyway, probably a no brainer for all you typophiles out there.
