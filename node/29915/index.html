Do you have figures only for paragraph/section openers, i.c. drop caps? What’s the range, I mean, do you have also 10, 11 &c., that consist out of two figures?

In case you have figures only, you might give it a try with ‘hanging’ drop caps, placed into the margins and both lining with the first and resp. second or third line of text.

Try to avoid lining figs as much as possible. They are for tabular work and all caps settings (titling) only. (At least, that’s my old fashioned dada.)
