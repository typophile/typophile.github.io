Grotesk Polski FA is based on Antykwa Półtawskiego, digitalised by J. M. Nowacki, and available for free [[http://www.jmn.pl/poltawski.html|here]]. You could also try [[http://www.2plus3d.pl/artykuly/danova-typograficzne-spotkanie-z-jackiem-mrowczykiem|Danova]], by J. Mrowczyk, which bases on both typefaces (then contact 2plus3d.pl magazine). You can use Danova for longer texts; and that’s probably important.
