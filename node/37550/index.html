Type and Brand Consistency

September 26, 2007 • aluminum

I don’t know a whole lot about Ruby Tuesday. I’m sure I ate there once in my life at some mall. Around here they’ve been advertising a lot and noticed their new logo and thought, while nothing spectacular, is definitely a lot cleaner than a lot of the generican shopping mall eateries.

However, I noticed that their web site features imagery of both the logo and the front of their restaurants. Notice any inconsistencies? ;o)

Well, as with any corporation that has various locations, it can be difficult and expensive to update exterior signage all at once. Sometimes they do it by region, or by A, B, and C level locations based on sales.

Also, some stores may have the updated logo, but marketing has not scheduled or budgeted for a photographer to shoot the store.

That said, it still looks generic albeit in a more aesthetically pleasing way.
