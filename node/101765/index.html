Other revivals: Cochin Fournier Fournier-inspired: Corundum And of course any of the Fleischmann-derived styles, such as Fleischmann, Freight, Farnham or Mercury. For display, Mercury or Freight probably your best bet, although may be over-familiar from periodical use.
