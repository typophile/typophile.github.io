An Outstanding Collection of Flair Alphabets

July 24, 2009 • bongo

A Flickr set of a small specimen book I found a few months back. Includes flaired Cooper and Univers, among others.

This is a great example of what people are talking about when they say that plenty of the photo-lettering alphabets should never be revived. And I learned that Bodidot is a classification! That’s right up there with “Garaldine”.
2009-07-25 00:23:08 • blank
The specimens tend to be a bit over the top, as they are required to show as many swashes as possible. Sure, a lot of typographers got carried away, but there were many lovely examples of flairing. Canada Type has done a nice revival of Goudy Flair.
