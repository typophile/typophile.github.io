Supremo and Super-Reflex were designed by Arno Drescher in 1930, according to the Jaspert Encyclopaedia of Type Faces. - Mike Yanega

Yes, the book shows 'Schriftguss' as the foundry source. There are some other typefaces shown from this foundry, such as scripts called 'Pentape' (or Originell), 'Marggraff Light Italic', 'Energos', 'Burgund', and 'Aktuell' (or Penflow). 'Butterfly' was also a script. I haven't looked at the other categories, except to find 'Supremo' & 'Super-Reflex', which are Lineales. - Mike Yanega
