Those are most often called catchwords, I believe. That should be a good search term for here and/or Google. FWIW that one there is from Brothers, which is one of the best known fonts for catchwords, which Emigre in this case calls «word logos». See here: http://emigre.com/fontpage.php?PBroW.html
