I was hoping someone would know the typeface used for this heading.

That |G| is highly unusual for a gothic. Does your sample date from the 1920s?

I’m not sure what the original metal typeface was, but here are a few digital typefaces (with a bit of surgery on the crossbar of the |G|) that could help you replicate the look:

http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/linotype/alternate-gothic-lt/ http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/flat-it/rama-gothic/ http://www.felicianotypefoundry.com/cms/fonts/grotzec-condensed https://klim.co.nz/retail-fonts/founders-grotesk-condensed/
