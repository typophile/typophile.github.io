October 21, 2007 • Bald Condensed

Dear all,

I’m currently writing up the long overdue second episode of my Type Design series, a collection of entry-level articles for Unzipped to promote awareness of type design. This instalment I’ll be looking at the technical aspect of drawing, optical correction and ink traps, then at spacing and kerning. If you have any images related to these subjects could you please send them to me. As you can see from the reactions to last episode (scroll all the way down), this kind of images is very popular with the readers.

If you’re not sure what I’m getting at, take a look at the first episode, and below is an image I will be using.

[[http://briem.ismennt.is/|Briem’s web site]] teaching type design has a lot of good drawings on the technical aspects and optical correction.

Mark Jamra’s essays at the end of [[http://www.typeculture.com/academic_resource/articles_essays/| this list]] also have nice illustrations.
