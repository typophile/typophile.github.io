No, stylistic sets are not accessible in Illustrator. Either you let the glyphs remain inaccessible in Illustrator (as they are in many non-pro apps), or you duplicate them to some other feature(s). If you only have one or two stylistic sets it isn't so bad, but it becomes impractical with many such sets. Of course, one might hope that Adobe would add the functionality to Illustrator. But I wouldn't hold my breath for that.
