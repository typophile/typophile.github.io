2008-04-23 21:52:20 • nert
You could use a typeface that has a “cultural” axis. By that, I mean that instead of the usual axes such as weight, scale and slant, there are, for instance, historical variants. Chalet, of course, represents several scenes. Hypatia has options too, with old-style and geometric as variants on the same body. Of my faces, Eunoia and Panoptica provide alternate vibes within the same general brand.
