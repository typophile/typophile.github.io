The R does, to my eye, resemble a faux-japanese typeface, you could have the straight edge of the top counter at the top and have the curve bottom right and reflect the leg so that the curve is coming away from the character. For the W try making it wider, epsecially the lefthand half where the curve is slightly cramping the counter, you could also look at making it from the lefthand curved section repeated or constructing it from a similar shape as the O. I find myself reading the Q as D, I wonder if reflecting the tail so that the square corner is protruding might make it more Q-like. The L could be a little wider, but it might be that the width of the R and D are driving that. Tim
