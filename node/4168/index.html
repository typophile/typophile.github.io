Looking for a futuristic font, but not too futuristic, digital, but not too digital. Think Emigre's Base 9/12 and Cholla. Any suggestions? Thanks!
Interesting request. 'Futuristic' and 'digital' seem to be other names for 'techno', which is not my favorite style, so when you said 'not too' in front of those I was intrigued. I would recommend looking in two places: Psy/Ops, which has Reform, Reykjavik One & Two, Transfer Sans & Default Gothic. The other is Device Fonts (Rian Hughes), with September, Gravel, Quagmire, Range & Register.

Of course there are many other foundries with techno styles, but these two foundries come to mind as having some of the less stereotypical 'futuristic' looks, in my opinion. T-26, which also carries Device fonts, has one of the most varied Techno collections.
