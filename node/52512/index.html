Kiko, I think I understand the problem, but I'm not sure I have the solution. The frames on pages lose the links to their corresponding Master frames when the page moves, due to adding or moving pages. I believe it only happens if, in the process of adjusting pages, any given page moves from being a Right to being a Left (or vice versa), even if only for a moment while other pages are being moved or added. Are you moving/adding in complete spreads only? I think the solution may have to do with *disabling* the Allow Pages to Shuffle command in the Pages palette. This makes it so that Right cannot become Left or vice-versa. And I think it will prevent your problem from happening. Just be sure you don't end up with single, floating pages in the middle of your booklet. On the other hand, if you're changing a Right to a Left, or a Left to a Right, on purpose, then there's no solution, as it will then always break from the Master. HTH -- K.
