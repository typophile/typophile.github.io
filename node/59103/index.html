It reminds me of Neville Brody’s [[http://new.myfonts.com/fonts/adobe/insignia/insignia-roman/characters.html|Insignia]] if you stripped it down and melted a bunch of it away, so to speak.

Note that there is an alternate S sort of like the logo’s. Even the lines through the stems have an echo in the logo, as do the foot serifs on the the L’s.

Saying all this only means I am suggesting maybe Insignia helped inspire the logo lettering, but I don’t think the logo is a font. It is simple enough to have easily been hand-drawn.
