To my eye, the hairline serifs are thinner on Karloff than on the sample, and the curl on the upper arm of the|k| is a bit more aggressive on Karloff (and the leg on Karloff’s |k| joins to the body with more of a point than on the sample). Also, Karloff’s |t| has a shorter crossbar. And the curls at the foot of Karloff’s letters are tighter.
