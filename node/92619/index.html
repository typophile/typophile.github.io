How do I sell multiple licenses?

April 30, 2012 • bobdigi

This might seem like a silly question. I never had to buy licenses for anyone but myself.

If someone wants to buy 5 licenses of a font I’ve created, how do I supply that? After payment, do I send the font with a EULA that specifically mentions how many licenses are included for their specific purchase? Or is it the font file, the standard EULA, and # of licenses included only on the receipt?

The receipt will suffice. As folks who offer freeware know, a single downloaded font file can multiply exponentially, all on its own.

Well, not really on its own: mostly with a lot of help. But you get the basic idea…
