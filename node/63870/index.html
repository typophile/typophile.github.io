The Offenbach Klingspor Museum knows a “Zeitungs Grotesk” from Bauersche: http://www.klingspor-museum.de/KlingsporKuenstler/Schriftgiessereien/Bauer/Bauersche.pdf Seems it was designed by Otto Weisert: http://www.klingspor-museum.de/KlingsporKuenstler/Schriftgiessereien/Weisert/OWeisert.pdf

The name is rather generic (“Newspaper Sans” for the non-German-speaking world) and there may have been more than one font design under that name.

Perhaps this link can get you further: http://dbs.hab.de/wbb/entrysearch.php?docID=28442&PHPSESSID=5ceb9bddb3064aeec2455f44effe472b
