Bitstream’s Classical Garamond family indeed includes the ‘fi’ and ‘fl’ characters, but NOT the OT features that automatically substitutes your typing of ‘fi’ with the ligature of ‘fi’, etc… they are older TrueType files produced prior to OT aware applications… thus, you will need to manually insert these characters via Indesign’s ‘Glyph’ panel or similar interfaces in other programs.

See here for specs on the Regular style: http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/bitstream/classical-garamond/classical-garamond/specs.html

InDesign has no problem understanding and auto-inserting the ‘fi’ & ‘fl’ ligatures from older Type 1 and (I think) Truetype fonts as long as the’re correctly encoded, which the Bitstream fonts are.
