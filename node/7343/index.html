Does anyone recognise this? http://img.photobucket.com/albums/v114/DeSevenFree/Type-Sample-BW.gif The closest What The Font comes up with is Solpera by Storm, but that certainly ain't it. It's not used anywhere else in the booklet, so it could be custom drawn.
The font is from Handcrafted Fonts (now called JM Fonts) and is called Exposition. It's by Jonathan Macagba, although Fonts.com doesn't mention the designer. I know this one because I use it in my Bowfin Printworks logo,and it was one of the earliest fonts in my collection. There are a Plain One & Two and a Rounded version. This is one of the Plain versions. Also available at Veer.com in the Umbrella collection.
