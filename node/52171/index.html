2008-12-03 04:20:27 • gerald

The eye is totally interrupted by the f t ligature. Basically this is Rockwell on speed – ditch the non slab-serif stuff. Coming late to this conversation and seeing where you started I have this to say: originally there was a forward ( left to right) movement of the slab serif. While overboard it’s now gone; the “C” and the “w” contain it, and to some extent the “o”. It now comes to a dead halt in the “r” and “k”.

The reader must still move forward at the end of the word– it should be sold to the client as “forward thinking” yet “traditional” - as indicated by the meaning implicit in the word.

Go back somewhat to your original ideas. Best of Luck.
