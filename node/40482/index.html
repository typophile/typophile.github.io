2008-01-04 20:02:29 • Florian Hardwig
Florian, you can access all of the opentype features in Illustrator that you can in InDesign. Kassie, first off check under Window>Type>Glyphs. That’s all the characters in the font and their alternatives! Secondly, you can try Windows>Type>Opentype. This gives you access to all the Opentype features turning alternatives, ligatures, swashes and that bunch on and off.
