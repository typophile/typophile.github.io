The decorative poem looks good, the trouble with critting this is the limitless options, a hundred different designers would probably do it a hundred different ways. The short story layout is a bit wide in the measure and the margins seem out of balance, the middle margins are a bit broad. For the Cage style you could look at justifying the right margin (while still keeping the aligned punctuation).

As for the title I quite like Generated Voices, (or perhaps Received Voices), the plural adds to the humanist feel to me.
