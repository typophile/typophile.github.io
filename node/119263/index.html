Decoro (2011), if you customized the E, is the closest commercial font I could find: http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/john-moore/decoro/

There is a free font called Lot (2009) that is an exact match. The general policy on this forum is not to link to free font sites if a commercial version is available, but Lot appears to predate Decoro: http://www.ffonts.net/Lot.font

[And before any other posters jump on me for linking to the free font, I tracked down info on the designer, Svetoslav Simov of Fontfabric, and every listing I could find lists Lot as a free font. Oddly, it is no longer offered as a free font on Fontfabric proper….]
