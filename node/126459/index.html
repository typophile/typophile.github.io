I don’t think it’s SofiaPro though, but I had a further look on Avant Garde from several publishers, and it turns out that Adobe has the charakteristic upper case “R” in all styles, which doesn’t fit the sample, while ITC has a slightly different upper case “R” when it comes to demi-bold and bold - et voila!
