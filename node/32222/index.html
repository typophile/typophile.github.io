Looking for a serif typeface for logo use

March 14, 2007 • alexfjelldal

Hi I’m looking for a serif typeface suitable for logo use. Typefaces like bembo and garamond don’t look good for this purpose. when they are set too big, they loose their beauty. I’ve played around with palatino for a while, and i really like its “sculptural” qualities. The only thing is, it’s a bit overused. Any suggestions?

And by the way, the logo is for a industrial design company.

thanks!

alex

If you want something that looks elegant when large, you should consider something with a Display variation (characterized by high contrast, and delicate features), but this isn’t always best for a logo, which is often seen at under an inch. Is that true for this logo?
