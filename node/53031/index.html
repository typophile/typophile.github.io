Clunic Released by Greater Albion Typefounders

December 30, 2008 • Rasendyll

Clunic is released today on MyFonts.com.

Clunic is a Blackletter font in the best traditions of Victorian Gothic revival—that is to say aesthetically marvelous but having no historical basis whatsoever. The design combines the perpendicular character of medieval manuscripts with modern legibility and a healthy respect for calligraphic principles.
