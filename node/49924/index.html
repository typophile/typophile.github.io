What works well is the modus operandi when it comes to design. If it happens to look good, even better. So you're fine in that regard! However by the title of your post, it suggests you'll be using this on the web. If so, be aware that not all users/browsers will have access to such fonts. You may want to Google "web-safe fonts" or "SiFR" to learn about such issues and possible solutions.
