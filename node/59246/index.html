Do not use such large letterspacing parameters. Keep it in the range of 98–102% and give the wordspacing some latitude (I often use 80 minimum, 90 optimum, 100 maximum, but it depends a lot on the way a typeface behaves). US-wordspacing s*cks — tighter looks better.
