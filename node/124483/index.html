2015-04-11 23:32:47 • JamesM

A little background:

In order to enable custom functions for Font ID, we had to move it outside the main Forums, where it used to live. At the top of the page, under General Discussions, it reads “All topics are welcome—except font ID requests.” If you try to post into General Discussions, it says at the top “If you’re looking for font identification, please post only in Font ID.”

That said, these notices go largely ignored. While Font IDs are certainly popular, we’re not ready to scrap the rest of the site to overemphasize newcomer posters who pop in and pop out without building community.

@quadibloc: It’s definitely an interesting suggestion, but we’ve moved away from such things years ago because they quickly become labor-intensive and unwieldy.
