2009-05-29 21:53:48 • Paul Cutler

Paul, I believe a musical notation font is what Dot said they don’t need.

This is interesting. Frode is right but it’s not quite un-tricky. Many Bodonis/Didots don’t have the soft/“scripty” instrokes in the italics; but then some Baskervilles, which would seem like good matches otherwise, have the semi-ascending “p”.

Here are a few faces whose bold italic cuts look like they might work: Scotch Modern Century 731 Berthold Bodoni Old Face BQ (quirky “p”) Also nice: Teimer (no foot serif on the “p”)
