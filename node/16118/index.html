2005-11-08 15:56:27 • Alessandro Segalini

All I can think of is Law firms and brokerage firms. They are all lists of names. Eventually they either succumb to logic and adopt a shorter name or use an acronym. Sometimes the full name is used until a brand is identified. Then they switch to something logo-able later. I have never seen a laundry list brand-name which works as a logo. You may be bucking with the partner ego problem where each partner wants billing.
