This is from Orpheo 9, by Tito Schipa Jr., yes?

I can tell you that the other typefaces on the cover—which you probably already know—are Gill Sans Ultra Bold (composer), Desdemona/Quaint Gothic (Orpheo), and the 9 is from neither typeface.

Windsor Elongated was the closest match I could find, but it’s only close—the bottom terminal doesn’t match: http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/urw/windsor/
