“Love is an endless mystery for it has nothing else to explain it.” quotation from “Fireflies” by Nobel Prize winning Indian Poet Rabindranath Tagore (http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rabindranath_Tagore)

11 x 15 inch limited edition of thirty-three; four-color (three inks and blind emboss) on dampened Rives BFK 250gsm paper. Designed and printed using 19th century wood type, hand-set foundry type and photopolymer (for the blind emboss) on our Vandercook 219 press at Slowprint Letterpress Studio in Dubuque, Iowa by Peter Fraterdeus.

Featured in two books on letterpress: “Impressive!” from Gestalten (Berlin); and “Adventures in Letterpress” coming from Mark Batty Publishers (New York)

This is a limited edition which will not be reprinted. The first forty un-numbered were sent to the U. of Iowa Center for the Book print-exchange.

Note that there are variations in the color density throughout the edition, some are very subtle others more saturated.
