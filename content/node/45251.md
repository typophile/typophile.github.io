  who is creative director of typotherapy+design, has created a series \r\nof original,

  language. The posters include original, typotherapy-created fonts along with edgy
  takes on classics including Helvetica and \r\nAvant Garde. \r\n\r\nNanton has been
  widely recognized in Canada, Europe and Japan for his original typography and graphic
  design. \r\ntypotherapy+design is a boutique, Toronto-based studio specializing
