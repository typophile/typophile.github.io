Hi Cheryl The 'g' in the logo is probably ITC Bookman (Light). The 'm' and the 'k' are from some other oldstyle face, possibly Caslon or Plantin. I notice there is only a half serif on the foot of the 'k', so you might have to mess about with a Caslon 'k' (just a little bit) to match the logotype exactly.
