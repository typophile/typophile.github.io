2010-06-14 23:12:35 • Miss Tiffany
What you want is quite feasible. Alternate glyphs of each figure would be required, two for 2-digit numbers, three for 3-digit numbers, &c. The feature coding is not particularly difficult, basically it is the same as for (calligraphic) scripts that have special forms for the beginning and ending of words. Nonetheless, a font requires productizing, which involves not just making, but also packaging, distribution, and marketing, and I’m not sure there is sufficient demand for this kind of thing to warrant the overall effort involved for a foundry to produce and publish it.
