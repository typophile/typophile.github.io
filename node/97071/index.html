Possibly custom. The closest I have, [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/ixipcalli/metrica/|Metrica]], almost perfectly matching, just needs to be vertically scaled. You could also consider it as totally reconstructed from the letter shape /o. More in the same vein: [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/ascender/titanium/|Titanium]] (/d and /p need to be reconstructed from /o), [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/paratype/magistral/|Magistral]] (same work as Titanium), [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/insigne/rendtphysic/|Rendtphysic]] (slightly too much extended) Free alternatives: [[http://www.dafont.com/electro-static-rain.font|Electro Static]] (overall design’s not matching but not too much work to do and free…), [[http://www.fontsquirrel.com/fonts/maven-pro|Maven]] (/o is pretty close, keep it and rebuild everything from it) and [[http://www.fontsquirrel.com/fonts/Sansation|Sansation]] (same work as Maven)
