I suggest you set aside a budget for this process as it’s going to be an important part of your identity as a whole. You might be more comfortable with a christian designer, but don’t write off a non-believer. A good one (from each category) would treat your needs with respect and dare to stand up against the design by comitee these things tend to become. A bad one (in this category you’ll often find those buddy favours that don’t cost you much) would probably end up just as you want it to be, which unfortunately is far off from what it could and should be judging by your samples.
