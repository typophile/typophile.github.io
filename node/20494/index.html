The original, predigital typeface was VGC's Ingrid (by Markus J. Low, I believe). Fontbank digitized it as Ingenius. There's an anonymous version out there called Chaucer that's much better spaced, but it's probably tough to find. I think that it's just a tweaked Ingenius.
