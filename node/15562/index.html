Modern Serif

October 11, 2005 • vigorgraphics

I know this is going to sound absurd, but I am in desperate need of a serif typeface that has a very modern feel to it. I'm looking for sharp serifs like in Cochin. However, Cochin has too many curves. Bodoni and Fenice get too thick. Filosofia is too curvy. I need it to be very tight, sleek and sharp with serifs. I think I may have to just create it myself.
Modern fonts with sharp serifs: Aurelia, Swift (2), Zingha
