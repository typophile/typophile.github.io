2009-04-15 16:27:32 • Renko

Arquitectura is the answer, as Renko said, but for historical interest, it is based on an old Spanish typeface by Carlos Winkow called Electra (shown in Jaspert “Encyclopaedia of Type Faces”). This typeface is also the basis of “Casablanca” from Red Rooster, and “Carlos” from Castle.

An interesting note is that the sample in Jaspert for Electra Estrecha Fina uses the word “ARQUITECTURA” as part of a sample phrase.
