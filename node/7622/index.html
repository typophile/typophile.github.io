2004-11-11 15:32:54 • maxim_zhukov
In fact, Super-Grotesk was not a Typoart typeface. Arno Drescher's original Super-Grotesk was designed around 1930 In 1950 it was redesigned by Schriftguss Dresden (pre-Typoart) for Linotype matrices, under the name of ‘Super Buchgrotesk’. Later, Karl-Heinz Lange redrew it for photocomposition during his time at VEB Typoart.
