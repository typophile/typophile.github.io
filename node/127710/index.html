while I’m awaiting a reply for my K. WEST request, I thought I could post a T-shirt I printed yesterday. Guess the fonts in use!

“all aboard for funtime” is the chorus for an Iggy Pop/David Bowie song called ‘Funtime’ on the album ‘The Idiot’.

FUNTIME is Eagle Book, by Font Bureau/David Berlow after the 1933 original by Morris Fuller Benton: https://store.typenetwork.com/fonts/?name=Eagle

all aboard for is Veryberry, by My Creative Land: https://www.fontspring.com/fonts/my-creative-land/veryberry
