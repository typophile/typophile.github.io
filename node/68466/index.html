Mafra, my debut typeface!

March 16, 2010 • Pedro Leal

DSType just released Mafra, my debut typeface! A type family suited both for editorial and corporate design, available in five weights, ranging from Light to Black with matching italics. Mafra is a contemporary typeface with plenty of style, asymmetrical and very dynamic serifs, and pleasent openness and balance. As always I would love to know what you guys think about it! Check it out at www.dstype.com.
