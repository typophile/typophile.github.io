MOTE, a black-a-licious type. [img:sites/default/files/old-images/Latinotype_Mote_6327.gif] Mote is the first typeface from Latinotype, a small new foundry based on latinamerican culture & local vernacular lettering. Mote, a black-a-licious type is a modern interpretation of latinamerican lettering founded in Santiago de Chile "Mote" drink store. The "Mote with Huesillos" is a traditional Chilean drink of sugary teaish water with a dried peach and bulgar wheat. We take this drink as a lovely chilean icon to represent our "Muy Delicious" lettering tradition in yummy tasty letters. - A unique & friendly black typeface - Up to 300 yummy glyph caps! - Opentype / Western European ready - Postcript flavoured curves More information: http://www.latinotype.com
