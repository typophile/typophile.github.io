I found their [[http://www.girlguides.ca/media/pdfs/14-3/14.3.7.5.pdf|Visual Identity Manual]] online (pdf). On page 10, under Fonts or Typefaces, it says “The signature is custom made, and is not available as a font or typeface.”

They [[http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Girl_Guides_of_Canada#History|changed their name]] from Canadian Girl Guides Association to Girl Guides of Canada-Guides du Canada in 1961, so the logo and signature may date back to then.
