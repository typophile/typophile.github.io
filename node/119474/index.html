Someone at typophile should be able to tell me!:

What do I need to do to determine–WITH ABSOLUTE CONFIDENCE–the legality of distributing this font?

2014-09-15 10:07:58 • Karl Stange

Thanks to you both. I sent my inquiry to Adobe and received just the information I needed in a response from Typekit staff. This definitely put the kibosh on using Myriad Pro for our development effort, hehe (As it turns out, this would have probably gone on 50k+ systems). Here’s the email, in the off chance that it may help someone else!

“At this time, we don’t offer licenses for inclusion in apps. We hope to in the future, but don’t have such a program at this time. In the meantime, I recommend that you use the App+ program from FontShop. www.fontshop.com They won’t be able to offer you Myriad, but they have a nice selection of fonts at reasonable prices."

Cheers!
