That is easily fixed, no problem.

You have to sort the glyphs in FontLab before generating your font. You can sort your glyphs in a couple of different ways, by namne, unicode or encoding. If not sorted, they will end up in an order that is based on when each glyphs was created, in which order they were created, that is.

In index-mode you can also drag glyphs manually if you want.
