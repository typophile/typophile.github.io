Cyrillic Fest 2008

June 18, 2008 • andrijtype

„Свято Кирилиці” („Cyrillic Fest”) is a typography and calligraphy festival, which traditionally takes place in 24-25 May in Kharkiv (Ukraine). This year it included type seminars and presentation by [[http://typophile.com/node/44376 | Vitalij Mitchenko’s book]], type games and exhibition of typefaces and calligraphy from Ukraine, Russia, Belarus and Bulgaria.

here is online exhibition (sorry, no English version): [[http://www.cyrillic.org.ua/ua/exhibition]]

and some photos from participants: [[http://community.livejournal.com/ua_typography/207861.html]] [[http://community.livejournal.com/ua_typography/208693.html]] [[http://community.livejournal.com/ua_typography/209218.html]] [[http://community.livejournal.com/ua_typography/209925.html]]

and publication about Fest in Novynar, Ukrainian newspaper: [[http://igordudnik.livejournal.com/194177.html]]
