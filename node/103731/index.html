Morris Fuller Benton's Othello was released by American Type Founders after the 1934 cataog was issued. The design was popularized in 1967 by the cover of Andy Warhol's The Velvet Underground album. In additions to the two versions mentioned by Ryuk there is an outline version in 24 Outline Display Fonts CD-ROM & Book by Dover/Dan Solo. Somewhat similar PL Fiorello Condensed by Photolettering. For a similar font decorated with spikes see Macbeth. Don EDIT: Corrected ATF release date which Jaspert stated as 1896.
