What do you think about creating a logotype for multiple brands. Let me be more specific: I must create a logotype not associated to anything(!?!). It will be use in handdryers, ladies brushes or even bandages (!?). The brand name? Not sure but something like “metaline”. You have a brand name (or will have) that is to be applied to different products, the problem/opportunity is that your logotype shouldn’t be specific to one product – many brands do this, some use a strapline so –

Metaline handdryers

Metaline bandages

Other approaches might use colour or shape to separate the strands of a brand.

Frankly though, it is not a new concept, these products are not so diverse they seem to be the kinds of things one might find in a pharmacy or clinic. Many retail chains have own brand products or carry a range of products from one supplier.
