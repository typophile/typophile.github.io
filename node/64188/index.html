I would like to open up a discussion for us to post free font sites. Here you can post where you get your fonts for free. To kick this off, I would like to post my absolute favorite site, [[http://www.fontsquirrel.com|Font squirrel.com]]. Check it out and post what you think are great free sites. Also, post what licenses this site primarily deals with (commercial, CreativeCommons, Demo, etc.). Looking forward to see what shows up.
I don’t know that free fonts are going to be a big sell on this site, where many of the users make and sell fonts as a part of their livelihood. Most of the rest of us don’t think much of the poor quality and limited value of free fonts, which often(not always) are worth exactly what they cost.
