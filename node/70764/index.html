According to the fontspec.sty file, MatchLowercase matches the \fontdimen5 TeX length, which is the x height. Similarly, MatchUppercase matches the \fontdimen8 length which, according to the XeTeX version history is the cap height. I could not find in the XeTeX source code where those values are taken. When I check with Arno Pro SmText, \fontdimen5 is 4.02054pt and \fontdimen8 is 6.20084pt (with an em size of 1000). According to ttx, the x height is 399, not 402, and the cap height is 616, not 620. On the other hand, the top of the bounding box of x is 402, and the top of the bounding box of X is 620 (values given to me by a FontForge script).
