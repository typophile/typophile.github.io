Need to know the “next to normal” part
This is reminiscent of Piotr Wozniak’s “bloor”, from 066.font. It is likely to be custom, however, a bold sans with the screen effect applied, rather than a font just judging by the fade-out through the word ‘normal’. You could achieve a similar effect in Photoshop, for example, by setting the type, applying a gaussian blur, converting to greyscale and using the colour halftone filter. Apply blur again (to a much smaller degree) to match the above example more closely.
