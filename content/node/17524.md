  body: "Straight line to curve, quickest way:\r\n\r\nOption-click on the line.\r\n\r\nFor
    more than one line to curves, select the lines, then control-click or right-click
    anywhere on the lines and use the popup menu and choose \"Convert > To Curves\".\r\n\r\nCurve
    to straight line, quickest way:\r\n\r\nShift-click on the curve to select it,
    then control-click or right-click on the curve and use the popup menu and choose
    \"Convert > To Lines\"."
