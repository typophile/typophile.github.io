I would like to hear suggestions & criticism so I can do it better next year.

Is there too much (or too little) of something?

For example : One change I made this year was that I didn’t start posting images until well after TypeCon was over. The previous year I tried to get them up that night. This let me take way more shots. Did you notice or care one way or the other?

Also, as I have been posting photos taken at TypeCon to our Flickr Stream I have been adding explanations and descriptions. Sometimes it’s just names but also in some cases it’s a little bit about what the talk was about. I wanted to encourage those folks who were there to add descriptions of what they heard & thought was well. That way folks who couldn’t be there can get more out of the photo stream.

I have been geotagging my images. Does anybody care about this at all? Or is it just silly?

And finally - does anybody want to help me shoot or process or post images for TypeCon 2008 in Buffalo?

Here is the stream:

http://www.flickr.com/groups/typecon/pool/

One thing that I’ve noticed is that some photos are tagged with “typecon2007” instead of being tagged with “typecon”. That is leaving some photos out of a standard search.

As to the stream, I think it is nice that things are coming in as a gradual thing. It gives you more reason to keep checking it, rather than one and done.
