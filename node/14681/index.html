Somewhat a “my font too” post - and late - but hopefully of interest ;-)

A year ago I released a font whose italics looks almost exactly the same (if you straighten them up ;-): http://www.luth.no/default.asp?Searchstring=Jacobs+Sans&side=fontprisliste

Incidentally this (my) font is used for the logo of the official Norway pages for norwegians (norge.no & noreg.no) whereas Aeroportal is used on all official Norway pages for foreigners (like norway.com).

These two fonts have been developed independantly by two different norwegians, so this look must really be norwegian in its soul ;-).
