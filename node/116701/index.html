Since the font-focused part of Bitstream was [[http://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20120319006604/en/Monotype-Imaging-Completes-Acquisition-Bitstream%E2%80%99s-Font-Business#.U3xbA1hdVMI|acquired by Monotype in 2012]], this digital interpretation of Futura is now subject to [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/bitstream/futura/licensing.html|their standard license agreement]].

You should also have a look at the [[http://www.typophile.com/node/18710|Typophile thread comparing digital versions of Futura]] and the [[http://www.quora.com/Typefaces/Which-foundrys-version-of-Futura-should-I-buy/answer/Stephen-Coles-1|Quora entry by Stephen Coles]], which (unfortunately) requires either a Google or Facebook account to view.
