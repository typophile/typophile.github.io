This is Wyatt, designed by Paul Barnes for the redesign of O, The Oprah Magazine by our friends at [[http://www.priestandgrace.com|Priest + Grace]], which just launched this past week with the September issue. You can see a little more info [[http://commercialtype.com/news/26|here.]] We’re hoping to get some images up on our Facebook page soon, as well.

The redesign uses Wyatt along with Kris Sowersby’s National and Matthew Carter’s Miller Text.

Incidentally, O did use Narziss for their special 10-year anniversary issue, but that was a special one-off.
