Not Avant Garde. None of the various published versions of Avant Garde have a closed R at this weight. However, Mostardesign has recently published Sofia, a family by Olivier Gourvat which, on first look, seems to be a revival of Avant Garde with certain characteristic changes; for example, the Sofia R has a closed counter throughout. Comparing PREMATURELY PLASTIC in Sofia Extralight or Thin appears to match your sample here.

http://new.myfonts.com/fonts/mostardesign/sofia/
