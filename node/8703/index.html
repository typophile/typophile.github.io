January 18, 2005 • pixelrecon

I have problem with export from Illustrator CS to Illustrator 10. Text field is just separate to individual words and latters Does anybody know how to sort this out?
The text engine in Illustrator was completely changed in CS to support Unicode, and other new advanced typographic features. Because of these changes, it is not possible to save back to earlier versions without losing the structure. This is also apparently why you now "export" to earlier versions rather than "save."

The only work around I have found is to have 10 and CS open at the same time. After you export the CS to 10, create text boxes of the same dimension in the recently exported/opened doc. Copy and paste. Other than that it is definitely one of the little problems with which we learn to deal. (And we say thanks that the new CS text engine is so much better even if we can't open proper text boxes in 10)
