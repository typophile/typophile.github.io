January 15, 2010 • sumitpaul

Hi everybody, I am currently working on a Kinetic Typography project. I have to animate the lyrics of a song. The one I chose was ... Rob Zombie - Two Lane Blacktop, because I recently watched the movie by the same name. If you've heard the song, you would know that, it is a very "in your face" track. I wanted to ask what kind of a typeface would go with it? ... I don't want to use "decorative fonts", and when I use Helvetica, DIN, Avant Garde, Rockwell etc. they seem to be very neutral, or just inappropriate.
