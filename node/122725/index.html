Didn’t find any match for the entire set of letters.

The I searched on everything except the |A| and |R|, and found a very good match to Gotham: http://www.typography.com/fonts/gotham/styles/

Mr. Eaves Sans by Emigre has a similar |R| (albeit with a much taller counter): http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/emigre/mr-eaves-sans-opentype/

Found a similar |A| in the free typeface Nottke: http://www.dafont.com/nottke.font?text=NEUE+GALLERIE
