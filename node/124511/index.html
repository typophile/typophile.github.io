Appears to be some variation of Scotch Roman, a typeface commonly used for setting books in the 19th and early 20th century: http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/mti/scotch-roman-mt/

The italic used for the footnotes looks like Oldstyle No. 7: http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/adobe/old-style-7/

I’m sure one of the resident type historians will be able to salt it down to a particular foundry and name….
