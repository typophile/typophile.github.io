Los angeles graffiti + blackletter

October 19, 2006 • ilynam

PingMag, Tokyo's premiere arts and visual culture online magazine has just posted my latest- an article about [[Los Angeles]] [[graffiti]] and the influence of [[blackletter]] typography. The article includes a world premiere documentary by Joe Forney about the work of Atlas CBS, a highly influential and active L.A. graffiti writer. Within, graffiti zine LOST editor EyeOne is interviewed about the connection between barrio calligraphy, blackletter, and their combined influence on contemporary graffiti in the City of Angels. Includes a number of images showcasing some of Los Angeles' most innovative graffiti. The article is here: http://www.pingmag.jp
