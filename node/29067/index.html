Jaz, What do you want? Do you want the typefaces to seem sympathetic, related? Or do you just have AG and Stempel Garamond and you want to use them? My opinion is they do not play well together, don't use them together. AG goes better with Century Schoolbook, Clarendon, Bodoni. Stempel Garamond goes with Syntax, Bliss, Mundo, Cronos, etc. ITC Garamond goes with Avant Garde.
