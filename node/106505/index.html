1920-1930 logo (script & two sans)

September 23, 2013 • Jon Woidka

This was a logo my grandfather created for his machine shop back in the early 1900’s. This was the only scan I had from an old business card, and I was hoping to recreate it in vector format so I can use it in other, larger applications (i.e. T-shirts, etc). I’m mostly hoping to find out what the script font is, but if you know what the other two sans are that would be great also. Thanks.
The a appears twice and is different. So it’s hand lettering. There may be a similar font but a quick check did not find it. Don
