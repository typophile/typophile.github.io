this maybe be related to your problem:

when embedding fonts in flash, you must make flash aware of which character set to embed. Under the properties panel, you’ll find an ‘embed’ button, click that, and korean glyphs are listed beneath japanese, basic latin etc etc. Ensure you are exporting these glyphs when you test.

Apple’s Myungjo should have the relevant glyph set, for a test at least.

Good luck!
