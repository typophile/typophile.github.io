August 7, 2008 • caracter2

Hey all. I recently finished making a Blink 182 kinetic typography video based on the song “Online Songs”. I believe this might appeal to a more adolescent audience and this site seems to have a rather more bohemian feel to it. Nonetheless, I hope a few of you out there will enjoy it:

[[http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=N0LIvrxf-R0|Blink 182 Online Songs Typography]]

I would appreciate some constructive criticism, but have in mind that this was my first typography video and I had to learn to use After Effects from scratch to do it. I am not an arts student nor professional. This was merely one hobbie which blew WAY out of proportion. I had to balance the time spent on this project with other college work.

You can grab a copy of the video in .MP4 format optimized for the ipod classic (for those “on-the-go” types) from [[http://www.filedropper.com/onlinesongs]]

Cheers! Jerry
