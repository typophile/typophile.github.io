Oft times I will duplicate letters and shapes with alterations to serifs, shape, length etc… livens it up a bit since most people want really tight stuff. You have to make small changes to keep it lively. To me it is not counterproductive… just a time saver. Make small changes and it appears different. Now if it is for editorial I don’t duplicate anything. I just keep doing ink roughs until the structure is there. Then the touch up begins : (
