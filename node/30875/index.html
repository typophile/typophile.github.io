2007-01-26 12:01:22 • cuttin shapes

Books: “Typography in Japan” “Applied Typography 2006” “ADC 2006” “Tokyo TDC, Vol.17”

Magazines: “Idea” “Design No Genba” “Axis”

Website: PingMag

Associations: Tokyo Art Directors Club Japan Typography Association Tokyo Type Directors Club

Type Foundries: Morisawa Iwata Screen Ryobi Fontworks Jiyukobo A-1 Kataoka Font Library Visual Design Laboratory Typebank Kinkido Type Project

Popular (New) Typeface: A1 Mincho Iwata Mincho-tai Old Zen Mincho Marumin Old Koburina Gothic Iwata Gohic-tai Old Yutsuki syogou Gothic Kana Axis Iroha Gohic

Characters: Japanese people loves characters. Many companies use characters as marketing tools. Penguin for Suica, passcard of JR East (railway company). Lismo (sciurids) for Lismo, music and video download service for AU (mobile phone company). Docomodake (mushroom) for Docomo (mobile phone company). Tarako Kewpie for Tarako Pasta Sauce of Kewpie (food company).
