I believe the ft crossbar should have a single even stroke, and that the ascender on the final t should not be curved on the left. These are shapes informed by the broad pen, so it would be wise to structure them accordingly. Also, there is no necessity to make the ball of the f the same size as the tittle, it might be better smaller, allowing more room for the ascender of t. At any rate, the open tittle looks too small compared to the ligatured tittle.
