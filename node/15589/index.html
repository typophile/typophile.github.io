[[TypoWiki]] : [[Foundries]] : Delve Fonts Founded in 1996, Delve Fonts is an independent type foundry based in [[Alameda]], [[California]]. Delve Fonts features an exclusive, acclaimed typeface library, which includes Helfa, Rieven Uncial, Uppercut Angle, Quara, Tilden Sans, and more. Delve Fonts specializes in the design and production of fonts for mobile devices, development of custom font production tools; Design and production of non-latin fonts; OpenType Pro fonts with advanced features; and Logo development for corporate identity and branding purposes. The sole proprietor, [[Delve Withrington]], draws from fifteen years of experience in typography.
