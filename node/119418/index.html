Good call Dick. Looks closer to me. Second time for me I have to deal with Graphicus DT. It looks so close to Paul Renner’s Futura. Some of the noticeable differences gathered from a [[http://typophile.com/node/42340#comment-265882|previous discussion]] on the topic:

Graphicus DT appears very similar to Futura; it seems to be a tad wider and the A,M,W are flattened on the top and bottom whereas in Futura they have points. Also, the j has a slight curve at the bottom instead of just a straight line, and the descender on the g is less harshly geometric and more open. Print Clearly is narrower than Futura and appears to have a rounded stroke. Also, the I, J, and 1 have serifs to make them more distinguishable.
