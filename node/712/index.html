It's Mom here, with a suggestion to any TypeCon presenters who post or lurk here. This year we had the amazing contributions of a professional AV person, JP Porter, who volunteered to run the show. I think she did fabulous work. I kept on asking her if she needed help or if I could bring her anything – she never asked for a thing and had everything under control. Please consider sending her and her company a card or letter of thanks and admiration. E-mail is OK, too, but cards can get hung up on the wall to impress the world! Here is her contact information: JP Porter Shoot the Moon Productions 5104 26th Ave So Minneapolis, MN 55417 shootthemoon@usinternet.com Special note: we typographers can impress her by not placing periods in her name per her request!
That's an excellent idea, Kristin. I found JP incredibly helpful and efficient.
