2006-01-24 19:39:47 • dezcom

In my untutored view, the faces from dstype are uniformly well thought out and professionally executed. I own several, including Esta and Quaestor.

With regard to ‘Esta’, I also find a few things I’d like to tweak, but overall impression is a more decorative text face with many options for setting. Somebody must like it, it’s one of the bestselling faces on myfonts.

Surely could use an OT version. The text weights, to my eye, set a little light on a desktop postscript printer, haven’t had time for production runs. IMHO, the regular text weight ampersand could be less linear to conform to the overall flavor.

No EULA came with pkg., nor is one immediately apparent at the web site.

Hope this helps.

John
