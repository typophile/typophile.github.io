All the signs above are made with wood and paint that hang from chains or are attached to poles or the building itself. These are unlike other places because many modern day signs are made out of plastic and lights attached to light up the signs. Also many signs in other places are not wriiten on a board but composed of individual letters posted straight onto a building. A lot of signs in New Orlans look hand drawn or painted which gives it an artsy look or vibe. New Orleans signs are unique because they stick to tradition and not what is new and fashionable.
Is there some sort of student project going on where the assignment is to create blogs about NO signage?
