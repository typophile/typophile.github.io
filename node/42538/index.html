when digital typefaces first appeared?

[[Peter Karow]] of [[URW]] developed [[Ikarus]], a digital format and software package to digitize and store type designs. After being shown first as a draft at the [[ATypI]]-Congress in Prague 1975 it soon became the leading digital type technology. — Wikipedia on URW

There was digital type before Ikarus, but that was a major milestone, especially for digital type design.
