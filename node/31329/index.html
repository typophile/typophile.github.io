Gallery 51 of North Adams, Massachusetts announces a juried exhibition of contemporary letterpress work to be held from March 22 through April 23, 2007.

We invite submissions from printers, studios and designers who have produced work with letterpress technology within the past decade. Download your entry form online at www.contemporaryletterpress.com. Provide the information required and send it to us with samples of your work.

The deadline for submitting work is February 28 , 2007.

Entries will be judged by a panel of distinguished curators of contemporary art, design professionals and educators. Selected work will be exhibited at the Gallery 51 space and presented online afterwards.

The exhibition seeks to showcase a full range of work from traditional print to web-based projects and focus on the artistic merits of the medium. We will be presenting work of the highest caliber and aim to raise awareness of craft of letterpress printing.

Please feel free to email us with any questions. We look forward to seeing your work.
