I looked through the 22 pages of Part 14 (the Strange, Distressed scripts) of the Script Font ID Guide. I did not find an exact match for this dry brush lettering, but I found a couple of very similar fonts. See <a href=“http://bowfinprintworks.com/Script14Pg14.html target=“new”>Young Itch, from Astigmatic One Eye; and <a href=“http://bowfinprintworks.com/Script14Pg16.html target=“new”>Teebrush Paint (and Alt) from Linotype.

I believe your sample is unquestionably a font, but it may be a custom one, since it falls under the hand-printed font category of fonts that can be made easily and cheaply from lettering samples.
