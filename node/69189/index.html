“Sans” is a shorter way of saying Sans Serif, which describes typefaces that are usually drawn in lines without any ‘flags’ or ‘feet’ (serifs) on them. Arial is a sans serif font; Times New Roman is a serif font.

‘informal’ was just a way to say that the letters looked like they were drawn without much precision, as if they were done in a hurry.
