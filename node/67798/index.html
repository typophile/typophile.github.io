February 25, 2010 • kalmar.nagy.andras

Does anyone know what typeface this is? The “k” and “v” are unique, it’s from a hungarian birth certificate from 1912, possibly from a german foundry (based on the types used in Hungary at that time).

Seems surprisingly modern looking to me, but I looked through Jaspert’s “Encyclopaedia of Type Faces” Lineale section and no sans like this showed up. Now your best hope may be that someone revived it. Something does seem familiar about those letters, so you may be in luck.
