I’m sorry, you’re not talking about Latinized type, but the other way around.

To me that’s a lot less bad than Latinized type, but it simply looks cheesy (classier term: naive) almost all the time. If you’d like to exhibit some cultural cross-pollination in type, you need to be very subtle (like in this case you might use a Latin font with reverse stroke contrast, a really nice one being FF Balance). Unfortunately, subtlety tends to be a lost cause among most people these days… especially among those who insist on not paying for fonts. :-/
