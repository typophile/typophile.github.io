I know of two InDesign (CS2&3) scripts that might be handy for what you’re trying to do: – Add Guides - draws guides around the selected object or objects – Make Grid - divides the selected frame (or frames) into grid(s) of frames

You can get the scripts on this page: http://www.adobe.com/products/indesign/scripting/ (click on the “Scripting Resources” tab)
