body: "<strong>[[Indices]] : [[FAQs]] : Where can I get free fonts?</strong>\r\n\r\nLegit
  free fonts. As always, please respect the [[EULA]] when using free fonts:\r\n\"Some
  are licensed for unlimited use, some only for private use but not for commercial
  jobs. You don\u2019t want to take advantage of the kindness and generosity of the
  people who designed them; it\u2019s really bad for your karma. And you don\u2019t
  want to risk that, do you?\" --[[Yves Peters]]\r\n\r\n<strong>Why doesn't this page
  list freeware font websites?</strong>\r\nMost of the sites below are run by type
  designers or foundries, and they tend to offer a few dozen fonts at most. Why doesn't
  this page list the huge freeware websites? Take a quick look at those sites and
  you'll see that the fonts there are of variable quality, with very few that are
  useful to a professional designer. But even more important, many of the type designs
  are clearly copied from other people's work. To be fair, mixed in with the knockoffs
  are thousands of original fonts made by amateurs who just love type. But the lack
  of oversight\u2014anyone can upload\u2014and the confusing way that those sites
  mix the bad with the good, makes it hard for us to recommend them. (In the end,
  those sites need no help; a Google search quickly turns them up.) Below, legitimate
