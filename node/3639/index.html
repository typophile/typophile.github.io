I think John Edwards also had this type of character, and although I disagree with him on nearly every political issue, I still would have considered his very strongly.

The point of way too much text: 1) Polarity needs to decrease. People need to be rational about what they say and willing to back it up. 2) Character is the most important quality in a leader.
