André

2015-04-21 06:02:57 • agisaak

Those files contain highly compressed spline outlines, (non-beziers of any kind), piecewise-linear hinting and require their own interpreter to even get the em data much less each curve.

It is a good thing you don’t need them, but if you did, the same designs are all available from Myfonts, in the Bitstream aisle. Happy hunting.
