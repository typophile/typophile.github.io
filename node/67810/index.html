Quadriga Antiqua

February 25, 2010 • ncaleffi

Speaking of undervalued/underrated typeface, has anybody ever tried Quadriga Antiqua by Berthold? The typeface was designed for photocomposition in the late 70’s and is historically based on a “Dutch Antique and Cursive” type cut in Berlin in early Eighteenth century. The roman shapes are related to the Garamond model, while the italic has a very strong handwritten touch (but unfortunately, it misses the old style figures in the only digital version available).
