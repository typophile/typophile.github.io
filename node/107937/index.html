Unfortunately, it’s their own corporate typeface called [[http://www.urwpp.de/cgi-bin1/dalcgi/source/schnellsuche_e.htd?searchchar=telegrotesk|TeleGrotesk]] and made by [[http://www.urw.de/english/fonts/identitype/telekom.html|URW++]]. If you’re working officially for them, they should provide you a complete package… If not, try switching with [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/urw/grotesk|URW++ Grotesk]] Narrow, far from perfect (main big difference is the single storey /[[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/urw/grotesk/urw-grotesk-t-regular-narrow/glyphs.html#glyphs/473308/66|a]]) but could do the job. Other alternative: [[https://www.klim.co.nz/retail-fonts/founders-grotesk-condensed|Founders Grotesk Condensed]], [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/mti/grotesque-display-mt|Grotesque Display MTI]]
