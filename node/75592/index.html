Read the posts in the General forum – they tend to only touch the fringes of Typophilia and may actually wander off into any direction.

The Build forum is intended for the highly technical aspects of font building.

The Design forum is for ‘general’ design (but not every design; it’s still related mainly to the typography); the Critique forum is where one would throw a freshly-created typeface to the wolves :-)
