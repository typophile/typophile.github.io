Jeffrey [[http://www.infraordinaire.com/wordpress|infraordinaire]]

FF Strada comes to late, but was designed by Albert Pinggera in 2003.

Eurostyle by Aldo Novarese will fit your bill. Released in 1962.

There’s also the open source collaboration Titillium by Accademia di Belle Arti di Urbino, released in 2008.

I’d take a look at Nebiolo’s Forma, an interesting italianate grotesque with a one-storey a, designed by Novarese and his team. It was never able to challenge the Helvetica/Univers dominance though. I don’t think there’s a kosher digital version in any case (but certainly some ripoffs).
