So far I have found: [[http://new.myfonts.com/fonts/baselinefonts/old-times-american/old-times-american/|Old Times American]] but this seems more in keeping with a newspaper or periodical than a book.

And: [[http://new.myfonts.com/fonts/t26/archetype/|Archetype]] which I like a lot but I am not sure whether it is from the right era

Does anyone have any thoughts or suggestions?

Archetype has similarities to the Janson type by Nicholas Kis, which he made around 1690.
