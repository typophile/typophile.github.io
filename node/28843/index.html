2006-10-16 19:50:05 • Solipsism

Helvetica with extremely tight tracking also works well. For the first half of a title, use the Thin weight; for the last half, Heavy. Either go all upper or all lowercase, or a combination of both (thin and lower, heavy and upper, or vice versa).

This also works with Avant Garde as previously mentioned, or combining a heavy Didone with a thin grotesque, or vice versa.
