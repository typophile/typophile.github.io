I actually think Florian was right the first time. If you look at the Bold weight of Kuenstler Script, you will see the same ‘r’ as your sample. It is different in the other weights. The G & D match the Medium weight. If the original of your sample was done with a digital font, then I’d say the Medium weight was only used for the initials. The reason I say this is that the ’s' is also different (and more like yours) in the Bold version. Kuenstler matches the looped ascenders and the initials better than any other font I saw.

Is it possible that your sample is pre-digital? Sometimes the metal versions are not exactly the same as the way the digital ones are done.

Anyway, that’s the font I’d use, possibly with some stroke adjustments to get the weight to look the same.
