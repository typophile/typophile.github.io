The letters R – both versions – and E are in a popular Art Deco style found in an assortment of fonts. Some suggestions: Limelight by Nicole Fally: http://www.fontsquirrel.com/fonts/limelight Anchor Jack by Al Paglia: http://www.dafont.com/anchor-jack.font Lorre by Pixel Sagas: http://www.dafont.com/lorre.font Ritzy Remix by Nick Curtis [using Tom Carnase’s Busorama as the source]: http://www.dafont.com/ritzy-remix.font Don
