  \u0160torm was born in 1966 in Prague, Czech Republic. 1985\u201391 he studied at
  the Academy of Applied Arts, Prague, in the School of Book and Type Design under
  Prof. Jan Solpera. He went on to work as assistant to Prof. Solpera until 1995.
  \r\n\r\nIn 1993 founded the [[Storm Type Foundry]], and today is a freelance type-designer,
  photographer, xylographer and howling computer programmer. In 2000 Storm\u2019s
  Biblon type earned the \u201CExcellence in Typography Award\u201D from the Type
  Designers Club, New York.\r\n\r\nBesides type and graphic design he occupies himself
  with wood engraving, etching, landscape photography, and letterpress printing.\r\n\r\nhttp://www.stormtype.com/\r\n\r\n<strong>Additional
