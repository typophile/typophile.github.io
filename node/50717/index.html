Glen Epstein's passing

October 21, 2008 • Graham McArthur

To all calligraphers and hand letterers on typophiles it is with sadness that I pass on the news that Glen passed away peacefully this morning with his wife and daughter by his side after a short two week battle with cancer. Rest in peace my friend.

Glen had one of the most unique and energetic approaches to the letterform of anyone in the field. He will be missed.

Thank you Graham for this.
