although we know, or think we know, that maggots are insect eggs, I believe the larvae from flies. We know. The particular superstition opposing this is called "spontaneous generation." Francesco Redi proved the origin of the maggots and disproved spontaneous generation in 1668, though it took Pasteur in 1859 to put the final nail in the coffin of the idea. Redi's experiment is quite celebrated in the history of science, as one of the first scientific experiments ever to use "controlled" vs "experimental" conditions. Cheers, T
