That is “Moderne Pica Block, Ra 280” by Alfred Ransmayer & Albert Rodrian Vereinigte Typenfabriken, Berlin.

Is that your own website, with the question about the typeface, or should I send him an e-mail? :)

The typeface was available for different models of typewriters. You could choose a typeface when ordering a typewriter, the types were then soldered to the typebars at the factory. Of course they couldn’t be exchanged later.

I have an old catalogue from 1940 which lists the typeface, I hadn’t seen a full character set before though.
