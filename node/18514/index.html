Stephan Gandl’s previous masterwork was entitled Designershock the users manual. Co-authored with Rob Meek and Birte Ludwig. The “system” came with a CD which enabled one to boot into their website and to play video-games to win font families which the DS team had designed. This is the only design book which managed to convince me of the utility of video-games. You should be able to find more info at

http://www.designershock.com/

and if you don’t have a copy of the DS manual, I’d certainly encourage you to find one. It is a rather lovely book, in which the cover doubles for mouse-pad.
