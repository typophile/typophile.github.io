Books containing advertisements and packaging from time periods from the late 1800’s to the 1940’s. Looking for book recommendations for my reference library. Any books that you love. Thank you.

A nice series of British ones are the [[http://www.robertopiecollection.com/application/products/opie/books1GB.asp|Robert Opie scrapbooks]]. His Museum of Brands, Packaging and Advertising is well worth a visit if you are ever in London.

For late 1800’s US there are reprints of the old Sears Roebuck catalogs. This book is amazing: L’affiche dans le monde by Alain Weill.

It has a lot of advertising design, mainly posters, from product, to art to politics from the type-focused beginning in th pre 19th century time, and the development of graphic design throughout the late 19th and 20th century, up until the 1990s a bit as well. It gives not only a more detailed account over many designs from up until mid 20th century, but as well a selected and sound over- view over advertising in Russia, China, Cuba, Japan, and other places of the world, as well as an overview over the second half of the 20th century.

It is not produced anymore, was published in the late 1990s and one can still find plenty of used copies on amazon.fr or ebay or with a simple google search.
