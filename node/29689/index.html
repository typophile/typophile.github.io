Actually, Charme and Slogan are not the same typeface. Helmut Matheis designed both a year apart. First there was Charme (1957) and then the heavier version (1958), which he (or the Ludwig & Mayer foundry) called Slogan. Slogan, however, has a very different D, I, J, V and W.

The Paratype website credits Slogan as the basis for Corrida, but its caps that I’ve listed above are partly from Slogan and partly from a bold Charme.

Credit goes to Philippe of the Myfonts forum for the historical info.
