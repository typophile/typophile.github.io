FontLab Studio 5 will come with a coll new way to generate glyphs: just highlight an entire Unicode range or encoding range and choose Glyph / Create Glyphs if Empty. This will fill in the glyph slots with autogenerated glyphs without the need to type in glyph names.

Also, FontLab Studio 5 will come with a brand-new template glyph image font (the one that shows in the grey background) that covers the entire Unicode 3.2 range.

FontLab Studio 5 for Windows will be released soon, with the Mac version to follow later this year.
