Some random thoughts. Colours don’t blend well together: the “im” blue is too bright. Grey on grey, seriously? Shape-wise, “im” stands out too much, while online.eu is too uniform. If the intent is to promote the url, “im” shouldn’t appear as something separate. If the intent is to promote the brand, “im” and “online” should work better together while .eu should be de-emphasised.
