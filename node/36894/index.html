2007-09-10 18:54:48 • Don McCahill

When we ship gear it means somebody actually has to wait in line at a Post Office to fill out a customs claim form.

Your shipping agent (Fedex, UPS, DHL, whomever) can handle customs clearances at both ends. Might cost a little extra, but it’s worth it not to have the hassle. You (the shipper) still have to do any commercial invoices, export declarations, and certificates of origin, though. And don’t forget those ITAR and USML forms and statements for the really good stuff.
