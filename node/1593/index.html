The most useful thing I can suggest for something that old, is to look at one of the sites that specialize in revivals of very old typefaces. Dave Nalle's Scriptorium has the most antique typefaces, as far as I know, although all the names have been changed. I am fairly familiar with the Scriptorium collection, and I don't think your style is in there, but you might see something that you'd consider similar enough for whatever your purposes are.
