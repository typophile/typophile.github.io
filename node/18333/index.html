Japanese fonts on a Mac-Hiragino Micho Pro and Kazuka Mincho Std

March 4, 2006 • Rachel Stone

I am doing a project on Japanese music and wanted to use Japanese text, but I do not know how the kanji characters are ordered in the glyphs in the unicode for the Hiragino Mincho Pro and Kazuka Mincho Std fonts. The characters are not ordered by stroke number, so does anyone know how to find the correct character that you need. Thank you for any help anyone can give me.
If you are using a Mac, open your international settings and allow all Japanese settings. If your Mac is set for American English, a little American flag will appear at the top of your screen. Drag down to Katakana, then phonetically type in the Romaji version of the kanji- teh corresponding kana will appear. Highlight it, change to the Kanji setting, and left- or right-arrown to get the correct character. (You may arrow for a bit to find the correct character.) :)
