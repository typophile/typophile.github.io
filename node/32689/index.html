The example has an old world charm, but the letters are really pretty normal aside from the embellishments. Just look at “Hotel”, the majority of the characters are slab serifed, but what makes it unique are the embellishments of the crossbar on the H and E, and of course the inconsistent outline. Same with the A’s in San Agustin. This has a modern feel, and I could see it being used often, but what sets it apart from a standard utilitarian font is the additional features (which could be Alternative figure set in a font).
