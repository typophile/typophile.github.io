  around the world, and continues to design and market new type designs. ATF is best
  known for two sans-serif Opentype families, [[http://www.atf.com.au/1/retail-typefaces/halvorsen|Halvorsen]]

  custom designs and typeface modifications for a large range of advertising clients.
