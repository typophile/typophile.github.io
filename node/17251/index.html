2006-01-15 21:15:47 • kris
You, Kris! Linotronic is the brand name for a high-res (generally 2400-3600dpi) imagesetter, which outputs to paper or film. They are slowly disappearing and being replaced by CTP (computer-to-plate) technology, which skips the intermediate step of output to film, which involves strippers in the process. And, no, they are not THAT kind of stripper...
