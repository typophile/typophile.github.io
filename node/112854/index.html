I found this beautiful type used in an old ad for the Yamanaka & Company. I plan to revive this typeface digitally.

Hand lettered – note the variation in letters. Style is probably best captured by Arts and Crafts fonts. Some examples Arts And Crafts Hand BA http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/bannigan/arts-and-crafts-hand-ba/ Arts And Crafts GS http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/bannigan/arts-and-crafts-gs/ ITC Greengate http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/itc/greengate-itc/ Ingram BT http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/bitstream/ingram-bt/ Syracuse http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/arroyostyle/syracuse/ Don
