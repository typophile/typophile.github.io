By the way, different sizes of Caslon from the same maker can have differences in the 'C'. I have samples of Lino Caslon Old Face where the larger sizes have the two serifs, and the smaller one, one serif. The sample of Caslon 128 in 'Types of Typefaces' is at 24 pt and has both serifs, but with Caslon it doesn't mean it will at 12 pt.
