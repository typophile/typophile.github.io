December 1, 2008 • Pablo135

What font are usually used in newspepers? I need about 20 names. Like Times new Roman, Baskerville. Help.
Ascender’s study found that the 10 most popular typeface families are: Poynter, Franklin Gothic, Helvetica, Utopia, Times, Nimrod, Century Old Style, Interstate, Bureau Grotesque and Miller. Further, 35 of the top 100 daily newspapers use a custom typeface or modified font in addition to retail, off-the-shelf fonts.
