Well, "NET WT. 1.76 OZ" is Goudy Oldstyle, but beyond that, I think most of the package is hand-lettered – "ALTOIDS" is almost certainly custom lettering.

"CURIOUSLY STRONG PEPPERMINTS" is definitely hand-lettered (three different P's, two different S's, probably more inconsistencies than that), but Letterhead's LHF Full Block is a similar style, and it's free!

"The Original Celebrated" is also probably hand-lettered – the face that this reminds me the most of is Windsor… if you tweaked one of the weights of Windsor and added some strokes, you could probably get very close.

"MADE IN BRITAIN", of course, is Helvetica Medium, maybe Bold. =)
