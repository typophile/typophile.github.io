If you want a contemporary slab serif, try Serifa or PMN Caecilia -- the latter seems to be popular lately, but I guess I don't have a problem with that as I love it. I've been using it and TheSans for my own materials (résumé, business cards, etc) lately. If you want something that is distinctive and will look nice next to Univers, I humbly suggest either Van Dijck or [[Zuzana Licko]]'s Filosofia. Van Dijck may be too delicate for what you're trying to do, but Filosofia (or another didone) will match the weight and width of Univers well.
