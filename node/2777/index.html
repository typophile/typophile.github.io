The California Soul, imo, is a straight scan from a vintage California tourist-type tee or postcard. It features a mix of italic and script forms. The 'Soul' looks tacked on House Industries, I think from the Vegas set… Similar fonts can be found by checking out myfonts.com and typing keyword 70s. Just three off the top, Lazybones, Candice, and Octopuss.
