Sometimes you will find fonts that have a "cameo" (reversed) weight... For example, Fiendstar Cameo Blitzplakat However, be warned that searching for the term "cameo" on a font website will bring up fonts called Cameo as well as fonts with cameo (reversed) weights, and even some fonts with no reversed weight at all! So you will need a bit of patience... For example, here is a search for "cameo" on FontShop's site. A search for the term "reversed" (on the same site) also yielded some results. I hope this helps!
