The following aren’t exact matches, but have the same general feel:

http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/typetrust/diego/ (thought the spurs are more asymetrical) http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/mti/davison-americana/ (more spurs than sample) http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/typeinnovations/mariamne/ (more spurs than sample) http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/parkinson/modesto-text/ (no spurs)

A mixture of Billhead lowercase and a Woodmere Spur or Hensler 2 capital S from Letterhead Fonts might get you closer:

http://www.letterheadfonts.com/
