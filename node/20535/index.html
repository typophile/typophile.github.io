2006-06-09 03:46:51 • pattyfab
Looks a lot like one of those generic photo-headline fonts like the ones on page 166 in the Solotype catalog. Solo give names to them ("Gay Gothic", "Gyro Gothic", "Gala Gothic", etc.), but in the old VGC catalog, the same fonts only have numbers ("B-12", "B-14", "B-15", etc.). Incidentally, I think it's pretty likely that they made custom fonts for the movie promo stuff based on Clowes' lettering. In turn, I am sure Clowes was inspired by seeing fonts like the ones mentioned here in developing his distinctive lettering style.
