Although out of print, you might do a search of online used bookstores for a copy of "Trademarks of the 20's and 30's" by Eric Baker. There are also numerous resource books on the history of the American gas station at Amazon.com. Just do a search for "gas station." A client just got several of the books for a restoration project of the same period. - Jeff Jeff Fisher Engineer of Creative Identity Jeff Fisher LogoMotives http://www.jfisherlogomotives.com

Another wonderful and amazing resource can be found through Louise Fili's book Deco Type: Stylish Alphabets of the '20s & '30s as well as any of her other books. While her books are not an all encompassing explanation on design theory from that time, they are amazing little time capsules for those of us which love these periods.
