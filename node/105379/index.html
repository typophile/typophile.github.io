2014-08-22 13:58:10 • Grafiker
If the point is to make mothers more visible, then why "invisible"? None of the logo's is inspiring, actually they make me think of an ordinary movie or reality show. My suggestions: - Don't "stack" the words on two lines, with same width, that looks too rigid. Let the words follow each other; - Stay away from predictable wishy-washy handwritten typefaces, unless it's masterfully well-done; - Try a font like the Aubade logo [[http://www.aubade.com/]]. It has a sense of style, with a feminine twist. - Maybe try to accentuate "invisible" (if the word makes sense to the brand) by leaving out certain elements of the word, a letter or the dots. Not sure this will work. - Maybe accentuate the "o". Phonetically, it's the name's "centre of gravity". Due to the round shape it is a more "friendly" letter. Try creating something within the "o", initials or whatever. An element being contained in a circle could communicate "protection" or belonging".
