Shaw's House

September 26, 2003 • hrant

Something I posted to the ATypI list recently:

"
I don't usually feel like expressing myself especially strongly
about magazine reviews of foundries or fonts*, but Paul Shaw's
article about House Industries (with a particular focus on their
Chalet and Neutra families) in the current issue of Print is one
of the most excellent such pieces ever.

* Not least because usually they're just fawning
placeholders between full-page ads - sometimes
ads for the font house in the review. :-/

The article doesn't just benefit from Shaw's usual good
writing, and isn't just distinguished by its handling
of both macro and micro issues, it's refreshing in its
combination of insight and candor. To me House Industries
has been doing increasingly better work, but they might
do well to heed Shaw's gentle corrective advice.
"

In fact I think their new Shag fonts are timed perfectly!
