Please take a look at [[http://www.TypeIsArt.com|www.TypeIsArt.com]] it was recently re-released with some additional features and a new interface design.

Take it for a spin… and let me know what you think. Any feature requests? Typefaces you think would make for a good Special Edition? I’m working up a list for the next version and would love to have your opinion.

If you happen to be in Buffalo for TypeCon2008 this week please stop in at [[http://www.224Allen.com|SP@CE224]] between the end of the day and any night’s festivities (5PM-9PM). If the door is locked, ring the white doorbell and I’ll open up. We are just a block South and a block West from the venue where Sagmeister is speaking on Wednesday.

Let me know you’re in for TypeCon2008 and I’ll uncork a bottle of Ex-Libris Cabernet Sauvignon.

Here are some tips for those who bothered to scroll down this far.

  • There are several key combinations that affect the selected object. (move/rotate/scale)
  • Hold down the space bar and use the arrow keys to move all objects at once.
  • Don't forget to save!
  • Let it sit for 10 minutes for an animated treat.
  • Check it out again soon to see the first Special Edition featuring a treat from the folks at P22.
