Unknown serif from TV sitcom titles

November 16, 2010 • HSR Classic

This one has been bugging me for a while. Its similar to New Aster but has some differances, like the lowercase G.
I think it's a really poorly done version of ITC Korinna from an 1980s-era video character generator (think bitmap fonts). A few of the unique oddities of Korinna are missing, but most of them are there.

I agree with Mark. I also checked the Serif Guide, and this doesn't match any of the 1900+ families I have there. When I deselected the W from the characteristics I got Korinna too. It seems like a 'homogenized' version of Korinna, or Benguiat (both done by Ed Benguiat), with anything too odd-ball removed and made more conventional, like the C, G and presumably the U. The M is one earmark or Korinna that was retained. - Mike Yanega
