Sampo Kaasila is a Finnish technologist with many contributions to digital typography. Probably his greatest impact has been the design of the [[TrueType]] font technology at Apple in the late 1980s. Since then, he founded [[Type Solutions]], which has created a number of tools (including the basis of the [[hinting]] tool Microsoft’s [[Visual TrueType]]), and a new rasterizer, T2K. [[Bitstream]] acquired Type Solutions in 1998, and now markets the technologies under its own name.
