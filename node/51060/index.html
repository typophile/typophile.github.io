October 29, 2008 • Lukas_

Recently, many done for the rally, and decently as icons and rally signs have not seen anywhere else. It decided to make a special character font. And now decided to share it with all

[img:sites/default/files/old-images/Rally_Symbols-2_3740.gif]

font contains the following:

  1. Standard signs of control in rally
  2. The icons that have helped create in the infographics, schemes rally routes
  3. Blanks for the design of logos and other graphics
  4. Symbols for signs Legends (transcript)
  5. Other symbols and marks

Font is Free, you can download here: http://www.2d.lviv.ua/uploads/media/Rally_symbols.zip
