As you have perhaps already noted, seeing your title, [[http://www.houseind.com/fonts/collections/neutraface|House Neutraface]] Condensed versions would get you pretty close. The R is more like the original Neutraface, and the E is more like No. 2.

[[http://new.myfonts.com/fonts/ef/mahlau/|Mahlau]] and [[http://new.myfonts.com/fonts/spiecegraphics/newport-classic-sg/|Newport Classic]] are somewhat similar in feeling, perhaps. The flavor is Art Deco, I’d say.
