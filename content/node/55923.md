body: "I tried posting this as news yesterday but somehow failed getting it through
  ...\r\n\r\nJust came across the ISTD awards call for entries in my emails again
  and thought I let you know -- the last ISTD Awards were held back in 2004, so it's
  really a rare opportunity taking part ;)\r\n\r\nThe International Society of Typographic
  Designers (ISTD) Awards 2009 are an opportunity for all practising designers to
  submit their work to be judged by today\u2019s leading graphic design and typography
  specialists.\r\n\r\nCategories range from book design, packaging, and signage to
  digital media and integrated campaigns as well as type design.\r\n\r\nTo enter,
  your work must have been published between 1st January 2007 and 31st January 2009.
  Typography should be an integral part of the design solution. There are three types
  of awards:\r\n\r\nCertificate of Excellence\r\nAwarded to all finalists \u2013 Work
  accepted as being eligible for a Certificate of Excellence shows a high standard
  of design in conjunction with a high standard of typography.\r\n\r\nPremier Awards\r\nAwarded
  to outstanding submissions in each category \u2013 Premier Awards are chosen from
  the work that has met the criteria for a Certificate of Excellence, but which is
  also outstanding in the view of the majority of the jurors.\r\n\r\nWinner of the
  ISTD International Typographic Award 2009\r\nNominated from the overview of all
  Premier Awards, in all categories. \u2013 Awarded to the item judged to be a significant
  typographical achievement, which catches the imagination of all the jurors, and
  exemplifies outstanding typographic excellence.\r\n\r\nThe winning work will be
  featured on the ISTD website and in the ISTD 2009 Awards Publication.\r\n\r\nThe
  deadline for entries is 31 March 2009.\r\n\r\nsee http://awards.istd.org.uk for
  details."
