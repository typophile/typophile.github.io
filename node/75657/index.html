LeBeaune: soon (very soon) available

October 26, 2010 • DamienGautier

LeBeaune A new version of this character drawn at first in 1995 for the famous town in Burgundy and its identity and public signage will be available in novembre at “Editions 205”. The font is entirely redrawn, digitalized. Many ligatures are added. In OpenType format, of course. http://www.editions205.fr/index.php?/fonderie/lebeaune--soon/

I love titling fonts like this one. I really like how it’s pointy, I also like the fact that the proportions are not strictly classical which makes the typeface a lot more versatile. I love the punctuation and the @ symbol. The numerals are elegant I think it would be useful for retailers and fashion magazines if you had various positions for the dollar sign, and also superscript for all the numerals.
