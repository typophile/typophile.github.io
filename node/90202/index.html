2012-02-21 09:51:14 • JanekZ

I really like the serif. It is very harmonious/classic warm. So I want to see the sans-serif to be as good. The “trapeze” where the vertical weight goes from thick, to thin, to thick is interesting. Personally on the /l/ & /h/ & /b/ & /d/ I find it creates a “top heavy” font, a little unbalanced for my taste. Also, I miss the beautiful bowls of the serif /d/ & /b/ … in this condensed sans-serif version. Additional I would love to see a sans-serif where you keep the original’s playfulness, like the /f/ ascender, and elegance like the /j/ descender.

I see great potential in this sans-serif font, maybe you could keep more of the original’s caracter.
