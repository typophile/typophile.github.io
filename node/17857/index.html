The Bieler Press was founded by its proprietor Gerald Lange in 1975. The weblog BielerPress serves as a news service for the company. A secondary weblog entitled BielerPressII is published as an article and opinion repository to complement their primary weblog. BielerPressII was originally published under the name [[TypeRoad]] - a much snappier moniker if you ask me.
