Logo for Kids Club!

January 12, 2006 • DaveG

Hey Everyone! I'm doing a logo for a birthday party room & retail space for girls. Basically, 1/2 the place is a retail store thats selling dolls, puzzles, etc & the other side is a dressup, karaoke for the kids who have parties. Its called bella club. I want you to critique my logo (so far). Black is probably not the final color. 1- The diamonds represent a black & whote harlequin counter thats inside the retail area. Do you think the white is TOO white? 2- Do you enjoy the outer glow? Will this be hard to quality control when in vector (e.g. printing errors, etc). 3- Too adultish? Age group is 4-12. Mind you, the soccer moms are the ones paying for all this (higher income target audience). 4- My clients are trying to add a crown to this. Do you think its missing something or do you feel its finished? Thanks in advance
The first things I thought of, when I viewed the logo PDF, were, 'Las Vegas' / 'Casino' ( Maybe thats just me, but, Yea, I think it may be too 'adultish', if its, as you stated, intended for kids :) I think this should rather be bright, colourful, and most of all, FUN ( Not as 'serious' as your current logo scribble would propose ) Something 'happyhappyjoyjoy' :), playful, slightly 'girly'ish' ( A sample type recommendation may be 'Novak', by Rian Hughes ) Dav

Not sure if they have Club Libby Lu in your area. Their logo has the crown your client is pushing for. Also a very "girly" typeface, like Dav is recommending. If Club Libby Lu is a direct competitor, what you've created might be appropriately different from the "expected" treatment. Black and white are probably a bit too sophisticated for little girls. And I'd lose the glow/shadow due to the control issues you've mentioned. It does look a bit "Vegas" to me, also. But then again, not all little girls are into pink ponies and princesses. If the store has a more Old Hollywood glamour feel to it, this kind of treatment might work.
