You can use Adobe Illustrator to make it. make an oval or star outline or any shape outline you like right click on shape- transform- rotate experiment with degrees, i would start small if you want it to look like those, 3 or 4 degrees hit Copy, NOT ok, this will create a copy, rotated a few degrees hit command(mac)/control(pc) and 'd' - this will repeat the action. keep hitting cmd 'd' until you are happy! I hope this helps, im sure there are better ways or software but this will work
