Jesus, I think you may have things in the wrong order regarding Univers and the “experiment,” by which I take you mean the famous illustration in Type-Sign-Symbol that superimposes eight of Frutiger’s typeface to reveal their mostly shared structure (or spine). This generated letter “a” does look similar to Univers' “a.”

The illustration, as far as I am aware, was created by Frutiger in 1980; Frutiger started designing Univers in 1953. Moreover, Univers is used as one of the eight overlaid typefaces.

So the exercise was one Frutiger did later in his career to show the common elements in his work, not as a basis for designing any typefaces.

Nevertheless, it would certainly be a worthwhile exercise to try overlapping typefaces as you propose.
