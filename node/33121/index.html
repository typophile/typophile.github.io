Display Type for Office Sign

April 17, 2007 • remy

Hi all;

I need to make a really elegant door sign that says ‘OFFICE’ - this is for a client who’s identity elegantly straddles classic/modern.

here’s my criteria:

• evocative of vintage without looking kitchy (perhaps a modern typeface with classic undertones?)

• or if it is kitchy, (ie some sort of extreme wood-type) then I’d like it to feel intentionally over the top - if that makes sense.

• readable from a distance - can be serif/sanserif, but not too thin

• all caps if possible

I’m open to anything interesting – for some reason I can’t stretch my brain on this. I keep coming up with Engravers Roman, and I know there are a zillion other ideas.

Thanks so much.

a modern typeface with classic undertones
