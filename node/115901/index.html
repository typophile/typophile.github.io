Seems to be lettering – note for example the slight differences in the counters of e. But put Friedrich Bauer’s Fette Gotisch of 1875 http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/linotype/fette-gotisch/ on a diet and narrow the letters, and this is pretty much what you would get. The pen-stroke technique of Morris Fuller Benton’s Wedding Text of 1901 http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/hihretrofonts/wedding/ is used for much of the finishing of the letters. Don
