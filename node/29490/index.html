Clicking on vague icons was just the ticket! You won’t find this toolbar under toolbars. Whilst in the glyph window, clicking on the ruler icon in the tools toolbar inserts the desired toolbar into the glyph window.

Super secret nested toobars. Brilliant! Back to regularly scheduled programming.
