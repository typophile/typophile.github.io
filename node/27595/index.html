Swell Houses is a product. Custom desiged houses, finessed and finished to a demanding level of detail for a young sophisticated market. The brand should reflect the coastal desires of the australian buyer and be contemporary and sophisticated.

Ditch the Trajan, I would guess that the marketing-speak gurus don’t equate hot-modernism with the modernism of the 20th century and that your first post was probably more what they were looking for.
