Hannes Famira

November 17, 2005 • Hannes Famira

[[Indices]] : [[Designers]] : Hannes Famira

Type designer and lecturer. Educated at the [[Royal Academy of Art]] in The Hague (Den Haag, The Netherlands), he has taught type design and new media at the [[SfG]], Schule für Gestaltung in Basel, CH as well as Lettering and Typedesign at the NJCU New Jersey City University. Hannes runs the website <a href=“http://www.typEdu.org” target=_blank">www.typEdu.org and the [[kombinat-typefounders]]. He currently teaches <a href=“http://www.cooper.edu/ce/design.html" target=_blank”>Digital Typedesign and Experimantal Typography at the [[Cooper Union]] and Typography at CUNY.
