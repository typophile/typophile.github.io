I speak German fluently, albeit with a horrendous Baltimore accent. However, one can get by as a tourist quite well in Germany with just English. In my experience, this has been a little more difficult in Italy and France. Here in Germany, though, many will immediately switch to English when they begin to hear me talk, which is not necessary, although I always appreciate that they are trying to be helpful. If you stay in Berlin and want to go to Antwerp and Zurich, it would be best to take the train to one of them, and then stay in that city for a night or two (or longer!) and then return to Berlin. Zurich must be 8 hours away from Berlin, even via fast train. Maybe Antwerp can be reached in four? But don't forget Dessau and Weimar. These are easy day trips from Berlin.
