2004-09-23 03:32:58 • type
I have bought from Adobe, MyFonts, FontBureau, and FontShop. I buy from whomever has what I need or want.

The fact that I can rely on Adobe and FontBureau to be of a high standard of craftsmanship does makes a difference to me.

I really wish there were some technical way to preview a font fully, and then decide to buy it or not. I really can't tell how good a font will be for what I want without trying it out.

The MyFonts and other previews are good, but only for something very brief. PDFs are also very helpful, but there is nothing like setting at the size and measure and leading you want, and seeing what it looks like.

Now that I think of it, maybe the vendors could have a program where you could fill in specs, and automatically get sent back a PDF short paragraph - now that would be helpful!
