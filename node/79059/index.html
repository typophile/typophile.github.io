2011-02-15 00:07:10 • jperrella4

I’m convinced this is a version of Jiffy, which was probably a film type. It was typical to have a range of weights, but each one may have had a different name. In old books I have they seems to have H, I and J initial letters (see the Solotype Catalog). Someone has probably done the same thing Nick did, and resurrected this old design, and made it connect better. The weight variations could be done by adding stroke thickness.

Have you looks at Ale Paul’s Bluemlein Scripts? It’s possible one of those includes this, since a number of those scripts once appeared by other names. [I just looked at those fonts at Veer, and didn’t see it, although Mrs Blackfort is similar.]
