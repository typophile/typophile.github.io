Welcome to Typophile Jessica. In the future if you could post smaller images, we’d be able to see it better without scrolling across the screen. I think the FAQ suggests a max of 500 pixels width (or so).

McDonald’s logo is almost surely custom, just because they are McDonald’s, but you could fake it by finding any Sans with a splayed M and using the ‘o’ to make the ‘c’ (with parallel jaws). The ‘a’ might also need some work, but pick one with a straight tail and you are mostly there.
