![](/images/old-images/Beltana Font Sample_4780.JPG)

Perhaps someone else recognizes an exact match. This is in a style called “Tuscan” which includes a large number of fonts with variations in the split ends shown in your sample. The style often includes the spurs sticking out at mid height. Your sample is a logo so it could be modified from its source. If you would be satisfied with something similar a good place to check for commercial fonts is MyFonts with tag Tuscan. Identifont has some Tuscan fonts not shown at MyFonts. Letterhead fonts has a Western category which includes Tuscan fonts. For free fonts, of varying provenance and credibility, you could check Dafont category Western which includes Tuscan fonts. Don
