Mosiac blocks in Illustrator 10

November 5, 2003 • Hildebrant

Ok, lets say I want to produce a huge grid of squares all at say 1", and a gap between each of say .1" – whats the best way to go about this, so I can have it percise. I really dont want to have to duplicate these all by hand, not to mention I need them to be percise. Ideas?
Kyle, try this: Draw a square 1" Cmd+Shift+M (Ctrl+Shift+M) Enter 1.1in in the Horizontal field (0 in other fields) Press Copy Press Cmd+D(Ctrl+D) for as many as you need horizontally Now select all your boxes Cmd+Shift+M (Ctrl+Shift+M) again Enter 1.1in in the vertical field (0 in other fields) (copies upward -1.1in would copy downward) Press Copy Press Cmd+D(Ctrl+D) for as many as you need vertically Hope this helps. I suppose you could make a pattern tile and tile the area but I hardly ever do this so I can't remember how. Jon
