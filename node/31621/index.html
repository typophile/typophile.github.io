IIRC Hermann Berthold updated the points/pica system to work with metric measurements in the late 19th century. But the British then got pissy about not being the ones who designed the measurements; they then created the current point/pica system. I believe that I read it in Megg's History of Graphic Design, but I don't have a copy at home so I can't look it up. As for how the current system came to the US, it seems that the US would have been following whatever Europe was doing up to at least the early 1900s, because the US was importing much of its type from Europe.
