In case you are looking for typefaces similar to the one in the sample you linked to: Try a no-nonsense Grotesque that has an R with a straight leg, like News Gothic, Akzidenz-Grotesk, Neuzeit Grotesk, maybe Avenir. More recent takes on this field: Akkurat, or, more in the geometric line, Proxima Nova.
