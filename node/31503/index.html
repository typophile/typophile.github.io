2007-02-16 14:02:16 • russellm
I agree with Chris that it is misguided to try to name a "#1". There are a lot of different ways to be excellent, and in the field of design there is a strong element of personal taste. --I can recognize excellence in styles that I don't personally like. As to Frutiger, he is certainly *one* of the best and the most influential type designers of the second half of the 20th century. However, I personally think this status rests on his sans serif faces; I don't find his serif faces either as ground-breaking or as exciting.
