Just for the record - I never said I liked it; but it works for the demographic. I’ve learned the hard way that obvious works - for a reason.

You can use a leaf to communicate “environmental” and reach 90% of your target market on first glance, or you can be creative, smart, and witty – and only reach other creative, smart and witty people. The brand name “Lobotomy Divine” doesn’t exactly scream individuality.

Selling jeans is all about selling image, and if the target market is young women who want to look like Jennifer Anniston in a pair of jeans, well, then, you need to use what speaks to them.

Also, just for the record, I may be painfully out of touch … what the heck is an emo band?
