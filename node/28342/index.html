Identifying this Greek font

September 19, 2006 • Percival Koehl

Hullo everyone,

Several months ago I posted here enquiring about a Greek font that I thought was a Greek form of Monotype Ehrhardt. I am not so sure about that now, but I have got an image of the font from the penguin edition of the complete works of John Milton.

Does anyone know what this font is and where I might be able to get my hands on it?

It looks like the Baskerville Greek from Linotype, based mainly on the alpha and Kappa. I am using the Greek type section in the back of the FontBook. I can’t see any differences between the sample and this font, but it wouldn’t be the first time I thought that erroneously.
