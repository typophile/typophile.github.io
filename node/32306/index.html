I’ve seen everything from a hairline stroke to stem-width. I don’t think there’s any “right” thickness, but I usually find it looks best if the slash in the ø is slightly less than stem-width. If nothing else, you can argue that ø quickly gets far to “dark”.

Nicks example is in general not recommended. It is frowned upon by the users of ø. The slash really has to go all the way through. Exceptions (like Futura XB) can be found, but these cuts are very rarely used in real life.
