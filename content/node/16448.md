body: "<strong>[[Indices]] : [[Designers]] : Michael Abbink</strong>\r\n\r\nMr. Abbink
  left Wolff Olins (NYC) in June 2006 after 3 years and is now trying his hand at
  freelance and developing his own design  practice. He was co-founder and creative
  director of Method, a design firm based in San Francisco doing graphic design, and
  web & interface design. He formerly worked at Meta Design and Apple Computer, and
  is the designer of [[FF Kievit]] and [[FF Milo]] typeface families.\r\n\r\n<strong>FontShop
  Bio</strong>\r\nMike Abbink earned two BFAs: one in Fine Arts, and one in Graphic
  Design and Packaging from Art Center in Pasadena. He has been designing letter forms
  since 1992, and his first completed typeface, FF Kievit, is used around the world
  and has won awards from the ISTD and [[ATypI]]. In 2000 Mike designed FF Milo which
  became part of the FontFont Library in 2006.\r\n\r\n<strong>External Links</strong>\r\n<a
