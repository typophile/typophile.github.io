I’d love it if everyone could check out my new design blog, [[http://www.jimhargreaves.com/twenty5ive|twenty5ive]].

Born in December of 2007, twenty5ive is a design blog for both students and professionals alike. Founded by myself, Jim Hargreaves - graphic designer and typophile, twenty5ive looks at design through an unaffiliated, unbiased, and unpretentious lens.

twentyfive’s mission is to show how design can improve the world we live in, or in some cases, how it may be failing us. Offering observations, fresh perspectives, and straightforward commentary on every subject of graphic design (especially typography), twenty5ive examines work being done across the world, as well as some of my own projects.

[[http://www.jimhargreaves.com/twenty5ive|twenty5ive]]

For more information on the founder, please visit www.jimhargreaves.com.
