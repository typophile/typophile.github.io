2005-11-22 15:30:35 • paul d hunt

There is no use speculating on an appropriate treatment unless we have more to work with.

Selecting a face (or a combination of faces) depends a LOT on the characters to be represented. By knowing which letters to compose, we have opportunities to suggest specific designs with unique standard, swash or alternate characters.

If, for example, the store were called “L’Antique” or “Empire Boutique” it would not be as difficult to suggest treatments. Also, if you intend to use an additional tag line or have a graphic to be integrated they should all be worked on together. N’est pas?
