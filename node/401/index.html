nice attitude - But why "…..using no grid…"? it's not important what is the size of the artwork/design = 2 by 5, 6 by 9, 24 by 54. rule #1 is to know the rule. there MUST be one spot or area to which the other parts are subordinate and to which the EYE is immediatley attracted. this , the starting-point for viewing, must be SIMPLE and uncluttered and have the essential ingredient of leading the eye on further into the design. so - call it grid, 2 lines, 30 lines, composition….etc., etc., - rule is a rule. you need to use it - but be creative, not MECHANICAL.
