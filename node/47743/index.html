Chacón

July 25, 2008 • Jaime Ayala

[img:sites/default/files/old-images/chacon_4992.jpg]

This typeface it’s designed based upon the work of the “brigade Chacón”, the graphic team of the Chilean Communist Party. I make it in a grungie style, because it’s used in painted paper over walls. I had to do the “no grunge” style for the work in Chacon, but i prefer the texture and the style of Chacon. Please critique.
