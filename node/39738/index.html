2007-12-04 17:56:00 • coleuk
Not a typeface: Lettering. Do the lettering by hand, specifically for this project. Do it on a transparency or tissue, so you can do it a few times and pick the best one. Lettering isn’t type. It can fit the exact shape of your layout, it can bend and squeeze, and it can be messy. Look at Sagmeister’s stuff. Sketch where the lettering will go with a pencil, to figure out how it will fit, and then take brush, pen, marker, crayon, whatever, and do the final lettering. And to your inevitable next question: Just Do It.
