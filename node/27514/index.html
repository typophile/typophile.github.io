I’ve dabbled with all the usual culprits, but starting out in a new job where they had no money for any font management software I thought IU’d give Explorer a whirl. Having used it for a couple of months now I can happily report I’ve been pleasantly surprised by its robustness, reliability and general features. Not to mention the appealing UI.

Activation is quick (although I think Fontagent seemed a little more responsive at times), organising fonts within the structure is intuitive and its handling of system fonts also seems refreshingly sensible. Most importantly for me it seems to be able to preview large lists of fonts with ease (something that suitcase always seemed to struggle with). Its worth noting I’m working with a small font library … nothing gargantuan and litho-house-esque, but overall I’m amazed at the quality of a product like this for the price ;) so much so on reinstalling my home machine recently it was the app I opted for there as well.
