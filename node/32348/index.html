Are you trying to create a logo or a book title design?

  1. Use true small caps. Notice how the strokes in “P” and “L” are thicker than in the rest of the letters. A true small caps typeface will offer proportionate strokes.

  2. The “f” is impeding the readability of “Photography” and “Latvia.” The words are legible, but less readable because the ascender and descender in that “f” interrupt the words.

  3. I’m not sure what the red marks are. They look like brush strokes which is confusing.
