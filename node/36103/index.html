Renewing your Typophile Membership

August 14, 2007 • Joe Pemberton

Some of you have asked about renewing your Typophile Memberships. We’ve found that tracking memberships and dues, in addition to shipping “thank you gifts” in exchange for your contribution requires a fair amount of overhead.

We’re making this much more straightforward. In exchange for your contributions, we will ship you the items of your choice. And we think the products speak for themselves.

Thank you, to those of you who have contributed in the past. Your ongoing contributions ensure Typophile continues to thrive. In addition to this year’s shirt, designed by [[Christian Robertson]] ([[Punchcut]]), limited quantities of past years' shirts, designed by Graham Hicks ([[Test Pilot Collective]]), [[Mark Simonson]] and Christopher Simmons (Mine) are available.

Lastly, we’ll be shipping items on or around the 1st and 15th of every month, so if you order today we’ll have it in the mail tomorrow.
