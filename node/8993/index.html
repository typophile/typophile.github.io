I would say that this logo was done pre-PC or at least using pre-PC methods, and that the shapes have been distorted a bit through reproduction, scanning, and/or possibly redrawing over the years.

Also, there are often slight differences between digitizations of fonts and other versions of them. ITC used to send master artwork out to its licensees but licensees were responsible for adapting it to their particular font format, leaving room for some interpretation and leading to subtle but noticeable differences between different vendors' versions of ITC fonts.

The Bitstream version seems to be the only digital version of this font. ITC doesn't even carry it. It was one of the first fonts they released in 1970 when they began and it's almost like they have disowned some of those early releases. Not hip enough for them, I guess.
