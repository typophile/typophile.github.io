In my portfolio I designed on 2000 I used a spread
for each work/research; the semi off-topic text
on the right, and the related images on the left.
I used A4 as spread, for economical reason.
The book has been binded with hard cover,
my name and the title "Antologia Grafica"
letterpressed with Quirinus, silver on dark blue.
To design it, I read the application for UIAH,
(http://www.uiah.fi) that is why I designed it,
to study in Finland.
