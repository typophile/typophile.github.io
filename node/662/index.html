I've not been to an AIGA conference, but Matt Warburton of the GDC had this advice for people torn between ATypI and AIGA:

If you are interested specifically in type and want to learn stuff, go to ATypI.

If you are interested in general graphic design and want to look at stuff (designer show and tell, was the phrase Matt used), go to the AIGA.

If you're not torn, come to both

ATypI is going to do it's best in future not to end up in the same city as the AIGA.
