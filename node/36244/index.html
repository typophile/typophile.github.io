Memorial Arts Charity Letter Carving workshops

August 18, 2007 • Seb Lester

Just thought UK typophiles might like to know about the letter carving workshops run by the Memorial Arts Charity. I completed one of the five day courses in London recently. It was well run and informative and I want to support them by recommending them to anyone interested in such pursuits.

http://www.memorialartscharity.org.uk/

I came away from the course with a new perspective and enthusiasm for letter carving, an idea for a new typeface, and some nice, albeit slightly wobbly, letters to put on the mantelpiece.
