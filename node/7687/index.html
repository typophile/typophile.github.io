2004-11-17 22:44:37 • mr_ampersand
Koen, Visit Santiago, of course. And when you do, take a bus or two out to the suburbs and check out some of the huge murals painted by the people who live there. Also, Isla Negra, one of the homes of poet Pablo Neruda, is a must-see. That's not too far away from Santiago and if leaving from Santiago in the morning you can be back by evening. I also understand that Chiloe, in the southern region of the country, is a real beauty of a place. Enjoy plenty of wine, and of course some pisco, too. But drink the pisco in tiny shot glasses, it's strong stuff! :-) Have a great trip!
