Launch of Illoz.com

January 3, 2007 • ER

Hello, I wanted to let you know about a new website resource with work by some of the top illustrators in the field:

http://www.illoz.com/

It was put together by a web designer/illustrator, along with help from a number of other artists, including myself. Registration for designers and art directors is free and there are some wonderful features at the site, including virtual workspaces and ways to pick and organize the work of artists that you like.

This website is the result of many months of discussion and thinking by some of the top illustrators in the field. We were frustrated with the online options that were available for us to show our work to designers such as yourselves. Please check it out, we hope to add more artists to the group soon. Any comments would be greatly appreciated, it’s still a work in progress. Thanks.
