slab serif from The Natural

August 18, 2013 • ralphsmouse

I got to staring at the poster for the Robert Redford movie The Natural.

I’ve never seen this particular slab serif before.

The image seems to be from one of the original posters for the movie, which was released in 1984. I suspect it is lettering adapted from a monoline slab serif font such as Rockford. The oversize bowl on the R and the ski jump leg could have been added to evoke a nostalgic retro/deco mood. At least I have not seen the combination in a digital or pre-digital monoline slab serif font. There is at least one digital font inspired by Rockford that has a ski jump leg on the R similar to the lettering: [img:sites/default/files/old-images/1984LetteringAndSanchezSlabWithAltR_3715.jpg] The lettering is in red and the font in black. It’s Sanchez Slab, using the alt R. Don
