Hi Guys

Some of you may recall that I am involved in setting up building letters, a charity font project which will sell a CD to raise cash to build two orphanages for kids with Aids in Africa.

Anyway, the project is now just a few weeks from launching, and we are wondering about price.

The CD Pack contains :

A magazine, A5, 32 pages with writings on Africian font design, font design, interviews.

A2 (Big) Posters by Max Kisman, David Carson and Joshusa Davis

A disk with around 20 fonts on it, by Max Kisman, P22, Baseline Fonts, Positype, Union Fonts, Fontmonster, SignalGrau, Thistype … etc

and a Christmas dingbat with icons created by Neville Brody, Erik Spiekermann, Joreg Alderete… etc

We are not sure about the pricing for this, does $35 Including Post and Packaging sound like something you'd be prepared to pay?
