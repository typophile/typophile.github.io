Diotima
2010-08-15 03:20:34 • Stephen Coles

I am a big fan of the work of Gudrun Zapf-von Hesse, especially Nofret, an elegant, calligraphic-influenced serif, which was one of the first fonts I used extensively for myself. I think it has beautiful lines and it is rarely seen, so it has a distinctive feel that comes from not being over-familiar. I also like her Alcuin a lot. It’s another strongly calligraphic, lovely, design.
