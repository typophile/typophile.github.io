Attached clip-art will feature prominently on main page. Header will be underneath. Looking for, Retro Slightly comical + self deprecating A little authoritative. I have been looking for a bold sans-serif (headline) but possibly with slightly irregular lettering. Maybe something like, http://new.myfonts.com/fonts/aerotype/pacifico/regular/ or http://new.myfonts.com/fonts/ortizlopez/olnewsbytes/bold/ So many choices its easy to get lost !! [[http://www.veer.com/products/typedetail.aspx?image=FBT0100016|El Grande]] might suit your needs. I searched in Veer's type collection with the [[http://www.veer.com/search/results.aspx?keyword=1950s%20retro%20bold%20sans&producttype=TYP&resulttab=vr&category=Type|search terms]] “1950s retro bold sans”. It may also be fun for you to have a rotating nameplate, sort of the way the T changes here at Typophile, or the way the banner changes over at [[http://new.typographica.org/|Typophile]]. Maybe your blog won't be so much about type, so it's not as appropriate, but sometimes fun should win out over propriety.
