WOFF TypeCon Party - Tuesday 17th August

August 3, 2010 • Si_Daniels

Following Microsoft’s recent announcement as the headline sponsor of this year’s TypeCon conference, the conference organizers cordially invite TypeCon attendees, speakers, and W3C Working Group participants to the event’s official kick-off party, in recognition and celebration of WOFF, the web font format that unites the web development and font development communities.

http://www.typecon.com/events.php#wtfwoff

TypeCon attendees should register for the party via the TypeCon registration site, W3C WG folks not attending TypeCon should RSVP to me SimonDa*.

Cheers, Si

*= @microsoft.com
