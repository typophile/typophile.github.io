Anglia comes in several 'flavors', and one of them is quite plain and un-decorative. See the top three samples [[http://www.bowfinprintworks.com/Script01Pg4.html|here]]. There are two lower case choices, both rather plain, and the Enhancements font gives alternate (plainer) choices for many of the initials. The other font with the round A was [[http://www.bowfinprintworks.com/Script01Pg3.html|Adage Script]] at the bottom of the linked page. Adage Script is Jason Walcott's take on Adscript, an old metal script face. - Mike Yanega
