“how could i approximate it as best as possible? that cool lower-case narrow, rounded r, for example,or the e, with the diagonal bar”. You’d get the rounded ‘r’ and the diagonal ‘e’ with “roberto” in ChaletComprimé CologneSixty or MilanSixty and and “cavalli” CologneSeventy or MilanSeventy from House Industries. http://www.houseind.com/index.php?page=showfont&id=11&subpage=viewfonts Still a long way away, though.
