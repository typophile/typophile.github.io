2005-11-07 02:06:51 • hrant

The Grauniad is a (now largely affection) nickname for The Guardian, which used to be notorious for its misspellings. I don’t think they still have the reputation, but the name has stuck.

The point of Kevin’s presentation in Helsinki was about the relationship between the pleasurable aesthetic experience of typography and measurable increase in cognitive performance, based on related work in which people are found to perform problem solving tasks more easily and quickly after an enjoyable mental experience such as watching an amusing video rather than a dry lecture. So not directly related to the crapshoot of trying to increase readership in a competetive newspaper market.

Fenway is in the back bay neighbourhood formally called Kenmore. The Fens is an area about a mile south of Fenway Park.

The Fens is a Lincolnshire area with the towns Boston and Spalding in eastern coastal England.

A.G. Spalding was a Boston Red Sox pitcher who founded the Spalding athletic company in 1876.

I was born 8/10 of a mile from Fenway Park in what is now the Beth Isreal Deaconess Medical Center, the official hospital of the Boston Red Sox. I now live in London England.
