Very pretty, poorly spaced. I’ve [in]famously equated it to a beautiful woman with a terrible speech impediment. Great to take to a party, just don’t let her talk. Robin Kinross has equated it to a loose bicycle. Either way, a tricky ride.

So don’t use it for text. Use it large for a couple of lines, and space it all by hand.
