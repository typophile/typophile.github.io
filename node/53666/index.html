[img:sites/default/files/old-images/fingerprint_6703.jpg] As I was buying my books for class, I decided to look for some design books that I could use for inspiration. This book, Fingerprint, intrigued me. The cover is simple: just the title, some abstract shapes, and a label explaining the contents of the book. I am a big fan of collaging by hand, and figured this book would give me some ideas on how to incorporate my handmade stuff with the digital. The layout is simple: unobtrusive text located on the edges and large images that allow the projects to retain the focus.
