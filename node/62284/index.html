September 23, 2009 • reedenger

Hello fine typofolks, I'm working on a wordmark for author Leif Enger. It's a clean, slender sans with an Urnes style ligature to hint at the client's Scandinavian ancestry. I'm shooting for 'dignified and literary.' The main applications will be standard business card, letterhead, etc. The forms were build from scratch, and I've been staring at them a bit too long; I'd love any advice on weight, balance, kerning, or anything other thoughts. Also, I'll likely be developing a version for web use, and I'd be grateful for any pointers to keep the lines crisp at web sizes. Thanks all! - Reed
