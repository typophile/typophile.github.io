Whenever I hear about bolder weights, I think of the FontBank collection. Rillo Heavy is exactly the right weight, I think. The middle part of this sample was made by stretching the height to 110%. The bottom two lines were lightened by adding white stroke, which created the pseudo ink-trap look in the N.
