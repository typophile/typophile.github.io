Actually, the answer isn’t quite right. Multiple adjectives before a noun are hyphenated:

This is my long-range rifle.

If they are put after the noun, no hyphen:

This is the rifle I shoot at long range.

BTW, everyone is detail oriented. What’s important is what details they are interested in. Only a certified compulsive is interested in all of them. Most companies don’t hire certified compulsives.
