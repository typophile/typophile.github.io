Maybe the term 'feminine' was a little misplaced or just a wrong choice of words... The idea behind the swashes was mainly to take the edge of the first draft, making it a bit less bland, more friendly looking. Basically just adding a little personality. Out of the 2 of us (or even 3 counting my other sister) Katja is probably the most 'centered' one, but that doesn't make her bland or boring by any means. Being convential doesn't make you narrow-minded by default at all; in a worst case scenario it just hides it better to the outside world. I guess what I'm trying to say with the swashes is that there is more than meets the eye. Altaira's first interpretation after the introduction of the swashes was precisely what I was aiming for: precise but personal, professional but not robotic.
