Forget Font Book; the question was, IN YOUR ORIGINAL FONT FILES, is the Family Name just that–the name of the family and nothing else–for all of the fonts? In FOG5, the “Typographic family name” should be the same, and Width, Weight and Slope should describe the other attributes. In FontLab, the “Family Name” should be the same, with Width, Weight and Style Name different in each version. I’m not sure where the proper slot for Exp or Alt might be; someone far wiser than I can probably help you there…
