Christian,

Thanks for kind words about Anziano and Luminance. Anziano is a typical text face and works best for books and magazines. Luminance on the other hand is more of a “text/display” oriented typeface. It probably needs a specific headline weight to work properly in larger sizes. And yes it its roots is from the Czech tradition. I find a lot of inspirtaion from that part of Europe. Storm is an excellent foundry, btw. They make wonderful typefaces.
