Smoking Club Typeface

January 24, 2013 • MATOI

New typeface released, it was inspired by movie tittles from old B&W Hollywood movies. The typeface was hand drawn using a high number of colors to produce a realistic 3D effect.Perfect for display use, large format graphics, magazines, posters and 3D design. Limited license for personal and commercial works.
