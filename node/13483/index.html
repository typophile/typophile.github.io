Klaus Hesse

July 6, 2005 • dan_reynolds

[[Indicies]] : [[Designers]] : Klaus Hesse Professor for Conceptional Design at the [[HfG Offenbach]] in Germany 1954, born in Elberfeld, Germany. Degree in Photography & Typography from the University of Wuppertal. In 1988, he founded [[Hesse Design]] with his wife, [[Christine Hesse]]. From 1993–1999, he was a design professor in Dortmund and Essen.
