1980 Hippie saloon banner font

March 31, 2014 • Cornelis

This logo was used in the Netherlands around 1980 in a campaign to promote mussel. I have a vivid memory of the image, but this capture of an old video is the best I can find. The letters ‘heerlijk! mosselen’ means ‘great! mussel’. I like to reproduce this logo but can’t find the font or a good look-a-like. Hope to hear your suggestions?

Cheers, Cornelis ![](/images/old-images/Heerlijk mosselen 1980_5027.jpg)
