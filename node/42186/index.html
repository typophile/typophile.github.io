I’m not sure about CS3, but on CS2, you can do it by enabling ‘Print Visible Guides and Baseline Grids’ (File > Print Presets > Define > Edit > General)

2008-02-17 18:09:56 • writingdesigning
If you’re not printing separations, you want to choose “Print Visible Guides and Baseline Grids” from General when you bring up the Print dialog box. Assuming you pick two blank pages, that’s all you’ll get.
