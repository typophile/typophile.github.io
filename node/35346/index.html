Personal Monogram

July 22, 2007 • kepano

This is a simple monogram I’ve been playing with, based on my initials (SA). I’ve only just realized how beautifully these two letters combine with Optima, but now I can’t make up my mind.

As you can see, progressing through the weights with this shape squeezes the small bridge of the a until it disappears. I can’t decide if I like that elegant but fragile detail or if I prefer the stronger but somewhat chunky look of the demi-bold. The medium weight is mostly for comparison.

Which do you prefer?

my preference is demi-bold hands down.
2007-07-22 20:54:44 • Juggling man

Take it from a professional graphic designer - the “S” does not read well enough to warrant such as extreme combination. The legibility of the “S” is lost, and it also takes on a secondary position because it is lower, thus making the viewer think your initials are AS, if they can even read it at all. It really just looks like an “a” with a strange tail.

If anything, perhaps you could try creating a separation between the characters by clarifying the boundaries of each character, possibly by stroking the outside of the “S” with a white outline.
