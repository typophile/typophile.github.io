Letter skeleton exposed!

April 30, 2009 • nina

[img:sites/default/files/old-images/skeleton_barissarhan_4342.jpg] Cover illustration by Barış Sarhan (in collaboration with Ahmet Eken) for a Turkish graphic design magazine. I think it’s a well made and somewhat funny illustration. They seem to be very serious about it as a visualization of the “skeleton model”, as “they have plans to make a scale model of the image to send to graphic design departments at various schools in Turkey” (quoted from source below).

Found at http://www.typetheory.com/?p=1803

Absolutely amazing (both the concept and the realization)!
