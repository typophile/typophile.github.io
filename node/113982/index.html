M & P: Chevalier http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/urw/chevalier/ 9.8.13: Sackers Gothic http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/mti/sackers-gothic/ Burin Sans http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/mti/burin-sans/ Sweet Sans http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/sweet/sans/ or several other similar fonts Mr. and Mrs. Robinson are small and fuzzy but the letters and the ampersand seem consistent with Sackers Gothic, Burin Sans, or Sweet Sans. Don’t think it makes a lot of difference which of these three you use for the these parts of the fonts. Don
