I know I’m not in the main target group anymore, but the new look Q Magazine is pretty hard to read at times. Normally I read magazines without reading glasses as I cook the evening meal, but this time it wasn’t that easy. Or what do you think of a 155 mm wide text in 7 pt? There are pages with sensible columns, too, not all the pages look like that (thankfully!).

I like the serif and sans fonts, but the techno leaves me cold and is pretty difficult to read, too
