And the third one contained the easiest solution: it's not like this process is particularly difficult, or even expensive, actually, since CFIA (Canadian Food Inspection Agency) requirements aren't nearly as anal as the FDA. La gouvernement yanqui wants to know what you're shipping out of your country considerably more than ours cares about what you're shipping in.... It ain't rocket surgery!
