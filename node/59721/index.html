The digital versions by [[http://new.myfonts.com/fonts/ef/trieste/ef-medium/characters.html|Elsner+Flake]] and Scangraphic both have an alternate 'g' that does not match your sample. The showing of Kleukens Antiqua in Jaspert's "Encyclopaedia of Type Faces" does show the same g as your sample. This makes me guess that your sample was made with a pre-digital version (either film type or metal). - Mike Yanega
