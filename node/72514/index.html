It looks very close to House Slant from the House Sign Painter set. Possibly a bit more stroke was added, but this is at least very similar to your sample. There were literally dozens of similar styles in the film type era, but a much smaller number were digitized. URW has some heavy brush scripts of this type, but I did not see an exact match.
