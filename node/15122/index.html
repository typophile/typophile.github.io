What sort of timeframe am I looking at to complete a good quality font? That is dependant on several factors, only a few of which include the complexity of the design, your skill level, your work habits, how many times you scrap the font and start all over…

what style of fonts is most likely to be a good one? make a font of some letterforms that inspire you. this inspiration can come from anywhere: other fonts, custom lettering, your own ideas and doodlings, &c.

How should a font be designed? with a good eye for what looks “right,” and with “a good eraser.” – (who said it?)
