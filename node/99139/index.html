What is the name of this font used in this picture?

Not “Typewriter” like, but like a Dymo Labelmaker. One possibility is [[http://www.fontspace.com/tension-type/impact-label|Impact Label]]. Others include [[http://www.azfonts.net/load_font/c14-blacktape.html|Carbon 14]], [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/typeart/dimeotype/|Dimeotype]], LabelGunTape, and Punch Label. There are others, such as DYMO (dafont.com) that don’t have the distinctive serif’ed /I in your sample.
