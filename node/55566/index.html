Typographic Mandala

March 6, 2009 • srbooker

I have this fascination with the Buddhist and Hindu tradition of mandalas. The are traditionally made from sand and I believe that once one is complete it is supposed to be swept away, I’m not totally sure about that one. I go a little bit outside that tradition. I put them everywhere; in my sketchbook, note pads, text books. So I decided to make one that incorporated type, and I love it! All there letters are in the middle and the outside is consists of little do dads I made up. I hope you enjoy looking as much as I have enjoyed creating.[
