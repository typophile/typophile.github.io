Vanna Likes Arial and Helvetica

October 10, 2007 • amyp

At the end of Wheel of Fortune this evening, Pat and Vanna were talking about their favorite fonts and Vanna said she uses Arial and Helvetica. Ok, so I am slightly embarrassed to admit that I was watching The Wheel, but it is worth public ridicule to bring this to the attention of typophiles everywhere.

Hey, Wheel of Fortune is awesome. I grew up with it and I still watch it. Vanna is still foxy after all these years, too.

Although I don’t know what the deal with her using Arial is. Maybe when she doesn’t have Helvetica close by and handy?
