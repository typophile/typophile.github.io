Be careful. Most East Asian sans serif typefaces are most definitely not geometric.

For Korean:

[[http://yoonfont.co.kr/shop/font_han_view.asp?itemidx=1702|윤체 (Yoon-che)]] [[http://www.sandoll.co.kr/SandollWeb/shop/product_down_read.asp?idx=728&category=211|아트체 (Art-che)]] [[http://yoonfont.co.kr/shop/font_han_view.asp?itemidx=1699|율려 (Yullyeo)]]

The last of these is definitely a display face, but for a magazine masthead it should work really well.

And last but not least, the classic geometric Korean face is [[http://www.hanyang.co.kr/font_pds/php/view.php?idx=35|안상수체]], (Ahn Sang-Soo, 1985), the eponymous design by Ahn Sang-Soo. This is also definitely a display face.
