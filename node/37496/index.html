GDI printing from Adobe Software on OS X

September 25, 2007 • blank

I have a printer that can spit out some really sweet-looking stuff, but it lacks Postscript support. Is there a way to make Indesign and Illustrator provide high-quality rendering on GDI printers?

I asked around a little, and found out that from InDesign there are 2 fundamental paths to print to non-postscript printers from OSX:

  1. print postscript and the print driver will use Adobe’s distiller to rasterize the page.
  2. have InDesign print a bitmap. There is an option in the advanced panel (I think) that controls this. Some other controls may become active/inactive based on this control. You probably should experiment.
