2003-10-13 14:51:47 • bowfinpw
No Mike, I'm pretty sure, because I recognize the methodics in constructing the letters, which struck me when I first saw the face. While Entropy* and Osprey** are hybrids constructed from two or more fonts***, this one is assembled by fusing parts of the next letter in the alphabet of the same typeface (Trajan by the looks of it). * Entropy is uppercase Missive fused to lowercase Memphis. Missive itself looks like a mix of Garamond and Trade Gothic. ** Osprey is basically Entropy with Park Avenue lower- case thrown in for added flavour. How far can you go? *** a popular practice in the deconstructivist heydays of the early nineties; see also P. Scott Makela's Dead History (Linotype Centennial and VAG Rounded) and Max Kisman's Fudoni (you can guess which faces).
