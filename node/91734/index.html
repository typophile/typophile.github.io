YVES is very close to Cheltenham indeed, but I think [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/berthold/sorbonne-bq/|Sorbonne BQ Bold]] (Berthold) is a better match

PEINTURES is very close to [[http://www.identifont.com/show?37X|Tiemann]]

PREFACE DE PASCAL CALUSE is similar to [[http://www.microsoft.com/typography/fonts/family.aspx?FID=236|Estrangelo Edessa]] an MS-Windows Font designed to support the Syriac script, the main differences being the strange /E of PREFACE and the /S
