If you’re looking for a digital version: There are a lot of – more or less – similar faces, under the names of Canterbury, Iglesia, Old(e) English, Old London, Cloister Black … The finer, more delicate versions are called Linotext, Mariage or Wedding Text. I’m not aware of a condensed one – but please don’t [[squoosh]]! ;°)
