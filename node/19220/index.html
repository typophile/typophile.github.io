New website of the Designiq studio

April 12, 2006 • filip blazek

In April, the graphic design studio [[Designiq]] launched a new English version of its website at www.designiq.eu. As before, it provides all elementary information about the studio, while its portfolio section has been significantly extended, offering a number of new work examples accompanied by a commentary. It also presents a new News section.

From larger recent projects, the website represents examples of new graphic design of Respekt weekly, of the book Móda / Fashion presenting the work of fashion designer Liběna Rochová, posters for the theatre festival Příští vlna / Next Wave, and many others.

[[Filip Blazek|Filip Blažek]]’s graphic design studio was founded in 1997, in 2003, its name has been changed to Designiq. It focuses on two main fields of work — the design of logotypes and corporate identity, and the promotion of groups and institutions from cultural sphere. The studio employs degree-educated designers with extensive work experience. The website was designed by Filip Blažek and Radek Sidun, and it is powered by Vizus CMS system.
