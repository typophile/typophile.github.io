No to be a total wise ass, but ... turn to the page where you read "Comping Text Type" (it's page 128) and now, slowly move your eyes to the left. No, no, stop! Look under your thumb. Yes, way over to the left column. Figure 2. See? That's a comped paragraph. Ok, so I'm a total wise ass. Oh well. Here it is on the net.
