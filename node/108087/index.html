The copyright of a digital font usually denotes it's debut year in digital form (for a specific publisher). If you go to this page: http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/berthold/ag-old-face-bq/ you can see this is a redraw of an older face:
In 1984 H. Berthold released this redraw of Akzidenz-Grotesk by Günter Gerhard Lange. This design emphasizes the 19th Century hand-setting roots of the original Akzidenz-Grotesk. Designers: Günter Gerhard Lange Design date: 1984
