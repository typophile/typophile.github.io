I'm pretty sure the sans is Apex New Medium [Small Caps], available exclusively from Village: http://vllg.com/Thirstype/ApexNew/mudTyper+Weights/ Look at the glyph table for Medium: http://vllg.com/images/font_family_members/Thirstype/ApexNewMedium_glyph.gif The "earmarks" in the glyphs are: A's crossbar and stance C's openness G's crossbar M's raised center R's straight leg S's center
