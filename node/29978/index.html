As I see it there are two ways to go here. Either you design something entierly new without inheritance from earlier motorcycle logos or you go for the similarity... If you go for similarity the brands you mentioned above are (according to me) slightly more "modern" than for example Harley Davidson or Triumph. I would go for a fat and wide font like Annonce from Canada Type ore something like that. Something with impact. http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/canadatype/annonce/ If you on the other hand wish to do something more classic you can always use a font like Euphonia from Sentinel Type or Maximo from Red Rooster. http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/sentinel/euphonia/ http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/redrooster/maximo-rr/ Just some spontaneous suggestions...
