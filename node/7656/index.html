My friend Harold Lohner has been looking into some typefaces used by Hitchcock in his films, as possible candidates for being digitized. (He has already done gaumont and gainsborogh, from the 39 steps and the lady vanishes respectively). He asked if I could tell him if this type has already been digitized, and as far as I could find the answer seems to be no.

Do any of you recognize this type, and do you know if it exists as a font?

BTW, this is Harold of Harold's Fonts who has asked me to see if you can help. He has been very good about recognizing the sources of his fonts, and does a good job of finding old fonts to digitize. I'd sure like to help him if we could.
