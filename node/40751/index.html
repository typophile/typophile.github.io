Sas, I don’t think there is such an option to check. You need to set up your grid in Illustrator properly. Great detailed instructions can be found here –

http://groups.msn.com/FontLab/tipsandtricks.msnw?action=get_message&mview=0&ID_Message=2675

This is what I used when I first started with FontLab. But after a short time, I realized how much of a waste of time it was to draw in Illustrator first. It is better to learn how to draw in FontLab.

As to your second question, there is no type foundry in Atlanta, good or otherwise. The closest are probably Phil’s Fonts/Garage Fonts in Washington, DC, House Industries in Delaware, H&FJ in NYC, and Monotype and Font Bureau in Massachusetts.
