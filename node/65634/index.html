You can approximate this probably custom lettering with [[http://new.myfonts.com/fonts/linotype/stymie/|Stymie]]. The Black weight is closest except for the y. Oddly, the Black y is different from the other weights. I think if you added stroke to the Bold weight you would be able to fake it. The M is also a bit different, crotch-wise, but you could edit it or ignore the difference.
