Fresh new batch of Hallmark fonts now available @ AscenderFonts.com

January 27, 2011 • billdavis

AscenderFonts.com is proud to release a wonderful set of new fonts from the [[http://www.ascenderfonts.com/foundry/hallmark/?sort=alpha|Hallmark™ Design Collection]]. And as a special introductory offer, all the new Hallmark fonts are on sale with a 25% discount now through February 9, 2011.

[img:sites/default/files/old-images/Hallmark_image_1_6072.png] This expanding collection contains a vibrant range of script and display fonts. These new releases showcase some exciting styles that offer distinctive ways to enhance a wide variety of design projects and personal communications. All Hallmark fonts are available immediately for instant download in TrueType® and OpenType® formats, and also for use as Web Fonts at www.FontsLive.com!

[img:sites/default/files/old-images/Fultoon_HMK_5994.png] The [[http://www.ascenderfonts.com/font/fultoon-hmk.aspx|Fultoon™ HMK font]] is an energetic, quirky script typeface from Hallmark. Fultoon has a fun handwritten style with the appearance of ink blobs.

[img:sites/default/files/old-images/Baa_Book_HMK_5041.png] The [[http://www.ascenderfonts.com/font/baa-book-hmk-family.aspx|Baa Book™ HMK font family]] is a clever set of fonts that comes in regular and bold weights. Baa Book HMK has a youthful appearance, with sprightly letterforms. Its hand lettering style is great for everything from scrapbooks to greeting cards.

[img:sites/default/files/old-images/Sandy_HMK_6355.png] The [[http://www.ascenderfonts.com/font/sandy-text-hmk-family.aspx|Sandy Text™ HMK typeface family]] was designed by the talented lettering artists at Hallmark Cards. Sandy Text HMK is a casual script with an engaging, flowing handwriting style. It comes in both regular and bold weights for added flexibility.

[img:sites/default/files/old-images/Twizot_HMK_3663.png] The [[http://www.ascenderfonts.com/font/twizot-hmk.aspx|Twizot™ HMK font]] is an engaging, alternative roman font from Hallmark. Twizot HMK has a has an uneven baseline that gives it plenty of energy, and bouncy letterforms that make it a fun font to work with.

For more information on the Hallmark fonts, please visit www.AscenderFonts.com
