Definitely not a font. Simon Walker’s lettering. Looks like stiff old Times Roman poured into a vortex to end up as a charming toilet paper lettering. To duplicate it, apply the buttery vertical strokes found in fonts like FF Tartine Script http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/fontfont/tartine-script/ or Aspic http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/alias/aspic/ to the letters of your choice add ball terminals, do swooshing and you are there. Don
