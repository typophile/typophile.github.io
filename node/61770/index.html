2009-09-08 20:10:57 • Ricardo Cordoba

To get the same effect (one stroke-weight) like in nr. 3 just expand your text grab all the anchor-points of the “n” except for the four on the baseline and move them upwards until the stem of the “n” shares the height of the stem of the capital “N”.

If it works it’s fine if not try to adjust the stroke-weight of “n” so it’s the same like in the orignal capital “N”.

I hope this is what you need.
