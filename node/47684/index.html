High-end, luxury type replacement for Times New Roman?

July 23, 2008 • kennymcnett

My high-end academic company has used Times New Roman as their “logo” for the last 17 years. It’s even in giant silver letters on the office wall. I asked for the logo file and they said “oh, just type it.” Cringe. Cringe.

As their new director of marketing, I’m trying to help them modernize. Fortunately, we’re re-branding and they’re up for a logo change, but I’m sure any extreme departure from Times New Roman is out of the question.

So, we’re shooting for a high-end, classy, luxury typeface that still has some academic tendancies.

Does anyone have any suggestions?

I will definitely look at them all. Thank you!
