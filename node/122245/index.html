This some variation of Clarendon, which resembles the Egyptian [once known as Antique] but has bracketed rather than rectangular [slab] serifs. Not an abundance of this style with height to width proportions similar to your image. Consider: Thorowgood https://www.myfonts.com/fonts/woodentypefonts/thorowgood/ Consort ExtraBold CondensedRR https://www.myfonts.com/fonts/redrooster/consort-rr/ CA Normal Serif Extra Bold https://www.myfonts.com/fonts/capearcona/ca-normal-serif/ Don
