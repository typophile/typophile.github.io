Mister Zee, if your real purpose here is to try to match the fonts for soccer jersey numbers, you should know that many of these numbers are custom fonts. Sometimes the best you can do is get something similar and then rely on the team colors to fool the viewer into thinking it's the right type style. Most people will not know as long as it's close. I'm not sure if B is custom numbering, but I am just saying this so that you don't assume that all numbers and letters are going to be fonts that you can find with a search engine.

Knowing how to describe the way they were made will not really help find them, as Patricia noted.
