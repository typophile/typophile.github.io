Scala/Scala Sans equivalent with Cyrillic support

March 2, 2015 • theanorak

I'm looking to find a suitable font to use in Russian-language publications for a client whose standard typefaces are Scala and Scala Sans (which don't support Cyrillic characters). At the moment it's looking like I'm going to have to make a list of all of the humanist faces and then check which have the necessary glyph support -- not an especially appealing prospect! Does anyone have a suggestion of a better way to carry out my search -- or possibly even firsthand knowledge of a suitable font?
How about searching Myfonts with the parameters "tag:humanist", "tag:superfamily" and "available characters contains:Д" ie: http://www.myfonts.com/search/tag%3Ahumanist+tag%3Asuperfamily+char%3A%D0%94/fonts/ Turns up a bunch of possibles.
