Yes, this is Cäsar Schrift. According to the PDF about Georg Schiller that Hans Reichardt has compiled for the Klingspor-Museum Offenbach and that can be downloaded from here, it was published in 1912 by Schriftgießerei C.F. Rühl, Leipzig. One year later, a bolder style was added.

A reproduction of the Cäsar Schrift specimen can be found in Type: A Visual History of Typefaces and Graphic Styles vol. 2 1901–1938, edited by Cees W. de Jong, Alston W. Purvis and Jan Tholenaar (Köln: Taschen, 2010).

I doubt a digital version has been published yet.
