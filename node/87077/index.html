New release: Equity

November 4, 2011 • butterick

A text font in the Janson / Ehrhardt mode, but with many contemporary details and conveniences. http://equityfont.com A snazzy PDF specimen is here: http://typo.la/eqts The basic family includes 6 styles: regular, italic, bold, bold italic, regular small caps, and bold small caps. All 6 styles are provided in two weight grades (the default grade + a lighter grade for office printers that add weight) The standard license allows PDF embedding, @font-face embedding, and editable embedding (e.g., in word-processing documents).
