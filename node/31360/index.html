Z, who said anything about splendid isolation? I too like to develop ideas in collaboration, but with people who bring something more to the table than "I don't have any ideas". All too often people come along saying "I'm stumped, I have no ideas, what would you do" (often not supported with any background information about the project or any resemblance or attempt of brainstorming.) My suggestion to you is to get away from the computer for a bit, you won't get to "work with like-minded people to stimulate ideas" sitting alone at a computer - in fact 'like minded' and 'stimulate' don't often go together. ;) Go exploring, jump on a train, or bus, or bike and travel further than you have previously, take a sketch book and/or camera. Document your journey and progression, collect things along the path, see where it all leads you. Perhaps your adventure will be driven by "further" or perhaps you'll forget about it all together, either way it's good. Then once you return ... if you decide to, return to your gathered inspirations and relive them, this time in a different environment.
