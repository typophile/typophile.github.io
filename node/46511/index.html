MGS4 Codec Screen Text

June 17, 2008 • AndrewSipe

I was playing MGS4 this weekend and my typophile senses were tingling.

During the in-game conversations between the main character and a remote character the text of the conversation would be shown on screen.

It looked great on my TV and I wanted to know what it was. The most unique thing was the slanted stroke under the eye of the “e”.

Also, it looks like a rounded sans in the screen grab, but I’m not entirely sure it is.

Boy, I really stumped them with this one…

Stephen. Jan. Mike. Angel. Anyone?!
