Now for the grumpy pedantic part: The way I understand the meaning of “font,” it’s a set of letters created for mechanical, optical, or electronic reproduction. The lettering in a tattoo can be a hand-drawn copy of a font, but it won’t be a font until they invent a tattoo robot.
