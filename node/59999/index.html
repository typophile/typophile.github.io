2009-07-15 17:29:14 • evanbrog

You can’t delete the path, however you can change the Fill and Stroke thickness to NIL and Zero respectively. But yes, after setting the text on path, if you convert your text to outlines, the path will automatically disappear.

For the second issue, try using two semi circles rather than a complete circle.

Actually, I can’t get the type to follow the inside of the circle, and appear upright. It’s either on the outside, upright, or on the inside, upside down.

The help file is called: Adjust the type on a path position

Basically, with the selection tool you should be able to click on the type and you will see a small line at the centre of the text. Click and drag, and you will be able to move the text along the path, and you will also be able to drag the path to the inside of the circle. (As satya says, you may need to use two circles, and you may need to use baseline shift to adjust the baseline to that the top of the text, rather than the base, is on a path.)
