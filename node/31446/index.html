Finding a companion to Futura just recently came up on Typophile here. The person was looking for a companion for a Futura logo to be used for short passages of text. The consensus as to what to use for text appeared to be Futura (again), Bodoni (what [[Robert Bringhurst]] suggests because of the two typefaces similar mechanical feel), a humanist sans like Syntax or Gill, or a slab serif like Beton, Memphis, or Lubalin Graph. You should note that the person looking for help was adamant about using another sans serif; most would suggest using a serif for extended body text.

In the thread, it came up that Futura came out of the Bauhaus and Art Deco movements, so I question your interest in using an Art Nouveau typeface, especially since Art Nouveau, to my knowledge, is most associated with France, not the UK.

Some suggestions: Avenir Next, <a href=http://www.houseind.com/index.php?page=showfont&id=18>Neutraface, and Relay could be good choices for sans-serifs that evoke both the geometric and Art Deco flavors of Futura. I’d, however, recommend a serif for body text, although other than Bodoni, no other one comes immediately to mind. The other thread I mentioned largely came to the conclusion that using sans-serifs for extended texts is not a great idea, so I’d really look into serifs.

I’m not so up on my Art Nouveau typefaces, so I can’t make any suggestions there.

Well, I disagree with your organization’s legibility guidelines – I think that using a sans here would hinder it if anything. The only Art Nouveau, readable sans-serif I could find is Benguiat Gothic. It’s by far the most readable Art Nouveau sans I could find, but I still wouldn’t consider it a terrific choice for body text – the letterforms are ornate enough to distract your readers. If you’re committed to Art Nouveau, use this, but if you’re not, look for a humanist sans-serif. May I recommend the newly released Beorcana by Carl Crossgrove, a fellow Typophile? It’s specifically designed to be extremely legible, although I’m not positive if I would want to combine it with Futura. Might the Display sizes of it work for headlines too?
