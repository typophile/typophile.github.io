Corporate BS type specimen

September 26, 2007 • fredo

Hello, I'm working on a type specimen and the chosen theme is Corporate BS, that hollow language used by corporations worldwide. I've found generators here and here. But as specimens go, some of the sentences are too long, and maybe not as annoying as I want them to be. Some examples: redefine integrated users reinvent seamless content orchestrate compelling channels expedite holistic platforms enable impactful relationships redefine world-class content incentivise holistic synergies competently extend cutting-edge interfaces energistically maximize 24/7 interfaces It could be just two words, somehow infected, like synergy effect. What expressions trigger your contempt? Would you be so kind to share? It'll be fun, it'll be a win-win situation! Cheers, Fredrik
