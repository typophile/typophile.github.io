Is there a way to do this direclty in Fontlab? One way would be to
  1. Select a radius for your rounded corners--say, 12 units);
  2. In the EFFECTS menu, select BOLD, uncheck "Keep glyph dimensions," then select -12 for H weight and V weight;
  3. In the same menu, check "Make round corners" and change the weight to +12.
You can fiddle with the values to get the effects you're looking for...
