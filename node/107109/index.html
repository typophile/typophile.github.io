2013-11-05 00:24:42 • R.
Thanks R. for the PDF link. I compared some letters from Mexico with other fonts and I agree with Ryuk that Industry is the closest. This is a comparison of K and S, with Industry Demi in red and Mexico in black – the caps heights of both fonts are standardized at 700: [img:sites/default/files/old-images/IndustryDemiRedandMexicoBlackK_4997.jpg] [img:sites/default/files/old-images/IndustryDemiRedandMexicoBlackS_5693.jpg] S is pretty close. K is the same design but wider. Other letters in Industry Demi are also wider. To replicate the effect of Mexico the widths of Industry Demi would need to be adjusted. Also, as noted by Ryuk, the G needs to be redrawn. Other revisions as required. Don
