body: "<strong>[[Indices]] : [[Foundries]] : Underware</strong>\r\n\r\nUnderware is
  a (typo)graphic design-studio which is specialized in designing and producing typefaces.
  These are published for retail sale or are specially tailor-made. The company was
  founded in 1999 by [[Akiem Helmling]], [[Bas Jacobs]] and [[Sami Kortemaki|Sami
  Kortem\xE4ki]]. Underware is based in the corresponding studios of each of its three
  principals in [[The Hague|Den Haag]], [[Helsinki]] and [[Amsterdam]] respectively.\r\n\r\nUnderware
  started [[TypeRadio]] and various type workshops. Underware designed the cover of
  [[Font_Magazine|font]] issue 004.\r\n\r\nUnderware fonts: [[Bello]], [[Sauna]],
  [[Auto]], [[Dolly]], [[Fakir]] and [[Unibody]].\r\n\r\nURL: http://www.underware.nl\r\n\r\n<strong>Discussion:</strong>\r\n<a
