[[Indices]] : [[Designers]] : Mark van Bronkhorst

Mark van Bronkhorst is principle of [[MvB Design]], [[MvB Fonts]] and [[TypoBrand]]. Through his firm, MvB Design, he has served clients [[FontShop]] and [[FontHaus]] among others.

Mark art directed the first three issues of [[Font_Magazine|font magazine]].

Mark has had great success with the commercial release of the quirky, DIN-like typeface ITC Conduit. His more recent [[garamond]] style font, Verdigris, is also well liked.
