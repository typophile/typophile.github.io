First "Critique Thursday"

August 15, 2005 • dan_reynolds

To help revive the type sections of the Typophile Critique Forum (and as my first official moderator shin gig), I’d like to inaugurate a trial »Critique Thursday« this Thursday, August 18th.

The critiquing will take place right here on Typophile, from 1–3 pm Eastern Standard Time (that should be 10 am–12 noon in California, 19:00–21:00 in Central Europe, etc.).

I’ll select one thread from each typeface style sub-section within the critique forum for special critique. That thread will have its name changed, just like marked threads in the Type ID forum. Everyone is invited to send some time in the alloted time slot checking out those threads and making great suggestions.

I haven’t selected any types yet. If anyone has suggestions, just e-mail ‘em to me. Self promo is ok ;-)

Are you soliciting fonts in progress, or is this limited to existing threads? In the spirit of self-promotion, I’d be happy to offer either my Century Catalogue (now very close to completion) or Christian Gothic.

Here’s some discussion I most definitely would like some feedback on: post numbers.

I’ve received several requests from designers wanting their fonts to be selected for critique, which is great. But some of those fonts have a lot of posts on their threads already (one even has 70+). I’d much rather pick out fonts that have gotten less attention… I think that Critique Thursday would be of more benefit to those designers than to others who have already gotten a lot of feedback from the usual suspects (posters like Paul and Hrant, without whom, there really wouldn’t be much of a critique forum to discuss). Of course we want as many people to post, but I think that is more likely to happen if so much hasn’t been covered yet.
