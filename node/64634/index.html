Oksana Sans Narrow & Condensed

November 23, 2009 • andrijtype

dear Typophiles!

I am happy to tell about new release. It is Oksana Sans Narrow and Condensed, new faces in Oksana big family in six weights each, with european latin & cyrillic codepages.

In this faces i try to experiment with cyrillic ligatures: after very usual ukrainian «ії» and «її» i add also «сл», «єл», «ељ» etc. I plan to expand list of cyrillic ligatures in my fonts and interesting to know your opinion about.
