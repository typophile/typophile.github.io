V. Depending upon the font which you are using to create your 3D object, I would recommend you contact the foundry. Some foundries won't mind and will not see what you have done as against their EULA, but some foundries will see it exactly opposite. Those foundries will view what you've created as dependent upon their typeface and will require additional licensing fees. I would guess that these foundries will be more likely to be generous if you contact them now, before you've created the product.
