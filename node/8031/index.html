I think it looks like Compacta with a modified S, G and probably the N. One reason I picked it, is that the C has the same longer lower 'chin', rather than being symmetrical. I know the poster for the thread James mentioned said it was NOT Compacta, but I think that was based on the mismatch of the modified letters.

Sure there may be a newer font out there, but other than House Gothic 23, I can't think of anything this close.

My vote is customized Compacta.
