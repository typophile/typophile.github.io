"Monotype Apollo can be seen as the Monotype version of this design" [Meridien] I don't know who said that, but these fonts are not equivalent. Both Meridien and Apollo were designed by [[http://new.myfonts.com/search/adrian+frutiger/fonts/|Adrian Frutiger]], but a casual look should show that they are not the same at all.
