What part of Toronto? Thorn Press in Don Mills is good, but they might be bigger than you are comfortable dealing with. There used to be a good small shop on Railway Ave in Don Mills called Trade Offset (or something similar) that was next door to the type house I worked at.

Most Canadian wedding invites are mass produced in a shop in Stratford. I don’t think they are set to do much custom work, although if you can live with one of their catalog formats, you can save a bundle. (But that sucks for a designer).

Wish I could think of more names, but it has been a long time since I left Toronto.
