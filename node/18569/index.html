Hi - this “font” comes from something called the MacSoft collection, copyright CompuWorks 1994. To paraphrase, this collection “is both good and original - but the parts that are good are not original, and vica-versa”. I expect that this outfit are out of the type business - a quick search not fruitful, but all it’s worth - you could use something like TransType to convert it to PC TrueType http://www.fontlab.com/Font-tools/TransType/
