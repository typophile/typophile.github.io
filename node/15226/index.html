Wow, sounds like it'll be a blast! When in Venice try to get a ferry ride & tour to the Armenian monastery on San Lazzaro. The Mekhitarist order housed on that island did the first Armenian printing from movable type (they also did printing in a couple of dozen other languages too) and as surreal as Venice is, that place is doubly so... It's like stepping back centuries. hhp
