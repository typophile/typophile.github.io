Now that TC06 and ATypI are done, our thoughts turn to next year's 'con, which will be in gorgeous, lovely, sunny summer Seattle. August 1-5, 2007. Planning meeting coming up next Friday. All type geeks in the PNW are welcome. More details at http://typophile.com/node/28637. Anyone not in the local area, please feel free to post your ideas here, and we'll talk about them next week. Email me at the usual spot - info aaaaat typesociety.org if you want offline talk.
