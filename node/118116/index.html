French Comic Font

July 20, 2014 • JHalderman

This is a font seen in a French comic.

[img:sites/default/files/old-images/Capture_4888.PNG]

The most unusual characters seem to be the very star-like asterisk, and the ‘i’, I believe it is upper-case, despite looking lower-case, as the whole rest of the book is upper-case too.

I have often commented about how easy it is to make personal fonts with samples of hand lettering, just by filling in a template. I would expect the cartoonist could easily have made a custom font of his own printing. I did use one of the online font tools with part of this sample to search for this, and did not turn up a match. The odds are good that this is not available because it is custom.
