Some of Manfred Klein's Blackletter fonts for FontFont have loops on the ascenders, but that is an unusual feature. I think your sample looks hand-lettered, however. Manfred has generously made most of his fonts available for free at TypOasis, and he has helped with some of the Blackletter revival fonts on that site too, but I don't think this is among them. - Mike Yanega
