Eslam some thoughts about your site. First it takes to long to load. You should have a percentage by the loading flash files and you need a link to by pass flash pages. I hate waiting on flash pages. Your name on the opening page has a thin orange line cutting across it. (I’m on a Mac G4 using OS 10.3.9 and viewed your page using Mozilla) Once I got to your sample pages It took forever for the samples to load and they are far to small to make a decision about them. Make it where you can click on the image and it will allow it to fill the screen. Your samples have no copy to explain what you actually did on the project. All the pretty pictures without copy will not make one sale. One last note the 2nd sample in the interactive the justified type on the first line is terrible. Either fix that type or don’t show that sample. I think you are trying to hard, trying to do to much and thus not doing it well. Try to focus on your strengths as a designer. If your work is mostly focused on print, focus on print and tell your clients you also do web as well but don’t try to be all things to all people. It doesn’t work.
