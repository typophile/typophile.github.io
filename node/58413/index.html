This particular logo type face was a custom crafted font originally used for Marlboro Team Penske. It was crafted back in 1991 by Charlie Hughes and commissioned to Leo Burnett/Philip Morris.

It does not exist as a standard font. If you are looking to use it for Penske promotional purposes you can contact Penske Racing headquarters in North Carolina and request a media kit.
