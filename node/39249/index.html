Surf mag logo design

November 20, 2007 • dendicott

Hi all

Wanted to gauge reactions to this work-in-progress, for a logo design for a surfing magazine.

The target demographic is 6-16 year olds, male and female, with a view to (strapline) promote grassroots surfing in Australia.

It’s a redrawn Riptcure font for the logo, and for all other type I’m using Gill Sans.

Toying with the idea of a serif to contrast against Gill - that’s also very contemporary - any thoughts?

Thanks!

2007-11-20 03:56:17 • Kat Larson

In its current state, I don’t think this is very successful. Chopping off the leg of the R and the bottom of the F to make room for “Australia” feels arbitrary, rather than clever. (Sorry, Kat, I don’t see a fin.) The area where the leg of the R leaves the bowl is too thick.

The curves of the S are lumpy, and don’t match the R and F very well. The U is too wide – its big, open shape dominates the logo.

Consider slanting “Australia” (in a more contemporary face, as Vanina suggests) to match the angle of SURF. And you might add a bit more weight overall; the whole thing feels a little spindly.
