2005-01-15 07:12:08 • James Gareth
A lot of the look and feel of Quadraat has to to with it's designers' views on type production (now and in the past).
Fred Smeijers has produced a wonderful little book (Counterpunch) about this -- you may want to check it out. It will make quite clear why Quadraat is a unique typeface. And why it's gonna be hard to find similar typefaces.
(I'm biased, I know. I've used Quadraat for a pet project, some time ago, and am completely smitten with it).
