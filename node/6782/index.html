A word from both moderators In keeping with Typophile's mission to further and protect type design, we strongly discourage references to free- or shareware rip-offs of available digital typefaces. Recognizing that there are gray areas in regards to what constitutes a rip-off, this policy doesn't apply to fonts which are interpretations of existing typefaces which are not yet made digital by their copyright holders, and maybe other special cases. The matter will be open to discussion at its dedicated thread in the (very) near future.
