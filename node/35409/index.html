the shadow faux-pas

July 25, 2007 • popluhv

It bothers me to see letter forms with a 3-D or shadow effect mixed with letters that do not have this feature. Furthermore, I feel that if you are going to mix font sizes with these fonts, the depth of the shadow or 3-D effect should remain the same depth (rather than in proportion to the letter form). Does anyone else feel the same way, or am I alone on this?

I’ll go along with that. I used to think 3-D and drop-shadows were the coolest thing there was (all my early fontplays used both effects), but along the way, I realized they cut down legibility, and didn’t really do justice to the letter shapes, and that seemed counter-productive. Now they usually bug the heck out of me when I see them used by others. The only way I like a dropshadow now is when I don’t really notice it.

Along with that, when I look at the fonts I bought years ago, I wonder what the heck was I thinking.
