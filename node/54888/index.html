You should try and think about a theme or concept before you start putting things down on paper. Graphic design that has no content always leaves me feeling slightly empty.

Also look into grids for layouts (www.thegridsystem.org is a good start) as it will give you a lot more control when piecing different elements together. I always wish someone had told me about them earlier!
