Land Registry’s corporate font, Geo, was designed by Dalton Maag circa 2003/4 and in association with North who were responsible for our overall identity programme. The family consists of four weights(regular, italic, bold, bold italic) at text size and a single headline weight of a distinct angular design. Panjapop has posted a copy of our all-staff guide on Flickr.

http://www.flickr.com/photos/panjapop/2634190823/in/set-72157605959784544/
