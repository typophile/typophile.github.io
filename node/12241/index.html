[[Indices]] : [[Designers]] : Adam Twardoch When Adam Twardoch was 14 years old, Pope John Paul II laid a hand on his head, somewhere in a field in Poland. Since then, Adam has been on a holy mission to improve global typography. Especially the way that western designers draw their ogoneks. Adam currently works for [[FontLab]] Ltd. He lives in Germany, on the Polish border in a small town named Frankfurt an der Oder (not to be confused with the larger west German city with a similar name: Frankfurt am Main). Adam is also on the board of directors for the [[ATypI]], whom he also serves as the country delegate for Poland. website: www.twardoch.com
