Typophile honoured in Design Week

February 8, 2007 • timd

Typophile is mentioned in the current issue of Design Week, specifically the thread on WTF ligatures. ‘The marvellous blog[!] typophile.com recently[!] ran a hypothetical exercise imagining what ligatures and letter-symbols we would have created had current phrases been prevalent before keyboards were truly established. It’s remarkable what distracted typographers can produce to symbolise LOL or WTF or my favourite RTFM. Of course, what we really need is a ligature for www (w3?) or even http://.’

Pictured are Lex’ and gmckenzi’s RTFMs and Daminox’, Mark Simonson’s, Cerulean’s and Ikean’s WTFs (And Mrs Eaves, the article is about ligatures and swash characters and their recent use). Tim
