Hitsville USA Motown Banner

April 28, 2015 • meanoldlady

I am trying to recreate the banner for a Motown party for my kids. I am having a hard time trying to identify what font to use.

The banner lettering artist seems to have based the lettering on Coronet Bold https://www.myfonts.com/fonts/mti/coronet/ Which was very popular as a do all script during the 1940s to 1960s. Don
2015-04-28 14:55:40 • donshottype
Looks like the lettering artist preferred the look of the S in a competing font from the era, called Quick or Trafton Script, which is the inspiration for a digital font called Parfum http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/rmu/parfum/ It also inspired a digital font called Pacific Script http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/scholtz/pacific-script/ Don
