2010-01-23 20:31:22 • hrant
Christian Schwartz, discussing the direction for Meta Serif with Erik Spiekermann: “[M]y grand plan (and the underlying reason why I think Meta Serif should be an Antiqua) is to draw Unit Serif as well, and let that one be a real Egyptian. […] In the end, I think we’d end up with a serif and slab that could be used together, and they’d be compatible with both FF Meta and FF Unit.” — metaserif.com ‘Unit Serif’ later was released as Unit Slab – after Meta Serif. But yes, Unit (Sans) came before those two. See also FontShop’s blurb for the Unit superfamily.
