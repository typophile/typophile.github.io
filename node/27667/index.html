Looking for help choosing some fonts for a new project

August 15, 2006 • Ashley Morgan

I’m looking for some font suggestions for a few projects.

The Ashley Morgan Quintet - Contemporary jazz and bebop. Trumpet, saxophone, piano, bass and drums quintet.

Enormous - Six piece indie pop band. Described by Mojo magazine as “mature song writing fused with horn rimmed pop with more hook lines than a slaughterhouse”.

Big Arena Records - A small independent record label, an umbrella organisation through which the music of the above bands will be promoted and distributed.

The fonts will be used for everything from websites to printed flyers to CD covers.

My own initial thoughts are to go with something blue-note-esque for my quintet, a big heavy sans for Enormous, but I’m open to all ideas. Thanks in advance for any advice or suggestions.

Font Bureau’s Interstate works well at large sizes and could work for the ‘big heavy sans’ you are looking for.

For the jazz quintet why not try a nice upbeat serif, maybe something like Dolly from underware.

I’m not sure what to suggest for the record label, it all depends what sort of image you are trying to portray. Have a look at emigre.com. Something there might take your fancy.
