You might be having a problem with your font cache files. Search your harddrive for files named: AdobeFntXX.lst (The XX will be a number, say "11" or "07".) Throw these files away and restart your computer. Important: DO NOT DELETE files named: AdobeFnt.db. If you are still having problems, you might want to contact M. Porchez directly; this might be an issue which he is familiar with, and if it isn't, I'm sure that he would like to know about your experiences.
