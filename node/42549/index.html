Hi Lisa,

I like that one small but when large it’s too heavy.

What you’re experiencing is the problem/the absent of [[Optical Scaling]].

Over the last years, several foundries have published good text font families with optical sizes. Check out Adobe’s line-up. Or [[Sumner Stone]]’s ITC Bodoni and Cycles. Or Hoefler & Frere-Jones’ faces. Or …

For Caslon: ITC Founder’s Caslon has optical sizes. You can also simply combine matching fonts and, for example, utilize Big Caslon by Carter & Cone for large sizes.
