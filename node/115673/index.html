I’m trying to find the font used for a couple of “10” I’ve seen on some race cars. Anyone know which they are?
This /1 is specific of “[[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/linotype/helvetica|Helvetica]]-ish” fonts. The only difference I could see between Helvetica ([[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/linotype/neue-helvetica|Neue]]) (Extra)Compressed is the /0 counter bottom which is not flat (but easily editable). You could also have a look to all other “Helvetica-Compressed-Black-like”: [[http://www.myfonts.com/search/inserat|Inserat]] ([[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/itfmecanorma/british-inserat|British Inserat]] looks decent to me), [[http://www.myfonts.com/search/haettenschweiler|Haettenschweiler]], [[http://www.myfonts.com/search/plakette|Plakette]], [[http://www.myfonts.com/search/compacta|Compacta]]
