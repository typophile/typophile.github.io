Yes. These days, Adobe are pretty much maintaining total parity between their apps on different platforms. One of the ways in which they are managing this is by not using common system text components; they are 'rolling their own' instead. Gradually, the core text processing of each Adobe app is being rewritten to support Unicode encoding. InDesign and Photoshop have this done already. My guess is that Illustrator is likely to be next, but Adobe refuse to comment.
