2008-02-19 22:14:49 • vinceconnare
It is not Helvetica because the lowercase 'a' right side goes straight down and does not have an extenstion like Helv. Also the figure 'one' in Helvetica doesn't have a straight top extenstion but a curved one. The parens are spot on Univers as is the lowercase 'g', comma, and numerals. http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QSvuP8dnz84&feature=related
