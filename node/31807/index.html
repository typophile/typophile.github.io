MetaSerif Preview

February 27, 2007 • Reed Reibstein

Doesn’t look like anyone’s posted about this yet, so I thought I’d go ahead. On his blog, [[Erik Spiekermann]] has posted a preview of the upcoming MetaSerif (or MetaAntiqua) by [[Christian Schwartz]] and [[Kris Sowersby]]. I’ve heard mention of MetaSerif on Typophile one or two times, but this is the first time I’ve seen it. Looks nice to me at first glance; it seems to blend with Meta “Sans” especially well, as one would expect.

Blends well, indeed. When we gathered typeface suites for our Serif/Sans newsletter, we were surprised there were so few families that do this well. Expect the Meta family to play together very nicely.

By the way, it may actually be named “FF Meta Serif” (with a space).
