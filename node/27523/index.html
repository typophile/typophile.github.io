Between humanist and gothic/grotesk ?

August 8, 2006 • eclectic

Hello typophiles,

I’m looking for a sans font that would look between a humanist font and a gothic/grotesk font. Serious and elegant.

For example, FF Meta is too humanist for my purpose and Helvetica Neue is too cold.

Frutiger seems like the right choice right now, but I’d like to test with a font a tad less humanist.

By the way, please do not suggest any fonts that only come in 200 euros + bundles (DTL, I’m thinking about you). As beautiful as they may be, we’re a small design studio and we can’t afford them !
