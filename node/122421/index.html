Concurring with Riccardo on custom for the main logo. But to me and even if the use case showcases some sort of type construction from scratch using circles, my hunch is it’s a customization of an existing typeface and, without too much thinking, I’d say from [[http://www.myfonts.com/search/din|DIN]] mainly recreating a custom /A (from /O or /G) and /Y (from bottom half /O + a vetical stroke) and manually softening and rounding finials angles.
