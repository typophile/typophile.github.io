The Mountain has come to Dan Reynolds

April 8, 2006 • dezcom

Take a look at Dan's announcement for his soon-to-be-released face "Mountain" at: http://www.typeoff.de/?p=44 I just looked at Mountain. Mountain has a constructivist base with a blackletter flavor--which does not surprise me considering Dan's affinity for blackletter:-) I quite like the cap D in particular. I think Mountain's italic is quite successful and may find its way into some metal band logos. Excellent work Dan! ChrisL
Mountain is not for sale yet, but it will appear soon with [[Volcano Type]], and the link Chris posted will be updated to include more info about that when it happens. A trial version of Mountain is bundled with the book Bastard: Choose My Identity. That is a beautifully book which I can heartily recommend. I just received copy from the designers today.
