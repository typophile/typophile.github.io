A different approach could be a sans serif version of Trajan. Or Novecento, which is inspired by european cities’ historical lettering (http://typography.synthview.com/). If you want/need something which references a period in-between those two, you will need at least some hint of blackletter. Or, finally, you could go the Typedia route (http://typedia.com/blog/post/behind-the-typedia-logo-design/).
