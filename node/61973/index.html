I hoped someone more expert than me could step forward. Anyway, here are my thoughts:

  1. You should revise spacing and kerning. The letters appears too tight and crowded. Furthermore, even if this is an unconnected script, the letters’ flow should to some extent follow the one of cursive handwriting.
