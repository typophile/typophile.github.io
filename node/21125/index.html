The website address - www.fripolisen.no - is set in Envy by Magnus Rakeng, published by Thirstype in the mid/late 90s. The type was subsequently taken off the market when it was licensed by a publisher for a (now-defunct) music magazine. The typeface will probably be re-released by Thirstype some time in 2006 or early 2007. As for "silver"... That appears to be a custom logotype. It is not in Magnus's portfolio, so is likely the work of one of many talented Norwegian designers.
