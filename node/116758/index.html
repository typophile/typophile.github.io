A question regarding font pairing from a newbie

May 22, 2014 • karenthring

Hi All, I am really struggling…..I am designing wedding stationery for a lady who loves Geosans light and desperately wants her heading text, extra large in it. However, I am really struggling with a body text pairing. Her design is very stark and modern. Any ideas? Thanks so much
Why not continue using the same font for the body text? Use larger than usual point size but reduce line space to make the text one well- defined block - it could even slightly overlap a very large heading. With generous white margins all round. Starkly modern!
