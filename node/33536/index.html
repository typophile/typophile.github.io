Try the FAQs for image posting, this is too wide to see the whole quote in one go and thus tricky to judge the kerning. My first advice is to work on the kerning in rfl in butterfly, this might involve redrawing parts of the f so the r doesn’t meet the crossbar. Try the print out and squint method to pick out any particularly dark or light patches and fix them. For the “To Dee…” section you might try an uppercase E on ella and work on the turnovers to get “We love you.” all on one line, then you could look at overhanging punctuation.
