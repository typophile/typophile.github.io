The boxy small serifs are somewhat similar to Cushing Two Book [img:sites/default/files/old-images/CushingTwoBookAsAustralia_6024.gif] or Cushing Two Demi [img:sites/default/files/old-images/CushingTwoDemiAsAustralia_5866.gif] http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/bergsland/cushing-two/ Consider also Bushing Thin [img:sites/default/files/old-images/BushingthinAsAustralia_3668.gif] http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/bergsland/bushing/ which has an _a_ closer to _Australia_. You might consider pasting Cushing Two serifs on a light weight low contrast font with letter-forms close to _Australia_. Don
