In terms of curve definitions, there are two: cubic (PS) and quadratic (TT). Most people design in the former, but most actual fonts end up in TT format. Ideally you design in the same curve definition model as the output format, but most people never get comfortable (or bother getting comfortable) designing in quadratics.
