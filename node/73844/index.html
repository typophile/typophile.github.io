I WANT YOU: a FREE art magazine

September 4, 2010 • Dumb Eyes

[img:sites/default/files/old-images/I_Want_You_3918.jpg] ORDER FREE PRINT COPY I WANT YOU magazine’s focus is to highlight artists’ work rather than dilute its impact by enforcing editorial perspective. Each page of the print edition consists of a single large format image from a series of each artist’s work and a url that leads you to their feature on iwantyoumagazine.com. I Want You magazine features a broad range of artists from around the world, exclusive art work, and spotlights on forgotten or overlooked artists.
