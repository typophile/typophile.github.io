My first typeface

September 23, 2014 • danmitsui

I posted here for the first time a couple of weeks ago, asking for some technical recommendations for creating a typeface from some letters I had designed. I have not designed a typeface before now; this is my first attempt. The replies I received were helpful, so I thought I would share the result: http://www.danielmitsui.com/00_misc/benedict_sample2.jpg http://www.danielmitsui.com/00_misc/benedict_sample.jpg I am an artist who specializes in ink drawing, mostly in the style of 14th & 15th century illuminated manuscripts. I am working to publish my own illustrated versions of blockbooks from that era; this typeface is what I intend to use for these projects. I wanted something that marries well to a late Gothic drawing style, but is more readable to modern eyes than blackletter.
