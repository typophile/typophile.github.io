Typeshop was Walter Brendel. Brendel did a lot of altering existing type to be heavier/thinner/wider/narrower to meet customer demands. He did have original designs, too, though. To my eye, Denver looks to be a narrowed Devinne. And perhaps the naming (Devinne -> Denver) would kind of reinforce that?
