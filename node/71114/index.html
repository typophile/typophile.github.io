This seems to be a pre-digital film typeface. I found an exact match in the 1971 Photo-Lettering catalog called “Alexey Brodovitch Albro” grouped in with styles they called “Twenties” (presumably for 1920’s looks).

I do not know of a digital version of this typeface.
