Serifed R with vertical leg

August 15, 2014 • donshottype

I am looking for Serif fonts where the R has vertical leg. The serif can be slab or Clarendon style like OL Contact Classic [img:sites/default/files/old-images/OLContactClassic_R_3587.gif] or Superba RR Bold [img:sites/default/files/old-images/SuperbaRRBoldHaas1928-30_R_3537.gif] or Map Type [img:sites/default/files/old-images/MapType_R_3881.gif] but without the small curve on the inside of the leg of Aachen Bold [img:sites/default/files/old-images/AachenBold-R_6022.gif] I was only able to find three examples. Perhaps there are no more? Don
My interest was prompted by the lettering for GOOD MORNING VIETNAM discussed in http://typophile.com/node/118657 Includes discussion of Map Type. Don
