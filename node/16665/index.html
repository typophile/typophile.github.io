2005-12-07 20:26:52 • our_turn

Many of the “CG” designated designs had a bad rap during the eighties and not all of them were purely “look-alikes”.

There were some that we used original source specimens and developed “revivals” of metal cuts. In fact, during the late eighties, René Kerfante (Monotype Managing Director of the Typography Division) mentioned to me that in his opinion, CG’s first release of Schneidler was one of the better versions.

This is only mentioned here in passing as an historic reference to “CG” and not intended to open any discussion regarding clones, revivals and historic type pirating. Let’s leave that stuff for another thread and time. :-)
