The more quirky fonts were probably hand drawn, as John suggests, but there are quite a few fonts out there that are quite similar. Here are a few suggestions from House Industries that I use (www.houseind.com)

Coop Condensed / Ed Interlock / Las Vegas Jackpot / Shag Exotica or Lounge

You might also try Font Diner (www.fontdiner.com) or Ray Larabie’s fonts (www.typodermic.com)
