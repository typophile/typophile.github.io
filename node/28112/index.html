In the Spring of 2007, Indie Fonts 3 will be released as a complementary volume to the previous 2.

The 20 foundries included in this volume are: [[Baseline Fonts]] [[Blue Vinyl Fonts]] [[Device Fonts]] [[E-phemera Fonts]] [[Font Bros|Font Brothers]] [[Handselecta]] [[Jeremy Tankard Typography]] [[Kenn Munk]] [[Letterhead Fonts]] [[Pizzadude]] [[Positype]] [[Process Type Foundry]] [[Rimmer Type Foundry]] [[Shinntype]] [[SparkyType]] [[Suitcase Type|Suitcase Type Foundry]] [[Suomi Type Foundry]] [[Village]] [[Virus Fonts]] [[Wilton Foundry]]

More info coming soon

Two versions of Indie fonts 3 will be released in early 2007. A Flex bound soft cover at $35 retail - available through usual book outlets via a major publisher. & A Hardbound version at $40 Retail - available only through the foundries involved and original series publisher P-Type Publications (P22)
