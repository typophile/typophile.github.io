Free Digital DIN

February 1, 2009 • Thomas Levine

I’m looking for a free digital version of DIN. There must be some free digital version of DIN, but the ones I found in quick searches all seem to be illegal copies of proprietary versions.

The price isn’t so much of an issue as the rights: I may want to be able to distribute it to many other people, but I definitely want to put it on a server that would generate images automatically, and it’s not unlikely that some situation would come up where certain aspects of most licensing agreements would be problematic.

I don’t have to use DIN exactly, but it would work quite well stylistically.

Where may I find a digital font that suits my needs?

First of all ‘free’ does not necessarily = free to redistribute.

Secondly, generating images on a server may not actually be against most EULAs.

Ideally, you’d be able to find an open source typeface, but barring that, I don’t think you’re out of luck…you just need to read through some of the licenses.
