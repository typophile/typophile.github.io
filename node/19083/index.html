Bank Gothic is a 1930s Morris Benton design, so FB Agency, a revival of Benton's square text face from the same time, would complement (not compliment) the old-fashioned look.
2006-04-07 17:24:02 • Nick Shinn
Agency wouldn't work for text though. To compliment Bank Gothic I might consider a particularly rounded font, such as Gotham, Avenir or Futura. I think to use a different squarish font - and Eurostile is another of course - might fight with the Bank Gothic and be difficult to read for running text. You could then use Bank Gothic or Agency for the headlines.
