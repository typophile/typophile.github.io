Hi, Thanks for the feedback! I didn't care that much about the meaning of the name, to be honest. I just felt the need of a font name started by the letter H :-) Regarding the name itself, it reminded me some poetry (Poe, Byron, Baudelaire) that I used to read, like the "Don Juan in Hades", and that's it! I never wanted the font to be connected to some sort of evil stuff. I think it's a pleasant font, inspired by the lettering in a beer house that I saw in Malmo in 2007. It was made just for fun, no profit in the horizon! All the best to my fellow typophiles. Have a wonderful 2013! Dino
