Quirky display on DTL Prokyon

September 26, 2007 • writingdesigning

The capital 'E's on DTL Prokyon seem to appear shorter than other letters on screen displays (View attachments). Does anybody have any idea why this happens? Any suggestions to avoid this quirk on an otherwise superb typeface?
It's a problem with the hinting. If you're making bitmap files, the answer is simple: just work larger and scale down. Play around with different screen magnification -- with the exception of fonts which have been painstakingly hinted size-by-size for screen (and there are very few of these) most fonts have non-size-specific hinting instructions which fall victim to glitches at certain sizes.
