Defiant Battle Type, More Progress

March 7, 2009 • Randy

Coming down the home stretch on the Moped Script. For the sake of getting it out the door, I'm going to release this, then possibly other weights or versions. It features the straight and curl versions, contextual alternates for appropriate joins, and language support covering latin for Europe. Specifically: Afrikaans, Basque, Breton, Catalan, Danish, Dutch, English, Finnish, French, Gaelic, German, Icelandic, Indonesian, Irish, Italian, Norwegian, Portuguese, Sami, Spanish, Swahili, Swedish, Croatian, Czech, Estonian, Hungarian, Latvian, Lithuanian, Polish, Romanian, Serbian, Slovak, Slovenian, and Turkish.
