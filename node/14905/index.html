I’d use Goudy Oldstyle or another of Goudy’s faces. His formative years were spent in Chicago, he comes from the mid-west, and his typefaces have the warmth and unpretentiousness that I associate postively with the midwest (I come from there). Goudy Oldstyle will have a reassuring familiary with the generous and caring overtone.
