It seems familiar. I remember someone popular at dA was making a similar looking font. (the red type) http://sbert-mgd.com.ar/

To me this looks like it was originally House Chalet New York.

S = 1960 or 1980 b = 1970 e = 1960 or 1980 r = ’t' from 1970 with top cut off t = 1970

“MOTION GRAPHIC DESIGN” looks to be Chalet New York 1960 as well (the ‘C’ in “GRAPHIC” looks too round to be Helvetica).
