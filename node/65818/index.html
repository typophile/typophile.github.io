Margaret Calvert on BBC TV this Sunday

January 2, 2010 • Richard Hards

For those who can catch it, Margaret Calvert is to be on BBC2 “Top Gear” on Sunday at 20:00 GMT

From the programme web page James May drives the new Vauxhall Insignia VXR, accompanied by Margaret Calvert, the designer of Britain’s road signs.

Being Top Gear, I doubt it will be particularly highbrow, but at least she is appearing with the sensible one.

It wasn’t particularly highbrow, she clearly disapproved of his driving and he ran out of sensible questions. I enjoyed the thought of a half dozen people designing the look for British roadside furniture and will be using markers to improve every men at work sign.
