so, this project was called “ekphrasis”. we, the students, had to produce ekphrastic [graphic description of a visual work of art] representation of a chosen site of a New Orleans neighborhood. For one of the two compositions, we had to transform our visual experience into a textual experience.

the ekphrasis that i have posted is my visual ekphrasis of the house of blues. i took a picture of it, pen tooled it in illustrator, printed it out, put tracing paper on it, hand lettered words, scanned in the tracing paper, pen tooled the words, then combined them. the result is by far my favorite project that i have ever created–and i thought i’d like to share it with all of you! its attatched! enjoy!
