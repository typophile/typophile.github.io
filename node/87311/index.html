FLF fonts are adaptations of existing fonts with some minor changes to avoid straight copying. To me this font looks like a slight modification of URW Stymie. The serif on the middle arm of the E is gone, but offhand the rest looks the same to me. The G has the beard, the Q has the same tail, and the R leg is fairly close to the stem just like the posted sample. Stymie's serifs are shorter than Rockwell's, but in the U.C. letters are quite similar except for the letters I mentioned. - - Mike Yanega
