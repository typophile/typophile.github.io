Ann: FontLab Studio 5.0.4 released

July 10, 2007 • twardoch

Fontlab Ltd. has just released FontLab Studio 5.0.4, a bugfix release available to all licensed users of FontLab Studio 5.

Version 5.0.4 is free to licensed users of FontLab Studio 5.

To obtain the free update, browse to our updates page and fill out the update request form. Please allow 1-2 business days for processing.

A critical kerning bug introduced in the recently released 5.0.3 build has been fixed. In addition, numerous other improvements have been made.

A list of changes made in version 5.0.4 is available at: http://www.fontlab.com/news/announcements/fontlab-studio-5.0.4/

In addition, a list of changes made in previously released version 5.0.3 is available at: http://www.fontlab.com/news/announcements/fontlab-studio-5.0.3/

I recommend all users of previous builds of FontLab Studio 5 to update to version 5.0.4. This can be done by requesting a free update at: http://www.fontlab.com/upgrades/

Regards, Adam Twardoch Fontlab Ltd.
