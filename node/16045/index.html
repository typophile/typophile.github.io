2005-11-04 21:29:51 • chronicle

FontLab Studio 5 for Mac is under serious development and is currently in beta test. I can assure everybody that the development cycle will take much less than that of FontLab Studio 5 for Windows. During the development of the Windows version we have been actively planning and adding new features and basically shaping the application. The functionality of Studio 5 is ready with the Windows version and our developers now are working hard on getting the same functionality to the Mac version. So the testing that is currently under way is about “does everything work as planned” and not about “what could we add here”.

Indeed, our plan is to release FontLab Studio 5 for Mac in December.

Regards, Adam
