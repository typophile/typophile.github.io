2008-07-08 13:01:45 • Lantz

Bringing back nightmares =) I design ads for a newspaper where I live and I have to go through these all the time. This is suppose to be a sign? Where is it going to be hung? Is it suppose to be one of those signs that hangs in front of a house they are selling? If so…you need to think about what is more important to the driver/walker by.

Right now, it looks like it should go in a newspaper as an ad. It’s basically saying hey are you moving? then call here, and then you finally get to the bottom where you see who you will be calling.

If you are doing a sign in front of a house on the market, then people want to know that it’s for sale first, then who’s selling it, then where to call. Everything else should take a back seat.

But for the record, the ads that I do are mostly for small/private business' that want to have an ad in the newspaper to get extra money. So those ads that you posted as the “stay away from” ads actually prove to be very successful in getting business, because they are simple and get across the point almost instantly. “Form follows Function”.
