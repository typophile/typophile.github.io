Newfoundland and Labrador - new branding

October 5, 2006 • dmajor

Recently the government of the Canadian province in which I live, Newfoundland and Labrador, came out with a new million-dollar branding scheme. The new logo features our provincial flower, the pitcher plant, and what I consider to be a debaucherous bit of type design which is, I guess, supposed to emulate the pitcher plant. I keep picturing the three flowers as space weapons poised to zap the repugnant f into oblivion. What does everyone think?
