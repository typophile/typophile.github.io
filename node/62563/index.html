Like the music industry, or perhaps like any art-related industry, technology has increased the volume in the market. Alas the producers output likely outpaces the consumers ability to consume it so there’s an overall lessening of the value of individual units. Granted, it’s also cheaper to produce the units.

Copying of IP law protected material has always been around, always will be.
