Hope to see you at TypeCon!

July 30, 2007 • Phan

Hi, folks. Some of you may remember me as a regular poster on the Typo-L and T-D lists, long before Typophile became the preferred internet forum for typographic discussion.

I disappeared several years ago. What happened was this huge slab serif fell on my head and I was in a comma for several years. When I finally came to, my memory was intact, except for all things related to typography, which I completely forgot.

However, I recently learned that TypeCon would be happening this year in Seattle. (The last TypeCon I had attended was the first one.)

Suddenly it all came rushing back to me.

Typography…of course! So that’s why I have so much trouble reading poorly typeset pages! And all those boxes of weird booklets – those are type specimens! And the portrait in the altar of my living room – that must be Bram de Does!

Anyway, since I live near Seattle, I figured I would attend TypeCon and try to reacquaint myself with the typographic scene.

I recognize a lot of personalities on these forums, and I hope to see many of you at TypeCon!

-Phan Nguyen
