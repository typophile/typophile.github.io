2007-10-16 22:26:09 • KenBessie
A Summary of Events: Steve showed up (hi Steve). New guy Jordan showed up as well. Lex rescheduled his movie project and made it out. Chuck and his students didn't arrive, but we still had "all the fun" even though the mini-van wasn't needed. Tamye was there in spirit. Veer was absent. After three months of discussion, we've decided not to give ourselves a name. Rather, we'll change names and logos on a rotating basis. We could be "Calgary Typos" one month, "Calgary Typostammtisch" another month, or "Calgary Typographic Society" some other time. We sort of like the C T S letter combination and we don't want to be tied down with details. Janine, our "idea-girl", has proposed our first project. (Janine is a virtual fountain of ideas.) We're all to take photos of type found within a city block in Calgary (maybe the block around our house or the city block where we work). Then we'll assemble all these and post them. It'll be wonderful and might evolve into a gallery show. Then maybe we'll come to your city and photograph your type. Just wait. The Show & Tell is still very popular. Linda and Derek contributed pieces (which had to be hastily removed from the table when the food arrived). (As an aside, the Kensington Pub offers a choice of two side dishes with each entree: don't order potatoes and fries together, you'll never finish them!!) It was a lovely evening of relaxed type-geek conversation.
