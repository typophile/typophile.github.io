I'm designing a 250-or-so page Hebrew book. It's my first, and as I'm not used to reading long stretches of Hebrew text, I'm wondering which font to use for the main body copy. Who is your intended audience of readers? Do you need nikkud alignment? Contact www.fontworld.com. They have a superior version of FrankRuhl. Also, Scott Keofsky's Milon has many advantages over FontBit's New Hadasa. Who is doing the typesetting? Only modern texts, like those used in Israel, use Narkiss for the body text. If your audience is the Mishna Brura crowd, Narkiss is not a good choice.
