Reminds me of the way lettering artists like the late Bob Alonso adapted classic fonts into something a bit less formal. His [[http://new.myfonts.com/fonts/bagraphics/wonka/?testdrive=custom%3D0%26dock%3Dfalse%26size%3D124%26w%3D600%26src%3Dcustom%26text%3DTHE%2520OZONE%2520DILEMMA%26fg%3D000000%26bg%3Dffffff%26goodies%3Dfit%252Csingle%26seed%3D24|Wonka]] doesn’t match this, but it shows a similar curvature that adds dynamic energy to the lettering.

There is the same sort of quality to this lettering in your sample, and it makes me think we are looking for a display font, and not a weight of a classic serif family.
