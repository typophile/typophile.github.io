June 7, 2006 • alfabet

I am planning to do a type & graphic design e-newsletter, distributed by e-mail to subscribers. The content will be :
  • type tips and tricks
  • tidbits from the type & design world

I already have an e-newsletter called alfaBITS geared towards the general business audience with tips on marketing, managing, etc. The winning entry will receive a collection of Alphabet Design fonts worth $100.
