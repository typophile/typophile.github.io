I wasn’t suggesting using Times, as I have no idea what your layouts are like. In the AG magazine, Lubalin’s heads were all caps and squared off–justified columns of Times text went well with that. It’s interesting to note that another famous art director/type designer, Neville Brody, used Times as the text face of The Face, again matching justified text columns with squared off heads (in Industria). Both art directors felt it necessary to become type designers as there were no faces available to fully satisfy the strict geometry of the layouts they wanted to do.
