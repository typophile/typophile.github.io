Patty, Eben, Lore, etc:

TECH TIP

To close crop your avatar on the grey Typophile background:

  1. Duplicate layer in Photoshop
  2. In top layer, use magic wand to select unwanted area. Delete this selection (its absence shows as “checkerboard” in the layers panel mini-view.)
  3. Delete Background layer.

Save as PNG.
