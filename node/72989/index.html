Dude, shouting won’t get you anywhere. Chances are that people (like me) have tried to find a match for your request and have drawn a blank.

The closest I’ve got are the following freebies:

Staubach by AbdulMakesFonts (http://www.fontspace.com/abdulmakesfonts/staubach); the centre bar on the E is a close match, but the S is wrong - the inside of the loops slope as they arrive at the serif. The K is incorrect also.

Academic M54 by justme54s (http://www.fontspace.com/justme54s/academic-m54); the weight is better on this one, but the center-bar of the E is too long, and while the top serif on the S is good, there is no bottom serif. The K is good. The point where the strokes meet on the 7 is cut at an angle, rather than vertical.
