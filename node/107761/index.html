Don’t know the precise font, but I can give you a font with the extended diagonals on the M – which was a lettering feature by Ozwald Cooper and others circa 1920. Packard New Style: http://www.identifont.com/show?2DOR which is a smoothed version of Cooper’s original Packard: http://www.identifont.com/show?2DOQ Also Packard Patrician NF: http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/nicksfonts/packard-patrician-nf/ Don
