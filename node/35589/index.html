2007-08-01 03:15:26 • Michel Boyer

Your problems may also come from the options when you generate the font (but I would remove all kerning pairs anyway). For instance I just downloaded the [public domain]* icon font marvosym.ttf (Für Puristen) and installed it on my mac. TextEdit would’nt even see the font. So I uninstaled it, opened marvosym.ttf with fontforge, clicked “File > Generate fonts”, then clicked “Options” and unchecked “Apple”, “Opentype” and “Old style kern” and then generated a new ttf font that I installed; everything seems to work nicely (always better to check the file with “Font Book” before installing, of course).

[edit] * I meant Freeware; here is the copyright: Martin Vogel’s Symbols are freeware - look for information and new versions at http://www.marvosym.com - Neue Versionen und Informationen finden Sie auf http://www.marvosym.de
