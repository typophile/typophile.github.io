Attention all musicians attending TypeCon2008 Buffalo!

May 8, 2008 • Bald Condensed

Hi all,

I once had this crazy plan of assembling an international, all-star typejam band that would perform on TypeCon. As it turns out this could very well happen in Buffalo. That’s why I’d need as soon as possible a complete list of people attending this year’s TypeCon who play (a) musical instrument(s).

Could you please send me an e-maill with:

(a) your name (b) which instrument(s) you master (c) which is your preferred style of music (for playing, might be different from what music you like to listen to!) (d) your proficiency for each instrument, ranging from promising amateur over semi-professional to seasoned pro (I’m really sorry, but beginners can’t apply) (e) if you have experience with playing in bands (very important!)

We will try to cook up a set list, which every musician will have to rehearse autonomously. Please keep in mind this will be an experiment, so I really don’t know where this will end.

Thanks everyone for your co-operation.

How many Type designers/Type people are musicians?

I know Ed Benguait was a jazz drummer and Jim Rimmer played a mean coronet. The House (house) band and [[http://www.fontshop.de/typo2007/index.php/Wolfraam;89/3|Wolfraam]] performed at [[http://www.fontshop.de/typo2007/index.php/1/3|TypoBerlin]] last year. Is there a connection between music & type? A similar predisposition?
