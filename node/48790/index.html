Does anyone know what font is used on Oprah’s magazine, i.e. the “O” as well as the caption beneath of “THE OPRAH MAGAZINE”? What would you suggest as the most similar fonts to hers that are available in Word? Thanks for your help![img:sites/default/files/old-images/OprahMagazine_5026.jpg]
The cover typefaces look like Vectora by Adrian Frutiger. Closest match in Word app would be Franklin Gothic. The O is some kind of Bodoni. Closest match for THE OPRAH MAGAZINE in Word would be Gill Sans.
