I just scanned my copy of the MIT Bauhaus book. Here is a highly enlarged image of the lower right corner showing the “B”. See how buildup of ink in not a problem for 2 colors (200% coverage) but starts to lift on 3 colors (300% coverage). This only shows at extreme enlargement and with a bit of level tweaking in PhotoShop. When you see the book on the shelf, it was not noticeable to the untrained eye.
