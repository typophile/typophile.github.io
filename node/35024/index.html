Adobe CS3 release OpenType Fonts

July 11, 2007 • Scott Leyes

May not be the right forum, but…

Does anybody here know if the Opentype fonts Adobe is shipping with CS3 (Creative Suite 3) are updated/altered versions of their fonts? The files' modification dates show March 2007, where my older Adobe files show 2003. I don’t want to replace my existing files, but at the same time, don’t want to have issues with older fonts.

I notice that some faces are “new” additions, while some have been dropped; Adobe Garamond Pro, for example, is included on the disk, but only in four weights (regular/italic/bold/bolditalic), while their earlier release (which I think came with CS2) includes the semibold/semibolditalic weights. Nueva Std, on the other hand, includes four new weights: boldcondensed, boldcondensedoblique, condensed, and condensedoblique. And they’ve thrown in venerable fonts like Prestige Elite – I’m hoping the metrics haven’t gone all wonky and will screw up older docs (though I’ll leave the PostScript version on my system just in case).

I’ve long since developed the habit of just updating the fonts. I’ve never had any re-flow issues … if that is what you mean.
