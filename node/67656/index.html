As Simon says - too many to list - so just some random picks: [[http://klim.co.nz/|Kris Sowersby]] (National), [[http://new.myfonts.com/search/scaglione/people/?view=list|Scaglione & Burian]], and many of the designers at [[http://www.ourtype.be/|OurType]] including Smeijers (Ludwig and many other superb designs), Thiemich (Alto). Dan Reynolds' [[http://www.linotype.com/5831/malabar.html|Malabar]]. Some of the work of [[http://www.felicianotypefoundry.com/main/|Feliciano Type Foundry]] (Merlo, Rongel, Stella). [[http://www.linotype.com/793/adrianfrutigertraces.html|Adrian Frutiger]] has done great work his entire career - more recently, new releases of classics like Univers Next and produced new designs such as Frutiger Serif. [[http://www.gerardunger.com/|Gerard Unger]] is notable (Swift).

For broader surveys see [[http://typographica.org/2009/features/our-favorite-typefaces-of-2008/|Typographica’s Favourites of 2008]], and [[http://tdc.org/tdc/tdc2-2009-winners|TDC Best of 2009]] and [[http://www.tdc.org/news/2008Results/|2008]] - which gives you an idea of who’s who, out there.
