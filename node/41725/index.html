Texas License Plate e-vote

February 5, 2008 • DrDoc

This week, the Texas DOT is having an e-vote for our new license plate design. There are five designs, including the current plate. As of this morning the vote totals are as follows: "Lone Star Texas"[img:sites/default/files/old-images/plate_sky_mountains_6076.jpg]39,165 "Natural Texas"[img:sites/default/files/old-images/plate_bluebonnets_6248.jpg]24,759 "Traditional Texas"[img:sites/default/files/old-images/plate_traditional_5056.jpg]18,935 "New Texas"[img:sites/default/files/old-images/plate_skyline_5640.jpg]12,285 "My Texas" (current design)[img:sites/default/files/old-images/plate_my_texas_6127.jpg]9.283 I feel like each one has some detail that bothers me way too much. The red text on the blue background of the current design looks a bit off (though it looks better when printed on metal). "The Lone Star State" on the "Lone Star Texas" design is written in brush script, and "The" is written smaller than "Lone Star State," which throws me off. The "Natural Texas" design is the most passable in my opinion, but that stretched "The Lone Star State" at the bottom looks a bit off. The "Traditional Texas" design just screams "Look at me! I know how to use Photoshop!" I feel like the "New Texas" design would be a lot more effective with a bold sans serif at the top — it would make the design look a lot more clean and modern. Here's the site for voting: http://apps.dot.state.tx.us/services/vehicle_titles_and_registration/vote_plate.htm What do you think?
