Qantas Frequent Flyer Typeface

January 2, 2014 • RBM

Hi, The Q symbol has probably been customised from the main typeface.

![](/images/old-images/Qantas Frequent Flyer_5542.jpg)

Knowing Qantas, this is likely to be a custom font. It is a very close match for [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/linotype/alternate-gothic-lt/|Alternate Gothic No. 3]] in terms of character shapes, widths etc. — though Alternate Gothic is quite heavy and doesn’t have the straight tail on the /Q/.

Qantas have rebranded recently. You may be interested in seeing their current corporate style book, [[http://www.qantas.com.au/guidelines/resources/Qantas_Masterbrand_Guidelines_June_2013.pdf|available here]].
