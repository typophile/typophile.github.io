Distinctive font for sports nutrition logotype?

September 11, 2009 • gcaplan

Hi folks We're piloting a new sports nutrition range. The product is innovative and technical and we're aiming to appeal to serious athletes rather than macho muscle-heads. So the image we're after is clean, scientific, upmarket and dynamic/masculine. The brand is quite design led - we're aiming to be the "Apple" of our marketplace. We're looking for a font that's cutting-edge and distinctive, but legible at relatively small sizes for labelling. To get the dynamic feel, we've mainly been focusing on Italic Slab Serif, but perhaps we're on the wrong track? We're really struggling to find anything suitable. We've done a lot of digging around on the mainstream font sites, so we're hoping you guys might know of something we might have overlooked - perhaps something in a small font-house or the public domain? Any help with this would be much appreciated!
PS The range has a Russian provenance, so something with a subtle hint of Cryllic might offer an alternative route...
