TYPE DESIGN INTERNATIONAL STUDENT COMPETITION “MILOSZ 2011”

2011 has been designated as the Year of [[http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Czesław_Miłosz|Czesław Miłosz]], and as such it will abound in momentous events making literature an everyday experience and enhancing the status of culture in public sphere. The Year will celebrate the book itself – the word and its setting.

The ZNACZY SIĘ New Art Foundation announces the International Student Competition for Typeface ‘Milosz’.

We hope the competition will highlight the importance of the written word and the craft of typeface design.
