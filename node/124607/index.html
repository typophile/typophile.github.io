ITC Kabel pairing

April 16, 2015 • Sergesolo

Hello, I would like to use ITC Kabel as a headline typeface for a book design. Could anyone please recommend a pairing for the body text? I am open to using both serifs and sans serifs. Thank you very much in advance! Vincent

The first US edition of George Orwell’s Animal Farm paired Kabel with Electra (to good effect, I think): http://fontsinuse.com/uses/4237/animal-farm-by-george-orwell-1st-us-edition http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/linotype/electra/

Some other suggestion for serifs in a similar vein: http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/linotype/fairfield/ http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/type-together/abril/ http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/linotype/new-caledonia/

I’ve also seen Kabel paired with Franklin Gothic more than once, but in those instances Kabel was the text face, and Franklin Gothic the headline: http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/linotype/franklin-gothic/

I’m sure other posters can recommend more….
