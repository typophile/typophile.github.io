What is the best font for a flooded basement?

June 27, 2006 • dezcom

My family and I have just returned home from a 4 day trip to Cincinati. We had a horrid drive through pouring rain. I just checked my basement (which has never been wet in 20 years) and found it flooded. It is still pouring buckets out and is supposed to rain for the next few days here. In an effort to take my mind off the frustration and damaged goods, I thought I would pose a typical Typophile question. What is the best face for a flooded basement. It can't have small caps because they would drown. No delicate wispy fonts either. Perhaps something along the lines of "Sandbag" or "Mould-in-the Making". Any thoughts? ChrisL
