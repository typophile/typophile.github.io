2009-09-30 23:07:37 • phrostbyte64
The "popular" designers and font lists are generated by sales so it is kind of a self-fulfilling prophesy thing. The tough part is cracking in to the lists with lots of sales. Once you are in, it becomes easier because there are more people looking at your work. Many of the things on MyFonts popular lists appeal to font buying groups like scrap-bookers. They love clipart looking stuff and frames and borders--also, cartoony and folksy hand written looking stuff. To me, much of this sort of thing looks unappealing but I am not in that demographic. Remember that sales success does not equal quality design. It means finding a market niche and feeding it what it wants. As a designer, you have to decide if you are more interested in sales volume than fine quality work. If you are a combination of very good and good at marketing, with a bit of luck, you might get both acclaim and wealth. ChrisL
