"Warning! Repent or burn!" Distinctive sign - enjoy!

July 22, 2009 • dinazina

I spotted these signs at Seattle’s Folklife Festival, a much-loved annual event drawing 100 thousand or so Northwesterners. Participants are gentle flute-playing, drum-circling, banjo-strumming, dope-toking hippie artists and performers of all generations.

The perfect place for a True Believer warning of eternal damnation! Look how dynamic the typographic design and color on these. One is professionally printed, the other hand-stenciled.

The sign he’s carrying isn’t enough, he also wears a sandwich board. This guy was dogged by the guitar-playing folkie behind him.

I love how “rebellious wives and kids” are destined for hellfire along with murderers, thieves and sodomites. Enjoy this example of true folk art!

It appears that the “Hatefull [sic] and Unforgiving” will also be hell-bound. Pity for the sign carrier, as he hardly sounds full of forgiveness.
