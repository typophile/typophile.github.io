Most of that, including the first R, matches Kanzlei Bold, stretched vertically a bit. The two digital versions I’m aware of are Dieter Steffmanns Fette Kanzlei and Paul Lloyd’s Plain Germanica (listed as Germanica).

The top of the k and l, as well as the tail swashes on the C and second R will require custom work.
