I was thinking that this looks an awful lot like some of the photolettering (film type) scripts that (for example) ended up in some of the Solotype Y- and Z-series scripts. I have hunted through several books of scripts and my 3 Photo-Lettering catalogs, and this seems closest to some of the film type scripts. There are a couple with the low connection in the 'o', but I could find none that showed the same T. Part of the problem is that the Photo-Lettering samples don't show full alphabets. Certainly it could be hand-drawn, by an accomplished lettering artist, like Benguiat, Bartuska or Davison, to name some of the ones who did the Photo-Lettering scripts, but this is not the work of a 'weekend calligrapher', in my opinion. Jason Walcott (Jukebox Type at Veer) has digitized some of these script styles, and that might be a place to look for something similar in digital form.
