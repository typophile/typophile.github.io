Characters Fonts Foundry launches RSS feed

January 5, 2006 • Rene Verkaart

Characters Font Foundry has made an own RSS feed available for anyone who wants to be informed. A lot of things will change this year and a lot of fonts will be released. Very soon 10 of my fonts will be sold through Veer. The complete Nordic Narrow family will be available in the next weeks at Fountain.

Here’s the feed address to subscribe to: http://www.characters.nl/rss/rss.xml

With this feed I will keep you informed on new releases, interesting newsfacts, updates and much more.
