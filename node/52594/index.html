It reminds me of the type reviews in the now-defunct Critique magazine. Type reviews like this still show up almost monthly in Print and Step; Paul Shaw writes the Print reviews with a degree of depth seen nowhere else. Occasionally a typeface critique/feature pops up in Metropolis. The Metropolis reviews tend to be about typefaces that would interest architects and city planners, ie. Vialog, and are written to that audience, so they can be more thought provoking than crits aimed at print/web designers.
