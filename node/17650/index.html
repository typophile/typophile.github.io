Thank you for the posting Paul. And thank you for the questions SI? Opentype: Yes. We plan on releasing all of our fonts as Opentype Pro versions eventually, with contextual alternates. We've been focusing on trying to develop glyphs that work on their own, as I believe that the lack of regulating characters is the reason why most previous graffiti-based typefaces have not been successful. Thats the long answer though, and a bit of an excuse. I either need to to (a) learn some simple opentype code or (b) find someone else to do it. But it is a must to have it done eventually. As for the sparse EULA: thank you for drawing my attention to that broken link. You can find our EULA here: http://handselecta.com/fonts_eula.html Basically you can modify for your own purposes but cannot rename, redistribute, sell or give away the font you modified. The license is the same and copyright remains with us. But if you do any modification, please let me know. I'd love to see what you've got in mind. Hope this helps. If you have any more specific questions drop me a line. Best, Christian
