Trebuchet isn’t the most readable web font – its quirks are distracting at screen resolution. The best 5 are Lucida Grande (Lucida Sans Unicode on Windows), Georgia, Verdana, Arial, Helvetica. Give them all a try in Maratz’s Typetester.
