2004-08-07 05:18:12 • negativespace
The lower case looks exactly like Spumoni, and the upper case looks like Spumoni with added/modified serifs. I agree with Mark.

For comparison, there are three other commercial fonts that have a strong stylistic resemblance to Spumoni (none of them match the samples):
Elroy by Christian Schwartz;
Heatwave by Panache Graphics;
and Sniff by Joan Barjau of Type-O-Tones (sample follows). Sniff has the most 'embellishment', but has the same sort of casual feel that I get from Spumoni.

The Nick Curtis font is all caps and is based (apparently) on Goudy Stout, which it resembles, with added bounce.
