June 1, 2011 • pigeoninpjs

Dear all,

I am dead scared as it is the very first time I post my own work on here but here we go… I am designing a logo for a small record label and thought here would be the best place to get expert opinions. The labels releases mainly nu-folk bands. So I thought an organic vintage script would a good choice. I would be so grateful if you could point me in the right direction for improvements. I am no logo designer therefore any help would be greatly appreciated.

Kind regards

PJ

Incorporating the type in the safety pin seems forced, not only because the type doesn’t look anything like bent metal – even if it did, bending a safety pin even once is a pain in the ass. Nevertheless, it’s a strong symbol with a distinct skeleton. I would look for type that reflected it’s shape somehow. Everyone can’t be an expert in all matters, so don’t be afraid of utilizing the expertise of a type savvy designer.
