New video is up: Top Ten Things Everyone Should Know About Fonts

November 27, 2012 • Jill Bell

This great little video, [[http://vimeo.com/54127183|The Top Ten Things Everyone Should Know About Fonts]], from type designer and lettering artist Jill Bell, has ten tips to help you get to know, love and use fonts better.

The video was revised and abridged from a presentation Bell gave at regional AAF Conference. [[http://www.jillbell.com|Bell]] has designed fonts for ITC, Monotype and Adobe as well as numerous proprietary fonts for companies. She has judged Linotype and the Type Director’s Club’s font design contests, and spoken worldwide including at many type conferences.
