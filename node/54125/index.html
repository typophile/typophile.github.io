I didn’t read them as birds to begin with not till I saw the comment. I don’t think that the top logo with the huge k e r n i n g is that effective. You have high kerning in everything. Tighten up the letters in nicole brown. Also, I don’t know if I would go with both of those n’s. I like one because it is unique two makes it look repetitive and they are not quite the same so its a little weird.
