Suggestions for Typeface for Photos

March 12, 2008 • Tommasino

I am new to this forum (but not to Typophile).
I am looking for suggestions on a typeface to put under photographs to be displayed at an exhibit.

The images (nature/landscape) are approximately 13x19 and wanted to add a few words on where/when the picture was taken (e.g. Yellowstone, January 2008).

I was looking for a script/calligraphy fontface that resembles a handwriting style; something like John Handy from LinoType ( I find John Handy a bit too hard.) http://www.linotype.com/886/johnhandyscript-family.html

Can anyone share any suggestions as to suitable types? Thanks

Just a thought, but I would consider something very neutral that could be read from a distance. Consider the job the type needs to do. You want people to get the info and not have to crowd or get too close, and you don’t want to make the labels large to compensate for a difficult to read script.

If you want some warmth go with Myriad.
