If this is a font rather than lettering I hope someone finds it for you. Whether font or lettering, the basic structure is similar to Bodoni Extra Wide Bold [img:sites/default/files/old-images/URWBodoniExtraWideBoldAsForma_3992.gif][img:sites/default/files/old-images/URWBodoniExtraWideBoldAsDomus_4828.gif] https://www.myfonts.com/fonts/urw/urw-bodoni/ The cross bar on the F and the top stroke and terminal on S are an old idea using a tilde [aka flame or cyma] stroke. Compare to a font from 1907 called Lining Anglo [img:sites/default/files/old-images/LiningAngloBBS1907SM_6547.jpg] Here the tilde is at the bottom not the top of S but it’s the same stroke. Don
