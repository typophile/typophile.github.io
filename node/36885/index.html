Being unfamiliar with the PC platform, what is the usual way to access accented characters?

The “usual” way for English speakers is to use the ALT key and the numeric keypad: ALT+0193 = Á through ALT+255 = ÿ. However, it’s likely that your Spanish-language clients are using a Spanish keyboard, and have direct keyboard access to the accented characters. Mousing over the various keys on this interactive layout will give you the Unicode desginations for the letters; just make sure in FontLab that your accented characters' Unicode match the layout numbers.
