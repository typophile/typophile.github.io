You can autofill cells in excel with consecutive numbers by selecting a column of cells and from the Edit menu choose, Fill / Series… Be sure that the first cell in your column has your starting number in it (whether it’s 1 or 212 doesn’t matter), excel will fill the remaining cells in a series from there.

You will probably want your images to coincide with those numbers and for that I think this video may help…
