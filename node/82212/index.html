Yes, Mike is right.

Tiemann Mediaeval was designed by Walter Tiemann. Klingspor published the Regular in 1909, with a Halbfett (Bold) following in 1911, and a Kursiv in 1912. Gerhard Helzels’s digitization comprises 5 fonts: all 3 styles based on the 72pt cut, plus the Regular and Italic based on ca. 10pt. Helzel’s Edition Romana Hamburg has one of the more eccentric website designs.
