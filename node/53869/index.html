Okay, well my book isn’t exactly a new discovery. I decided to post a book that I purchased based solely on the cover. I do that more than I probably should. It turned out to be a really interesting memoir. When I first spotted the book in B&N I was drawn to the overall vintage quality of the cover. I love the way the designer incorporated the the “paperclip” with the photo, and the hand drawn doodles on the cover. The inside flaps are made to look like notebook paper. I did not know it when I purchased the book, but I believe the title and author text is Mrs. Eaves. I used Mrs. Eaves with my final type project last semester and really love it.
