I failed to find a match for the faux Arabic used for the cover of a Hoover Institution Press Publication in 2011 titled Jihad in the Arabian Sea by Camille Pecastaing [img:sites/default/files/old-images/FauxArabicJihadInArabianSea_6336.jpg] Some previous Faux Arabic threads: http://typophile.com/node/98828 http://typophile.com/node/83948 http://typophile.com/node/70459 http://typophile.com/node/29593 Some interesting suggestions, but I did not spot any that were a match for Jihad in the Arabian Sea The repeating letters in Jihad in the Arabian Sea and the author’s name look identical, but I appreciate that this does not itself guarantee that this is not lettering. I would love to see any suggestions you might have. Don
