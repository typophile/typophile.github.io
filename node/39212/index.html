The G is the hardest part to match, but if you made the G from a C with about the right closure, all you need to find is a sans with a low crossbar on the A. I thought of House’s Neutraface, but if you don’t want to make that kind of investment, you could work with Neuzeit Grotesk (Geometric 706) and use a small amount of added white stroke to thin the strokes like your sample.

This is assuming you only want to do a limited amount of lettering this way.
