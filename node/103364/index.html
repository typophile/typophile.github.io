I suppose you mean the “Infinity” script at bottom right? It is published as [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/grouptype/carpenter/|Carpenter Script]] by James West (GroupType, 1993-06), also published as [[http://www.findmyfont.com/index.php/fonts/font-preview?fset=Image-Club-Graphics&ffam=Carpenter%20ICG%20-%20Regular&fid=ed5f15f3d21a6471339c40baed679c07&fsize=60&text=Infinity&fit=1|Carpenter ICG]] by Image Club Graphics and digitized as Carpenter Script by Dan X. Solo. (1999, Dover Publications CD) It’s also available as OPTICarmella HandScript (Castcraft Software, Inc. 1990-1991) and cloned under different names by several well-known font forgery companies like NovelFonts, FontBank & SoftMaker.

I don’t know what “sweet paper” is, but some contrast & perspective correction might help somebody else to find out: [img:sites/default/files/old-images/sweet-paper-01_5594.jpg]
