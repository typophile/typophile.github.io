[[Indices]] : [[Typefaces]] : Myriad

A [[humanist]] [[sans serif]] design that was the product of the collaboration between [[Robert Slimbach]] and [[Carol Twombly]]with [[Fred Brady]] & [[Christopher Slye]]. Myriad was orignally released in [[1992]]. A prominent use of this typeface is by [[www.apple.com|Apple]] (it is actually Myriad Pro Semibold).   • This is actually Bitstream’s Humanist 777, which is based directly on the Frutiger typeface.
