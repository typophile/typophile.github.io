Edited: Looks like a version of Hermann Hoffmann’s Block of 1908 to 1936 for Berthold. Digital versions include Berthod’s Block https://www.myfonts.com/fonts/berthold/block-pro/ and Bitstream [condensed only] as Gothic 821 https://www.myfonts.com/fonts/bitstream/gothic-821/ Also a Paratype version as Bloc.
