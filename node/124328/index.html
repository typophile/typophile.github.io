Gotham appears to me only as a very good ready-to-go option but I don’t think it’s the one. I comes very close for all letters except /a but I assume it would be not so hard to customize it. A geometric sans like ITC [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/itc/avant-garde-gothic|Avant-Garde Gothic]] Demi-Bold may work for everything but /a (plus a slight edition on /P). /a is something completely different reminding me of the approach of [[http://www.myfonts.com/search/frutiger|Frutiger]]’s one. Something like [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/linotype/neue-frutiger|Neue Frutiger]] or [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/paratype/free-set|FreeSet]] could work. My best option here would probably be [[http://www.myfonts.com/search/avenir|Avenir]] which combines the geometry of AVG and a double storey /a close to Polaroid.
