for some reason, I haven’t been able to get on all day today (was typophile down or was it just me?)

Anyway… WOW! I had no idea there were so many options out there. Here’s the rundown (my opinion only)

  • Escrow - Not quite curly enough (old looking)
  • Precosia - Very nice, might be a bit hard to read at smaller sizes, lightbox material
  • American West - Close, but a bit too cliche western looking
  • Maverick's Luck - Close, add that one to the lightbox
  • Kilkenny - might be a bit too light
  • Missionary - I couldn't have asked for a more exact font for what I asked, but it's too thin, too
  • Calliope - getting really close here, add this one to the lightbox too
  • Eccentric - I didn't even think of something like that, could work
  • Berolina - Again, a direction I didn't even think of, I love this board!
  • Cafe Corina - VERY CLOSE! In fact, I plugged away in that site a bit, Billhead and Tyler are almost perfect. Added all three to the lightbox

Okay, I just got some further direction. The logo type will read “Neverthelesser” and it is to be a short animated film set in 1906 San Francisco on the day of the big quake. I think we have the typefaces here to work with, does anyone have any suggestions as to how to maybe mix & match them to make an interesting logotype for a short animated film?

I would love you thoughts and thank you so very much for these suggestions. Huh, education can be fun! I guess my teachers were right all along. Who’d a thought?
