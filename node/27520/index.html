The importance of an oblique.

August 8, 2006 • Goran Soderstrom

I am completing a display face and considering wheater it should have a slanted as italic or not. It's a typeface that more or less are kind of geometric so the "italic" _will_ be just a slanted version. I can imagine that it's very easy for the user of the font just to slant it in InDesign or such (since it will produce the same effect) and therefore I am considering not to produce the oblique version and leave that to the user. But on the other hand, many fonts have this as a version in the family, so maybe it's a good idea anyway. What are you thoughts on this?
You definitely need to make the oblique cut. If you just slant the letters, the bowls get messed up.
