October 4, 2009 • ReadyType™

New to Typophile. I’ll have to do some browsing & see myself around to find what’s good on this site.

THU OCT 1st, [[http://www.ReadyType.com|ReadyType.com]] was launched. I put up one font family, “Action™”. It consists of two fonts, Action-Regular & Action-Oblique. I’ve been sitting on it since 2001.

I recently decided to reprogram Action™ and use it as my first font release. I can’t remember why exactly. I had a few to chose from which were all different. I will release more fonts over the coming weeks. Tangent™ will be the next release. I posted the letter “e” on the site to see what kind of response people will have.

Aside from Typography, I’m a design director, & knee deep into a project at the moment. It’s very exciting yet time consuming. I don’t see myself taking a day off anytime in the near futue.
