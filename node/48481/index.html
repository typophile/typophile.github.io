If people are coming from afar explicitly for this service, then Baja's more important. However, if their customers are mostly people already traveling through the area then Kayak's more important. If you really felt the need to emphasize one word over the other, you could perhaps create two versions: a Kayak one for all signage and local advertising, and a Baja one for the company website and ads targeting international travelers.
