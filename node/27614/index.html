Where to find old specimen books?

August 11, 2006 • BradB

Yesterday I found the Type Specimen Pool on Flickr. I was pretty impressed by the old specimen books. But I have to ask, where does one find such books? Ebay? Flea markets? Somewhere else?

Some of them are rare to find at all. And others are expensive if you find them. But there are still chances to find some in well sorted antique book stores. Many of them are connected to the Internet. Here are two links:

http://www.antiqbook.com/books/speedsearch.php http://www.svaf.se/morris/

The later one is a great store just outside Stockholm, Sweden. He has a fabulous selection of books covering Typography and more. I have found several older type specimen there myself. I think there also might be a list of specimen books to look for here on Typophile?

Good luck
