2005-11-09 20:55:37 • sebsan
Hello Sebsan, Yes you can apply if you don't have a background in type design. The participants have a broad range of backgrounds and interests. Not so specifically type design, but typographic design and a few with no formal design background at all. There seems to be a big emphasis on content and giving appropriate form to the content – whether it be self generated or given At the moment my year are working on a 'reader' project which will eventuate in a book on the subject of biography Also the WT is open to commision work, and every so often something comes in. For example a student here has just completed a series of stamps for the TPG Post. As for the review process, it is quite informal here, but we have an evaluation in december – so then I can tell you more! Hope this helps, David
