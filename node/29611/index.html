It's worth paying for if you're going to use it, just like clothing. And, like clothing, there are bargains and there are extravagances. We all have the $50 jeans we wear nearly every day and the fancy stuff we trot out for parties once or twice a year. Generally you will get better value if you buy a large family (Adobe Garamond, for example) - various weights, itals, small caps, alternate figures, etc. Those families tend to be versatile and useful especially if you are just starting to build your font library and the "per style" (not sure that's the right term) price tends to be lower. However it can be well worth your while to buy a fancy one-off if you love it.
