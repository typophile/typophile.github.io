2 serif faces

March 5, 2009 • juhani

Dear typophiles, Here are two serif faces that came out of my calligraphy study with Edward Johnston’s writing manual. The first type is based on his “simple-written” method and the 2nd on the “gothic” variation of the “built-up” method.

These are intended to be used for continuous text (~8-12 pt) printed on paper.

All comments welcome.

I love them both. :)
