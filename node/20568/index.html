I believe you can do better than that, since the lower case is very close to a number of formal script styles. (I think the ‘O’ is not from a script face at all – probably an italic ‘O’.)

For the lower case, have a look at Shelley Andante (or Allegro, for that matter, since the l.c. is the same). Others could be Palace Script, or Embassy Script, but you would want to flatten and bold them a bit. Look for the un-looped ascenders. There are a number in the Solotype Catalog in the Z-families, so this may have come from a film type. Some were digitized.
