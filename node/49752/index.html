Colin, Regular [[http://www.myfonts.com/search?search%5Btext%5D=broadway|Broadway]] does not have the inline. The original [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/adobe/kabel/|Kabel]] dates back to the late 20s (not the ITC Kabel which was redrawn in the 70s and has a much higher x-height. Futura of course also goes back to that era, as does Bank Gothic, designed in the early 30s. Something closer in weight to Broadway would be [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/ef/radiant/|Radiant]], a great typeface designed in the late 30s. Good luck - have fun!
