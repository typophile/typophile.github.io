LeFrancois : soon available !

September 15, 2010 • DamienGautier

LeFrançois is a typeface in OpenType format based on 3 series of capitals. It seems to be classical but some glyphs show that it’s not so conventional. It’s seems to be a typical French type, that’s why its name is LeFrançois (with the accent please !) With a particular work on kerning (about 5000 !) and ligatures, you’ll be able to compose words as logotypes thanks to capitals and small caps.
