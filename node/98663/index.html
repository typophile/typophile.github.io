Announcement: Monokrom Type Foundry release

December 5, 2012 • Frode Bo Helland

It’s with great pleasure I’m finally able to make this announcement: Today (at noon, CET) we are launching our type foundry, Monokrom, and with it, the five typefaces Faunus, Satyr, Vinter (née Noir), Telefon and Aften Screen. The full press release (mostly news material, but also a free Telefon sampler) is available at https://monokrom.no/press/index_en.php Our website is here: www.monokrom.no – Sindre, Hans Ivar and yours truly.
Congratulations, friends! I’m very happy to see all your efforts and tireless work come to fruition. Best of success to you with this great selection of beautifully crafted faces.
