Seen your first request for this Breitling vintage USAF clock but I was not able to identify any of them. What I could say though is most of typefaces used for such devices (even more when we’re talking about Army) are manufacturer exclusive so far from an answer (previous discussions on this kind of topic: ‘[[http://typophile.com/node/99439|Art Deco vintage watch dial digits]]’, ‘[[http://typophile.com/node/95364|Max Bill clock face design for Junghans. Custom typeface?]]’, ‘[[http://typophile.com/node/99271|Font needed for Neon Clock face]]’…

Central dial digits (ELAPSED TIME/MINUTES) Remind me of [[http://www.myfonts.com/search/engravers+gothic/|Engravers Gothic]]/[[http://www.myfonts.com/search/blair/|Blair]]/[[http://www.myfonts.com/search/sackers+gothic/|Sackers Gothic]] with some ‘stencil’ adjustments (mainly on /6 counter to avoid a too small part). Some more in the same vein: [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/insigne/aviano-sans/|Aviano Sans]], [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/insigne/montag/|Montag]], [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/sweet/sans-pro/|Sweet Sans]], [[http://www.houseind.com/fonts/luxuryfonts|Luxury Gold]], [[http://typography.net/try/?ff=42&f=410|Capline]], [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/storm/zeppelin/|Zeppelin (41)]], [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/betatype/doublewide/|Doublewide]], [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/chank/adrianna/|Adrianna]], [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/typodermic/biondi-sans/|Biondi Sans]], [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/positype/halogen/|Halogen]], [[http://www.typography.com/fonts/font_overview.php?productLineID=100043|Idlewid]], [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/mti/burin-sans/|Burin Sans]], [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/redrooster/ronsard-crystal/|Ronsard Crystal]], [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/baselinefonts/worn-gothic|Worn Gothic]], [[http://processtypefoundry.com/fonts/bryant-2|Bryant 2]], [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/scrowleyfonts/rondell|Rondell]],

ELAPSED TIME/MINUTES Again, some reminiscence of discussions about [[http://typophile.com/node/79271|Leroy Lettering machine]] without being it. Alternatives: [[http://www.dafont.com/engineering-plot.font|Engineering Plot]], [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/nicksfonts/planscribe-nf|Planscribe]], [[http://www.fontspace.com/character/leroyletteringlightbeta01|Leroy Lettering Light Beta 01]], [[http://lineto.com/The%20Fonts/Font%20Categories/Text%20Fonts/Gravur%20Condensed|Gravur Condensed]], [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/jnlevine/rendering|Rendering]], [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/larstornqvist/simpliciter-sans|Simpliciter Sans]], [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/urw/gordon/|Gordon]], [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/btn/register-sans-btn|Register Sans]] (open counter digits), [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/larabie/primer-print|Primer Print]], [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/typodermic/report|Report]], [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/bedoodle/oxymoron|Oxymoron]], [[http://www.dafont.com/tgl-0-16.font|TGL 0-16]]

U.S. AF TYPE A-10A 8 DAYS/BREITLING WAKMANN Some ‘[[http://www.myfonts.com/search/din|DIN]]’ might work well even if, again, that’s far from matching. Some interesting details to note: /A large flat top, low /W centre vertex Similars: [[http://lineto.com/The+Fonts/Font+Categories/Text+Fonts/Storno|Storno]], [[http://omtype.com/type/en/default.html|Default]], [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/typodermic/fenwick|Fenwick]]
