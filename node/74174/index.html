By oxymoron, I mean types which combine contradictory principles. Humanist sans faces, for instance, which marry the clean, reductive, mechanical ethos of the unadorned monoline with something of the quality of hand-made lettering. But finding the balance is up to you. A new humanist sans, from Shinntype, Sensibility. Another of my oxymorons, Bodoni Egyptian.
