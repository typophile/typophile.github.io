Howdy! I have played around with your type a bit and what I have changed boils down to this:
  • Changed the horizontal overshoot of the O.
  • Added to the width of the slash character.
  • Changed the ending of the Rs upper bowl to vertical as none of the other letters had a diagonal termination. I felt it would fit in better.
  • Changed kerning between those pairs: HR RI ST EN NS EN.
