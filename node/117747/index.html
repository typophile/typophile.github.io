PRESENT and BY appear to be some form of Bank Gothic (though very hard to tell given the tiny size of the sample; when scanning, focus only on the type you need identified and scan as large as possible): http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/bitstream/bank-gothic/

My best guess for AUDRA and LANIE is Trade Gothic Condensed: http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/linotype/trade-gothic/

The text time is garden variety Times (without a larger size sample, hard to determine whether it’s Times, Times New Roman, etc.): http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/apple/times/
