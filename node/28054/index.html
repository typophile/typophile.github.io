Skincare Association Logo

September 5, 2006 • BrentSpore

I am working on a logo for a skincare association and I am thinking of making tweaks to the Dauphine typeface. The name of the association is "DermaTrust". At first glance, the typeface seems a bit thin for smaller sizes as well as screen media (tv) so I increased the thickness a bit to compensate, but have I committed a cardinal type sin? I throw myself before the group. I would love your thoughts/suggestions. Also, I didn't like the "e" so I made one real quick to start off. [img:sites/default/files/old-images/DermaTrust_LogoIdeations_6225.jpg]
I think you have a strong idea going here. But... A little more space would work wonders. Adjust everything to the same spacing you have around the second "R". And: get rid of either the special "M" or the special "R". As it is they're competing for attention. I would lose that gap in the first "R", it annoys me.
