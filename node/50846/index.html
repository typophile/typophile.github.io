Hi everyone, my first post here so hello :-)

I am a magazine designer in the UK and am interested in what are the most popular magazine fonts at the minute.

I have been researching on the internet but am finding it hard to find out the most current fonts people in this industry are using, if anyone could make any suggestions i would be very grateful!!

Thanks everyone

Here is what I can think of right now.

The free UK magazine Shortlist is pretty much only using Benton Sans from Font Bureau.

British GQ is using Sebastian Lesters Soho, Berthold Akzidens Grotesk (and a serif).

GQ (US) is using Titling Gothic from Font Bureau as well as a few others for features etc.

Esquire US is using Mercury (Hoefler and Frere Jones) and Christian Schwartz' Stag and Stag Sans.

Sorry that links aren’t included. Busy at work. You can search for specific magazine titles here at Typophile and probably find a lot more info.
