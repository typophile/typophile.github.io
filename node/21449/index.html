2006-07-25 08:40:04 • Bald Condensed
This is a *great* logo. I don't think you will find this as a font unless commissioned by head office. Here is info from "Changes in logos and trademarks in Japan": The trademark with the Chinese character TAKA in a circle, the identical character when seen from the front or behind. version of circle-mark shown from 1886 The mark registered in 1904. In 1968, the prototype of the mark was established to avoid distorted shapes by branches.
