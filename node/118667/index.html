I found out that the font in question is called [[http://www.onestarpress.com/BOOKMACHINEPARIS/TYPO_BMP/PDF/weiner_typo.pdf|Margaret Seaworthy Gothic]]. It was drawn when [[http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lawrence_Weiner|Lawrence Weiner]] didn’t want to use an off-the-shelf typeface for his “sculptures” (as he calls them). Other similar typefaces are [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/marksimonson/refrigerator-deluxe/|Refrigerator Deluxe]] and [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/k-type/mandatory/|Mandatory]].
