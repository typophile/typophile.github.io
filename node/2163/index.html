Alright, this may well prove unpopular amongst you true experts and
entirely off base (so just rebut me!), but, without meaning to point
fingers at any Quiz offering in particular … could it be that it had gotten
way too arcane and difficult?.

I mean, I was waiting for a combination of: A tiny portion of a rarely used
ligature from a non-latin font that hadn't actually been released yet to be
posted! The number of you on this forum who are able and care to hunt
up such an animal can probably be counted on one hand.

The number of active, non-lurking participants in this forum are relatively
few already. Therefore, in my humble opinion, would it not, perhaps, be
better to involve more participants by offering more accessible fonts/glyphs
in the quiz?

The answer could still be made tricky with choice of letter/number, size of
sample image, crafty clues, etc. And if somebody guesses it the same day?
Fine. Next offering. Would a quicker turnover be so bad?

Just my single cent worth.
