As I said “made from scratch” which means that no existing font was used. It’s not rocket science to make this sort of letter, including the stub leg R and the peaked apex W. The O in the lettering is thicker than the other letters and perhaps smaller in scale. I compromised by keeping the scale and thickening the top and bottom strokes. BTW, the S has to extend considerably lower than O to have a similar thickness and allow the 3D effect to work Once the basic outlines are about right, it’s relatively straight forward to use a 3D extrusion font making tool and to adjust the results to approximate the original hand lettered 3D effect. Some letters, such as S take some tinkering. Don
