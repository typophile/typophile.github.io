Suggestions for good printers in NYC?

November 5, 2010 • AlexisNYC

DO NOT GO TO “5 Star Printing” in NYC. West 26st. They are running a con game. The paper stock they use is the thinnest I’ve ever seen. They do not use printing 101 quark express to cut the paper so that the edges are white from mis cutting and the sizes are all off. They give you a “proof” card that looks completely different then the actual order. It’s a total fly by night, con artist place that should be out of business. Stay away!

I work in NYC and have used the following with good results

Rose Press in Mt. Vernon http://www.rosepressinc.com/

Classic Color Systems in Plainview Telephone: 516-576-9779 I think, but that may be wrong

Graphic Service Bureau in Manhattan http://www.gsbinc.net/
