The Worst Years of Your Life

January 18, 2009 • blujai

I was searching bn.com and this book cover caught my eye. ![](/images/old-images/book cover_4542.jpg) This book is a collection of funny and embarrassing stories from teens so the cover emphasizes that. The layout is very different but it’s interesting and appropriate for this particular book. The typewriter font reminds me of the biology tests I took in high school. Those tests seem to have been copied over and over again for the past 20 years so the type on the cover is really appropriate. ;)

And the typewriter type looks purple, the way mimeographed school papers looked! Very appropriate design.

P.S. A search on Google reveals that the designer is Catherine Casalino.
