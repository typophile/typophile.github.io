Greater Albion Releases Paveline

October 8, 2009 • Rasendyll

We have just released Paveline on [[http://new.myfonts.com/fonts/gatf/paveline/ | Myfonts.com]]. Paveline is a punctuated script; by which we mean it has the look and character of handwriting, but the glyphs are discrete entities to aid legibility. It is actually based on a moderately stylized adaptation of our chief designer’s handwriting. Use it to give a handwritten touch to your work. Paveline works well as small handwriting or large scale poster captions. You can see some more samples of Paveline in action on our [[http://gatf.wordpress.com | blog]]. [img:sites/default/files/old-images/31234_5327.png]
We've just added details of an introductory promotion for Paveline to our [[http://gatf.wordpress.com | blog]], as well as a promotional offer on the established Sabio family.
