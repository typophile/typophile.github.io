Dell has a lot of good deals on cheap laser printers. Regarding brands, try to stay away from oddball brands and go with the good stuff: Lexmark, Xante, KonicaMinolta, Canon, Xerox. I think Dell sells rebranded Lexmark models.

Make sure that you check and see what toner stores in your area stock. If you have to pay $10 to have Okidata ship you toner whenever you run out, it might not make sense to get their printer…

I’ve been having good luck with my KonicaMinolta magicolor 2430DL. I’ve been using it with no hassle for nearly a year, and when I use glossy laser paper, the photographs look almost as good as high-end inkjet prints. Not bad for a $350 printer, especially since I haven’t bought any new toner since I got the printer.
