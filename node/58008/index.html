Ascender Uni/Ascender Uni Duo may be identical to [[http://www.ascenderfonts.com/font/andale-sans-family-4-fonts.aspx|Andale Sans]]/[[http://www.ascenderfonts.com/font/andale-mono-family.aspx|Andale Mono]] respectively – except for the fact that they contain 53,000 glyphs each.

so, good news for Special-K: chances are, Andale Mono is present on your system already, as it comes with both Mac OS X and Windows. it’s a monospaced font. still, you could create short strings of text such as “gabriel” with very little editing; “GROUP” seems unmodified.
