VX, I guess actually there’s no font used for the ASUS logo letters. It is only a non typographic design that, from my humble point of view, lacks of some typographic attributes: unions between curved and linear strokes are inconsistently sharp, shapes are imbalanced, spacing is poor. If you need a complementary face for it, I suggest you any light sans typo (Eurostyle would be fine) that barely resembles the characters but contrasts in weight as well, too.
