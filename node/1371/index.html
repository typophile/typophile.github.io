In Washington, DC: Franz Bader art and design bookstore on the corner of 19th and I Streets. This store not only has a really nice selection, but the design of the store itself is very cool: custom designed streamlined Deco-esque wood shelving and furniture, a mini-display of 1930's-era telephones, and their logo is set in Cassandre's Bifur. Check it out if you have the chance.
