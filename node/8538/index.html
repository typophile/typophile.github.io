The standard way to explain how to pronounce the German ch is that it is like the ch in 'Bach', the composer. It is usually pronounced correctly on classical music radio, and many Americans can handle that rather easily - much more easily than the umlaut vowels in German.

If he pronounced it in some dialect rather than standard German, then I guess it might be the hard k, but I believe the standard way would make the ch as in Bach.
