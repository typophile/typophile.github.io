2007-09-22 05:20:00 • Ricardo Cordoba

Haley,

I’ve had many friends that need a business card - fast. Our solution: I buy cover stock #110 from Staples. I run it through 10 up to a sheet on a 4/c laser printer (Okidata) and cut (well, I have a person who does superb, accurate xactoknife slits….)

If there is a bleed - then we use an 8-up format, so we can give (till it) bleed(s)…

They go home happy - and we are cheap, cheap, cheap.

And… yes, they look professionally printed and we receive many-a-smile.

Or… you could go to Popular Printing in NY - fast, reliable, not expensive. Here are their business card prices: http://popularprinting.com/BC.html

P.S. Ricardo - I just did check out where you asked - and boy are they expensive 1 color 1 side 500 cards $170 (and maybe a 10% off) (and they don’t even quote 4/c just 3/c 500 cards for $285) Guys I use - 4 color 1 side UV coating applied 500 cards $39 bucks - no discount….
