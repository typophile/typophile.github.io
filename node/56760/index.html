Trouble with 'Merge Contours' in Fontlab

April 7, 2009 • clauses

I have until recently used Ben Kiel's 'Font Generator Version 1.1' script to generate OTFs. After I changed the shape of my serifs (they are components placed in the relevant glyphs) the merging of contours have started to fail in some places. If I do the procedure manually, the result is the same as with the script. I have tried to optimise the paths, set pathdirections, cut and paste from working glyphs to bad ones, re-draw sections, set new start points, but to no avail. Sometimes a left side serif is missing, sometimes the stems and serif parts get entangled after merging instead of merging. What to do? /Claus
Make sure each segment of the glyph has counter clockwise contour for OTF. If not, cut the good segments, reverse the contour of the bad ones, then paste the good ones again. That works for me.
