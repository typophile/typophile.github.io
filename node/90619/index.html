Typography on an Easter Egg! Otherwise known as Easter Egg Typography or EET. Whether it be TEE or EET, this awesome challenge has two categories!! Typography drawn directly on a whole egg shell just like traditional Easter Egg decorating (except now you buy plastic egg shells and they will be accepted in this challenge too) - or - typography drawn in the form of an Easter Egg. Either one is totally awesome!! Bonus points if you can submit both :)!!

The Typophile powers that be will have to sign off on this awesome type battle, but here are the details copy/pasted the Type Battle Forum…

// REQUIREMENTS

  • Image size: 550 width x 400 height. 72 dpi
  • Color: Any
  • Format: JPG or PNG
