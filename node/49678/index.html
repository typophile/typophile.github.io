<self-promotion>

The [[http://www.kentlew.com/WhitmDisOverview.html|Whitman Display]] fonts were developed originally for newspaper headlines. (Minneapolis Star Tribune commissioned the initial styles.) The x-ht/extender proportions are a little more classical than some other recent news headletter styles and might be an easier transition from Garamond (if that’s important).

The fonts haven’t yet been put up for Retail download on Font Bureau’s website, but they are available for license by contacting FB directly.

You can see samples at my site: http://www.kentlew.com.

Also, there’s a PDF showing available (not linked on the site) here: http://www.kentlew.com/Type/WhitmanDisplay_Sample.pdf

</self-promotion>

– Kent.
