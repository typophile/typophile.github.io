December 20, 2008 • Frode Bo Helland

FF Strada is a beautiful typeface, but what's the deal with that licensing? Offering italics and caps separately? I'd love some suggestions for other similar designs.
Offering italics and caps separately? As long as you choose the OpenType version, the small caps are not separate. For the PostScript format: there was/is no other way, as there are not enough slots. For the Italic: It looks like you can license all upright weights in one bundle, and all italic weights in another. Of course, you can always assort your desired styles as single fonts. So, is it a Regular/Italic/Bold/Bold Italic core package that you are missing?
