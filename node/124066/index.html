Is it even possible?

Nope, you cannot call unencoded glyphs in websites directly. But unencoded glyphs are usually connected to an OpenType feature. So you can call that and the browser might substitute the regular Unicode-encoded glyph with that unencoded glyph, if it supports that feature.

http://caniuse.com/#feat=font-feature
