Once I showed an ad to a client and he said “make the logo bigger”. The next day I showed him a layout with it 20% SMALLER and said “This is yesterday’s layout”, and then showed him the original layout and said “Here’s the new one”.

My client smiled and said “Now it’s perfect!”

I rarely do that kind of thing, as I think it’s best to be honest with clients, and feedback from clients can sometimes result in a layout that really is better, but in this case the client had a lousy sense of design and I knew that making it bigger would look awful.
