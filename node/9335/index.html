I have a client that strikes through their previous price in their ad, then shows a new, lower price next to it.

Laura, if there is a specific font you would like to use, don't let the lack of a strikethrough option hinder you.

If you have an image editor, such as Photoshop, Illustrator, or even MSPaint, you can type the words you need in the font you want, and then add lines through it as needed with a Line tool, and usually Photoshop or Illustrator has some neat looking line options to really make it look as though it were crossed out with a marker, for example. Hope this helps!
