Years ago, a friend of mine designed a logo for a large regional bank. In the course of designing it, he mocked up a couple as a joke between himself and another designer, and set them aside. The presentation was moved up while he was on vacation (holiday), and a nitwit account executive dug around in his drawer, finding one of the joke designs, and presented it. The client chose it. When you looked at it sideways, it was clearly a masked robber. The design firm was sued after signs were made, stationery printed, etc.
