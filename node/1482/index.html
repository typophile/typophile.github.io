hhp
Great work…

He's working with one hand in the very old tradition of typedesign, and on the other, he's pushing the limits of it. His 'Balance' typedesign is a sort of hommage towards Roger's Excoffon Antique Olive (very un-dutch in it's design; compare it with the 'traditional' dutch-designers like Gerard Unger & martin majoor). His other typefaces, like 'avance' & 'cocon' are very modern and unique.

His typefaces are always from extreme quality.

I'm sorry, for my bad english. I'm dutch. (I've just finished my study at the art-academy in Arnhem, the netherlands; where Evert Bloemsma has studied also.)
