Typophile to speak at Chronicle Books' 555 design event

October 2, 2009 • Joe Pemberton

Chronicle Books Design is hosting “5 Speakers / 5 Minutes / 5 Questions”; an evening of design-minded conversation and drinks, featuring five bloggers currently reporting on our visual world:

1 Dave Cuzner / http://grainedit.com GrainEdit is focused on classic design work from the 1950s-1970s and contemporary designers that draw inspiration from that time period.

2 Joe Pemberton / http://www.typophile.com Typophile is the end all, be all internet forum for typography lovers. It’s a “gig” owned by Punchcut.

3 Suzanne LaGasa / http://www.chroniclebooks.com/blog/?cat=41 Design Desk is the regular feature on Chronicles' blog written by the in-house designers.

4 Lauren Smith and Derek Fagerstrom / http://smartsandcrafts.com Smarts&Crafts is the blog component of the Curiosity Shoppe, an impeccably curated store focused on all things crafty, arty, or well designed.

5 David Pescovitz / http://boingboing.net A moderated culture blog whose main contributors were or are still involved with Wired magazine.
