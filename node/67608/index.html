Jean Paul, I think this design is very good, with a nice feel, readability and personality. Italic is also very proportional and well done. But I guess the bold is somewhat excessive – it's almost a black style. The /e/ and /a/ storeys became very closed. It would interesting to have a medium height to expand the family. You may improve some little points: 1. in /j/ the vertical nodes are misaligned; 2. in /k/ the bottom leg is very heavy; 3. in /T/ inclination of the right cut is out of font's general style; 4. in /s/ and /S/ there are small vector problems (bumps, misalignments) in curves; 5. difference in strokes of /A/ and /K/ are higher than one finds in other letters (N, W, Y). Good job! Soon I also would like to hear your critique for the serif I'm developing.
