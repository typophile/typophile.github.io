2010-05-17 20:38:28 • Kwesi Amuti

I do know how to punctuate

When folks come on Typophile with eccentric capitalization or punctuation, they are almost always recently out of school. And no successful pro that I have seen on Typophile does this kind of thing. Why? I think it’s because they understand that not following the conventions puts a distraction or barrier in the way of the reader. To the writer it may look adventurous, but to many readers it is off-putting, and signals being wet behind the ears, or a crank.
