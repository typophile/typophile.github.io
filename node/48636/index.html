Face for a strange film title sequence and posters

August 21, 2008 • Mr Brown

I have been asked to compile some film titles and a poster for a strange but beautiful film. I can’t even begin to explain the plot! See attached stills for a flavor.

The piece is called ‘Wintering’ I liked the idea of a strong typeface that would work well on screen as when you move typefaces on screen they fill in a blur any small details. I enjoyed the Assignation of Jesse James' which used the attached face (I don’t know what this is)

But I was thinking a swash on the W could look nice. but I’m open to suggestions from you guys what do you think…. I’m on the side of Univers at the moment or Aachen (is that how you spell it?)

Let me know your thoughts … x
