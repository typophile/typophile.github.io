Very nice on the whole! A couple things: why the large space between "whom" and "we?" Also, do you have a non-system cut of Garamond? That minuscule a is an eyesore. To be clear, I think Garamond is a great choice for this piece, just try a different cut if you've got one. Garamond Premier Pro would work beautifully if you've got it... Also, it should be "who," not "whom," as you it refers to the subject, and is not the objective case. Congrats, by the way. Our first is coming up on 11 months of age. They're not joking when they say it flies by. Good luck!
