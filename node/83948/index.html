Thank you Renko. Khaled, your experience is exactly the sort of thing I've been warning about for years: when you make one script feel like another you're reducing readability, at least for anybody also familiar with the "imported" script.* The other big reason to avoid such migration is it threatens scripts whose survival is at stake. * Here's an example of what I'm talking about: http://www.flickr.com/photos/ninastoessinger/4986274299/ Two caveats though: - In a display font the readability issue is much less important. - For a script that's not threatened importing the feeling of another script can actually enrich things. For example Sakkal Majalla is pretty cool in my book. hhp
