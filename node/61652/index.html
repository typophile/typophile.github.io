I ran your fonts through a test tool, FontQA, and these are the errors that were found:

Some contours have less than three nodes.

CourierPCfB: zero: (x=388, y=392) CourierPCfB Bold Oblique: mhook: (x=1163, y=16), rhookturnedsuperior: (x=688, y=529) CourierPCfB Bold: mhook: (x=1149, y=16), rhookturnedsuperior: (x=565, y=529)

Some composite glyphs have empty components. CourierPCfB: zerowidthspace->(uni0000), zerowidthnonjoiner->(uni0000), zerowidthjoiner->(uni0000)

I believe the last error, the empty components, are responsible for the wrong linespacing calculation. When you delete the empty components from these three glyphs, it should probably work okay.
