The bulleted locations on the following link have a reservoir of images which may be of interest to those who like old Greek stuff.

Excellent find, Chris. I’d seen the previous project on Roman stilus tablets, but not the newer Greek stuff.

On this topic, I learned a new word this year: stoichedon, which refers to ancient Greek inscriptions in which the letters are arranged in a grid of vertical columns as well as horizontal lines (they are still read horizontally). There are some good examples in the Oxford image bank.
