Greater Albion releases Keynsia

June 12, 2009 • Rasendyll

Greater Albion has just released our latest new family of typefaces, Keynsia.

The Keynsia family revives the spirit of the 1950s-in fact it’s our first venture into the design ethos of that decade. Its simple and elegant lines make for an eye-catching set of display faces.

A range of different faces make up the family, all having an extensive character set. Keynsia is available on [[http://new.myfonts.com/fonts/gatf/keynsia/overview.html | Myfonts.com]] and we’ve put some examples of it in use on our [[http://gatf.wordpress.com | blog]].
