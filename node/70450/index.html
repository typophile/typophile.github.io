Here is the link for Tempus Sans. By the way, this was a new design in 1995, by Phill Grimshaw, where ‘vintage’ usually means a 19th century design, and ‘uncial’ means all letters are the same size (unicase). This mixed case lettering is usually called “Title case”, where the first letter of each word is capitalized. I am not trying to be a wise guy, I just thought you might like to learn the terminology for future posts.
