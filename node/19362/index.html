2006-04-19 22:05:05 • Lex Kominek
These are indeed not "fonts" in the sense that they would be available in digital format to use on your computer. These characters are made specifically for the plate-making machines that produce them. There are a number of typefaces available that either were modeled after or resemble various types of license plates. In this instance I would look into [[Tobias Frere-Jones]]'s Garage Gothic or [[Christian Schwartz]]'s Pennsylvania. Also check out the collection of license plate fonts from Lineto.
