looking for a font close to “evil spirits”
This lettering is a pastiche of styles. Note for example the median spurs – found in many wild west fonts –and the foxtail terminals There are some fonts that catch some of the flavor of the lettering. An interesting substitute is Jonathan Barnbrook’s Mason http://www.emigre.com/EF.php?fid=104 and Others include Midnight-Show https://creativemarket.com/Alterdecofont/15306-Midnight-Show-typeface Exocet by Jonathan Barnbrook http://www.emigre.com/EF.php?fid=95 and the Dogma movie font, a fan effort as Avqest http://www.fontspace.com/gemfonts/avqest.
