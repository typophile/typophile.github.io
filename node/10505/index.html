I'd guess maybe they are 'pseudo-names', such as the ones Printers and Stationers make up to avoid using the copyrighted names of commercial fonts.

E17 looks like Alternate Gothic No. 1, or one of the other numbered Gothics.

E10 could be another of the Gothics or even Helvetica compressed.

In my opinion one of these commercial fonts would be nearly indistinguishable to all but a Typophile fanatic, and you would know what you were dealing with, license-wise.
