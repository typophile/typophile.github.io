Are you the only one there? Unless this is a brand new newspaper, and you’re the only designer, there should be some existing standards to follow. Can you open up old files and see how they were done?

But definitely talk to the press supervisor. I used to work at a newspaper, and because of all the factors of printing on newsprint and their ancient presses, they way we did things was VERY unusual, and tailored to get the best quality (if you can call it that) from what we had to work with. Without talking to your press person, you’ll just be doing a lot of guessing.
