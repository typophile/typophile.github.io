The base for the A (the ornaments appear to be added) goes by several names–Celeste and Adine:

http://www.whatfontis.com/Celeste-Normal.font?text=A http://www.whatfontis.com/Adine-Kirnberg-Regular.font?text=A

Evidently designed by Pulitzer-Prize-nominated composer David Rakowski, who is known for creating revivals of 19th century typefaces. This is based on a Bauer & Co typeface from the Art Nouveau era called ‘Schreibschrift Romana’ (reproduced in “Treasury of Authentic Art Nouveau Alphabets” by Ludwig Petzendorfer, Dover Books).
