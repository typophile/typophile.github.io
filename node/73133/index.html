Granshan 2010 – "Eastern Type" Competition

August 12, 2010 • hrant

A reminder to submit your best work to Granshan, the nicely focused, aptly labeled “Eastern Type Design Competition”:

http://www.granshan.com/

Previous winners have included Sibylle Hagmann, Type Together, and Manvel Shmavonyan. As is customary in such competitions the deadline has graciously been extended, to August 20. Submissions are made via emailed PDF so you have plenty of time! Also note the minimal entry fee.

May the best Eastern fonts win.

hhp

Oh, and something minor: The grand prize is 1000 Euro!

In past years there have been multiple prizes, but smaller amounts.

Please spread the word.
