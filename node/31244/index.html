Jo De Baerdemaeker, born September 8 1974 in Brussels, Belgium, is considered one of the two active typedesigners of Belgium. Frederik Berlaen is the other one.

He is an experienced designer who has a passion for type and typography. Recently graduated at the University of Reading, Jo De Baerdemaeker designed and developed Lungta, a Tibetan-Roman typeface, and is eager to continue to work as a typeface designer, teacher and consultant. He understands the complete creative process from concept to final output and keeps himself up-to-date regarding recent technological developments.

De Baerdemaeker is also ATypI Country Delegate for Belgium.
