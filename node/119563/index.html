The company was sold in 2005, and I could get none of the archive.org links to work for 2005, 2006, or 2007. February 2008 is the first instance I could find of the logo in it’s current form:

Based on this progression, I think it’s pretty clear that the logo has been redrawn and refined from earlier versions (the most noticeable difference over the last decade being the removal of the horizontal foot on the P and the redrawing of the crest). I suspect the original was based on Daniel Porthault’s own signature (or one drawn by his wife Madelaine, who was the creative force behind the company).
