Is there an older predecessor to Clearface that might be closer?

Clearface was designed by Morris Fuller Benton for American Type Founders in 1907. In 1978, under license from ATF, the International Typeface Corporation commissioned Vic Caruso to develop it into a family of four weights with corresponding italics. … And then it went digital. So, transformations happened.

To answer your questions: Not in digital form.
