Well, you probably don't care any more, Rob, but I know that the forum
participants are gnashing their teeth, desirous of knowing the identity
of that grungy script. Or maybe not ...

It seems to have begun its life as a version of a typeface that appears
in the Solotype Catalog as Chopin. Dieter Steffmann digitized it as
Chopin Script. Here's another Chopin Script by a 'Diogenes'. And you
can also find it as Chase Callas by the defunct Soft Horizons.

One of these, or yet another version, seems to have begat Radagund,
which I believe to be the font used.
