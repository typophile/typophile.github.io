2009-09-07 02:11:25 • bnyc

Well, if you’re after the boldness and high contrast you might want to take a look at this collection.

Hoefler & Frere Jones’s Didot with optical sizes is also worth a look.

There’s also Leitura and Leitura Display by Dino dos Santos. It’s not a didone, like Bodoni, but it has a rather high contrast and the display cut is very expressive.

I’m a sucker for Bodoni Egyptian (predominantly for text), by Nick Shinn, which would pair nicely with most other Didones.
