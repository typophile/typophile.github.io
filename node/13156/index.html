2005-07-12 12:30:38 • Dav

I haven’t used either of these types, but they both look wonderful in their showings. I think it is just a question of how much ‘juice’ you want in the look.

What I really like about Stalemate is that it manages to be a ‘neutral’ face and have warmth. -Most of the neutral faces are too chilly for my taste.

Auto is gloriously exuberant. The question is whether it is too much for your use, or you want that exuberance. So for ‘fun’, Auto. For a bit quieter, but still warm, Stalemate.
