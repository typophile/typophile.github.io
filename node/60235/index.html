It is a Bauhaus-style font, in my terminology, because it uses the geometric simplified letterforms. The House Chalet collection includes a good mix of Bauhaus-style fonts, and by mixing and matching, maybe with an e-edit, you could replicate this. You might even be able to make this from Avant Garde Gothic, inverting the u to make the n. - Mike Yanega
