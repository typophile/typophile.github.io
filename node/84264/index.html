If it's going to be an attachment (as opposed to being viewed in the body of the email) put it together in InDesign and then export as a pdf. Generally speaking doing the whole thing as a bitmap will make the file unnecessarily large, and the text would look ropey when viewed at anything other than 100% zoom.
