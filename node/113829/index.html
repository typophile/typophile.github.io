Orator, designed in 1962 for IBM, is fairly close for all numerals, including an open 4 and banker’s flat top 3. Substitute I for 1. https://www.myfonts.com/fonts/linotype/orator/ Don

I agree Riccardo, Manifold it is. Unfortunately we are stuck with having only the Orator digital versions.
