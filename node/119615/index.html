2014-09-17 00:51:06 • kthomps5

Brothers is definitely an excellent alternative (some more after). I’m pretty convinced it’s a custom, after having a look to their weibiste, from [[http://www.dafont.com/freshman.font|Freshman]] (used for th rest of the online identity). Serifs has been edited (or chopped off) and turned into flared latin ones. /R leg has been transformed (/P + oblique leg) Some more in the same vein, not matching either: [[https://creativemarket.com/Gumpita/49869-Dealers|Dealers]], usual LHF’s [[http://www.letterheadfonts.com/fonts/roebuck.php|Roebuck]], [[http://www.letterheadfonts.com/fonts/antiquehalfblock.php|Antique Half Block]] and [[http://letterheadfonts.com/fonts/oldstock.php|Old Stock]], [[http://creativemarket.com/swistblnk/75323-Reidfork|Reidfork]], [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/laura-worthington/charcuterie|Charcuterie]] Block, [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/fort-foundry/gin|Gin]]

Quick’n’dirty job starting from Freshman
