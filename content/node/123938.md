  body: "The letters are an edited version of De Vinne, which was available in many
    widths and weights circa 1900.\r\nThe bowl on _P_ and the terminals on _S_ are
    very distinctive. The serif style is a match. Here is a digital rendering by Jeff
    Levine of one of the wider versions as Publication JNL  [img:sites/default/files/old-images/PublicationJNLAsPKSFTIN_5734.gif]
    http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/jnlevine/publication/\r\nThe TV station logos add
    serifs to the bottom of _V_ and _W_ and clip spurs from the top of _T_ and _F_
    and the bottom of _L_. Make these edits, narrow the width, and Publication JNL
    would look the same to most readers.\r\nDon"
