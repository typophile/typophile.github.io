Frans, what’s the source of this sample? Is it pre-digital? As far as I can tell, all digitally available options have been named by Tamas and Jan. Here’s a comparison – from top to bottom: URW’s Goudy (aka WTC Goudy) with the G from the Swash Italic Sorts Mill Goudy, with the included alternate swash G LTC Goudy Oldstyle with the G from the Cursive (there is also a Pro version with swash alternates and short descenders.
