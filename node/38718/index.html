2007-11-02 06:11:18 • akira1975

Nice work Akira. In Jaspert it says that ‘Gracia’ (Amsterdam) is one of the names it was sold under; also ‘Donatello’ (Niebolo) and ‘Hertha’ (Berling). The original design goes back to 1926 to Wagner & Schmidt, then cast by many foundries in 1927. Troubador Open was a Haas design by E. Thiele from 1931, if I read this right.
