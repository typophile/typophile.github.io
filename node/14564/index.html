Chris, you might be in luck! Hiragino Mincho Pro was not designed in Cupertino by Apple. They licensed it. Maybe the company they licensed it from will license you a PC version? Some designer/distribution info is embedded in the font (I viewed this information with Linotype's FontExplorer X utility, which will be publicly available soon): The font was designed by JIYUKOBO Ltd. Their URL is http://www.jiyu-kobo.co.jp/ The font's manufacturer/distributor is Dainippon Screen Mfg. Co., Ltd. Their URL is http://www.screen.co.jp/font/ Hiragino is a trademark of Dainippon Screen Mfg. Co., Ltd. registered in Japan. Dainippon Screen's site is all in Japanese. But maybe you can find an e-mail link?
