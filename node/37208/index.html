I wanted to let everyone know that there is a great class starting in the fall at Cooper Union.

I went from barely clunking around in FontLab to producing a type family which was accepted by FontShop and Village, the latter of whom I will distributing with.

I recommend it to anyone in the New York area who is serious about type.

You can register for his class here: http://www.cooper.edu/ce/calligph.html#p105
