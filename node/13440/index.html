2005-07-13 17:40:23 • bruno_maag

April Greiman is an American who also went to Basel in the early 70s where she met and married the late Dan Friedman (a classmate of mine at CMU). Her typography was very Basel influenced even though she was annointed with the “New Wave” label by the design press. When I took classes with Weigart in the mid eighties, he spoke of April fondly and referred to her as a “Painterly typgrapher” .
