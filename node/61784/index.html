Well, I would think it’s safe to assume that in this case, the pitch would not be verbal. But it might also mean taking a verbal pitch and turning it into a visual one! Which is why it might be good to have a dialog about what the input and expected output of your work might be. Why not simply ask for an example of something in both input and output formats? Preferably the same pitch, but even one pitch in “input” format (the kind of thing you’re supposed to fix) and a different one in “output” format (the kind of thing you’re supposed to deliver) would be helpful.

Cheers,
