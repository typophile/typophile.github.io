Typeface for landscape gardening?

September 12, 2009 • Christian M.

Hi everybody!

I found this nice forum while researching for fonts for a one man landscape gardening firm. Im in the process of designing logo and print materials.

The font should suggest the enjoyable, comfortable garden, possibly be playful but not too crazy. It should convey an organic natural and enjoyable feeling, but also bring across seriousness and tidieness. It must appeal to private homekeepers and hobbyist gardeners ranging from 30-75 but also to other companys.

As of now Im going with Clearface Gothic but Im not so happy with it because it tends to be a bit of a cliche here and also is somehow too simple minded in my eyes. (A plus of it being that it’s a step up from Hobo wich also tends to get used here.)

Christian

Came across Phaeton from Umbrella Type after reading your post. It’s a bit more organic but has a relaxed feel with a touch of playful flare, which may work well for a gardening firm.
