Linotype.com offers a 30% discount on all font products. I am pretty sure that this applies to all the third-party fonts that we sell in our webshop, too (Typejockeys, TypeTogether, ParaType, etc.). But you should check it out yourself (I am not a student anymore!).

Your account at Linotype.com has to be “studentified” for this to work. Just email Linotype a copy of your student ID (you could also fax it), and your account will be “upgraded” so to speak, so that this discount is taken off of purchases in your shopping cart.
