What is the font used in the “The 53 Places to Go…” text? It doesn’t look like Georgia because the lowercase letters are far shorter than the uppercase letters.

That’s the Times' Cheltenham, the typeface they use for all headlines in the print version (so this graphic was probably from the print edition and migrated to the Web). I believe it’s custom-made for them by Matthew Carter.
