2004-01-15 05:29:59 • skyrocket
Adobe Sans and Adobe Serif are multiple master fonts that ATM (Adobe Type Manager) uses to match the exact proportions of fonts that are missing from files generated by Adobe programs, therefore causing no layout oddities even though the original fonts are not present. I guess the "aaaaaaaaaaaaaarrrrrrrrggggggghhhhhhhs" are because the font isn't exactly a beauty Edit: for clarity.
