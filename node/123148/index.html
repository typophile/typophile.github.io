Wooden Plaques on the side of our sailboat

February 7, 2015 • keywestbetty

The pics are of a piece of paper I used to do a rubbing over an engraved plaque on the front of our sailboat. I used a black crayon to rub over the letters. The boat was built in Taiwan in the 70’s and I believe was delivered to England in 1976. The name of the boat is “Seance” and I think it was named and registered in England. So, I imagine these wooden plaques with the engraved name where made at that time. We have purchased 2 more sailboats and I’d like to have similar plaques made, using the same font but I can’t figure out what it is. Any help would be greatly appreciated. Thanks : )
