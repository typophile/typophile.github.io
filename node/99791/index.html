Metrically equivalent replacement for Comic Sans

January 17, 2013 • nineplymaple

I know everyone loves to hate on Comic Sans, and I was having trouble finding a metrically equivalent replacement for it, so I made one. My concept is “what if Vincent Connare had a ruler?” I’m trying to maintain the character that people love about Comic Sans, while removing the oddities and irregularities. Using the same metrics means there is still plenty of WTF in here, but hopefully it is an improvement. This is my first font, so it’s probably pretty rough.

![](/images/old-images/spans preview_4341.png)

Here’s a comparison to Comic Sans

I also need a name. I was thinking “Liberation Comic”, but it wouldn’t officially be a part of the Liberation fonts. Maybe “Comic Spans” because it spans the same space as Comic Sans.

I could never understand why people hate Comic Sans. I for one think that it serves it’s purpose well and those who dislike it are just misdirecting their dissatisfaction of seeing lay people use it in their designs towards Comic Sans. Comic Spans sounds reasonable but I have a feeling that if Mr. Connare used a ruler then the outcome would have been different than what you have done, like the sample below.

I don’t hate Comic Sans, but it certainly does have some less desirable aspects. Mostly, it is super inconsistent. I tried to line up the vertical strokes, and make everything the same height, and avoid the handwriting squiggles. I know there are tons of other comic fonts out there, and the world doesn’t need another one. I just wanted to give people an alternative that fits in the same space, and doesn’t suck quite as much.

I figured out the name, too. Comic Relief.
