My assumption is that MyFonts.com cannot resist the opportunity to make some extra money from SoftMaker's collection and "legitimized" them by selecting about 1000 fonts of their 10s of thousands forged fonts they had in their collection. My whole experience (by trying to exclude clones from Find my Font database) leads to the same result, expressed many times here in Typophile: The type publishing & distribution industry is a total mess!
