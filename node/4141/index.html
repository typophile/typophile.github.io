2004-04-08 17:00:53 • Chris Rugen
The typophile email is an alias- meaning you can place it in your "reply-to" field in your email client and/or give it out to friends and family.. But being an alias, you'll still need your original email account. Emails sent to (yourname)@typophile.com will automatically be sent on to your actual email address. If you set it up right, you'd be the only one who knew that your actual email address was something other than typophile.com.
