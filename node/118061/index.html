I don’t think this is a font, it’s probably a Frankenstein construction of different fonts (not only the style but also the weight of the letters is different). If you insist to construct something similar, you can take “GO” of [[http://www.findmyfont.com/index.php/fonts/font-preview?fset=ITC&ffam=AvantGardeGothicC%20-%20Demi&fid=606569c8748e3bce41a5067fe31b6c8f&fsize=60&text=GO&wrap=2|Avant Garde Gothic]] (cut a bit the upper end of /G) and add to the recipe “RUP” from [[http://www.findmyfont.com/index.php/fonts/font-preview?fset=Dafont-1&ffam=Aero%20Matics%20-%20Bold&fid=f278dccbd0e251a66a2528eeac4a00d8&fsize=60&text=GROUP&wrap=2|Aero Matics (Bold)]] free font by Grandoplex Productions [2009] - you probably need to embolden the later.
