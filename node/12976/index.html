Eric,

Most employers will notice the “package” of your work – that would be option 2 – but few will hire you because of it. They will hire you because of your work and your personality. Focus your strengths on editing your portfolio to your best pieces and present them nicely and simply – that would be option 1. It’s all about the work and your ability to talk intelligently and passionately about it.
