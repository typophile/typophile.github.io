body: "<strong>[[Indices]] : [[Designers]] : Luc(as) de Groot</strong>\r\n\r\nLucas
  de Groot  (b. 21 June 1963 in Noordwijkerhout, the Netherlands) is a well-known
  Dutch type designer, the head of the type foundry Fontfabrik (AKA [[LucasFonts]]).
  De Groot is mainly known for the large font family or system, [[Thesis]], consisting
  of [[TheSans]], [[TheSerif]], [[TheMix]], [[TheSansMono]] and later [[TheAntiqua]].
  Also [[Corpid]] (previously AgroSans).\r\n\r\nLuc also designed custom fonts such
  as [[Calibri]] and [[Consolas]] for Microsoft Vista, for the newspapers like Der
  Spiegel with \u2018SpiegelSans\u2019, Die tageszeitung with \u2018Taz\u2019, Folha
  de S. Paulo with \u2018FolhaSerif\u2019, and Sun Microsystems corporation with \u2018SunSans\u2019.\r\n\r\nLucas
  de Groot teaches at the Design faculty of the University of Applied Sciences at
  Potsdam, Germany.\r\n\r\n<strong>External Links</strong>\r\n<a href=\"http://www.fontshop.com/fonts/designer/lucas_de_groot/\">Luc(as)
  de Groot</a> at FontShop"
