Two New Releases From Greater Albion

March 3, 2010 • Rasendyll

Greater Albion has just launched two new releases on [[http://new.myfonts.com/foundry/Greater_Albion_Typefounders/ | Myfonts.com]].

Crucis ornaments is the first in a series of traditionally inspired (but newly designed) sets of printers ornaments. Deva is a modern interpretation of classic Roman text and Display faces, with a range of opentype features such as ligatures and stylistic alternates. You can read more about them on our [[http://gatf.wordpress.com | blog]] or try them out for yourselves on Myfonts.com.

Deva has been launched at a 30% introductory discount.

The sp and ct ligatures in Deva feel very clunky to me, and fight against the elegance you seem to be aiming for. There’s also a misspelling in the sample graphic on your blog. (The third ’t' is missing from “interpretation.")

I join others on this board in encouraging you to slow down a bit and pay more attention to the details of fit ‘n’ finish (and spelling).
