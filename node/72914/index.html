So much depends on the name. Some names look harsh, merely through the accident of letter forms (or through being short), while others are naturally soft – and there is also the sound and meaning of the name to consider – you may want to counteract or enhance these qualities, at the least they should be negotiated. Get designing, for goodness sake!

Clearface (U&lc) is very friendly, IMO, and compact, which is useful for making the name big in signage. Choice of serif or sans.
