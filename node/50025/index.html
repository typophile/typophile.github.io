The Society of Typographic Aficionados (SOTA) is pleased to announce that [[www.typecon.com|TypeCon2009]] will be held in Atlanta, Georgia, July 14-19, 2009. SOTA's going to be on location in Atlanta to meet with local crew and others interested in helping to develop the TypeCon2009 program and extracurricular activities. We are looking for volunteers and brainstormers from all walks of typographic, design, book arts, and lettering fields. Everyone is encouraged to join us for our first official local planning/brainstorming meeting for next year's conference. Refreshments will be served - please let us know if you are coming.
