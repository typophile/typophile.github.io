Well, first of all I feel that "rhythm" is a misleading term (at least in the realm of text fonts). But terminological issues aside, the one (possibly counter-intuitive) thing I've discovered about designing caps is that you can't make them too harmonious with the lc - that backfires. You just have to let them be what they need to be, and stick to ensuring overall stylistic consistency, even color, etc. Unless you're trying to break new ground… hhp
