That style is called Elzevir. It started around the middle of the 19th century in France (as a reaction to Didone typography), and was taken up around the turn of the century by US foundries, where it was sometimes called De Vinne. There are some digital versions of the American stuff, but the original French stuff is harder to find. Our J P Knox has been working one on though: http://typophile.com/node/7596
