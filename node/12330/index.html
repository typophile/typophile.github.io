Amondó Szegi

May 10, 2005 • paul d hunt

[[Indices]] : [[Designers]] : Amondó Szegi

Amondó Szegi is a Hungarian freelance designer, painter and performer. He has previously worked as a newspaper editor, bookshop assistant, electrician and boilerman. Amondó is the co-founder of [[Fontana Type Foundry]] and has designed typefaces for six years. He currently lives with his family in Szeged.
