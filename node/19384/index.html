sushi whisker, After drawing the shape add the gradient mesh. Then you cold start by coloring all the edges with one colour, then add/change colours as needed. You also might need to add occasional mesh point. That quick one was done with 4 x 4 mesh and just three colours from the default pallette. I moved the points around a bit, too. Transparency of the elements is 70%. The middle bit has 9 mm feather in the edges and a basic radial gradient. It's also transparent (70%) and on top of everything. Hope this helps, good luck![img:sites/default/files/old-images/mesh.jpg][img:sites/default/files/old-images/radial.jpg]
