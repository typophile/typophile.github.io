Looks like you’re after a Circus-style wood block type here, also (as has been said) called Western. There are lots of shareware, free (0$) and commercial fonts in this genre. Adobe Wood Types are probably even cheaper than $30, it’s the cost of a pair of socks. My system seems to have several, probably that came with some software; it can be hard to keep track, sometimes.

A couple of links: http://simplythebest.net/fonts/western_fonts.html http://www.1001fonts.com/fonts_overview.html?page=1&category_id=28
