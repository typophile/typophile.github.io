Punchcut Seeks Design Intern

October 15, 2008 • Joe Pemberton

Hello. We’re looking for a second intern to join us in our San Francisco office. Rather than Typophile duties, we’re looking for a candidate to help with Punchcut marketing efforts. Please send a cover letter and PDF portfolio or portfolio URL to marketingposition [at] punchcut [dot] com.

DESCRIPTION

Punchcut is looking for a graphic design intern to aid with graphic design, event planning, publishing and public relations initiatives.

RESPONSIBILITIES

You will primarily: • Coordinate design and production of online, print, mail, e-mail or other marketing campaigns: including web site updates, collateral and other items as requested

You will also help: • Maintain a marketing initiatives calendar, coordinate meetings with directors • Maintain an event calendar and manage speaking submissions for events • Coordinate marketing events and logistics (printing, shipping, etc.) • Coordinate design and production of online, print, mail, e-mail or other marketing campaigns: including web site updates, collateral and other items as requested

QUALIFICATIONS

You must: • exude pro-activity and possess a positive, energetic attitude • have a creative approach to solving problems • thrive in a flexible and evolving, entrepreneurial environment • be detail-oriented and organized • communicate well verbally and in writing • have a knowledge of Adobe Creative Suite, Microsoft Office is a plus

Additional Qualifications: • Either you have 1-2 years working in a professional design environment, • Or you are a Junior or Senior in a college level program focused on Design and/or Marketing communications

BENEFITS

The position is part time and requires working on site in our San Francisco Union Square office. We offer flexible hours, starting immediately. The duration is is negotiable and there is potential for full-time hire for the right candidate. This position reports to the Marketing & Brand Director.

HOW TO APPLY

Interested? Come build something. Please send your resume and cover letter to marketingposition [at] punchcut [dot] com or fax them to (415) 445-8857.

ABOUT PUNCHCUT

Punchcut is a user interface design and development company specialized in improving users’ experiences with device, desktop and television user interfaces. Punchcut provides strategy, design, and development services – from original user research and usability studies to the complete design and development of custom interfaces and applications. Punchcut’s clients span the consumer electronics industry including device manufacturers, technology providers, service providers and content brands. Punchcut partners with clients to create solutions that engage customers, streamline transactions, and enhance digital user experiences.

Everyone at Punchcut shares real impact on the decisions we make. Our team of designers, developers, strategists and researchers combine their perspectives and ideas to create great user experiences. And we won’t be interrupted by partitions and job titles. At Punchcut, we’re striving to create a different kind of company. One where people are the priority and the future is the focus.
