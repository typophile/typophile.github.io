Introducing myself

June 24, 2009 • ConwayAnderson

This is the internet. So, I doubt anyone gives a shit, haha. But, I figured I would try anyways.

I’ve lurked around for awhile and I know a bunch of the guys from LogoPond.com have recommended this site over and over gain. So, I decided to finally get off my ass and register.

I live in San Francisco. I work for Pandora.com designing “digital brand experiences” for advertisers (that’s right, I am the guy that annoys you while you listen to music) and every once in awhile I take some brand identity projects on the side as well.

I am slowly becoming a typeophile as I play ‘Guess that Typeface’ while I walk down the street and make my own type treatments for logos.

Anyways, that is all. Flame on.

Welcome! This is just the right place for type guessing. Hope you will enjoy it!
