It looks like some variation on a Bauhaus-style font. It’s possible a tail on the ‘a’ has been chopped off. Often with Bauhaus-looks, if it isn’t actual ITC Bauhaus, you can often piece it together with some of the fonts in House Chalet.

See the [[http://www.bowfinprintworks.com/BauhausFaces.html|Bauhaus-style Font ID Guide]] to scan through some of the related styles.
