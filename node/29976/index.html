Quite similar to “Element”, one of the Neugotische Schriften came up in the (end of) 1920s/1930s in Germany. Some people connect them strongly with the 3rd Reich, the nationalsocialist Germany, anyway. “Element” you could buy here: http://www.romana-hamburg.de/fraktur6.htm (search the list)

Something more - Here you see 2 others that were popular – Tannenberg and Potsdam. Other fonts from this categorie were, for example, Lichte National, Standarte, Deutschland. And… The red A is still used nowadays as a sign for pharmacies all over germany.
