Your faith is touching, but some things may not be commercially available, even if they are fonts. I have not found much that I can say is even very close to this, and I have been looking, believe me.

So far the best alternatives I can suggest are Scrivano, by Ricardo Esteves Gomes, and then what at first seems like an odd choice: Post Antiqua, an old calligraphic typeface from 1932, by Herbert Post. If you add serifs to this it resembles the inscription lettering better than anything else I have seen so far.

I am still looking, but the fact that this is all you have gotten for answers suggests that this just isn’t a font that anyone knows.
