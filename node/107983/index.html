I think you may need more info about ascender and descender settings. Hint: descender is always a negative number in normal fonts. There is an image here [[http://fontforge.org/overview.html | FontForge Documentation]] that should give you a lot of insight on Baseline, Ascender and Descender. Other than that, read all of the FontForge documentation, or find a good book or website on the subject, such as this one [[http://ilovetypography.com/2007/10/22/so-you-want-to-create-a-font-part-1/ | I Love Typography]].
