All though this is an audio book cover and not an actual book, I think this is a perfect example of type being used with an image. I love how the main part of the title, “Shooter”, is in the scope of a sniper rifle. It relates perfectly to what the story is about instead of a random image that could be misleading like some covers out there. Overall, I think it’s just really cool and well put together.
