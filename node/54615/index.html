Can anyone recommend a decent good value printer they’re using at home?

Ta

If you do not need A3 and laser, I am really impressed by my HP Photosmart C7280 all-in-one: wireless and ethernet, good scanner, great photo printer, quite quick B&W A4 inkjet (plus photocopier and fax, if you need them). Used to have A3 lasers costing thousands, this little gizmo is fantastic (and for less than the cost of a replacement toner cartridge for my old GCC Elite XL).
