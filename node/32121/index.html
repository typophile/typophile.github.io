Firstly, thanks to everyone who gave me input on this logo & sign design. I took your advice and am a lot happier with the result…I hope you all like it, too. The first image is of the original idea and the last is our sign design including logo. I think part of my problem was that I was hung up on the sign rather than the logo itself, simply because in real estate, the sign is what you see the most (well, we hope so anyway!) Once I worked through that, things became much easier.
