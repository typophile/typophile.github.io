Hello,

Idea: Logo in the making in Illustrator, The top one is kerned by ‘Auto’, the bottom one is kerned by using ‘pair of 3 letters kerning principle’ (By lying white strokes above the rest of the type). Pouring water between the characters principle isn’t always usable I think, squinting and looking at a whole is in my opinion more usable.

Feel free to post a comment on the kerning or design.
