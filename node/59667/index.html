2009-07-08 09:05:20 • Bert Vanderveen
I have a CD, specimen book, and poster of "The Serials Collection" of B & P types. This was sent to me at the agency where I worked in about 1993 by B & P Graphics in LaGrange, Illinois. It is full of "fresh recuts" of well-known faces. Neither "Walter" nor "Romana" is among them. We never put any of these faces into use. "Walter," I reckon, denotes Walter Florenz Brendel, the man behind the the work (&, I imagine, the "B" in B & P). But indeed, a scan of the face will help. powers
