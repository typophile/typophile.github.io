Hi I am designing a program for an early music group and instead of putting "a song from C18th Italy" I would like to have the "C" go round the "18th" Is this possible? Thanks for your help
I'm not familiar with this kind of stylistic abbreviations but technically I see no reason why it couldn't be possible. Make the C bigger (set lighter style if available, for strokes' optical consistency), lower it's baseline; analogically, scale down the "18th" (heavier style) and raise it. Then set negative kerning between both, simple and quite obvious if you're working with InDesign or similar software.
