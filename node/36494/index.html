Ascender Corporation is a provider of advanced font products specializing in type design, font development and licensing. Ascender’s founders [[Bill Davis]], [[Steve Matteson]], [[Thomas Rickner]], and many others are font industry veterans and have been involved in developing fonts used in computers, phones and many other hardware & software products. Ascender provides multilingual, custom font development. Ascender also offers high quality fonts for download from the Ascender Font Store website.
