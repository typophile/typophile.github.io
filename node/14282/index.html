As for buses and public transport it’s fairly ok - trains are great too in between larger cities. I “bused” from Caen to Honfleur a few times: a few back and forth rides per day are available - on this ride you will cross Deauville and Trouville which have many fine beaches (and amazingly beautiful houses). Houlgate and Cabourg are also nice - so I heard.

As for lettering, most probably in older parts. Dont realy recall. The shops in Honfleur’s Port must have some good stuff…
