I’m sure Stephen’s list is just a starting point. As I said earlier, most of the Open Type releases, especially of new serif families, will probably include these ligatures. For example Adobe’s [[http://store1.adobe.com/type/browser/pdfs/MINP/MinionPro-Regular.pdf|Minion Pro]], [[http://store1.adobe.com/type/browser/pdfs/1737.pdf|Garamond Premier Pro]], [[http://store1.adobe.com/type/browser/pdfs/1739.pdf|Arno Pro]], [[http://store1.adobe.com/type/browser/pdfs/BIOQ/BriosoPro-Regular.pdf|Brioso Pro]],[[http://store1.adobe.com/type/browser/pdfs/1716.pdf| Jenson Pro]], [[http://store1.adobe.com/type/browser/pdfs/WARP/WarnockPro-Regular.pdf|Warnock Pro]] and probably most of their other “Pro” releases all have st, ct and sp ligatures (although Garamond Pro has only the ct, as Stephen stated).
