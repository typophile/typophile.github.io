The one on the right (‘CAREERS’, ‘CONTACT US’…) is one of their exclusiive and proprietary font called ‘WMF Sketch Carton’, a sketched version of [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/fontfont/ff-scala-sans/|Scala Sans FF]]. You’d find some good alternatives browsing fonts tagged ‘[[http://www.myfonts.com/search/tag%3Asketch|sketch]]’ at MyFonts. For the ones on the left, sorry but can’t recognize any. Could be either bespoke or freebies. Having also a look at MyFonts to fonts tagged ‘[[http://www.myfonts.com/search/tag%3Achalk|chalk]]’ should be helpful. Google is also your friend. Some searches like ‘[[https://www.google.com/search?hl=en&source=hp&q=best+chalk+fonts|best+chalk+fonts]]’ should work perfectly. Many people have posted their best picks.
