“THE BEST OF BLONDIE”: confirming [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/redrooster/quest-rr/|Quest]] First sample song titles and text: possibly [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/linotype/optima|Optima]] Second sample song titles: [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/softmaker/adelon-serial|Adelon]] is Softmaker version of [[http://www.myfonts.com/search/albertus|Albertus]] (see [[http://typophile.com/node/93218#comment-510520|here]] for more implementors of it). Bitstream version [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/bitstream/flareserif-821|Flareserif 821]] is another option to get such boldness. There are also [[http://typophile.com/node/27712|some extra-bold versions of Albertus]] (Alaska) shipped by HP and Brother with their laser printers.
