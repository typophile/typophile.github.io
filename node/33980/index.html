Where to start? The terminals on the c, r, e, s, and a are lumpy and do not transition into the hairline in an elegant way. The tops and bottoms of all the letters have that tell-tail sharp transition at the tangent point. Very abrupt and not attractive. All the curves are sloppy, not a taught curve in the bunch. The r is too wide, the lower bowl of the a looks like it was drawn by an alzheimer's patient, the t is weak and that te ligature does not work at all. The spacing is poor and there is absolutely no rhythm anywhere. James
