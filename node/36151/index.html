Villatype - a blog about found type

August 15, 2007 • milks

After admiring the street type on my walks to work, I wanted to document and share what I had found. So I created villatype, a visual record and discussion of typography in the public domain. If you are interested in submitting your own found type please feel free to contact me. Visit villatype at villatype.blogspot.com [img:sites/default/files/old-images/villatype_3553.gif]
