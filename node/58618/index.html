who to send them to? <– nearest (small or medium) design studio that you think worth sending.

what to say? <– that you live around the neighborhood and interested to gain experience through them. do some research about things they’ve accomplished and if you’re amazed by their works/reputation, tell them.

what not to say? <– lies.

send resume? <– offer it, and send if they ask.

what to do when they just say thanks? <– depends, if they’re asking for the resume that you offered, that’s good. if it’s just plain ‘thanks’ then that’s not so good. IMHO.

Good luck. :D
