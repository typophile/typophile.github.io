it sounds like Adobe applications are looking for ‘fi’ and ‘fl’ ligatures that don’t exist in the font, or that DO exist, but have not been designed. a simple way to resolve this issue would be 1) to disable auto ligature replacement in the application, or 2) add these ligatures to your font.
