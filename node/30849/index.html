2007-01-26 15:51:12 • aluminum

First off, I like your design. I’m not an authority, but I think the .com has a stigma attached to it because of all the other independent “.coms” out there. Notice that all of the biggest internet companies don’t have .com attached: Google, Yahoo, AOL, etc. Most people assume (many times wrongfully) that the URL is companyname.com, so in this case, they would be right and you have nothing to worry about. Additionally, the “Web” part gives away that it’s a website, so it’s somewhat redundant.

As for the braces, IMHO, I don’t think they matter because people will know it’s not part of the company name (they wouldn’t say, “Let’s give ‘Brace Focused Brace Web dot Com’ a call!")

And lastly, I like blue for the color - just make it a bright blue and not the typical navy of startup .coms.

-Scott
