Making shapes of certain sizes in FontLab

December 6, 2005 • abecedarienne

Hi Folks, I would swear there was a way to create a shape of a certain size in FontLab but I can't find it! Let's say I wanted to make a circle or a square 100 units wide and high. How do I do that!? Thanks in advance, Amy
Open the Primitives window (Window > Primitives). Select Ellipse and click on the Place button. In the tiny window that appears, click on the "..." button. A dialog box will appear where you can enter the dimensions.
