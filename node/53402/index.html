Making my mark

January 13, 2009 • robofunc

I’m starting to do freelance design work, so I’m making a logo. The idea behind it is a shortening of my last name–Von Kannenberg–to vonk. The lame visual pun I’ve got is “v on k”–literally fusing the forms of a v and k. I’m hoping to make an animated version that starts with “vonk” and collapses down, leaving the logo.

I’ve started exploring with one of my favorite Didots–I love the letterforms and they are combining pretty well so far, I think. The problem is that I’ve started seeing uk instead of vk. Then I’ll go do something else for a while and it’ll be vk again when I come back. It’s maddening.

What do you see when you look at these? Is this a good direction?

*versions E and F are intended for use at large sizes.
