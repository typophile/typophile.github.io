This is very close to many Gothic like Trade Gothic/Alternate Gothic. It also gathers influences from DIN mainly with spurless /G and /M centre vertex position. Closest I’ve found are [[http://www.houseind.com/fonts/chaletcomprime|Chalet Comprimé]] (mixing 1980 styles) and [[http://www.fontsquirrel.com/fonts/bebas|Bebas]]/[[http://www.fontsquirrel.com/fonts/bebas-neue|Bebas Neue]] (Bebas closest for letters and Neue for digits). And if it’s come from Nike (what I believe), there are many chances it could could be bespoke.
