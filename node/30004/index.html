Jan van Krimpen

December 9, 2006 • DB

Jan van Krimpen (1892–1958) Dutch typographer. His type-designs Lutetia, Romanée, Romulus, Cancelleresca Bastarda, Romulus Sans, Sheldon, Spectrum, Haarlemmer, Antigone Greek (not a complete list). His punch-cutter at Joh. Enschedé en Zonen was Rädisch. The relation between Van Krimpen and Rädisch was difficult. DTL is currently digitalising a few of Van Krimpens designs. His stamps (designs for the Dutch PTT post published in 1946) where well known and liked throughout the Netherlands. Articles and books by Van Krimpen: 'On Designing and Devising Type' (1957) Articles and books about Van Krimpen: Dreyfus, John – The Work of Jan van Krimpen (1952)
