Attached title screen capture for the TV series Tom Corbett Space Cadet (1950-1955). What typefaces are being used here? The top looks kind of like Wichita, but isn’t. The lower face looks kind of like Flash, but isn’t.

I realize that both may very well have been one-off scripts done by the graphics person for this particular show, but I thought I’d ask anyway on the off chance that they’re not.
