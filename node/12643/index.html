Very nice.

It does bother me that the spline control points in the FontLab Studio icon are wrong. I think those could be fixed without detracting too much from the design of the icon.

It’s also a little disconcerting to see that the split-up pieces of the BitFonter logo contain partial pixels of the B glyph. The pieces should be split on exact pixel boundaries.

Finally, I’m confused as to the purpose of the r that exists in the negative space of the TransType logo. It seems to just clutter up the logo – probably more so in the smaller versions of the icon, such as the Windows-typical 32×32. It also doesn’t work at all with the upside-down T. I’d probably keep the two Ts and eliminate the r entirely.
