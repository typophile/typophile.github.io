For a very coofee-tableish book on typography, maybe you want to take a look at Typography, by Friedrich Friedl, a big enclyclopedia. The information is sometimes incomplete or short, but it has a lot of images…

2006-02-02 17:10:32 • crossgrove

Something antique might be a good idea.

Perhaps an issue of “The Colophon - a Book Collector’s Quarterly” from the 1930s. The whole set, on sale at Oak Knoll for $1500.

They were beautifully printed (and hardcover bound), and each issue used a variety of classic letterpress fonts, on a variety of interesting stocks. fabulous design and illustration. And the articles are fascinating, still.
