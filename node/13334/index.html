Well, that version of Zapfino is an AAT font. Other than Apple products, I’m not aware of any that support AAT features!!!

Macromedia products do not support OpenType features, and older versions of MS products don’t either. If they don’t even have OpenType support, I doubt that they’d have AAT…

Zapfino can be bought as four single fonts (Zapfino Extra One through Four), but that probably isn’t the solution you are looking for. I don’t know what other solutions you have, unless you use Adobe software (or other OpenType support products). Newer Adobe products would do this no problem.
