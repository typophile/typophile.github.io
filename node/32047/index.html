Massimo Vignelli Designs a Condom -- Sort of

March 7, 2007 • BrooklynRob

New York has produced its own city-branded condom, using packaging graphics lifted from Vignelli's subway signage system. Oddly, the color palette is wrong, which any New Yorker would easily recognize, but the designer seems to be going for visual punch rather than accuracy. They're giving these away for free by the truckload in NY, and tourists seem to be eating them up (so to speak). I think this is an interesting situation of using design to make a potentially embarrassing item "fun." Take a look at the design: http://www.designdrivethru.net
One of my first freelance projects was promotional materials for a movie, Party Girl. They had a condom as one of their promotions. I couldn't quite bring myself to put my condom wrapper design into my portfolio but I still have it. I'm sure the condom is quite brittle by now.
