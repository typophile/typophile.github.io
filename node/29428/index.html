Paul Duensing Passes

November 11, 2006 • jim_rimmer

The evening of November 9, [[Paul Duensing|Paul Hayden Duensing]] lost a length battle with ill health. Without setting out to do so, he was one of the finest teachers in both the private press and avocational typefounding fields. In an act characteristic of his great generosity, he had in the last year of his life made gifts of his typefounding machines, matrices and effects to promising young people. All who knew him will miss him greatly. He is survived by his wife, Ginger. Jim [img:sites/default/files/old-images/]
