Bowne & Company, Stationers is part of New York City’s South Street Seaport Museum. Set up as a New York job shop from 1875, they have several printing presses to demonstrate and many examples of their work for sale.

They are located at 211 Water Street, New York, NY 10036 (Phone: (212) 748.8651)
