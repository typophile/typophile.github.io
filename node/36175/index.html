A six year-old designer

August 16, 2007 • AndrewSipe

I thought I’d post this here and see if I can’t get a few reactions.

Part of it was designed by the company’s CEO’s six year-old daughter. (I’m guessing she did the computer).

It was submitted as the worst logo ever and featured on the front page of Digg (http://www.digg.com/design/The_Worst_Logo_EVER) today.

I actually don’t mind it; it’s nice to see a more artistic approach to the static and cold designs of computer based logos. (I might have used a yellow background instead of a pink one though.) At least it’s not a web 2.0 style!

I like the 6-year-old’s computer rendering a LOT better than the snooze of a font.
