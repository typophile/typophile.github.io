Patricia - About FF Nexus and FF Seria: they are indeed
very similar. Both from the same designer, Martin Majoor.
FF Scala is also similar, but with a larger x-height.
The key differences lie in the fact that FF Nexus is newer,
designed from the ground up in three styles (slab, serif, sans)
and available in OpenType Pro with lots of features.
