Finding this kind of font is often a matter of luck more than anything, because it's something anyone could make, and this looks like amateur work. I searched Dafont and Abstract Fonts looking for this, or anything similar. A foundry called [[http://www.abstractfonts.com/designer/305/Fonts%20For%20Peas|Fonts for Peas]] had a number of very similar scripts, although none were an exact match. See Pea Sara, Pea Amy Rica and Pea Johanna Scripts for some that I thought were quite similar. - Mike Yanega
