Hello Everyone,

I am currently pursuing my MFA in Graphic Design, and my thesis topic is strongly related to ligatures. Now that I am writing my paper, I need to find references for any information on ligatures. I love them, but have found little information about them—especially enough to satisfy a thesis paper—online or in my University’s library.

Any information would be greatly appreciated.

Mike

Just about any good book about the development of typeface design and writing broaches the subject. Ligatures predate printing and have existed as long as type has, so there is no shortage of information. Use the Design and Applied Arts Index to search relevant publications and then start pulling books off the shelves.
