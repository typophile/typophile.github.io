Manicotti

June 9, 2010 • David Jonathan Ross

Hi everyone, I wanted to let you know that my typeface Manicotti is now available for licensing on MyFonts. It was honored by the Type Directors Club in 2007, and since then I've expanded it to include lowercase, unicase, more swash variants, and ornaments. Manicotti was a whole lot of fun to draw, so I'm hoping that it is equally fun to use! 2010-06-23 18:23:31 • blank
Many thanks to all of you guys for your kind words about Manicotti. I agree that it's funny, and at the same time a little sad, that this style has been pigeonholed to the extent that it has. While Manicotti doesn't follow the old French Clarendons very closely, it does take almost every opportunity to play into that faux-Western stereotype. This is the exactly the kind of thinking that encouraged me to return to this style and draw Trilby. Among other things, it was an attempt to cast off some of the long-held associations of reversed-stress slabs.
