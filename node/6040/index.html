Step one: go to the Kulturhuset and pick up a Stockholm Card.

Stockholm is packed with museums of all sorts. I highly
recommend the Stockholm Card which gives you free admission
to all and transportation for a week.

Next door in the same building is a great design bookstore and
down the hall is Design Torget - a great store with all sorts
of well-designed gadgets and gifts.

The Modern Museum is excellent and generally free. The Museum
of Architecture and a small design gallery is next door.

The National Museum has a nice collection of Swedish product
design. The bright orange globe televisions are my favorite.
