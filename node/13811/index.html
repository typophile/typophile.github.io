Christopher Simmons is a San Francisco based graphic designer. He is an emeritus President of the San Francisco chapter of the [[AIGA]] and served on it’s board in previous terms. In 2004 he left Alterpop to form the studio, MINE. He is the author of Logo Lab, and judged Letterhead and Logo Design 9, also authoring and designing the annual of the same name.

Christopher designed the 2005 “Gas, Food, Type” Typophile tee.
