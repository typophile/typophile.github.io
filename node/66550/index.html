Some other pictures from Frammenti della Bellezza by Stefan Seifert

This is my first test balloon. I am working on a manuscript written in German and Italian language (though for the second one I will need help). It treats about designing my typefaces not meant for daily use in rough publishing or commercial area. In a sense they are artistic researches on the symbolic search for female beauty. I inspire myself with photographers as Paolo Roversi or recently I go back to Renaissance painters. The manuscript shall as well contain technical aspects as also poetic views on my everyday wondering of what is beauty all about. I love to express myself with the letters of the alphabet. And for the future I hope that someone will enjoy this ride with me through thoughts, feeling and shape. I am working on it.

Thanks Stefan
