Similar flavor: Ramsey AKA Hrabanus AKA Lombardic Capitals
2007-08-26 18:59:30 • Stephen Coles

My feeling is that this is actually a customization of Blackmoor. This is the only font that has some of these characteristic simplified Lombardic-inspired letters, especially this T. The H looks like two lines joining two I’s; the E and S seem like Blackmoor simplifications. The A and N are the most modified, and to me look similar to some of the letterforms in Aquitaine Initials, another Letraset font.
