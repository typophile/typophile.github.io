It is very reminiscent of [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/linotype/madison-antiqua/pro-italic/glyphs.html|Madison Antiqua]], which, according to Jaspert, Berry and Johnson’s Encyclopedia, was originally called Amts-Antiqua, and issued in 1909 by Stempel. It also says the typeface was redesigned in 1965, maybe that’s when the descender of the lc f was cut off at the baseline etc.
