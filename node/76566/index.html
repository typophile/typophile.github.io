Sharing: 2011 Typograhic Wall Calendar

November 21, 2010 • Harald Geisler

I thought this would be the right place to share my project:

I’m working on a typographic wall calendar, made of two thousand and eleven keyboard keys. Last year I did one made of 2010 keys.(http://typophile.com/node/65810)

This year I turned the design into a Kickstarter project. Kickstarter is a public funding platform for design and art projects. Here is the link to the project-website with a little video: http://kck.st/20eleven

I also was invited to a lengthy interview about my design process: http://blog.kickstarter.com/post/1360375408/creator-q-a-harald-geisler-of-typographical-calendar

On my website i wrote a bit more in depth about the piece: http://haraldgeisler.com/2009/12/31/typographic-keyboard-wall-calendar/

And there is even a concept text and picture as downloadable PDF: http://2011.haraldgeisler.com/wp-content/uploads/2009/12/Harald-Geisler-Typographic-Keyboard-Calendar.pdf

I hope you enjoy the work. Typographic or non-typographic feedback either directly or through mail is welcome. Spreading the word is appreciated (since i need to get backers to fulfill the typographic project.)

Yours Sincerely, Harald Geisler
