When you say different, how different do you mean? American Airlines use AA.com as their primary web address but as far as I know (I'm not American) is not known as AA. It's complicated by redirection from multiple addresses, e.g. British Airways promote ba.com but it just redirects to britishairways.com. continental.com belongs to Continental Airlines not the tyre company (who use conti-online.com). ms.com belongs to Morgan Stanley and not the more obvious software giant.
