I agree with the iStockPhoto suggestion; it’s probably the quickest and easiest solution.

But if they don’t have what you want, another approach would be to check out the stock art books by Dover Publications (http://store.doverpublications.com/). They have several books of flower illustrations which are copyright free, and many libraries and bookstores have their books. Some of their clip art is also sold on CDs.
