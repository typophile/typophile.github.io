Agree wholeheartedly with Ryuk—this is a custom wordmark.

As a base for recreating all but the cap initials, try Austin, by Commercial Type (will require editing): https://commercialtype.com/typefaces/austin

For the initial caps (and that |O|), these faces from Letterhead Fonts may be a decent starting point for editing: http://www.letterheadfonts.com/fonts/confection.php http://www.letterheadfonts.com/fonts/englishrose.php

And this one: http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/typotheticals/koster/
