2007-06-16 00:40:46 • blank
There is this method by Walter Tracy, for determining the sidebearings and also testing it afterwards. It was published in the book Letters of Credit: A View of Type Design. Emil Ruder also used a similar method, published in the book Typography, a Textbook of Design. They are both also in Designing Type by Karen Cheng. Very useful for testing especially horizontal spacing. Miguel Sousa made Adhesiontext, which generates dummy text with a given string of characters. Can also be of use.
