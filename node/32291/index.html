Typeface and title for over 50's mag

March 15, 2007 • dendicott

Hi all

I’m redesigning a tabloid sized (full colour) magazine targeted at the over 50’s age group. The demographic is increasing exponentially and over the next 7 years someone will be turning 50 on average every 7 seconds. They also have a large degree of free time and of course all important - a large disposable income!

I’m looking for a body font which is very readable, sophisticated, modern and warming. I also need to come up with a proposed name for the title - so any suggestions would be very welcome!

It’s very nice to be thought of as a demographic… :)
