You won't get anything for free. Either you invest time to learn Wordpress Syntax (and HTML, CSS and some PHP if you don't know that yet), or you pay someone to do it. Either way, there's no such thing as a free lunch with this. The proper way will depend a lot on what your site is and how clean your code needs to be. You can hack together a Wordpress-based site quite easily, but if every piece of text on the site needs to be controlled via the backend, you may need quite a bit of customization, depending on the original design. An experienced WP-Themer will be able to tell you more about this. And don't ask for pirated content on this forum, your requests will not be met warmly.
