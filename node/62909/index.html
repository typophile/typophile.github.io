Hi folks…got another one for you.

[img:sites/default/files/old-images/sea-side-tf_5517.jpg]

99% certain the “Seaside” is hand-drawn, so I’m already retracing that in Illustrator; however, I’m sure I’ve seen the font used for “Sanitation” somewhere before, but darned if I can place it right now. Any ideas?

Oh, and if anyone could ID “Commercial, Residential, Industrial” as well, I’d be tickled pink.

“SANITATION” is set in [[http://typophile.com/node/33713|squooshed™]] [[http://www.fontshop.com/fonts/downloads/bitstream/clarendon_condensed_ot/?&sample_text=SANITATION|Clarendon Bold Condensed]] by Bitstream. The “Commercial • Residential • Industrial” line is almost certainly [[http://www.fontshop.com/fonts/singles/linotype/times_italic/?&sample_text=Commercial%20%E2%80%A2%20Residential%20%E2%80%A2%20Industrial&sample_size=24|Times Italic]] or [[http://www.fontshop.com/search/?&q=Times+new+roman+italic&x=0&y=0&sample_text=Commercial%20%E2%80%A2%20Residential%20%E2%80%A2%20Industrial&sample_size=24|Times New Roman Italic,]] though with all that ink spreading it’s hard to call (my guess is the former because of its smaller eye on the e that in this case is very nearly lost). Times also sports the bigger bullet, though these definitely have been enlarged.

Good Luck! William Leverette
