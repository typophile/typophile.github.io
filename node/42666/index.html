That would be quite a project! Are you sure you wouldn’t rather just buy a small tabletop press like [[http://www.boxcarpress.com/flywheel/manuals/cp-pilot-manual.pdf|C&P]] or [[http://www.boxcarpress.com/flywheel/manuals/kelsey-printers-guide.pdf|Kelsey]]? Then again, if you are crafty with your hands, you can try [[http://www.instructables.com/id/Build-a-Letterpress-&-Use-It-to-Print-Things/|this]].

Lastly, http://briarpress.org is an excellent resource for all things letterpress. Any questions you have, could probably be answered there much better then on Typophile.
