Hi, Did you ever see the Vialog? It was designed by Werner Schneider (he designed Schneider Antiqua for Berthold) and is used for the Underground and other public transportation in Munich. Even Vienna uses Vialog for signage. All the Vialog fonts are part of the Platinum Collection at Linotype. The special set of signage symbols and arrows is online at www.linotype.com. The pi-fonts are used by different other public transportation systems. Even Pfizer Medicals Germany uses Vialog for their house style. Enclosed the PDF with all the Vialog signs. Great for signage.
