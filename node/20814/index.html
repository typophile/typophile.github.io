I don’t think these ‘dotted o’s’ are the usual ones you may have seen, which would probably be Mark Simonson’s ‘Felt Tip Roman’. (Notice the ‘a’ is the wrong style, for starters.) I also just scanned through the 300 Handwritten Fonts from the ClickArt CD, and this one isn’t one of them. Dennis’s (fontplayer) suggestion of searching Font Garden is not a bad idea, because it looks like it’s a home-made font from personal printing. It’s pretty likely, as Patty’s FAQ link suggests, that this could be a one-of-a-kind custom font, or something you may never find among all the thousands of free fonts like this that are at all the free font download sites.

My suggestion as usual in these cases is to find something with a similar feel, and use that, because you may never find the original.
