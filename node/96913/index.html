Would you elaborate on the use for this? Size it will appear? Feeling you're trying to convey? The 'retro' idea is coming across, for sure. Some nice curves in there too. You could get better legibility from your r if you gave it a little more thick/thin contrast. The -lab- doesn't have as much sense of a continuous pen stroke as the rest—that could be ok. Overall the strokes might benefit from standardizing their angle and weight, and the rhythm between them. Or you could go the opposite direction and push the hand-drawn quality, maybe by accentuating the different heights of the ascenders.
