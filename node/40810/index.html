You should shop around at Hoefler & Frere-Jones. They are bursting with classy modern faces. Their website showcases them in a nice way as well that may help you see qualities in typefaces that may not be apparent at first.

2008-01-11 19:42:02 • Chris Rugen
Might I suggest Nicholas, Semibold or Bold, in capitals, for the destination? It’s a display face with the contrast and fine detail necessary to suggest the upscale, but enough personality for the individual destinations.
