2007-03-12 03:23:07 • Addison Hall
It’s metal Linotype Janson. The oddness of the italic f’s is characteristic of that technology, which did not allow any part of letters to extend past the boundaries of the matrix (unlike Monotype).
2007-03-12 17:14:51 • raph

That seems to be absolutely right. I have a Linotype catalog from the mid-30’s and sure enough, the italics match your samples. It does show that they had ‘one-letter italics’ that included a more conventional italic ‘f’, but the basic italic is the same as your samples.
