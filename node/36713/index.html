Personal Letterform Logo

September 2, 2007 • iamcoel

As part of my studies I needed to create a personal logo using only my initals (C.M). This was never going to be an easy task. But I am reasonably happy with what I came up with.

I think if I looked long enough I would be able to see some faults with the design, but for the time being it will stay as it is (I’m not going into business any time soon I don’t thin).

I am just wondering what might be some suggestions for slight/major alterations and just general thoughts on what I have designed.

Thanks for any thoughts.

Where the thick strokes overlap at the top of the ‘C’ looks very messy to me. Try thinning out the stroke that leads into the ‘M’ so the ‘C’ will read better.
