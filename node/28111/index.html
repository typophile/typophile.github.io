Another acquaintance has asked me to ID this specimen. It would seem to be Futura, but he swears there are subtle differences (perfect uniformity of stroke width, equal size of arms/crossbars of the F and E, perfect symmetry of the S and the angle at which the stroke terminates, etc.). He also seems convinced that this specimen does not correspond to any currently available weight of Futura. It is a vintage US Army specimen from the 1950s or thereabouts. Did the Army have its own version of Futura that differs from commercial versions, or are these differences the result of rough printing with old metal type?

There are always going to be differences in appearance between metal type, lino, and digital. This is certainly Futura, the closest I can find is Futura Heavy.
