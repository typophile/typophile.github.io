New Bold Monday update: Nitti 4.0 – now with italics

August 22, 2012 • boldmonday

We’re happy to announce a new version of Nitti! We’ve (finally) added italics to this mighty popular family, and Pieter took the opportunity to tweak and improve many details in the roman styles as well.

Now, with five weights, and support for Latin, Cyrillic and Greek this is our most complete typeface family yet. And we’re offering a temporary 25% discount on all styles of Nitti to celebrate its release! More info: http://www.boldmonday.com/en/nitti
