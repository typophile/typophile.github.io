2007-10-22 13:06:16 • ghurman

If you want the TV shape I would suggest smooth points at the sides and top/bottom, and corner points in the corners. If the latter are too sharp, change to smooth points with the control points very close to the point.

I doubt you will find a filter for this, but you should be able to do a decent job with a lot of guides to align your control points correctly.
