The Oldtype "Psalterium" Font, by Alter Littera (AL).

May 19, 2012 • Alter Littera

Short Description: A clean, smooth adaptation of the gothic types used by Johann Fust and Peter Schöffer in their famous Mainz Psalter (Psalterium Moguntinum) of 1457. Font Details: [[http://www.alterlittera.com/al_htm/oldtype/psalterium.htm|Font Page]] at [[http://www.alterlittera.com|Alter Littera’s website]].
