I'm with the Brand.

January 19, 2009 • cncasabi

What drew me to this cover initially, was literally what the title read, “I’m with the brand”. Then after taking a closer look I realized the actual letters were arranged and formed by these objects which were whited-out while the title popped out from the red background. Anyways, I thought the way the images created the illusion of type was appealing and the way the images represented the “brand products” corresponded well with the book’s plot.
