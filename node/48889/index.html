The closest I can find to this is Canada Type’s [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/canadatype/sterling-script/|Sterling Script]] which is a revival of Stephenson Blake’s “Youthline”. At least I think the lower case is a very close match.

I actually think your lettering was done by hand, but it seems very close to the style of Youthline and other copperplate scripts, but in a more condensed form. The initials are the things that I cannot match anywhere, and to me this gives weight to the hand-drawn theory.
