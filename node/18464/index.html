Sofa sans: cosy but simple

March 11, 2006 • Sebastian Nagel

Dear sebilar-suffering forum readers,

this is the third part of Sofa super-family, a sans serif-version of the original Sofa design with heavy serifs.

There is not too much to say about it, mainly I have clipped serifes away and have redesigned the now-nude line endings. This was done for all except ligatures and some glyphs I’ve forgotten so far. Spacing probably is too loose at the moment.

Any comments on mistakes I’ve made when removing the serifs, and general annotations are appreciated.

Sebilar
