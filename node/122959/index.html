I am not clear as to whether you are looking for a rough or smooth base font to uses as a basis for redrawing. For a rough font consider Chancellor http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/pintassilgo/chancellor/ Also Berliner Grotesk BQ Med http://www.linotype.com/261314/BerlinerGrotesk-family.html http://www.fonts.com/font/berthold/berliner-grotesk-bq/volume?SiteId=15 would work. EDITED ADDITIONAL INFO: This style of lettering seems have originated with Lucian Bernhard’s Sachplakat –object poster or Plakatsil – lettering. From about 1908 onwards versions were produced by German type makers such as Heinz Hoffmann, who designed the Block fonts http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/berthold/block-bq/ Don
