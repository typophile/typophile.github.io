If I understood you correctly, you are basically looking for an italic f with descender. There are plenty of options. The one you sketched looks rather light and maybe from a low-contrast sans-serif family. Here is the f from Facit Italic, just as one reference: http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/justanotherfoundry/facit/italic/glyphs/280414/71 Note that most fonts also have a character called ‘latin small letter f with hook’, which can look similar, or even identical: http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/justanotherfoundry/facit/italic/glyphs/280414/101 It was mainly used as a currency symbol for the Dutch guilder.
