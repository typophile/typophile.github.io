Hi, I was hoping could help me ID this serif font form an old Diet Coke logo. Thanks!

Appears to be garden-variety Times New Roman Bold:

http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/mti/times-new-roman/

Your sample most likely predates the digital type era, so some details (for instance, the angle of the stroke end on the lower curve of the C and the upper serif on the k) won’t be a spot-on match.
