2010-08-14 12:35:48 • Ramiro Espinoza

For the Typophiles who don’t read Dutch:

The top part of the pole (that pink thing) denotes the location (the neighbourhood, I guess). The set of arrows below that (orange) are the directions to other neighbourhoods in Antwerp. And the lower batch of arrows (gray) indicate the main attractions of the neighbourhood where the pole is placed. And on certain locations there is an extra shield with pertinent info. The system is modular to facilitate adding extra info and directions.

(In excerpt, of course)
