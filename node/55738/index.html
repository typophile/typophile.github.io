The old Font Bank collection included some extra heavy versions of familiar type faces and [[http://www.buyfonts.com/value_for_money/bianca.htm|Bianca]] was their name for what looked like a very heavy high x-height version of Bembo.

In a more general answer to your question about heavy weight serifs, if you don’t want Clarendon styles, avoid slabs, but any other Black versions of serif fonts might be worth looking at. For example,the Black version of [[http://new.myfonts.com/fonts/adobe/itc-galliard/black/|Galliard]] is pretty attractive, in my opinion. There are many others to look at though.
