I don’t know this…are there names for the various descenders for the letter ‘g’?

You know, one looks more or less like a ‘j’ and many others look like an upside down ‘q’ (or something like that)…

For the whole letter g if it has two counters (closed or almost closed) is called double storey, the other type is referred to as single storey. Tim
2005-09-26 11:59:41 • timd
The bottom stroke of a single storey g is often called a tail. The bottom part of a double storey g is often refered to as its lower bowl.
