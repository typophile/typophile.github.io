Anybody know if there is a postcript or opentype version available of a font commonly known as Prisma, I have a TT version which wont outline in illustrator. The typeface is made up of four geometric thin lines and it looks like it is based around Futura or similar and as far as I can tell ther is only a caps version.

The only versions I could find were also TTF. I think this font was used as a film typeface. I show a number of old scans as well as some current multi-line fonts in the Lined Fonts ID Guide. That might help you find an alternative. Sources are listed in the Guide.

Another possbility, which may not work well, is to convert the font from TTF to Type1. I say it may not work well, because the TT outlines may not be good quality, and that would make ‘garbage in- garbage out’ situation.
