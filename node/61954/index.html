'Infinity' ambigram

September 14, 2009 • mattmc

This ambigram didn't have any specific purpose, more of a personal challenge. I used Bodoni as the basis. I wanted to make a different amibgram than a blackletter or script, since they seem to be the most forgiving to the form. I thought a modern serif could work & look somewhat unique.
