Magazine Redesign and Type

June 26, 2009 • BraggMedia

My next big project is assisting with a design overhaul for a city magazine. Their current stock of typefaces are difficult to work with and, not to mention, to read.

I’m looking for type for the following elements: • Body copy: I’ve been attracted to Hoeffler’s Chronicle Text and Sentinel

• Headlines: I would like to narrow down two faces: a serif and sans-serif

• Infobox copy/Captions/Photo Credits: I would like to use a sans serif condensed font. Topping my list: Frutiger, Myriad, Gill Sans

I’d love some feedback. If anyone has any resources, books, advice, tips or tricks, please send them my way! You can e-mail me at heather@braggmedia.com.
