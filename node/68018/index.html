2010-03-04 21:34:22 • mike_duggan
The still is from “The Trouble With Harry” (1955), directed by Alfred Hitchcock. The character, Mrs. Wigg, is not the artist who painted the pictures. She’s trying to sell them at a roadside stand for one of the other characters, Sam Marlowe (played by John Forsythe), who is the artist. (Who actually painted the pictures for the movie is another question.)
