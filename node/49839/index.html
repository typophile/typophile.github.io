Independent Truck Company

September 25, 2008 • Nick Sherman

This typeface been used by the Independent Truck Company since at least 1978.

I always figured it was hand-done because I had only seen it used in their logo, but I just noticed it in use on some of their packaging as well, which led me to believe it’s a typeface.

I’ve only seen it used in all-caps.

Any ideas?
