I'm really touched how much everyone liked typecon. It is truly amazing how this conference and organization has grown. For those of you who weren't at the first 2, (I don't think anyone here except maybe Kent has,) you wouldn't believe it. It used to be about 30 people gathered in a room talking about type, and then going out for Chinese food at a groovy Polynesian resturant. Since this is my first foray into Typophile, I'd like to say hello to everyone! I think that these forums are an excellent way to increase awarness and spread the good word about type. Again, I'd like to add my 2 cents about how good TypeCon 2003 was. My head's still spinning! Hello to all, and especially to Hrant, who doesn't deserve all the negative attention he receives.
TONAAAAYYYYY!!! I remember the golden days of Typecon! Still I am overwhelmed by what it has become. This conference is like crack for me and I cannot get enough of it! It was such a delight to see so many of you in my hometown and I'm very much looking forward to seeing you all in SF if not sooner! Stuart :D
