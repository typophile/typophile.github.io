Linotype's new Avenir NEXT, redesigned by Adrian Frutiger (designer of the original Avenir) and Akira Kobayashi!

http://www.linotype.com/85940/avenirnext-family.html?PHPSESSID=f84294bd75a2274840b189cb6d7d5e88

This is like Futura, but Adrian Frutiger-ized... so you have two storey a's instead of Renner's single storey a's. Plus, because of OpenType, Frutiger has made available never-seen-before Avenir OldStyle Figures and Small Caps as options.

This would make your CV rock. (Avenir is the official typeface of the city of Amsterdam, among other things. Those Dutch designers know a thing or two about type)

Kabel and Nobel (and Avant Garde) are showing their age much
more than Futura - as Futura has more timeless proportions. But I agree
that Futura is overused. Avenir is cropping up in a lot more
places too, but that's not necessarily a bad thing.

Check out Gotham. Gotham's ascenders aren't so tall, which
makes it less fussy to work with and gives a slightly more
"friendly" flavor. This also makes Gotham's condensed
weights more natural. Futura Condensed has always made me
queasy.
