Hello from Trek in Waterloo, Wisconsin. I have some additional info that you may find interesting:

The wordmark in the decals above appeared on the very first Trek steel bicycle frames that were hand-built in Waterloo in 1976. Over the next several years this type treatment could be found on the top tube and seat tube decals, some seat stay cap wraparounds and the head tube badge (without the shadow). This of course predates the digitization of Premier Shaded by Eisner+Flake. However, Premier Shaded was originally designed by British typographer Colin Brignall for Letraset transfer sheets in 1969 or 1970 (depending on who you ask).

The comparison below shows that while the Trek wordmark was inspired greatly by Premier Shaded, it was in fact customized type meant to soften some of the art deco qualities found in its muse:
