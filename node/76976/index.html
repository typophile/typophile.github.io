These are named Braceleft and Braceright in Unicode. The brackets shown in Riccardo's post are a stylistic variant of the editorial (square) Bracket, AFAIK. I recall first becoming aware of them in some of Goudy's fonts, seen in old printed matter. However, I don't believe that any digital Goudy revivals have incorporated that effect into the bracket glyph.
