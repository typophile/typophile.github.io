I think the idea of obliquing the Roman 's' is a key. If you do that with a heavy weight of [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/adobe/itc-century/|ITC Century]] (or adding stroke thickness might be even better), and use the Italic for the rest of the letters I think it's very similar. Century has the flat top serifs you need. - Mike Yanega
