I suppose this is custom designed/hand lettered. If you need a
close match to go with it I would suggest (as Mike pointed out):

Baker Signet as a companion roman text face.
Mercator or Marigold as a companion chancery script.
Oxford as a companion semi-uncial.

If you need a complete family of fonts, you could consider ITC Tiepolo.

My own recommendations would be two of Bouwsma's calligraphic faces: Mantegna Italic and Polenta (formerly Donatello) Black Italic.

These were the closest I saw in running through the Calligraphic scripts in Part 4 of the Script Font ID Guide. Polenta has a lighter weight too.
