The use and abuse of Eurostile/Microgramma in science fiction is very well documented, see http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Microgramma_(typeface).

IMHO The Andromeda Strain is the film where the face performs better, numbering endless corridors that add to the claustrophobia in some scenes.

If you are looking for less artistic uses, the regular weight version is used in the gauges of several cars. I have it in my Honda Civic and have no complaint (no complaints about the rest of the car either).
