2008-03-20 18:38:37 • Hofweber

This isn’t Gotham.

Colors has used a variety of sans-serifs over the years: Franklink Gothic back in the Tibor Kalman years, and Avenir more recently. In fact, I believe that’s Avenir on the cover in your “colors20” sample.

The body copy in 22 is a humanist sans of recent vintage: it’s not FF Kievit, but it’s in that ballpark.
