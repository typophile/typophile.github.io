These are letters made by sign companies for use by small businesses. They are not based on a font as such. The 3d molding gives stability to plastic letters. I can’t find any digital fonts that both match the narrow constructivist/gas-pipe design of the letterforms and the embossed effect. The closest 3d fonts where both the S and the R match this design are Prismatiq JNL http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/jnlevine/prismatiq/ and Theatrics JNL http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/jnlevine/theatrics/ but both are too wide. Non prismatic/embossed fonts with generally matching letterforms and narrower width include Linotype Case Study No One http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/linotype/case-study-no-one-lt/ Fonts with closer match to the width but some mismatch in S or R include Komu A http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/dizajndesign/komu/ Dharma Gothic Bold http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/flat-it/dharma-gothic/ VALUCO Base which also has an embossed version VALUCO Midtone http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/aesthetic-apparatus/valuco/ Don
