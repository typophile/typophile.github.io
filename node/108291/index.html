The A&W typeface is very late 1950s when “tipsy” or “bouncy” serifs were the height of fashion. A good fit with the A&W retro values image. AFAIK A&W is not going to make it available to the public. If you want something with a similar treatment of the lettering you could try a couple of fonts by Alex Kaczun, Beatnick: http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/typeinnovations/beatnik/ or Beatnick Barbie: http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/typeinnovations/beatnik-barbie/ For a more funky version try Alex Kaczun’s Hippyfreak: http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/typeinnovations/hippyfreak/ Funky versions from Filmotype, Orlando: http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/filmotype/orlando/ Panama: http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/filmotype/panama/ Parade: http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/filmotype/parade/ Another funky version, Fontdinerdotcom Loungy: http://www.dafont.com/fontdinerdotcom-lou.font Don
