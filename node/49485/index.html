In general it’s best to hire a [[lettering]] artist to get this flavor, but if a typeface is required here are a few decent options that emulate the look:

FF Prater FF Bokka Futuramano Blockhead Mundenge Rock Quickstep Afrodite FF Amoeba Smile Jacoby Cafeteria Linotype Aspect ITC Coolman

See also Hand Drawn Serifs and Spam Lettering by Linzie Hunter.
