yes, with K.L.help. i copy his solution to keep it here for anybody else:

You need to do two things – (1) Go to the OpenType Panel (which contains the feature code) and delete all the class definitions from the bottom-right part. (2) Go to FontLab Options/Preferences (depends on whether you use Windows or Mac), click through the submenus “General Options” and “Unicode and OpenType” – and therein activate both options: “Add all glyph classes […]” and “Do not add metrics classes”

Now go to the OpenType Panel again and compile. Over here this worked well without error message.
