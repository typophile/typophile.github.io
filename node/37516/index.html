It's my understanding that Linotype's strategy with the "Platinum Editions" is that they only be sold as packaged families. Hopefully [[Dan Reynolds|Dan]] will stop by and confirm or deny this. From a font seller's perspective I understand the desire to sell packages over singles. It makes a lot of sense both financially and logistically. And the customer often benefits as well in the long run. The rub: when the packages are this large and above a certain price point it becomes prohibitive to a big chunk of the buying population. I think a good compromise is to offer usable subsets of the full family.
