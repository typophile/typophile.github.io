What is it for? A book? Does it have to be a space saving typeface? Do you want to stay in the same area? humanist, venetian e?

With big typographical contrast a lot of typefaces would work, I mean contrast like light/bold, condensed/broad, colour, white space. For textfaces you could start looking in the humanist department – how about Quadraat, or Tisa? Or check out this fontlist for suitable typefaces: http://www.fontshop.com/fontlist/applications/book_text_recommended_alternatives/
