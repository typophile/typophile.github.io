MyFonts opens WhatTheFont Guru Forum

December 21, 2004 • twardoch

MyFonts has launched the WhatTheFont Guru Forum, a companion to the site's popular online font identifier WhatTheFont. Users can upload graphics to the WhatTheFont system which automatically finds the closest match in MyFonts' database of 39,000 fonts. If the machine fails, the image can be automatically forwarded to the new comfortable forum where a group of volunteers is regularly helping identify the fonts in question.

Due to problems in maintaining the old forum software, MyFonts has closed the remaining online discussion forums and directs participants to the excellent Typophile forums as an alternative.
