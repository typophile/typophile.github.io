Person or font?

November 8, 2008 • ill sans

I just saw last night’s ‘Have I got news from you’ & I was laughing out loud at a story in the connections round where a radio presenter was said to have confused a font name (Helvetica bold) for a person’s name. Searching the internet to read more about this, I came across an article that stated ‘Helvetica Bold’ was actually the listener’s nickname which kind of burst my bubble of anyone being that ignorant about what must be one of the most used & known fonts. But it’s still fun to watch & I wanted to share it nonetheless… http://blogs.manchestereveningnews.co.uk/ianwylie/2008/11/helvetica_bold.html
Sometimes, well I try to restrict myself, I have a nickname “garamond italic”. It’s certainly not that common :-)
