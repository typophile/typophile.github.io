2004-01-22 16:04:33 • bowfinpw
OK. Here is Grace replicated in Delphian Open, which I converted to outlines and either removed the white parts or moved the black parts together. I also manipulated the outline of the R to pull the tail down and outward a bit. I used a white square to block out the left leg of the A and it even created the same gap between the R and A. If "Squooshing" includes stretching, that's what was needed here (maybe you could do that as vertical "squooshing", but I actually widened the characters). If exactness was needed I would have played much longer, but I think I showed how this was probably done.
