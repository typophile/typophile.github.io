Is this the wrong way to do it?

Not necessarily. FL offers more than one way to put together a MM font; hopefully these will all produce a reliable source for generating instances.

Since the issue here is the kerning data, when you blend the fonts, do any of them have kerning? If so, do the pairs ‘overlap’? (Meaning, a given pair exists in both fonts)

When you open a Metrics Window, you should be able to see the various kerning values for each master. Do they look alright?

I’m using FL 5.0.2 Mac, and from the tests I’ve done with a 2-axis (4 masters) font, I had no problems. No pairs were dropped or added, and the kerning was interpolated.
