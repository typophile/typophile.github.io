Evidently from 1941 onwards IBM was manufacturing typewriters that did have proportional spacing. There were also typewriters available after 1966 that had superscripted th's, nd's, and st's. These typewriters would have been available to the Texas ANG. Also, as has been pointed in numerous places, Killian's wife and son's denunciations don't really mean that much as neither worked with the guy. Folks who DID work with the guy haven't denounced the documents, and according to CBS have confirmed that they were consistent. Furthermore, if you look at the military documents put out by Bush's campaign last February, many of them do have superscripted th's. My point: I don't know if these documents were forged or not, but if they were, the article above doesn't convincingly demonstrate that. *** A more thorough analysis is on the dailykos.com blog at: http://dailykos.com/story/2004/9/10/34914/1603
