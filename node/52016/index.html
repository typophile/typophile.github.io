Most of it is Franklin Gothic Condensed and Extra Condensed. “ROCKY II” is probably an extra heavy film font version of Franklin Gothic that pre-dates the DTP era or it may be custom. The newly released version of ITC Franklin by Font Bureau has an extra heavy weight that may be close.
