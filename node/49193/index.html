Conference on Typography and Literature, Berlin

September 7, 2008 • Stephan Kurz

A german-speaking conference on “Typography and Literature” will be held in Berlin from Sept 25-27. The scope is mostly scholarly, but there is one typographer/designer mentioned in the programme: Friedrich Forssmann. Typophiles from the area might still be interested in the conference. For more see the conference website (in German only).
