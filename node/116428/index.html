“Fashion” is such a weasel word. A cool logo for a target clientele of 50+ with about $100,000 disposable income per year could be quite different from one for 25 year olds with $30,000. Also it could be quite different for a new line of dressy shoes, tailored suits, or Lolita look outfits, to name only a few. If it’s to cover a wide range of categories, this might push the logo towards those that already exist. Anyway these sort of deals can only work where there is a clear understanding of what the client wants to achieve. Only then can someone say I have what it takes to make this work, or nah, I’ll pass on this one. Don
