Hi Chris That serif headline is set in some kind of Old Face Baskerville. Many here at typophile seems to rate the recent versions by Frantisek Storm.

The sans serif strapline is er, something that will doubtlessly be recognised instantly by someone else here; all I can say is it’s not News Gothic, Trade Gothic or Franklin Gothic because of the hook of the ‘r’ and the position of the ear on the ‘g’. It appears to be more contemporary than those, but they’re the nearest I can suggest at this point.
