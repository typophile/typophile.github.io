2007-03-05 18:58:17 • Erik Fleischer
Terry, actually, I think you sort of can plop an st lig into find/replace. I do it with H&FJ's Mercury when trying to add in some of the ligatures that I don't believe are normally integrated. When I copy and paste, for example, an ffi lig into the find/replace box, it comes up as another character (omega is one I've seen often). When I replace all ffi's with omegas, it does just what I want -- probably because Mercury lacks an omega glyph, so the ffi takes its place. But if you were referring to OpenType contextual ligs, then that's another story -- find/replace won't help unless you use it to replace the inputted characters' OpenType formatting with ligatures on or off.
