Surely there will be someone that will tell you to hire a professional designer… ;-) And it will be for a reason, especially if by “my personal photographic website” you mean “my website as a professional photographer”. But, being an almost self taught graphic designer myself, I can understand the need to try by yourself. Two little bits of advice:

  1. Always start with black & white (and, dealing with photography, you can well stick with it).
  2. The easiest and most trivial idea you can tinker with is the identity of form between the “O” (as in phOtO) and the lens (iris/pupil). So you could search for a typeface with a perfectly rounded O, or substitute the O with a circle.

And don’t forget to submit your efforts to the Critique section of the forums! :-)
