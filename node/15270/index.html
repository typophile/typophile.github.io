Tahi rua toru wha!

September 21, 2005 • kris

Which translates to one, two, three, four! from Maori. It is a poster designed by Gus and I to promote our spanking new graphic design business, (here is the website critique.) The letterforms were made by tearing pieces of paper and scanning directly into photoshop with minimal adjustments. Here is a closeup of the 'R'. Vital stats about the poster: A1 (840mm x 594mm) folded to A5 (210mm x 148mm). Printed on Munken Lynx 100gsm. Colours are black and Pantone 485.
