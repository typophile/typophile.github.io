Maybe it's a font including OT ligatures and alternates: The "n": In "everyone" the o_n is a ligature, but in "ends sunday" the 2 n are the same. The "e": Are the same in the first and last in "everyone"; In "loves" and in "end", but are different in "s_e" and "c_r_e" in secret, and in "e_r" in "everyone". The "s": In "secret" is a "s_e" ligature; but the in "ends", "sunday" and "ends" are the same. I don't look handwritten to me, since the connections don't look good in: "ev" in everyone "yo" in everyone "ne" in everyone "es" in loves "ds" in ends
