2009-10-19 03:10:04 • TF22Raptor

I usually call this style Techno, because it has simplified letterforms intended to look futuristic and/or high tech. My complaint is that it has become too much of a cliché. There are actually some ‘techno’ fonts that show an effort to add style and design to the high tech look, but what seems to be used so often are amateur freebie fonts made up mostly of straight lines that look like most of the other tech-look fonts. I suspect these are popular for amateur font designers, because they are relatively simple to design, since curves are kept to a minimum.

The site where I tend to look for more ‘interesting’ techno fonts is T-26, but I also like some of Psy/Ops fonts. That said, ‘Techno’ is not my favorite style, by a long shot.
