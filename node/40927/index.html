Hi Gregorius,

so you want to design a [[typeface]] and produce yourself a [[font]]. And as you want to use it for type-setting your thesis, I assume it’s about a Latin text face – right?

How many time do you have? You have to be aware of the fact that creating a font will take a very long time, at least if you’re doing it for the first time, and if you want the outcome to be reasonably good and usable.

Given that setting an academic paper usually requires more than just one weight (e.g. Roman, Italic, Bold, maybe Small Caps, several sets of figures, …), I strongly recommend to rethink your plan. You’ll be better off with a little less ambitious aim. You could design a nice display face for titlings/headings, for example.

To answer your question – have a look at these older threads: Advice for a beginner Type Design tutorial anywhere? – How to create fonts? What books are there on type design? and of course the [[How-To]] section in the Typophile Wiki. F
