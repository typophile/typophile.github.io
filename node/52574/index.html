The whole object of this exercise is for me to encounter and overcome problems. As it is Christmas-time, and we are living in a credit crunch, I can’t dash out and buy all the typographical technical guides that I would like - but I think that’s a good thing. I’ve always been better at getting stuck in and getting my hands dirty with stuff…

Dropping a few more letters in has galvanised a couple of thoughts that I have. I love Gotham Rounded - http://www.typography.com/fonts/font_overview.php?productLineID=100030 - and I’ve frequently thought (as mentioned before) that I might round some of this font off. I really think it could be quite an interesting typeface if select parts were squared and others rounded. That thought has really been cemented in my mind now, having had a pop at the ‘r’ (which I like) and the ‘f’ (which I don’t like at all). This typeface ‘squared’ also looks like a lot of other ones… and I’d like something distinctive, so the next update I hope may be a departure!
