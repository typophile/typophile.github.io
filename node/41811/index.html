Prof. R. K. Joshi (1936-2008)

February 7, 2008 • Uli

Prof. Joshi, who was a calligrapher of Indian scripts and who also made the Mangal Devanagari default/fall-back font for MS Windows, died on 5th February. For details read http://www.cdacmumbai.in/index.php/cdacmumbai/prof_r_k_joshi

By all accounts a remarkable personality, Prof R K Joshi was one of a small group of inspirational design educators of the old school, in India. Friends who studied under him at the IDC in Mumbai refer to him with a mixture of awe, near reverence and deep affection. His enduring legacy would probably be the many designers he trained over the years, who now work across the country in diverse languages and scripts.
