Four fresh fonts for freelance designers...

August 8, 2009 • Engzell

… and all the other designers of course. I have listed Four stylish fonts i fished up from the design flood.

Typography is literally alpha and omega when designing and most designers are constantly looking for new fresh fonts, either as inspiration or for a specific project.

Therefore i have gathered together a gang of stylish way to reproduce letters and other characters.

Lace (HelloHikimori Exclusive). HelloHikimori are an award winning studio from Paris, France.

Chunk is an ultra-bold slab serif typeface that is reminiscent of old American Western woodcuts, broadsides, and newspaper headlines.

Even the legendary “quick brown fox” looks lovely in Squab.

Recommended for litho and high quality digital print.

I have included a font from the site [[http://www.theleagueofmoveabletype.com|The League of Movepagetext Able Type]] is an initiative of the free, open source fonts well worth to check out!
