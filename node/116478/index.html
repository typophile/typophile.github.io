Looks like a wood type, highly condensed slab serif. Illustrator identifies the missing font as Collier Heading Plain. Searches yielded no result, so it doesn’t look like it came from a major foundry.

It looks like OPTI Carmi Five Caps by OPTI/Castcraft OPTI is a known font-forgery company and they almost always renamed the fonts they copied/digitized.

If you go to the OPTI Archive, Letter “C” here: http://opti.netii.net/fonts-c/index.htm you can see the following note bellow “Carmi Five Caps”:

Original is Collier
and this is further indication it's the font used on your sample ;)
