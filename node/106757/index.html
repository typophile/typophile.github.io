Death Gate Cycle/Bantam Books 1990s cover fonts

October 14, 2013 • immora-com

Primary interest is in the font used for the titles on The Death Gate Cycle book covers published 1990-1994 by Bantam Books under their Spectra division. Credits say that the book designs were by Robin Hessel-Hoffmann. I'm not sure if they used the font for any other books, if it's a house font, or what. No luck with Whatthefont and Identifont. I isolated the titles only portions from two books for this sample: The attachments below: the top seven are from each of the actual paperback covers, and an additional font is visible in them. Note that the book's title (ex. "DRAGON WING") is not the same font as the serif style in the top blurb and author names... I'd be curious to know that font, but most interested in the one used in the actual titles. On the hardcover versions, they appear stretched horizontally compared to the paperbacks (not wider kerning/space between letters-- see 1DragonWing-HC-Flap and 3-FireSea-HC-Spine for what I mean) which is part of why I think it's a font and not drawn (plus the letters don't have variations as if hand-drawn).
