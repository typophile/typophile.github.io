[[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/celebrity/parisian-ornamentals|Parisian Ornamental]] is match. Quoting description text on MyFonts:

Beautiful, richly ornamented shadowed letters in the Empire fashion, similar to the fonts of the Parisian type founder J. Gille', cut around 1810.
Released in 2010 by Celebrity Fontz Jose Jimenez, I really doubt this could be the only digital version ever done.

[[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/celebrity/parisian-ornamentals|Parisian Ornamental]] is a match. Quoting description text on MyFonts:

Beautiful, richly ornamented shadowed letters in the Empire fashion, similar to the fonts of the Parisian type founder J. Gillé, cut around 1810.
Released in 2010 by Celebrity Fontz Jose Jimenez, I really doubt this could be the only digital version ever done.

EDIT: 2 minutes later. [[http://moorstation.org/typoasis/designers/steffmann/samples/l/lettres.htm|Lettres Ombrées Ornées]] by Dieter Steffmann, his digitization originally designed by Foundry J. Gillé, 1820.
