There is a three-line typeface called ‘Hotline’ that is shown in the Solo ‘Art Deco Display Alphabets’ book, published by Dover. It matches your sample for the way the letters are formed and lined (unlike Prisma, by the way), just that it has three lines and not four. I could not find a digital font with this name, however, so if it exists, the name has been changed (most likely).
