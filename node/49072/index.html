DPI is “dots-per-inch” which can be converted to dots per mm, DPmm.

Measure the 1600 pixels of your screen in ‘real world’ mm.

Divide the measured mm by 1600 to reach DPmm.

There are 72 points per inch, or 2.835 mm per point.
