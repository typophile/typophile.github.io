Type in Zurich?

June 25, 2006 • mili

I’m going to spend a few days in Zurich soon, and was wondering if anyone can give me some tips of interesting places type or/and design wise? Any good bookshops with a good English section (my German is not too good)? Beautiful type on the wall?

Any ideas would be gratefully received.

http://www.daltonmaag.com/news/43.html - if its really soon, this would be a possibility. but dont expect too much, its a very small exhibition - near tram station limmatplatz, tram nr. 4 and 13.

http://www.museum-gestaltung.ch/exhibitions/program.html - there’s an exhibition on chinese typography in the poster room at the moment, and a small one about haettenschweiler in front of the museum. tram nr. 4 and 13, tram station “museum für gestaltung”, its 5 mins footwalk from the first one.

there’s the bookshop “orell füssli krauthammer” at marktgasse 12 in the niederdorf (oldtown of zurich), its the biggest art books store here i know. not that many books specialised on type, but some. its near the station ratshaus, accessible with tram nr. 4 and 15. if you want english books without design context, there’s a “orell füssli english bookshop” at the bahnhofstrasse right near rennweg.

i dont have too many more ideas at the moment.. zurich is beautiful in the summer, take the tram 4 till the station “tiefenbrunnen” and walk all around the lake till you get to the “sukkulentensammlung” (a collection of cactii and so on), you can go even further but thats enough i think :)

do not hesitate to ask further questions, and i’ll try to find some locations with interesting type on the wall.
