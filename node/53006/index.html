The "Double-O" ligature

December 29, 2008 • Glenn Kramer

Does anyone know of any logos or another clever use of the “double-O” ligature? The only well-known logo that I can think of is for Bloomingdale’s. I have attached the logo to this post.

I would like to use the double-O ligature in the title of a publication, but feedback from someone said that overlapping O’s reminded her of unity, or gender symbols (male and female).

I think the double-o ligature looks classy and is reminiscent of the Arts & Crafts movement as well as the Art Nouveau style.

What are your thoughts?
