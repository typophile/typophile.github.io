Fonts with a nice K

January 12, 2013 • knitsofacto

I find the K in most web fonts to be one of the least attractive letters yet have a blog with a name beginning with a K. I am looking to change my current title font, Pupcat, to something with a similar retro feel but a nicer k, and something that will look good used alongside Trebuchet. So far the only possible font I have identified is Bistro Script. I would really welcome some other suggestions. Thank you.
I am not sure if these would suit your blog (though I think so), but having re-read a recommendation from Hrant elsewhere on the site, the Ks (upper and lower) in [[http://www.fontshop.com/fonts/downloads/fontfont/ff_pitu_pro/|FF Pitu]] are beautiful! I am also a big fan of Monokrom’s designs, [[https://monokrom.no/fonts/faunus|Faunus]] and [[https://monokrom.no/fonts/satyr|Satyr]]. Finally, a little less conventional, with a slant to the left, I really like [[http://www.fontshop.com/fonts/designer/mark_fromberg/|Canary by Mark Frömberg]].
