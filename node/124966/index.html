Fordscript does not match the logo. Fordscript is a clumsy version of M.F. Benton’s Commercial Script of 1908. The main original feature of Fordscript is the r. Fordscript is closer to the Bitstream digital version in width to height ratio etc than to the versions produced by Linotype and others. Don
