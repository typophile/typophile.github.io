Directions – ideas?

February 2, 2006 • stephen_k

I am working on a document that involves copy that is basically just directions written down how you would say them eg Go to the bottom of x road, turn left. Take a right along the enbankment etc.

I dont want to just have it as an ordinary paragraph and as there is no higherarchy for headers/pull quotes etc im looking for other ideas to make it more interesing?

I was thinking along the lines of using // instead of full stops or dingbat arrows?

Anyone done anything similar or any ideas?

Depending on the use to which the document is to be put you could do a rally roadbook, if it slightly less important to have the directions as useable as that, you could do a ‘map’ with the directions following the roads from the top left corner to bottom right. Tim
