Here’s a little NY bookstore tour. Start at the new Taschen bookstore on Greene just south of Prince. 2007-12-06 17:26:57 • pattyfab

Its really a shame that Untitled closed down. But some of the general book shops in the city do have decent selections devoted to the area of design and typography.

The Strand MoMA (either Soho or uptown) Zakka Giant Robot Cooper-Hewitt New Museum (haven’t been yet) Kinokuniya (everything is 50% off at the original location, new store just opened across Bryant Park) St. Mark’s Bookshop Rand McNally Skyline bookstore (great for used type specimen books and annuals)

Hope you have a wonderful time in New York! If you can you should see if its possible to set up a meeting at the TDC (don’t think any books for sale, but their collection is incredible)
