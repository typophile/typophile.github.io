A little browsing in the Jaspert 'Encyclopaedia of Type Faces' comes up with an Italian Sans with the same letterform structure called 'Forma', designed by a team led by Aldo Novarese for Niebolo in the mid-to-late-60's. I think this is the origin of 'Formula'/'Fortana', even though an Extrabold version is not shown in the samples. Nice of them not to change the name too much, isn't it? - Mike Yanega
