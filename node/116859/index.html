The curve back on the ascender is a feature found, in modified form, on the h for Troubadour http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/rmu/troubadour-pro/ and Wagner Script http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/canadatype/wagner-script/ but “Grossdeutschland” is a much more cursive script. Troubadour dates from 1926 (Wagner & Schmidt). Some variations: Butterfly (Schriftguss), Donatello (Nebiolo), Hertha (Berling) [Info. from Jaspert’s Type Encyclopedia.] Any suggestions? Don
