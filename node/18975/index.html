Bert, what someone posts on their own site is their own business, and if they get sued because of it, that is a possible consequence they presumably acknowledge (Uli acknowledges it explicitly). Posting material to a public forum is a different matter, though, because then the owners of that forum may be sued as the publishers of that material. As an example, a few years ago, ATypI was sued because of a remark made by one member in the public archives of the member discussion list. ATypI hadn't written the remarks, hadn't endorsed the remarks, hadn't in fact, done anything other than made public the archive in which the remark appeared. If Uli wants to pick fights with Berthold on his own website, that is his business, but he shouldn't be putting other people at risk in the process. His post is a deliberate taunt for Berthold to sue him, and by publishing the post on Typophile he is effectively inviting Berthold to sue Typophile also. The Typophile moderators would be showing nothing but due diligence in deleting this thread.
