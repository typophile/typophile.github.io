Oswald Spengler was a very famous political author in Germany, born 1880 and 1936 he passed the way.

“Der Untergang des Abendlandes” (engish title: Decline Of The West) was, what you would call, a bestseller. First edition 1919, last one anywhere in the 30ies. (And some more after Germanys surrender ‘45).

I know all his books and hang me, but I am sure: This title shown in the picture, is a fake. The german word “Untergang” is written with an U at the beginning. Yes, sometimes they tried to give a title a kind of latin touch, but I do not believe to the V instead of the U in the 1920ies. Another thing are the different capital R at the end of the lines. I do not believe its a standard lead letter font with three versions in it.

I guess its changed later electronically. The copyright of UDA was (or better: is) at Beck’sche Verlagsbuchhaltung, Munich. The shown title does not fit to them.
