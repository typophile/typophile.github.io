InDesign Pagenumbering pullout?

March 8, 2005 • P_P

Im trying to work out how to pull out pages from a InDesign CS document and keep the pagenumbers. So if the document is 100 pages long and the pages are numbered and I pull out page 20 to 40 and save it as a new document and I still want the pages to be numbered from 20 to 40, how do I do it?
Go to the Pages palette. In the flyout menu, select Numbering and Section Options. Select the button for Start Page Numbering At: and enter the page number that you want the document to begin with.
