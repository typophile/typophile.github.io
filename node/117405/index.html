This is a 19th century font patented by Herman Ihlenburg on October 6, 1891 and sold by MacKellar, Smiths Jordan, and other foundries as Columbian. Digital versions, compiled by the Solopedia, include P22 Victorian Swash http://www.fonts.com/font/p22/p22-victorian/swash and http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/p22/victorian-gothic/ Glorietta (Solotype), Trinigan FG (Fontgrube), Goodfellow (Scriptorium), Bluegum Forest (Tris Nguyen, corrected by MEG).
