I’m 99.9% sure it’s completely custom. Ignition Print (the company that created the campaign) will have been responsible for localising the posters for the international market, and therefore will have had to adapt the logo and copy as appropriate. If you examine the Russian poster, most of the Cyrillic characters have been adapted from the Roman ones in the original logo.

If someone else knows and can prove otherwise, I’ll be convinced. Until then I stand by the not-a-font diagnosis.
