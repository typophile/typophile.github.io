April 1, 2014 • CeraJean

![](/images/old-images/font query_5307.png) I am trying to locate this font. It was used by my church for this December, 1990 bulletin. I can find similar fonts, but not all the letters and numbers match up. The numbers 0 and 2 and letters A, G, R, C, and K seem to never match up. I have been searching font sites for over a year without any luck. Any help would be appreciated.
Judging from the year and the look & feel, this is more likely a custom OEM raster font stored in some specific dot-matrix printer model of the era. Chances are you will not find an exact match in a modern vector font format (i.e. TTF / OTF).
