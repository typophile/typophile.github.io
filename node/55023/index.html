So I stumbled upon this charming birth announcement and I thought it was a nice example of bad typography. First it uses the font Papyrus, which alone doesn’t completely ruin this card. But the Papyrus announcement is printed on Mylar which is then overlayed onto a Curlz-like font reading “Merry Christmas.” The maker of this card though they were being clever in announcing the birth of their child and wishing their family and friends merry Christmas in the same card.

We learned in typography that type should be used to cleanly and clearly relay a message. The two fonts do not go together at all and to overlay them just makes the card messy and hard to read. What killed this card is the overlaying of two very ugly fonts to convey to completely different messages.
