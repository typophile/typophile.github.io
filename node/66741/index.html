Don’t spend too much time trying to unravel marketing copy. The secret of Trade Gothic’s success is that it was a refinement/expansion of one of the best typefaces of the Twentieth Century—News Gothic, which was designed by a man who spent years refining the best American Gothics of the Nineteenth Century.
