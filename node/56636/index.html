Overwhelmed at which school to choose!

April 5, 2009 • spamfan

Hi there.

Currently, I’m in the midst of choosing which school to go to for graphic design. So far, I got into CCA in SF, Art Center College of Design in Pasadena, Pratt Institute in Brooklyn, and School of Visual Arts in NYC. I didn’t think I would get into any, so this is a great surprise.

Part of me is leaning towards Art Center, but they haven’t sent me any financial aid/scholarship information and want me to wait a month to 6 weeks. And, that would be past my deadline for the other schools which is May 1st. I have asked my design teachers for their advice, and when they found out I got into Art Center and CCA, they suggested Art Center. But now, I have two more schools to consider, Pratt and SVA which was not part of the discussion since I found out this week. My dream was to go to SVA and the fact that I got in made me happy. I still have to wait for financial aid info from Pratt. So, I have a lot to decide. My mentor told me that whatever school I choose, I’ve chosen great programs and that I would prosper in the program. Currently, I’m experiencing an emotional rollercoaster b/c I’ve waited for so long for this to happen and dedicated so much time as I am getting my second degree. And, its happening!

Suggestions? Advice?

Thanks!
