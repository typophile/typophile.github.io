The New Art

December 31, 2008 • _

This is inspired by the artist Joseph Kosuth; specifically his pieces labeled “art as idea.” (http://www.diacenter.org/KOS/images/kosuth.jpg) He would present the definition of “Art” or “Nothing” or various other words and this would be his art piece. Effectively, he questioned the conventional notion of art.

I like this piece because according to the guests in my house or just on my mood, the canvases can be switched to say “Is this art(?)” “Art is this” or the other combinations.

This is Art, Acrylic on canvas, 2008

Reminds me of that sordid cartoon with the rabbits where every object has a title above it.
