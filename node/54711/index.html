Your description tells me these are too old to be 'fonts' in the digital sense. Your best chance to replicate them is to look for 'license plate' or 'monospace' fonts. For example, [[http://new.myfonts.com/fonts/brassfonts/corpa-gothic/|Corpa Gothic]] is a fair match for the letters and some of the numerals, while [[http://new.myfonts.com/fonts/brassfonts/corpa-serif/|Corpa Serif]] has a pretty close 4 to your top sample. - Mike Yanega
