You will not find such a font. Your best bet is to find different fonts that convey a similar feel in different scripts. This version supports Spanish: http://new.myfonts.com/fonts/efscangraphic/frankfurter-sb/ For Chinese/Japanese, there is a style called “nar” or “maru”, with rounded terminals. For Hebrew and Arabic, you could ask in the specialised boards of the forum: http://typophile.com/forum/373 http://typophile.com/forum/366
