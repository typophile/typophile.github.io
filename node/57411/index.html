2009-05-07 05:09:12 • Höfe

The font that immediately sprang to mind seeing your sketch was Parsons, available in digital form as Parsnip NF. This will give you a very old-school flavor, perhaps not what you’re looking for.

In the original, there are lots of alternate characters and long extenders, but none for the caps P, B, and S specifically. In general, that kind of thing would make it easier to weave.

Probably just a wacky idea, but maybe it leads you somewhere.
