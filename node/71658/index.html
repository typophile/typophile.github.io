Cover 1 (Bad Reputation):

I don’t think “Joan Jett” is Kabel - the ‘a’ is wrong. I’m guessing it’s Avant Garde Extra Light with the alternate ‘e’. I can barely see “BAD REPUTATION” but it’s consistent with Helvetica Bold.

Cover 2 (I Love Rock-n-Roll):

“JOAN JETT & the blackhearts” looks like Wexford, which is available as Wexley. “I Love Rock-n-Roll” is hand-drawn.

Cover 3 (Glorious Results…):

“Joan Jett…” shows up as Spectrum in my old Letraset catalog. It can be found digitally as New Spectra but without some of the alternates used in your sample. The hand-drawn looking font might be harder to track down.
