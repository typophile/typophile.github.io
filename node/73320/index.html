2010-08-18 09:44:04 • akos.polgardi

This is pre-digital, so any suggestions will likely be close but not exact.

“BACKED WITH” appears to be Futura Book (or Light) Italic.

http://new.myfonts.com/fonts/adobe/futura/book-oblique/

“DANNY KAYE” is Century Schoolbook Bold Condensed. Bitstream’s is good, but the strokes are a tiny bit heavier than in this image.

http://new.myfonts.com/fonts/bitstream/century-schoolbook/bold-condensed/

“MYTI KAYSI” and “SONG”…based on the S and M, I like Railroad Gothic or Gothic No. 13. However, Railroad’s A has a much thinner crossbar.

http://new.myfonts.com/fonts/redrooster/railroad-gothic-rr/ http://new.myfonts.com/fonts/adobe/gothic-13/

“DODGERS” appears to be Akzidenz Grotesk Bold Condensed, or one of its derivatives.

http://new.myfonts.com/fonts/berthold/akzidenz-grotesk-bq/bold-condensed/

Agree that “Oh, Really?” is Century Schoolbook Italic.

http://new.myfonts.com/fonts/bitstream/century-schoolbook/italic/

I would hazard a guess that “R-20,105” is News Gothic Bold. It could also be Franklin Gothic, bue News Gothic’s 1 seems to me to be a better match.

http://new.myfonts.com/fonts/bitstream/news-gothic/bold/
