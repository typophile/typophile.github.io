Old Italic from a book of Ordinances of the Northwest Territories (1881)

February 8, 2005 • Lex Kominek

These samples come from a book of Ordinances of the Northwest Territories printed in about 1881. The book is mostly set in Times Roman, with its corresponding italic. However, the titles of the ordinances are printed in a different italic, the one shown here. Note the lc 'g' with it's funny ear and tail. Sorry for the poor quality, but it's from a microfiche. - Lex
