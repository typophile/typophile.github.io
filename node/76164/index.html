Celebrating traditional newspaper design

November 10, 2010 • type addict

I am happy that the traditional look isn’t dead in newspaper design.

The New York Times, The Wall Street Journal, The Washington Post, great examples like those.

I don’t like the convenient tabloid look, though, but the old-fashioned look is king with me.

I am happy that the traditional look isn’t dead in land animals.

The Tyrannosaurus Rex, Stegosaurus, Iguanodon, great examples like those.

I don’t like the convenient mammal look, though, but the old-fashioned look is king with me.
