Hi, A very simple way to make alternates visible is to put a lookup with something like this into the liga feature:

sub [a c m s] a' by a.alt1; sub [e i o t] a' by a.alt2;

(means: after a c m s glyph a is substituted by glyph a.alt1 after e i o t glyph a is substituted by glyph a.alt2 )
