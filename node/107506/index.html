From a 1963 Wayne King album (Decca label).Thanks so much in advance for your help![img:sites/default/files/old-images/Wayne King-Have Yourself A Merry Little Christmas-Font sample_5596.jpg]
The lettering looks like it was done with a flat pen as a softened version of a Textura design with Anglicized capitals. The repeating letters seem pretty much the same, so I suspect this was a font from the photo lettering era that, as far as I can find out has not been digitized. I can offer substitutes that have some of the flavor of the lettering, mainly for the lower case, but are not all that close: Kingthings Petrock Pro http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/cheapprofonts/kingthings-petrock-pro/ Claudius http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/rmu/claudius/ Fine Gothic http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/finefonts/fine-gothic/ Clairvaux http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/adobe/clairvaux/ Goudy Text http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/adobe/goudy-text/ Perhaps there is something closer?
