Hello. I’ve been enjoying some of the many art/design/illustration/culture publications available for free download from [[http://www.pdf-mags.com/|Pdf Mags]] It gives people the chance to publish a magazine or zine without the uphill struggle of paying for print,distribution and amusing advertisers! Lovely, having worked in magazine world for a while, it’s nice not feel owned by the bloodsuckers!

I have noticed though, that although many nice mags, virtually no font or typography magazines. Theres a ‘select by category’ bar in there and under fonts, there appears to be just one.

I thought i’d give all you typonuts a heads up! as this section is crying out for some beautiful type pdf magazines. Maybe even a Typophile publication.

anyhow. Enjoy if you haven’t seen this site before..
