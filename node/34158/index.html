I always thought they should both add up to 1000

No, you have to factor in the talus (internal leading) as well. For example in a font I’m working on the ascenders are 704, the descenders are 256, the upper talus is 30 and the lower talus is 10. You typically need some talus to avoid collisions when leading is zero. But too much talus will make your font less efficient and look discouragingly small on the body.

BTW you don’t have to use 1000, although it’s the safest so if you have no reason not to use it, use it.
