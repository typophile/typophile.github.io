Inline Rally Car Number

October 23, 2014 • LanceKendall

Hi,

I’ve spent an embarrassingly long amount of time trying to find the typeface used in the number on this car.

Also as I’ve been searching for this, I’ve realised that the best term to describe the font is “inline” but it seems the vast majority of inline typeface don’t have the line continue out the ends of the character thus I’m wondering if there’s a specific term for these kinds of typefaces.

I noticed the 3 is one of the ways to define it by the sharp corner in the top. From my limited knowledge of type anatomy I’m not sure what you’d call it as most typefaces have it as rounded and then it’d be called a “bowl” but since it’s sharp, I’m not sure what it’s called. Is there a term for this kind of 3?

A three with a flat top is sometimes called a banker’s three. Don
