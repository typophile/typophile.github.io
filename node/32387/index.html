You did fine on the CSS. The site has a nice design with nice, clean whitespace and good black-and-white contrast. I saw a few hyphens in a row on the home page, but that’s more a matter of opinion than faith. ;-)

If your clients weren’t so arts-oriented I’d say the emphasis on such things as Haas Grotesque being renamed Helvetica would be superfluous, since most clients are primarily concerned with the bottom line. At the same time, I’d still keep that in mind since such cultural institutions are usually the first to lose funding (in the U.S. at least).
