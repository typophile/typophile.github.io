Anyway, you could ask Castaway’s author (http://rotodesign.com/contact.html) about the origin of the design. A similar (albeit in my opinion less vibrant) take on the genre would be House Industries’ Tiki Palms (http://www.houseind.com/fonts/tikitype/viewfonts).
