[[Avenir]] – [[Adrian Frutiger|Frutiger]]’s [[Futura]] – would be an obvious answer, no? Avenir Next comes with old-style figures.

Have a look at [[Thomas Phinney]]’s Hypatia Sans: 6 weights from Extralight to Black (though no Italics yet), and all kind of figures you can imagine. Once you found your way throught the various OpenType Stylistic Subsets, Hypatia Sans will turn out to be a very versatile system.
