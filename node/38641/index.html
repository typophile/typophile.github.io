Leslie Cabarga’s book Learn FontLab Fast is essential if you are using FontLab, and his Logo, Font and Lettering Bible is also very informative. (from the referred thread).

I have to vouch for these books. I just got them, and recently finished reading the latter, and have browsed the former extensively. They are absolutely the best resources I have seen for beginning type design. (Note: they expect you to have mastered Illustrator or another vector program first).
