I agree that these are the correct non shaded versions. URW also has Roman Shaded http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/urw/roman-shaded/ which should be used rather than the OPTI Roman Shaded because of the questionable legal status of OPTI fonts. If you also want a lighter weight font with the letter-forms of Roman Solid you could use Solid Antique Roman http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/mti/solid-antique-roman/ or Sackers Solid Antique Roman http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/mti/sackers-antique-roman/ The design of all three fonts pre-dates the digital era but I have been unable to find out anything about the origin.
