2007-09-21 10:27:40 • SuperUltraFabulous

it was a great presentation. a lot of the ususal House history lesson, but i really enjoyed what they showed about their involvement with Eames and the new Girard fonts. Rich said a couple of interesting things:

  1. he made the claim that noone else does the OT thing quite as well as House. i agree that they do it very well, but are they THE best? i’m not sure…
  2. he said that they now try to include some fonts suitable for text with each release. i thought this was quite an interesting approach, and looking back at their last few releases, you can see that this is the case. it was one of those things that i kind of knew in my mind, but didn’t fully realize until Rich said it. I really this approach: marketing a set of related typefaces that include both text and display faces with a similar flavor. i hope this kind of thing catches on more.
