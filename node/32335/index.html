Comments about Anchorage, Alaska logo design contest?

March 17, 2007

I'm curious what the typophiles think of the idea to have a design contest for a new logo to represent the city of Anchorage, AK. The logo they paid thousands for (I think 200k!) is a big brown splat, and people just HATE it. I can't tell if the pixelation is intentional or not? Restrictions? Must be a resident of Anchorage to submit work. The prize? "An unspecified package of Alaskan items worth $3000." IMHO, and in the name of good design, we should pepper the forum with our take on such contests ... http://community.adn.com/?q=adn/node/100260 AK
Given how well hiring qualified and expensive designers worked out, I really don't think that giving them flak about running a contest is appropriate. If I spent $200,000 and got a logo that looked like a random ornament clipped out of a threadless shirt I wouldn't hire another professional designer either.
