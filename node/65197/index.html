Åbolands Teaterskola

December 9, 2009 • kattttor

Logo for a swedish theatre school for kids. The name translates as follows “Åboland” is the name of the county and “Teaterskola” means theatre school.

Not much intructions from the client. They want some sort of an emblem and that the school theme comes across.

Been working on a concept that brings out the theatre part, based on the theatre masks you usually see with theatre related things.

Gonna do some more school based scetches and work on an emblem.

Any feedback is much appreciated.
