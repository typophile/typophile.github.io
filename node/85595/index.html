Unfortunately it looks to be hand-lettered - the slight differences between the /E/s and /R/s are a dead giveaway.

The only typeface I’ve been able to find which mimics Old Church Slavonic (which is the writing system that’s been inspirational here) is ParaType’s [[http://new.myfonts.com/fonts/paratype/izhitsa/|Izhitsa]]. Unfortunately it’s not as good as the above ans shares few other characteristics.
