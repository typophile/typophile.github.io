Souvenir! Exhibition, Design Museum, Helsinki

September 21, 2005 • vesa

Souvenir! is a poster exhibition, that presents type design from different North European cities. 25 posters from Denmark, Estonia, Finland, Norway, Poland, Russia and Sweden are presented at the Design Museum in Helsinki from September 15 to September 25, 2005.
