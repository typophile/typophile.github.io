TORRES GARCIA: very close to some versions of [[http://www.myfonts.com/search/Radiant|Radiant]] (Normal, Condensed or sometimes Extra Condensed) NOVEMBRE-DECEMBRE: “[[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/linotype/peignot|Peignot]]-like” with main difference on /R; [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/profonts/euro-sans-pro|Euro Sans]] is close too; [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/g-type/accent-graphic|Accent]] is interesting and possibly closer for ‘1955’ which actually throw me off.

Couldn’t find a match for the first one. Still searching… You could try to mix different styles of [[http://www.houseind.com/fonts/chaletcomprime/viewfonts|Chalet Comprimé]] but that’s far from matching.
