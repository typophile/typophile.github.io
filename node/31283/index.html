Sorry here it is…

I’m seeing way too much going on here. Six levels of headlines in two typefaces and three colors, two kinds of pullouts, transparent floating captions, and lines of Chevrons. You’ve got a lot of good stuff going on, it’s just going on a little too thick. Try turning the volume down a little and let the great photos do more of the work.

Also, be careful with your gradients and shadows. You’ve got them lit from below, it’s emphasizing the top heavy layouts and destabilizing the pages.
