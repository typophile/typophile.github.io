[[Indices]] : [[Foundries]] : No Bodoni

No Bodoni is a small, independent type design company, founded by designer [[George E. Thompson]], and inspired by [[Giambattista Bodoni]]’s dedication and drive.

“I felt bad because I had no shoes, until I met a man who had no Bodoni.”
