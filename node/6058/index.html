2004-08-13 03:53:13 • doihaveto
If the school requires not just 12 point type and double spacing but also a fixed margin (as I recall one inch was usually the magic number) your main problem might be line length – you might be getting too many characters per line. You might want to look for a wide face if that's the case (and, yes, a larger x-height because of the big line space).
2004-08-13 04:51:06 • xensen
The terms 12 point and double line space are meaningless. 10 different fonts rendered at "12 point" could appear as 10 different sizes. Lines space will also be affected by the various font ascent and descent values.
