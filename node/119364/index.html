Hey, can anyone tell me what font was used in this sports club’s logo?

This could be the Bitstream version of Handel Gothic, or a typeface based on Handel, called Fourche: http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/bitstream/handel-gothic/ http://www.shyfoundry.com/fonts/shareware/sffourche.html

NOTE: only the Bitstream version of Handel has the straight-legged R and the rounded U–all the others have a curved leg R and/or a U with one straight side.

TSVOI looks artificially widened, if it is Fourche.
