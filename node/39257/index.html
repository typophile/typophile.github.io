2007-11-20 13:54:44 • dan_reynolds

Dan — yeah they used Unica a lot. Between 1993 and 1995 they developed a font which took into account the problems of anti-aliasing and aliasing in on-screen typography. It was known as ‘Interact’ and was based on a grid — the 45 degree line’is rendered by a series of one by one pixel steps.

Hamish Muir also has a website, which I guess you can find on google.

mmmihela — try and buy or borrow from the library the 8vO book ‘On the Outside.’ You will find info on the typeface in there.
