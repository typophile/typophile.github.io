Seems to me that 'RANGED left' is a description, while 'RANGE left' is an instruction. I remember writing 'Range left' on typescript that was going off to typesetters (metal and, in time, film), and describing the results as 'Ranged left'. So in answer to Miles' question, 'range' may be more common, but 'ranged' is probably grammatically correct.
