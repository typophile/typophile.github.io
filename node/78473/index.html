2011-01-28 00:27:18 • Ace Typesetter

The body faces you have chosen are very middling, in terms of weight, proportion, and style (although the Eurostile does have a bit of personality). Therefore, they are not sufficiently extreme to counterpoint the heading, which is highly condensed, finely detailed, high contrast, and mannered. Overall, the effect is one-sided, not balanced. I recommend Beaufort Extended (my own faces always come to mind, for some reason :-) It holds its own against the extremism of Anaconda, by being extended in proportion (contrary), finely detailed in serif finish (similar), low contrast (contrary), and somewhat mannered (similar). http://new.myfonts.com/fonts/shinn/beaufort/extended/

I also think one has to have a reason for using a condensed face. Often the layout calls for it. If that is not the case, then a pairing with an extended face seems reasonable.
