Tweakin'

March 8, 2009 • CaliTraina

[img:sites/default/files/old-images/tweakblog_3645.jpg] This was a sketchbook assignment for my Typology class. The word was tweak, and we could take it any way we wanted to and this is where it lead for me. I did handlettering in my sketchbook, then traced it in Illustrator. I changed it up a bit though. My teacher suggested I make some 3D so that's exactly what I did. I did the word tweak to give it emphasis. My only real inspiration for this (other than the word "tweak" itself) was the drink special sign I draw every week for the restaurant work. I alternate these typefaces above on the sign so I combined them all in this one piece. If I had to pick a time period this reflects, I'd have to say Dada, only because of the combination of multiple typefaces. My grid obviously contradicts that though. How about you tell me...what time period to YOU GUYS think this piece reflects. I'm interested in knowing what you think.
