The condensed typeface is one of the condensed faces from the old Monotype Grotesque series. The one also looks like it’s from that family. They may have substituted it for the standard Gill Sans one, which is indistinguishable from an I or an l. I am pretty sure there was also an alternate one for Gill Sans, but I don’t recall that it looked like that.
