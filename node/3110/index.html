Being somewhat of a non-connaisseur when it comes to wine I tend to buy wine strictly with special kind of labels, i.e. not with your average winetypography. There's also certain themes i try, like
Oh, for this weekend I'll only buy wine with illustrations of elks.
You'd be surprised how many there are (at least two).

Anyway, if you really care, and want to do some generic wine label typography, go to a Shop Where They Sell Wine and take a Good Look.

Ask yourself what kind of approach you want. Simple elegance, like a chanel perfume bottle, or is it something less classy? Fraktur? Hand lettering, something that wraps around the bottle?
What kind of wine would You like to buy?

Maybe you shouldn't concentrate on the typography, but instead focus on an illustration on the bottle, of, say, an elk?


Best of luck,
