Can anyone tell me what font "PARIS" is?

February 2, 2015 • typotype99

Based on the |S|, it appears to be a custom adaptation of Riesling: http://www.dafont.com/riesling.font?text=PARIS

Nick Curtis has a similar typeface (which, since it predates Riesling, may have been the basis of Riesling) called Mighty Ditey. Mighty Ditey is based on a 1970s Photolettering face called Aphrodite: http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/nicksfonts/mighty-ditey-nf/
