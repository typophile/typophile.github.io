Which version of Garamond do you like? The pre-installed one in the Microsoft Office Package, which is just called “Garamond”, or a Garamond that came with an Adobe application, like “Adobe Garamond” or even “Garamond Premiere Pro”, or another differently called Garamond?

If you have only the one from the MS Office installation, then you can buy a different one. The Office Garamond is actually a from a different designer, not Garamond himself, and it is as well too light to use it for text. So buy yourself a decent version. The ones named from Adobe are very good choices. Alternatives are Sabon, Augereau, GaramondFB by FontBureau, Granjon, and many others.

Or get a free (and still incomplete, and maybe not as neat) one. URW “Garamond No8”, based on Stempel Garamond can be found in the URW font package here: http://www.artifex.com/downloads/ or the ambitious “EB Garamond” in [[https://github.com/georgd/EB-Garamond/tree/master/otf|Regular]], [[https://github.com/georgd/EB-Granjon/tree/master/OTF|Italic and Bold]].
