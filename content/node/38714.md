body: "<strong>[[Indices]] : [[Designers]] : David Quay</strong>\r\n\r\nDavid Quay
  was born in London, England, and studied graphic design at the Ravensbourne College
  of Art & Design. After graduation he worked in various London design studios until
  he became a freelance designer in 1975, specialising in lettering, logotype and
  graphic design.\r\n\r\nIn 1987 he formed David Quay Design in London. Clients included
  [[Monotype]], H. [[Berthold]] AG, and the [[International Typeface Corporation]]
  (ITC). In 1990 he co-founded [[The Foundry]] with [[Freda Sack]] and [[Mike Daines]],
  to design, manufacture, and market PostScript fonts.\r\n\r\n<strong>External Links</strong>\r\n<a
