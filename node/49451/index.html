2008-09-15 23:32:30 • milne.ross

Joke Gossé just finished this year’s MATD course, and will be at the ATypI conference in St. Petersburg. Reading typeface is Meville, an her specimen is on the typefacedesign.org site at – http://www.typefacedesign.org/resources/A5specimen/2008/JokeGosse_Melville_specimen.pdf

Also there is Jo de Baerdemaeker, who graduated from Reading’s MATD course in 2005, and is now writing a PhD about Tibetan type forms. His website is http://www.typojo.com

Both Jo and Joke are proud, fine Belgian typeface designers. Keep them bookmarked.
