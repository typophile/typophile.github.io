For straight text use, Linotype Metro was a familiar option from that period…in old movies that show newspaper pages, you would often see Metro for both display and text…also pre-ITC Kabel, as used in the Monopoly game…and if you want a text face like Gill Sans but with oldstyle figures, look at Freight Sans.
