I'm looking for a font similar to the Ben & Jerry's Ice Cream font. I realize the font was originally hand drawn by a Ben & Jerry's former Design Director (and named after her). As the company grew, she couldn't keep up with all the writing, so her hand lettering was converted to a computer format. It is a proprietary font, owned by Ben & Jerry's and is not available for sale or downloading. But does anyone have any idea as to what font would look similar
Try Beton Extra Bold or Stymie Black.

They don't have the roughness, but the original handlettering was probably based on something like these.
