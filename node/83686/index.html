In the 1950s and 60s, Saul Bass (1920-1996) was the superstar designer of film titles and film posters. His film credits included: The Man withe the Golden Arm Anatomy of a Murder Around the World in 80 Days Psycho It’s a Mad Mad Mad Mad World North by Northwest Vertigo Spartacus Exodus West Side Story Like many of his contemporaries, he was a talented creator of handlettered headlines, so some of his best work may be described in terms of graphic design or letterforms, but not as typography per se.
