This type of font can be made by cutting serifs on a Didot and making some other edits. Can't recall off hand if this one is a bespoke font for New York Magazine. If so, presumably it would have been made by legitimate means, i.e. from scratch or a scan of a printed Didot specimen rather than from an existing digital didot.
