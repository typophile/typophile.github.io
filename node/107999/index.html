Looking for an ID on the font used on this patch. Looks like a popular font used by bikers (Hells Angels, etc) on jackets and patches.

Subject discussed in previous thread http://typophile.com/node/104210 AFAIK there is no current design patent on the design. In sum the lettering is a pretty generic rendition of some 19th century “Tuscan” designs with “median spurs” – the pointy bulges on the sides of the vertical strokes. Google Tuscan and you will find plenty from which to choose. Discussion of another spurred font for jackets http://typophile.com/node/103278 Don

It’s close but this one is not the same as the one for the Hells Angels. It’s [[http://www.dafont.com/bosox.font|Bosox]] by Lee Gordon, most probably a digitization attempt of MLB Boston Red Sox jersey font.
