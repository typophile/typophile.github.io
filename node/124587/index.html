Probably a custom(ization?) job and by [[https://www.behance.net/gallery/23292553/Neptune-Suite|Denis Olenik]] (but you probably already knew that). The only uncommon letter here is /S to me, rest could be easily approximated starting with a contrasted sans. Even if it’s too wide comparing to the original, Aviano [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/insigne/aviano-gothic|Gothic]]/[[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/insigne/aviano-contrast|Contrast]] is nice alternative with a /S in the same vein.
