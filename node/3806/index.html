I am currently building a "receipt" gallery, (that means "printed bitmap type"), and if you got original receipts with rude or well-done fonts, or crappy logos, please send 150 dpi scans to greg@bmap.info. oh, and for stimulation, check also Martin P. Pfeiffer's Receipt 1.0 font
