Following the growing Type Specimen pool there is now a Flickr group for pictures of fonts in use: http://www.flickr.com/groups/fonts-in-use/pool/

The best thing about it is that it is not only usable within Flickr, but the image pool can also be used by any website that supports RSS inclusion. A foundry website could use the pool to collect images of their fonts in use; Typophile.com could use it to add images of fonts to the wiki articles and so on and so forth.

See examples of the dynamic RSS inclusion at Typografie.info here or here.

And feel free to add your images of fonts in use!
