Alexandra,

Do not post links to pirated fonts on Typophile. Thank you!

LET Smudger is a commercial font. You need to hold a license in order to use it. Other designers may be able to provide you with fonts, but they can’t grant you the right to use them. And they most likely violate the [[EULA]] by passing on the fonts.

Andreas (Fontgrube) posted a link to one of the distributors where you can purchase a license (and get the necessary font files). Read about the value of type.
