So… I’ve no idea why, but whenever I type “/” on my keyboard, when trying the font I’m designing, the slash doesn’t appear. But then when I type “/” again, and it appears. So, every time I type “/” 2 times, only 1 “/” appears on my screen.

Thanks!

To preview glyphs that aren’t on your keyboard, the slash is a delimiter to indicate a glyph name. For instance, “r/eacute sum/eacute” in the plaintext field gives you “résumé”. This necessitates “//” to mean “this is not a delimiter, the following is not a glyph name, just put a slash here.” Nothing is wrong with your font. I didn’t know there was a similar feature in Illustrator, but there must be.
