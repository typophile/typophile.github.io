Alphonse Mucha.

February 28, 2009 • angarcia

So we have to write about our favorite designer and/or typographer. I chose Alphonse Mucha, simply because I am a huge fan of the Art Nouveau style. I love the details he put into his illustrations. Also, he wasn’t limited as an artist, he painted, sculpted, illustrated, etc. I love his use of soft, pastel colors to match the emotion that he was trying to convey. Lovely work. <3
