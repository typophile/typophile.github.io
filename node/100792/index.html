Welcome.

I do not recommend ‘Just My Type.’ I couldn’t finish it. ([[http://www.salon.com/2011/09/20/just_my_type_imprint/|Relevant review]])

I haven’t read ‘Anatomy of a Typeface.’ (But I plan to at some point.)

Of these three, ‘Thinking With Type’ would give you more details about the uses of typefaces. I think ‘[[http://www.amazon.com/Shaping-Text-Type-Typography-Reader/dp/9063692234/ref=sr_1_1?s=books&ie=UTF8&qid=1361638565&sr=1-1&keywords=Shaping+Text|Shaping Text]]’ is an even better introductory text.

Good luck!
