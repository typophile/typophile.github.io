Good variety of English vernacular Roman lettering. In particular I love the _G_ in the first image, which looks like it's sucking a lemon! Some of these memorial lettering designs were turned into full alphabets. For example check out the book by Day called _Alphabets_ from circa 1900. As for digital versions, there are no exact matches but you might be interested in some of the fonts by Greater Albion Typefounders http://www.myfonts.com/foundry/Greater_Albion_Typefounders/ Don
