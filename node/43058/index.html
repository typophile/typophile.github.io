Hi Nathaniel, this is most likely hand-lettered. If you look closely at the 3 ‘e’s, you can see that they’re not identical. I don’t think there were any fonts with such subtle alternates around in the late eighties. Are you looking for alternatives, too? [edit] Nah … I first thought that Englische Schreibschrift, or Shelley Script might be a little closer, but in fact, Künstler Script is pretty good. Unfortunately, they all have the ‘wrong’ r, even the OpenType versions – no alternates. Diplomat features the desired form. A fresh Anglaise, also with the ‘right r’: [[Font Bureau]]’s Novia, by [[Cyrus Highsmith]]. F
