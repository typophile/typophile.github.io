Evetts' book is interesting but it may give the mistaken impression that the Trajan letters were planned out geometrically. There isn't any evidence of this. You should try to find a copy of Fr Edward M. Catich's The Origin of the Serif, which a good university library should have, and the rarer Letters redrawn from The Trajan inscription in Rome. The latter is likely to be kept in special collections, although there are six copies for sale on ABE, beginning at $138.
