“Jabberwocky” is particularly interesting to typophiles, with our tradition of dummy text in foundry specimens. Alastair Johnston’s Alphabets to Order is a brilliant study of the dada poetry of 19th century American specimen books. Font Bureau and Hoefler & Frere-Jones have been instrumental in maintaining this tradition – part of which is the anonymity of the authors, which strikes me as a shame, but hey, it’s copy writing. Shinntype.com features specimen text written by Eric Shinn, utilizing various software tools.
