That's an odd one. I wonder if it was made by customizing a narrow sans type. The A, N and maybe even the V could have been made from the letter U. The 'I' could have had serifs added. The T, O & C were used as is. That tells me find a font with that C and you can make the rest. If it was me, and admittedly I am crude about such things, I would look at a font like [[http://www.bowfinprintworks.com/BauhausFaces4.html|Tantalus]], shown here in the Bauhaus guide, and edit and squoosh as necessary to approximate your lettering. It's a brute force approach, but it requires little skill (and that's all I have). - Mike Yanega
