There's a difference between setting type for reading by humans and for processing by the machines of the postal service. The U.S. Postal Service prefers all caps and no punctuation, among other things: http://pe.usps.com/businessmail101/addressing/deliveryaddress.htm They like two spaces between the state (province) and zip (post) code, too.
