I don't see an exact match. But here a few digital fonts based on condensed wood type to compare to your sample. I would focus on the issue of the ratio of width to height, the weight and the letter-forms of _R_ and _S_. An exact match would have the little kick on the end of the leg on _R_. Issues about other letters, including the tail on _Q_ are not a major problems as _Q_ is rarely used -- unless you are writing about the Queen :) Silvertone Woodtype, already suggested by Kevin [img:sites/default/files/old-images/SilvertoneWoodtypeUC_5967.gif] Cheap Pine Sans [img:sites/default/files/old-images/CheapPineSansUC_5880.gif] Gothic XSHand Fill [img:sites/default/files/old-images/GothicXSHandFillUC_5365.gif] http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/hvdfonts/cheap-pine/ http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/urbanpixel/gothic-xs-hand/ Don
