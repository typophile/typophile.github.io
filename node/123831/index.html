IMO custom lettering. Note the windswept T and E, a feature on fonts derived from Jenson Roman, such as Centaur, designed in 1914 by Bruce Rogers for the Metropolitan Museum in New York which in revised form looks like http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/mti/centaur/ Otherwise, the lettering is fairly generic Roman, with some of the flavor of fonts like Laudatio Light http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/berthold/laudatio-bq/ The red letting is simplified Old English. Don
