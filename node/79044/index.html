You’re looking for every “i” in the document? Or are you just looking for special ones, like ones in a certain font or style?

The “Find/Change” feature in InDesign works pretty much the same as it does in most other applications, except that InDesign gives you a lot more options. Be sure the “text” button at the top of the Find dialog box is chosen; otherwise you may be accidentally doing a different type of find (such as GREP); perhaps that’s what’s confusing you.
