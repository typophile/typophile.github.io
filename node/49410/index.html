Trondheim Type Club

September 13, 2008 • Trond Aslak

TTC(Trondheim Type Club) started out in 2008 by Trond Aslak Øvrum and Aasmund Hegglid. The idea was made in 2007 as something we missed in our town. A breeding place for type-fanatics. We are now a group of people with relations to the design/advertising industry in Trondheim, Norway.

We all have passion and love for typography, and want to share our ideas, inspirations and knowledge with everyone. Kern or die!
