2009-05-09 04:32:20 • Reed Reibstein

If you want something in that kind of plain oldstyle vein, but not quite as soft as Garamond No.3, then take a look at James Montalbano’s [[http://www.terminaldesign.com/fonts/characters/?ID=56|Rawlinson]] – an underappreciated family, IMO. I like its sturdy, plain-spoken qualities. It’s not as sharply cut as Christian’s DB News, but it shares some DNA.

– Kent.
