2006-10-05 12:44:56 • Stephan Kurz

To me, this is both very funny and kind of sad -

Why say it with words and be unintelligible only to illiterates and speakers of other languages when you can say it with a picture and be unintelligible to everyone?

Equality of ambiguity for all. (It’s in the Bill of Rights, isn’t it?)

I am working on a bilingual signage system right now and I get so much pressure from people who either believe it is easier to say practically everything with pictograms than to translate words into another language, or else so resent having to use the second language that they are willing to sacrifice the very concept of communication to avoid doing it in the second language.
