This may have been custom-created for Vogue by one of lettering artists of the time (Wes Wilson, Victor Moscoso, Rick Griffin). I'm not finding a digital match. And the style is actually based in Art Nouveau/Secessionist-era hand-drawn type, of the kind created by Alfred Roller. Here are some more typefaces in a similar vein: http://www.identifont.com/list?3+Mojo+0+RU+1+1WC0+2+PF8+2+PF9+2+HYB+2+34L+2+ART+2+ARU+2+ARV+2+83C+2+5HJ+2+F7M+2+5GG+2+CAN+8+CVZ+24+HYI+24+HYD+24+2GOD+24+248Y+24+O3+24+IE8+24+I8+24+EX+24+1WDE+24+1WG8+24+1WHK+24+1WE4+24+1WCA+24+1WHS+24+7IK+24
