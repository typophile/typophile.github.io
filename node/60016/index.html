Based of the age of the typewriter I discerned from looking at this [[http://www.flickr.com/photos/18378305@N00/3755496011/sizes/l/in/photostream/ | picture]] and examining more of the letters, this typeface has not been digitized. Its prior to Folio, and Helvetica, but not Akzidenz which was made in 1896. The Underwood No. 5 was made 1924.

Most of the letters are Grotesk but W and V are Geometric. As Spock would say, “Fascinating”.

If it’s this kind of quirkiness you like, then you’ll really dig Figgins Sans: http://new.myfonts.com/fonts/shinn/figgins-sans/
