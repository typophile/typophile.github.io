DTL Contour Master vs. Font Audit

October 27, 2004 â€¢ Ramiro Espinoza

Lately I've been needing to check a lot of versions of a recently made type family.
I used for that Contour Master by DTL and was very accurate and fast. The program checks in batch process all the fonts at the same time and gives a report in detail of all the possible problems for every character.
Far better than the messy Font Audit in which you have to go letter by letter, point by point cheking what is happening.
With Contour Master you can read the reports and to know very fast if they are serious overlapping or just a bezier not very well adjusted. I less than half an hour I had all the serious mistakes solved.
