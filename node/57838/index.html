Surreal use of Monotype 'Computer' type

May 9, 2009 • John Hudson

Walking in the woods this evening, I came across what may be the most surreal sign I’ve ever seen.

In a time when everything seemed so new that nobody was sure how to fit it together, there was such a thing as “computer camp”. My best guess is that this is a remnant of one of those.
