A long-defunct publication "The Colophon". Case bound; great quality type and printing. This book was produced quarterly from the 20's to the mid 30's I think. It can be found at most antiquarian book stores. There are printed submissions by Goudy, Dwiggins, Bruce Rogers, Press of the Wooley Whale, Peter Pauper Press, etc. They usually run about $25 a copy but are well worth it. Jim
