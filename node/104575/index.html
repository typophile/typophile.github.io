Bruce Rogers designed these letters for the Metropolitan Museum in 1914 using 15th century type by Nicolas Jenson as his model. Monotype sold it as a font under the name Centaur in 1929. Various digital versions of Centaur are now sold by Monotype, as Centaur, Adobe, as Centaur, Lanston, as Metropolitan, Bitstream, as Venetian 301, and Softmaker, as Cambridge Serial. Don
