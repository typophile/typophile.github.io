2005-06-08 16:23:14 • aluminum

Do a search for “wood type” on myfonts.com. Here are a few other ideas (sorry but don’t have time to link these):

  • Rosewood (Adobe) although it’s overused it’s still great
  • Brothers or Council (Emigre)
  • Egyptienne Condensed
  • good ‘ol Clarendon

Also keep in mind that when you say “country” it could mean a lot of things. There’s a big difference between Hank Williams (classic country) and Shania Twain (contemporary). I’m blowing hot air a bit here since I don’t listen to, or care for country music. I do however live in Nashville. Good luck.
