Hi Henri,

I’m not a font designer but I’m Greek and although I don’t know which is this typeface, I wonder why one should have to use one of the ugliest Greek Polytonic (=multi-accent) typefaces I have ever seen!

Especially the accents are awfully designed: 2nd letter: The Vareia accent should point to the horizontal center of the eta (η) letter, not to the next letter! 5th letter: The Perispomeni accent should be much lower and more close to the eta (η) letter 15th & 27th letter: The Psili accent should be centered above ypsilon (υ) and epsilon (ε) letter.

If the specific font is not a absolute requirement I will highly recommend to replace it with another.

Look at the image I have attached, to see how different is the same text if you use a well designed Greek Polytonic Text (GFS Didot).
