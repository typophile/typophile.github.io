Morbid but Necessary Feature Request

February 21, 2012 • hrant

http://typophile.com/node/90115#comment-495970

Replying to a post by a dead person benefits from knowing that the person cannot reply.

Finding out after the fact that the person you replied to is dead is disconcerting.

So I think we need a way of marking usernames of dead people; and their profile should show the date of death.
