Reliable, Capable Printer in NYC

October 25, 2005 • Chris Rugen

I’m working on an identity package for my full-time employer that requires something more than online business card factories can provide. I’m also getting fed up with some of the copy shops we use for a lot of our other materials who either assume I don’t know what I’m talking about, don’t pay attention to the quality, or don’t have the capabilities I need.

I’m looking for a printer that can take direction but also offer it, and can do solid offset printing for a reasonable price. I’m not looking to do anything crazy or unusual, but I’m specing stock and inks, etc. and want a conscientious printer. Any other capabilities (Indigo, low-run color copy, etc.) are a bonus.

I know almost nothing about printers in the NYC area and any help/advice would be great. Even a resource for or list of decent printers would be great, as there are so many to wade through and I can’t afford for this project to come out half-baked. Many thanks in advance.

2005-10-25 22:11:43 • jupiterboy
Chris the problem with printers in NYC is they aren’t printers, they are print brokers. You need to find a print broker that you trust and your print jobs will be handled in places as close as 26th Street Manhattan and an hours drive in New Jersey and even a flight to Atlanta. Your print broker will have enough contacts to keep the quality and the cost in line for your job. Altag Press was a print broker I used for years, but I’ve used others and got good results.
