Short Description:The third release in our series of [[http://www.alterlittera.com/al_htm/initials.htm|Initials]] fonts. A comprehensive set of initials, frames and borders, adapted from American Type Founders (ATF) Company’s American Specimen Book of Type Styles, Jersey City, 1912 (pp. 944-5). The font contains over one hundred glyphs, including clean renditions of both Missal Initials and Caxton Initials, plus adaptations of Department Store Initials and French Cast Squares.
