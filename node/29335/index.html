It is called UPM Fine from UPM-Kymmene (Finland) and is a publication grade that they sell directly to web printing publication houses. Apparently it is not really sold through US west coast merchants at all and is not a sheetfed item. Word is they do sell sheets of this in Finland and other places in Europe.

The specifications on this are that it is a wood-free machine finished uncoated pub stock.
