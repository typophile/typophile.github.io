Just like Meadow - but condensed!

October 11, 2010 • peterbruhn

[img:sites/default/files/old-images/meadowcondtop_typphile_5863.png] I guess the subject line says it all. Before the weekend we released [[http://www.fountaintype.com/typefaces/meadow|Meadow Condensed]] designed by Göran Söderström. A version that saves a little space but has the same character. Meadow Condensed works well on its own or as a family member. Character sets are identical, finish and quality is the same, it's just narrower to make room for more words. Meadow Condensed is well suited for strong headlines all the way down to small size settings. [img:sites/default/files/old-images/text_meadowConde_4026.png] Just as the first release – Meadow Pro – Meadow Pro Condensed range from Light to Extra Bold with matching italics, in a total of 10 fonts. It come with Small Caps and a wide range of OpenType features, allowing extensive and versatile use. If you already bought a Meadow license you can upgrade by simply subtracting you initial cost. Please [http://www.fountaintype.com/contact|contact us]] for more information.
