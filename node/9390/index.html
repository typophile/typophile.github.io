Well, for Helvetica Cyrillic and Helvetica Greek you're in luck.

For Japanese, I recommend Heisei Kaku Gothic. Dynalab has has a similar Chinese font (Hei) in one of their paks.

You can browse through Monotype's list of Non-Western fonts as well. I didn't find a good match for all scripts, but for Bengali I'd go with ITR Dilip, for Devanagari ITR Mitra (although Chakra, Kable, or Yogesh would probably also work), for Gujarati ITR Mohan, for Kannada ITR Sarita, for Telugu ITR Prabha. Their Arial Hebrew isn't bad. Arial Thai is also serviceable, but I'd go for UPC Browillia instead. For Korean, HY Gothic will do, but I find it aesthetically flat.

I'm sure I'm doing a disservice to smaller, independent foundries who have made fonts to harmonize with the Most Ubiquitous Font in the World
