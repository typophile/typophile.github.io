Chemical War Revolution Long Live the Revolution! Reds and Whites Aerial Conflict Aerial Combat Cycling Race (?)

Circular Race rather Travel by Plane across the USSR Polar Expedition of Krasin and Malygin Icebreakers Travel the Wealth of the USSR Electrification - not to be mixed with Electrocution :) Moscow-China Dobrolet - a made-up word, literally - “Kind/Happy Flyer” Alphabet of Young Osvodovetz Your Friend of Avtodor - Avtodor is the same as Osvod, but applied to the cars instead of water :) Maneuvers by Young Friends of Osoviahim - as far as I remember it was a society dealing with training volunteer reservists for the defence department. “-him” stands for “chemical”, probably capturing the fascination with gas masks of that period :) …
