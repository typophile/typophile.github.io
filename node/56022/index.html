Designer Spotlight: Martin Venezky

March 19, 2009 • gcrivera

Martin Venezky is one of the many graphic designers who leave me awe inspired…I cannot WAIT for his lecture!!! I chose this particular piece for its distinctive collage look. This piece is in celebration of his solo exhibition in San Francisco. Martin cut out and placed every piece of this poster by hand. Just brilliant! What a refreshing look at collaging!

This is really quite an amazing collage. I was wondering though.. can an equal amount of mileage be achieved if we were to use any colour other than yellow here? If so which colour? (It’s a genuine question; not a rhetorical comment!!!)
