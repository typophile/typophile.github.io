Feature request: Cyrillic Typography & Type Design

November 3, 2013 • Indian

In the ‘Special Interest Groups’ we have now:       -  Arabic Typography & Type Design       -  Hebrew Typography & Type Design

I suggest to create a similar group:       -  Cyrillic Typography & Type Design

Because the Cyrillic type design is an area that is interesting enough for everybody here with its rich history and its boom in nowadays. And the Russian type designers need a suitable place to join their efforts.

Also a non-Cyrillic authors can post here their requests to add a Cyrillic glyphs to their typefaces.

What do you think?
