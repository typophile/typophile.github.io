Just a quick update to let you know that more weights have been added. Terminal Dosis is now a 7 weights Family (Extralight, Light, Book, Medium, SemiBold, Bold y ExtraBold)

The lighter weights are minimalist. The bolder weights have more personality. Also featuring lots of alternates. The overall result is a family that’s clean and modern, and can express a wide range of voices & feelings.

Download the new version at http://www.impallari.com/terminaldosis/
