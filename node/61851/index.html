Futura meets Akzidenz Grotesk

September 10, 2009 • Nick Sherman

I saw this signage at an auto shop in the LA area. At first glance I thought it was Akzidenz, but there are some noticeable differences that give it a Futura look as well. And if that weren’t tricky enough, the numerals have the flavor of Avant Garde.

The closest thing I could think of off the top of my head was Nobel, but that’s not quite it. Any ideas?

Maybe the designer went crazy and set things in different typefaces. I’d estimate three different were used here. Anything in particular you want us to ID?

The phone number is very close to ‘Avant Garde medium’ but the “8” indicates another face.
