typefaces for a liberal arts university?

June 10, 2005 • ashlea

Hi all, What typefaces (serif, sans serif, script) would you recommend as part of a graphics identity “package” for a small, liberal arts University in the mountains of North Carolina?

The school has a good academic reputation and is known for its small class size, rigorous academics, research, holistic approach to learning, community involvement and devoted faculty. Its history only dates back to the early 1920s, so it is not a school with a strong historical slant as many other schools in the East are. In other words, not an ivy-covered campus with gorgeous old buildings!…but a campus with mountain views :)

Also worth noting in the school’s identity is its location in the city of Asheville, known for its outdoor recreation and arts/crafts/music scenes.

Needs to have a typeface identity that will hold up for 5 years, more or less.

In short, the type families need to say good/serious academics yet also be able to say friendly, eclectic, and fresh, too.

Thanks in advance for your ideas! ashlea
