a moment of silence. calligraphy for peace.

January 3, 2009 • Yaronimus-Maximus

hey people. i made a small movie clip, calling for a moment of silence, dedicated to the dual casualties of the current war between israel and palestine. i created the calligraphy based on the "wilhelm klingspor gotisch" font, with my own hebrewed version to it, adding slight hebrew serifs called "Okatsim". i used a red calligraphy ink ("Winsor & Newton") on a smooth surface paper (although the movie has low quality, so the nuances are lost). i would be glad to show better resolution scans of the calligraphy if you want to. link: link to the movieclip.
