2010-07-02 18:27:50 • paul d hunt

Optima can certainly complement Palatino, in a rather obvious manner, as you infer. They have similar letter forms, and a similar degree of gloss representing the trace of process: the flaring and concave terminals in Optima, and the flexed serifs of Palatino.

If you are prepared to focus more on that surface element of style, rather than the letter proportions, you might consider faces such as Laudatio or my Brown Gothic.
