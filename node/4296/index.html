It's a near certainty that it's handlettering.

"Come in We're Open" and "Sorry We're Closed" signs exactly like this have been sold at hardware stores for as long as I can remember. I'd say the designer scanned and combined them for this hybrid version.

You might find a font with a similar style to the script, but I doubt very much you will find an exact match.
