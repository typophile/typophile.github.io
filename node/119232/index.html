_Travel On A Dream_ seems to have mini serifs. If you want to try and modify Avant Garde Gothic, you might look at a well made mini serif font. I like Gert Wiescher's new font Dylan-Copperplate Bold [img:sites/default/files/old-images/DylanCopperplateBoldInUseSM_6636.jpg] http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/wiescherdesign/dylan-copperplate/ Don
2014-09-05 21:16:50 • donshottype
Or you could skip the work and follow Dick's suggestion of Serif Gothic Bold. Your image is a cameo or negative, light letters on a dark background. Letters in this form can look optically identical to a lighter weight. Nice explanation in the book on Frutiger's life [img:sites/default/files/old-images/FrutigerTypefacesP333DAvenir_3938.jpg] But your negative letters look heavier than Serif Gothic Bold. I conclude from this that your letters are a further emboldened version of Serif Gothic Bold. Or are a modified version of Serif Gothic Heavy. Don
