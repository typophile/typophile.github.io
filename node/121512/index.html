Compare to an undocumented near clone of Canterbury, with the name Landsowne Commercial [img:sites/default/files/old-images/LandsowneCommercialAsDoktor_4710.gif] Not quite a 100% match. Canterbury Old Style was designed by Morris F. Benton for ATF in 1920, it was not completed for production until 1926. You can also use yet another font based on Canterbury, which credits the source, Londonderry Air NF, by Nick Curtis [img:sites/default/files/old-images/LondonderryAirNFAsDoktor_3789.gif] https://www.myfonts.com/fonts/nicksfonts/londonderry-air-nf/ Don
