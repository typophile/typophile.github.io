AFAIK it’s the only font that Slimbach has made that has trapping*. I’ve heard from a few sources that he doesn’t believe they’re useful. And since Twombly’s first famous font (Mirarae**) has some juicy trapping, I suspect she had to put her foot down to get trapping included in Myriad. And if it’s true that the two didn’t get along, I’m sure that didn’t help.
