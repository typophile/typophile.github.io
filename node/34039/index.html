This Day in Type - Call for submissions

May 26, 2007 • munsonbh

This Day in Type is looking for a few good typographers to submit original artwork for June 2-4. The work is for the ongoing calendar art project, and the early days need a few helping hands until more people become involved.

Quick guidelines:

  • 720 x 450 pixel space
  • You may use only one type family
  • The art must primarily communicate the date
  • Submit the art between 3-4 days in advance (depending on your time zone)
