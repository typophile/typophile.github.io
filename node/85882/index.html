Page design by Adonis Durado

September 28, 2011 • John Lyttle

This cover of the 32nd annual Best of News Design book was designed by Adonis Durado, design director of the Times of Oman & Al Shabiba in Muscat, Oman. As he explains in [[http://www.snd.org/2011/09/meet-the-man-behind-the-cover/|this article]], Durado used Whitman (designed by Kent Lew) as the main text, and chose Ingeborg Block (by Michael Hochleitner) for the nameplate. No photos, just typography on this cover. I think it looks great. Anyone care to discuss the design and typography of this cover? [img:sites/default/files/old-images/SND32-cover_4426.png]
