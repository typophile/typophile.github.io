2005-06-25 01:15:32 • Mark Simonson
Eklektic and Legerdemain are both digitizations of the old VGC typeface Elektrik (two k's) and either is about as legit a version as you are going to find.  Eklektic came with my version of CorelDraw (8) as well.  Eklektik has a Corel copyright,  not BT,  and Buyfonts is offering the old Fontbank fonts. Firenze ITC is bundled also on the same CD.
