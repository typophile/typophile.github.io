A good certificate needs…

  • lots of white space
  • a pattern somewhere
  • black ink + red emphasis
  • to be letterpress printed

A typical certificate has…

  • a bunch of blackface type
  • a bunch of calligraphic type
  • printed on a lazer printer
  • been stuck in a $2 frame

So, my advice would be to design a certificate that you’d fine aesthetically pleasing enough to hang up on a wall as an actual piece of art.
