Brand Book sources

April 9, 2009 • ladydeas

Is there a definitive source for brand book inspiration? Anything I can source is slightly 1990’s… and not in a good way!!
It kinda depends on the market of your client. For inspiration, I’ve always kept a crib folder on my desktop and dumped anything I see online that I find interesting. It’s really easy to run through this folder and pull things just to get some quick ideas for directions a project could go. Starting from scratch is much more difficult. Magazines that target similar aspects of your client’s business are a good place to start. Design Annuals are also good, I’ve always pulled a lot of good things from the Society of Publication Designers and Type Directors Club books.
