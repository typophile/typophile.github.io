Independent skateboard trucks

September 10, 2009 • Nick Sherman

This typeface been used by the Independent Truck Company since at least 1978. I always figured it was hand-done because I had only seen it used in their logo, but I've been seeing it recently in a lot of their packaging, DVDs, etc, leading me to believe it’s a proper typeface. I've only seen it used in all-caps. [img:sites/default/files/old-images/Indy_3824.jpeg]
