Adobe AIR rasterization environment?

September 28, 2010 • MaximT

Now that Flash gets another jolt http://blog.digitalbackcountry.com/2010/09/rims-playbook-and-adobe-air/ , what sort of antialising/rasterizer are we dealing with when font is used on Adobe AIR?
The rasterization of non-HTML content is handled by the Portable Font Rasterizer (PFR) which will give you subpixel rendering. The results are comparable to CoolType which is what you get when viewing a PDF in Acrobat/Adobe Reader. The PFR has been optimized for mobile and has some rasterization enhancements which CoolType currently lacks. HTML content is rendered via WebKit and will match the platform the AIR app is running on, I believe.
