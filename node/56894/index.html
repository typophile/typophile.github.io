tops of font cut off in Font Book

April 11, 2009 • Bloodtype

Does anyone know how to make a font display correctly in Font Book on the mac? I made it in Fontlab, and generated a TTF, which looks and works fine in Illustrator and other software, but has its head cut off in Font Book. Sorry for newb question. Cheers

Usually this means your ‘em square’ is not set to the size the letters have been drawn. Check in particular to make sure your tallest letter above the baseline is the same, or ideally less tall than the value in Font Info/Metrics and Dimensions/Key Dimensions/Ascender.

Increase that Ascender value to cover the tallest letter, or scale the whole font so the tallest letter fits inside the Ascender value.

It could be something else, but this is usually checked first.

Cheers!
