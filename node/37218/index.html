Is it possible that the font you remember was just similar to Zubilo? The bouncy, irregular, blocky shape style is not really a new thing, if you go back to Artistik, and Harquil (which has been digitized as "Harlequin", and is shown in Solotype and Petzendorfer books, and very similar to Gaiety Girl), which are both Art Nouveau era (over 110 years old). There are others, including Softie, that MyFonts Uses all over its site. - Mike Yanega
