The sample of Bernase Roman shown on that other thread shows an ‘a’ with that shape. The way film types were used, as I understand it, they could be distorted using lenses to make them shorter, longer, slanted, etc. So those proportions should not detract from the fact that the basic shapes of all the letters in your sample seem to match letters available in Bernase Roman. The only other typeface that has much similarity is Romana, but it has more differences than Bernase. I think Florian’s saying ‘Similar to ITC Bernase’ is a good way to express that this looks like a slightly customized version.
