I’ve done several co-ordinated schemes.

Brown (Sans) and Worldwide (Serif) are both semi-condensed, have a similar finish, with display and text weights.

Here’s a PDF. http://www.africawoman.net/images/educatinggirlsinafrica.pdf

Walburn is a serif headline face that also matches Brown.

They are the heads in this PDF, today’s Globe and Mail: http://www.newseum.org/media/dfp/pdf7/CAN_TGAM.pdf

You can also see them at Myfonts.

For a Jenson-style combo, Nicholas is a display weight of Goodchild.
