There are two different ways of describing figures (as represented by Quark XPress and InDesign), and I’ve mixed them up in that chart, which isn’t correct.

The icons are supposed to represent the four figure qualities: Proportional, Tabular, Old-style, and Lining,

rather than the four instances: Oldstyle Proportional Oldstyle Tabular Lining Proportional Lining Tabular
