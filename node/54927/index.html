2009-02-18 04:32:38 • aluminum

Ok, I’m going to be brutally honest. Brace yourself. I don’t mean to offend.

I quite literally winced and pulled away from the screen. I suspect this is not the response you’re going for … man, the font just could NOT be worse. Choose something that is the most opposite that font.

A quick google search shows the [[http://www2.hud.ac.uk/news/index.php|university colors]] are a much darker navy blue and burnt orange. Why not use those actual color values rather than these neon versions?

The ladder metaphor is cliche, but in this example it’s too fragile to even execute the metaphor properly.

Bottom line? I don’t think you need a logo mark – I’d just focus on a logotype alone. It’s a lot of words; and with the right type treatment, it can be more than enough by itself.
