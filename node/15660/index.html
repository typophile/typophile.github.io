Wandered out and saw Domino this afternoon. No specific fonts mentioned, just Walken looking shocked as he sees a mock-up graphic for the reality show “The Bounty Squad” and he starts freaking out, repeatedly, to the point where he interrupts a meeting with the bounty hunters themselves to take a call so that he can ream out the designer of the mock-ups: “Why did you change the fucking font? WHY DID YOU CHANGE THE FUCKING FONT??!!!” This while the bounty gang sit pondering whether or not to sign up for the show. What’s interesting is that whether or not the Walken character actually cares about the font change (maybe he’s just putting on a show for the hunters), the use of a given font to “represent” them is portrayed with some sense of import. If nothing else, it was nice to hear the word font in a movie theatre.

The problem, however, is that the opening & closing credits, and the sporadic textual markers throughout the movie, are typographically horrible.
