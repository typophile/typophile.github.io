@populuhv: don’t castigate me for the quality, but I went out and shot some pics for you, just before dawn. You can temporarily see them at [[http://niels.kicks-ass.org/public/buildings/|my site]]. It is a block of 91 houses in the style of the [[http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Amsterdam_School|Amsterdam School]], so bowfinpw was pretty right in his art nouveau-assertion.

I would not be surprised if these numerals were either designed by the architect or if their design was especially commissioned for this project. As the block of houses is on the list of protected monuments and is currently being renovated, there is actually some chance I will find out.

In the mean time, I set about digitising these figures, as a way of learning how to use FontForge. I’ll keep you posted on either development.
