hey. i’m working on my first serious typeface. my aim is not to create something highly original, but to get into the type and construction rules. now i got to a point of the process when some feedback would be very helpful for me.

thank you for your critique, comments and advices.

I like this, it has a hap-hazardous feel to it which gives it originality. I think the strokes are to be credited with that feeling, I’ve noticed that they taper at the top and fan out as your eye moves down.

The J/j is very unique as well. The only characters that don’t seem to follow the tapering effect are h, n and m, they start with the taper at the top and the proceeding stroke starts thin, thickens at the apex of the curve and then thins out again. I think this works fine on the m, but the outside stroke should probably be consistent with the vertical, based on your serifs.

The a does seem to be inconsistent with the rest of the characters though. It’s very top heavy and the bowl doesn’t seem to support the weight well. I might try opening up the bowl more. The s feels as if it’s falling forward, you might want to thin the top curve a bit.

All in all, this is a very nice typeface. Are there plans to do an italicized version or bolder weights?
