On pairings that just don't match up

February 18, 2007 • blank

Lately I’ve been pairing Eureka with Monotype Grotesque. In theory the pairing doesn’t work–they’re not remotely close historically, the x-heights differ obviously, and Eureka has a slight stress whereas MT Grot has none. But for some reason they just seem to work together, and nobody seems to mind. I realize that this is partly due to my only using it in situations where the typefaces are not use where they’ll line up and the differences are noticeable, but there’s more to it than that.

Is this just one of those odd exceptions to pairing conventions? Or is it common to come up with such couples that contrast well as long as they’re not up against each other?

I’d love to hear thoughts on this. I did an untutored pairing of Univers LT and Minion Pro, without realizing there were guidelines to pairing. So, although it’s too late to change anything with that client, I can’t wait to hear the insights brought on this subject.
