Mojo got it.

As I recall, the American Monotype company could not get a license from the Klingspor foundry to make Kabel available in the USA. So they copied it. It was available in metal in regular and condensed widths and in a variety of weights. I’m not sure that any of the digital incarnations of Kabel offers condensed weights.

It is still available as metal in the USA from a few places, including M&H Type in San Francisco.

Some Monotype shops in the USA sold it under the name Cable. Supposedly the original name Kabel was given to honor the laying of a transatlantic communications line.
