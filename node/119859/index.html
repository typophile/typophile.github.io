Berthold Baskerville Book appears to be a good option to recreate most of this (if it was indeed typeset in 1969), but it doesn’t have non-aligning figures.

It does have a 2 with an upward-pointing serif at the foot, and a C with two serifs, which some of the other versions of Baskerville do not.

http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/berthold/baskerville-book-be/

If you want the non-aligning numerals and don’t mind a 2 with a slightly different serif and a bit more of a loop, then ITC New Baskerville isn’t bad:

http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/adobe/itc-new-baskerville/
