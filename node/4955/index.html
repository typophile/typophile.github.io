2004-05-27 19:22:41 • squeeze
Oscar, a few issues here imo ....

* The dot on the i cutting into the F creates
an odd counter shape and sharpness that
doesn't fit in with the rounds.
* the Vertical stem widths appear consistent,
but the horizontals are inconsistent.
-- the counters on the m are too tall.
--the crossbar on the F seems too thick.
--the top horizontal stem on the F seems thick
in relation to the other letters, too.
You might set some guides, adjust,
and then eyeball a print out. (Actually, not you
but your friend.)
* the m seems too wide.
