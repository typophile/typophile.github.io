Hi, I am after some opinions as to a nice sans face to compliment Joanna MT. Sorry I know this could be very broad but I would just like to see other peoples suggestions? Thanks.

Please refer to Joanna MT here: http://www.fontshop.com/search/?q=Joanna+MT&x=0&y=0

The (too?) obvious choice is Gill Sans, who designed both Joanna and his namesake sans.

But how about one of the more modern takes on the the British sans, like Agenda (Font Bureau), Today Sans (Scangraphic), Bliss (Jeremy Tankard), or Foundry Sterling? Or the sans that inspired Eric Gill in the first place: Johnston’s Underground signage face?
