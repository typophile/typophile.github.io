August 26, 2008 • Jill Shimabukuro

We are publishing a collection of poems by Gendun Chopel next Spring, and I’m hoping this group might be able to recommend a typesetter who specializes in Tibetan. This is a bilingual edition, with the original texts to be composed in native Tibetan script, or Uchen.

I would recommend contacting Jo De Baerdemaeker. He is just finishing up his PhD and his main focus has been Tibetan. He has done extensive research and is also a typographer and designer.
