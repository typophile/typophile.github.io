I’ve found no match on any of the databases, only similars.

Hiroshige gets the A right, but you’d have to edit the leg of the |R| and the terminals of the |S| to get it to match (plus add the distressing): http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/linotype/hiroshige/

These two fonts would require a bit less editing (serif on the |A|, tweaks to the |R| and |S|, plus distressing): http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/bitstream/arrus/ http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/itc/legacy-serif/

Dafont.com has a category called Fancy, subcategory Eroded; you may be able to find a distressed serif there that might work for you.
