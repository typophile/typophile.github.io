...a contemporary interpretation of a heritage brand... Modern Suite The Scotch Modern was the prevalent serif style immediately prior to the Russian revolution. During the Soviet era, it was revived as New Standard, which, like revivals of the Scotch Modern elsewhere, was "cleaned up", so this finely-detailed digitization of the letterpress effect represents a fresh take on the genre.
