> Any thoughts on a good font for hand made road side signs? It's too broad a question. It's like someone you've never met asking you to pick clothes for them. In general you'd want a font that was easy to read (not highly condensed or stylized), probably in upper & lower case rather than all caps, and big enough to be read at the distance people will see it (you might want to do a test). Beyond that your choice of fonts will depend on picking a font that's appropriate for the event. What kind of event are you advertising? What kind of people do you want the sign to attract? And so forth. For example the appropriate fonts for an old-fashioned ice cream social might be different than ones for a hot-rod show. As far as saving ink, I doubt if it's going to be a big factor if you're just making a few signs.
