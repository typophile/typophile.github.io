ID request: font used by Westboro Baptist Church protesters

June 10, 2009 • GSnyder

Do any of you recognize the font used in these placards? (My apologies for the content; it’s what they do. There really isn’t a milder example…)

It’s not recognized by Identifont or WhatTheFont. I have the impression that it’s probably an Internet freebie. But they’re unlikely to be typophiles – it’s probably a relatively widely distributed font.

Notable characteristics are the canted line endings on the uppercase I, E, F, and L; the angled-but-squared-off line endings on A, M, and R; and the legs on K and R just touching the verticals.
