Laser printers last forever. I got one from my old job once, it had been used by a department of 8 for years, I got it home and it lasted me another 2 years before giving up the ghost. The other thing to bear in mind about laser printers is that the toner is expensive but one cartridge will last you for years. And the paper is cheap. I’m still on my first cartridge for my Ricoh which I’ve had for 2 years.
