2011-09-26 15:06:29 • J Weltin

Scotch Modern (2008) and Figgins Sans (2008) have them as default (for mixed-case setting), as well as alternate, cap-height figures weighted and proportioned for all-cap settings.

http://www.fontshop.com/fonts/downloads/shinntype/figgins_sans_family/ http://www.fontshop.com/fonts/singles/shinntype/scotch_modern_regular/

Compare the difference by clicking on the “Gear” icon and applying “case sensitive forms”.

And by “recent” do you mean more recent than Miller Text (1997)? MC maintained Austin’s original three-quarter lining figure scheme, which is somewhat endemic to Scotches (as witnessed also in Nick’s interpretations).
