Common Things is a looser version of the kind of lettering used on billheads and letterheads at that time. [[http://www.letterheadfonts.com/|Letterhead Fonts]] might have something with a similar appeal to you, especially [[http://www.letterheadfonts.com/fonts/billhead.php|LHF Billhead]], or the [[http://new.myfonts.com/search/coffee+bin/fonts/|Coffee Bin fonts]] at MyFonts, although they have a more precise, engraved look than the drawn lettering on the book jacket.
