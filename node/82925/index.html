Special characters for Frisian?

June 9, 2011 • Andreas Stötzner

In [[http://typophile.com/node/33647?page=3|this conversation]] A. Twardoch points at a document that indicates Đđ (0110, 0111) is used for Frisian. This was new to me. Unicode chart annotations don’t hint at this. A quick search in the web revealed next to nothing. Does anyone know something about it?
According to the samples on these pages: http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/North_Frisian#Samples http://de.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nordfriesische_Sprache#Sprachbeispiele some dialects use ð. So it could be that either StAGN or some Wikipedia contributor didn't knew the difference between Đđ and Ðð.
