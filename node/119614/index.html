2014-09-18 10:12:14 • donshottype

The Wikipedia article http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Litre#Symbol gives more precisions on the various symbols used for litre. Concerning current usage in Canada, Druide has a long article (in French) http://www.druide.com/enquetes/quel-symbole-pour-le-litre and states that

Quant au symbole l minuscule cursif (script, bouclé), il serait officiellement interdit au Canada depuis 1981.
i.e. use of the script l for liter would be officially prohibited in Canada since 1981.

You nevertheless find it in this article on food labelling of the Canadian Food Inspection Agency.
