Ok these two are not exactly new, but have never been posted here. Comments are appreciated!

http://kateliev.com/images/TYPOCallista.png Callista Short Description: Callista (from the Greek for “most beautiful”) is a fat cursive typeface, inspired by the works of François Boltana in the early 1970s and

those of Milka Peykova in late 1970s.

Further reading and links: Behance http://bit.ly/kabehance MyFonts http://bit.ly/kacallista

http://www.kateliev.com/images/TYPOGaytan.png Gaytan Sans & Serif Short Description: Gaytan (Bulgarian for braid) is a fresh new insight on archaic letter forms. A family of two unicase typefaces – a modern looking sans and

more classic looking serif, equipped with many alternates, so they can suit any typographic taste.

Further reading and links: Behance http://bit.ly/GaytanBehance MyFonts http://bit.ly/MFGaytan
