There are a lot of really nice calligraphic fonts, Chris, and what you choose all depends on what you are looking for, how much text you need to set, the size of your document, etcetera. If you want a truly calligraphic certificate, why not consider employing a calligrapher? Veer has a great selection of calligraphic fonts - why not start there? I don't know of any sources depicting the right layout of a certificate. I've designed a few myself and have always just been creative with the layouts.
