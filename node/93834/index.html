Pastiche Brush released

June 12, 2012 • daverowland

Happy to announce that my new font, Pastiche Brush, is now available on [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/schizotype/pastiche-brush/|MyFonts]].

It’s inspired by the film titles of [[http://annyas.com/screenshots/updates/imitation-of-life-1959-douglas-sirk-lana-turner-john-gavin-sandra-dee/|Imitation of Life]]. The aanyas.com site is a great source of inspiration.

Thanks for looking, Dave
