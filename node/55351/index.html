I was thinking about my last visit to Europe and got to feeling nostalgic. Last summer, I went to the Guggenheim in Venice and had the pleasure of seeing pieces by my favorite sculptor, Alexander Calder.

[img:sites/default/files/old-images/762553137_ph_web_4074.jpg]

I’m not really big on sculptures but I love his mobiles because they appear so delicate. I get inspiration for my own work from his pieces. It forces me to pay special attention to the minute details and try to embellish on them in subtle ways.

Here is a link to the Calder Foundation: [[http://calder.org|http://calder.org/]]
