49 lead letter prints added to "Schriftensammlung"

January 5, 2008 • Bleisetzer

Peußisches Bleisatz-Magazin added 49 prints of old german lead letter full character sets to its so called Schriftensammlung (font collection) Thank you all, especially Mike Yanega (with respect.. huhuuu...) for your support helping me to identify these old and wonderful types. Here's the list... You may use the search function "stichwortsuche" down left on the website to find a font you want to see.
