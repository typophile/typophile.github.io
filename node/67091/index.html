Looking for a modern elegant replacement to Minion suitable for setting magazines. The x height needs to be very similar. Thanks in advance.
  1. What kind of magazine?
  2. How is it printed?
  3. What is it printed on?
  4. Who are the readers?

There’s dozens of choices. Answering a few simple questions regarding context makes the task far easier and removes aesthetic guesswork. Garamond and Sabon are pretty good fallbacks. Nick Shinn would be a good person to ask.
