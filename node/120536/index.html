You said that you have used Storm’s Walbaum 120. I presume that you know that this is the display cut of the family (it takes its name from the approximate point size it is best used at; cf. this PDF). You might not know that there is also Walbaum 10 for text (at around 10 point) and even Walbaum 10 XL with increased x-height. I can recommend both. Have a look at them!
