Acording to [[http://www.google.com/webfonts/specimen/Della+Respira|Google Web Fonts]] page:
Della Respira a revival of the Della Robbia typeface by American Type Founders (ATF).
Della Robbia is currently published by both [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/bitstream/della-robbia/|Bitstream]] and [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/tilde/della-robbia/|Tilde]]. MyFonts.com has no reference to ATF and no design date for either of those.
