Sears Holiday "Wish" typeface

December 3, 2007 • BlueEyedPeas

This year, Sears brought back its old "Wish" catalog that was absent for 14 years, visiting a Sears store over the weekend I came across heavy promotion of their entire "wish" theme, there must have been close to a hundred red in-store signage boards with the word "wish" prominently in the center, inspiring the holiday spirit and nicely decorating everything throughout the store. "wish" :) I could've made a picture of these posters for you to ID the font, so here is a banner I found online using the same script font.
I gave this one a good college try, as they say, but I think it is custom. The style reminds me very strongly of Rob Leuschke's work, and it's possible he did this lettering for Sears, since he does a lot of custom work. - Mike Yanega
