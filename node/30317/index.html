SOTA Type and Design Education Survey Now Open

January 3, 2007 • tamye

Attention: Type & Design Educators

Are you a typography instructor? Is your school planning to offer a typography or type design class, or would you like to propose one? Do you teach design but want to include type design, usage, and history as well? The Society of Typographic Aficionados (SOTA) will be presenting an Education Forum at TypeCon2007, SOTA’s annual type and typography conference, and we want your input on how to most effectively meet your needs.

Please take this short survey, and pass it on to anyone else who might be interested. It will be available until January 31, 2007. The TypeCon2007 Education Forum wants to hear from you!

Thanks, Ilene Strizver Chairperson, Education Forum TypeCon2007
