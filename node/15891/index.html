That page is pretty funny. Several of those are not what they say they are:

  • The TIME logo is custom drawn. Definitely not Times New Roman, despite the name.

  • Before they switched to a custom version of Myriad for their branding, Apple had a custom version of ITC Garamond called Apple Garamond. It was more condensed than ITC Garamond, but less condensed than ITC Garamond Condensed.

  • The National Geographic logo is custom, though it’s based on Times New Roman.

  • Mobil has their own custom font, sort of a cross between Helvetica and Avant Garde Gothic.
