as Miss Tiffany said, it begins at your cusor or active page. the "jumping around" that you describe is the result of the stacking order of elements on the page. when checking an entire document the app doesn't move from one text frame or story to the next by left-to-right, top-to-bottom as a reader might. in my observations, it moves from top to bottom in the stacking order on the page.
