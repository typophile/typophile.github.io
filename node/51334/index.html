Magazine Re-Design

November 5, 2008 • Ryan Jarrell

I am doing a re-design of our magazine. It is about art, design, new media/video, technology, and communication. We have been using the Univers family for everything and it’s gotten stale.

I need a tech feel that also has a friendliness to it. Right now I am looking at Klavika, Purista, and Aaux Pro as displays, but possibly body text as well. I also want to find a good serif typeface to pair it with.

Not sure if I am way off track. Any thoughts?

Check out the [[http://www.myfonts.com/search?search%5Btext%5D=camingo|Camingo]] family. Tech with a friendly feel IMO. Camingo was first, and I guess Camingo Dos is a new version with some changes.
