January 15, 2013 • thierry blancpain

Today I’m happy to announce our new website (including an all-new shop system) as well as our newest release, GT Pressura.

GT Pressura Designed by Moiré

Inspired by type stamped on shipping boxes, GT Pressura uses the visual effect of ink spreading under pressure as a stylistic device. The condensed sans serif alludes to a scientific background but with its rounded corners shows a soft and friendly side in larger sizes. With proportional and monospaced variants it is able to hit a wide range of notes whenever needed.
