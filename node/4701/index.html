TypeCon 2004 Auction

May 10, 2004 • kegler

Of the many events planned for TypeCon, this year's benefit auction will be a great way to support your typophile habits and support SOTA at the same time. We will have a preview list posted soon that will give you some idea of the great items you can bid on at the Friday night auction.

More to the point, we are starting to collect donations for the auction. If you have a spare 1923 ATF catalog, signed Frederic Goudy smoke proofs, or even more modest typographic items, we will grateful accept all donations. We will acknowledge all donors (unless you wish to remain anonymous) and all donations are tax deductible in the US since SOTA is a 501(c)(3) not-for profit organization.

Send an email to richard@typesociety.org if you would like to donate any items and we will send you additional information on how to donate.

Thanks
