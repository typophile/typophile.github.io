Animated tribute to Granule typeface

September 29, 2009 • Corey Holms

http://vimeo.com/6746313

Student Steffen Knoesgaard’s Motion Week assignment for Hyper Island was to make a short animation to be completed in an accelerated schedule of only 24 hours based on something that you like.

I am honored that he chose a typeface of mine, and am blown away by the results. Hopefully you will appreciate it as well.

Motion design by Steffen Knoesgaard Audio by The Battles & DJ Koze
