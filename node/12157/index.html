[[TypoWiki]] : Books A wealth of information has been published about the art of type. “The [[Triumvirate]] of Typography”—selected primary works of three authors—offers a foundation for every great typographic library. Included below is an evolving index of notable books written about the art and history of [[typography]] and [[type-design]].
