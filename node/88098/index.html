Cheers.

The most similar font I was able to find was Ray Larabie’s Moon Cresta. It could be modified without too much gruesome work to resemble the Yachting masthead, especially if one were not examining them side by side. The t crossbar could slide over, and the stem be truncated. The g tail could be similarly truncated. I’d use the l.c. y to enlarge and then tweak for the Y. The rest could pass without changes, as long as you were not using the calibrated eyeballs that many people seem to have here.
