"Gare De Lyon" and Dijon, Vallorbe e.t.c. match Chambord Condensed, also by Roger Excoffon 1945-51 and released in several weights and Italic by the Fonderie Olive. As far as I know Chambord has not been digitized. For a full alphabet specimen see Jaspert's Encylopedia of Type faces, 1970, at p. 259. Don
