Hi Bob, I believe you probably have it. The unusual ampersand matches. The HPLHS fonts are very poor quality -- designed as prop fonts for the Lovecraft Society games -- but this one could be expanded to be a fair match for the lettering. Indeed this may be where the poster makers obtained the letters. The HPLS headline font is from a 1920s newspaper. All I know is that I didn't spot it in the 1923 ATF specimen book. Don

If you like the ampersand, and are prepared to use a narrower headline font of slightly different design, I have a couple of suggestions. Toronto Gothic http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/ephemera/toronto-gothic/ Still has rounded corners and irregular lines that are meant to make it look worn from years of use, but much better made than the HPLS prop font.
