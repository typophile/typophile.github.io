2007-08-24 18:08:13 • Michel Boyer

This looks a lot like a specimen of what is now Monotype Modern Wide (it was very likely originally sold as Modern No. 8 or somesuch, but you’d probably need to go to a contemporary specimen book to get the exact citation).

However, the digital version is a large optical size, and the scan you posted is a smaller one. You won’t get the effect.

Fortunately, the Computer Modern faces Michel mentions are reasonably similar, and gloriously do come in a complete family of optical sizes. In fact, they deserve credit for being one of the first optically scaled families in digital form.
