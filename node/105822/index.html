Can’t find a match and actually doubt there’s one, commercially available speaking. The man behind Editions 205 is [[http://www.bureau205.fr|Damien Gautier of Bureau 205]], some of his typefaces are [[http://www.myfonts.com/foundry/Damien_Gautier|commercially available]]. This one may be a bespoke typeface he created for the occasion. He may have started for instance with his [[http://www.bureau205.fr/colonel_article_f.html|Colonel]] and created a light rounded weight, some sort of DIN rounded stencil light. Try asking him, he may answer. Some alternatives, out-of-the-box: [[http://www.hypefortype.com/browse-fonts/what-s-hot/argn.html|ARGN]], [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/parachute/pf-din-stencil|DIN Stencil PF]], [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/reserves/vacant|Vacant]], [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/reserves/scape|Scape]] (may be the closest), [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/coreyholms/ne10|Ne10]], [[http://www.fontshop.com/fonts/downloads/club21/skye_ot|Skye]], [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/words-pictures/flute|Flute]], [[http://www.cataloguestudio.fr/filter/all/Signo|Signo]]
