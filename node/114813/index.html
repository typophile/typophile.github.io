Postopia Font - What font is this? Thank you
Three different forms of o suggest that it’s lettering. There are fonts that give a similar effect, sometimes tagged party, cartoon, crazy, funny, fun, comic book, bouncy, expressive, etc. Here are a few that I like. Team Spirit and Toon Casual http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/fontry/script1-team-toon/ Bucktooth http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/tymime-fonts/bucktooth/ Rocha http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/eurotypo/rocha/ Best Party of the Weekend http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/essfonts/best-party-of-the-weekend/ Thats All Folks http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/comicraft/thats-all-folks/ Wayoshi http://www.dafont.com/wayoshi.font?fpp=100&text=postopiacom Foo http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/typodermic/foo/ Louis, mainly for the alternate M which matches the M in .com http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/canadatype/louis/ Don
