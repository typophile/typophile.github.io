Hi Yotam,

have you seen the wiki entry for [[blogs]]?

Most of them aren’t (purely) focussed on type design, though.

Unzipped regularly has type design related articles. This old thread lists a couple more. There’s Ask H&FJ by Hoefler & Frere-Jones, Thomas Phinney’s Typblography, Paul D. Hunt’s Pilcrow Blog, Joke Gossé and Josh Scruggs, Ramiro Espinoza’s free thoughts on lettering and type and Dan Reynold’s TypeOff. P22 has a blog, Underware has its Walhalla and House Industries do show & tell, too. Several typophiles are blogging right here.
