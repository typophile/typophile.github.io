Your vertical metrics values exceed the UPM (901+250=1151) and this behaviour would indicate that you have not balanced the metrics accordingly. If you read through [[http://www.kltf.de/downloads/FontMetrics-kltf.pdf|Karsten Luecke’s guide to font metrics]] carefully and adjust your settings accordingly you should find that the vertical metrics start behaving as you would expect. At the very least you will have a greater understanding of what those settings change.

Additionally, the Typophile threads listed below all deal with this issue in detail:

http://typophile.com/node/77906 http://typophile.com/node/13081
