2009-06-01 00:45:25 • Frode Bo Helland
"wouldn’t a bold weight look higher next to a regular weight?" Actually, the reason you increase xheight as you go up in weight is that there is an illusion that the xheight looks smaller in bold weights. The increase is just a compensation just like overshoot is a compensation. ChrisL
