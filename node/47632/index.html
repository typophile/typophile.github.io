How to Write a Personal Letter

July 22, 2008 • nancy sharon collins

Here is a .pdf of the instructions for writing a personal letter (“Writing Dictum”) that I presented at TypeCon. It is written tongue-in-cheek but every word is true.

Pass it along.

Personally I do my best bits of writing randomly. Sentences come to my mind at the most bizarre times and hours. I jot down when they spring to mind, and then when I have enough to cover the bulk of what I wish to say, I assemble all the bits and start to construct the actual letter.

The warning was quite humorous.

Jon.
