body: The gutter is an interval spacing that resides between two facing pages in a
  layout spread. This measure is usually twice the size of the outside margin. Additionally,
  the term gutter may be used to define any interval space that serves as a buffer
  or separating unit for blocks of type, photography, and other artwork in a page
  layout.
