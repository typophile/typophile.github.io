ataxia: new font from justi

October 16, 2010 • justi

Good evening! I am pleased to announce my new font, called Ataxia. I would like to hear opinions about the project!

Ataxia was designed to be used in long texts such as books and magazines. The font has the weights regular, bold, italic and bold italic as well as ligatures, small caps, oldstyle numbers and support for many languages, with more than 500 glyphs.

http://new.myfonts.com/fonts/danieljusti/ataxia/
