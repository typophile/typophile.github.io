Anthony, in general converting text to outlines is regarded as circumventing the EULA.

I believe the intention of most EULAs is not to forbid embedding the font an sich, but instead to forbid using the font at all inside a PDF. Therefore, you are violating perhaps not the letter of the EULA, but certainly its spirit.

EULAs tend to lag behind technical possibilities – that is, if ‘just outline the fonts’ becomes common practice, it’ll surely get mentioned that is allowed/not allowed next time.
