Blanka and Candy in association with Veer present ‘50’ celebrating 50 years of Helvetica

50 image makers, 50 original works 50x50cm, each in an edition of 50 Epson archival Giclee prints £50 each via blanka.co.uk

[The Brief] Each artist was allocated a random year [1957 - 2006] from
Helvetica’s lifetime. From this they were asked to illustrate an
event of creative or cultural significance which occured during their
allocated year. There were no other rules applied other than if they
used a typeface it must be Helvetica of of their own hand.

Helvetica 50 at Espeis Gallery 90 Wythe Avenue (at n. 11th st.) Williamsburg, Brooklyn New York City

http://www.espeis.nu e: hola@espeis.nu

Opening: September 15th, 7pm With music by Activaire.

Supported by Epson, Commonwealth, GF Smith, Generation Press, Activaire, Build & Victionary.
