Two-minute Type-Management Lesson

December 5, 2004 • as8

Lesson One A famous calligrapher was sitting on a tree resting, doing nothing. A small type designer saw the man of letters and asked him, "Can I also sit like you and do nothing ?" The calligrapher answered : "Sure, why not ?" So, the type designer sat on the ground below the calligrapher and rested. All of a sudden, a foundry appeared, hired the type designer and took advantage on him. Management Lesson : To be sitting and doing nothing, you must be sitting very, very high up. Lesson Two A sad type designer saw a bull and started to tell him his thoughts. "Oh, bull, I would love to be able to get to the top of the font design tree," sighed the sad type designer, "but I have not got the energy." Suddenly the bull voiced : "Well, why don't you nibble on some of my droppings ?" replied the bull. "They're packed with nutrients." The sad type designer pecked at a lump of dung, and found it actually gave him enough strength to reach the lowest branch of the font tree. The next day, after eating some more dung, he reached the second branch facing a slab typeface. Finally after a fourth night spent in front of FontLab, the type designer was proudly perched at the top of the font tree with an OpenType in his hands. He was promptly spotted by a craftsman, who shot him out of the tree. Management Lesson : Bullshit might get you to the top, but it won't keep you there. Lesson Three A little Finnish type designer girl was flying south for the winter. Getting to the airport, it was so cold that the girl froze and fell to the ground into a large field in the middle of a forest. While she was lying there, a reindeer came by and dropped some dung on her. As the frozen girl lay there in the pile of dung, she began to realize how warm she was. The dung was actually thawing her out! She lay there all warm and happy, and soon began to sing for joy, "the ABCs of love…" (http://tinyurl.com/4f84f). A passing Art Director man heard the girl singing and came to investigate. Following the sound, the AD discovered the girl under the pile of reindeer dung, and promptly dug her out and took advantage on her. Management Lesson : 1.
