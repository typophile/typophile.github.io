The strokes are the kind made with a classic wide-nib pen–I suspect this is a hand-drawn logo from the late 19th/early 20th century.

Steelplate Textura by Typographer Typographer Mediengestaltun has a similar feel (save for the K): http://www.1001fonts.com/steelplate-textura-font.html
