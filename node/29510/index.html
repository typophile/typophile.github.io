[img:sites/default/files/old-images/squashA_1_6514.jpg] I'm designing a new logo for the Hungarian Squash Federation, and i'm not sure if the mark of my mockup is recognizable enough. A few comments on my layout:
  • i still need to work on the colors, this just the first approach
  • the kerning of the the title is default kerning of the font. Will be finalised at the end.
  • Magyar Fallabda Szövetség means Hungarian Squash Federation
Any feedback is welcome, thanks, Péter
