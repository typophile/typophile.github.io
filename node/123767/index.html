Hi, does anybody know what is the name of the font that is used in the TBS SuperStation Graphics photo? The second font is very similar to Univers 39 Ultra Thin Condensed, but the ampersand “&” is slightly, The “R”, “S”, lowercase “a”, “g” and “y” are different. That would be great if anybody knows.

The condensed sans looks like Arizona, published by Brendel & Pabst. This company was sued for forging lots of fonts but also selling some unique ones. I don’t know if this is an original.
