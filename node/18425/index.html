I have client who has been using Magistral ( http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/paratype/magistral/ ) for most of their materials. I want to improve on the typeface, so looking around I found Neosans/Neotech ( http://www.monotypefonts.com/WhatsNew/Releases.asp?show=neo ) which I love but the price doesn’t love me so does anyone know of any other typefaces similar to Neosans that are more affordable

I hate to sound like an aßhole, but… You get what you pay for. An affordable font is likely not as well-made as an expensive one. (This is not a hard and fast rule, but it tends to be true most of the time.)

If you only purchase the weights you need, Neo might be exactly what you’re craving.

Anyhow… I’m sure that someone who is better-acquainted with all of the new releases from all foundries will be along in a short while to provide some alternatives for you.
