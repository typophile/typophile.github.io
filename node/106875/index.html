Can’t find match and really doubt there’s one. This might be hand-drawn, by [[http://www.madebyband.com/portfolio-item/lowbrau-beirhalle|Band]] (try asking). Actually, after having a [[http://www.lowbrausacramento.com/LB_FoodMenu_2413.pdf|closer look to this woodmark]], outlines look very irregular, more irregular than what could be seen on this pictures. May be they started from a font and manually roughened it in order to communicate also this rustic/hand-made/unique/raw feeling through the mark itself. Some in the same vein: [[http://www.myfonts.com/search/antartida|Antartida Rounded Ltt]], many [[http://www.myfonts.com/foundry/Suitcase_Type_Foundry|Suitcase Type Foundry]]’s (Katarine, Vafle, Magion…), [[http://www.myfonts.com/search/quan|Quan]] (Rounded), [[http://lineto.com/The+Fonts/Font+Categories/Text+Fonts/Brauer+Neue|Brauer Neue]], [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/aah-yes/thick-goth|Thick Goth]], [[http://www.losttype.com/font/?name=cubano|Cubano]], [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/corradine/neuron|Neuron]], [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/mti/cachet|Cachet]], [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/ephemera/toronto-gothic|Toronto Gothic]], [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/virtuecreative/the-woods|The Woods]]
