Small print…

August 1 is Swiss National Day, and we’re celebrating in style! SOTA is proud to present the Seattle premiere of Helvetica, Gary Hustwit’s indie film about a typeface.

Helvetica will be screened Wednesday, August 1, at 7 pm at Kane Hall on the University of Washington campus. A discussion with the director and Matthew Carter, one of the designers featured in the film, will take place after the show.

The screening is free and open to the public. Tickets for the general public are now available on a first-come, first-served basis. TypeCon2007 attendees and SOTA members guaranteed seating.

This critically-acclaimed festival favorite is a Swiss Dots production, in association with Veer. This special evening is sponsored by Veer and hosted by the College of Arts and Sciences at the University of Washington. Register now to get a ticket to this fantastic free event.
