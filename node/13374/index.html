Silas

I’d likely want a Baskerville with a bit more character to it than ITC New Baskerville, but not so much character that it is a bastardization (Mrs Eaves). What about the Storm Type Foundry version? Or the Monotype?

But yes, you do want something a bit thinner and with a bit more contrast to it if you are intending to print this letterpress. Mainly to offset the gain you will experience and also because most digital typefaces are drawn a bit heavier than their letterpress brethren. This was a tendency inherited from the photocomposition years.

You can also thin down the face, which I would recommend, with Fontographer or FontLab. Otherwise you are going to end up, on the printed page, with a demi-bold version.
