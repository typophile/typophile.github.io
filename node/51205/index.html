Fonts geared toward event-planning, parties, nightclubs?

November 3, 2008 • Darren Hoyt

I’m doing a website for a large event-booking organization. Their existing design assets have a slick and urban feel and their audience is young adults on the east coast. Their logo is set in Futura currently, but elsewhere on the site they need large headers with a bit more pizazz. The organization provides entertainment for plenty of weddings, but it didn’t feel right when I tried something scripty/formal.

I’d like to find a typeface that is “fun” and speaks to the nightlife they offer: DJs, parties, bands, comedians. Any suggestions would be great!

It may depend on the specific music genre, but in general [[http://www.houseind.com/index.php?page=showfont&id=4|House’s Chalet family]] works well for many of these situations. It’s not too far removed from Futura, but much more glamorous and feminine which goes over well with the wedding business.
