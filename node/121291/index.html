2014-11-13 19:13:07 • AngelFlonne

Just an update for anyone reading this. We got replies from Adobe and Monotype. Their standard license doesn’t include use for games as you’d expect. I had to ask though because we were being told it did by another party.

You have to contact them directly and request a quote for use in games. Why there is no transparent pricing policy is beyond me, fonts in games must be a common request and it all seems a bit opportunist of the big font foundries to me.

2014-11-15 14:31:05 • AngelFlonne

“Why there is no transparent pricing policy is beyond me”

Primarily because the font or a derivative is included with the game itself, and most foundries will charge for redistribution based on the projected distribution of the product, so a lower fee for an indy game vs a font for say Halo or Madden.
