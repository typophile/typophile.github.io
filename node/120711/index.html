I share the opinion of Fivos that this is probably hand made. The lettering is in French, so I suspect a French design as the source. Peignot is one possibility, but I see it’s competitor Roger Excoffon’s Chambord of 1946 as more probable, particularly if the width is expanded to 110%. Chambord was quite common in post WW II France, including signage for the railroad system. Don
