In college I used to work at Armani Exchange, and I remember seeing a style guide saying that the Armani logo font was Bauer Bodoni manually condensed. Also, I saw this style guide back in 1993, and I believe the switched to a Didot face a few years ago, especially since Fabien Baron (ex Harpers Bazaar art director and Didot fan) does a lot of graphics work for them. My personal belief is that they now use a customized version of Didot for the logo and HTF Didot for their sub-brand names, like “armani mania” perfume, which you can tell by looking at the lowercase a.
