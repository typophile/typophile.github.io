St. Louis Outdoor Typography Project

October 16, 2005 • ChuckGroth

This is not so much a question, but an acknowledgement of an interesting project I have my students working on. They are to travel around St. Louis (Missouri, USA) and photograph interesting uses of outdoor typography. They are then to identify the typefaces used, if possible, and the results are posted on this website. Although we’ve only just started, it’s been really interesting, and exciting as well. My students are much more atuned to the type they see around them everyday, and are learning a great deal about type identification. I thought it might be a subject this board would like to hear about.
