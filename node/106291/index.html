Reminds me of Eagle Bold – the symbol of American recovery, drawn in 1933 by Morris Fuller Benton for the National Recovery Association – with the counters blacked out. Metal Font by ATF. Digital font by Font Bureau: http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/fontbureau/eagle/ Don
