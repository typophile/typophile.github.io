[[Indices]] : [[Designers]] : Dr. Rudolf Wolf

German, born 1895 in Hechingen, Germany. Died by suicide on September 7, 1942.

Designer at the [[D. Stempel AG]] in Frankfurt am Main, Germany.

Among other things, Dr. Wolf was the designer of the famous [[Memphis]] type family, and the possible creator of the famous type design test string [[hamburgefontsiv]].

Dr. Wolf received his PhD from the University of Frankfurt, and taught typography in addition to his designing and advertising duties at Stempel.

The [[Klingspor Museum]] in Offenbach has a PDF online listing all known typefaces attributed to Dr. Wolf.
