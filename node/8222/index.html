You can buy all of these fonts directly from Bitstream, or from MyFonts.com. If you just want these weights, you'll probably have to buy each one individually. LT usually means Linotype, but here it definitely means Lights. IT is Italic (the International Typeface Corporation's abreviation is ITC and not IT). BD is bold, and I haven't a devil what ITE is. But check on MyFonts, you'll probably find it there.
