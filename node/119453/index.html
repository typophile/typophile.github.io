ArtDeco fonts are so easy to make and so abundant, it’s not surprising we did not find this exact one. For a well made Art Deco, with your R, I suggest Mark Simonson’s Mostra Nuova http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/marksimonson/mostra-nuova/ Note that it has your demi C and something close to your M as alt letters. I you want an exact match, Mostra Nuova can yield it with just a little tinkering. Don
