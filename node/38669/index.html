Hi! This is my first posting on typophile and though my question isn’t specifically typography realated, I do need educated opinions on a debate that I’m having with my (non-designer) boss.

Now rather than go the entirely traditional route (name, title, address, numbers, email, webpage etc) I wanted to ditch the name line, and just do email. Everyone’s email in the company is first.last@ourcompany.com Similar to this:
