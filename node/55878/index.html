Hello everyone, can anybody please help me identifying this font? It is hand painted, but really really beautiful. Looks like it is a street artist.

As you say, it is hand painted. I don’t think it is based on a font. The ‘a’ is similar to some Celtic style scripts, but the rest of the lettering is nothing like them. Except for the ‘a’, the closest similar style I can suggest is [[http://www.bowfinprintworks.com/Script04Pg14.html|Flourish]], by Judith Sutcliffe (shown in the top three samples on this page in the Script Font ID Guide).

It really is a mix of styles; Compressed oblique n, a few celtic characters, and some really strange top heavy s'. A bit too inconsistent for my taste. I think it is just letters the sign painter liked grouped together rather than a font. It might be worth your while to look up either celtic or uncial for styles and see what you get.

Here is Uncial search from MyFonts: http://www.myfonts.com/search?search%5Btext%5D=uncial

One with similar a and e. http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/neufville/carlomagno-nd/
