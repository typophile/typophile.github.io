I was interested in this font, because it looks like a blend of styles, almost as if it was a hybrid of several designs. It comes from The League of Moveable Type, and they seem to have a noble sounding goal, but I have my reservations about their originality. For example, their Fanwood is a version of Rudolf Ruzicka's "Fairfield". Chunk, from which ChunkFive comes, seems to be blend of two or three existing slab styles, like mixing Giza with Rockwell. I am interested to examine their other fonts to see how much is 'borrowed' and how much is new work. - Mike Yanega
