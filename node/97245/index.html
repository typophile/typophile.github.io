2012-10-19 18:38:17 • timd
Blado, all lower case. The effect of using a foundry text type in display is that of the blow-up, a graphic effect which enhances the roughness of the image—the same effect as that of using a “hand-written” face. There is something of the objet trouvé in this, which gives it an artistic quality. The no-caps setting, which was a popular concept in the 1950s, further serves to distance the setting from a commercial title or headline; there is something poetic in this, in the manner of cummings, and something modern, in the manner of Bayer.
