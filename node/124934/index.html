Space Monkeys Band Logo Circa 1997

April 26, 2015 • haylo

This is the logo / branding for my band “Space Monkeys” developed by Wink Associates for Factory Records in Manchester UK circa 1997.

My research has thrown up “SoftSoul Bold” and “Plakette Serial” as the closest matches but while both are close in spirit they’re not quite right.

Note the unique shape of the “s” “a” and “t” characters.

All samples I have are lower case and I don’t have any special characters or numbers.

Thanks

Neil
