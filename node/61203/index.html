Hope this isn’t spam-bait, as the answer is so obvious :-)

Private Label… http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Private_label

“Private label products or services are typically those manufactured or provided by one company for offer under another company’s brand.”

So its designing a product that can have another company’s logo slapped on it - Apple, Wal-Mart, Halliburton or whatever, rather than designing a product specifically for a brand.
