Hi All, This one is escaping me at the moment. It shares many characteristics of Univers–the only differences I’m noticing off hand are the condensation of certain letters (like the E), the legs of the K not meeting at a point, and an overall more roundedness. The source of this scan is from 1972:

Not sure you’ll find a digital typeface to match this exactly. Futura Bold will get close except for that G. Gotham is also a nice geometric sans but doesn’t have the condensed E or similar K.
