Hi Jeremy!

I live in nearby Pawtucket, Rhode Island, with my partner of twelve years and his elderly pug… :)

As ChrisL suggests, RISD would be a good place to meet fellow typophiles too.

There isn’t much for bookstores, but in Downtown Providence there is a nice “closeout / remainder” store called Symposium Books with a nice selection of art books. On nearby Matthewson Street is the best used bookstore in the state, Cellar Stories, which also has a decent art/graphic design area (IMHO).

Feel free to contact me at jshallri@cox.net if you like.
