As was brought up in the recent thread, the ITC version is not the same as the version shown in Richard's sample, especially in the lowercase. The original Bookman Italic had a slanted roman lowercase, not the cursive italic of the ITC version. This is the biggest difference between the two. There are also a lot of other less obvious differences.
