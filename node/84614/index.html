It’s a typeface by Hal Fiedler, developed for Photo-Lettering, Inc.

Not sure what it’s called exactly, but it can be found in one of Photo-Lettering’s Alphabet Thesauruses. No digital version exists.

The closest digital types are Fielder Gothic and California Grotesk Medium. Both can be found at [[myfonts.com]].
