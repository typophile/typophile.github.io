I don’t think my answers are substantially different from those previously posted.

Futura Light for the subhead (32e Grand Prix….): http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/linotype/futura/

Monaco was most likely custom, based on something like Nobel: http://www.dutchtypelibrary.nl/Nobel_rdrct.html

You could also use one of these modern fonts as the basis for recreating the look (with some editing): http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/softmaker/napoli-serial/ http://www.fontshop.com/fonts/downloads/monokrom/telefon_ot/ot_ps/specimen/tester/ http://www.houseind.com/fonts/neutraface [Neutraface Text Bold]
