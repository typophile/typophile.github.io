“KINDLY SAVE THE DATE” -I suppose you mean the bottom right version- must be a 19th century font (circa 1900) published as [[http://www.findmyfont.com/index.php/fonts/font-preview?fset=Font-Bureau&ffam=Desdemona%20-%20Regular&fid=532933507f12839677ac54eebd68d0af&fsize=60&text=KINDLY%20SAVE%20THE%20DATE!&wrap=2|Desdemona]] by Font Bureau (1992?) and also available in a solid letter version as [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/spiecegraphics/quaint-gothic-sg/|Quaint Gothic SG]] by Spiece Graphics (2002)

None of the three links offered at the FindMyFont page have Desdemona available. The originator, Font Bureau, no longer offers it, MyFonts only finds the solid Spiece Graphic version, and FontShop displays “zero results”.

However, it IS available at these sites: [[http://www.fonts.com/font/font-bureau/desdemona/regular?SiteId=15|Fonts.com]] [[http://www.linotype.com/896580/Desdemona-family.html|Linotype]] [[http://www.ascenderfonts.com/font/desdemona.aspx|AscenderFonts.com]]
