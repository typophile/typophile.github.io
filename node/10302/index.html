From Myfonts:

ITC Berkeley Old Style is based on the California Old Style of Frederic Goudy, which he designed in
1940 for the University Press. Monotype released this font in 1956 under the name Californian and Tony Stan (1917-1988) designed the ITC version, Berkeley. This clear, legible font gives text a
classic, elegant look.

From FB:

In 1958, Lanston issued it as Californian. Carol Twombly digitized the roman 30 years later for California (magazine); David Berlow revised it for Font Bureau with italic and small caps; Jane Patterson designed the bold. In 1999, assisted by Richard Lipton & Jill Pichotta, Berlow designed the black and the text and display series
