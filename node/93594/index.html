Landscape and interior design people use it in their technical drawings and plans. Please tell me what kind of font this is. Thanks, Neb

There are a number of “architectural” fonts intended to simulate the careful hand-lettering on documents such as you mentioned. They include:

Tekton (Adobe) Graphite (Linotype) Schema (Fonthead Design) Architect (many different with the same name from diffeent sources) Technical (Corel/Bitstream)

Not sure why I couldn’t ID your sample - it may be a vertically compressed instance of one of those mentioned, or could be yet another. There are innumerable free similar fonts as well.
