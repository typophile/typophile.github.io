Hello everybody, Well I'm new here, and I'm about to start a kind of poster project about type and the characteristics of it, I was thinking of having a school of type, where for instance, Helvetica would be the cool kid and Comic Sans would be the kid nobody liked. Thats the basic idea, but what I was looking for, is what you guys think about typefaces and the reputation that suceeds them. A few questions... Is there a gay typeface? Is there a typeface that would win Miss World? Maybe the worlds strongest man? What is the slag of all typefaces? I would really appreciate your comments, as I am aware that you are all much more experienced than me in this field... Thanks guys, Tom Graphic Design student
Helvetica's probably not the cool kid. He's more like the kid who's got good grades, but not enough to be on the honour roll. He's got a lot of friends, but he's nobody's BEST friend. The cool kid might be Gotham or Neutraface right now, although who knows who will be cool tomorrow. - Lex
2006-10-15 03:57:39 • Lex Kominek
The Cool Kid: Neutraface is a nice option, but so are a lot of other House Fonts. The Gay Type: United from House Industries. See, Fox sports uses it, and trust me (I'm queer as a three-dollar bill) football is overflowing with gay guys, so use United. The Miss World Face: The new Luxury family from House wins again. If you can't get that one, just use Mrs. Eaves The World's Strongest: Akzidenz-Grotesk super wins here. The Slag of all typefaces: Arial. As for Helvetica... man, you're a design student. Use cool fonts now so that when you get stuck using Helvetica later you haven't already gotten sick of seeing it. Hell, unless you can do it at 200 picas or bigger, just don't use Helvetica at all.
