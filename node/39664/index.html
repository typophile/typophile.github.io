Seeking Dwiggins essay

December 2, 2007 • ilynam

Hello there. I am on the hunt for an essay that W.A. Dwiggins wrote about the way that commercial artists/graphic designers are treated in American society. I read it years ago, but have completely forgotten the name of the piece. Anyone have any idea what it is or where I could find it? Thanks!!
I believe the piece you are looking for is "A Technique for Dealing with Artists." It's a little bit tongue-in-cheek, in a Jonathan Swift sort of way. The original article was published in 1941 by the Press of the Woolly Whale. It's since been republished in a number of places. One is the 1947 Typophile Chapbook XVII -- MSS. by WAD. It looks like it's also included in the more recent Looking Closer 3: Classic Writings on Graphic Design, from Allworth Press. I hope this helps. -- K.
