Well every typeface has some «personality» – it would be interesting to know what kind of personality you're looking for. Should that "S" be fluid? Sweet? Cute? Perky? Aggressive? Dynamic? Stoic? Lazy? Mechanical? etc. You get the idea. Knowing this would make it easier to suggest typefaces, but I'd also second what Hrant said – especially if you're depending that much on one letter, a more exclusive approach would be to not make your logo rely on a pre-made "S" (and spend a long time looking for the right one), but rather make one yourself that is exactly the way you need it, or have it made.
