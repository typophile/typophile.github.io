Old serif typeface (before 1915)

March 20, 2014 • typolis

The letters “A” (double-sided serif o top), “g”, „ß“ and the and the steep serifs on „b, d, l, h“ are very special. This Typeface was used by a Munich publisher called Carl Kuhn, from at least 1915 through at least 1937.
