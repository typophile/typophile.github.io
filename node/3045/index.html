Just for a laugh, my last post to Mark (and his earlier comment about needing a 12-step program) prompted me to make the following List. Maybe some of you can suggest better/additional items? These are the Twelve Warning Signs that you may be a Fontoholic (alt. spelling: Fontaholic): 1. You dream about fonts. 2. You tell other people (who could not care less) what font the menu is set in. 3. You look at magazine ads to identify the fonts. 4. You know the urls of over 10 font sites by heart. 5. Your copies of font books are falling apart from being used. 6. You know the names of over 20 type designers. 7. You name things after your favorite fonts. 8. It ruins your day when you can't think of the name of a font you KNOW you know. 9. You hang out all day waiting for ID posts at newsgroups and forums online. 10. You need to buy a bigger hard drive to house your font collection. 11. You spend days developing elaborate font classification schemes for your font files. 12. You hate it when you know a font, but someone ID's it before you do.
