2007-01-25 17:19:29 • rvalenzuela
That doesn’t work, because “nisi” introduces a dependent clause in which the tense of the verb (and hence its ending) must change – note the “aedificaverit” in the full version. (Subjunctive.) if you’re going to have an abbreviation/contraction of the complete text, with the rest of it elided (implied), then why not go with the traditional “Nisi dominus frustra”?
