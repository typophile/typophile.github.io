ALAS. Is as you read an international gastronomy club. Their subject is rather various. They don't just read or write recipies. They are a kind of gastronomy scientists (that's why the glasses on the frying pan). On the first image you see the finalists and on the next the logo they choose, but they are not sure about the typeface. I am pretty sure that the most of you are tired of Helvetica, as I am.
