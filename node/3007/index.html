Gerald, here is the formula in case you have styles in Photoshop 5.5. Bevel/Emboss > Pillow Smooth > 390% > Size 2 > Soften 4 Inner Glow > 100% Opacity > Solid Color (choose your own) > Technique: Percise > Source: Center Inner Shadow > Multiply Black > 75% > Distance 7 > Size 7 This will give you a good starting point. When you make the effect put the type above your wood image and change the blending effects to Multiply
