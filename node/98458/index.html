Most probably all hand-painted (meaning not-a-font). Some you might find interesting: [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/device/korolev/|Korolev]], [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/t26/tempus-gothic/|Tempus Gothic]], [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/coniglio/nomad/|Nomad]], [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/context/cougher/|Cougher]], [[http://www.myfonts.com/search/karben/|Karben]], [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/urw/engschrift/|Engshrift Austria D]], [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/rsantos/van-condensed/|Van Condensed]], [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/urw/snv/|Snv]], [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/t26/tsjecho/|Tsejcho]], [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/urw/saa/|Saa]], [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/jnlevine/sign-kit/|Sign Kit]], [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/jnlevine/narrow-stencil/|Narrow Stencil]], [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/jnlevine/inventory/|Inventory]]… Have also a look to both [[http://www.myfonts.com/foundry/Jeff_Levine/|Jeff Levine]] and [[http://www.myfonts.com/foundry/Nicks_Fonts/|Nick Curtis]] collections, they’ve done many sign paint, wooden type and stencil revivals that could fit your needs.
