In my opinion the LIFEGUARDS lettering is not from a digital font as such, but rather is based on the same lettering guide that Jeff Levine used for his font Three Day Pass JNL http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/jnlevine/three-day-pass/ The stenciled 9 seems to be a variation and the stenciled 3 is from another design that uses squared shapes.
