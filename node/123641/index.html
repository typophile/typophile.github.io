Anyone know the different fonts being used in this? http://www.flickr.com/photos/milliemotts/2223407793/in/photostream/

The sans serif (TO SIDNEY and the smaller labels), could be various weights of Monotype Grotesque: http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/mti/grotesque-mt/

|TEAL| could be replicated with Antenna Compressed Bold Italic: http://www.fontbureau.com/fonts/Antenna/

Hoefler & Co.’s Numbers (Indicia) could help you replicate the sequential numbering: http://www.typography.com/fonts/numbers/styles/

PsyOps Crash Numbering is also an option: http://www.psyops.com/shop/typefaces/crash-numbering/
