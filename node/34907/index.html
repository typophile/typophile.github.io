This is s logo for a technology managment and recruitment company. I’m thinking of Meta as the corporate typeface. Any comments welcome. Thanks!

[img:sites/default/files/old-images/pr_5340.gif]

I would use the arrow only in one letter (P would be my choice), is there a specific reason why you are using it in the P&R? What happens when the arrow in the R is looking leftwards? Also try to enclose the arrow(s). Or rotate them 45 degrees in order to symbolize success. Maybe using two colors, one for Pro and one for Resource would make it more vibrant. Over all, I think it needs a little bit more playing around. Do you have other drafts? Meta is a solid typeface, what about using a roman typeface? Tracking is to tight…

Derya

2007-07-05 15:26:10 • William Berkson

Basically the arrow is a strong sharp edged shape that may annoy the eye while it is scanning the logotype. If you have to use it, I would recommend to make it smaller in size, and more slim. I would also increase the tracking to give more air for the eye too; for same reason.
