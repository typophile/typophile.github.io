I think you should try draw this form by hand and make it truly your own. There’s great potential in your initial sketch. However there’s no harm looking some other typefaces. I’m a big fan of the work of Argentine typographer [[http://www.myfonts.com/person/paul/alejandro/|Alejandro Paul]] of [[http://www.sudtipos.com|Sudtipos]]. He does some excellent calligraphic faces from which you could probably take some inspiration. Have a look at [[http://www.veer.com/products/typedetail.aspx?image=UMT0000181|Affair]] or maybe something a bit more tame like [[http://www.sudtipos.com/fonts/3|Chocolate]]. Go n-éirí leat.
