I think most of us were immediately frightened off by the fact that you were taking advice from a myspace poll. Also, typophile was 404 for quite a while earlier this week. I'll give you my impression of what you've got so far. Overall, it's way too detailed, and the mix of photographic elements with (what looks like) pencil lines doesn't achieve rugged or grunge - it just looks sloppy. The third version is headed in the right direction, but it's far from complete. I think (?) your spikes are meant to be a platform, but the way it looks now, it's really more of a violent-looking star. I'd scrap the four "spikes" on the bottom and replace them with a mirror image of the top three spikes (which, btw, I would redraw to center the middle spike, and/or exaggerate the angles for drama.) Repeat after me; simple is GOOD. Simple is memorable, and simple will save you money. I suggest spending some time looking at vector art (icons and logos) and find some microphone art you like to inspire your rendering. (but stay away from gradients - $$$) Of your three versions, none will scale down AND up and still look like a microphone. The last one is looking ... well, naughty. In short, I think the silence on this thread is collectively saying; "The font is the least of your worries." Sorry if this seemed harsh, but it's honest. I hope it helps. AK
