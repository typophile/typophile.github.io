Are you looking for headings, body, etc? Serif? Sans?

I like the way Trajan works with Futura, since the skeletons of the letters are similar but it doesn’t have a lower case, and I wouldn’t really call it retro-modern.

Bodoni, Didot, or Walbaum also contrast nicely with Futura due to their, well, contrast.
