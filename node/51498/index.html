In other words. Univers 55 is regular weight standard width roman Univers 65 is medium weight standard width roman

I’m not sure exactly what your teacher’s asking for, but if i was going to pick 4 fonts from Univers to go together I’d do this: choose one to start, then find a bold, italic, and bold-italic version of it.

For example: 55 (normal) 56 (italic) 75 (bold) 76 (bold italic)

or: 47 (“normal” in this example) 48 (italic) 67 (bold) 68 (bold italic)
