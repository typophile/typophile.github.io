When inserting a drop cap, should the top of the character align with the baseline, x-height, or cap-height of the first line in the paragraph?
Not the baseline, rest at your discretion. If you set in a language that utilizes ahellafalotta ascenders, or many accents (or both!) maybe higher than x-height (which is what I would normally aim at as a starting point). In short: Utilize eyes and critical observation ;-) K
