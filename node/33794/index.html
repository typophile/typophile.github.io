2007-05-15 14:55:33 • hrant

Hrant,

I was lucky enough to see Helvetica on a day when Gary Hustwit was there for a question and answer session. He indicated that, while there should certainly be more films about graphic design or visual communication in general, that he didn’t know if other individual typefaces would stand up as the subjects of those films.

One of the things that I really like about Helvetica is how the typeface became a sort of filter through which a larger discussion about the merits of modernism vs. postmodernism was conducted. Do you think other typefaces could do this, generally speaking?
