Riccardo and Electrolyze, I hate to burst your bubbles, but the capital M in Univers has a center stroke that touches the baseline. The M in the sample has a center stroke that stops above the baseline by a considerable distance. Also, the leg of the R in Univers is curved/convex. The leg of the R in the sample is straight. The sample may be to too small to definitively identify, but it is not Univers.
