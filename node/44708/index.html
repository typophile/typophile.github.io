the lack of recognition given to type designers is a significant issue.

Before you do any other work on this project, you need to back that statement up with a lot more thought about why it’s a significant issue.

If you think the purpose of most type is to be recognized, you’re sorely mistaken. And recognizing the big names in type is pretty easy these days; a quick Google search brings up everything most people need to know, buying a book or two will explain a lot more. People have better things to do than keep track of who designed all of the important stuff in their lives, from type to computers to coffee cups. If everyone spent time worrying about designers, nothing else would ever get done.
