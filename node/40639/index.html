The Album Cover Game

January 7, 2008 • Number3Pencils

I came across this terrific game on another forum I frequent. Here’s the procedure as given [[http://forums.xkcd.com/viewtopic.php?f=14&t=16834&start=0|there]]:

1 - Go to wikipedia. Hit “random”. [[http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Special:Random]] The first random wikipedia article you get is the name of your band.

2 - Go to Random quotations: [[http://www.quotationspage.com/random.php3]] The last four or five words of the very last quote of the page is the title of your first album.

3 - Go to flickr and click on “explore the last seven days” [[http://www.flickr.com/explore/interesting/7days/]] Third picture, no matter what it is ((SFW of course)), will be your album cover.

4 - Use photoshop or similar to mix it all up. Host it and bring it here.
