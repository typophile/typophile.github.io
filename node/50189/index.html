So I emailed Ken Garland, guy who initiated the manifesto and this is what he said:

fushia

nice to hear from you. I’m glad you found the manifesto of some use, even after all these years.

as to your query: the headning type was hand-drawn by me; the text type was made by Ludlow Typograph Company of USA (now defunct) and was called Recod Gothic.

I hope you soon get pissed off with catfood, stripey toothpaste and shaving cream: no sort of diet for a forward-looking young person (which I assume you are).

ken g
