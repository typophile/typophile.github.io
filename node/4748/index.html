May 13, 2004 • mr_ampersand

Hi there,
When the beginning of the euro sign was launched, i always heard the placement was BEFORE the numbers. Nowadays, it seems like it is used more after them than before. I thaught atypi described the placement also before the numbers.
How do you guys use the euro sign?
When using the symbol of the euro I never have seen it after. When written 298.07 Euros it is written after. Banks like to stack things in the same column. So they usually when showing 'pounds sterling' 'dollar' and 'euro' they are before ..see Loyds TSB.

You probably see it after in Belgium or France and Spain since the people are use to using the older monetary symbols after. as 200 F …25 ptas.
