Outlier Italic: Feedback Please. First Typeface

May 5, 2010 • poonpoon

This is a very quick and rough (very rough) draft of my typeface. I was just looking for feedback and everyones first impressions keep in mind this is my first typeface and I am relatively new to the process. I have currently only done the lowercase just because I'm not 100% sure what direction I should be going in. I was going for an italic serif loosely based off of Bodoni and I tried to go with the Old style/ Transitional style. First impression, critique, technical aspects any comment will be helpful thank you. I'm really having trouble with c/e/f/y/k/s/ but of course I will take any advice on all or any of the letters.
Let's address first the salient feature of the face, the square ball terminals, or block terminals I guess. I feel they fall between two preferable treatments: Either the stroke should be narrower where it meets the block, the inner curve being about halfway across, or the inner curve should turn all the way to meet the corner. The thins are extremely thin, and I would expect them to agree more with the weight of the serifs and crossbars.
