Years ago I did some design work for a publisher of children’s books. One thing they told me was that there isn’t a generic “design for children” style; that it had to be designed for the target age group, gender, demographic, etc. A design that would appeal to a 6 year old girl might be wildly inappropriate for a 12 year old boy. Teens are particularly sensitive to this and wouldn’t be caught dead with a book that looks like it was intended for someone younger than them.

Maybe that’s obvious but thought I’d mention it.
