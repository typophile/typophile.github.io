[[Indices]] : [[Designers]] : Frederic W. Goudy The preeminent American type designer who had created over 100 classic faces was the Art Director of [[Lanston Type Co.|Lanston Monotype Company]] from 1920-1947. Thirty of his designs were created specifically for Lanston and all will be available in the near future as a fitting tribute to Goudy’s typographic legacy.
