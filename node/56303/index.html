There is a nice list of all Ludlow fonts in Mac McGrew's American Typefaces of the 20th Century. This one is Eusebius Open, by Robert Hunter Middleton, 1929, based on Eusebius, by Middleton's teacher Ernst Detterer, 1923. Eusebius is Ludlow's version of Jenson. Lovely.
