As I mentioned above, if, by “move,” you mean that objects (text boxes) shift slightly towards/away from the spine you might have the creep settings set oddly. If that’s the case you’ll notice the same shift in the PDF.

I’m assuming, however, that you’re familiar with creep settings and that this is not the issue.

If, when creating a new (imposed) file, your objects are jumping around significantly and text flow is getting screwed up, printing to PDF should fix that (at least that’s been my experience.)
