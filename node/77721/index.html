2400 DPI Postscript printers aren’t cheap. For that you have to buy a high-end A3/tabloid (or larger) unit from Xerox or Xante, and those are expensive. If you are on a tight budget I recommend that you look at the 1200DPI letter/A4 units from Ricoh. The only drawback I have found with Ricoh is that the toner tends to be more expensive than other brands, but that’s because Ricoh uses better toner.

If you want a tabloid/A3 printer on a small budget watch online classifieds for used units. Small businesses are constantly dumping good printers because they don’t need them for one reason or another.
