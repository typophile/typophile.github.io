It’s my opinion that these initials are custom(ized), and not from any commercial font. The curlicues have the look of add-ons to me, particularly on the A and G. The S you mentioned wasn’t shown on that site was it?

My suggestion is to look for other ornate script fonts and pick something that you like the look of. There are samples of many such scripts in [[http://www.bowfinprintworks.com/Script13Pg1.html|Part 13 of my Script Font ID Guide]]. Looking through these you can see that none is close to the letters shown on the site samples. Canada Type’s [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/canadatype/sterling-script/|Sterling Script]] has swash alternates that seem similar in style to your requested look.
