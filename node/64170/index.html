Font-O-Matic

November 12, 2009 • nr0000

Together with Amsterdam based agency Mainstudio, nr0000 is working on a publication ‘Font-O-Matic’. This is a small booklet with ± 20 selfmade fons. All based on transient fonts. This means all fonts are subject to change. Think about the moon, icecubes, melted chocolate, and more of those processes. Most created fonts come in two types: The ‘before’ and the ‘after’. Hopefully we will get a publication allowance wich is enough to get the booklet printed properly. The fonts will be offered as downloads on http://www.font-o-matic.nl

This site will be online as soon as we’re finished. Keep you posted.
