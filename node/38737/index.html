2007-11-14 18:33:09 • txo
Like Koppa mentioned the area of white in the g where the counter meets the descender is distracting in its weightiness, a little work on the open counter might help. Of the m iterations the second of your latest version is an improvement, although the transition of curve to vertical on the right isn't quite there yet. Overall there is too much overshoot on the characters, exacerbated by there only being three which aren't curved top and bottom, the r especially is jumping. Rework are the heaviness of the junctions in the e, a, g and, to a lesser extent, n. Tim
