I think your instincts are right-on ... Optima (as well as its bastard cousin, Zapf Humanist - both technically sans serifs, btw) is way too close to Panorama to use them together .. a serif would be a MUCH better choice. Maybe Life from Adobe .. http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/adobe/life/ Personally, I always thought Caslon (maybe 224) worked well with Optima, ergo should match with Panorama .. basically a "chiseled" sans serif with a "chiseled" serif .. -fQ-
