Non-Static Logo Systems

February 21, 2006 • BrooklynRob

Hello I'm a grad student working on a research project involving non-static logo systems: ID systems comprised of some elements that are consistent and other elements that change from one implementation to another. The most well-known example is probably the MTV logo -- the shape and configuration are consistent but the color, patterns and animation style are always changing. Other examples are the "Nick Jr." pre-school Nickelodeon tv identity (illustrations of parent and child animals) and of course the Google logo. Can you think of other examples? Or even better, have you seen anything written about this that I could track down? In books, in magazines, on blogs, whatever. If you have ideas or resources to share I would really appreciate it. Thanks, Rob
