Some Wingdings characters

June 13, 2004 • boole



Can anybody tell me what are those Wingdings characters? What do they mean/represent?

I've seen them one in print (just the first one) and once in the interface of a database software (first and second).
A good symbol dictionary (like Liungman's*) will tell you that the first is Scorpio (although it seems that usually the arrow either curls to point up or goes diagonally down-right, not straight), closely associated with birth, sex and death; it's also the symbol for Separation in alchemy. The second one is Virgo; Distillation in alchemy. The third sign I couldn't find (I checked in Koch's "Book of Signs" too), but it seems to be closest to Saturn/lead, although it's also similar to the sign for Leo/Digestion. Or it could be a "facilitation" of Virgo.

* Interestingly, the first two signs are on the back cover of his book!
