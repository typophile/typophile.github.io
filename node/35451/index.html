CS3 Indesign is the only logo in all caps. why???

July 26, 2007 • zigavoo

Why is it that, in the Adobe CS3 suite, indesign is the only logo in all caps? I know Indesign is one word and that if they did the letters for Adobe indesign there would be a conflict with illustrator. But still, why not just make it Id instead of ID?

The official spelling is “InDesign,” perhaps set with what Bringhurst calls “an extra helping of capitals” because indesign is a made-up word. Then again so is “Photoshop,” so who knows.

Edit: also check out InCopy. whose icon also, arguably, contains a capital (albeit a small one, it’s hard to tell).
