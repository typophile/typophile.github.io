Though I realize this is hand painted, can anyone help me find typefaces that are similar to the the lettering styles in this photo?

For context, it is a door on a building at Amherst College:

“ohnson” & “hapel” can be approximated with [[http://www.findmyfont.com/index.php/fonts/font-preview?fset=Berthold&ffam=Sorbonne%20BQ%20-%20Italic&fid=ee8fec06bf2df4dc115f256ce4561fde&fsize=60&text=Johnson%20Chapel&fit=1|Sorbonne BQ (Italic)]] and you could even use [[http://www.findmyfont.com/index.php/fonts/font-preview?fset=Berthold&ffam=Sorbonne%20BQ%20-%20Bold&fid=3bbbac0928e65c43555c5efb60e507ce&fsize=60&text=BUILT%201827&wrap=2|Sorbonne BQ (Bold)]] for the “BUILT”

but I doubt you can find a single font to match both |J| & “C”. If you don’t need a full alphabet and your intention is to restore the original or create a simple logo, I propose to take high resolution photos of both left and right part (one photo each) and hire a professional graphic designer to convert them to vector format.
