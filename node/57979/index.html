James, I don’t think you’re seeing this right. You had a similar problem with the S in Recovery. This is the way the S is supposed to look in this style, if you’re doing a fairly close revival. Try and keep the personality and balance it according to your eye (use drag skew tool), rather than departing from the model entirely. BTW, the SB grots are distinctive and British, but not typically British, as there were other sans faces at the time that weren’t so funky.
