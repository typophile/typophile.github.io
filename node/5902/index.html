2004-08-09 08:28:38 • Bald Condensed
I think the biggest, most important, thing to keep in mind when dealing with font management software is that it is fallible. Especially if you really want to keep all of your fonts active at once.

For instance, I use Suitcase X1 and Panther. Most of the time it runs smoothly. I generally only have 10 or so fonts active, but I do keep most of my library loaded. Granted I do not use the auto-activate. Occasionally Suitcase will decide a font is corrupt. I say bollocks to that and under the TOOLS pull-down (after selecting the so-called corrupt fonts) I use the "unmark corrupt fonts" and all is well again. This is just a little bug. Doesn't happen often. But I think it is directly due to the fact that my library is a little large.
