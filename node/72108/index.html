Wondered if there was a similar looking font about or perhaps one with a similar latin american feel? Suggestions would be much appreciated.

Your sample looks like it may be hand-drawn, partly because the stress is unusual. Normally the O would not be stressed on one side like that, and usually the other leg of the A is the stressed one, I think. One font I know with a similar O is Parisian, and a ‘Latin’ styled font could be ITC Juanita, particularly the condensed versions.
