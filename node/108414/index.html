If you’re interested in poster graphics, you should check out the Plakatsammlung (Poster Collection), although it seems they are in limited service these days. http://www.museum-gestaltung.ch/en/collections/poster/

There are some great museums and galleries in Zurich (and even more in Basel, which is an hour away). If you want some printing/type/book-historical background I highly recommend the Swiss museum of paper, type and printing, which is in Basel: http://www.papiermuseum.ch/en/the-museum/

Bookstores: Buchhandlung Kunstgriff, Limmatstrasse 270, between Limmatplatz and Escher-Wyss-Platz http://www.kunstgriff.ch/

Sphères (combination bookstore and bar/café), Hardturmstrasse 66, west of Escher-Wyss-Platz http://www.spheres.cc/

Buchhandlung sec52, Josefstrasse 52, between train station and Limmatplatz http://sec52.ch/

Culture / Clubs / Books / Art / Meals: A list of suggestions intended for students of the Zurich university of the arts: https://www.zhdk.ch/index.php?id=38591

Museums and exhibits: The Rietberg museum has a lovely garden http://www.rietberg.ch/en-gb/foyer.aspx

Museum Haus Konstuktiv http://www.hauskonstruktiv.ch/en/

Poetry will be made by all http://poetrywillbemadebyall.ch/

A list of second-hand stores and thrift shops in Zurich: http://www.nichtneu.ch/liste/liste/a-z

Design stores: There is a yearly design weekend in May. The website has the addresses of stores, designers, cafés, and establishments who participate: http://www.kreislauf4und5.ch/en/index.html

Vintage furniture and accessories: http://www.walterwalter.ch/

Shop with household objects selected for design quality: http://www.editionpopulaire.ch/

Design furniture and objects: http://bogen33.ch

Food: Vegetarian restaurant with buffet: http://www.tibits.ch/

Garage-style restaurant: http://www.les-halles.ch/ http://www.inyourpocket.com/Switzerland/Zurich/Restaurants-and-Cafes/Mediterranean/Les-Halles_63932v

Markets: http://www.inyourpocket.com/Switzerland/Zurich/Sightseeing/Markets
