Now Showing! Typophile Film Fest 5

December 1, 2008 • Zara Evens

The Typophile Film Fest 5 is a one hour selection of typographic films hailing from all over the world including Argentina, The Netherlands, Russia and the United States. The films vary in subject matter and style; creating a dynamic presentation of motion design, typographic animation, short stories, documentaries and interviews.

Venues & Dates

27 August 2009 - Design a Trois Punchcut and AIGA Portland are proud to present the premier of the Typophile Film Festival 5, taking place on the final night of Design à Trois in Portland, Oregon on August 27th 2009 at the Art Institute of Portland. Tickets are $10 at the door.

23 October 2009 - PHXDW Punchcut will bring the Typophile Film Fest 5 to Phoenix, Arizona for Phoenix Design Week (PHXDW), October 23 2009.

29 October 2009 - ATypI Typ09 Conference - Mexico City

Punchcut and the Association Typographique Internationale will host the TFF5 in Mexico City as part of the annual, week-long type design conference, ATypI Typ09.
