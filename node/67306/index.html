The Commerzbank logo used to feature a custom bold stressed sans, similar to Optima. The recent redesign brought a more contemporary logotype by [[Luc(as) de Groot]], see the article on Fontblog. Apart from the logo, there’s a corporate typeface based on Linotype’s Compatil, Commerzbank Compatil. Before that, there was a bold oblique slab called Commerzbank Head. More info in this article in DMIG 2 [PDF, 0.85 MB ]. F
