I’m interested to know if anyone has any experience with budget color laser printers. (Minolta Konica 2400w, Hp Color Laserjet 2600 or 1600, etc.)

  1. How is print quality in color?
  2. How is text in color?
  3. Is quality passable for printing serious invitation stuff(type extensive)?

Joseph

I just bought a Xerox phaser 8500 (solid ink laser).

The colour is great - pretty accurate, but I’m not super impressed with the resolution.

Laser printer text will be pretty sharp as long as you’re not using too light a screen of any color. The ink will also have a bit of a sheen to it.

If invites are your biggest concern, I’ve had good luck out-sourcing the laser printing to a high end service bureau. You can source the paper yourself from any of the large manufacturers.
