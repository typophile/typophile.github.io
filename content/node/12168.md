body: "<strong>[[Indices]] : [[Contests]] : [[Current Contests]] : The Type</strong>\r\n\r\nThe
  Association of Graphic Arts and  The Design Studio of Vassiliy Shishkin produces
  a new international contest-exhibition \u201CThe Type\u201D. The Exhibition will
  take place in St. Petersburg from 21 to 26 of June 2005 at  the Artist`s Union of
  Russia Exhibition Center (B. Morskaja, 38). Works of  the Russian and foreign participants,
  typefaces and type compositions will be presented there.\r\n\r\nWe  would like to
  invite you to take part in the work of the Exhibition.\r\n\r\nAll materials presented
  at \u201CThe Type\u201D will be published in The Catalogue of The Exhibition. \r\n\r\nHere
  we attach technical requirements for files, which are to be printed in The Catalogue.\r\n\r\nTechnical
  Requirements\r\nA participant sends by e-mail a files created in a vector format
  using one of those\r\nprograms: Adobe Illustrator, Free Hand, Corel Draw for a press
  in a catalogue. The participant receives a turn. The format of the catalogue is
  240x315 (VERTICAL), black-and-white, the turn is 480x315. On the turn both a font
  and the letter, some letters or some fonts can be placed. The file in Word format
  with the brief description of fonts and short information about participant are
  also required. Works should be send in black-and-white. Texts should be in curves,
  raster images linked, 4 mm from a cut line. Participants receive one catalogue free.\r\n\r\nThe
  Terms.\r\nAll materials for the catalogue should be given not later then the 10
  of June, 2005.\r\n\r\nWe hope that our offer is of considerable interest to you.
  Please do not hesitate to write us if you require further information.\r\n\r\nThanking
  you in advance for the cooperation,\r\nYours sincerely,\r\nVassiliy Shishkin\r\n\r\n\r\n\r\n\r\n--
