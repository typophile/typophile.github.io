ITC Honda variation

September 2, 2014 • donshottype

Tom Carnase and Ronne Bonder designed a very simplified blackletter called ITC Honda in 1970. A modified version of the font is used in two books, published in 1977 and 1980. I spotted differences in f, t, and s. There are probably others. Squadron/Signal Publications Aircraft No. 30 1977 ISBN: 089747029X Squadron/Signal Publications Aircraft No. 44 1980 ISBN: 0897471067 The changes could perhaps be the result of editing by the cover designer, but I would be very interested to learn if there was an alternate version of ITC Honda, perhaps under another name. Larger scale images are attached. Thanks. Don
