2008-07-31 18:45:36 • simundo
It looks like it’s somewhere between heavy and black, but it’s been changed so much that you wouldn’t know if there was a stroke around the whole thing. I’m not aware of any other typeface that looks like this so I’m almost certain this is actually Serif Gothic we’re looking at. It has also been know to be a frequent target for ‘type customisers’ like you have in your pic. Other examples are Non-Format, who have used it a lot in their work : http://www.non-format.com/archive/channel-4/ As well as Serial Cut : http://www.serialcut.com/
