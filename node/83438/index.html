http://www.flickr.com/photos/48413419@N00/5878716141 Is the Armenian font in the above photo based on a Latin original? It sure looks familiar... To me it has a late 90s FontFont feel. hhp
There's a whole lot of similarities (ie the strict monoline roundedness, the slightly awkward geoetricity) to typefaces based on Leroy mechanical lettering machines or carbide engraving machines, stuff like [[http://www.fontshop.com/fonts/singles/scangraphic/din_17_sb_regular/|DIN 17]] or [[http://www.optimo.ch/typefaces_Gravostyle-Basic.html|Gravostyle]] or [[http://www.lineto.com/The+Fonts/Font+Categories/Text+Fonts/Gravur+Condensed/|Gravur]]. I'm sure there are ones that are even closer though - I can't quite remember which one, but I swear I've seen "gravure-style" fonts that have an /n/ that looks even more similar to the armeninan /ո/ in that picture.
