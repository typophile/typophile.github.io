JC.

Arthur is probably one of the few typographic/lettering artists whose work is readily identifiable and individualistic. The word idiosyncratic comes to mind.It is borne out of his personality, the tools he uses and his studies.

If you are looking for something with his “trademark style” you’ve only to search the myriad faces he has designed over the decades. Look at Hiroshige(Sans) as a possible alternative or just google his typefaces. Most are calligraphic but there are others to choose from.
