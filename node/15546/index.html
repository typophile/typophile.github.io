Kirk, American Uncial and San Marco have more than just the glyphs needed for the English language in their character sets.

Here is a link to the character set pages for American Uncial (or at least the Mac PS version… PC PS and PC TrueType versions are also available). Use the drop down menu to get at all of the supplemental Latin glyphs. American Uncial can set all Western European languages, but a CE version is not for sale yet. Are you looking for a CE version? I might be able to direct you in the right direction for that.

Ditto San Marco (http://www.linotype.com/1439/sanmarco-family.html). San Marco even has DFR (Deutsche Fraktur, with a long-s) versions available, plus a separate Cyrillic font. Are you sure that the glyphs you need aren’t in here? Again, if you are looking for CE, please let me know. I don’t have any links to show, but we could discuss the matter.
