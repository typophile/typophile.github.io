FYI, the typography term for the general style is “blackletter” (“gothic” has other meanings). Mind you, this is a sort of simplified blackletter, not a common form of the style.

Do a search on myfonts.com and use “simple blackletter” and “clean blackletter” and you get quite a few alternatives. Most of these seem plausible:

http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/volcano/trigot/ http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/volcano/black-sirkka/ http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/catharsis-fonts/gryffensee/ http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/catharsis-fonts/Backstein/ http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/otto-maurer/freiheit/ http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/volcano/domstadt/ http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/canadatype/leather/ http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/adobe/san-marco/ http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/ingrimayne/zimple/
