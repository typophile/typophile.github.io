Welcome to the world of quality digital typography. It’s generally true that you get what you pay for, and remember you are helping support many of the people who you might see on this forum – many of them are type designers.

If you have ever tried designing a font, and doing it properly, with adjusted kerning pairs and alternate glyphs and ligatures, you would understand why licenses are expensive. That’s also (unfortunately) partly to offset the losses due to piracy. We encourage licensing from the designers, and choosing carefully, the way you would weigh any other purchasing decision. When you have built your own font library you’ll find it something to take pride in like a collection of fine books.

At least that’s how I look at my collection.
