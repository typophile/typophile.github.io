Offhand this does not ring a bell. If I had to make the letters I would start with a font such as Scout Cond Black by Cyrus Highsmith http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/fontbureau/scout/ The outline curvature and weight are pretty close. Do a slight drop in the bottom of the bowl on R – which results in separating the leg from the lhs stroke to match the image – and raise the midstroke on B. Prune terminals on S. Prune G. Shift diagonal on N to make pointed junction with vertical stroke. I, A & V pretty much as is. Minor job. Don
