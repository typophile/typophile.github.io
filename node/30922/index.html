Great deal on a laser printer

January 27, 2007 • hrant

Something barely on-topic, but hopefully useful:

If you want a great deal on a decent low-end laser printer, this is it: Office Depot is offering $20 off the $200 HP 1022 (not to be confused with the bare-bottom 1020) which is a speedy true-1200dpi model with a very small footprint. And Staples has a $25-off coupon for any HP printer $200 or higher. The trick is to get the manager of your local Office Depot to agree to honor the Staples coupon, then take the 1022 to the register… It’s a great little printer, a steal at $155.

Note that the Office Depot $20 off might be expiring today.
