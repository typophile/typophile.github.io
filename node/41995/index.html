2008-02-13 17:45:45 • Dan Weaver

Are you being sincere or ironic? I’m not trying to be obnoxious, but I honestly can’t tell.

From a purely aesthetic perspective, I find the question mark to be very odd - it doesn’t look like a question mark, and is encroaching on the L. The huge pixelated arrow in the background is competing in space with the second “W”, and the first Ws of each line are also competing with each other in a distracting way. The “wnload” in color and style reminds me of Howard Dean’s campaign materials from a few years ago, which is probably not what you’re intending. Instead of the font & image mashup you have here, I’d stick with one font for the WWJDL, and one for for the “What would Jesus download”, the latter being in sentence case and italics. Then you can use color to set off the “wnload” if you need to, although that just hits you over the head with the joke (which is why this reads as parody to me).

2008-02-14 17:28:57 • jupiterboy

Well, from a quick glance at your website, it’s clear you’re not being ironic. Unfortunately, I think that your branding is off the mark; namely, it doesn’t reflect the same intention as the other “WWJ*” messages. What Would Jesus Do / Drive and Who Would Jesus Bomb both intend to make the listener apply a critical eye to his/her attitudes or behavior in regard to personal conduct, choice in vehicle or political opinion about military violence. Do you intend to have people question their internet usage? I suspect not, since your site seems like some sort of Christian portal. Let me know if I am off the mark here. Also, I want to second jupiterboy’s observation that DL strays too much from the expected 4 letter abbreviation.

As a result, although this may be slightly off topic or out of line, I suggest your reconsider your branding concepts behind the creation of your ID. It is my view that, in fact, this is the most confounding factor in your process. Since your starting point is unclear, I can’t see how your ID could clearly express an unclear idea. (Or the only thing that will be clear is that your idea is unclear … but I digress).
