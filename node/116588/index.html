I would love to see more lettering in this style – a very evocative expression of Germany in 1931 – heavy, crisp and jarring letters that somehow still have a disciplined structure, that look like they were written with bamboo or a broad pen nib. Would make a superb font. A few substitutes: Matura http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/mti/matura-mt/ Designed by Imre Reiner in 1938. Mercurius http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/mti/mercurius/ Designed by Imre Reiner in 1957
Lotto http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/canadatype/lotto/ Designed by H. Thannhaeuser in 1955 Time Script Bold http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/linotype/time-script-lt/ Designed by G. Trump 1956. Gneisenauette Black http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/linotype/gneisenauette/ Designed by G.A. Grinbergs in 1977. Kompakt http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/linotype/kompakt/ Designed by H. Zapf in 1954. Fox http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/urw/fox/ Designed by W. Rebhuhn in 1955. I would also include India Ink CP, already mentioned by Dick Pape. Don
