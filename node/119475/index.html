2014-09-23 22:07:00 • donshottype
Finding Greek letters looks like a long shot. The most efficient way to make the letters in the logo is to find a Clarendon that already has an Italic with the thin upkick tails and modify it. One such font is Patrick Griffin’s Clarendon Text http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/canadatype/clarendon-text/ Starting from Clarendon Text Bold Italic, I condensed the letters to 70% and applied an extra slant of 4 degrees to compensate for the condensation. This is the result A good basis for whatever further modification you wish to apply to approach the letters in the original logo. Don
