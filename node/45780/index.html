Hi, I’ve been working on this logo concept for an estate agent company. I am very interested to get anyone’s thoughts on it. I could do with some fresh eyes really. I’m not sure about the type yet, whether it’ll be all caps etc.

I like how you’ve handled the wording, but the main logo seems lacking.

It looks half-way inbetween a blackletter ‘r’ and a house, and the ° symbol looks like a sun?

I think you either need to make it a house with a ° symbol, or else make it an ‘R’.

The thought of that symbol though, a house or R with a ° is a wonderful and genius idea, take it right to the bank, that’s the money-maker right there.

I wanna see more work on it and have it resolved better because it’s not that often that a concept with that potential comes along.

EDIT: it’s very ‘paul rand’ in it’s simplicity and clarity of message
