Chennai Slab Released

October 28, 2009 • Jeremy Dooley

[[http://new.myfonts.com/fonts/insigne/chennai-slab/|Chennai Slab]] is an extension of the original [[http://new.myfonts.com/fonts/insigne/chennai/|Chennai]]. Chennai Slab is a simplified slab serif with over sixty OpenType alternates. These alternates include alternates for the ball terminals, unique simplified alternates and more traditional capital forms. Use Chennai Slab when you need a fun and versatile slab serif. The sans-serif Chennai makes a great complement. Six weights are available.
