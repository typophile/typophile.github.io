California "Q"

December 24, 2009 • drstiver

Lets get that silly, OUT-OF-CONTEXT “Q” off the California license plates. All the characters in the “font” are clean, broad-stroke Gothic… and then there is this strange, shrunken “zero” with a cursive rat tail coming out the lower right corner Who did that?

Somebody working within the [sensical] vertical & horizontal constraints and realizing that making the tail internal would be dumb?

Ugly does not equal dysfunctional. In fact, aesthetics changes constantly, while functionality takes much much longer. People complain about the German plate lettering. I think it rules.

A car is not a painting. Well, except for this one: http://www.flickr.com/photos/48413419@N00/4164643116/
