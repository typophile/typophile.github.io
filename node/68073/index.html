The top one was originally made by Bill Horton in 1990 and called MacHumaine. It was a very badly made font given away for free or as shareware. Copies can still be found easily.

Diet Steffmann later fixed alot of the technical problems with the font. I think it was he who added a few ligatues as well. He offered it then as Alpine.
