http://cdn.myfonts.net/s/aw/720x360/35/0/18247.png Hi Everybody, How to create this kind of typeface? Mark pen ??or certain kind of photoshop brush? Best for you all
Since the letters are a pseudo rough brush look a natural brush would not give you that affect. Old scary movies from the 1940's and 50's used these type of hand drawn letters for titling. You might be able to get close with a large house painting brush and thick paint. Ultimately the ends of strokes are drawn, so you wouldn't need to paint a lot of letters if you were to try that. Just enough to base the look on.
