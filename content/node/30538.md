  to make fonts with FontLab in a casual, fun atmosphere? Then, come join us for GRPH

  CLASS INFO\r\n\r\nGRPH 152 Digital Font Creation\r\n8 Monday Evenings: 1/29, 2/12,

  in Fontlab Studio 5 for Macintosh, in practice and in context. \r\n\r\nWhether you
  want to make a ton of fonts or you just want to get more intimate with the ones
  that exist, you'll find we cover a lot of interesting topics like these:\r\n* Altering
  existing fonts in Fontlab\r\n* Drawing letterforms in Fontlab\r\n* Importing letterforms
  from raster and vector drawings\r\n* Automating accented characters in Fontlab\r\n*
  Building Kerning pairs into a font\r\n* Avoiding common font snafus\r\n* Pixel Fonts\r\n*
  A quick survey of the history of type design tech\r\n* Font files and management\r\n*
  The state of the industry, inc. licensing and sales\r\n* Specimens and critique\r\n\r\nYou
  will come away with a font of your own design and some basic skills in FontLab Studio
  5 and ScanFont.\r\n\r\n\r\nTEXTBOOKS\r\n\r\nThe required textbook is \"Learn FontLab
