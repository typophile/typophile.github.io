I have redesigned each and every element of “Beat the Whites with the Red Wedge” as characters in a typeface by the same name. As I have seen all lettering has been handrawn, and have based on a San Serif typeface. It should be easy to find out which one by looking through typefaces designed before 1919 with a similar “M” [ in клином ]and “C” [ in красных ] that were in use around 1919. One should take into account the fact that while being handrawn the letterforms were either condensed or based on a condensed weight of the typeface in question. The closest typefaces that are similar are ITC Johnston (1916) and Gill Condensed (1928-32), also Erbar (1922).
