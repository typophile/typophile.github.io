There are quite a few blackletter fonts that could give you similar “rather flourish-y” effects, but almost none that are Italic. If you can slope the font yourself you can pick and choose. For example, consider Laura Worthington’s Sepian http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/laura-worthington/sepian/ or Paul Lloyd’s Minister font http://fontpro.com/minster-font-14241 and http://moorstation.org/typoasis/designers/lloyd/ or Black Swan http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/bomparte/black-swan/ or Authentic Ink http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/schickfonts/authentic-ink/ Don
