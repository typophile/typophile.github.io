The sign looks hand-painted, based on the variations of identical letters. The very large counters on the P and R will be the hardest aspects to match. The closest thing I can think of offhand is [[http://www.houseind.com/index.php?page=showfont&id=8&subpage=viewfonts|House Bullet]], and a number of letters would need to be modified, especially C, M and N. You could backslant it to make the vertical letters.
