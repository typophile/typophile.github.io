Hi David Years ago I knocked up this one for my own amusement; all gradients applied within Indesign. [img:sites/default/files/old-images/Somedays-poster_5638.jpg] As James says, the postscript routines in both Indesign and Illustrator are pretty much the same thing. However – vector-based gradients can go horribly wrong – the only time this happened to me was on an A1 poster in which the banding was a direct result of the shift between two colours being too subtle over too wide a distance. A 10 or 15% shift on one of the plate colours was not enough, and the gradient was replaced with a blend containing the maximum number of steps. Wearing the cost of remaking the film & proof certainly learnt me a lesson...
