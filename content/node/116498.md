  body: "Thanks Hrant, \r\n\r\nThe 'experiment' here was mixing a fairly geometric
    regular, with a more calligraphic italic. To make them different enough have an
    interesting typographic color, but blending in well.\r\n\r\nMany font foundries
    don't do real italics anymore, that way they can commercialize the font sooner.\r\n\r\nSo,
    as I wanted the typeface to 'stand out', I spend more time working on the italics
    than on the regular versions, which was a first for me! :)\r\n"
