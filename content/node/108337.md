    [Several digitizations] by Frederic W. Goudy 1925: \r\nhttp://www.myfonts.com/search/Goudy+heavyface/\r\nGator
    by Canada Type, based on Poppl Heavy by Friedrich Popppl:\r\nhttp://www.myfonts.com/fonts/canadatype/gator/\r\nRobur
    \ by Canada Type, based on a Georges Auriol design from 1909:\r\nhttp://old.myfonts.com/fonts/canadatype/robur/\r\nHenriette
    Black by Typejockeys:\r\nhttp://www.typejockeys.com/fonts/Henriette\r\nWindsor,
    originally by Stephenson Blake circa 1905:\r\nhttp://www.myfonts.com/search/windsor/fonts/\r\nVerona
