If Medieval means blackletter, you’re in a trouble, but if that is the case I’d choose some rotunda in the spirit of [[http://new.myfonts.com/fonts/linotype/san-marco/|San Marco]] (not really Medieval, it’s early Renaissance). If your Medieval concept accepts Carolingian times, I’d say something like [[http://www.paratype.com/fstore/fonts/Alcuin.htm|Alcuin]]. For nice capitals: maybe overused, but [[http://www.paratype.com/fstore/default.asp?fcode=609&letter=C|Charlemagne]] could be an option.
