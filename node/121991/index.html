Hoping someone can identify the typeface that's currently on the Cartier watch face. Much obliged!
It’s not a typeface, but a custom logo/typography. Typefaces with very similar proportions are the slab serifs Donnerstag and Aviano Slab: http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/insigne/donnerstag/ http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/insigne/aviano-slab/ You’d have to do some serious editing to them to match the logo—widen (Donnerstag), edit the serifs and round off the serif transitions. EDIT: Based on Don’s samples, Aviano Wedge might make a good base, as well: http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/insigne/aviano-wedge/ Columbia Titling is another candidate for editing: http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/typetanic-fonts/columbia-titling/ If the details aren’t a big deal, House Industry’s Luxury Diamond evokes a similar feel: http://www.houseind.com/fonts/luxuryfonts
