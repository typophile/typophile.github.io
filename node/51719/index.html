Best beginner pen... Schaeffer fountain pens, I still use them for commercial work. They flow freely and give you an unimpeeded relationship to the paper. Pentel Cartridge brush, the freedom from dip and a good stroke for the money. Books are another animal... check with John Neal Bookseller. He has a great selection. Michael
