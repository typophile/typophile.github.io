Hello Typophiles! So, I after giving my second round of this logo to the client, she has gotten back to me and told me that she cant decide so she wants me to…and i just can’t handle this kind of pressure! I was hoping I could get some opinions from any and all on which version of the logo they like the best. Critique on how I can make the logo better (without modifying it too much as the client is expecting to see something similar) would be great as well!

Client: Salon

The variations: On the top you will notice the “M” is a bit wider. I drew this type by hand and kind of just eyeballed it at first and this is how it ended up. Upon shrinking the logo down, i noticed that (at least I thought) the M seemed a bit wide for the rest of the set. I know that that is allowed, but it seemed to look odd to me so i created the bottom two with this is mind. The only other variance is of course the flourish off the the last A.

Please let me know what you think, like, and dislike! TIA!

I like v3 more than the others. The M is definitely overpoweringly wide in the first two, and the flourish on the A seems more natural on v3 than v

It’d be cool if you could break that M somewhere to match the D, all the variance seems to be at the end of the word… but you said you’re picking from these 4, so I guess that’s of little-to-no-importance.

All that said, version 3 would be my choice.

What I would suggest is to also try and join the D (I don’t see a reason if the A doesn’t have a cross bar that D should be broken) and make M just as big as other letters. I still find it distracting in V3 and V4. You’re having some problems with ID combination at the bottom because D is broken.

Is this some hair salon or something like that?

And when you present the next image of the logo (especially this one that will have a lot of breathing space around it), please make it breathable and use some more spacing. It’s easier to see the goods and the bads if the surrounding area is not distracting.
