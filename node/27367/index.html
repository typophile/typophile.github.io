I was hoping someone could recommend a nice headline font for a trade magazine, something with some character and interest (not same-old), but at the same time not too over the top. Would prefer something relatively new. Need multiple weights – extra bold, bold and medium – and would love it if a condensed version was also available. Any ideas? I was looking at something called Flama on Village.

On a different note, what are your thoughts on the following fonts for body copy - Miller, Whitman, Joanna - and Proxima Sans for sidebar boxes?

Appreciate your help. Steve

Much of this depends on your audience. Can you tell us more?

Heads: Flama is great. Amplitude FF Quadraat Display Ubik Tasse Titling Gothic

Body: Miller or Whitman. Joanna is older and less functional. Consider also Delicato

Sidebars: Proxima Nova (revision of Proxima Sans) is great. Any of the heads I suggested would also work well in sidebars.
