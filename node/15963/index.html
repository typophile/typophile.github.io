Exciting and Fresh Swedish Typographic Designer On National Television!

November 1, 2005 • fredo

Yesterday as I was making a sandwich in the kitchen my spouse shouted FREDRIIK! THERE-IS-SOMEONE-DRAWING-TYPES-ON-TEEEVEEEE! Knowing this could be ever so brief I ran to our beloved Tube and lo and behold: Peter Bruhn of Fountain in a cosy beard showing a reporter some basic design aspects, pencil sketches, some digital work and his latest typeface, named after his grandmother (I think). It lasted for at least a couple of minutes. And for members and lurkers of Typophile from Sweden and those of You in Finland, Norway and Denmark who can view SVT1, there is a rerun tonight at 23.20. Don’t miss it!
