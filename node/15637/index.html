Chinese Space Program Propaganda

October 14, 2005 • Joe Pemberton

Take a look at these beautiful and cute promotional posters from the Chinese Space Program. As a commentary, it's interesting to see the ones with clearly Chinese imagery (the Great Wall, bow and arrow, toddler with Panda, etc.) juxtaposed with posters that took all their cues from Russian communist propaganda (workers united in exuberant cheering holding red flags).
