August 25, 2005 • dave bailey

Hey guys/girls, I had a little bit of extra time tonight and this is what became of it. Purely a collage of a lower case Didot Bold 'y'. Maybe I was thinking about Pirates of the Caribbean that I watched earlier, not really sure. Opinions? Throw it out? Keep it? I kind of like it...but what do you think? It was kind of random so either way it's all good. My only feeling is that it might be a tad top heavy, I'm thinking maybe to add a few y's in the bottom left corner (black) to give some more movement to it. Maybe even move the title down there? Sometimes I do random stuff like this with no real intent and I like to see what others think because as 'art' it's hard for me to critique myself. Thanks. -Dave
I agree with it being top heavy. I'd make the tag line "y marks the spot" more prominent by modifying you layout: try playing with the darher letters converging towards the white area that contains the tag line. Also, maybe consider making it a bit rougher and dirtier in texture. You could add some transparency to the letters to give it some colour differentiation, thus building up the texture that you have going there. Try to play with the concept that the text is bringing to it: If y marks the spot, what is the spot it is marking? Enjoy!
