Hubert was kind enough to respond to my email. He said that Mommie was finished only a year before the cover was done, and so he did not do the cover. He went on to say, "...inspiration for Mommie was a calligraphy by Tony Di Spigna. For me the Spencerian Hand was quite new." Recently I have been taking a deep look into the art of cursive type. I'm not only interested in the technical aspects, but also its history and in the way one can 'style' the medium too. http://www.iampeth.com/index.htm http://www.iampeth.com/artwork.htm
