Welcome Florian Hardwig - Help us solve the last unidentified requests

January 3, 2010 • Bald Condensed

As I sideways mentioned in my New Year’s post, Florian Hardwig’s Typophile account was upgraded to Short Cap (Moderator) status. Specifically, he will pair up with me and Stephen Coles as co-moderators for the Type ID Board. I’d like to welcome him aboard our team of hard-working volunteers keeping this place clean and organised.

Now it looks like he takes his new role seriously, as he has been working backwards tagging loads of past threads. The advantage is that you now can easily see which ones have been solved, and which ones haven’t yet. I invite you to all dive in and have a go at the few unidentified ones. What a way to start the new year! :^)
