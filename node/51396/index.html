isn’t the montessori method all about adaptation and interaction and environment?

why would a school that focuses on this method require a structured and repeated ‘mark’ I think it would be more suitable and unique to have an adaptive mark (i.e, a series of images supplied by the students, or are representative of the ideals of Montessori)

or if that proves too absract a single mark ABOUT the kids. get the students to draw symbols (not illustrations) of learning, of what they do - and use them. or .. get them to draw their world outside the house shape.

seriously … it’s such an amazing opportunity to explore some abstraction and difference and without wanting to sound totally negative - what you present leaves a lot to be desired in my opinion.

schools are about education, fun and interaction. create a logo that communicates and (perhaps on another level) educates whoever interacts with.

in short … make it flexible like the schooling method.
