The top and bottom are calligraphy in decorative blackletter styles. They are two different different designs. Nothing really matches either one. But here are some fancy blackletter fonts that have some similarities: Beckinslade http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/gatf/beckinslade/ Black Swan BF http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/bomparte/black-swan/ Monkeytails http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/wiescherdesign/monkeytails/ Kingthings Spike http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/cheapprofonts/kingthings-spike-pro/ KingthingsSpikeless http://www.fontsquirrel.com/fonts/Kingthings-Spikeless Baroque Text JF http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/jawfonts/baroque-text-jf/ Aeronaut http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/facetype/aeronaut/ Rheingold http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/scriptorium/rheingold/ Carol http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/classic/carol/ Celebration http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/rmu/celebration/ Savoy http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/classic/savoy/ Uthan http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/intellecta/uthan/ Sepian – already suggested by Ryuk – http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/laura-worthington/sepian/ Dearest http://www.fontsquirrel.com/fonts/Dearest Portcullion http://www.fontsquirrel.com/fonts/Portcullion Zenda http://www.fontsquirrel.com/fonts/Zenda Kelly Ann Gothic http://www.dafont.com/kelly-ann-gothic.font You might also browse through an interesting collection of free fonts by Paul Lloyd which includes some interesting fancy blacketters http://moorstation.org/typoasis/designers/lloyd/ Don
