MetaPhile: Cannot view all posts by a user or favourite or block users

January 24, 2010 • joeclark

If I want to read all the posts and/or comments by user X, I can’t. Nor can I favourite or block a user.

There is limited support for this kind of thing. If you click on a user’s name on the left of their post, you can “Track” the threads they’ve started or replied to.

I’d like to see the links on the Track page go directly to the user’s post in that thread.
