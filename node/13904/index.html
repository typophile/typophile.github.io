Tor Slotmann

July 17, 2005 • tor_slotmann

Tor Slotmann is a danish designer who graduated as a graphic designer from Danmarks Designskole in 2004. He works as a freelance designer with type and identity design. He graduated with integritype – a typeface for signage in a hospital. He is currently developing identities and typefaces for various clients.
