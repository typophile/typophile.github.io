The graphical use of arrows first appeared in cave paintings and carvings - arrows depicted in flight pointed to the intended victim, the destination if you will.

Also as most bowmen were right handed, drawn in profile they would point from left to right - hence the left to right arrangement of characters in most western writing systems.
