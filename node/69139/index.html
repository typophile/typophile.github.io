This is a strange genre, and hard to categorize for searches. I think it seems between Art Nouveau and Arts and Crafts, which would make it late 19th - early 20th century. It looks hand-drawn. In the following suggestions, which are to me only suggestive of this font, you could either add small rounded serifs, or make the existing serifs smaller, to make these fonts resemble this sample more (sorry I am not linking this list): Eccentric, Clichee, Nevins Hand, Kolo, Berolina (Solo), Korinna and Stoclet. You could mix the round Kolo O’s with stump-seriffed modification of Eccentric and get a rather similar look I think.
