I don’t mind a lot of fonts, well hierarchied, and am impressed you have the hutzpah to use one of the very few “Unicase with small unicase” typefaces for your main style!

However, the main subhead is a bit off. It is distinguished by size, position and color, so a separate font/style is not necessary. Also, the countdown numbers are odd as the only sans on the page, and don’t seem “techy” enough if you are using them in an illustrative, denotative manner; they also don’t sit very comfortably on the convex gradiented surface.
