I'm not positive that it's Antique Olive, but I think it might be, if you consider how it looks when you add stroke to it. In the following sample I started with Antique Olive Medium and added 5 pt of stroke thickness. Then I converted to outlines and in the first A I grabbed the points for the crossbar and the counter, and moved them downward. You'll notice a little dot on top of the other 2 A's. I think that dot is an artifact of adding the stroke thickness, because it is not showing in the modified A with the counter moved downward. - Mike Yanega[img:sites/default/files/old-images/AntOliveFat_5907.gif]
