Hi Arran,

although, at first sight, the bold cut of Scangraphic’s Grotesk looks quite similar overall (more so than Schulbuch), it’s not it either (the most striking difference is the G, but other characters don’t match either).

Have you searched the booklet for info on the font?

I went to their website (http://typeneu.com/size-format-stock) and found that it’s quite aptly named: An Odyssey in Typography. I could stay there for only about 20 seconds, as my patience didn’t last any longer and I sensed a serious headache coming. So if you can, try browsing the site yourself.

In case that nobody can ID your sample, and as you have the booklet at hand, could you take a straight, frontal close-up of the font? It’s hard to recognize the finer details of the type at 15 pixel height, like in your photo.

Jens
