POLL: "Edumacators" or "Educators"

September 11, 2005 • Norbert Florendo

In the hopes of expanding the Typographic Edumacators topic area (located within Special Interest Groups topics) I invite members to weigh in on the term “Edumacators.”

Several members (hopefully more will join in) are interested in making it into a resource area not only for teachers but for anyone interested in furthering type and typographic education. I do not (nor want) the “power” to change the topic name, but I do want the title to be more inviting to Forum members and “lurkers.”

That being said, I hope the topic will not become an excuse for “hot debates” and stray off-topic.

My vote: I prefer “Type Education” since I feel the term Type is inclusive of both type design and typography, and that Education is open to both teachers and individuals seeking informational resources.
