Favorite Designer

February 27, 2009 • srbooker

To tell you the truth I am new to Graphic Design so the only Graphic Designers or Typographers that I know of are the ones that I have studied in class. I have not yet found one designer that I like in particular but there is a design style that I have been very attracted to since I started in the Graphic Design program at Loyola and that is Swiss Design, which is also known as International Typographic Style. Swiss design is usually asymmetrical, grids are noticeable on site, and when using type it is usually flush left san serif. Swiss design is simple and clean and I like both. I think my favorite thing about the design style is the Swiss fonts. My favorite font, I do know, is from the Swiss design era and it is Univers. Armin Hofmann is one of the designers that I learned about and a lot of his work I do like. Here are two piece that I like and below are some sites that talk about him.
