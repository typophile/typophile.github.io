Should be a grotesque, to match the tone of the era. The grots of that day were generally pretty gnarly, and Vicky sans faces (of which there have been quite a few recently), cleave to that quality in varying degrees -- the temptation being to clean things up and smooth them out. Julian Morey's Sigma is quite true to the originals. My own Figgins Sans has similar proportions to Andron. Also Armitage, Knockout, Monotype Grotesque, National, and Ludwig (by Smeijers, offbeat, but interesting). ** I note that you say illustration rather than illustrators. Do you have much about Edmund Evans?
