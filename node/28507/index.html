October 1, 2006 • Goran Soderstrom

I get this result on a test:

Glyph-Repertoire Not all glyphs with Dot-Names have valid counterparts. g_y.alt, j_y.alt, y_g.alt, y_g_g.alt

Due to my limited english knowledge and technical limitations, I can’t figure out what it means.

Could someone please explain?

Thanks

Goran

If you have an alternate glyph (like g_y.alt), there should be also a glyph named g_y.

If these glyphs are just ligatures, name it g_y and not g_y.alt.
