Sodachrome, a typeface based on colour overlays

April 27, 2009 • Jongseong

This is the coolest thing I’ve seen in a long while: This is Sodachrome, a typeface designed by Ian Moore and Dan Rhatigan of The Colour Grey for Sodabudi, a forthcoming online store for artworks inspired by folk art from India. Dan Rhatigan blogged about it here. When the two parts of the typeface are screenprinted in different colours on top of each other, they produce a charming effect. Not only is this clever, I think it successfully achieves a hand-crafted aesthetic and is fits perfectly for the project.

I used the search function and Google, but this doesn’t seem to have been mentioned yet on Typophile. It looks like Sodabudi will have an exclusive licence for Sodachrome until the end of 2009.
