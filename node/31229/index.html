I’d go for something with proper [[small capitals]]… faking it is never pretty. It seems to be a Trajan style you’re after. Trajan Pro seems to have small caps. You might also like Minerva Display Antiqua by [[Gabor Kothay]] (which doesn’t seem to have small caps unfortunately).
