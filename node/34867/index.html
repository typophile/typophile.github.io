July 4, 2007 • Si_Daniels

Our friends from Boston are visiting for TypeCon - no not David Berlow and the Monotype gang - The Boston Red Sox professional baseball team. And as it happens apart from the waysgoose and Matthew Carter exhibit (which is happening right by the stadium) we’re on our own Friday night. If you’re interested in seeing the MC exhibit and then coming to the game let me know. I have 10 cheap seats ($14) in the out-field bleachers up for grabs. Drop me an Ayrton and a ball-park beer and you’re in.

Cheers, Si

PS. Through my numerous big-league contacts I did suggest that Matthew throw out the first pitch. The fact that they even considered the suggestion, before declining, just shows how far type design has come in the last fifty years.
