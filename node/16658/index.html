Real quick: the term “hotmetal” is best reserved to mean Monotype, Linotype, etc. where the type metal was continuously remelted and reformed (and hence was often warm). In contrast the other -real- stuff is best called “foundry”, or maybe “coldmetal”. To mean both/either, just use “metal”, or maybe “composition”.
