The term is “spurless (geometric) sans serif”. Alternatives include Moderna, P22 Bayer Universal, Solomon and Karbon. Also have a look at some of the bauhaus-inspired fonts out there. Dessau is great though. I’d rather spend some extra and have a decent set of tools.
