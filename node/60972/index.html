FIFA World Cup US Bid 2018/2022 Custom Type

August 14, 2009 • mattmc

I was really excited to see that one of the first projects I’ve gotten to work on while working at Pentagram has been posted on their [[http://pentagram.com/en/new/2009/08/preview-fifa-world-cup-us-bid.php|blog]]. I’ve been designing this custom typeface under the creative direction of Michael Gericke and Luke Hayman.

It’s currently being used on [[http://www.gousabid.com/page/invite/trailer|the GoUsaBid.com]] site. I’ve been incredibly excited to see what the talented designers at Pentagram are doing with the typeface. Look forward to a future Pentagram blog post with all the applications of the bid message and ‘Game’ typeface.
