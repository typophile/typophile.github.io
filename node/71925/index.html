Look in your file/font info menu under metrics and dimensions/key dimensions… Font Lab does not like it if the typoascender and typodecender don’t add up to 1000 units. Yours adds up to 1001. Ignore the negative in the descender when calculating the total. You’ve got ascender 757 and descender 244 = 1001
