It looks like 구름 M (Gureum, meaning "cloud", and M standing for medium weight) offered by the Korean font outfit 윤디자인 (YoonDesign). In fact, you can see the Cyworld logo on the front page for their website. I can't find any further information on the design, though. Korean fonts include latin alphabet glyphs by default in addition to the hangul (Korean alphabet). It is pretty common, I imagine, for Korean designers to use the latin alphabet component of their Korean fonts for latin-alphabet logos.
