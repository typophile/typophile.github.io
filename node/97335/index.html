The most similar font I could find was Nick Curtis's [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/nicksfonts/gandy-dancer-nf/|Gandy Dancer NF]], his revival of an old ATF face called Tabard, from 1912. Other sort of similar styles were Fonthead's [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/fonthead/el-franco/|El Franco]] a recreation of 16th century lettering; and A Scholtz fonts number called [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/scholtz/figment/|Figment]].
