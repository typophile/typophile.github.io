It all depends on the volume you'll be printing, along with what you hpoe for as far as unit cost and sale price, and what you want the end result to look like. Embroidery is another option. Most even slightly developed areas will have a screen printing shop, often specializing in T-shirts, and they can outsource work that is beyond their on-location capacity. If you're doing a very limited run (less than 20), a thermal transfer print might be ideal. The transfer material its self is costly, but on a per unit basis, it may be cheaper than the set-up charges for a silkscreen, unless you intend to print a couple dozen or more.
