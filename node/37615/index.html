Hi Anna,

what’s the name of the book? Could it be hand-lettered, too? (Hard to say from 4—different—characters …)

All I can contribute is that the capital ‘A’ with the minuscule structure is typically Italian (—and so is the ‘B’ with loop). There are some free fonts that feature that contrast caused by using a pointed flexible pen, but all of them show the ‘other A’, as it is used in France. I’m not aware of any digital version (of the Italian school script) which has contrast.
