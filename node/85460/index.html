Eyechart Bold is very similar (rip-off?) to Engravers Gothic. Part of the thickess, MR PORTER woodmark is very close to [[http://new.myfonts.com/fonts/paratype/engravers-gothic/|Engravers Gothic]], something in between regular and bold weight (some kind of semibold). Could be Engravers Gothic (regular) artificially thickened with an outline stroke…
