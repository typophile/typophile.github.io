Anyone know what font is being used for the words L'Avion in the logo? http://us.lavion.com Thanks in advance.
I think it is a custom design, as most logos are. The closest techno font I could find at MyFonts was [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/larabie/xirod/|Xirod]], by Ray Larabie. You could use an inverted U to get a similar N, and flip it for the V. It would be a starting point, if you didn't want to redraw everything. - Mike Yanega
