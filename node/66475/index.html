This is not a single font but rather a general style (art-deco-ish) of fonts. These things were not designed with computer fonts back in the 50’s ;-)

Broadway is the most familiar one of that class of fonts. You should have that one. find [[http://www.identifont.com/find?similar=Broadway&q=Go|similar fonts here]].

[[http://fontenwerkplaats.blogspot.com/|Richard Keijzer]] likes this kind of fonts very much and [[http://home.tiscali.nl/r_keijzer/lettertypes_english.htm|re-creates them]] in digital form. Help yourself.

This high-contrast top-heavy Art Deco style was very popular in the Netherlands. Have a look at the work of Anton Kurvers (p65, 74) or H.Th. Wijdeveld (p69) in [[Jan Middendorp]]’s Dutch Type.

Also related: New Amsterdam by [[Arjen Noordeman]], AT Muntel by Attack. F
