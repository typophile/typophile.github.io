And I'm the kind of guy that mucks about with minute details a lot! For example, look at the top left corner of the top counter in the uppercase B. Those kinds of differences are just placing a seed of doubt in my mind. While I agree that Life is very close and may indeed be the original font, due to those differences I'm thinking it's a little more likely that the original font is something else, which we may never discover.
