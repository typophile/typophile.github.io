2007-09-17 00:42:50 • Hiroshige

I have an incomplete set of Letraset supplementary catalogs from the Seventies and Eighties. Only a few are what you could call Letragraphica catalogs, but most of them have Letragraphica faces in them:

18 New Type Faces / Letragraphica / Featuring Letraset International Typeface Competition Winners 1973 (1973)

Range Extension 1974

Letragraphica 16, 17, and 18 (1974)

Letragraphix Number Two (features Ray Cruz; no date, probably 1975)

New Typefaces 1976

Letragraphix Number Three (designed by Kit Hinrichs, 1976)

Catalog Supplement ‘77 (1977)

Letragraphix Volume Four (designed by SVA students, 1977)

The Letragraphica Gallery (purple, orange and red cover; no date, but probably 1977 or 1978)

The Letragraphica Gallery (black and brown cover; no date, but probably 1979)

Letragraphix Volume 6 (no date, probably 1979)

Sweet Treats (1980)

Letragraphix Volume 7 (1980)

Graphic Treasures (1981)

Letragraphix Volume 8 (1981)

Art-I-Facts (1982)

Celebrate New Letraset Products (1984)

LetraGazette Issue No. 1 (1984)

LetraGazette Issue No. 3 (1985)

LetraGazette Issue No. 5 (1986)

(The ones where I have guessed at the dates are based on what typefaces were announced in the catalog.)

One other thing: These were all U.S. editions.
