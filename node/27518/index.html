August 8, 2006 • Si_Daniels

Barbarians at the gates! The Apple Typography Wiki - http://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Apple_typography seems to have been messed up, half the content has disappeared and heretics are ranting about Apple Garamond! Maybe the typophiliMac faithful can step in and sort it out. Is “logography” even a real word?

In my short time posting here, I gather OS-bundled fonts are not regarded very highly…with Arial for Windows being at the very top of the S#!^ list. Comic Sans is a close second for encouraging the most seething hatred.

Being a scumbag PC user for the last decade, I guess I don’t have all that much sympathy for Apple Garamond or Myriad Apple. Myriad Pro, Garamond Pro and Garamond Premiere Pro in OTF seem to do just fine when they can fit into my sign design needs. Myriad is a very good typeface to use in making neon filled channel letters.
