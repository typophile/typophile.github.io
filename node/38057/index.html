typefaces for technology school prospectus

October 13, 2007 • buddhaboy

I have a brief for an A4 prospectus that will have a glossy outer cover, and silk art inner, all printed offset litho.

As the school wants to promote a modern, technological look I would like to use Meta for the cover masthead, and inner headlines. I know it’s omniscient these days but it has the right amount of personality without decending into technogimickyness…

I would like to buy PMNCaecilia as a standfirst typeface where some pages will have a preamble narrative.

However, I am undecided on what to use as a body type… I’m inclined to stick with meta, as I don’t want to extend to 3 faces on a page, so my feeling is to only choose something other than Meta for body if it presents readability problems.

I’d be grateful for advice if anyone has done anything similar (which I’m sure you have!), and if my type choices are wrong diddly wrong.

Steve
