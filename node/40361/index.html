[[Indices]] : [[Writers]] : Andy Crewdson Andy Crewdson is a type historian and critic (perhaps from Bethesda, Maryland) who studied history at UC Berkeley, and more recently in Washington, DC. Crewdson created the popular blog [[Lines and Splines]] and later replaced it with [[New Series]]. He has been noticeably absent from typographic discussion and publishing since 2003, a fact bemoaned almost yearly with cries of "Whatever happened to Andy Crewdson?" on all the type blogs and forums.
