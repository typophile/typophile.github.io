body: "Introduction to type design in FontLab Studio v.5 taught by Hannes Famira.
  Participants will learn FontLab Studio v.5 by drawing and producing their own functioning
  digital type faces. Topics include tracing artwork, perfecting curves and building
  diacritics, as well as the secrets of spacing, kerning, and hinting. Participants
  should have basic typographic knowledge and some experience in a vector-based drawing
  application such as Adobe Illustrator or Macromedia Freehand.\r\n\r\n9 Tuesday evenings,
  May 30-Aug. 1, 6:30-9:30.  $625 plus $25 registration fee.\r\n\r\nHannes Famira
  studied graphic and typographic design at the Royal Academy of Fine Arts in The
  Hague, Netherlands. After apprenticeships at Studio Dunbar and the Font Bureau,
  he worked at Meta Design and at the Buro Petr van Blokland. Hannes established his
  studio, Das Kombinat, at the SfG, School for Design in Basel, Switzerland. He now
  lives in Brooklyn.\r\n\r\nFor information and registration, call 212-353-4195."
