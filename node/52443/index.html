2008-12-08 20:39:04 • Bert Vanderveen
I found that rather than Exporting to pdf, printing the same file to PS and distilling reduces the image overhead to nearly nothing -- apparently storing it only once. In one case I sent a client vastly downsampled proofs -- up to the point he started complaining -- because of a single huge image that occurred every other few pages. Even after downsampling, the PDF exceeded 10 MB. After his final approval, I made a PS file for the printer, converted it to PDF to double check -- and it was about 5 MB, with the highest image rez possible. Embarrassed looks all around. Your mileage might vary.
