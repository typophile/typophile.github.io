Hmm, if you want something in the general direction of Helvetica but fresh and not as tired – how about Breuer* maybe? (There's also a Headline variant.) Or Paralucent? Warmer / less tech-y: National? If you want less quirky and more geometric/clean, maybe look at Graphik, and Founder's Grotesk. These are just a few that popped into my mind, there are many more… Maybe also look at the Helvetica Alternatives Fontlist over at FontShop. (* Be sure to read the EULA, IIRC Typetrust requires special licenses for logos and such.)
