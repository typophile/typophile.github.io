"Published by Orion" and other bits use Olympia. The only digital version I'm aware of is Olympian by Dan Solo on the 24 Victorian Display Fonts CD. It used to be made and sold by Dover Publications, but they quit offering it a few years ago. Copies can still be found on Amazon and eBay. Edit: I forgot that Olympian can be had also as Rochambeau by Scriptorium.
