Darden Studio and Pampa Type updates

August 17, 2008 • SuperUltraFabulous

Hi guys et gals:

Josh Darden has finally updated his new studio website and that means for you and me lots of goodies:

https://www.dardenstudio.com/

The free font Birra Stout (Birra is eyetalian for beer) is super chunky and cute, tho I’m pretty Nick Curtis would roll his eyes—it’s more his thing :oP https://www.dardenstudio.com/typefaces/birra_stout

Freight is now in OpenType Std form for only slightly more, tho I’m not sure of all those nifty features are integrated and contextual : https://www.dardenstudio.com/typefaces/freight_text

The Untitled face that won a TDC award in 2007 has been released as Jubilat. Its available in OpenType Pro too: https://www.dardenstudio.com/typefaces/jubilat

There are other gems all over his page:

This italic seems like an extension Corundum, but notice the gothic with the funky number eight… I need that!

Read the blog. There are plenty of previews of coming attractions. Corundum News and a commissioned face for Mucca’s rebranding of Canton Liqueur. There are some interesting designs in the tshirts section:

http://www.dardenstudio.com/notes/ http://www.dardenstudio.com/shirts/

There is an in-house branding technical sans that I really like. Argent? Notice the capital R: I spied that in the Birra PDF specimen.

I am truly impressed Mr. Darden versatility as a type designer… the next Matthew Carter?

But lets no forget our friend halfway around the world—PampaType released Garrone: http://www.pampatype.com/php/2008/Tipografias_GR.php?secc=cara&menu=menu_T_C.html&sub1=sub1_T_GR_C.html&sub2=sub2_T_G_C.html&pag=pag_T_GR_C.html

It seems to take the best qualities of Rayuela Chocolate and Alrt and transform them into something exciting and expressive, yet still entirely suitable for serious book/text work and related to his other productions. Yummy!

Enjoy and happy font shopping… next payday I promise!!!!!

Mike Diaz :-)
