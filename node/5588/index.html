And while we've been doing our best to amass a pile of tunes that will please the discerning typographer, we're left with a question: What are the must-have tunes to please a Typecon crowd? Mind you, I'm not fishing for "what's your favorite song?" here - I'm looking for specific songs that mention type, alphabet, fonts, letters, etc etc. Spoken word or musical.
