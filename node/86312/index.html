Hi

the guides started behaving crazy. i can no longer right click on them and move them to my desired location. If you are on a PC right-click will bring up a context menu. To move them use left-click and drag. Also you may have inadvertently locked your guides in the Editing Layers Panel.

And also when i am trying to overlap some masks they are not getting overlapped properly i mean some weird white spaces are appearing. Make sure your contours are all going in the same direction. Mixing contour directions will give strange results when removing overlaps.

M
