Wijdeveld

January 27, 2002 • bardram

Another new font, based on the art of poster designer Wijdeveld from the Netherlands.

Cool!

And the hairline solutions on "B", "K", "R", and especially "N" are particularly interesting.

"N" perhaps should be widened by a pixel or so to provide a bit more counter room, though. It's the darkest glyph of the set in color, and the one most in danger of optically clogging up.
