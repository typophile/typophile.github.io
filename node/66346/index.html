The M and T are similar to Latin Wide. I don’t think you will find a closer match. Artcraft is as close as your going to get for “ickey” and “hompson”, but it’s been stretched horizontally and fairly tightly spaced. “EQUIPPED” is Microgramma or Eurostile.

“Schiefer…” is Century Bold Italic (a.k.a. Century Expanded Bold Italic). The other font in the second one is Futura Bold Condensed.
