The letters are in an old style called stovepipe. FAIK They are not a font as such. I can offer several fonts that generally match the letterforms shown in the image --_A_ with apex top, _R_ with vertical leg, _S_ with horizontal center stroke. Valuco http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/aesthetic-apparatus/valuco/ Gaz http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/typodermic/gaz/ Liquor Store http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/chank/liquorstore/ Iwan Reschniev http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/fdi/iwan-reschniev/ Lobby Card JNL http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/jnlevine/lobby-card/ There are quite a few that that match the _R_ but not the _S_ and vice versa. Run through the stovepipe tag at Myfonts http://www.myfonts.com/search/tag%3Agaspipe/fonts/ and you will see what I mean. All are wider than your letters, although Iwan Reschnie is closer in width than the others. Don
