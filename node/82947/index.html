Most of time, having lots of !important declarations indicate a poor understanding of the cascade. But of course, it can be used in an appropriate way, too.

The z-index comes with positioned – and stacked/overlapping – elements. It depends on your design ideas and intentions whether you need it or not. You sure can do splendid things without it.

Vendor-specific prefixes like -moz can be used to serve declarations that haven’t been standardized (yet) to certain browsers that already understand them.
