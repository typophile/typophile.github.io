Just as an update on Scribus' support for OpenType: I have a copy of v1.3.3.8 for Windows, and on this version the answer is that it's happy to use OpenType fonts, and it has a glyph palette for accessing all characters, as well as a menu item for inserting standard ligs, BUT it doesn't seem to apply OT features: ligatures aren't substituted automatically; if you apply small caps, superscript etc it just scales the regular characters like Word would; and there aren't any tools/options specifically about applying OT features. Just so ya know.
