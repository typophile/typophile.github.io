Since the mark is way more dynamic than the type, you could try something different: less boxy, maybe more condensed (since the name is pretty long). The byline’s typeface, for example, looks more in line with the mark. If the client will not budge, you could try to change the way the text is laid out (multiple lines, perhaps even tilted).
