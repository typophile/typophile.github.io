Improv is not free font and, since Agfa/MT took the ICG fonts over, they've been rather pricy. I can't think of anything free like Improv. You can do a search by Theme (style, really) on Dafont from the link to Flowerchild above to see if something free there is acceptably close. I'd suggest Postino (actually a backslanted Postino Italic would be even closer) as a cheaper commercial alternative.
