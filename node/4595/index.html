Stephen: Nice catch. I served as the first art director of "Venceremos," which is indeed still being published as a quarterly tabloid directed at the "Latina/o & Chicana/o" community here in Salt Lake City. It struck me as a great name for a font, although the publication has yet to utilize it. ;) That looks like good advice on the uc "R", too. I'll play with it & post the developments. David Thometz
