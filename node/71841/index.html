I downloaded “SF Transrobotics” from Dafont and used the Bold version to make the following simulation of the RX7 lettering. I added three black rectangles (one at each corner of the X and one below the counter of the R). I converted the R to outlines and moved the points of the leg towards the stem of the R, to widen the leg. I made a slim white rectangle to make the cutout on the R, and then I slanted everything by 12 degrees. Could have chopped off the corner of the 7 with a white square.
