2007-02-15 01:40:06 • jupiterboy

Well, any face could theoretically or potentially have its stencil version, if you need to paint a sign with it… :-)

Have you ever seen Avia? It’s inspired by hairlines that go missing in “faulty platemaking or light inking”… There’s no real “need” for a font like this, but I think that it’s fun that someone was inspired by this kind of error to make a typeface from it. (Maybe I’m crazy for seeing a parallel to Garamond Stencil in this, but it just popped into my head when I read your post.)
