A couple of recent designs with both Cyrillic and Latin characters, shown in the Language Culture Type book from ATypI/Graphis:

Rouble by Andrey Belonogov

Serp'n'Molot by Tagir Safayev (based on letterforms designed by Sergey Chekhonin in the early 20th century: not strictly Constructivist, but very wonderful).

I'm not sure where you could get either, but the good people at www.paratype.com should be able to help you.
