Presentation Paper

January 26, 2014 • Classic Font

Who manufactures the absolute finest white laser presentation paper? Something with a nice weight and takes color well. Currently, I am using a 32lb white paper for my resume and power point presentations. Thank you very much for your time.
Try Mohawk and their various product lines (Strathmore etc). They’ll send you free swatches. Mokawk Superfine Ultrawhite (or whatever the brightest shade is called) is one possible candidate for “the absolute finest white” paper. Also try Monadnock, or, for a cheaper solution, Finch and Neena papers.
