Each Fontographer serial number is only eligible to one upgrade to FontLab Studio 5, and we keep track of serial numbers (for all our applications) that are being posted in illegal download sources.

I believe that Fontographer 4.1 (the old Macromedia product, which is still the latest version on Windows) actually displays the serial number in the About box, while Fontographer 4.7 (the relase that we prepared for Mac OS X) and our other products diplay the product number there, which is not the serial number. This is because it is in the users' best interest to keep their serial number private – so that others cannot spot it, write it down and then use it to upgrade or serialize an illegal copy of their own.
