This style of lettering goes back to Rudolf Koch’s Neuland of 1923. I didn’t spot an exact match – it might not be digitized – but here a couple of fairly similar fonts: Xavier Sans Black http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/castletype/xavier/ and Aarde Black http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/scholtz/aarde/ Also an assortment of somewhat similar fonts, including Lithos Black http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/adobe/lithos/ but they don’t seem as close as these two.
