Given the age of the sample (turn of the 20th century) I doubt Tungsten etc. can be considered. I am thinking about a narrow variant of Monotype Grotesque (perhaps No. 77), while “RIVISTA” etc. could be a wider variant (No. 10?). Another candidate for DINAMO is Advertisement Grotesque Condensed, also known as Narrow Grotesque, it had been around since 1865.
