Gentium and Charis updated

November 13, 2014 • J. Tillman

Two free SIL fonts have been updated.

Charis: Overview at http://scripts.sil.org/cms/scripts/page.php?item_id=CharisSI Download and List of Changes at http://scripts.sil.org/cms/scripts/page.php?item_id=CharisSIL_download Note the Charis SIL Compact; the “Compact” refers to the leading.
