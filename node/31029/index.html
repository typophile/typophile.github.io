I checked this one and I believe we are looking at two different typefaces here. While the “copy” indeed appears to be House Chalet (good one Sebastian), the logo shares several character traits with Neuzeit Grotesk, apart from the more closed UC ‘C’.

Ultimately though, I think the logo was hand drawn, as the lack of optical corrections where the curves hit the the straights seem to indicate.
