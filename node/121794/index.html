Lots of near matches (30 typefaces matching in a range from 94% to 97%) when I searched using FindMyFont. Nothing with both the stepped back upper story on the |B| and the exact same length (short) crossbar on the |E|.

Maxine Sans Heavy topped the list: http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/activesphere/maxine-sans/

EDIT: A post on this forum from two years ago claims it is ITC Blair. Good for the |E|, not so much so for the |B|: http://typophile.com/node/96757 http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/linotype/blair-itc/

According to this, the type is bespoke: http://www.kms-team.com/en/referenzen/bentley#details
