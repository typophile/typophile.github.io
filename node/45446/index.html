At one point during the four-day-bindery-athon the conversation turned to watermarks. The 9# vintage, 100% cotton rag onion skin we were using in the Japanese bindery project is watermarked, so, I took the opportunity to talk about watermarks and paper making with my LSU graphic design student assistants.
