2011-07-02 11:06:24 • Moon.

2011-07-02 12:29:05 • Moon.
Lettering can be handmade OR produced on a computer. The real difference is that a piece of lettering is produced as a one off solution to a specific problem, whereas a font is made to work as well as possible in a large number of unknown applications.
