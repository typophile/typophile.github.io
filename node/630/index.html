We have always designed treacyfaces.com to be accessible to the greatest number of people possible.

Unlike some sites, we choose not to shut out or frustrate some users by forcing browser or plug-in upgrades right at the entry. I want a happy prospect coming through the door, not one or one who feels frustrated or shunned.

There have been no customer complaints from that buyer, nor from any of our world wide customers who purchased via treacyfaces.com last week.
