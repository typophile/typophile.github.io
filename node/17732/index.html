The typeface was designed about two years ago by Ludovic Balland (studio: the Remingtons, Basel, Switzerland). He named it »Stanley« in honor of Stanley Morison. He also used it in a series of bookjackets for »Das Magazin Schweizer Bibliothek« which can be seen here.
