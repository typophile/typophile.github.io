Hi guys This is scanned from the cover of a book. Sorry for the quality. I remember the font from other books in the 1970s. Can anyone tell me if it’s available these days? I’ve tried myfonts to no avail. Thanks.

Hercules/Herkules. The most readily available version is Dieter Steffmann’s Herkules. The download link says T1, but there’s an Opentype font in the download as well.

It was also available from Opti as Huk Light.
