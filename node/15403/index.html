Hello typophilers,

it’s been a long time since my last post and i missed being around the block.

So, here I come up with a series of posters I did for a small exhibition of unpublished works of mine created during the last 5 years.If your way happento cross Thessaloniki you could contact with me to visit the exhibition. It will be open untill midof November (maybe it will last even more).

About the posters, I used two layers: a constant one with the information in handmade lettering and the background layer which is changing, taking different photos I shot around the city or in friends' houses.

The posters are not trying to describe the exhibition. They are a theme by themselves, though, inevitably they give a hint of my graphic style, which is what you are going to watch in the exhibition.

By the posters I wanted to address to people who live in the same (urban) environment as me. The photos present this environment, which at this point is filtered by my eyes. So, actually I state my existence in this place we share.

christos
