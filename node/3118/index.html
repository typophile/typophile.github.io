Good question -- I never knew what it was either. But a couple of searches on Google finally revealed an answer: it's a symbol that indicates that though the particular package you scanned may not be exactly 5 fluid ounces or 150ml, if you took, say, 100 of those packages, they would average out to 5 fluid ounces or 150ml.
