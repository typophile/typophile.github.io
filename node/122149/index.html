Easy? You have got to be joking.

First, the sample is ridiculously small. In order to do a visual database search, the letters have to be at least 100 px tall and at a decent resolution. Your sample is neither.

Second, do you have any idea how many vaguely similar sans serif typefaces there are? Hundreds.

Third, matching such a generic style of font to an actual typeface based on a seven-character sample is damn near impossible. The best you can hope for is finding something similar, or locating an interview or portfolio by the designer online in which the typeface is named.

After locating a larger sample and using Photoshop to get a better image, my best guess is Proxima Nova Extra Condensed. It’s a decent match to |GRAVITY|, but not so great compared to the other title text that precedes that frame: http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/marksimonson/proxima-nova/
