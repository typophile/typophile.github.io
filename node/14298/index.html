2005-08-03 03:47:39 • WurdBendur

While they both could be called humanist sans, Myriad and Lucida are two different typefaces.

Myriad was designed by a team at Adobe, led by Carol Twombly and Robert Slimbach: http://store.adobe.com/type/browser/P/P_1706.html

Lucida Grande is a display version of Lucida Sans. Both were designed by Kris Bigelow and Charles Holmes: http://store.adobe.com/type/browser/P/P_1359.html
