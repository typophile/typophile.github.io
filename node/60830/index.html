A mixture of [[http://www.pia-frauss.de/fonts/fl.htm|Francisco Lucas Llana, Francisco Lucas Briosa]] and [[http://www.pia-frauss.de/fonts/tg.htm|Tagettes]]. All UC characters except for the C are from Francisco Lucas Briosa. The C is from Francisco Lucas Llana. The lowercase characters are set in Tagettes.
