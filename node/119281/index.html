_Eau Cologne_ and _Saigon-Hanoi_ are not the same design. Note for example the different _N_. For a striped font with the _N_ in _Eau Cologne_, you can use Archive Copperplate Head [img:sites/default/files/old-images/ArchiveCopperplateHeadInUse_5607.gif] http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/archivetype/archive-copperplate-head/ which is an accurate, but indifferent quality, digital rendition of Hermann Ihlenburg's Copperplate from the 19th century. Change the convex inner curve on the terminal serifs for _E_, _L_, _C_, and _G_ to a concave inner curve by blanking out the unwanted part, and you have a fair match to _Eau Cologne_ . Don
