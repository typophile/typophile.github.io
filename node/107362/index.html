Actually, the more I look at it the more I think it’s a customization of existing typefaces. Nothing is really consistent:

  • “WTAPS”: /S should be “slab-serifed” too but it’s not, making me thinking they could have started with some “[[http://typophile.com/node/99935#comment-539470|United Sans]]-like”, manually adding slab-serifs
  • “LIMA” VS. “WTAPS”: nothing matches (different weight, different /A…). “LIMA” is actually matching many “NBA” font from Eriq Jaffe’s collection I’ve already pointed to.
  • /76 counter: also inconsistent with both “WTAPS” and “LIMA” designs. Counter can’t be square but hexagonal/faceted if had to match “WTAPS” or /7 would have been bottom serifed to match “LIMA”.
