May I suggest my own Proza? It was initially intended as a sans-serif companion to Garamond, and still shares its humanist skeleton. You can read all about it here: http://ilovetypography.com/2013/11/01/making-fonts-proza-typeface/ For similar proportions you might try this: http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/storm/jannon-sans/ It's based on Jannon, rather than Garamond, but it's probably the closest of a match you'll find.
