April 8, 2014 • CoralieLG

Do you know any font that is the same style as UltraCondensed Sans Serif?
Too many copies/rip-off/clones on the market for this one and UltraCondensed Sans Serif is one of them. I guess you could go with Michael Gill’s [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/letraset/iris|Iris]] or David Berlow and Kelley Ehrgott-Milligan’s [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/fontbureau/empire|Empire]].

You may like: [[http://www.findmyfont.com/index.php/fonts/font-preview?fset=Letraset&ffam=Iris%20LET%20-%20Plain&fid=9dea0a69b9da7a09bdd65248850c707e&fsize=60&text=NOPQRSM&fit=1|Iris]] by Michael Gills [1930] available from Letraset, ITC [[http://www.findmyfont.com/index.php/fonts/font-preview?fset=Font-Bureau&ffam=Empire%20-%20Regular&fid=548f10893e9e2d53585aba24c61658fc&fsize=60&text=NOPQRSM&fit=1|Empire]] by Morris Fuller Benton [1937] available from Font Bureau, Tilde, Bitstream, …

There are also many clones (under different names) by the usual font forgery companies like Fantazia Concepts, Cambridge Fontworks, Bay Animation, Arts & Letters, etc.
