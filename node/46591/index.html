Question? is Ondine and Calligrapher different names for the same font? If not, who designed Calligrapher?

Hi Michelle,

[[Ondine]] was designed by [[Adrian Frutiger]].

Digital versions of Ondine can be purchased via a number of foundries. These versions may vary, in terms of character count, quality, price, design details &c. There’s also a version called ‘Formal Script 421’ by Bitstream, which is (now) legitimate (please someone correct me if I’m wrong).

The free font ‘Calligrapher’ is an unauthorized rip-off of it. It’s very rare that you can get a quality font for free. Most of the time, it’s plain plagiarism. Read also the FAQ on free fonts.

My suspicion is: all they did is rename the font and change a few glyphs. The ‘ß’ is different, for example. And the ‘Q’ is different from the version I have – which doesn’t necessarily mean that those glyphs haven’t been designed by Frutiger. They might have been included in other versions.
