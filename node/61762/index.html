Fears of @font-face

September 8, 2009 • bibigoogles

I recently wrote a [[http://blog.twg.ca/2009/09/4-fears-of-font-face/|blog post]] about some of my concerns about the upcoming widespread use of @font-face. I’m really excited about the design possibilities but am also worried about bad typography uglying up the internet…

Please view the post here: [[http://blog.twg.ca/2009/09/4-fears-of-font-face/]]

I would definitely love comments and to hear additional fears from typophiles!

“Hopefully @font-face will make the internet a more beautiful place, and not like a bunch of designers got drunk on the punch and barfed fonts everywhere."

Also, I’m curious if typophile.com plans on switching to @font-face once the copyright and browser compatibility issues are resolved?
