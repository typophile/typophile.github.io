2008-10-30 19:54:51 • Desi

I was trying to avoid recommending a specific font for this project, since I have never been very fond of most mid-20th Century casual scripts. I thought it might be best to let others who enjoy those fonts make suggestions, but having now seen more of your proposed design, I want to remind you of what you said in your original post:

“…I don’t want the font to feel dated - I want it to come off elegant and high-style.”

I do feel that all of the fonts you’ve been considering come across as being dated to some extent – they’re too clearly late 50’s-early 60’s in feeling.

Would you consider a slightly more formal font such as [[http://www.linotype.com/62286/itcisadoraroman-font.html|ITC Isadora Std Roman]]? To my eye this font meets your stated criteria and also would blend well with your pattern and the Helvetica Neue.
