PMN Caecilia is my studio's house font and I've found that it survives the horrors of the fax machine quite well. you might also consider anything created for the rigors of news print (try Unger's site or Garcia Media). just stay away from fine lines and cramped shapes – you're ahead of the rest of the pack just for thinking of it.
