There are a bunch of Clarendons – it’s as much a style/classification as it is the name of a specific font. In addition to the many typefaces with Clarendon in their name, you might consider these, which follow the same model with varying degrees of fidelity:

Sutro http://new.myfonts.com/fonts/parkinson/sutro/
