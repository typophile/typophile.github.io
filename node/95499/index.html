2012-08-11 14:56:48 • EBDesign
The numbers you’ve listed refer to the series number (128) and the point size (48). Those are Giant mats (as opposed to Composition or Display mats), so you need to track down a listing of Monotype Series numbers made in Giant mats and that will tell you what you’ve got.
