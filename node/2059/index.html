Thoughts on ordering CS

November 12, 2003 • Dan Weaver

I ordered the CS series directly from Adobe, mainly because I've always bought directly from them. This time they messed up. When I ordered, I never mentioned I needed the Mac version and the sales person never asked, so (you guessed) they sent me Windows versions I ordered individual upgrades so each one came separately on different days. I ended having to send them back to Adobe and that was a chore. I had to get a RMA (Return Merchandise Authorization) that was promised to me in 48 hours, well 6 days later I had to spend another 1/2 hours on the phone to get the e-mail with the RMA. Life is good I got three of the four upgrades, wonderful, until today. Instead of sending the Illustrator upgrade, they sent me another InDesign upgrade. The problem will be resolved by tomorrow when I fax the Letter of Software Destruction and my Illustrator is being shipped. The customer support people are great, but I have to ask: Would I have had these problems if I had ordered from lets say Mac Warehouse? Dan
