What be this large type? It’s from Triboro who has been known to make their own fonts, but this isn’t at all like their others. Definitely in the Kis/Janson realm, but not Hoefler Titling.

The other type is Chronicle.

Quoted from [[http://www.septemberindustry.co.uk/triboro-design-september-industry/|September Industry]]

AI WEIWEI — CIRCLE OF ANIMALS Book on the artist Ai Weiwei. Typefaces Used: Miller, Gotham and Custom
As I wasn't able to find any Miller ([[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/fontbureau/miller-banner/|Banner]] or [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/fontbureau/miller/|Display]]) on these samples, I guess we could considerer it's the custom typeface they're talking about and, as you already suggested, very close to (or even based on) Chronicle...
