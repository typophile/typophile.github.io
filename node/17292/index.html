For the entire first chapter, only every other set of pages (one side of each piece of paper) is printed. Oddly enough, you’d expect the same near the end of the book, but it doesn’t seem to be the case. That's because the book is comprised of a series of 32-page signatures. Look at the top or bottom of the book, near the spine, and you will see the signatures and how they fold. Or, open up the book at pages 16-17 (or 48-49) and look at the spread's gutter: you will see the sewing that keeps those 32 pages together. (I have a copy of version 2.4 here, and it is made up of 11 signatures.)
