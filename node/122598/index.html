Can’t identify it but it doesn’t mean no one else will find it out or it’s not a font. This kind of lettering is actually a trend, probably coming from the wedding invitation business. Some similars as alternatives: [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/emily-lime/bombshell-pro|Bombshell]], [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/fontforecast/salt-spices-pro|Salt & Spices]], [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/fontforecast/chameleon|Chameleon]] Pen Italic, [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/eurotypo/tansy|Tansy]], [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/eurotypo/aleka|Aleka]], [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/magpiepaperworks/ondise|Ondise]], [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/magpiepaperworks/dasha|Dasha]], [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/great-lakes-lettering/asterism|Asterism]], [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/great-lakes-lettering/kailey|Kailey]], [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/great-lakes-lettering/feast|Feast]], [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/trial-by-cupcakes/anna-clara|Anna Clara]], [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/trial-by-cupcakes/katie-rose|Katie Rose]]… I suggest not stopping to each individual font but having also a look to their corresponding foundries: [[http://www.myfonts.com/foundry/Magpie_Paper_Works|Magpie Paper Works]], [[http://www.myfonts.com/foundry/Emily_Lime|Emily Lime]], [[http://www.myfonts.com/foundry/Debi_Sementelli_Type_Foundry|Debi Sementelli]], [[http://www.myfonts.com/foundry/Great_Lakes_Lettering|Great Lakes Lettering]], [[http://www.myfonts.com/foundry/Eurotypo|Eurotypo]] and [[http://www.myfonts.com/foundry/Trial_by_Cupcakes/|Trial by Cupcakes]].
