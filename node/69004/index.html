SIAS: Two new Andron fonts now at MyFonts

April 1, 2010 • Andreas Stötzner

Two new specialised versions of Andron Corpus are now listed at MyFonts: [[http://new.myfonts.com/fonts/andreas-stotzner/andron-2/|Andron 2 English and Andron 2 Deutsch]].

Based on the normal Latin alphabet, these two fonts feature certain charactersitics inspired by Frakur type, yet without actually applying the aspect of broken strokes. As a result, the fonts offer an interesting alternative e.g. for literature typesetting, when a kind of traditional appeal is wanted but not any ‘blackletter look’.

Both fonts contain comprehensive ligature sets and other fancies. .

Looks very well done.
