2011-08-16 20:47:47 • Andreas Stötzner
I like old signs in raised lettering (cast or stamped iron). But then, I prefer letterpress to offset lithography. I prefer all caps, because I know that U&lc are trying to control me efficiently and quickly; that may be OK in an airport, but I ain’t always in a rush. Also, I consider that a few words are more like a title, so capitals look nicer and more appropriate. Perhaps this is because any individual sign in a system of U&lc may have peculiar word shapes, so the layout of individual signs will be neater with all caps. Slow down and touch the typography.
