[CRITIQUE]: Too blasphemous for print?

September 19, 2007 • paul.w

So I've been commissioned to do an ad for a local restaurant promoting their good-old Northern-Irish Sunday Lunch menu - the proprietor wants a quirky message which will entice the customer to dine in their place instead of opting to do it at home, hence the tagline. I'm trying to convey the idea that Sunday is indeed a day of rest, and they should let the business do the cooking for them, but I'm unsure whether it could be conveyed as blasphemous as it's bastardising the Bible.. kinda. Anyway I'd like your oppinions - note - this is NOT anywhere near the final design, rather just an initial concept. http://img210.imageshack.us/img210/1773/picture2dh4.png Regards, Paul
