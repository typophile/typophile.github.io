(x) Seattle Space Needle type cut in vinyl - Gill Sans {Mark S}

March 31, 2006 • Miguel Sousa

On my visit to Seattle's Space Needle I found a rather interesting sign. It said, Please wait on the other side of the glass partition. Obviously, it was not the message that called my attention, but the typeface it was set on. Well, actually I should more accurately say, what struck me was how the (well known) typeface was rendered. The letters, cut in vinyl, were so badly* done that I wouldn't been able to identify the typeface, if some of the shapes weren't so ubiquitous. So, here's the deal: I'll post one letter at a time, and you type-freaks (grin) try to guess the typeface.
