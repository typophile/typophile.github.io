Please help determine the future: PTL Vielzweck or FF Max

February 17, 2010 • kaplan

I’m trying to decide, in order to make a recommendation, between 2 typefaces my client has picked as favorites. The client is a pretty hip industrial design firm that designs and builds department store displays. The project is an updated website, I’m the developer (with a little design in my background). I’m assisting in the visual design of the site and we’re doing a bit of design back and forth. So, there’s 2 faces that my client really likes, one option is PTL Vielzweck, the other is FF Max.

I think I’m torn between what I like personally and what might suit them. I feel FF Max has a dated feel already and wouldn’t work that great in small sizes on screen. I really like DIN, so I’m digging Vielzweck, which I think would really stand the test of time and taste, but maybe it’s too plain and lacks ‘style’. I guess I should mention when my client did the initial layout he used screen grabs of FF Max, so he might be leaning that way.

What do you think, should I let my own taste determine? Is this the type of thing it’s fair to ask of a Design forum here at Typophile?

Thanks. Kind regards, Dave

Well you hardly mentioned anything about the nature of your client so you’re really just deferring to our personal taste, which is even less relevant than your own. Use the font that says what it needs to say.

That said, I think Vielzweck in a medium weight has a structural feel that would suit an industrial design firm and is also hip looking.

Or just use DIN. No designer will ever reject DIN Schriften.
