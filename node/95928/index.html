If I clearly remember, this is an exclusive typeface made by Terminal Design for Vogue and called “Vogue Avant-Garde”. It mixes [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/itc/avant-garde-gothic/|Avant-Garde]] and influences from (light weights of) [[http://www.myfonts.com/search/futura|Futura]] (pointy /A, /N, /W…). [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/ortizlopez/olround-gothic/|OL Round Gothic]] is close too (as Century Gothic you’ve already found). I like [[http://commercialtype.com/typefaces/graphik|Graphik]] and [[http://commercialtype.com/typefaces/platform|Platform]] even if I assume they can’t be considered as similars.
