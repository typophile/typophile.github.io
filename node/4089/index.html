When I think "beautiful and striking," PsyOps comes to mind. (Of course, one can't leave out Fountain's beautiful and striking Pussy as a possibility, nor Mark Simonson's equally beautiful but more demure Coquette for something, uh, coquettish.)

But more generally, you can go to MyFonts and do keyword searches such as "elegant," or you can go to FontScape, which groups typefaces in various categories. Hope that helps.
