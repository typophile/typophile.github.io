Long short, Marlboro is no longer allowed to put their logo on the F1 car they used to put it on.

Instead, they have what looks like a bar code, and I suspect it to be a subliminal add in which the “bar code stripes” correspond to the vertical strokes of the wordmark. When I saw this for the first time, approximately 2 minutes ago, my first instinct was “is that some sort of font/wordmark?” With Phillip Morris footing the bill I’m positive they have a team of psychologists on this job.

I am currently trying to find a good shot of this bar code, yet I have also come across certain variations. Lhe link at the top of the page is the one I just saw. It also appears on the backs of the pit crew’s shirts &c.

Can anyone help crack this code?
