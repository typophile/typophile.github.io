2007-06-01 18:22:06 • Sharon Van Lieu

“That’s what i’m trying to understand Darrell :) I need to know the pros and cons.”

Well, it depends on what you’re going to do with it.

If you’re comparing hardware, then you need to realize that it’s not a PC vs. Mac issue. It’s an Apple vs. Dell vs. HP. vs. Sony vs. Lenovo vs. Gateway vs. etc vs. etc vs. etc

And then you have to compare the multitude of models available by each company.

In terms of the operating system, Windows and OSX and Linux are about 90% the same. They all do the basic same things, albeit in slightly different ways.

In terms of software availability, again, 90% of the tools an average graphic designer would use are available on either Windows or OSX platforms (or there’s an equivalent for either one).

In the end, if you are truly looking for a comparison, then you need to tell us your requirements for a machine. Based on that, then we can give you specific pros and cons.
