body: "<strong>[[Indices]] : [[Designers]] : David Thometz</strong>\r\n\r\nDavid Thometz
  was born in Everett, [[Washington]], USA, in 1966.\r\n\r\nHe moved to South Jordan,
  [[Utah]], [[United States|USA]], in 1979, and studied Graphic Design and Illustration
  in the late 1980s and early 1990s at the [[University of Utah]] in [[Salt Lake City]],
  Utah, USA. He served as the Communication Specialist in Graphic Design at the [[Westminster
  College]] Communication Office in Salt Lake City from 1996\u20131998, and has run
  his own graphic design studio, David Thometz Design, since 1996.\r\n\r\nIn April
  of 2004, he moved his office to Hampton, [[Tennessee]], USA, in the Tri-Cities area
  of the Northeastern Tennessee Appalachian Mountains, where he now lives with his
  partner, Bryan, their three cats, three dogs and hundreds of wild birds which visit
  their feeders regularly.\r\n\r\nAmong his typeface designs are the DTD Hefeweizen
  family, the DTD Silvertone Woodtype family and DTD Seriatim Ornaments, all of which
  appeared on [[Typophile]].com and are now available throught MyFonts.com.\r\n\r\nFrom
  2004 through 2006, he designed the DTD Erwin Text family for the weekly newspaper,
  The Erwin Record, in Erwin, Tennessee. The DTD Erwin Text family is a large-bodied
  newspaper text face based on Cloister and Plantin."
