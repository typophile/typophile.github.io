I think these are all Helvetica derivatives (both Franklin and Arial have the diagonal cut on the upper terminal of the 'a', while Helvetica has the horizontal cut). "arr" started off as Helvetica Black, but it seems like the r's have been mucked with, or a version from some weird foundry was used.

'Laboratories, Inc.' is Helvetica Bold, and 'Making medicines work for everyone' looks like it might be Helvetica Neue 86 Heavy Italic, although it's hard to tell at this resolution.
