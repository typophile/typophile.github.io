[[Indices]] : [[Publications]] : Linotype Matrix

  1. Trade Newspaper produced (sometimes) four times per year by [[Linotype and Machinery]] in the UK from the 1930s through the early 1960s.

  2. Magazine produced by [[Linotype]]. Although released only “from time to time,” two issues are scheduled per year.

The first issue was released in Fall 2005. http://www.linotype.com/matrix

The second issue, focusing on the Life & Work of William Addison Dwiggins (amoung other things) was released in May 2006. http://www.linotype.com/2720/linotypematrixvol4no2.html

A third issue is planned for Fall 2006. Stay tuned!
