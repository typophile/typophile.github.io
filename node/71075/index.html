That BT means Bitstream, and as I understand it, Bitstream was allowed to rename and sell many of the early fonts that they digitized for other foundries (before other foundries had the tools, or know-how to do it themselves). URW had a similar agreement for fonts they digitized for other foundries with their Ikarus system.
