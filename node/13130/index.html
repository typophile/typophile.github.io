Hi there! Does anyone know about this one? It is 'similar' to the Dot-Matrix but is drawn like a real typeface, not as 'gridded' as the dot-matrix. It got used in the visual identity of the Olafur Eliasson installation at the Tate Modern. http://www.tate.org.uk/modern/exhibitions/eliasson/ Bests/ Gallo
