The Merrymount (not Merrymond) was Daniel Berkeley Updike's --of Printing Types undying fame-- company, founded in Boston in 1896 and apparently the most important enterprise aligned with the Arts & Crafts movement in the USA. This beautiful and dark Venetian style type design was commissioned from Goodhue when Updike decided to move away from historical printing in the William Morris vein. I'm unaware if there is a digital version.
