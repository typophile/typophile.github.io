2007-02-15 02:16:51 • Norbert Florendo

T.M. Cleland did a lovely promotion for a paper company in the 1920s, in which he illustrated six social occasions that centre on a paper and printing. All in the 17th century.

  1. The Enclosure – in a garden, a lady reads a love letter that her beau has just given her
  2. The Letter – in a busy office, the boss dictates a letter to his secretary
  3. The Book – in a busy market square, readers peruse a bookstall
  4. The Broadside
  5. The Calendar – in a print shop, the publisher confronts the printer, pointing at the calendar, his job is overdue
  6. The Menu – an aristocratic dinner party.
