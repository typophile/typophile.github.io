For more “broken sans” [Gebrochene Grotesk] alternates, do a Google search for “fonts Jackboot” – the English translation of the term “Schaftstiefel” used by socialist and communist printers to describe Grobe Deutschmeister and other Neugotische Schriften of the late 1920s and early 1930s like National and Element. Interesting wiki in German http://de.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gebrochene_Grotesk Don
