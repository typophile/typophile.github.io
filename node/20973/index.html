I think Yves is right. This looks one of the weights of Rodin from Fontworks, a Japanese company in Fukuoka.

[img:sites/default/files/old-images/fwrodanm_3847.gif]

The company site is www.fontworks.co.jp, though you need a Japanese enabled browser to view it. There is a PDF at www.fontworks.co.jp/pub/typeface/rodin_m.pdf

BTW a good place to look for Japanese fonts is at ohkadesign.cool.ne.jp/wabunfont/library.html Again a Japanese enabled browser will help.
