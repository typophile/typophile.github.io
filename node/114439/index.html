Logo lettering with no close match in mini serif fonts. Agenda URW has some similarities, but has significant differences, notably that it is a demi-serif, i.e. serifs on only one side of the base of the vertical strokes, and none on the R leg. One possibility is to compress a sans with a moderately thick and thin character and a similar S and R, square it somewhat and tack on mini serifs. For example FF Page Sans Bold https://www.fontfont.com/fonts/page-sans/bold?aid=4 Don
