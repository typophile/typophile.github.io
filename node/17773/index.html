Art Deco serif suggestions

February 8, 2006 • adnix

I am working on a packaging project designing sleeves that slip over boxes of chocolate (sort of like a slipcase). I am looking for Art Deco-ish typefaces to go with the existing packaging, which although it strictly isn’t Art Deco, has a very 1920s feel to it. I work for a retailer that carries the brand and we are splitting the cost of the sleeves 50/50.

I’ve done a few searches, and I came across Goldenbook by Mark Simonson which I’ll probably end up getting, but nothing else has really caught my eye. I want something that is elegant and feels luxurious, but isn’t too avant garde.

In their latest printed promo, House Industries showed Paperback used on packaging and it looked very lovely. Used correctly it could feel the right way. I think.

Have you been down the road of sans deco types like Anisette, Bernhard Fashion, ITC Vintage and Arwen?
