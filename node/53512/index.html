I’m curious…does anyone know if there’s actually a difference between Font Bureau’s Nobel and Dutch Type Library’s Nobel?

I gather they’re similar but not the same… maybe someone can enlighten me on the differences? (if any?)

There are many subtle differences, the most obvious being that DTL’s A, M, N, V have pointed apexes throughout the weights. I prefer this, but it does make the design more mannered and less neutral.
