Critique my list of designers and practice areas

August 21, 2011 • blank

I am requiring all of my introduction to graphic design students to research a designer and his/her work within a specific practice area of design, write a 1,000-word essay about the designer, and develop the essay into a presentation. The designers I chose were picked primarily because it would be easy for students to research in a library as opposed to online. I ended up with a list that is mostly white Americans and I would like to add some diversity.

Also, I still have not come up with a great interface designer for my list. Suggestions there would be especially helpful.

Following is my list of designers and practice ares. Please suggest alternate designers and any practice areas that I have neglected. The most important criteria is that the designer has been written about in commonly-available, English language, books and magazines. This is not a research and writing class, so I do not have time to guide students through interviews, translations, locating limited edition books, etc..

Kenya Hara, art direction Roger Black, publication design Alejandro Paul, type design Kyle Cooper, film title design Jason Santa Maria, web design Debbie Millman, identity and branding Matteo Bologna, packaging Jessica Hische, lettering Jonathan Ives, product design Paula Scher, environmental design ?, interface design Nicholas Feltron, information design
