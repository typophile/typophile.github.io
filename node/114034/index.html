As for what to call the style, the inward curving vertical strokes are a feature of the mid Victorian fonts called Tuscan [Gothic] Concave. The font for Home Brew Good God is a later style that mixes this with features from other styles such as the extended flat bottoms & tops on D and B first found in a font patented by James Connor in 1878 Don
