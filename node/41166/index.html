2008-01-23 02:38:13 • William Berkson

One of the most versatile sans serif types is Meta Plus. I use it frequently.

Despite your dislike of Helvetica, consider Helvetica Neue. It comes in six weights, in condensed, regular and expanded widths. If you have to set type in different sizes, and have to balance the weight of the type, this variety is extremely useful.

Interstate is also a good choice, though a little cold.
