Erik Spiekermann has the same complaint about Adobe’s version. You want the Heavy from Elsner+Flake or Bitstream, though E+F’s Futura is more geometrically round overall. Neufville’s Futura ND (likely different from yours) could be considered the most direct digitization as they own Bauer and the original drawings. There is no “Heavy” weight, but the Demibold is close.
