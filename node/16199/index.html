I like the Mercano Empire Regular, but for some reason I keep wishing the 2 to be “fatter” instead of “taller”, though only slightly. Maybe I can play around with it in Photoshop and see what I get. Or if you have another suggestion that is similar to that but “fatter”, please let me know.
