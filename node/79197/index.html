Is Ghostscript font an open source font?
2011-02-20 21:35:45 • kevintheophile

The Ghostscript fonts are open-source, I think, either GPL or AFPL. The AFPL does not allow commercial distribution, so as long as you don’t sell the fonts, be it separate or bundled with other products/services, you should be in the clear.

Mind, you can use them to print books or magazines and sell them, no prob. Hacking on them is OK, too. The Arkandis Digital Foundry has produced a style kit for Classico, Garamond #8 and Palladio that gives you small caps and OS numbers. With this kit, you are getting close to semi-pro.
