Sure it’s dated, like a Lambo. So you gotta ask, how will you make 1960s high-tech go-faster styling work for a social justice non-profit? With some retro styling, perhaps?–try negative tracking the hell out of it, or at the very least, “tight but not touching”. Because when Eras isn’t set ultra tight, it’s just not right. So to go with that tight set, choose a small x-height text face and set it solid in narrow columns rag right. Choose from any contemporary ITC face–light weights of ITC Souvenir or ITC Garamond would be cool. My Pratt Pro would work that look too.
