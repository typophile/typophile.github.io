ATypI 2007 conference venue announced

The Board of ATypI (Association Typographique Internationale) is pleased to announce that its next annual conference will be held in Brighton, United Kingdom, in September of 2007. Final dates will be announced shortly.

Posted on 16 November 2006 by Mark Barratt, Reading, UK

The conference will be held at the Faculty of Arts and Architecture at the University of Brighton, in the center of this charming city. A newly remodelled theatre and gathering spaces will be an added enhancement by next year. Brighton is easily accessible from London and its international airports, and offers a wide variety of accommodations.
