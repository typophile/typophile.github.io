Borobet - a humble pixel font

April 29, 2009 • Boros

This is my first try in making type. As it seemed easiest, I chose to make a pixelfont on a grid. I haven't drawn numerals yet, but I'm thinking it would be good to have old style figures as well. Being a large pixel font, I think it should be no problem. What do you think? [img:sites/default/files/old-images/Borobet_6191.gif]
It being a large pixel typeface, it is nice and readable, and has some nice serifs to it. Because of the density of lines at the bottom of the lowercase A, the lowercase A looks a different weight to the rest of the typeface.
