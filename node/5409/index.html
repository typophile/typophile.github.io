I'm struggling to find some well-designed (Latin) typefaces that have either an Asian flavor or are Asian-influenced. Specifically typefaces that would reflect the sophiscation and complexity of the Asian culture. My searches so far have yielded only a handful of "take-out" faces – Sum Dum Goi, Chow Fun, Won Ton, Katana or AGFA Miyama, etc.; as well as techno-flavored T26's Fung Foo and CS Takahashi. None of these seem to reflect the elegance or intelligence that I'm looking for. I vaguely remembered a study of Yakuza by Jared Benson here somewhere. Although I don't believe it's out for distribution yet.
There's always the splendid (but overused) Present. Compliment has a similar asian calligraphic brush look to it also. And there's the quirky Chineze LT.
