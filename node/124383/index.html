i’ve tried wtf and identifont as i always do before asking around here. they haven’t been conclusive even though below fonts don’t seem to be very exotic; part of the problem might be that the specimen derive from texts imprint, thus figures are somewhat imprecise.

ad 1.: IV might just be an extended version of font II with I it is obviously th R and the K that stand out

ad 2.: 2 and 1 III. / 3. are similar but not the same, if one looks closely. (i am somewhat amazed that during an era of fierce patent battles corporate branding seems not to have been a priority)

ad 4.: II. has some very interesting Bs and Rs

ad 5.: the A certainly hints to a condensed cheltenham; the R, the 3, the a and the iin the shape of a minoan pillar are way off however

may i say: good hunting?! :)
