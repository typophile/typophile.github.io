Almost exactly the same as Futura in all aspects, but with a straightened capital "M", very similar to Avenir's. Capital "T" has as narrow orizontal bar as Futura, and lower case "a" is round as it is in Futura, without Avenir's curly shape on top. [img:sites/default/files/old-images/example-01_6058.jpg]
