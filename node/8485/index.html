Oops, just noticed that the Font-a-licious link doesn't go directly to the page with the font on it (reason: frames).

To get to it: Starting from the fonts page, click on the icon next to the "FONTS" label; this will take you to a yellow page with some font names; click on the ">>" below the samples four times to get to the page showing Dimitri Swank; click on the name to get to the page where you can download the font.
