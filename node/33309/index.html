I would also look at the Nouvelle Vague movie posters, because there's was an important shift in the esthetics from the style of the previously mentioned posters to the new wave realism, end of 50's, beginning of 60's. There's a lot of material on the site, but you should maybe search for Truffaut, Godard, Alain Resnais or Agnes Varda for the most iconic posters. When I think of French movie poster layouts of the time, I think of something like the Paris Match example Nick posted, with a similar typeface and the one-sided layout.
