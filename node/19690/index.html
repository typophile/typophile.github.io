2006-05-03 15:05:48 • timd
I've been using lots of Akkurat for the last year, since it had already been specified as the primary type for a client of mine. I actually think it's a lovely design, with more personality than Helvetica. It has a certain crisp, clean feel to it, but doesn't feel nearly as cold and precise as Helvetica, IMHO. It works pretty well as text in short bursts, although I find it the regular weight a little heavy for most text uses. The light weight works well, and has nice contrast when mixed with the bold. The spacing overall feels pretty good, although I tend to tighten it up a little bit for text use. For the moment I still have a couple of sample postcards you can look at for an idea of how it works as text versus display: http://transfer.ultrasparky.org/ps122/postcards/Danceoff_postcard.pdf http://transfer.ultrasparky.org/ps122/postcards/Hell_postcard.pdf
