Designer identity logo

June 7, 2009 • Miriam Martincic

Hi, everyone: I’m trained as a fine artist in drawing, sculpture, and painting, and my husband is trained as a political theorist, researcher, and writer. We’ve decided to begin freelance design work together, and we’re making a logo to use on our website, business cards, and stationery. Both our initials are MM, and we chose to call ourselves M234. Some of the qualities we want the design to capture that reflect our work: —no frills, sometimes complex but never cluttered —a bit playful but restrained —strong abstract components —we like design solutions that are simple and direct but also engaging —we like discovered shapes and relationships, for example between negative and positive space, line intersections, tangencies, etc. The characters in the logo are drawn from scratch but based on Neutra (we’ll probably license the face). We’ve gone through many variations of our central idea, and we’ve narrowed ourselves to these four. We’d appreciate any feedback. [img:sites/default/files/old-images/m234_logos_4550.gif]
