TC07: TypeCon is underway!

August 1, 2007 • Si_Daniels

Just got back from the hotel, where I dropped off six participants from the first TypeCon workshop. The workshop, on Math fonts, took place on the Microsoft campus. In true TypeCon tradition we over-ran by an hour, but on the plus side the adjoining cafe was closing just as we came out, so we all got free BBQ!

The weather is beautiful and the mountain is out - made the commute over the 520 bridge more than bearable.

We also just finished the first day of volunteering. We stuffed the goody bags full to the brim with goodies like type specimens of Arno Pro and Beorcana. And then we stuffed them again, all 500+ bags, when we found an entire extra pallete of goodies we had missed.

Lots of free swag. You’ll enjoy most of it.

People are arriving every minute now, and you’re bound to run into somebody in the lobby if you hang around enough.
