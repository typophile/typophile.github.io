Initial cap or drop cap don't convey the 'colorful or stylish background', since these may be plain.

A rubric is properly a piece of text, traditionally coloured red (hence the name), indicating appropriate movement or gesture during liturgy, e.g. 'The priest crosses himself with the paten'.
