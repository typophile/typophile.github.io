body: "<strong>[[Indices]]: [[Designers]]: Neville Brody</strong>\r\n\r\n<strong>Neville
  Brody</strong> (b. 1957) is a London born graphic designer. Studied at the [[London
  College of Printing]]. Primarily known for his work as Art Director on <em>The Face</em>
  magazine and for his studio's work on the late 1990s [[Macromedia]] identity. In
  1990 he and [[Erik Spiekermann]] started [[FontFont]] and <em>[[Fuse]]</em>. Currently
  he operates his [[London]] studio, Research Associates.\r\n\r\nSome of his early
  design work was for British bands. Fetish, Hanibal, 23 Skiddo, and Cabaret Voltaire
  all have album covers designed by Brody.\r\n\r\nSee a collection of his designs
  for the Dutch postal service [[http://www.katranpress.com/stamps_brody_1_1.html|here]].\r\n"
