2005-07-30 21:52:01 • edwh

A lot of very nice work. I like the ‘lechem eretz’, which retains its readability even with the elegant swashy design.

The ‘Chinese’ sign looks fake and awkward because the stuff in the center violates the rules of how Chinese characters have to be formed.

Your effort at reversal of weight for Hebrew characters in Slorca doesn’t work for me. But my Hebrew is poor and I am not used to reading it, so I am probably not a good judge. To me the Hebrew font ‘Erica’ does pull off such a reversal (but only a slight one) very well, if I remember rightly. If you were doing a cross of horizontal and vertical strokes in a Roman cap, you would if anything thin the horizontal near the intersection, as Albertus does. Here thickening the horizontal looks awkward to me. But as I say, I am not looking with eyes thoroughly accustomed to Hebrew, so I may be off in this comment.
