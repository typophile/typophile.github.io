Of we go high in the sky into the deep blue yonder with these wide speedy air force letters: [img:sites/default/files/old-images/PompeiEditedslant15DegL80PctH_4828.jpg] You won’t get this out of a box because, in my opinion, a font probably does not exist in this form. It’s probably a manually sloped and squooshed Frankenfont. I started with Albert Boton’s 1996 design called Pompei http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/mti/pompei/ – partly because of the similarity in a – sloped the letters 15 degrees right, cut a gap in R and substituted the Q tail from Baker Signet. Further editing could pretty well duplicate your image. BTW if this looks heavy, there is also a light weight version of Pompei. Don
