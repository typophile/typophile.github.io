Glyphr Beta 2 released

October 24, 2012 • mattlag

Well, after more than a year of casual coding, I’ve released Glyphr Studio Beta 2 out into the wild!

It’s incredibly young and crude, but I think the idea of an HTML5-based font design tool is very exciting. I encourage you to take a look at it over at glyphrstudio.com, I’m trying to get the word out because i’d love real font designers to try it out, and give me feedback. Pretty soon i’ll start working on Beta 3, and your feedback could guide what I do next!

And a big thank you to everyone who has already looked through it and given me feedback!

Stay tuned for more info.

::Matt
