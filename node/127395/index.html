Hand lettered alphabets were typically creatures unto themselves, and were not directly based on fonts used for printing.

Letterhead Fonts has an all caps face called Thick and Thin Sans that is very similar to the capital letters in your sample: http://www.letterheadfonts.com/fonts/thickandthin
