hi mike,

I don't know if this will help you, but I've got a keyboard from apple for the G4. (it'a a little compact keyboard) The font what's been used for the typography of this keyboard has a great resemblance of the 'Univers Condensed Italic Light', according Berthold entitled no '48' (you know the Univers got a system of numbers)

The difference is that the typeface of the keyboard is slightly less slanted than the original Univers. The numbers and capitals are almost exactly the same, the $, § also. The & is different, but I think apple made a few corrections to the typeface. It's probably used exclusively by Apple.

But I don't know of your keyboard-type looks like the desktop-keyboard from the G4, but there might be a great change.

Otherwise, when I'm wrong, please try to upload a picture from the keys
