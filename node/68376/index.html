The contrast is most like [[http://new.myfonts.com/fonts/linotype/serpentine/|Serpentine]], but the G is quite different and the serifs on your sample are smaller. The only other approximately similar style was [[http://new.myfonts.com/fonts/urw/agenda/|URW Agenda]], but the contrast is less and the serifs are still too big. Almost anything else I saw that may have had similar serifs was much more monotone in stroke weight (like [[http://new.myfonts.com/fonts/urw/bluejack/|URW Bluejack]]).

If I were trying to reproduce this, I think I would try to customize the heavy weight of Agenda by playing with outlines to change the contrast.
