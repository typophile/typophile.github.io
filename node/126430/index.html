I couldn't find any forum that was dedicated to user introductions, so I figure a forum with the word "general" in its name would be a good place to start. Having said that... My name is John Isles. I'm a lifelong resident of Lancaster, California, a city you've likely never heard of, that situated in the Mojave Desert roughly 75 miles (or 120 kilometers) north of Los Angeles. That means if the rumors of Los Angeles traffic are consistently true, that's about a five-hour drive on a good day. I've always been fascinated by typography. In my younger days, I would often look around and say to myself, "Hey, the font on that speedometer is the same one as the one in that Mario instruction booklet", despite not know what the font's name was. In recent years, I've been looking into getting into selling my own typefaces, and I join Typophile so I can help educate myself on the finer points of type design through community discussion. By the way, I do have a page on Fontstruct, though most of what I have published is recreations of printer, computer, and video game fonts. (P.S.: If I have happened to place this in an incorrect forum, my apologies.)
