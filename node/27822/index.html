Right on Patty. Ellington is one of my favorite fonts, as is the sans companion font - Strayhorn. The designer, Michael Harvey is a jazz fan -- Billy Strayhorn was Duke Ellington's friend, and arranger on many of his famous pieces.There is also the display face Braff, named for Ruby Braff, a top cornet & trumpet player who had recorded with Ellington, among many others. End of typographical jazz lecture. :) - Mike Yanega
