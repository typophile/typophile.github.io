Just checked my library and rechecked it, because I could not find a specific book… But what I noticed is that I have a lot of translations of British manuals. Let me postulate that Dutch typographic conventions are heavily based on the British. Where small differences occur they -- in some cases -- can be traced back to NEN (our equivalent to DIN in Germany), eg the way postal codes are handled and addresses in general. Let the discussion begin!
