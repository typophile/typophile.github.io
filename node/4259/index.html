(x) "Latin" script font from "13 Ghosts" - Ex Ponto (backslanted) {Yves}

April 13, 2004 • darjeeling

In the movie "13 Ghosts" there is a "Latin" handwritten style font used through out the set on the glass. (It's the only redeeming quality of this movie.) I'd love to find something as close to it as possible. The production notes about the set only gave me the following clues: "The Latin “etchings” were actually rendered on a plastic overlay and adhered to the glass walls to achieve Hargreaves’ “Arcanum” design motif. " Thanks for your time. Dar
We're looking at a backslanted Ex Ponto Pro. The basic character shapes match perfectly, and there's more than enough alternates in the Pro version to account for the more unusual glyphs.
