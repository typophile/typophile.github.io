  body: "Eric Gill's work; he started as a stone cutter. Most of his typefaces derive
    from the alphabets he learned from stonecarving teachers. \r\n\r\nPerpetua, Joanna,
    Aries, Golden Cockerel\r\n\r\nMichael Harvey also has done a lot of carved lettering,
    as well as a bunch of very nice lapidary typefaces. One of Michael's other major
    influences is his prolific work as a letterer for book covers, so his typefaces
    reflect this as much as they reflect his stonecarving work. \r\n\r\nStrayhorn,
    Mezz, Ellington, Andreas"
