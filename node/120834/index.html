There is a common misconception that logotypes are derived directly from typefaces. Many are custom-drawn by lettering artists, others are only loosely based on one—or more—typefaces. This is particularly true in the realm of consumer packaged goods.

Perhaps someone else will see a similarity to another typeface, but there may not be a match.
