The type in the photo appears to be iron-on lettering from a craft store. Hobby Lobby sells an almost identical version although they didn’t show it in white. It may have originated as a font or may not have. It has some unusual characteristics of certain letters which don’t match any blackletter font in Fraktur mon Amour, a comprehensive specimen book of blackletter and similar designs.
