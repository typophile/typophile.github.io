Calacalacity - Invitation

November 8, 2006 • gberry

This post is an invitation to take part in Calacalacity.

Calacalacity is an installation and a website project that take place in Lille, France. As part of “Bombaysers de Lille”, a cultural event dealing with indian arts, we were asked to realise a project hosted at “La Maison de l’Architecture et de la Ville”.

The project is composed of a fresco and the calacalacity.fr website.

The fresco aims to be the towncenter of this imaginery indian city. Visitors have the opportunity to create and develop it through a flash interface and to see their creations placed on the map.

It is indirectly link to typography as you may see in the image attached.

Please feel free to have a look around and to participate.

http://www.calacalacity.fr

Best regards.

The télescopique crew
