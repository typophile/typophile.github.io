This particular style of ornamentation dates from the 1870s or 1880s, during which time most American foundries simply numbered their ornamented faces, rather than naming them. There were hundreds of such faces and, assuming that whoever made this flag actually copied an existing typeface rather that just concocting this particular one (always a possibility in a non-print project), your chances of finding an exact match may be slim and/or none...
