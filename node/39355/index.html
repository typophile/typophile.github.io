You need to get a font creation program (Fontographer and FontLab are two, but there are others) and bring your Illustrator drawings into it. Check out the How-To section of the TypoWiki for more info on the practical aspects. Check out the Books section to find literature on the subject of designing or creating a typeface. Also, a Google search will probably get you some online tutorials on the basics.
