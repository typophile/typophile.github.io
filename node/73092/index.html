After looking at everything I could find that was calligraphic I think this looks more like Arthur Baker's work than anyone else I could see. However, I could not find an exact match anywhere at MyFonts. Pretty close were P22 Matador, New Amigo, Duckweed and Oakgraphic (all at MyFonts, I got lazy making links). The only pretty close non-Baker font I found was Waters Titling, especially the condensed version. If Bert's right about being able to do this with autotracing, then I would say Waters Titling might be my candidate, but in terms of actual fonts, Baker's work seems much closer in most stylistic details. It would be easy to believe this was a new Baker font. - Mike Yanega
