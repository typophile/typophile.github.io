2014-11-18 01:25:26 • eeblet
Printed in 1961 in the UK, it was probably set in Monotype, and the decoration is most likely a Monotype border. Monoytpe printed [[http://wolfsonianfiulibrary.files.wordpress.com/2011/07/87-1273-19-1_000b.jpg|catalogues of their borders]], and you can research to see if you can find one that matches your friend's book. But since the border seems to be a fairly simple herringbone pattern, it might be easier to take a good scan and trace it in Illustrator.
