[[Indices]] : [[Typefaces]] : Pill Gothic

Geometric [[sans serif]] by [[Christian Robertson]], released in 2004 in [[Veer]]’s Umbrella collection. The font contains both [[OpenType]] and [[AAT]] features, including alternate characters, [[small caps]] and [[old style figures]].

Pill Gothic was one of the first fonts posted to the critique area of [[Typophile]].
