Yes, I think there are several customized fonts that are based on El Greco. The differences are in the initials, and mostly they add the same shapes to the top of the letters, like the A and M. “Caligrapher” may be closer to [[http://new.myfonts.com/fonts/berthold/el-greco-bq/|El Greco]] itself. Did you look at the one at TypOasis that I linked? I think it matches pretty well except for the P, which might have been borrowed from Poetica, or another calligraphic style font. Most of the letters in your sample were certainly based on El Greco, or one of its derivatives.
