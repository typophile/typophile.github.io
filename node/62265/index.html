September 22, 2009 • Silver

Hello, I need to create lettering for an old wooden boat, a Hoogaars from the 1930 the ARM-27. Trying to find a font that was used or was created on old ship lettering around the 1930. I don't really know if it even exist but i was curious if there is such a font available. Trying to google for such a font but no good match. Cheers
In 1930 a font was something made of metal (or wood) and intended for putting ink on paper. Any large lettering on an irregular surface (like a boat) would have been hand-painted. Try searching for Dutch signs from that period (shops, bars, restaurants, hotels, etc.) and you'll probably find the sort of lettering you would have seen on boats.
