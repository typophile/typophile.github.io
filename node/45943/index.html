Definitely custom. Most logotypes (logos made of words) are hand drawn. Note the two lowercase a’s are different styles, and the different tones (fonts are all black and white (or color and non-color). My thought is that you could reasonably duplicate the logotype by just scanning in a pencil/ink drawing.

For info on: the different font rendering/editing software, check out the typowiki entry here. the How-to section, check out the typowikii entry here. free typefaces that may be close to what you’re looking for, try here.

Making a typeface is no small task, it’s not really something you can just casually ask.
