[[Indices]] : [[Designers]] : David Lemon (1955?-)

After working at the San Francisco Examiner newspaper, David Lemon began working in the type group at [[Adobe Systems]] in 1989, reporting to [[Sumner Stone]] and working alongside [[Robert Slimbach]] and [[Carol Twombly]]. He soon became a manager, responsible for all new font production. In 2003 he became the manager for the entire type group.

David Lemon has one published typeface to his credit, Copal, an ornate display face which was inspired by Mayan carvings.
