Looking for a font for a wordmark

February 28, 2017 • DumbType

I had been browsing, several days, for a font and got lost. I wonder if you could make a couple of suggestions on suitable fonts for a wordmark conveying education, culture. It should look modern, active and slightly quirky. Thank you so much.
What type of education, and what type of culture? How you set and/or alter the type (wordmarks or logotypes are rarely straight settings of a typeface) will also influence how the mark “reads.” Perhaps start with one foundry, like Sudtipos: http://sudtipos.com/home They produce beautiful work in a range of styles; run your cursor over the typeface names to see samples (they have a typeface tester on the site, but not for all of their offerings; you can also find most of their catalog on MyFonts).
