As long as we’re swapping book tips - Berlow had mentioned a book on another thread called Empires Of The Word by Nicholas Ostler. Sub-titled “A Language History Of The World”. Terrific. It also intersects with Richard Dawson’s idea of memes - ideas and, I believe, possibly the languages that express them - that replicate from mind to mind and are therefore subject to a kind of natural selection.
