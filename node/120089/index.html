Yes, in 1984 some books were typed on an IBM Selectric typewriter, or a similar good quality typewriter from another source and the results were photographed for printing. Note the handwritten Greek phrase. We can check the design against some of the popular typefaces used on IBM "elements" -- the thing that looks like a golfball -- and see if it is digitized. They had names like _courier_, _prestige_, _elite_ etc. Here is a selection of these typefaces compiled by Luc Devroye http://luc.devroye.org/fonts-44934.html If you can find some of the distinctive letters in the book, like _Q_, _R_, _g_ and _3_ it makes it easier to decide on a match. Don

Here is a list of reasonably well engineered monospace typewriter fonts at Myfonts, that could perhaps be substituted. None is a proper match for Delegate. First, several fonts based on IBM'S Prestige for the Selectric Prestige Elite, several sellers including http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/linotype/prestige-elite/ Alix FB http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/fontbureau/alix-fb/ Prestige 12 Pitch, also based on IBM'S Prestige for the Selectric http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/bitstream/prestige-12-pitch/ Also-rans, in alphabetical order: Bulletin Typewriter http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/itfmecanorma/bulletin-typewriter/ EF Techno Script, apparently based on a Royal typewriter http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/itfmecanorma/bulletin-typewriter/ ITC American Typewriter, century old design http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/adobe/itc-american-typewriter/ Italian Typewriter, Ollivetti circa 1940, more stylish than most http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/flanker/italian-typewriter/ Remington Elite Typewriter http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/intellecta/remington-elite-typewriter/ Remington Typewriter, with well designed Italic by Frederic Goudy http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/lanston/ltc-remington-typewriter/ Smith Premier, an old ATF design, as Smith Premier NF http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/nicksfonts/smith-premier-nf/ Tired of Courier http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/ingrimayne/tiredofcourier/ Typewriter by Monotype, source unknown https://www.myfonts.com/fonts/mti/typewriter-mt/ Plus, perhaps of lesser quality, Intellecta Typewriter, old design http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/intellecta/intellecta-typewriter/ Don
