Typographic Excellence Made In Basel

January 23, 2009 • Oliver Wehn

Today I've nearly gone green with envy! My fellow students Julia Kaltenbach and Thomas Lehner were selected by the judges of the [[http://www.tdc.org|Type Directors Club]] to receive a «Certificate of Excellence in Typography» for their concept of the yearbook 2009/2010 which they did for the Theater Basel. Read more about it [[http://www.visualattitudes.com/blog/article/44-typographic-excellence-made-in-basel.html|here]].
