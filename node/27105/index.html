Whoops, this thread slipped by so fast I didn't see it, and
now I noticed nobody mentioned my favourite unicase
designs. Eidetic Neo, in itself a beautiful and very forward-
thinking serif face, has a great unicase weight Omni.

Also, both Emigre's Platelet and Democratica have capitals
that are x-height sized, so they lend themselves perfectly to
unicase setting.

And I know this is probably stretching it a bit thin, but
combining Mrs. Eaves lowercase with the Petite Capitals
- smaller than the Small Caps and also x-height sized -
allows one to set unicase as well.
