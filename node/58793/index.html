suitable type for a café

June 7, 2009 • alexandracama

I got some friends opening up a bar/café in Barcelona and they’ve asked me to make their identity design. The place will be called d505 which is the name of the street and number where it is. It’s going to be open from 8 to 6 and although it’s in a rather classy neighborhood almost all its clients would be office people that work nearby. They’ll basically serve breakfasts, coffee and some small dishes for a quick lunch. It would be more of a place to stop by for a while rather than sitting and eating for an hour.

I’d like the concept to be modern but not too hype as I think the target of the place is going to be kinf of conservative. I’ve started to sketch stuff using franklin gothic and a combination of dark grey and turquoise blue. If someone has a suggestion or a bar identity site I can browse through it would be very much appreciated.

Thanks a lot!
