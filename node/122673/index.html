|LITTLE MOON TWINS| appears to be a manually compressed setting of Bubblegum: http://www.dafont.com/bubblegum.font?text=LITTLEMOONTWINS

|SIMS| is most likely a custom wordmark. The closest typeface I could find is Opel Sans Condensed Extra Bold, but it is a custom typeface and I can’t find a commercial source for it: http://luc.devroye.org/fonts-54817.html

With a little editing, Triplex by Emigre might work: http://www.emigre.com/EF.php?fid=128
