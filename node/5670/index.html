A lot of interesting things in there (much to support, and counter), but I think the original topic was the most interesting.

I think "fauve" is a good term here. "Chunky" is not. Cheltenham is not fauve. Electra is.

The champions of fauve used to be the Czechs, now it's the South Americans. Down there it even seems to be becoming a full-blown Trend, with original thinkers but also bandwagon-hoppers.

It all feels very original and meaningful, but might just as well implode one day, in the same way that the Emigre/PoMo stuff did. I hope it doesn't though: it's the perfect antidote to the naive desire for total control of Modernism.
