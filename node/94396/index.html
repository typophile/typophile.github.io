Webadvanced

July 2, 2012 • Stefan Seifert

Once again the sensibility of Paolo Roversi inspires me drawing my letters. These photos are from an old Ferretti ad campaign (1998 I think). The characters are from my „Webadvanced“ font. A thicker but meant to be elegant character which I’d love to keep some elegance and liveliness even in the smaller sizes on monitor. What a hard way to go! I am listening to the beautiful semiclassical music of William Orbit.
I add a new image of the almost final version of this font. It has become still bolder to make it work in small monitor sizes. I love its character. The ‘a’ has particularly drawn my attention.[img:sites/default/files/old-images/Image_Text_a!_4504.png]
