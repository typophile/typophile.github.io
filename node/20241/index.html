Design Hangover

May 26, 2006 • Conor

How can design give you a hangover?

Simple. Somebody decides to leave a company you used to work for, they throw a party and invite you because you know them better than the bosses do. Throw the party on a Thursday night. Ex-bosses throw plenty of cash at the barman then make a swift exit leaving you and the remaining typoholic heros to down a few rounds of «Sambuca con mosca». The inevitable rush for the pool table raises spirits and adrenalin levels. Testosterone rampant as each male fights it out for chest-beating glory. You win. You lose the next morning as you sit in work, bleary-eyed with re-runs of Woody Woodpecker floating around in your alcobattered noggin.
