The completion of [[http://www.ccny.org/buildingtour|this church]] predates Herb Lubalin and Tom Carnase’s design for the Avant Garde font by some 20 years. A more likely inspiration for this lettering is Lucian Bernhard’s [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/spiecegraphics/bernhard-gothic-sg/|Bernhard Gothic]] (1929).
