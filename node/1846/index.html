Hellenic Wide isn't available in digital form, but there are a couple of alternatives. If your purpose isn't too serious, there's a free take on Hellenic Wide by Swede Per Ellstrom called HiFi Stereo. Find courtesy of Stephen CSA Images has a version they call Platon. Mike Yanega pointed us to another version called Hefty, which was in an old FontBank collection. The font is still sold for $2 at BuyFonts.com. Antique Central is a good alternative if you need a full family with italics.
