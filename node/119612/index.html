Indicate a Pregnant Pause?

September 16, 2014 • billy_

Something like: “Why did the chicken cross the road? […] To get to the other side."

A normal ellipsis doesn’t seem to convey enough and emphasizing it (using brackets here) looks a bit much. And it’s confusing, like the intent is to remove text. Is there a more eloquent way to represent this?

An ellipsis can be used to signify several things and a pause is one of them. A long pause could be indicated by your choice of words.

Don’t think most fiction has lots of pauses in it, but if you’re writing instructions (like how to tell a joke) you could just say “pause here”.
