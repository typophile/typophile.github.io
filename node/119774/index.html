EDITED Good find Ryuk. The only other font I found with a golf-club leg on the k where the diagonal strokes meet at or near the vertical, is Fresco Informal Sans https://ourtype.com/#/try/pro-fonts/fresco-informal/ but it also has golf club terminals on i and n and top serifs. I found a few fonts with the gold-club leg where they diagonal strokes meet in a “T” – such as Parisine Plus, Plume, FF Sanuk, or Convergence, a Google web font – or connect to a horizontal bar – such as Andes, FS Lola or Magallanes, or the lower diagonal stroke connects to a loop – such as Aspect or Sassoon Infant.
