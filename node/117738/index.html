Forgot to mention in my previous post that Manuscript Capitals are in the Letraset catalog [at least in the 1985 edition]. I don’t know when Koeiekat will finish his version. If I wanted to make it I would do a high res scan of the letters in the Letraset catalog, delete the outlines and refine the letters. I would then recreate the outlines using a parallel lines feature. I suppose that the letters could be classified as neo-Lombardic or neo-Uncial. Don
