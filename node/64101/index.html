There’s also URW’s [[http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nimbus_Sans#Nimbus_Sans_L|Nimbus Sans L]], which pretty much is Helvetica. A subset of it was released under a free licence to be included with Ghostscript. There are numerous (legal) derivatives floating around the web, e.g. [[http://www.gnu.org/software/freefont/|GNU FreeFont]].
