A Photostudio logo

March 16, 2005 • jack_daniels

Hi,everybody. I'm showing you a logo I created for a photostudio of a friend of mine in Brazil. The name is "Olhodepeixe", which in English would be "Fisheye" (olho=eye and peixe=fish).
The idea was to refer to the wide-angle lens that have the same name. However, the photographer didn't want any fish in the visual identity, we both agreed that it would be too literal and silly.
Therefore I came up with a reference to the wide angle lens, which is also a (not so) subtle image of an eye. I wanted a strong and simple logo, to be applied in any color, so I'm sending you the black version.
Well, I would like to see your opinion and suggestions to improve or even change it radically. I'm sure it will help me.

Thanks,
