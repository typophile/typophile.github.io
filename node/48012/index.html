They are obviously based on traditional late 19th & early 20th Century pub signs, etched or gold-leafed on glass & mirrors. It could be that they were done by someone who makes & restores those. It might be worth checking here: [[http://www.handletteringforum.com/forum/|The Hand Lettering Forum]].

A couple of the UK specialists in this work, Arthur Vanson & Dave Smith, have designed fonts for Letterhead Fonts.
