Is that the right order? 1. aalt 2. locl 3. frac 4. c2sc 5. smcp 6. fina 7. onum 8. case 9. lnum 10. pnum 11. tnum 12. sups 13. sinf 14. numr 15. dnom 16. sso1 17. salt 18. liga 19. hist 20. dlig 21. calt 22. ordn 23.ornm 24. zero 25.cpsp 26.kern
There is no "right order," but of course there are better orders than others... I would advise putting 'sups', 'numr' and the like ahead of 'onum'. The way you have it there, you (probably) would have to make both default and oldstyle numbers as input to 'numr' etc. which is just cluttering up your code. For similar reasons, you might want to have 'ordn' before 'smcp'. You probably want 'numr' and 'dnom' ahead of 'frac'. (If you have arbitrary fractions in 'frac' you can use a lookup from 'numr'.) I think you'd want 'hist' before 'liga' (depending on what's actually in there).
