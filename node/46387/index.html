2008-06-13 18:06:31 • jupiterboy

I think it looks closer to Janson, but the R leg is not serifed. Gábor Kóthay’s [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/t26/tyrnavia/|Tyrnavia]] is based on Kis’s lettering, but has an R with a tapered leg, although it extends below the baseline. The other Janson problem is the M, which in most Jansons looks lopsided (including Tyrnavia).

I think this might be a custom face designed for the Tate, but I haven’t tried Googling this subject yet.
