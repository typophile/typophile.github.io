2006-11-19 16:21:32 • Alessandro Segalini

As mentioned, the most reliable free font sites are listed under Typophile’s FAQ section: free fonts. Of these, I recommend, as the most refined and stylish for display faces, Nick Curtis' free fonts at

http://www.moorstation.org/typoasis/designers/curtis/pages/a.htm

Storm Foundry offers a somewhat condensed version of Times Roman, four fonts for free.

And go to MyFonts for free Larabie fonts; use the search to find others. MyFonts also has lots of sales.

Quantity is easy to find, but not quality.

And see if your school has fonts already acquired, ready to use.

If you want to go to the trouble of making your own font, you can use a free tool like FontForge to do it.
