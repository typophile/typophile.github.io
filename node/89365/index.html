Careful: don’t confuse axes and masters. You designed a font with 2 axes and 4 masters.

I would do Generate Instance to generate the second master as a separate font temporarily. Then use the Assign Master command to get it into the MM font again. Assign Master is more reliable than Blend Fonts or Mask to Master.
