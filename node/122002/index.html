Fake Latin, USA, c.1966

December 15, 2014 • Nick Shinn

Cute, but for some people who read Cyrillic a Faux Russian with Cyrillic letters substituted directly for the wrong Roman letters is a painful mish-mash. But I do like the artwork. A charming children’s storybook style that evokes the beloved family characters of Tolstoy’s War and Peace. Don
