LOVE this book. I’ve always wanted to send in my secret(s), but time has never allowed me to do so. BUT anyway, if you don’t already know what Post Secret is, I’ll tell you. The book is filled with postcards that people send in anonymously (to the address on the cover) and each card contains a secret.

I think the cover is pretty neat. I gives you a clue to what’s inside the book, but still leaves you a little curious. I also like how the whole address is on the cover, a good way to get that out there. There’s just enough detail, not too much. Not to mention, I am quite a fan of hand lettering which this cover appears to have.

Cheeeeeeck it out. http://postsecret.blogspot.com/
