body: "<strong>[[Indices]] : [[Conferences]] : TypoTechnica</strong>\r\n\r\nTechnological
  focused conference organized by [[Linotype]].\r\n\r\nTypoTechnica is a forum for
  discussion regarding font design and production. The focus of the conferences is
  always on the cutting edge of font technology development. The conferences are organized
  by Linotype for the benefit of the entire community, and many of the lectures are
  organized by [[Adobe]], [[Microsoft]], [[Letterror]], etc.\r\n\r\nThe first TypoTechnica
  (at the time named the [[Linotype Font Technology Forum]] took place in 2001, in
  Heidelberg, Germany at the [[Heidelberger Druckmaschinen AG]]'s [[Print Media Academy]].
  Documentation may be found <a href=\"http://www.linotype.com/14483/impressions-doc.html\">here</a>.\r\n\r\nThe
  second TypoTechnica took place in 2003, also at the Print Media Academy. Documentation
  may be found <a href=\"http://www.linotype.com/8-1678-8/linotypetypotechnica2003.html\">here</a>.\r\n\r\nThe
  third TypoTechnica took place in February 2005, at the [[St Bride Printing Library]]
  in London. Documentation may be found <a href=\"http://www.linotype.com/8-2168-8-18142/alookback.html\">here</a>.\r\n\r\n[[TypoTechnica
  2007]] conference, the fourth Typotechnica coference, was held in Frankfurt, Germany,
  from April 27\u201328, 2007."
