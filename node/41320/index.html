Bryant [[Condensed]] Designed by [[Eric Olson]] 2002–2005

Bryant [[Condensed]] is the mid-width family of the [[Bryant]] series.Though [[condensed]] in name, this family shares a page color and body width similar to many standard [[grotesque]]s. In other words, it’s equally suited for [[text]] and [[display]] work.
