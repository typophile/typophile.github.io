In your 3.34pm post, the first sample use colour and the second does not even use shades of grey. I don’t think you would get that kind of difference just because of different fonts. I mean, the rendering stays the same throughout your system unless the software overrides it. Under Standard greyscale rendering, the only thing a font might add is shades of grey. Under Cleartype rendering, you’d never get that binary pixel look, not even with a pixel font.
