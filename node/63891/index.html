New Retro and Nostalgic Families Released and Discounted

November 5, 2009 • Rasendyll

Greater Albion Typefounders has just released out latest two families, [[http://new.myfonts.com/fonts/gatf/slatterine/ | Slatterine]] and [[http://new.myfonts.com/fonts/gatf/stannard/ | Stannard]]. Slatterine is a ‘Streamline-era’ family drawing its inspiration from the ‘futuristic’ designs of the 1950s. Stannard is a pure piece of advertising inspired Nostalgia from the 1920s. Starting from November 5 both families will be offered at a 30% discount on myfonts.com.
