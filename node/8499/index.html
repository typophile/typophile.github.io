2005-01-06 21:50:33 • Chris Rugen
I would recomend reading Muller-Brockmann's book "Grid Systems Raster Systems" for info on the highly structured end of grids. Then I would just move some block shapes around on a blank piece of paper to try to learn visual relationships with no grid at all. The two experiences together will give you a basis for getting something to use in comparison.
