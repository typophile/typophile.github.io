Glad you noticed the little refresh, Bram. You have it right. FSI FontShop International sells fonts from many foundries at FontShop.com. FontFont is the "house brand" and its fonts are available both at FontFont.com and FontShop.com. Personally, I prefer the experience of FontShop.com, but the prices are the same at both sites. In short: FontFont is a foundry. FontShop is a reseller. Other facts: FontFont is one of the oldest digital foundries and just celebrated its 15th anniversary. FSI is celebrating with FiFFteen, a traveling exhibit. As for your last question: yes and yes.
