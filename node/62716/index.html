Hi there! I'd have to say, I think the second version of the logo (in the header) is a lot less confusing. Even so, it is hard to make out the K and the R, especially because the K is backwards. I only figured it out in the header because your name was listed, so I was able to make out the initials. I just don't see any connectivity between the design. Yes, it looks nice, but I think the reason it's confusing is because there's no sort of... unifying concept. For example, moving the line of the K to be in the same position as the line of the R just higher up (creating, in essence, a diagonal) may help unify the image slightly. Then again, that may be less readable. Also, I think the whole "D" part of the R is confusing, especially because you don't have a D in your initials (or at least aren't branding yourself with your middle initial, which could be a D I suppose). I'm sorry I don't have more solutions to this. My best advice would be to go to the drawing board, bring us some concept images, and then we'll take it from there. I'll mull over this and post back if I have any more ideas, though! -- David
