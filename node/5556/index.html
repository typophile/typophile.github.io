2004-07-12 16:47:22 • magnus_gaarde
It is indeed a custom job.

From the exhibition catalogue:
"The title font, based on the letterforms on a 1940 California license plate, was created for the exhibition."

The catalogue was co-published by the Los Angeles County Museum of Art and the University of California Press. It credits the design to Scott Taylor.
