2007-03-13 15:04:06 • James Scriven
In Illustrator and Photoshop, change the colour pallette to HSB and remove the Hue and Saturation values. This will give you a direct grayscale representation. All logo's should be designed in grayscale first imo, to establish a good contrast between the various elements, as well as possible background colours. After that it shouldn't be too hard to choose colours with a brightness similar to what you have in grayscale.
