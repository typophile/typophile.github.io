Sounds like you are looking for a “single storey” a.

Century Gothic is very similar to avant garde.

Futura is a good font to invest in, in my opinion. I have found it very useful over the years.

You could also try something with a slab serif, like Geometric Slab 703

Chalet from House Industries has a retro-modern feel to it.
