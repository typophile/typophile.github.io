This is going to sound monotonous to some of the regulars here. Perhaps something for the FAQ:

Many different typefaces will work with Bank Gothic. To narrow down the list it would be best to know more about your project. What is the message of the organization? Who is its audience? These are the first questions you should ask before you think about pairings.

Great brief. Thanks. Gotham seems okay for this, but it has the drawback of being overexposed right now. So if he wants to be unique, best use unique type.

Bank Gothic has a vintage pedigree, but you can make it live comfortably in a modern design if you want. Suggestions in that vein:

Amplitude Utopia Descendiaan FF Nexus Serif PMN Caecilia Présence Freight

This is sort of a scatter shot. If one or more of these hit near the spot for you, let me know and I can offer more in that genre.
