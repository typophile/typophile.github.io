Why do I even bother?

May 13, 2005 • Bald Condensed

A colleague of mine wants to use Blue Highway for a project. I advise against it: we own a license for Interstate which covers the corresponding weights she needs. She insists. I tell her I fail to see the reason, and explain Blue Highway is a poor digitisation of exactly the same model. No she won't have it, she will accept no substitute. Exasperated, I ask her why we should spend money on an inferior design when we have the real deal!? She says it looks a bit different, and she wants that exact look. ... Some days, authoritarian rule inexplicably sounds very appealing.
Sometimes the magic finger in "Bewitched" sounds appealing too. The main character in the old TV show was a pretty witch who, with the wave of a finger, could make anything disappear (perhaps a client or two or just that portion of their brain that makes logical thought impossible). ChrisL
