Obviously very “[[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/itc/bauhaus/|Bauhaus]]”. Many freebies (an endless list actually) share the same backbone ([[http://www.ffonts.net/BN-Internet.font|BN Internet]], [[http://www.ffonts.net/Quadranta.font|Quadranta]], [[http://www.ffonts.net/Radii.font|Radii]], [[http://www.ffonts.net/Radius.font|Radius]]…) but as I can’t spot a match, it could also be a complete custom job (part of the /e which could have been simply rotated), not so hard to reproduce as it looks very geometric and only made with circles and strokes. As the tagline (“See it, hear it, try it”) looks like [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/mti/century-gothic/|Century Gothic]], I would probably start from this one and customized it: /t from /o bottom left quarter adding a vertical stroke and a middle bar, same for /r, rotated /e, /n from half part of /o plus descenders, /d from /o plus ascender…
