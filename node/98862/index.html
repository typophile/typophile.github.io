The Naked Truth

December 12, 2012 • quadibloc

No, this post isn’t about Gypsy Taub.

I came across a preview of “The Naked Truth: From the Shadows of the Bible” on Google Books when doing a search for “Can’t Take My Eyes Off You”.

I was able to find out that it was “Windy” by The Association that held that song back to the #2 spot, just as Lesley Gore’s “You Don’t Own Me” never got above #2, being sandwiched between two hits by a new group out of Britain… “I Want to Hold Your Hand” and “She Loves You (Yeah, Yeah, Yeah)” on its highest week.

In any event, [[http://books.google.ca/books?id=lXWDZnersAAC|this book]] has its body copy set in… Papyrus.

Fortunately for the typographic world, I suspect it won’t be topping the NYT Best Seller list any time soon.
