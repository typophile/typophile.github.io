I kind of doubt that there is anything wrong with your font. You say they are printing offset, so: 1. They are using film to make the plates, or 2. They are going direct-to-plate. So I believe the problem might lie in the imagesetter or printer software since your proofing process came out correct. It may be that they are using a Postscript emulator rather than true Postscript, which can certainly cause problems.
