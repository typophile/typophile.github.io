I think Jim’s suggestion of Pump (Bold) is as close as I have seen. The changes you mention would be very simple to make just by converting to outlines and moving some points around. For example, the A counter could be closed this way just by moving up the bottom corner points of the counter. You could move up the 3 rows of points by the E arm to widen the bottom arm and narrow the space on either side of the middle arm. Minor tweaking of the S is all you’d need. In other words, I think this logo is just tweaked Pump. The basic letter shapes are already there.
