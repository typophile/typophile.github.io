For any who are interested, I found Garage Gothic Bold which is similar, the 3 is as good as the same and the 5 is similar. The 7 has a curved vertical stroke and the one has a slab serif bit on the bottom which differs from the sample. In the capitals, the W and M middle strokes do not extend to the base line / cap height as shown in the sample - and is overall a little wider. I have also looked at other Frere-Jones faces, Champion Gothic Bantamweight & Knockout No. 66 Full Flyweight, however these don’t appear to have the angled 3 or irregular 5 (however the 1 is similar).

Any clues are most appreciated. Thanks again.
