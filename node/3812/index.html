My only info for this sample is that it was printed in 1990 and the supplier of the PDF claims it was called "New York Vogue". I've tried all the usual Futura suspects. None have this 'M' with the straight sides. Not Drescher or FF Super Grotesk either. I feel there's a good chance it could be a cheapo knockoff due to the inconsistencies in stem width (rounds are thicker than straights and so on). The figures are also wacky. ny vogueNYVogue_example.pdf (20.0 k)
There is an old metal face called Vogue that was almost certainly the basis for that. It has come up here before.
