Seattle gathering!

October 30, 2007 • MichellePerham

After the excitement of hosting Typecon in Seattle this past August, several local people expressed an interest in having a regular gathering for “type geeks” in Seattle. We had our first gathering last month and there was lots of enthusiasm to continue. We’re going to meet at the Pub at 3rd Place books in Ravenna on Tuesday November 6th at 7:30pm. The pub is underneath the bookstore at the corner of 65th Street and 20th Avenue NE. http://www.3rdplacepub.com/ No meeting or agenda, just stop by and have a drink. Michelle
Just a reminder this is happening this evening - seems as if the Seattle chapter of ATypI and SoTA board members will be present, so maybe the drinks will be on them? Or maybe not.
