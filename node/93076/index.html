Contest for young deigners „MAKE ME”

We invite you to take part in the 5th edition of “make me!” contest for young designers.

The contest is directed to young artists aged between 20 and 30. We accept works from various areas of design, including fashion.

The main prize of PLN 20 000 as well as awards in the form of study tours and internships create the possibility of development and improvement of one’s skills.

Works are to be submitted between 15 March and 30 June.
