Answer #1 - Click on "My Account" in the right-hand column - Click on "Edit" in the black bar - Scroll down until you see "Picture" - Upload your image following the given instructions Answer #2 - 450 Pixels wide Answer #3 - Click on "Edit" in the black bar - Scroll down to "Attach new file:" - Click on "Choose File" - Choose your file and follow the remaining instructions. Note: The creator (and moderators) can always edit the original thread to modify the text and/or add images/PDFs. Answer #4 - Start a new thread
