Hello,

this is my first typeface and it's based on the idea of cutting one shape out of another. I thought it would be a fairly simple typeface to start-off with because of this simplicity and I'm fairly happy with the results so far. I wanted the typeface to have a futuristic funk (a la Parliament, George Clinton, P-Funk etc) vibe to it. Something that would be displayed on the side of their Interstellar Time-Travellin' Funkmobile in the year 3030 (hence the name).

I've found the typeface starts lookin pretty  once you have more than 4 letters in the word which kinda sucks. I may just be paranoid though

Just an idea: Maybe you should try to direct the 'movement' of the letters in one, ehm, direction. I don't know if I'm making myself clear, but that way you'd be able to read longer words.
