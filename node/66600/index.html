January 24, 2010 • malbright

Greetings, Would really appreciate your sage advice and tyopgraphical wisdom.

Working on a magazine redesign using Christian Schwartz' delicious Brunel (see samples below), which he describes as an anthology of the late eighteenth and nineteenth century English foundries.

I need to find the perfect sans to pair … something that evokes the same era and feels like part of the family with that lovely period flavor. I’d also like to find a compatible slab … so basically the whole works.

What would YOU match up? And if you have the time … please tell me why.

Many, many thanks!
