Well I'd personally avoid woodcarver (assuming you're talking about ICG Carver). It doesn't fit the mold of old letterpressed woodtype and it's pretty illegible at any size. I think Corner Bakery does a decent job of incorporating different faces for a rustic quasi-show card feel. Perhaps combine Rosewood & Rosewood Fill with some other woodtype faces like Brothers, Council, or some of the Adobe woodtypes
