Hi all, In follow-up to [[http://typophile.com/node/64158|my last post]] regarding the development of my display font (or 'Elementary', as a working title), please find most up to date visual attached for some ever-constructive feedback/comments. After going around in circles, I've opted to keep the 'bulletholes', but as you'll see they're now hopefully counter-balanced with the addition of heavy strokes and filled-in counters. Also, I strongly feel this adds more impact to the typeface compared with previously, especially when viewed from a distance. As this is still primarily intended for headline usage and due to the embellishments, it felt like natural progression to keep this in caps. I consider this to be still in relatively early stages, but Let me know what you think. Thanks in advance, Graeme[img:sites/default/files/old-images/elementary_font_08_5377.jpg]
