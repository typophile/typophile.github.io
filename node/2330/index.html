Mike:

I have been working on the letterpress printing of books. Have completed a book of selected poems by E. Pauline Johnson; Dickens" A Christmas Carol. Am presently working on Mark Twain's The Adventures of Tom Sawyer.
I've cut a new roman typeface for the latter, which I am casting at my foundry for the book.

Equipment:
20" Reprex Proof Press (poor man's Vandercook
14x22 Chandler & Price Platen press
11" Potter Proof Press
Chandler & Price Paper Cutter 22"
Separate Little book bindery (converted garage)
Thompson Type Caster
British Monotype Super Caster
American Monotype 15x15 Composition Caster
Approx 150 boxes of Display flat mats and Super
Caster mats, plus 40 comp diecases of mats
Pantographs: Weibking/Ludlow; Taylor Hobson;
Ogata RS 260 (currently making missing parts for Ogata)

Searching with little hope of success, for a 14x22 Colts
Armory or Laureate or Hartford platen press.

Other work in progress: drawings for Cree language which I will cut in punch form at about 16 point. (long term)

Currently casting the 12 to 36 point sizes of Bulmer Roman and Italic for Nemo Press.

If you are visiting Vancouver, tours welcome.

Jim Rimmer
