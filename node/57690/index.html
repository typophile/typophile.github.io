It’s shown as “Chantry” in the Solotype Catalog published by Dover Books, which only means it was a film type, or something older. Many of these designs have been digitized, and I usually note them in my copy of the book, but I have no names by that sample, alas.

If anyone has digitized it, Dan Solo, David Nalle (Scriptorium), or Dieter Steffmann would be the first places I would look. Then HiH fonts, who also does revivals of old designs.
