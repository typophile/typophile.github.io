Garden Type Logo

January 28, 2009 • Journey

First off, let me say I am no designer. I quite suck at it. And, by the end of all this, I'd most likely have to hire a designer. But you see... my issue is that I seem to pick sucky designers that need to be told every step of the way what to do. I do realize this is probably because I can't afford top-notch and so I reap what I sow. But I thought I'd give this wonderful forum a shot ... to glean ideas and feedback to see what direction I should take this logo. The concept is based on the Victory Gardens of the WWII era. [[http://images.google.com/images?q=victory+garden+posters&btnG=Search+Images&um=1&hl=en&sa=2]] except instead of VICTORY, I am using FREEDOM. Here is a very rough idea. I haven't yet found the fonts that "spoke" to me. Something that says garden / dirty / modern, etc. I would like to evoke the feeling of the old victory gardens but take it into modern times. One of the marks would be this fist holding a garden trowel. But how to integrate the two? Thanks!
I would stay away from that "cartoon/comic book" font and anything similar. The bottom type is more what you want. As for the placement of the garden trowel...you can separate the two words (on same line) with it in the middle, have in "grow" out of the word garden or you can have that as the main image inside a circle and have the type work around the image. Those are just a few suggestions... What exactly do you mean by: "I would like to evoke the feeling of the old victory gardens..." JonE.
