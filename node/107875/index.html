Here’s the answer I got from Ryuk (27 Jun 2013): Don’t have a match unfortunately. Some in the same vein needing editing/customization: (DIN) Engschrift Austria D (mainly reworking /G), Korolev, Komu B, Pakt Black (/R off), Tempus Gothic Alternate

The Brazilian and world distribution rights were sold to Columbia Pictures, so more than likely the movie posters were printed in the US. A search for foundry and phototype matching the title doesn’t turn up anything that matches. Given the size of the original poster, the era it was produced in plus some of the details of the type and its spacing, I believe this was hand-lettered.
