I love that ampersand… The A is now well balanced (though I cannot feel yet comfortable with the top serif, but don’t take it in account). I’d make the spur of the G sharper and less heavy. Your J –the first one– now looks great; perhaps the left serif is still long a bit. I like the newborn K, but the weight of the strokes is odd: the upper is too thick, while the lower is too thin (the should follow the same logic of the M or the V); it happens in both versions –the latter is pretty good; the fat J bothers me, but it’s just me.
