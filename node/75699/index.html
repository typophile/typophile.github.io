Bauwelt

October 27, 2010 • beetrootman

This is the logotype used for Bauwelt magazine in the 60s. It is very similar to FF Bau, or Schelter & Giesecke’s Grotesk in it’s pre digitized life. It is more extended than any version of those two typefaces that I know of and has some small differences, mainly in the ‘a’. Perhaps this was custom drawn for the magazine. Does anybody know anything about it, or anything about Bauwelt magazine from that period?

Thank you
