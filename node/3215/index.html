Egyptienne (F) is indeed a good match.

You might also try:

Candida
http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/urw/candida/

Edison
http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/ef/edison/

Marconi
http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/linotype/marconi/

Melior
http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/linotype/melior/

Schadow
http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/bitstream/schadow/

ITC Slimbach
http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/linotype/itc-slimbach/

ITC Zapf Book
http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/bitstream/itc-zapf-book/

or ITC Zapf International
http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/bitstream/itc-zapf-international/

Essentially, any text face that has an affinity for
the square or super-ellipse should be a pretty
good match.
