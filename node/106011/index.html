September 3, 2013 • Mahogany

Font used in the opening sequence of BULLITT (1968)
1968, three years after Geoffrey Lee designed Impact for Stephenson Blake. Also similar: Helvetica Inserat released in 1966 by the D. Stempel, which has an R with a kick at the end of its leg, and an old timer: Grotesque No. 9 by Stephenson Blake and the very similar Headline Bold from Monotype – not digitized under this name – also with a kicking R. Could also be something from photo lettering. Don
