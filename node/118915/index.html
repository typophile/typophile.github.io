Lindau released

August 22, 2014 • Andreas Stötzner

I admitt, there are more ideas for fonts than time to always elaborate them to a whole family package. Usually, the ideas lay dormant in some hidden file for that reason, having little chance to see the light of the day. But sometimes I want to release the baby for I feel that it would be rather a pitty to ‘keep it imprisoned’. So I decided to [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/andreas-stotzner/lindau/|release Lindau now]], although it is such a “lonely child”. It may be of some usefulness, anyway.

Glad you let your baby out, looks very nice!
