Stephen, Type Tours of the City of London, organised by Type Events are very good.

See also:<a href=“http://www.typevents.com/index.php?id=81,0,0,1,0,0" http://typophile.com/node/33877 http://typophile.com/node/29958 http://www.flickr.com/photos/timbones/sets/72157594392158650/

You don’t have to sign in at the library. However, in my experience it is best to contact the library first if you have a specific request. Most of the collection is tucked away, as apparently the reading room contains just 7% of their collection.
