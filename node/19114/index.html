Now I'm looking for a texty (some paragraphs of text, but not too much) font to use. The ideal candidate would work as a heading font as well. * Referred to in this thread: http://typophile.com/node/19096 Specific suggestions (or wanton brain dumps) highly welcome. hhp

For full resonance, you would have to use a version of Jenson that had the "extra" serifs on the M -- Adobe snipped them off in the default version of Adobe Jenson, but the proper version is in the alt characters. My Jenson, Goodchild, has the full serifs. Stone's Arepo has a similar quality to your logo, so Cycles could be good, and comes in optical scaling, handy for the back label on wine bottles -- I hate squinting at 6pt Snell to figure out what I'm supposed to be drinking the wine with.
