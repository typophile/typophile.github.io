Hey, look what I found when Googling for "Corpid" "Frutiger" "de Groot": Metro International (…) With this redesign, the newspaper is saying good-bye to one half of the original Metro typography from 1995. A new sans serif font, named Corpid and designed by Lucas de Groot of the Netherlands, will replace Franklin Gothic and Agenda. Corpid is to be used for headlines as well as for logos, captions, fact boxes, quotes, etc. For the news section, Lucas de Groot has developed a high contrast version of Corpid which will be used exclusively by the Metro newspapers all over the world. The body typeface, Gerard Ungers Swift, will stay untouched.
