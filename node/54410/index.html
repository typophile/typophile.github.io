Migration finally released :^)

February 4, 2009 • sim

After almost 4 years, I’ really pleased to annouce the release of the Migration Sans typeface.

This project start in may 2005: http://typophile.com/node/12592 As it is my first typeface you will probably understand why I’m so excited. I did’nt know where the projet would go when I started it, but now it’s a real typeface and I want to tell my gratitude to all typophile members who gave me their comments or advices all along the process.

Special thanks to Hrant, Chris, Mark and Nick on Typophile and also Xavier Dupré and Ian Party on Typographe.

I also want to thanks Allan Haley and Jim Wasco from Monotype Imaging for their great support.

You will find Migration at Monotype http://www.fonts.com/findfonts/SearchPage.htm?kid=migration http://www.itcfonts.com http://www.itcfonts.com/Fonts/NewFonts/2009/MigrationSans.htm

For those who would like to see more, I insert a character set.

Congratulations! It looks a versatile and useful face.
