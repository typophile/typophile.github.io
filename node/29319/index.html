Edit: A Photo-Lettering specimen book from the 70s calls this typeface Graphique Shaded. Fast forward to the digital era: as far as I can tell from a little reading, Corel distributed it as Griffon. I also have an old Image Club font from 1994 called Graphik Shadow that is almost identical.

A variation on Griffon is available as freeware, but after my reading I’m doubtful of its legality. I’ve removed the link I originally posted (to save the moderators the trouble!).
