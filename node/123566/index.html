This lettering is in a style called textura. In particular, compare the letters to the shapes found in Fette Gotisch, designed by Friedrich Wilhelm Bauer in 1875 http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/linotype/fette-gotisch/ The image shows signs of having been horizontally compressed. The pre-compression image might have been hand lettered. Don
