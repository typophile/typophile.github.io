Alphabetics: The only glyph that really isn't working is the "Q". And in the "g" I would raise the horizontal bar a bit. In the "y" you can "cheat" by raising the part that sits on the baseline (to make more air for the descender). Numerals: The "4" is too small - lower the bar. The "7" is too big. Other: The acute/grave accents aren't working. I'm not sure about the comma/quotes. The period (and the others) would probably be much nicer larger. The bars of the Euro are too close.
