Could be wrong but as I don’t know any geometric sans with such a leg for /R, could be custom. I’m not even sure if /W is not customized as well. My choice would probably be [[http://www.myfonts.com/search/avenir|Avenir]] (mainly because of x-width and bowl and /G crossbar upper position) customizing /W centre apex (or overlapping 2 /Vs and chopping off crossing stem part) and adding a custom leg to /P. More candidates to customization: [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/marksimonson/proxima-nova|Proxima Nova]], [[http://www.typography.com/fonts/gotham/overview|Gotham]], [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/font-fabric/nexa|Nexa]], [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/northernblock/acrom|Acrom]], [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/synthview/novecento|Novocento]], [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/canadatype/gibson|Gibson]], [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/okay-type/alright-sans|Alright Sans]], [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/letterheadrussia/red-ring|Red Ring]], [[http://www.fontsquirrel.com/fonts/Raleway|Raleway]], [[http://www.fontsquirrel.com/fonts/montserrat|Montserrat]], [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/gautier/maax|Maax]], [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/northernblock/corbert|Corbert]] You could also try asking contrast8 [[http://contrast8.com/#watergate|Deividas Bielskis]].

Mixing Parisine and Parisine Plus you could approximate the look, but |G| isn’t wider enough. Pluto, on the other hand, is too wide overall (once you cut the inner “branch” of |W|). There are others sans with unconventional legs on |R|, but of those I saw none has the same of the sample, and all have wrong details for other letters (examples: Filson, Fox Grotesque, Cabrito Sans). I found especially difficult finding a matching |W|.
