Custom, or what?

March 23, 2009 • Theunis de Jong

Some CEO dumps a logo on my desk and tells me to make a new one with an entirely different text but using the same font. Me: “Sure. So… what’s this font?” Him: “How should I know? That’s your job innit?”

It could be a custom job; however, I rate that as unlikely, given the subtleties – the left and right leg of the “U” are of slightly different widths, and the “N” has some surprising curves I just know I’ve seen before (perhaps Friz Quadratish?). OTOH, I’m not too wild about those “E” and “F"s. Still, I’d like to know.

Unfortunately, without a working image, we’ll have to close this one down. Theunis, perhaps you can enlighten us - What did you end up finding?
