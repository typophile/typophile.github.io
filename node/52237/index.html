Typophile Film Fest 5 // Coming Soon

December 1, 2008 • Ngoc Ngo

UPDATE Punchcut and AIGA Portland are proud to present the premier of the Typophile Film Festival 5, taking place in Portland, Oregon on August 27th 2009.

For more details visit: http://portland.aiga.org/events/2009/08/33102869

Punchcut will bring the Typophile Film Festival to Phoenix, Arizona for Phoenix Design Week, October 23 2009.

For more details visit: http://www.phxdw.com/

The Typophile Film Fest 5 is happy to be featured at ATypI: Heart of the Letter 2009! Come see us in Mexico City October 29th. Please visit http://www.atypi.org/04_Mexico

Thanks to all those who submitted work to the Typophile Film Fest 5!

Typophile Film Fest 5 // Trailer from Typophile on Vimeo.

Dozens of amazing submissions were sent to us from all over the world. Reviewing each and every one was a pleasure and very inspiring. The quality of work and the talent of all designers involved has really raised the bar for all future entrants.

The event is currently being scheduled for a few select cities across North America with our first showing sometime this summer. Inquiries are coming in on a regular basis and we expect to have a handful of exciting cities lined up for this year and next. Please keep an eye out on here on [[Typophile]] where we will make all the official announcements for TFF locations.

Congratulations to the following people for being selected to be a part of TFF 5, and a big thank you on behalf of our team at [[Punchcut]] for your efforts and we look forward to showcasing your work to thousands of people across the globe.
