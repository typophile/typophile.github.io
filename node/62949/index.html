Keeping up to date with the latest fonts being released? How do you do it?

October 11, 2009 • TF22Raptor

Hi all,

I am a website / graphic designer and are just interested in how others keep up to date with the latest released fonts. What is your habit? Is there a few must visit websites you visit each week that does a round up of the week/months releases etc?

I’d love to be able to go to one or two website each week, or be email once or twice a week with the latest releases without having to go and visit every font designers website etc.

Any help would be great

You can start by subscribing to MyFonts.com or Fontshop.com mailing list. Furthermore, you can try visiting each distributed type foundry’s websites and subscribing to their blog feeds.

This forum also host a ‘Release’ section at http://typophile.com/forum/7

ILoveTypography.com also reviews newly released typeface every once in a while.
