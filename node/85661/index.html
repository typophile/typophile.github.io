Selectric Composer Typefaces

September 20, 2011 • quadibloc

I don’t know if people here will consider this to be a valuable resource or not, but I had been looking for something like this online before and could not find it:

On [[http://ibmcomposer.org/joomla/index.php?option=com_content&view=article&id=54&Itemid=56|this page]] just click on “here”, and you can download the complete portfolio of typestyles for the IBM Selectric Composer.

These include Aldine Roman, similar to Bembo; Journal Roman, similar to Garamond; and of course Press Roman, similar to Times Roman.

Thus, one has a list of the most “essential typefaces” from at least one perspective:

Bembo (imitated by Aldine Roman) Baskerville Bodoni Century Expanded (called Century here) News Gothic (sort of imitated by Classified News) Copperplate Gothic Garamond (imitated by Journal Roman) Times Roman (imitated by Press Roman) Memphis (imitated by Pyramid) Optima (imitated by Theme) Univers

Of course, the Selectric Composer could only set text, not headlines - for that, you could always use Letraset.

Thanks for the link John - remember the Composer well - we used to set whole publications with one before computer typesetting.
