Hi Lou,

#1 looks like ITC Firenze, by [[Tom Carnase]]. Not really available at the moment, see this older thread. #2 is Pistilli Roman by [[John Pistilli]]. Also not available anymore, see this thread. There’s a new version by [[Jason Walcott]], named Eloquent.

For alternatives, see this FontList of Extra Bold Didones. Ingeborg Fat by [[Michael Hochleitner]] is a marvellous fresh option with accompanying italics; it has a few swash cap alternates and all the other bells and whistles you could wish for. F
