PJ, are you taking relative size into account? The request is for a body text sans to accompany Centaur at display size.

As soon as Centaur acquires any size, its details start to make it look chiseled and horsey, as if drawn with a big old pen on rough paper. That’s not stately.

Therefore, an angular, humanist sans such as Seria Sans or Fedra, at a smaller size, will harmonize with it.
