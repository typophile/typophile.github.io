Romesh,

Instead of ITC Weidemann, you could use Octava (ParaType), that has similar proportions and is also equipped with smallcaps:
http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/paratype/octava/

You can also take a look at Adonis (ParaType):
http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/paratype/adonis/

Instead of Trade Gothic, you can use Bell Gothic (ParaType):
http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/paratype/bell-gothic-bt/
or New Letter Gothic (ParaType):
http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/paratype/new-letter-gothic/
