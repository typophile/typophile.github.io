The TR Avalon at fontsplace looks like a clone of Avant Garde, designed in 1970. As for Spartan, this was produced in many variations from 1939 onwards by Bauer & Linotype, as a knockoff of Futura. Only two versions have been digitized, as Book and Heavy [img:sites/default/files/old-images/SpartanBookNumbers_6172.gif][img:sites/default/files/old-images/SpartanHeavyNumbers_3638.gif] For _5_, top but not bottom is similar to your manual. For _6_ some curvature of the back, but not as much as your manual. No real difference from Futura. It's almost as if the font designer took the bottom of a Futura _3_ and pasted it on _5_ or lifter the bottom of the _5_ in Helvetica.
