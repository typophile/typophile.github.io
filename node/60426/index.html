The CS suite has several backdoors to get fonts to work. Put the files in C:\Program Files\Common Files\Adobe\Fonts and they should be visible in all CS programs. Put them in C:\Program Files\Adobe\Adobe InDesign CS3\Fonts (or whereever your InDesign resides) and they'll only be visible in CS3. Similar for Illustrator and Photoshop. I’m student, so i ain’t got any software licences. You are mistaken. A student license is also perfectly valid: [[http://www.adobe.com/aboutadobe/openoptions/student.html]]
