January 5, 2012 • n0logic

Hi,

I am having a hard time identifying this font. It seems pretty familiar but haven’t been able to identify it.

Sounds stupid and I’m probably wrong but looks like a mix of several fonts to me. “you” could come from [[http://new.myfonts.com/fonts/itc/avant-garde-gothic/avant-garde-medium-bold/|Avant-Garde]] Medium, “zee” from [[http://new.myfonts.com/fonts/bitstream/futura/heavy/|Futura]] Heavy or “z” from [[http://new.myfonts.com/fonts/bitstream/futura/heavy/|Futura]] and “ee” from [[http://new.myfonts.com/fonts/adobe/avenir/black/|Avenir]] Black. The more I look at the /z, the more I think it has been customized. Thickness of the oblique bar looks “strange”. If I had to recreate it, I would start from Avenir Black (e looks better), cutting the /y tail and moving some points on the /z or, from [[http://new.myfonts.com/fonts/urw/futura-no-2/futura-no2-d-demi-bold/|Futura no2 Demi-bold]] using a 180°-rotated /n to recreate the /u.
