Typophile Welcomes Ngoc Ngo

October 9, 2008 • Joe Pemberton

We're pleased to announce Typophile's first dedicated employee. Ngoc Ngo is a seasoned consultant and an MFA candidate focused on graphic design at [[Academy of Art University]]. He has joined [[Punchcut]] at our San Francisco office and will be engaged on a number of initiatives for this site and for the Typophile community. We're looking forward to the traction that he'll bring to our modest world domination plans. Please say hello!
