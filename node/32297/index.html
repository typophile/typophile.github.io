March 16, 2007 • Marco

Hi Everyone,

I’m currently in the process of designing an Identity for a client. I’m having trouble finding typefaces. I’m currently ‘stuck’ on FF Meta, and considered FF Unit, Info and Fago. The problem is in my opinion Meta is a bit generic and overused, so I want to go with fago, but I see some problems in kerning and reading longer texts. On the other hand if Meta works, I should be happy ( and shouldn’t care about how I - as a designer -look at the face…)?!

I like to know anyone’s experience and opinion about Fago and could give some alternatives.
