The lettering in AND NOW! AT LAST! has a sort of Looney Tunes style. It would be difficult to create the overlap effect with a font, but Marvin Shadow captures the spirit http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/canadatype/marvin/ For more fonts similar to Marvin go to http://www.identifont.com/find?font=marvin&q=Go and follow the link to Show more similar fonts.
