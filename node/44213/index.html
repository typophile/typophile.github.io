Blurb.com

April 14, 2008 • j. cullen

has anybody ever used blurb http://www.blurb.com/ to create a book? The prices are incredibaly low & the quality seems decent enough…there must be a catch..has anybody had any experience using these guys?

I used Blurb to make a book with my vacation photos and I was really happy with the [[http://www.blurb.com/bookstore/detail/143191|final result]]. I didn’t end up using their templates, I designed the whole thing in InDesign and placed entire pages as images. The only thing that I didn’t really like was how the book block was attached to the hard bound spine. The white paper looks a bit flimsy, but the dust jacket covers most of that. The paper quality is ok and the images turned out nice, not super crisp but really decent. I also wish they had a choice for the color of the book cloth on hard bound, it is black.

I hope that helps, I couldn’t find any helpful reviews of their books when I was deciding.
