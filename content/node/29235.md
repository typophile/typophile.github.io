  [[Old Style]] type style of the baroque period. Like the [[Venetian]] types, this
  style is based on [[calligraphy|calligraphic]] principles, including modulated [[stroke]],
  oblique axis, [[adnate]] serifs and modeled terminals, and moderate [[aperture]].
  The Garalde [[italic|italics]] are closely associated with their corresponding [[roman]]
  fonts and are designed to be used together with them in a subordinate role.\r\n\r\nSee
  also [[Garalde Typefaces]]."
