2014-09-08 03:08:29 • Mike F

For Salem Wingate JNL, already suggested by Mike, would work but it is an all caps font with no size difference between the upper and lower case, and the m is roundtop without an ascender on the lhs stroke.

An alternative is Perron No 3, Light or Regular http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/fontforecast/perron/ Perron No 3 has a size difference between the upper and lower case, and the m is roundtop with an ascender on the lhs stroke. Neither font is a perfect match. Don
