The closest font I could find that seemed close to the W, P and S was [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/adobe/rotation/|Rotation]], but it seems to have less bracketed serifs. Either squooshing (horizontally) or stretching vertically, would possibly increase the similarity. That's the best I can suggest so far. - Mike Yanega
