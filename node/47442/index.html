Well, I took the plunge...

July 15, 2008 • P A Hardy

Forgive this rather breathless post everyone, I am a casual user & recent lurker on this forum, with no professional use for new type. Also at home I use (the love which dare not speak its name) linux, which of course comes with free fonts of varying distinction. But I wanted a neo-grotesk other than the included rather staid “stand-ins”, chiefly designed to allow document compatibility with your non-linux colleagues. So I bought my first font (other than with an application). I feel quite excited really. I chose Dyna Grotesk R, in TrueType format. Four styles for about 17GBP - amazing! OpenType is better I am sure, but TT gives me instant access in my word processor. There are some very handsome grotesks out there, including National, FF Bau, Brown Gothic etc, but after flipping for a while I chose the Storm product. For one thing I liked the subtle distinction between upper-case ‘i’ and lower-case ‘l’. I use “The Iliad” as a type sample to test this out!

Sorry for taking up your time, but if anywhere understands my pleasure it surely must be here!

Paul England
