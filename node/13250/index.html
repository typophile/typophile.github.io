House Industries Job Posting

June 23, 2005 • richroat

[[House Industries]] is seeking a motivated, enthusiastic and talented lover of typography to draw little letters and make them into unique and innovative working typefaces. Must be a reliable self-starter who can take multiple projects and run with them while working with input from multiple sources. Strong bezier drawing skills, a knowledge of FontLab and willingness to learn proprietary techniques also a must. All House Industries projects are team efforts, so loners, big egos and “artistes” need not apply. Must be willing to relocate to the area and work in our Yorklyn studio, which is a quiet, relaxed setting with few distractions. We will provide relocation assistance. If you are interested, please send a brief synopsis of your qualifications and a few samples of your work to getajob@houseind.com.

Thanks!

Rich
