We chose the name for a couple of reasons: Aluminum is half right on one of them. It does represent the plain, generic office cubicle-land. I'm half asian, so if you take a yellow asian man and mix it with a white caucasian man what do you get? Bad joke. The two L's in manilla stand for my sister and I -- it's our company.
