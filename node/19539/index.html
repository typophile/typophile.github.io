The New York is custom lettering that has been tweaked and refined over the years by, among others, Milton Glaser and Ed Benguiat. Here's a discussion between Roger Black and former NY mag AD Luke Hayman that mentions the evolution of the logo: http://www.rogerblack.com/blog/conversation_with_hayman The condensed sans serif is the now-retired Elderkin from Process Type Foundry: http://www.processtypefoundry.com/help/updates.html You can see a picture here, although the download link is dead: http://www.fontfinder.ws/12204/Elderkin-Medium.html
