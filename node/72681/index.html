2010-07-28 13:57:14 • Nick Cooke
Type won’t capture the charming irregular quality of words like LIBERTY, or GREENSBURG’s bend. Your best bet is to draw the letters as paths in Illustrator, and then stroke those paths. That will also provide consistency of line in different sizes (e.g. FIFTH and VIRGIN). It will be meticulous and time-consuming work, but will produce far better results than auto-tracing letter outlines. The effect will still be a somewhat unnatural, compared to the original engraved script, but that should be mitigated by the medium of granite etching.
