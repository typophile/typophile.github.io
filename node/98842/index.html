Seamstress logo

December 11, 2012 • Caffeinomane

Hello,

a friend of mine is a seamstress and, when she gave me her busines card, I thought that she needs a logo. The business card is in one of those default styles of an online company that makes business cards. Since I am not an expert and can not afford to hire one, I took a typeface that I like ([[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/fenotype/mercury-script/|Mercury Script]]), tweaked a little bit and added a needle. I am not sure about those four dots but they were in the font…

nice story. thanks for sharing.
