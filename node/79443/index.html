For the interested parties who can't read German – It's a food stamp of the Buchwald concentration camp canteen for the SS-staff worth 2 Reichsmark. Buchenwald, near of Weimar/Germany, was one of concentration camps during the nazi era, where the SS murdered and forwarded opposition members, jewish people, homosexuals etc. I link to a chart where you can read about the numbers of killed persons respectively the numbers of people, who were forwarded in the eastern extermination camps like Auschwitz-Birkenau, Majdanek, Treblinka etc. http://totenbuch.buchenwald.de/information/index/reset/true Apart from the typefaces and the design that should be perceived. That makes that seemingly harmless food stamp really vicious. T.
