2009-03-23 18:14:43 • shielddesign
There are three things you can do. As it is a rare occurrence, a little manual work on the "d" is OK. 1. Reduce the size of lower case "d", then increase its weight by applying a "Stroke" value. 2. Use a "d" from a smaller optical size, if your typeface has one. 3. Choose a "d" from a related font that has the appropriate weight at the size.
