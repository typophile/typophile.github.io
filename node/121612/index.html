As far as I can see, this is a modified FF DIN. Some characters (‘i’, ‘s’, ‘l’) were left alone; most were customised, many just by tacking spikes on (‘U’, ‘n’, ‘r’, ‘d’), others more radically (‘v’, ‘e’). In any case, it may very well be that only the letters required for the wordmark were created instead of an entire alphabet.

The subline is Gotham Narrow, designed by Tobias Frere-Jones.
