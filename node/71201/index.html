2010-06-10 00:50:55 • Tomi from Suomi

Hm… I’d be willing to be that use of Inkscape may be causing you some problems.

One thing font software looks for that Inkscape does not are off-point extrema; are you making sure you don’t have any? If you aren’t, doing so may help you smooth out the a and u. Also, if you ever change your mind and decide to expand this into a full typeface, it would save you a lot of redrawing trouble.
