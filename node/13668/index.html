Fuenfwerken

July 9, 2005 • dan_reynolds

[[Indicies]] : [[Designers]] : Fuenfwerken FUENFWERKEN Design AG and FUENFWERKEN Digital Business GmbH. Sometimes just referred to as Fünfwerken. German design studio in Wiesbaden and Berlin. Principals include: Jan Heinritz Marc Mundt Charly Frech Holger Schmidhuber Rolf Mehnert [[Helmut Ness]] Among other things, Fünfwerken worked with the city of Munich to design the latest navigation system for their transit authority. This project used the typeface [[Vialog]], which Helmut Ness designed together with Prof. [[Werner Schneider]]. fuenfwerken.de
