Hi I accidentally found this article (and many others following) while searching other typographic related stuff over your forums, and I was suddenly shocked by the general misunderstandings that westerner people have about [[Cyrillic]] language and alphabet, so I’ve updated the sections of your site concerning the disputed language and writing system. I found it very non-polite and offending that a group of people professionally involved in typography, which I may consider for high educated, have so little knowledge about a culture, for which they actually are trying to produce typefaces. And two more remarks: I’m not trying to produce a cultural scandal… Don’t actually believe all those Russian authors, cited in your posts – do not forget that, in the time of our little (45 years) of Russian occupation of Bulgaria, a rumor was widely spread that the actual developers of Cyrillic alphabet were from Russian origins…. Kind Regards, Kateliev PS: in the coming weeks,when i have time, i'll try to upload some works of famous Bulgarian typographers that are worth mentioning
