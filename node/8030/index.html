Alphabetic Robot

December 6, 2004 • Jon Whipple

I was suprised this wasn't mentioned anywhere I could find when I searched the archives…

Alphabot by Nikita Pashenkov at MIT. Click anywhere inside the applet (near the Alphabot itself), then type any letter(s) on the keyboard. Drag the mouse to rotate alphabots.

Find out more here which also has movies including a protoype model of the robot.
Now you can do a robotic version of the YMCA dance (for those of you who remember such things)!
