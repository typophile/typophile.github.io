  whilst others are initiated by the studio (such as  lectures, publications, forums

  regular contributor to international type-culture. Other contributors the studio's
  output have included Niels Oeltjen, Wendy Ellerton, Morice Kastoun and Sarah Findlay.\r\n\r\nRecent
  developments include opening an e-store selling fonts, tshirts and publications.
