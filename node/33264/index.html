These notches are called [[ink traps]]. Their utilitarian purpose is to prevent the tight corners of a small letterform from getting too much ink in the printing process. But they can have an aesthetic value as well. I agree that Olga is very interesting. Here's a statement from designer Christina Bee. Other typefaces with conspicuous ink traps: Bell Centennial Amplitude FF Hydra Sys Dynamo
