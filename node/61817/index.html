I was hoping someone else would comment on this as well. I'm more of a student, so I would take my words with a grain of salt. Anyhow, here's my two cents: The type look more suiting to the audience, just remember to give your caps some breathing space. Normal capitals are not spaced to work together in an all cap setting and need to be tracked out a bit. Thus, professional fonts have Small Caps especially made for these situations, and also slightly lower (and wider) making them stand out less in text. Two things are sticking out. The numerals (are they scaled up old style figures? – they are much heavier) and the first line (set in lower case only). Both are bugging me because they're not being echoed anywhere else, and I'm generally bugged by a sentence not starting with a capital when there's no apparent reason for it not to;) I would love to see the island on it's own, with no type on top of it! Perhaps a few different versions with smaller type (It think it might be a bit large).
