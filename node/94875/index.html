2012-07-23 20:59:52 • erealuv
'Cutting Corners' has a VERY NEGATIVE connotation in English. It means to do things on the cheap; to bypass necessary steps; to use the cheapest raw materials, doing less than a thorough or complete job, etc. The origin of the term was probably from horse-drawn carriages, literally making a very sharp turn at an intersecti0n, illegally going over the pedestrian path. - Herb
