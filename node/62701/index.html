2009-10-04 15:25:00 • paragraph

Kerning: horizontal space adjustment between two glyphs. Part of the typeface design. Tracking: horizontal space adjustments during typesetting. Not part of the typeface design.

IMHO the problem is rather the narrow space between H and I.
