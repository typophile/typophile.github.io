You pose an interesting question.

If the band itself is a hackneyed cliché, then a cliché solution seems appropriate. Music for simple-minded people=logo for dummies.

If, on the other hand, the ladies are creative, inventive and somewhat “ahead of the curve,” then you have your work cut out for you.

Do something surprising; do something unexpected. Hell…even Comic Sans can be used to great effect if you have the “serious irony” thing going—and can convince people that

Think in the box; think outside the box; or think WAY, WAY outside the box. Amaze yourself, and amaze everyone else in the process.

IMHO…
