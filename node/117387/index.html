2014-06-20 21:25:04 • michellebea
Late 19th century book designer, type designer and fontographer who revived medieval designs and was a major influence on the style of published books. His fonts are a fusion of Blackletter and Roman. His books used very ornate designs often involving vines and leaves. Your _O_ is a comfortable companion to his designs. More http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/William_Morris There is a lively discussion of his influence at http://typophile.com/node/114418 Don
