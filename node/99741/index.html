Tourism Seems To Be Growing Every Year

January 16, 2013 • •••GildaBottgenbach-spammer

Many people are working more, working harder, and are looking for a way to relax. Some of us don’t want to just get away from it all, but we also want to get away from it all without sacrificing sustainable development.

The environment is always an important factor in our lives, so how can we figure out a way to get our rest and recreation while promoting minimal environmental damage? One way to do it would be to take public transportation while you are at your vacation destination.

Many popular destinations offer various types of public transportation, and it is becoming more progressive in plenty of major cities. Leave the car at home and save on gas and parking fees, all while giving the environment a break. Another tip would be to eat locally.

Find out where the farmer’s markets are and stock up. Eat at the local restaurants and support small businesses. Resist the advertisements to eat at the popular chain restaurants. Bring up the global economy.
