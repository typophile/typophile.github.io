Bureau Grot from Font Bureau (with the same alterations) would work nicely as well: http://www.fontbureau.com/fonts/BureauGrot/

If STEREO was a font it is unusual in that the horizontal width of the counters of R and O and the corresponding interior portions of S resemble a modern font like Newhouse DT Extra Black [img:sites/default/files/old-images/NewhouseDTExtraBlackAsStereo_5545.gif] but the outside curve is significantly expanded. Do the horizontal expansion of the outside curves and compress the height and the result could be in the ballpark. Don
