Just wanted to reply to the thread. The details on this one are a bit different than those you commonly see, as the chap has built an actual exploit. More commonly researchers find a theoretical hole, but don’t produce an actual exploit.

Reading the details (and not being a developer myself) the issue seems to relate to one particular technology, affecting all users of that technology. One would hope that hole is being plugged by the owners of that technology. But just to be safe I’ve forwarded the details to technology owners at Apple and MS to make sure the scope of the issue is indeed limited.

Having said that I personally don’t see any harm in linking to the article (although it would be good for the moderators to chime-in) as it does serve as a reminder that fonts are code, and share some of the same issues as code when it comes to security.
