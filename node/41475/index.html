[[Josef Müller-Brockmann|Brockmann]]? Then it is [[Akzidenz Grotesk]]. Possibly a pre-digital version. Take into account that this family used to be quite patchworky, and still – even after GGL’s recut(s) and digitization – is far from being as systematical as Univers etc.

From the Wiki: The original series remained quite divers, individual weights showing not much resemblance but in name. […] That only changed when they cut Series 57, and then Series 58, named for the years of release. These had some sizes (but not all) recut under the direction of [[Günter Gerhard Lange]], who was their (freelance) artistic director at the time.
