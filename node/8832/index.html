Symbolist typefaces?

January 24, 2005 • csr

I'm doing some process work at school, setting type. The text i'm using is an excerpt from the Symbolist poet Stephane Mallarme. Are there any typefaces which are associated with the Symbolist movement? I would think nothing too humanistic, but then again I'm a little rusty on my history.

any pointers would be much appreciated.

-christopher
One approach would be to work in the style of the avant garde graphic artists of Mallarme's time, using the typefaces they would have used, ie what was usual in trade typesetting shops, mainly Numbered Moderns (not Bodoni or Didot) and Grotesques.

A setting of Mallarme's "Un coup de des" is in many graphic design history books.

But by the same token, you could also use present day typefaces, if you associate yourself with a way of working, rather than particular typefaces.
