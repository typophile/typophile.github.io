For the sake of argument I will pick [[http://www.fonts.com/findfonts/detail.htm?pid=406034&grab_id=0&page_id=35232&query=Felbridge&SCOPE=Fonts|Felbridge Bold]] and say that it would match if you just widened it a bit. The truth is it could probably be many fonts, with no other letters for comparison. - Mike Yanega
