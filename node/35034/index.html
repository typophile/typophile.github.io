2010-01-13 11:07:03 • Nick Cooke

Adam means the following:

Q: I am having problems with the user interface of FontLab Studio 5 for Mac OS X. Some icons are missing or mislabeled, or some windows do not appear A: You can manually reset the user interface and the settings of FontLab Studio 5 for Mac OS X. For that, please quit FontLab Studio and then remove the files: ~/Library/Application Support/FontLab/Studio 5/Workspaces/CurrentWorkspace.rc ~/Library/Preferences/com.fontlab.fontlab5.plist After that, launch FontLab Studio and enter your serial number. Note: ~ stands for your user folder, e.g. /Users/JohnDoe/

One correction: you won’t be asked for your s/n but only your name. It is also good to trash the CurrentWorkspace.rc file after updating or downgrading FLS. ;)
