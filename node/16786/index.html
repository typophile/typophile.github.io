Hi, I’m going to be giving a presentation about designing identity system and I was thinking of staring my presentation with a famous quote or some words of wisdom by a designer, typographer, etc. regarding designing a an identity system or even design discipline in general. Ironically, Erik Spiekermann provided me with some quotes relating to this subject just earlier this evening. I hope he won't mind my sharing them here. 'You cannot not communicate' - Paul Watzlawik 'You are what you are seen to be' - Erik Spiekermann 'Appearances are not false' (Nur der Schein trügt nicht) - Paul Klee 'Form is the outside of content, and content is the inside of form' - Jean-Luc Godard Of course, one implication of all these quotes is that identity design won't do anything for you if you suck.
