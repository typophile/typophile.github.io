Might be unique to the game. I take it your main interest is in trying to match the lower case. There are some fonts with squared lower case letters but most are incompatible with your g, r, s, and y, even at this small scale.

Here are three pay fonts that might work “out of the box.” DeLuxe Gothic http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/alphabetsoup/deluxe-gothic/ ArchiType http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/archiness/architype/ FF District http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/fontfont/district/

And four runners up. Foundry Gridnik http://www.linotype.com/1284323/FoundryGridnikStdMedium-product.html Kimberley http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/typodermic/kimberley/ Industry http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/hold-fast-foundry/industry/ Vox http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/canadatype/vox/
