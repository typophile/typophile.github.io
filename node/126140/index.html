Unexpected Enlightenment

May 16, 2017 • JCSalomon

Looking for this font, used in the chapter headings of L. Jagi Lamplighter’s Rachel Griffin books. In the title pages, this seems to have been tweaked, but it’s definitely a font not hand-lettering. WhatTheFont and Identifont have not helped.
Other font ID sites found nothing, but I just discovered WhatFontIs.com which suggested Ray Meadows’s RM Victoriana. This is very close, except for a few details (top serifs on the T, cross-bar of the A, tail on the N)—I’m guessing this was a slightly tweaked variant of that font.
2017-07-05 20:39:53 • JCSalomon
A bit more searching, and it turns out I had it backwards: the font in use is Ray Meadows’s RM Ginger, which he later polished and turned into RM Victoriana.
