I agree with Epsilicon, but if you want to keep the power button theme then I suggest you have a look at it because the G looks a lot like an Euro symbol (except the Euro symbol has two horizontal stripes, instead of one), or in other words, it strikes me more like an E than a G. So I suggest looking into that (maybe make the bottom half of the G come a bit more up or make it connect with the horizontal part of G).

I also suggest to increase the space between smarty & grants, because (to me at least) it seems to be one word, instead of two. (or was that intentional?
