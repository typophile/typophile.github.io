Hi All!

I’m a thesis student at OCAD (Ontario College of Art and Design) in Toronto, Canada, and I’m trying to start up a typeface foundry at our school called Xheight.

The foundry will promote, nurture and distribute student typeface design through the web. There’s also a printed annual component.

The main content will be typefaces that students have designed for the school’s type design courses, other design courses and on their own time. As the site grows, other content may include visual typographic experimentations (such as typographic illustrations or novel use of typefaces) and writings on the subject of typography.

The community will be centered around helping improve each other’s typefaces through critical feedback, ratings, and advise. Students can recognition by having an outlet to distribute their typefaces for use by the public.

We are also looking to get the school faculty involved in curating feature typefaces, as to add a layer of credibility to that process.
