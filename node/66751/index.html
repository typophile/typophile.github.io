I believe this may be a custom(ized) font. That M may be two inverted V’s. The widths of some letters seem to have been altered. The R is a strange shape and may have been drawn by adding a leg to a P. If I were going to try to re-create this, assuming no one finds an actual font, I would start with [[http://new.myfonts.com/fonts/processtype/bryant/|Bryant]]. It’s basic feeling and letterform shapes are close, and might not take much manipulation to match pretty well.
