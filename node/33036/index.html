You have come to one of - or maybe the place to ask I think. There are plenty of solid type designers here.

Some questions: Do you want original designs based on reference models or do you have the rights to existing artwork and want it placed in a font? How many items do you need done? How will the font be used? For a magazine? Inkjet? Or to help with designs in ceramic or cloth? The use is important to know to get the design technically right. When do you need it to be complete?

Feel free to work out what it is you want further here.

BTW: This is something I would be ready & interested in doing. Certainly there are others here who might also be willing as well. If you like, click on my name to go to my profile & contact me with details. Then I can offer you a quote.
