Pricing is so tricky - it's frustrating to have to buy a whole family when you only need one weight or style. House Industries makes this particularly difficult. On the other hand, you usually get much more value by buying a family. I try to consider whether I think it's a font/family I will get a lot of use out of in the future, in which case I don't mind forking out the extra dough, or a one-shot, in which case I usually pass and seek a less expensive alternative, or a single weight. Or a font I already have. Kind of the same way I approach buying shoes.

Usually when I am on the hunt for a new typeface, I am looking for a specific weight or something that will fit in a certain space. However, more often than not, I'll license as many weights as I can, or be fine with licensing the family, as I will be able to use the other weights later.
