2009-06-30 18:15:13 • Stephen Coles
Found an old GarageFonts catalog, and I remember how much I love this family, despite its amateurish quirks. Now that he’s matured, I hope Joshua remodels and reissues it. For posterity, those seeking Profundis should probably contact [[GarageFonts]] or [[Joshua Darden]] directly. If you’re looking for alternates, there really isn’t anything that comes close, but Barnbrook’s Priori, Mason, and Tourette have similarities in their ornament. The Sans and Serif are based on classical models so faces in the Optima and Trajan genres could work as replacements.

Profundis is a gorgeous concept. I have been waiting for years for Josh to finish the super black weights. I think it would seriously take off in popularity if it were ever spruced up and put back out for public consumption.

I’m a fan of these Barnbrook faces, too, some beautifully modeled on unexpected architectural forms. But Profundis was my first Darden swoonfest and, as Stephen says, there’s really nothing like it.
