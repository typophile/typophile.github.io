Newt Serif released with Veer

January 6, 2009 • Number3Pencils

Hi! How's it going! I'm excited. Because my first font [[http://www.veer.com/search/results.aspx?keyword=Newt%20Serif&producttype=TYP%2CIMG%2CILL|is now on the market]] with Veer! I developed Newt Serif with the help of the Typophile Critique forum ([[http://www.typophile.com/salamander|here]]). After a long time, it's finally come to fruition. The character set includes Basic Latin, Latin-1, and Latin Extended-A codepages, along with a few other characters here and there. There are four weights -- Light, Regular, Demi, and Bold, each with an italic. Thanks for the help, everyone! I hope you like it.
The results are quite charming! Many congrats, and may it bring you some bacon. hhp
