Google found several things. First, there’s a bug in Word’s handling of JPGs from Photoshop. Supposedly, there was a fix for both Word 2004 and 2008; do you have all the updates? Other found suggestions: (1) use a TIF instead of a JPG; (2) open the JPG in a third program (e.g., Preview) and resave it; (3) convert from CYMK to RGB.

If you use Word’s insert picture from file, does your JPG show up as a thumbnail in the dialog box?
