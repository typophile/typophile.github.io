Visible from space?

May 13, 2005 • John Hudson

Today’s National Post featured this image of Jebel Ali ‘Palm Island’ being built on the coast of Dubai. As you can see, some of the floating islands are in the shape of Arabic text, making up what must surely be the largest piece of lettering in the world.

I’ve heard it said that size doesn’t matter (but I haven’t heard that from a typophile).

According to the palm’s website the “elevated retreat homes linked by boardwalks” spell out a passage from a verse by H.H. Sheikh Mohammed bin Rachid Al Maktoum, “Take wisdom from the wise – not everyone who rides a horse is a jockey.”

Helpful, if you thought all horse riders were jockies.
