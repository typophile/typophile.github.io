body: "Gallery 51 of North Adams, Massachusetts announces a juried exhibition of contemporary
  letterpress work to be held from March 22 through April 23, 2007. \r\n\r\nWe invite
  submissions from printers, studios and designers who have produced work with letterpress
  technology within the past decade. Download your entry form online at www.contemporaryletterpress.com.
  Provide the information required and send it to us with samples of your work.\r\n\r\nThe
  deadline for submitting work is February 28 , 2007. \r\n\r\nEntries will be judged
  by a panel of distinguished curators of contemporary art, design professionals and
  educators. Selected work will be exhibited at the Gallery 51 space and presented
  online afterwards.\r\n\r\nThe exhibition seeks to showcase a full range of work
  from traditional print to web-based projects and focus on the artistic merits of
  the medium. We will be presenting work of the highest caliber and aim to raise awareness
  of craft of letterpress printing.\r\n\r\nPlease feel free to email us with any questions.
  We look forward to seeing your work.\r\n\r\nwww.contemporaryletterpress.com"
