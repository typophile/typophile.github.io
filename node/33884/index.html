Hi all, I am currently in a job which is primarily a PA for a finance company. However I have taken on a new role of redesigning Powerpoint tutorials which are converted into online tutorials. Does anyone have any good links about designing in Powerpoint or online tutorials. I would like to avoid the usual cheesy clip art and hope to redesign these learning materials in an attractive and modern way.
I took a technical writing course this winter, and there was a section on PowerPoint presentations. Try to keep the text concise - maximum 6 lines per slide, 6 words per line. Colours should be high-contrast, and the text should be in a font that is readable on-screen. Graphics should be relevant to the text. As for avoiding cheesy clip art, avoid cheesy clip art. - Lex
