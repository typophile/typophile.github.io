Fracmetrica Black .otf

January 26, 2010 • jakob runge

the free-font »Fracmetrica Black« is a modern and geometric blackletter with several opentype-features like ligatures, case-sensitives, text figures. It’s kind of condensed and has a high contrast. The typeface’s construction is based on an isometric 60°-grid.

[img:sites/default/files/old-images/jakob_p2_fracmetrica_intro_3878.jpg]

Created by Jakob Runge 2009 during the [[http://gestaltung.fh-wuerzburg.de/blogs/werk_statt_villa/?cat=6|Workshop »fancy_fonts_&_porno_pattern« at the university of applied sciences würzburg]]
