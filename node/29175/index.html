This is a very good question. Some applications, such as InDesign, group fonts without traditional style linking. However, other applications such as MS Word still rely on style linking to chose the correct face.

The advantage of separate Suitcases is that they can be activated separately. It would be nice is separated fonts worked well in all applications as well.

2006-10-30 23:00:56 • canderson
When you pull fonts out of suitcase files or move them between suitcase files, the family information (the FOND resource) travels with them. So, yes, in the pre-OS X Mac Finder (or for that matter, the old Font/DA Mover), you never need to worry about fonts losing their style and family bindings when you move individual fonts around.
