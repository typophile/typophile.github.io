Good find Kevin. Another interesting Art Deco style serifed font digitized during the first wave of making digital fonts [1991] by a font-maker – Sterling Court Publishing – who left no records as to provenance. I found nothing matching from other sources. Note some really unique letters, like the J that looks identical to the T but with a backwards facing tail. If anyone wants more fonts from Sterling Court Publishing Luc Devroye’s page http://luc.devroye.org/fonts-36181.html has a font list. Unfortunately his link to a package download no longer works. See also http://luc.devroye.org/fonts-36183.html

UPDATE Some or all of the fonts by Jim Pearson as Sterling Court Publishing are here http://www.fontage.com/foundry/jpearson.html Don
