2009-04-14 17:16:43 • Nick Shinn

I just finished one for sustainability publication for my local government. I used Priva Pro, and have to say it worked out really well. The four weights (plus italics) really offered up a lot of flexibility throughout the various documents., whether it was for a headline or text paragraph.

I originally thought it would be too rigid and cold, but after using it on both a 4c executive summary and a down & dirty 200+ page b/w document (over 100 pages of tables, sweet!) I found the end result to be quite refreshing. I was initially concerned at using a sans-serif on pages set pretty solid as they have a tendency to overwhelm the eyes, but it actually displayed some good humanist characteristics even at the smaller text sizes.
