Aaron needs to know if the job is:

one-sided or two-sided, known in printer-talk as 4 over 1, or 4 over zero, if the job is 4 colors on one side, and either 1 color (usually black) or no color on the other side;

the size if the print run, like a thousand copies or ten thousand copies;

the cut size of the paper, like 6 inches by 9 inches (standard for books), or 8.5 inches by 11 inches (like many magazines), or 11 inches by 17 inches (like many newspapers);

the quality of the paper, such as thickness and finish (glossy, flat, or matted);

trims and folds.

All these factors effect the quote.
