Interesting. I’d never had any reason to use Google Books before. Using the links supplied above by Manlio and John, I easily found two of the few books I’ve ever designed in Adobe Caslon.

One is “Boosters, Hustlers, and Speculators,” by Jocelyn Wills:

http://books.google.com/books?id=8OH2OdbE5FgC&pg=PA292&dq=%22adobe+caslon%22&lr=#PPP1,M1

Google only shows the dubble-col backmatter notes and bibliography, though, no text pages. (It is a business history of Minneapolis & St. Paul: yawn).

The other is a book of wonderful poems by Mary Leader, “Red Signature” (one of the most enjoyable design projects I’ve ever had). This does not seem to show any text pages, though Amazon does (poorly).

Since Taryn is near a university, I bet an inter-library loan could get these books easily enough.
