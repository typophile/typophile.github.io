2007-03-23 21:59:12 • sandrosandro
On the whole I like it. I agree that the leg of the 'R' extends too far. I also think that if you bring the arm in, the bowl should be made wider to compensate for this. 'RA' is always a tricky kern because of the opposing angles of the 'R' leg and the 'A'. A wider bowl on the 'R' would fix this, as would loosening the kerning of the rest of the logo a bit. I actually like the short crossbar on the 'E' - it gives some air to the "EL" pair. One last thing - watch the curves at the top of the 'R' bowl where the straight section meets the curved section. The curve is looking a little lumpy and might need to be smoothed out. Otherwise, great job. - Lex
