Pls correct me if I am wrong. Is TheSerif by Lucas de Groot a Slab Serif?
The difference between a slab and a serif can sometimes be quite subjective. In TheSerif’s case i’d definitely say slab given the shape of the serifs. I believe the name comes from the fact that it has serifs of any sort, and they just happen to be slab serifs. TheAntiqua is the true oldstyle serif in the Thesis family.
