It looks like the typeface called "Becker Series" from the Inland Type Foundry of St. Louis in the late 1800's. It is shown in the 'Treasury of Authentic Art Nouveau Alphabets' by Ludwig Petzendorfer (Dover Books). Dieter Steffmann has digitized many of these blackletter styles, including Becker. - Mike Yanega
