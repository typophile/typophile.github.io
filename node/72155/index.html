I think Kermit's advice is good. As another approach look at Part 12 of the Script Font ID Guide (Childish lettering) for 'Young". 'Dumb' fonts might be in Part 14 or Part 5, but you're on your own for what you consider has a 'dumb' quality. If I was to pick a 'dumb' font one choice I'd consider is Doodlebug, but 'Dumb' means different things to different people. You may have to show your client some choices for that one. - Mike Yanega
