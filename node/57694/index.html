The other one is Rotis Semi Serif Black Schoolbook with the alternate Venetian ‘e’.

No, seriously: this looks rather like an unbalanced homebrew mix of a number of different typefaces. Some characters/terminals suggest it might have started out with Rotis in mind once, cf. its Semi Serif Bold (there is no Rotis Black, and of course no ‘Schoolbook’ style with single-storey ‘a’ either).

For a lusty semi-serif, have a look at Areplos by [[Jan Solpera]].
