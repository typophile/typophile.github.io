body: "Estimo is an unusual, yet elegant type family of three styles in five weights.
  Originally developed as upper-case-only family, Estimo was inspired by the works
  of Bulgarian type and graphic designers in 1980\u2019s and especially the philatelic
  works of Stefan Kanchev (late 70's early 80's). It is characterized by its lack
  of diagonal strokes (wherever possible), thus experimenting with letterforms without
  losing legibility.\r\n\r\nAdditional links:\r\nBehance http://bit.ly/behestimo\r\nMyFonts
