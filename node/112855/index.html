Can’t state if they used it but you can go with [[http://www.dafont.com/superstar-m54.font|Superstar M54]], slightly squooshed. It looks to match all earmarks: /M centre vertex pointy, serifless /E median crossbar, square counters, serifless /C and /S… Alternatives: [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/nicksfonts/joe-college-nf|Joe College NF]] (wrong /M), [[http://www.myfonts.com/search/princetown|Princetown]] (wrong /M as spotted), [[http://www.houseind.com/fonts/unitedcollection|United]], [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/thomas-kaeding/undergrad|Undergrad]], [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/jnlevine/return-pass|Return Pass JNL]] (possibly the closest, everything is present, including boldness and wideness)
