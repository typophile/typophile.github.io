Numbers problem

March 18, 2009 • mili

Someone I know asked about this font. It’s some kind of Garamond, I suppose, but I haven’t been able to pin it down exactly. Can you?

As far as I could find I don’t think this is Garamond, Granjon or Sabon, although the last is the closest. In most cases the 5 is wrong, or the 6 is not fully closed, or the 3 shape isn’t right. Galliard is another possibility. I am having trouble finding sites that show the full range of glyphs, including OSF numerals, so it is hard to confirm any hunches. I am using FontShop’s FontBook98, because it includes Adobe and Berthold fonts. It also seems that Caslon may be a candidate family for these numerals, although the OSF bold 0 seems to have a round counter.

Do you know the age of this sample. Am I seeing inkspread, or a poor scan?
