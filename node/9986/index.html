Hi everyone,

This might sound like a very basic question, but there goes:
I have designed a custom lettering for a logo project and I would like to present it in 2 more weights- light and medium (the current one being bold as I see it). What is the easiest way to modify the exisiting outlines into lighter versions?
There is no "easy way" to do a decent job. I have tried the assorted methods in FontLab and Fontographer and find it is faster and better to just draw it in the new weight than to try to clean up the mess the "easy way" makes.
