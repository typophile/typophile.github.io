If you haven’t already been through it, Grid Systems in Graphic Design by Josef Müller-Brockmann is probably the best historical source you’ll find—a great piece of high modernism.

Making and Breaking the Grid by Timothy Samara is a decent book about Grids in practice, and has a few bits of history in there as well. Maybe not the best book research though, it’s more of a guided tour for brainstorming ideas. Still, with full color and bleeds, it’s pretty amazing that this can be had at under $20. There’s plenty more also, I’m sure you’ll get plenty of good input.

Good luck!
