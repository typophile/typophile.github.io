2007-11-16 07:47:43 • David Rault

Hey David, I’m interested in Evert’s work, too. What I found so far are chapters from Dutch Type (available in full-text at Google Books) and Made with FontFont (announcement, review). There’s also the wiki page about FF Legato here on Typophile that links to the About FF Legato PDF. Me and Joe enquired briefly about Evert to Luc(as) de Groot in Brighton, but he doesn’t seem to know much more than we do. Jan Middendorp, the author of Dutch Type might be the person to get in touch with about it. I also read that Evert used to publish a lot of articles about his theories in type design, but I can’t find any (or I’m not looking in the right places, as I don’t speak any Dutch).

Since you’re working on typography in France, I would suggest you take a close look at FF Balance as well if you haven’t already, because it stems from Bloemsma’s fascination with Roger Excoffon’s work on Antique Olive.

Do let us know. Cheers!
