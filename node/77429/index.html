The typeface is Bell Bottom. For your own self respect, try to to evoke the 1960s without piling cliché upon cliché. There are many “hippy” fonts that you could work with. For instance, why not use Mojo in Illustrator, bending it to resemble a Wes Wilson poster? Alternatively, cue up Live Dead, take a couple bong hits, and start drawing.
