2007-07-28 03:04:19 • bankbot

Before digital there was lead type. That’s what Lex means by metal. Unfortunately, it’s near impossible to find “exact” digital matches of metal type. A lot of it has to do with the fact that you’re looking at a metal type impression and offset printing works so differently.

One thing you could do for stouter caps is use Futura small caps with the <a href=“http://www.fontshop.com/fonts/singles/paratype/futura_medium_ot/&sample_text=special%20microphone&sample_size=36'>lower case. But that’s probably too stout.
