Legibility vs. Readability?

November 20, 2012 • npgraphicdesign

I feel like sometimes it's as bad a topic (that requires explanation) as the 'what is resolution' question. Simply put, does this sound about right? Legibility - How well you can tell apart the different glyphs Readability - How well those glyphs work together and how well they read when assembled into words, sentences, etc. Or does someone have a better definition?
