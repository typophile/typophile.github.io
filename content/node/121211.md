  perfect pangram out of current chemical element symbols, but it is possible using
  two disused ones. Unq, for unnilquadium, now known as rutherfordium, Rf, is in every
  pangram, as it is only one of two chemical symbols with a Q. The two Us in Uuq (ununquadium,

  Hf), or Jo (for joliotium/dubnium, Db).\r\n\r\nHere is one of many possibilities
