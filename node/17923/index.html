According to Martin Kotulla, SoftMaker is finally updating the lowercase letter "y" in altered glyph form to have no extra line at all for "F761 Deco" (an actual clone of Florentine), which appears to be just like FontBank's Flourish and Southern Software's Toque Display SSi/SSK. And yet, SoftMaker is finally creating "G855 Blackletter" in the design of Florentine, numbered from Gyros Gotisch, and comes in "regular" and "dfr" layouts. Those two font names are finally appearing on "MegaFont XXL 3.0" (for home users) and "infiniType 4" (for designers) - both premium-quality typeface collections from "SoftMaker Software GmbH" that are finally being released around May 27, 2014.
