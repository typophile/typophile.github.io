Looks like a photolettering era version of a 19th century heavy Clarendon or "Antique" [Now called Egyptian] As for digital fonts, I can give you several with a similar ultra heavy Clarendon leg on the _R_ which would give you an approximation 0f _MOZART_ and thick slab serifs but other details would need to be edited for a closer match: the thin strokes on lhs of _M_ and _A_; the clipped corners to make the serifs angular; and the octagonal _O_.
