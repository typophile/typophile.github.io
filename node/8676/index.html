Here is a story from David Earls' interview with Tankard in his book 'Designing Typefaces':

"I designed Disturbance for my RCA [Royal College of Art] thesis during the holiday. The lecturers found it 'too distrubing to do this to the alphabet' hence the name! After graduation I redrew the type for FontShop International in 1993. It still needs redrawing - bad curves etc - but it's a legal nightmare to redo it."

See the book for more of the interview.
