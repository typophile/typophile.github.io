FontClock

May 30, 2007 • Duckworth

How cool is this? I’ve just found that the sister company to the design agency I work for started stocking Font Clocks, like the office clocks you’d get in banks here in the UK, but they’ve got 12 fonts that cycle through and mingle together rather than purely Helvetica. As far as I know, there’s three sizes (I’ve got a mid-size one on order, the big one’s too big for my house!)As far as I know, I don’t think icône can deliver to the US :( but I know they deliver Europe-wide…
Well, I love “flip clocks” to begin with, so this is definitely an eye catcher. But in all honousty, I prefer the “clean” look of the all Helvetica ones. Still, I wouldn’t turn of of these babies down ;-)
