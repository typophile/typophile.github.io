  can be summoned by means of the [[Phinn-signal]]. Designer of the typeface Hypatia
  Sans.\r\n\r\nPhinney has an MS in Graphic Arts Printing, specializing in design
  and typography, from the Rochester (NY) Institute of Technology, and an MBA from
  the University of California at Berkeley.\r\n\r\nFor most of his time at Adobe,
