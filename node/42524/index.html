Times Ten has SC & OSF
2008-02-26 15:51:46 • Chris Dean
Your best bet is the Adobe OpenType Times Ten LT Std, where you can switch globally to old style figures (in Quark 7), plus the extra character glyphs usual with OpenType. Do not get their Times LT Std or Times New Roman MT Std bundles; they don’t have OSFs. The Times Ten is also, for my eyes, the biggest, crispest, and most legible Times on the market. Check the glyph coverage at the Adobe type website.
