Help with these two European newspaper titles: blackletter mastheads of Aftenposten and FAZ - custom

January 24, 2010 • cky

Greetings type enthusiasts. On a recent trip to Europe I was browsing the newsstands and came across these two lovely typefaces. I thought they were great. I’d like to buy them/download them if possible for my own uses and I was hoping someone out there could help me identify them. Much thanks in advance.
I don’t think that there is an exact match for the masthead of »Frankfurter Allgemeine Zeitung« (FAZ). You might browse Dieter Steffmann’s [[http://moorstation.org/typoasis/designers/steffmann/index.htm|collection]] for a start. For the headlines within the paper, FAZ employed a special version of Fette Gotisch called »FAZ Fraktur« made by [[http://www.urwpp.de/deutsch/home.html|URW]], which unfortunately is not used anymore since about two years.
