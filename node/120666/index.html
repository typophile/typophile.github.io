IMHO these letters, whether or not made into a formal font are made by compressing the top 2/3 of a font vertically and stretching the bottom 1/3 vertically – not necessarily to the same degree or using the same method. Consider Kuzanyan https://www.myfonts.com/fonts/paratype/kuzanyan/ Some further torturing of the letterforms produce a match. Try it yourself. There are probably other fonts that would work better as a starting point. Kepler and Utopia are not quite as close as Kuzanyan. Other candidates? Don
