Has anyone tried Kalliculator [[http://kalliculator.com]] or LivePen [[http://01-lab.com/livepen.html]]?

I think this is a great idea, though by no means do I think they will be able to generate fully-formed quality typefaces. They do seem like a great tool for generating consistent contrasts and making it possible to test a typeface’s color at dozens of weights before committing to anything.

Has anyone had the chance to try them out? I’m on CS3, so I haven’t been able to try LivePen, and Kalliculator is yet to be released.

Frederik said that he’s working on the release of an opensource beta version, or something like that.
