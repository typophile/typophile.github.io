Thank you Tiff. That’s something I really never though of. It’s not easily acomplished tho.

  • Select two objects.

  • Path>Intersection

  • select and copy the intersection

  • undo until the two objects are in their original states

  • paste in place the intersection

  • change the original color of intersection to the desired blend color

  • Voilà

In Illustrator there must be an easier way right and certainly with more control. :-(

Making incremental changes requires you do all the steps again. I haven’t tried more than two colors but it does not look possible here.
