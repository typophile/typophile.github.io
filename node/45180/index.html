Any suggestions for body copy when using Aviano Bold? Your help is greatly appreciated. Thank you.
Often I have read that when you use a Serif for headlines/titles, then it is a good idea to use a sans for the body copy. For some candidates I looked at the Aviano letterforms and looked for a few sans faces with some of the same key structural traits (splayed M, long J, G chin, and R legs). Based on those guidelines I came up with Font Bureau's [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/fontbureau/agenda/|Agenda]], Typotheque's [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/typotheque/jigsaw/|Jigsaw]], and FontFont's [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/fontfont/seria-sans/|Seria Sans]]. Ultimately, you need to try a few things and see what you think works. Our judgment can't replace your own. - Mike Yanega
