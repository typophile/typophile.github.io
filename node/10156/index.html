2005-04-06 18:25:59 • Stephen Coles
That exact specimen is also in Rob Roy Kelly's "American Wood Type: 1828-1900" first published in 1969. The caption (not included in the ABZ book) reads: "Gothic Extended. This particular cutting made by the Hamilton Company after 1900. Lowercase is missing." Wood type Gothics like this go back to the 1840s or earlier.
