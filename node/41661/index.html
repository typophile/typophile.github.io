Handlettered. The fat numbers could have been inspired by something like Lo-Type. BTW: Mr Fokker was the ultimate opportunist. Being Dutch he took the German nationality to further his interests during WW I and made a fortune during that war, using a lot of other people’s ideas in his products (as in building the first inline, synchronised machine-gun). After the war he moved his assets to Holland, started a plane factory there and was (amongst other things) the first to build a monococque passenger airplane (beating Mr. Douglas by years, my US-friends). He appears to have been not a very nice person.
