[[Indices]] : [[Organizations]] : TypeOff

Small collective of designers from the city of Offenbach, Germany. Founded in June, 2004.

Current members include [[David Borchers]], [[Lara Glueck]], [[Till Hopstock]], [[Tanja Huckenbeck]], [[Peter Reichard]], [[Dan Reynolds]], and [[Lukas Schneider]].

Five of the members study, or have studied, at the [[HfG Offenbach]]. Three of them intern, or have interned, at [[Linotype]]. Two of them work at [[Typosition]], where they also produce the magazine [[Spatium]].

They sponsor a monthly drinking fest called the [[Typostammtisch]]. All designers who happen to be in the Frankfurt-area at the time are invited.

Their website is TypeOff.de.
