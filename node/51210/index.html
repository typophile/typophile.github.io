THIS FORUM WILL SPECIALISE IN THE PROBLEMS OF iNDIC FONTS. MOREOVER THE FORUM WILL ALLOW FONTOGRAPHERS AND CALLIGRAPHERS TO PUT UP LINKS TO THEIR SITES FOR STUDYING FONTS WHICH THEY HAVE DESIGNED. FINALLY UNICODE AND OPEN TYPE FONT DESIGN WHICH ARE CLOSELY TIED UP WILL ALSO BE STUDIED
