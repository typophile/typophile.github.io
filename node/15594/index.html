[[Indices]] : [[Foundries]] : KLIM Kia ora! KLIM is a typographic design studio run by [[Kris Sowersby]]. The KLIM studio has been set up to deal specifically with typographic issues within the realm of graphic design. If you need something that is typographic by nature, then I am at your service. A thing well made… …is a song by legendary New Zealand band The Mutton Birds. ‘When a man holds a thing well made, there is a connection, there is completeness…’ This philosophy is at the very heart of my approach to typographic design—for art is long and life is short.
