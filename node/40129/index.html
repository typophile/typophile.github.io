Logo for Cookie Company

December 17, 2007 • smongey

Below is a logo I designed for a homemade cookie company called “Love & Enjoy”. The company is quite small-time so the logo would be applied by rubber stamp on the packaging and stationary to further emphasis the homemade nature of the products they supply. I’m still unsure whether the logo or the concept is there or not and would appreciate some constructive criticism as to the direction it could be taken.

It’s a very good design concept but if you want to emphasize “homemade” and “small-time” you’ll want to move away from Helvetica.
