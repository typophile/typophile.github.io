Custom, or heavily customized if it was based on a single typeface. Some typefaces that—with a little editing—could mimic the look:

Seconda Soft Standard Bold: http://www.fonts.com/font/durotype/seconda-soft/basic-pack

Barmeno Bold: http://www.fonts.com/font/berthold/barmeno/be-volume

Contax Sans 85 Black: http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/typeinnovations/contax-sans/

Epoca Classic Bold: http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/hoftype/epoca-classic/
