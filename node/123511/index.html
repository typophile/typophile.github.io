It appears to be a modified version of Stratum. However the type also appears in other materials as well, not just the logo, which leads me to think a typeface exist. It's like Stratum and Serpentine have been merged together. Has it just been modified or does a typeface exist?
Definitely based on Stratum 1 (this /W and those numerals can't lie) and massively used on their [[http://realalebrewing.com|website]] with [[http://www.fontbureau.com/fonts/Vonness|Vonness]] Condensed. I also don't know any "Stratum-like with flare serifs" like they did. You may like some fonts by Mattox Shuller of [[http://www.myfonts.com/foundry/Fort_Foundry|Fort Foundry]] like Industry, Industry Inc. and Gin (some sort of "condensed Stratum-like with latin serifs") that looks pretty appropriated for such branding identity.
