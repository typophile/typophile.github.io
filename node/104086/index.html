Copperplate Gothic: what has this to do with Eminem and hip hop? It’s an old face that was used for small text on biz cards 100 years ago, and more recently at display size because it was bundled with a lot of apps, and people didn’t know any better. The problem is that the teats on it are too big at display size, giving such typography a clumsy appearance. So the experts will always consider its use in display as inappropriate. But if your client likes the way it looks, feel free to ignore them!
