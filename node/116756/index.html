Chevalier was designed by Emil Alfred Neukomm for Haas’sche Schriftgiesserei in Münchenstein, Switzerland in 1946. Named after the French actor Maurice Chevalier. Softmaker cloned it as Maurice. Legitimate versions, as cited above, by Linotype, Letter Perfect and URW +++. There was also a Berthold version, which seems not to be currently available. Don
