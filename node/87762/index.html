November 26, 2011 • cirdan

I'm looking for the right Bembo. I've checked out Bembo Book and Bembo MT. Bembo MT is a touch too thin, and Bembo Book's x-height is too low for my liking---I prefer Aldine 401's proportions, and Bergamo's even more. Neither Aldine 401 nor Bergamo are dark enough to go well, as far as I can see, with Gill, which is what I'd like to pair them with. I would be writing articles, and the occasional book. I guess what I'm looking for is a dark Bembo with Bergamo's proportions (or similar).* Any ideas? *I've heard of Berthold Bembo, and looked at some pdfs, but I don't know how to get it.
You may give Andron a try. It’s not an actual Bembo, but it was inspired by Bembo. And it was designed even to produce a proper grade of darkness on the page, which other Bembos (you mentioned) fail to do. – Any questions welcome.
