Claes,

Instead of looking for only 3 families, you should look for foundries who have educational discounts. I would email the foundries who have the typefaces you/they would most like to license and ask if they have any discounts for education. You should also consider CD collections insead of just families. For instance, Storm Type Foundry has the several different collections which has a bevy of differenty typefaces.
