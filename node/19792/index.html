What’s this?… Play the video and watch the titles. Don’t say Futura.

The main title is set in some predigital version of Koch’s sans. The more recent Kabels from Linotype and ITC are similar, but more regularized, lacking idiosyncrasies like the “upside-down” cap S.

Credits could be in any predigital Futura or Futura copy such as Twentieth Century. Lanston’s No. 206 version is the closest match I find. Here is the most recent digitization of this version by the folks at P22, but it is missing the italics.
