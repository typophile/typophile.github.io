It’s the Premier League proprietary font (called Premier League!) and is used on all shirts (not just Everton’s).

It’s a custom design by The Brand Union (possibly when it was still Enterprise IG). Click on the flash and you can see all the letters. Also a very interesting project sketchbook to look at.
