body: "The <a href=\"http://museum.antwerpen.be/plantin_moretus/index_eng.html\">Plantin
  Moretus Museum</a> is located at the <em>Vrijdagmarkt</em> in Antwerp, Belgium.\r\n\r\nThe
  Museum is the only printing-office/publisher from the Renaissance- and Barokperiod
  that is kept till this day. Plantin Moretus is the continuation of the <em>Officina
  Plantiniana</em>, founded in 1555 by <em>Christoffel Plantin</em>, the first industrial
  printer in history.<!--break-->\r\n\r\nThe unique typographic treasure chamber holds
  two of the world's oldest printing presses.\r\n\r\nThe old library houses the most
  complete overview of the <em>Officina Plantiniana's</em> production from the 16th
  to the 19th century.\r\n\r\n<ul>\r\n<li><strong>4 September 2001:</strong> the Plantin-Moretus
  archives are added to the list of <em>Memory of the world program</em>, because
  of it's possession of the oldest documents about the art of book-printing and as
  a reference to some of the most important cultural and social tendencies of the
  16th century.</li>\r\n<li><strong>15 July 2005:</strong> Unesco adds the historical
  monument, including all its collections and archives, to the list as <em>Complex
  woning-ateliers-museum Plantin-Moretus</em></li>\r\n</ul>\r\n\r\n<a href=\"http://museum.antwerpen.be/plantin_moretus/index_eng.html\">Plantin
  Moretus Museum website</a>"
