Brandon Grotesque font combination

September 4, 2014 • Jean Louis

I’m considering a Brandon G./Arial combination for a project I am working on. It works for [[http://www.spacex.com/]]. However, I’m not quite convinced about Arial (which has been reinforced by this [[http://typophile.com/node/119103]] interesting discussion).

What I think works pro-Arial is that the font is so neutral that the Brandon headlines are empahised, but that’s about it.

For some specific parts of text I’m using Brandon as well, but the major part will be another font. I’ve considered a Google workhorse like Open Sans or Source Sans Pro, but as they are less “clean” than Arial, the contrast with the headlines is somewhat softened.

Any suggestions?

Another advantage of Arial, in this case, is that you don’t need to serve it as webfont.
