As for Verdana and Tahoma, both are Matthew Carter designs. Tahoma's letters are narrower and it has tighter letter spacing. There is a still narrower version called Nina. Verdana Pro from Ascender has a Black weight in addition to the Bold. Compare: [img:sites/default/files/old-images/VerdanaProBoldInUse_5302.gif][img:sites/default/files/old-images/VerdanaProBlackInUse_3879.gif] http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/ascender/verdana-pro/ If I was attempting to duplicate the _CORKTOWN_ sign I would create an intermediate weight of these two fonts. -------- UPDATE: However, IIRC, when white letters are placed on a dark background they appear thinner than when dark letters are placed on a dark background. It's worth converting Verdana Black to a white on dark version and comparing it to the _CORKTOWN_ sign. Don
