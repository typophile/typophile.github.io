The magazine was discontinued and the staff was fired in June 2007. The title cover shown above is that of the last but one issue. In the final issue all members of the editorial staff were depicted one by one with private photos and short reports saying goodbye to the readers of this magazine which had been published for a period of sixteen years. Hearing that they would shut down, I bought the final copy out of curiosity, but dumped it later into the dustbin, for it was not worth storing it for historial purposes, as there are too many computer mags that went broke in recent years.

A PDF file of the final edition (22 page sample, typeset in Minion and Frutiger) is downloadable here
