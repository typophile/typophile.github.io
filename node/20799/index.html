This one of my latest ones; Suomi Slab Serif. A friend of mine started (h)ranting that American Typwriter is too lanky and had everything much too round and overly polished, so I started buggering about with the idea of Egyptienne/Slab Serif version of American Typewriter...[img:sites/default/files/old-images/SuomiSlabSerif_6528.png]
