AUSTRALIA, if it is a font, might be found by looking for fonts tagged _decayed_, _destroyed_, _eroded_, _damaged_ etc. The base font looks like some interpretation of Morris Fuller Benton’s Stymie Extra Bold -- note stub slab serifs and straight inner leg on _R_ [img:sites/default/files/old-images/StymieExtraBoldAsAUSTRALIA_4649.gif] http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/bitstream/stymie/ Sol Hess designed a variation of Stymie Extrabold with squared corners where round shapes would normally be, Squareface [img:sites/default/files/old-images/LTCSquarefaceAsAUSTRALIA_6619.gif] http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/lanston/ltc-square-face/ It could also be an edited version of Aachen Bold [img:sites/default/files/old-images/AachenBoldAsAUSTRALIA_5208.gif] http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/itc/aachen/ Don

I agree that distressed versions of digital fonts created by another digital fontmaker are of very questionable legality. I know that if I was a the owner of the rights to Colossalis, and had the financial resources to protect the IP rights, I would sue anyone who used a distressed version in a commercial project without seeking my permission. The base font of "AUSTRALIA" must be [[http://www.findmyfont.com/index.php/fonts/font-preview?fset=Berthold%20%2F%20%28Commercial%29&ffam=Colossalis%20-%20Bold&fid=6a8c47e6c95d4964852d1c601b345009&fsize=60&text=AUSTRALIA&fit=1|Colossalis (Bold)]] by Berthold There are at least 3 distressed versions (it seems that they copied/cloned each-other) and I don't know what is the original (if one could ever call "original" a distressed version of a commercial font): 1. Distress - Copr.1996 Immortal Graphics 2. Disaster - Copyright 1988-1999 IBS -Future Corporation 3. Simulated Evolution - 1999 12design (randy humphries) www.i2design.net The 3rd one is more distressed than your sample and the 2nd one comes from a well-known & confirmed font-forgery company, so it's better to avoid using it on a commercial project. "on the back of a Truck" is [[http://www.findmyfont.com/index.php/fonts/font-preview?fset=Linotype%20%2F%20%28Commercial%29&ffam=Helvetica%20LT%20Pro%20-%20Black%20Condensed&fid=eb306a49817b626a7c65bf8398b6a8ed&fsize=60&text=on%20the%20back%20of%20a%20Truck&wrap=2|Helvetica (Black Condensed)]] "zezzco" is [[http://www.findmyfont.com/index.php/fonts/font-preview?fset=Dafont-7%20%2F%20%28Freemium%29&ffam=Space%20Age%20-%20Regular&fid=ce43c6569134811c6828717671d38916&fsize=60&text=zezzco&wrap=2|Space Age]] by Justin Callaghan [2002, Free for personal use]
