You can’t find it because it appears to be a hand-lettered logotype, not a typeface.

Similar: LHF Billhead 1900 http://www.letterheadfonts.com/fonts/billhead.php

Black Velvet 2 (which may very well have been used for “SWEET TEA,” with a few modifications): https://fontbundles.net/twicolabs-fontdation/11551-black-velvet-2
