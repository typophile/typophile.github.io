Hi Jure,

I think it is Alpha Headline by [[Cornel Windlin]]. From the Lineto website: “Mitsubishi Motors Europe acquired exclusive license for the ‘Alpha Headline' fonts for the duration of ten years, expiring at the end of 2012.”

Quite similar: Autoscape, also by Windlin BDR Mono by Lopetz/Büro Destruct The British license plate typeface: 1, 2 Trans Am by [[Lorenzo Geiger]]

Quality alternatives: United by [[House Industries]] Foundry Gridnik, inspired by [[Wim Crouwel]] FS Sinclair by [[Philip Garnham]] & [[Jason Smith]] Regular by [[Nik Thoenen]] Vafle by [[Tomáš Brousil]] Stratum by [[Eric Olson]]

Also, have a look at these older threads: http://typophile.com/node/49640 http://typophile.com/node/50414
