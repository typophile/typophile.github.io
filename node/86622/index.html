I am putting together a product catalogue for furniture. Catalogue will have large photos (mainly set on dark background) and some areas with copy. Look & feel: contemporary/minimalistic. I want to use all serif for header, sub and body.

Why not consider Fedra Serif then? It would look completely unified since you would use fonts from the Fedra typeface together.

Jigsaw Light might be too similar to Fedra Sans to serve as a good contrast for the sub-body text relationship. Irma Thin is slightly different, but still a sans and would work only if your headers are big enough to contrast from your body text. Both of these are sans-serif, though — did you have any serif choices you were looking at already?
