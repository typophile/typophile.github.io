What is this font used by design-theorist Christopher Alexander

July 6, 2011 • Evlyn

This font is from the article by design-theorist Christopher Alexander, related to the A NATURE OF ORDER book series. He is is well-known for the [[http://www.weepingash.co.uk/index.php?option=com_content&view=article&id=195&Itemid=17|15 elements of good style]], and it would be very interesting to know what font he himself uses in his books and articles, since the font is a design aspect as well.

He seems to use the same font in his books. I’ve tried to identify it using both myfonts.com and identifont.com, but alas, the font could not be found. The closest I got was [[http://new.myfonts.com/fonts/garagefonts/freight-text/|Freight Text]].

Looks like Adobe Serif being subbed in.
