Digital Type - HUH?

April 11, 2007 • Jackie Frant

I just had to share, because no one in my household would really appreciate this. A company that came through on my phone as Digital Type Sol just called - they have a special on SuperType… wahooo! LOL

It was a kid - who had no idea about typefaces, didn’t know his PC was an IBM clone - and… didn’t know what a .ttf was - but he was selling them – a whole graphic package…

Oh boy - he also let me know that they had a whole graphic arts team creating these “Super Fonts” - all original (yeah, sure…)

The last time I heard from these folks they had introduced Font Expert 3 – of which they were in shock that I owned #2. Then it came with a Super Font collection that seemed to be Brown Fox repackaged… oh well.

Thanks for listening…

That’s why they call it ‘graphic’ software Jackie; it usually makes one want to scream…
