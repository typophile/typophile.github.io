There are fonts having this “neon feeling” but the reverse is not usually true: The majority of neon signs are not actually based on existing fonts, mostly due to technical and construction restrictions. Having this in mind, you might like the following similar fonts:

#2 : “Cocktails” (http://i.imgur.com/XTNWG.png) [[http://www.findmyfont.com/index.php/fonts/font-preview?fset=Dafont-2&ffam=TGL%200-16%20-%20Regular&fid=4a170d976b90c99adfec61a30c408f6e&fstyle=i&fsize=60&text=Cocktails&fit=1|TGL 0-16]] by Peter Wiegel [[http://www.findmyfont.com/index.php/fonts/font-preview?fset=Typodermic&ffam=Lonsdale%20-%20Regular&fid=5a3dc784a2641051372e8b1043dd9d0f&fstyle=i&fsize=60&text=Cocktails&fit=1|Lonsdale]] by Ray Larabie (Typodermic)

#4 : “MACAU” (http://i.imgur.com/t1yPR.png) [[http://myfonts.us/td-V8KfeC/|Saloon Girl]] by Michael Hagemann (FontMesa, 2004)

#7: “Erotic Museum” (http://i.imgur.com/ydmOQ.png) [[http://www.findmyfont.com/index.php/fonts/font-preview?fset=ITC&ffam=Savoye%20Std%20-%20Regular&fid=34e41ec35a86f2f7d6a861cfe4b1523f&fsize=60&text=Erotic%20Museum&fit=1|Savoye Std]] by Alan Meeks (ITC, Linotype, Letraset) [[http://www.findmyfont.com/index.php/fonts/font-preview?fset=Dafont-1&ffam=CAC%20Pinafore%20-%20Regular&fid=b9a64496fa89457c970be32c6ab503bb&fstyle=i&fsize=60&text=Erotic%20Museum&fit=1|CAC Pinafore]]

#8 “ARCADE” (http://i.imgur.com/if1YZ.png) [[http://www.findmyfont.com/index.php/fonts/font-preview?fset=Adobe&ffam=Helvetica%20Rounded%20Black%20-%20Regular&fid=4acf9382e71e0c7c46295a393ad0df21&fsize=60&text=ARCADE&fit=1|Helvetica Rounded Black]]
