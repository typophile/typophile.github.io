2006-08-25 16:08:59 • Matt Squire

you should remove the battery by turning the screw thing on the bottom to release the battery.

on the back of the battery will be the battery model and serial number.

Check your numbers against the ones on www.apple.com/support/ Then select the link from ‘Exchange and Extensions’ and ‘Battery Exchange’

There you will find the models affected and the serial numbers affected.
