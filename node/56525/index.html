Nice, I like it. It looks quite similar to the light weight I’m designing for my typeface [[http://typophile.com/node/37075|Furet]] (sorry, that thread isn’t really up to date at the moment).

I’m not really digging your a, h, m, n, r, s, u. Going into that:

a – I think the sudden angle of the shoulder (top right) is a bit too angular for the typeface. h – I miss the notch. m – Sticking on the second arch to an n doesn’t work. n – Also misses the notch. r – I think the ear should start a bit lower. s – Interesting place to put the notches, but not very consistent when compared to the other characters. u – Missing the notches as well.
