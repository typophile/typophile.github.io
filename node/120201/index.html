It’s close but if you look at the lowercase w then the middle stem in Adobe Caslon is slightly lower than the outer stems. This is not the case in the font I posted.

Also the capital T in de sample doesn’t show any upward shape in the serifs on the top (only downwards). In Adobe Caslon the serifs on the T go slightly upwards too…

So I guess it’s another Caslon type.
