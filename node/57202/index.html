Norwegian:

“Med vennlig hilsen” and its abbrevation, “Mvh”, is widely used, but wrong according to Norsk Språkråd. The correct way to put it would be “Vennlig hilsen” or “Med hilsen” in Norwegian, and “Vennleg hilsen” or “Med helsning” in New Norwegian. Other forms include “Beste hilsener” (N) and “Beste helsing” (NN).
