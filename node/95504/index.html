I’ve tried to identify it some years ago with no success. Came up with nothing enough close to imagine starting a customization on. Most probably a custom job. I’d compiled some kind of list though, probably out of interest: [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/linotype/rory/|Rory]], [[http://www.houseind.com/fonts/bullet/fontspecimens|Bullet]], [[http://www.dafont.com/nolo-contendre.font|Nolo Contendre]]. For what it’s worth, if I had to do it now, I would probably start with [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/linotype/aachen/|Aachen Medium]], manually slanted and probably redesigned almost all letters starting with /o and /c. Designer in charge of [[http://bueroink.com/ink/#25|Lightscale design]] was Markus Schäfer ([[http://bueroink.com|Büro Ink]]). He has designed some other (custom?) logotypes in the same vein ([[http://bueroink.com/ink/#12|Heron]], [[http://bueroink.com/ink/core/user/uploads/various-logos/logos_02_roadies.gif|Roadies]]). Try asking him directly and if he answers, drop some lines here.
