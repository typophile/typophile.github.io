Hrant, rather than add more clutter to the Top 10 thread, I thought I'd start a new one for our Fenway discussions.

I finally had a chance to stop by the local B&N magazine rack and take another look at those men's health/style mags.

In fact, Men's Fitness is indeed using Fenway. (Along with plenty of super chunky FF Bau by Mr. Schwartz – mmmm. And some slab-serif I don't immediately recognize.) Interestingly, they're using Fenway ff-ligatures, which IIRC the original cut didn't have. (And they aren't in the Fenway showing in the Indie Fonts book either.)

I looked again at BusinessWeek and I still think that their setting is a little too tight. I think some uneven combinations are being created. Plus, those f-lig rsb's are still driving me crazy.

BTW, it's Men's Journal that's using Farnham – sparingly: it looks like mostly some subheads, decks, leads. The main text face is FB Eldorado. Seasoned with plenty of Parkinson, Glypha, and Akzidenz.

– K.
