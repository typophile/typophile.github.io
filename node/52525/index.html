just to be clear...Emigre has not filed any font names as trademarks, though they* assert trademark rights. They could always send a nice C&D letter, but cannot file a federal suit without having filed an actual trademark. also, dilution as a legal term involves famous marks and would have no basis here. finally, a reasonable person would ask: Is Maison going to create a likelihood of confusion among font buyers?
