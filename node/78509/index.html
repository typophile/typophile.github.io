Hey everyone, I’d like to introduce a fresh new face from Niramekko. Plau is the name and it’s available at MyFonts: http://new.myfonts.com/fonts/niramekko/plau/

The description: Futurist typeface from the programming era, Plau is a sans-serif with rounded corner personality and interestingly deliberate lettershapes. Comfortable in headlines, reads surprisingly well in longer passages of text.

Also looks quite interesting on the screen.

Hope you guys like it. Cheers Rodrigo
