Shinn's Richler - availablity?

November 10, 2005 • Palatine

According to Shinntype, this font is not for sale, which I find rather disappointing, since at first glance it looks like an incredibly warm and readable face. Its connection with Mordecai Richler and his works makes it even more desirable. As a Canadian (former Montrealer, now Torontonian), it would be nice to use a quality face that carries with it some historical and cultural meaning. So, Mr. Shinn, will this ever be released? Is there any way one can purchase this font?
Sorry Christian, not till 2012. The face has a 10-year exclusive licence. That will give me plenty of time to prepare full multi-language support and OpenType features, complete with Mayan hieroglyphs for full dimensional translation.
