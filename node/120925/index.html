Not finding anything with the same proportions and double-serifed A; it may be custom.

To replicate the look, you could play with the proportions and soften the edges of a Clarendon Black (you’d also need to add the serifs to the top of the A: http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/tilde/clarendon/

Ironmonger would require more work (rounding off the sharp edges, adding the serifs), but it has very similar proportions to your sample. http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/fontbureau/ironmonger/
