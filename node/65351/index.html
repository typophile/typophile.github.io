I am guessing that Bickham has been customized to get this look. The “k” and other letters don’t appear to be included in the font.

I’m not saying one of these is the same, but I have noticed during searches for this question that [[http://new.myfonts.com/foundry/Flat-it/|Flat-it]] foundry has a number of scripts which have many of the elegant forms, similar to Bickham. See Daisy Lau, Machia, Lily Wang and Pansy Bo, in particular. It seems that all of these scripts are the work of Riyoichi Tsunekawa. If what you are looking at is a customization of Bickham, or even just a similar font (perhaps custom), here is one designer who seems to have similar skill. I’m sure he’s not the only one, but this style seems to be one of his strong suits.
