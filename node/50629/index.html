Looking For A Good Small Font

October 18, 2008 • buzlink

I’m currently working on a beer label project for class. I’m needing a good font for small type items for the labels. What works well at small type, 6pt or so, that is an easily available font?

Thanks

I printed [[http://vllg.com/Underware/Dolly/mudTyper+Weights/|Dolly]] out just today at 6pt. The whole typeface has a nice dark complexion to it so you can read the characters well even at small sizes, nice and crisp, whereas so many other typefaces start looking brittle and weak around this point range. (I was surprised to see how sad the mighty Adobe Garamond Pro looks at 6pt.)

Dolly also looks great in a lot of other settings so it’s worth licensing it as a workhorse in your personal collection.

All the best, Rahim
