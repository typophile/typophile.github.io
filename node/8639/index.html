Chris, I have to agree with Yves that your excuse/justification is weak.

I understand that it's a pain to shop online without a credit card. But luckily, there are several other ways to make payment to acquire goods legitimately.

You can make payment by bank wire transfer to many reputable international font distributors, including FontShop at www.fontshop.com.

Since you're in England, you could contact a UK-based distributor such as Faces at www.faces.co.uk to get these fonts. They should be able to take a money order or cashier's check, but I am pretty sure your Switch card would work online or over the phone with them. Failing that, you could make arrangements to visit their physical address and pay with cold hard cash.

Another option is to give the cash to a family member or good friend that has a credit card and let them make the online purchase for you.

Most people come up with a way to pay for the things they want and need. I'm sure you'll find a method that works for you and is fair to those who create and/or supply the goods and services that you wish to acquire.

Good luck!
