2010-01-02 02:53:25 • klokwerk
There are several digitisations of Eurostile available from various foundries, and they are bound to all be slightly different in design and spacing. Finding the exact version starting from your sample is nigh impossible, so your best bet is to manually adjust the spacing and tracking until you get the desired results.
