Glyph Groups on VOLT

July 4, 2009 • AmBonet

Dear Typophile, I am building Open Type features on Volt for a Devanagari font. I understand the basics of the software but some bits remain blurry. Right now, I am wondering on an efficient way to group the few glyphs I have. I do not need to build features for a large range of signs; I have in my font 45 conjuncts as well as the inde vowels, the vowel signs, the consonants, the half-forms.

I hope it could be simple and straight forward!

Thank you

Here is what I did last year in your shoes ;-)

(not including the Latin groups…) One group for all Deva glyphs One for all the Deva conjuncts One for all the Deva ligatures One group for each of the full consonant forms and their derivatives (i.e., one group would include the Ba, the BRa and Bba) One group for all the full Vattu forms One group for all the half forms Another for all the half Vattu forms (I actually did this group twice…) Then a group for the above the base marks Another for the below the base marks

I think that is it! But you have my VOLT project file, so you can take a closer look.
