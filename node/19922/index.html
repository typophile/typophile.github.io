I’m a little late to the game, and this has nothing to do with producing a mock-up with white, but - despite the simplicity of the design - does no one else find the typography busy? Or the blackletter a closed fist to the face?

The latter is way too overbearing to my mind and is effectively screaming at me (sshhhhhh). At the very least I think I’d pull the tint back a bit.

The former has too many competing styles going on: the top italics, the middle caps, the bottom caption; each of these three units has a different personality, and they’re jockying too much for distinction. Any reason that these three units aren’t in the same type?
