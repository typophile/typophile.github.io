[I posted this comment almost 3 years ago, but it's still true.] Every tattoo is done by hand, and therefore is unique, which is the beauty of tattoos. Now for the grumpy pedantic part: The way I understand the meaning of "font," it's a set of letters created for mechanical, optical, or digital reproduction. The lettering in a tattoo can be a hand-drawn copy of a font, but it won't be a font until they invent a tattoo robot.
