I’m looking for a font that looks like the one used on the Evil Spirits logo. What kind of font is this?

Thanks for the help!

This lettering is a pastiche of styles. Note for example the median spurs – found in many wild west fonts –and the foxtail terminals There are some fonts that catch some of the flavor of the lettering. For example, Exocet by Jonathan Barnbrook http://www.emigre.com/EF.php?fid=95 and the Dogma movie font, a fan effort as Avqest http://www.fontspace.com/gemfonts/avqest. Also some other fonts by Jonathan Barnbrook, including Mason http://www.emigre.com/EF.php?fid=104 and Jeremy Dooley’s Newcomen http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/insigne/newcomen/ Don
