When Gerald Giampa was the holder of the remnants of Lanston Monotype, he had as part of the collection the patterns for Lanston’s version of Centaur/Metropolitan.

I recall that he had some correspondence with the Met where it came to light that they still had the metal type down in the basement that had come from their in-house letterpress printshop.

It might be worth while to contact the museum and see if they can give you any rare insde information on the type.
