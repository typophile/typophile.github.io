According to Martin Kotulla, SoftMaker is finally updating “Huntington” (an actual clone of Elsner+Flake’s version of Handel Gothic) to include the following 5 styles - Light, Medium & Bold (all for the “regular”-widthed part), plus the “narrow”-widthed part appears in the form of all-uppercase only by appearing in the styles of Light & Medium. “H652 Sans” (an actual clone of Bitstream’s version of Handel Gothic, appearing in the design of FontBank’s Niamey and Southern Software’s Simplix Display SSi/SSK, and with the lowercase letter “r” in altered glyph form).
