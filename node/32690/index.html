Uniqlo custom typeface

March 30, 2007 • Elliott Walker

For the opening of their new flagship store in SoHo, the Japanese company Uniqlo introduced this custom typeface for all of their in-store signage, as well as, the New York version of their logomark. I wondered if anyone knew anything about this typeface (who designed it, etc.) I think it's pretty great. I pulled this image off of one of their shopping bags.
