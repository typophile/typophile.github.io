2015-02-28 09:38:21 • Anthony Barnett
Generally speaking, serif faces are empirically best suited for immersive reading (such as it would presumably be needed in a philosophy essay). That doesn’t mean that there couldn’t be beautiful and readable longer texts set using sans serif faces (or that a book about philosophy couldn’t have a different form than the traditional treatise). However, I’m afraid something like TheMix would be distracting for anything other than short paragraphs.
