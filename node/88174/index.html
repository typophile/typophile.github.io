The S is clearly an addition (it’s shorter than the rest too). It looks almost like the original name was Aluminum Techique and when they wanted to change it, they din’t have/know the original font anymore. So they added the S from Arial Rounded and kept it smaller to retain the stroke weight.

All the letters which would normally have overshoots look wrong. Top of C & Q, bottom of U’s in Aluminium, although it looks better in Techniques.

My thought is that it is all Frankfurter, but was put together by someone who doesn’t know about overshoots and so they’ve tweaked those curved letters to sit on a guideline drawn from the straighter ones.
