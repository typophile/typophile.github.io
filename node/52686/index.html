You mean, you exported the ID document to PDF and then exported that to Word? Try exporting to RTF (Word, in later versions) directly from the Export menu. You can only export text, so be sure to click first into the story you want.

Or is there a way to import a PDF into InDesign?

Rather than ‘just placing’, you could buy [[http://www.recosoft.com/products/pdf2id/index.htm|PDF2ID]]. I’ve read mixed reports about it; it seems you get at least something editable.

Why these two different work flows, in two opposing directions? What are you hoping to achieve? There might be reasonable alternatives.
