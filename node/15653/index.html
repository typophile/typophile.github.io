I first designed the bitmaps for the phone displays, then drew some outlines over these. Ole Schäfer then drew testwords for text weights which i made into complete alphabets, but not complete character sets. I supplied more sketches and weights, and Monotype then finished my rough data, interpolated the hell out of the fonts and delivered production data.
