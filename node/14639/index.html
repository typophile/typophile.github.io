You really should say what your "this" is, but it's FontExplorer, and a bit heavy of a program for his purposes. Although, if he needs all that functionality, it is useful. They have a Free PC Version and Free Mac Version but at about 180 Mb each, it's a pretty big program. A smaller program, only 658k, is a free version of Advanced Font Viewer called Easy Font Viewer. Still, I prefer Advanced Font Viewer and it is still small but powerful. It's main feature is just what he's looking for. A text box to type in the text you want to show in a particular font, and then a window that shows that text in all your fonts, listed neatly and quickly.
