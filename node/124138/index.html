Here is the logo, taken from the angled image on pink background, with some correction for perspective and skew About the closest I can find to the unmodified letters is Hurme Geometric Sans No.3 Bold http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/hurme-design/hurme-geometric-sans-3/ Make an arc for the crossbar on A, drop the bowl on R and use a segment of the arc to make the leg. Most readers would not notice any difference from the logo but minor other adjustments could be made for an even closer match. Don
