Three things that I noticed: – I am not sure about the legibility of the ‘Fi’ ligature. In any case I think the combination is too wide. Try to match the counter with that of ‘n’. – The serifs seem short to me. And if this is the display version of the wordmark, I would expect a more spectacular thick/thin contrast. – ‘e’ seems wobbly on the lower and upper left.
