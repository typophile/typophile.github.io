The type on this album cover appears to be hand-drawn (I’m seeing variation among the repeating letters).

Closest match I could find in a digital typeface is Caviar Dreams: http://www.nymfont.com/2009/06/new-caviar-dreams-font.html

Add a crossbar to the G, roughen up the edges, and add the eye details, and it comes pretty close.
