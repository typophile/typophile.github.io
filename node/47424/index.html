The first appearance of “MERZ” in Schwitters' work was indeed a collaged bit from an advertisement of the KOMMERZ- UND PRIVATBANK (“Merzbild,” 1919), but it did not use the tall grotesque letters that he used for the masthead of his journal (which began four years later).
