Depending on the “mood” of the enterprise, try various fonts such as:

  1. Appleton, by Decade Typefoundry: http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/decade-typefoundry/appleton/#index
  2. Tropical, by Sudtipos: http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/sudtipos/tropical/
  3. Typnic™ by Corradine Fonts: http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/corradine/typnic/

A good first step is to go to a site like myfonts.com, search for, say, “fruit”… and Bob’s your uncle !
