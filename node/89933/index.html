Sexto here is short for Decimo-sexto, also abbreviated 16º or 16mo (and sometimes called “sixteenmo”).

So this “illegal” publication of Copenhagen Cats was printed on double elephant–size paper (about 26.75 × 40 inches) and folded in sixteenths (which makes 32 pages per signature).

So the trim size on this edition was around 6.6875 × 10 inches.
