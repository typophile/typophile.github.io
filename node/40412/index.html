Editor, [[typographer]], writer and critic. Founder of the publishing imprint [[Hyphen Press]] in 1980. Kinross has contributed much to magazines, including Eye, Blueprint, Information Design Journal, and the Journal of Design History. Hyphen Press now publishes a range of titles on typography and design, recent Hyphen Press publications include the second edition of Modern Typography (2004), The Stroke (2005), and Gerrit Noordzij's theory of writing.
