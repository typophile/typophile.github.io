Vectormagic

November 14, 2007 • nert

For those of you stuck in Illustrator/Inkscape or some other such vector malarky, you might want to give [[http://vectormagic.stanford.edu|this]] a quick look.

It’s a free web app that incredibly quickly converts your raster images to vector. I’m seriously impressed - it easily out performs Illustrator’s Live Trace by a factor of lots.

I couldn’t find a mention here and if this is old news, I apologise. Sod it, no I don’t, go and have a look, it’s fantastic. :)
