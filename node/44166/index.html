Tiffany, if you are just starting out and don’t really have a budget to speak of you can always find some useful free fonts. The best place to start would be Typophile’s Typowiki entry about free fonts.

Because you’ve said you are new to this I will say that it is important to read and understand any EULA (End User License Agreement) that is part of all (99%) of fonts available whether for license or shareware/freeware. Some of the free fonts you might find, for instance, might not allow usage on commercial projects.

Good Luck!
