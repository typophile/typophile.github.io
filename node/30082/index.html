Is this the biggest letterpressed "M" ever?

December 14, 2006 • swiss dots

We just printed another limited-edition print for the Helvetica film, this one designed by Zurich-based designers Norm. The studio that printed it, Coeur Noir in Brooklyn (great guys… if you need letterpress in the NYC area) said they thought it was the largest character ever printed via letterpress. I chuckled, but then thought, maybe they’re right?

That “M” is 1008pt. Have any of you ever seen a character larger that 1008pt printed via letterpress?

Here’s a pic, as proudly displayed by Helvetica project coordinator Christina Ross:

M for Monsterous:-)

James Bond’s M isn’t even that big! Send it in to Guinness Book of World Records. :-)
