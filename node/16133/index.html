Your best bet would have been if this was one of the 300 Handwritten Fonts that came from the orignal ClickArt CD (and are now part of a Broderbund ClickArt fonts collection). Bad news -- I looked through them all, and though some are close (especially Wolf), I didn't find a match. As Yves said, there are 'a gazillion' of these things. You can see about 500+ of them in Part 11 of the Script Font ID Guide. Your best option if you don't see it, is to find the closest and go with that. This is one of those times when it's really not worth an extended search. These fonts can be made by anyone for almost no money. - Mike Yanega
