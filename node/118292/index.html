I’d like to know what the font used to spell out The Lumineers

This is all hand-drawn, and while the main text is loosely based on a number of fonts, “The Lumineers” is just basic block printing.

Search for “hand-drawn” and “sans-serif” on Myfonts.com, and you get more than 200 hits: http://www.myfonts.com/search/text%3AHand-drawn+text%3Asans/fonts/

Typefaces that mimic the look above include: http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/pintassilgo/oyster/ http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/gaslight/kiddy/ http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/laboitegraphique/borden/ [minus the dot over the I] http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/thinkdust/york-handwriting/ http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/device/valise-montreal/ http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/jcfonts/hand-gothic/ http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/pintassilgo/amarelinha/ [bold] http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/juraj-chrastina/baronessa/ http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/kenrusselldesign/farmstand/ http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/hanoded/oranjerie/ http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/juraj-chrastina/baron/

…and too many more to mention.

Check out the search link above and pick one that works for you.
