No it's brilliant. If you want to go to Wroughton, you don't have to go all the way around a single large roundabout like Vauxhall or Hanger Lane. To go to Wroughton you, signal Right at the first round-about, give way Right then signal Right again, give way Right again and you'll be laughing. And if you need to do a U-Turn you don't have to go all the way around just signal right and loop around the first round-about, it's Magic.
