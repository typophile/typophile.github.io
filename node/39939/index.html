The one on the mug is an old typeface called Rustic. It’s been modified a bit. The original is pruned (no branches sprouting out the sides) and has a dropshadow, otherwise it appears identical. I don’t know of a digital version, but Pinewood looks roughly similar.
2007-12-10 23:31:12 • Mark Simonson

I know of two digital approximations of Rustic. One is called Birch. While rather nice in its own right, it’s much lighter than Rustic was - and hard to find given that there’s a popular commercial font called Birch as well.

The much better version (more faithful to Rustic, that is) is called Twig.
