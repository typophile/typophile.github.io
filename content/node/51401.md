body: "Dear Friends,\r\nFlower Crosswords are distinguished by their Rules + Clues
  + Goals:\r\n_________________ Words in a flower _________________ \r\n1. are filtered
  out of a School Dictionary, \r\n2. are of the same length (3,4,5 or 6-letter long),\r\n3.
  go clockwise + CCW, from the tips of the petals toward the flower's heart,\r\n4.
  are controlled by a SINGLE letter forming an OUTSTANDING letter wheel,\r\n\r\nIn
  this sample, all the 4-letter words end with K, forming a K-wheel (blue in color).\r\n[img:sites/default/files/old-images/K-flower_6516.jpg]\r\n_________________
  A flower can be made Cluless if _________________ \r\n\r\n1. only the vowels (A,E,I,O,U)
  are given/taken away, or\r\n2. one or more letter-wheels is given/taken away, or\r\n3.
  a list of the CW +/or the CCW words is given.\r\n\r\nOut of a single n-petal Flower
  Crossword, 2n Flower Crosswords can be made simply by CW+CCW rotating it one letter
  2n times, thus helping teachers to test classrooms of 2n learners without allowing
  them to copy from one another.\r\n\r\n______________ Flowers are set with these
  Goals _______________\r\n1. Multiplying ones vocabulary with less effort+time by
  exploiting their Word-Doubling property:\r\n_ If less than half of the clues is
  answered, the crossword is done.\r\n2. Building error-free spelling with the help
  of the error-detecting & correcting letter-wheels.\r\n_ Example: BOOK can not be
  BOKE to go in line with the K-wheel?\r\n3. Emphasizing narrowly distinguished tones
  & subsequently\r\n4. Strengthening Tongue-Twisting with the help of keywords.\r\n_
  Hold the K-Flower for example & go on twisting+turning+singing a song with the 4-letter
  K-words.\r\n5. Developing memory efficiency & intelligence by attacking words from
  different positions:\r\n_ If FISH is attacked 3 times (in F,S+H flowers), won't
  it be fully digested?\r\n6. Encouraging communication in English through an integrated
  approach where grammar skills are not formally taught but naturally assimilated
  through constant practice of the language.\r\n7. More to be discovered.\r\n________________________________________________\r\nHappy
  Exploring with Flowers\r\n"
