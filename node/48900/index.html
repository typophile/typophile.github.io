2008-09-01 01:58:49 • ben_archer
More garbage. Chartjunk was the wrong word, Tufte uses it to describe unnecessary decoration. He called charts not based on data "relational", for instance, showing the time-based maturation of a seed into a plant. He also speaks of "distortion", when the visual does not match the data. So these time-line charts are relational, because the choice of typefaces is not based on statistical data, but is rather based on a subjective interpretation of history. In as much as they purport to represent actual data, they are fiction.
