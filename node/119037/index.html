Though based on a typeface, it was heavily customized.

Antique Olive Compact Italic gets close to the general letterforms, save for the a and the customized r and i: http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/urw/antique-olive/

Filmotype Ford is a modern font that is also close to matching the letterforms, but it would have to be artificially italicized, and the same customization done to the r and i: http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/filmotype/ford/

Futura Extra Black Italic is the best match for the a, but you have to shorten the ascender on the h and customize the r and i: http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/bitstream/futura/

(NOTE: Antique Olive dates from 1962, Futura from 1932. Filmotype Ford was designed in 2011, but based on a font released in 1953)
