Chalet is a good candidate for customization. It is also deeply rooted in [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/itc/bauhaus|Bauhaus]] (or [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/itc/pump|Pump]]). To me, it’s either a freebie or a complete custom job based on the same /o shape (mainly no optical correction):

  • /t = /o half bottom + 2 strokes
  • /a = cut /o + 1 stroke
  • /n = /o half top + 2 strokes
  • /g = /o + (/o half bottom + 1 stroke)
  • /e = cut /o + 1 stroke
  • /d = /o + 1 stroke
