2013-05-31 06:53:05 • Evgeny Grigoriev
Exactly, Deberny & Peignot upon the idea of the art director Maximilien Vox acquired rights to produce a version of Futura from Bauer. They released Europe in 1931 in France and french colonies. It seems the greek variant is specific to Deberny & Peignot as they produce it punchcutting while Bauer use a pantographic process to directly produce Futura.
