I have a version also called June (an American name for Pierre Simon Fournier le jeune [the younger, like Junior]). It was derived from a copy issued by Deberney & Peignot in 1913, digitized by Alan Prescott (who used to have a type shop in NYC). It has capitals, ampersand, numbers and punctuation (but no parentheses).
