Schriftgestaltung.de: Freefont

March 20, 2007 • schriftgestalt

The font Olivegreen Mono Light from Georg Seifert is available for free at MyFonts.com.

The rather upright geometric design of the monospace font is contrasted with the very vibrant, playful italic.

Schriftgetaltung.de [german, Typedesign] is a small foundry that published fonts by Georg Seifert and offers a platform for friends to present their own fonts.

  • Specimen: Schriftgestaltung.de/seite/Schriften/OliveGreenMonoSpezimen.pdf
  • Purchase: MyFonts.com/fonts/schriftgestaltung/olive-green-mono
  • Georg Seifert’s Website: reets.de

The font is cool, Schriftgestaltung font family with 8 styles priced from $26.00. it include Regular, Mono Light, Light Italic, Italic, Medium, Medium Italic and Bold. it is worth.
