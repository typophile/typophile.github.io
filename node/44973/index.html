Numbers 1 (title) and 3 (body) are one and the same typeface.

Originally it is called Fino, designed in the 1970’s by Maurice Schlesinger.

Some digital recuts are: Fina (Image Club Graphics), see PDF here: http://www.tekntype.com/webs/tntfonts/pdf/02144.pdf Fina Heavy (Image Club Graphics), see PDF here: http://www.tekntype.com/webs/tntfonts/pdf/02145.pdf Finale (CompuWorks) Feena Casual (ZETAfonts) http://www.1001fonts.com/font_details.html?font_id=548
