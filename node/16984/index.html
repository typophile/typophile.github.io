Sad: NYC design bookstore "Untitled" closing January 15

December 27, 2005 • Ricardo Cordoba

Last night I found out that Untitled, a bookstore at 159 Prince Street in New York City, will be closing its doors for good in just a couple of weeks… They have carried Emigre magazine for as long as I can remember, in addition to all sorts of books on graphic design, architecture and art…

The good news for typophiles is that they are offering a 40% discount on their remaining books (there are still quite a few). Even the framed, out-of-print Emigre posters that they have on their walls are for sale, at $65 each.

Their old website has been replaced with a generic page, but perhaps you can contact the owners at bevandavies@worldnet.att.net

Oh man, I’ll have to give it a visit when I get back to Manhattan next week and see what I can scrounge up!
