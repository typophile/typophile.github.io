I suspect that your sample is most likely a PhotoLettering font from the 1960s or 1970s, but very little of their catalog is digitized. A competitor, Filmotype, has several digitized fonts that are somewhat similar, particularly Filmotype Hamlet and Filmotype Harmony: http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/filmotype/hamlet/ http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/filmotype/harmony/ Also Filmotype Honey, as already mentioned.
