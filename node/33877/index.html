Here’s some suggestions for Manchester as those who live in London get a nose bleed past Watford.

  1. The John Rylands Library. Recently refurbished and now open. Rare books and manuscripts housed in an amazing building.

http://www.library.manchester.ac.uk/

http://rylibweb.man.ac.uk/data1/dg/exhibition/unlock/

  1. Manchester Art Gallery
