In typesetting something, I’ve come across this phrase in Greek, attributed to Archimedes I believe : Δοϛ μοι που ςιω και ταν γαν κινασω

If there are any Greek speakers on the board, have I matched the characters correctly? Does sentence work?

Appreciatively, Luca

I don’t read the language, but I know the alphabet, and a final sigma (ς) should only be at the end of a word, so in “ςιω” it should be replaced by a normal sigma (σ).

In the first word “Dos” you appear to have used a stigma instead of a final sigma; it looks very slightly different and has a different codepoint. Stigma isn’t normally used in writing.
