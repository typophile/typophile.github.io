FontLab: Repeat last session?

November 6, 2006 • Goran Soderstrom

Is there anyway to make FontLab remember the last task I did.

Lets say I lower the height of a stem, by highlighting the upper nodes and drag it down a little bit. Or by hand move a nod little to the left.

Could I just repeat this last “session” in some way?

Hey, that’s a good idea. Simply allow redo even if you are at the end of the history. But I do not think it is possible. You should post your suggestion to the official FL forum on msn.

In case it is just shifting nodes you want to repeat I suggest to use the transformation panel, which “remembers” the action. This should do what you want to do.
