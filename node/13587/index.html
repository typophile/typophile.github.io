Fred Smeijers

July 8, 2005 • Co

[[Indices]] : [[Designers]] : Fred Smeijers

Fred Smeijers is a type designer born in the [[Netherlands]]. He has designed typefaces for [[FontFont]], [[TEFF]], [[DTL]] and for his own foundry, [[OurType]]. Apart from his retail-design work, Smeijers designs custom typefaces for clients like Philips and Canon-Europe. Fred Smeijers also published various written works, among them [[Counterpunch]] and [[Type Now]], both at [[HypenPress]], London.

Among his typefaces are: FF Quadraat TEFF Renard DTL Nobel OT Arnhem OT Fresco OT Sansa OT Custodia OT Ludwig

Fred Smeijers is currently Professor for type design and digital media at the [[Hochschule fuer Grafik und Buchkunst | Hochschule für Grafik und Buchkunst]] in Leipzig, Germany and visiting professor at Type and Media [[KABK]], in The Hague, the [[Netherlands]].

Next to his type design practice and teaching, Smeijers has a particular interest type history and is currently involved in several research projects.
