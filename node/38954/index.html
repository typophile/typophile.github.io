George - First of all, your assumption that the House fonts are not original designs is false. As such, any alternatives would only be similar, not the same. Also, it rather discourages suggestions from us when we couldn’t possibly have a clue as to what fonts you have on hand.

So , with no thought as to what you might have available already, here are two suggestions:

For House Holiday Gothic, see what you think of Larabie’s Mikadan - a combination of two typefaces from the early 1900’s.

For House Holiday Script, see the thin scripts in some of Stuart Sandler’s various inexpensive packages.

I know of nothing quite like House Holiday Sans.
