Spotlight: Justin Shiels

February 28, 2009 • Reba Joy 

I present: Justin Shiels, Graphic Design extraordinaire

Some of you may have already heard of this budding designer, 
and some of you may have not.
Nonetheless, you should get accustomed with not only this man, but with his work. 
[img:sites/default/files/old-images/urbannext_3485.jpg]


[img:sites/default/files/old-images/web-curious_5886.jpg]
Justin is most known for his blog, http://www.curioustribe.com
CuriousTribe is a blog, created and designed solely 
by Justin Shiels that strives fill a void in society. 
CuriousTribe enables viewers to find an alternative 
filter or lens in which to view the world that is infused 
with innovation and creativity.

Why did I select this designer?
Because he embodies all that a designer should be. 
He sees the opportunity that exists within design 
and is not afraid to rise to the challenge.
He utilizes the tools given to him through design to effect the world
 and that is pretty awesome in my book....
AND because he's very talented! 

Check out the blog!
