Machine tags for typefaces now possible on Flickr photos

September 30, 2009 • joeclark

Some beavers have been busy in the shadows, and today they announced a syntax for machine tags you can add to your Flickr photos of fonts – things like type:face=cocon and type:designer=porchez. The advantage of machine tags is regularity of syntax. Other applications can use the Flickr API to assemble e.g. collections of every photo of Cocon or every photo of a font by Porchez, and they can show those photos on their own sites, not just at Flickr. (There are numerous other machine tags in use; see documentation on the Flickr Code blog.) I have about a thousand photos in my stream thus tagged. You can too.
