FontFont offers FF QType Extended Book for free download.
The four free typefaces offered by MacRhino are top quality stuff,
as are Fountain's Pro Bono Fonts. Font Boutique's Samba is very
nice as well.

Try searching a bit yourself: amongst the masses of poor quality
rubbish there are some hidden gems to be discovered.
If you can't pay, you should at least be prepared to put in the effort
in order to find them. I'm not going to do all the work for you.

Make sure to always read the license agreement or Read Me file!
