That’s an antique typeface called Tuscan Ombree, and several other names according to Mac McGrew’s “American Metal Typefaces..” book. Also known as Carnival. “Originally Bruce’s Ornamented No. 847. It originated about 1849.” The font Jan found (above) is a closer reproduction of it than Adobe’s Zebrawood, though they are both based on it.
