March 16, 2010 • agisaak

Given that the moderators of this group are not actually empowered to have people like “Mbtshoes” drawn and quartered, I’d like to suggest that it might be a good idea to make it slightly more difficult to register a typophile account in an effort to cut down on such rudeness. I have virtually no knowledge of designing web sites, so I don’t know whether any of these suggestions are actually feasible without dumping a huge amount of work on someone.

A couple of ways to do this might include:

(A) A short time-delay between the creation of an account and gaining posting privileges (say a couple of hours which might be sufficient to encourage spammers to go elsewhere).

(B) Some sort of rather trivial quiz to demonstrate an actual interest in type (I’m thinking along the lines of a series of multiple choice questions with answers easily tracked down on the typowiki, e.g. ‘An example of a neoclassical design would be (a) Bembo, (b) Charlotte Sans, (c) Bell, (d) Avant Garde Gothic’, (e) Fette Fraktur).

Don’t know if others agree whether such a step would be desirable.
