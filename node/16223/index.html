Steve Matteson

November 13, 2005 • gthompson

[[Indices]] : [[Designers]] : Steve Matteson Currently working for [[Ascender Corp.]] in Elk Grove Village, Illinois, Steve formerly was at Monotype where he worked on the Windows core fonts. He is a graduate of Rochester Institute of Technology, having studied typography and printing. Among the many typefaces he has designed are Truedell, a Goudy revival, Goudy Ornate, Andalé, and Microsoft's company font Segoe. Related link: http://www.ascendercorp.com/stevepage.html
