You're right, the Linotype site doesn't make it easy to find the info. Here it is: http://www.linotype.com/1697/opentypeinfo.html The difference between the com pro and std is basically how many characters beyond Western Europe they have. Beyond that, the designer generally gets the biggest cut when you buy from him, or the first company that issued the font, I think in this case Linotype I believe. Now owned by Monotype :)
