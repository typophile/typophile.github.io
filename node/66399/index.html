2010-01-20 02:46:04 • blank

Needs a bit of Dutch . . . Dolly, Arnhem, etc. Look into a super-family, like Scala/Scala Sans or Freight by Joshua Darden.

I’d try to avoid Times and Minion. Substitute Times with StormType Lido (which is free and very well done), and although Minion has its place, it’s rather sterile. Why not have a faithfully revived classic like Bembo Book in your collection? Or something a bit more modern and versatile like Expo Serif and Expo Sans by TypeCulture? Mark Jamra will even send you a letterpress specimen or two in the mail (they’re gorgeous.)

The possibilities are legion.
