2007-03-21 18:40:03 • dezcom

An honour it was. I first read the book in 1967 as it came out. After almost fifteen years I decided to translate it (from original German, of course). A contract with Arthur Niggli was signed by Kniga, so we used the original films received from Zürich. Phototypesetting in Univers 55 was done by Pravda on Linotron 202.

And yes, there was plenty of space, so, based on the same grid, I used either single-, or double-, or triple-column page layout.
