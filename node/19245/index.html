Let’s rattle off all the humanist sans that are somewhat close to Scala Sans:

Gill Sans Syntax Today Bliss Mundo Agenda Charlotte Legato Freight Sans Kievit

etc….. I’ve excluded any that really aren’t close, like Cronos, Frutiger, Formata, and Chianti.

Your problem isn’t that you need an alternative, it’s that you need a budget. If they don’t want to pay, you could:

  • Buy yourself the best Humanist Sans family for your general needs, and use that, since it will have to be close enough

  • Buy Scala Sans for yourself and use it for this job, and every other job you can get away with it since it’s so trendy and desirable

  • institute a policy that you will always keep a healthy budget for type in future jobs as part of your expenses
