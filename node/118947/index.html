Apollo from Fonderie Typographique Française

August 23, 2014 • sander s p

I stumbled over a [[http://collections.bm-lyon.fr/mil/search?query[]=title_s:%22Apollo%22|link]] to some specimens of this typeface called Apollo. I like the quirks that sets it apart from similar faces, the C in particular, with the top terminal cut horizontally and the bottom terminal cut at an angle. Has this typeface ever been digitized or revived under another name? I hadn’t heard of it before.
¶ It’s one of the two French ersatz of Renner’s Futura. The other is Simplex from the Olive foundry.
