Hi Everyone! You all have helped me in the past, so I'm turning here again. I am a pastor who needs to get information to my congregation all the time in the form of fliers, & brochures, (Church clean-up, spaghetti dinner, etc.) It would be great if we could hire graphic designers to do the work, and someday we may be able to do that. For now, I need to find a way to do the fliers myself. Apple PAGES, which is part of their iWork package. A bunch of good looking templates are included, it's not overly intimidating, and it will grow with you as your design skills improve (ie; works well for beginners AND intermediate designers/typographers). http://www.apple.com/iwork/pages/ Good luck!
