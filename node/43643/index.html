LuzSans... is an exclusive HP corporate font, created by Hoefler & Frere-Jones. It is also known as HP New Futura.
The copyright information for LuzSans reads:
08/03 Luz, HP New Futura The Hoefler Type Foundry, 1998-2003.

LuzSans is used on the keyboard of HP Mini netbooks, and also on said computers as the default Window title font in HP’s Mobile Internet Experience (MIE), a Linux distro based on Ubuntu 8.04.
