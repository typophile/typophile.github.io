Prisma/Prismania/Prismatic?

January 26, 2015 • jch02140

There is a typeface called "Prismania" by Headliners Inc., which appears to look similar to "Prisma", a typeface available in Mecanorma/Letraset dry lettering catalogue. Also, there seems to be another typeface called "Prismatic" which revived by Mr. Nick Curtis. https://www.myfonts.com/fonts/nicksfonts/pentaprism-nf/ All of them appears to look the same except that Prismania and Prismatic have some alternates and variant designs... Curious which one is a knockoff of which?.... Prisma was originally designed by Rudolf Koch and first cast by Klingspor in 1931.
