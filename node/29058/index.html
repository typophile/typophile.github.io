Alessandro, although it’s really late in coming since you’ve posted the link a while ago, thank you for a really interesting collection of Estonian lettering.

Some of the lettering styles remind me of some Finnish lettering styles, in particular that on the cover of an edition of Kalevala I saw as a child (and that I cannot now find an image of, to my annoyance). It had thick, angular letterforms with idiosyncracies such as the top bar on the As. To see a similar lettering style pop up in Rudolf Koemets’s design for Kalevipoeg in particular is exciting.

I’m vaguely reminded of Basque lettering as well, so maybe there is something going on with late adopters of the roman alphabet by non-Indo-European speakers?
