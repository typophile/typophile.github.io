Hi,

Can you please provide recommendations for similar typefaces to Cymbria and Blanch? We need to purchase a commercial license for these fonts, but they don’t seem to be available. It appears that Blanch might have a license available through LostType, but I am not certain at this point that the EULA is sufficient for our needs.

Cymbria: https://www.fontforestry.com/cymbria

Blanch: https://www.losttype.com/font/?name=blanch

Thanks for any assistance you can provide.

Josh

Josh, Lost Type’s commercial license is rather broad: http://www.losttype.com/images/samples/License_Commercial_Sample.pdf https://www.losttype.com/contact/

As for Cymbria, it is posted as a free font for both personal and commercial use (see the header text on the Font Forestry’s Freebies page): https://www.fontforestry.com/freebies-1/

If you go to Font Forestry’s commercial fonts page and click through the purchase links (to Creative Market), the license states that those fonts are may be used for “Commercial Use; Unlimited Number of Projects; Unlimited End Products For Sale”. I suspect their freebie fonts share the same permissions. Contact them if you are unsure about how you intend to use them: https://www.fontforestry.com/contact-1/
