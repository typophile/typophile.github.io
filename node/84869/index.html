Yes, the letters are by Frank Blokland – it says so in the back of the book. (The overall design isn’t mentioned but may well be by Gerrit Noordzij who assembled the book & wrote the introduction.)

He drew the letters while he was a student at the KABK. There’s an alphabet of similar letters on p.30 of the book and a few lines (roman + italic) on p.29. I don’t think it was ever released as a typeface. The book is from 1983. His first typeface Bernadette (which looks a bit like this one but is bolder and has heavier serifs) is from 1985 & was never released digitally.
