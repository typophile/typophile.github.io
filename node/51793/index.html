logo limbo, product designer's quest for help

November 17, 2008 • lamoore

greetings all! I post here with hopes of any and all feedback, critiques, suggestions, etc. regarding the visual identity for my one-woman company, Lamoore Designs (Based in NOVA Scotia, canada.) i’m a full-time artist and product designer/maker. i am not a graphic designer, but have hired one to completely overhaul my branding/visual identity. my preferences are for “old fashioned” looking typefaces and layouts, and i’m looking for both a mark and a type that will be my new look. my avatar shows the logo i’ve been using for the last 4 years. the new proposed design is here in this post.

All of my creative work features found objects, my line of jewelry is called “Finders Keepers”, and my tagline is “Redefining Precious.” (not “precious redefined” as seen in the file)

i have played with the idea of having my biz card printed on actual cereal boxes or some other “found” sort of material, but i am open to simply having a design that “feels” old. the design will be used in everything, including: storefront signage/hwy signage packaging and promotional biz cards and letterhead, etc. website labels

some key words that define my work would be: retro/vintage, repurpose/reclaim, eco, environmentally responsible, handmade, original…the opposite of commercial, mass-produced, common, hi-tech.

thanks to anyone who wants to jump in here.
