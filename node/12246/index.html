Ottmar Mergenthaler

May 8, 2005 • dan_reynolds

[[Indices]] : [[History]] : Ottmar Mergenthaler May 10, 1854–October 28, 1899 Ottmar Mergenthal was born in southern Germany. During the 1870s, he moved to Baltimore. In 1886, he invented the [[Linotype Machine]], the world's first commercially-viable typesetting machine that would later be mass produced by an America company called [[Mergenthaler Linotype]]. Newspapers were the primary users of these machines.
