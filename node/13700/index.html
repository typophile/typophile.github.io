TypoTechnica

July 10, 2005 • dan_reynolds

[[Indices]] : [[Conferences]] : TypoTechnica Technological focused conference organized by [[Linotype]]. TypoTechnica is a forum for discussion regarding font design and production. The focus of the conferences is always on the cutting edge of font technology development. The conferences are organized by Linotype for the benefit of the entire community, and many of the lectures are organized by [[Adobe]], [[Microsoft]], [[Letterror]], etc. The first TypoTechnica (at the time named the [[Linotype Font Technology Forum]] took place in 2001, in Heidelberg, Germany at the [[Heidelberger Druckmaschinen AG]]'s [[Print Media Academy]]. Documentation may be found here. The second TypoTechnica took place in 2003, also at the Print Media Academy. Documentation may be found here. The third TypoTechnica took place in February 2005, at the [[St Bride Printing Library]] in London. Documentation may be found here. [[TypoTechnica 2007]] conference, the fourth Typotechnica coference, was held in Frankfurt, Germany, from April 27–28, 2007.
