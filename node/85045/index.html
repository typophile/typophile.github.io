2011-09-01 21:13:50 • Frode Bo Helland
Courier is a public domain design, but expect to pay between $350 and $24,250 per weight for a custom (designed from scratch) distressed typewriter font. Price will be dependent on designer, their research, character set and number of iterations. Price does not include hinting, or expenses (such as purchasing old typewriters or trips to the International Typewriter Museum in Oakland, CA.)

2011-09-03 14:45:47 • Pat2
To get estimates, you’ll probably need to contact foundries directly. You’ll also need some more details: technical level, timeframe, character set, weights, styles, level of exclusivity etc. OEM licensens are often priced as a percentage of the end product retail price. An exclusive design might mean a higher one time fee upfront, but on the other hand no running expenses. You could also deviate in style within the same widths and metrics as Courier, giving your product X a unique voice.
