Speak Up is a style that I use in many variations. Slanted, vertical, compressed, expanded, extended etc. ad infinitum. P22 Pooper Black and P22 Sneaky are the incarnations of these variations. You could skew Pooper Black and achieve the same feel. You of course could add the appropriate joins in Illustrator (the cheap way) and make adjustments after the skew or pay for a custom design.
