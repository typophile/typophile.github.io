2012-11-02 00:23:59 • inkexit
Lower case is the complement to upper case. It traditionally comprised the minuscule characters in bicameral systems, but now also accomodates other alternates to majuscule glyphs, in typefaces which do not have minuscules. Lower case characters have separate code points in Unicode, enabling them to be linked by the shift key on keyboard devices, or by other casing operations.
