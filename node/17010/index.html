[[Gudrun Zapf von Hesse]] designed the Diotima type family for [[D. Stempel AG]] during the late 1940s and early 1950s. [[Hermann Zapf]] set their wedding invitation in this face, instead of in [[Palatino]]. What more could attest to its inherent quality? A digital version of the face is available today from [[Linotype]]. Click here to see all of the Diotima weights on the Linotype website. Zapf von Hesse designed wo sets of titling capitals to harmonize with Diotima: Ariadne Initials and Smaragd.
