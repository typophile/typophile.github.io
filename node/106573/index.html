Hi! Can you please help me with this font? I have been lookign for its name for quite a while. The source is at pag.76 of [[http://www.scribd.com/doc/117655597/Planescape-D-D-2nd-In-the-Cage-A-Guide-to-Sigil-OCR |THIS]] old 1995 Planescape manual

Probably Quaint Gothic SG. See http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/spiecegraphics/quaint-gothic-sg/ Note the alternate caps which match the S and O. An Art Nouveau high waist design with an Arts & Crafts flavor, sold as Quaint by the Dickinson Type Foundry in Boston circa 1900. Also known as Desdemona by the K.K. Hof & K. Brendler. For another version see: http://www.will-harris.com/store-h/desdemona.html Don
