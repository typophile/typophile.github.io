TypeCon 2004 in San Francisco

July 21, 2003 • Joe Pemberton

As you may know, Typophile HQ is in San Francisco. You can bet we'll get involved as much as we are able. It will be great to see what locals show up. Here's an incomplete list of type designers / foundries in the Bay Area: Jim Parkinson Mark van Bronkhorst FontShop / Erik Spiekermann Delve Withrington Typebox Psy/Ops Test Pilot Collective Who else?
The conference is officially taking place in Oakland, but all the shoe shopping will take place in San Francisco.
