Hi Diane,

First off, I’m not quite a professional yet, I’m a student, so my suggestions may not be 100%, but here they are anyways :) If you’re going to be using a sans for the body copy, I’d recommend a serif font, or a sans with some character for the display type. Here’s just a few of my suggestions…

Democratica if a quirky font that is modern/hi-tech, and I don’t know it seems kinda ‘medical’ to me http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/emigre/democratica/

Modern 735 is more conservative, but certainly is professional, and modern. http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/bitstream/modern-735/

Kingsbury Conensed is interesting. http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/spiecegraphics/kingsbury-condensed-sg/

Ultra Bronzo is a sans, but has a good deal of character, fairly clean and high tech. http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/thirstype/ultra-bronzo/

Ditto with Copperplate Modern… http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/wiescherdesign/copperplate-modern/
