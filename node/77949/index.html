2011-01-12 12:49:31 • jasonc

As Jason pointed out, ALL measurements must equal whole pixel values for the font to work. Therefore, the Ascender AND Cap heights MUST equal 896 and the Descender MUST equal 128. The Left Sidebearing should be zero, and the Right sidebearing 128.

BTW, if you scale everything 200% and generate a TTF-flavor font, your measurements would be ideal (2,048 UPM) for the format.
