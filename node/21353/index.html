It’s cmyk, I could see it, so I’ll upload an RGB.

Nice pallette and elements, however, I think you are going to be limited in the uses for this, the proportions need to be more even between cycle and type, they are too separate at the moment; which leads onto the illustration I would guess that at small sizes you would need to consider either a simplified drawing or a detail of the drawing, details around the spokes, skirt guard and pedals are going to fill in (btw the cycle doesn’t appear to run parallel to the type). I think you can also investigate stacking the type element into three lines, you could then enlarge them closer to the width of the illustration (this will be tricky because you don’t want the weight of the type to overwhelm the illustration).
