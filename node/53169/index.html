Hi Omar, thanks for linking to my article.

Most typefaces used for airport signage is indeed Frutiger, first designed for Charles de Gaulle, Paris. Other typefaces that can be used for airport signage are eg. FF Info (Spiekermann), FF DIN (Albert Jan Pool), FF Transit (MetaDesign). I have seen uses of Helvetica and Gill Sans, although I find those typefaces not very suitable for airport signage. Good luck.
