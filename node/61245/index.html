Ed Rondthaler RIP

August 21, 2009 • Diner

I had hoped somebody else in the know would’ve posted about this before I did but I was just made aware of this news: http://www.lohud.com/article/20090821/NEWS02/908210360/-1/newsfront

I did have the good fortune to meet Ed at Typecon a few years back and celebrate his birthday with him. . . He was a very kind soul and always in great spirits.

One of my favorite memories of him: http://www.houseind.com/movie/

Also, if you get a chance pick up a copy of his book Life with Letters as the Turned Photogenic, a wonderful tome that recounts the history of his company and life . . .

He left and indelible mark on the type industry and will be sorely missed . . .
