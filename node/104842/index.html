There are similar fonts, commonly labeled as demi or semi serif. To make them the designer snips off various serifs, and voila! a new font. This one may be a custom snip job. The crossbar on A has joined various serifs in the trashcan. Compare the snipped version on the lhs with the one on the rhs, which is probably the source font. Don
