With the insanity of Bourbon Street just outside I think most folks were having a good time in the hotel. I tried drinking Sazeracs in three different bars, and the hotel bar made the best one by far. I turned Petra Weitz on to Negronis, she was trying to blame me for getting her drunk before the TypeQuiz. I think the conference organizers did a terrific job, and from a content point of view, the best TypeCon I ever went to. One of the more compelling presentations was done by three gentlemen from the Cherokee Nation, encouraging the type design community to develop more fonts with Cherokee language support. No location for 2012 was announced. Too many organizational decisions yet to be made I would gather.
