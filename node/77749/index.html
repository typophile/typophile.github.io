Bold as a verb

January 5, 2011 • Chris Dean

Believe it or not, a PhD lab mate (cognitive psychology) just taught me a new word:

em·bold·en –verb (used with object) to make bold or bolder; hearten; encourage.

Also, imbolden.

“Embolden your headings”

I seem to recall coming across “boldfaced” as a past participle, referring to the practice of setting the names of celebrities in Bold, in Regular running text.
