You should just email me and ask! To save you the trouble, it’s a filled-weight version of Rudolf Koch’s Prisma which I’ve designed with Alex Rich and Rafael Koch. The font is soon to be completed and released with Lineto in a new multi-weight upper and lowercase revision named Prismaset.
