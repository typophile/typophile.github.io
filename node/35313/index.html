The Römisch-Germanisches Museum in Cologne is also great. They have a lot of Roman stonecarved inscriptions. A lot! The museum is right next to the cathedral, which is also worth visiting, and like most churches, it is also free (which the museum is not). The chapels around the cathedral’s choir-end have a lot of nice lettering in them, too, if you look carefully. Like here.
