I am not sure what it’s based on but it sounds like you want something really close. That is even less likely. Most type designers are less interested in making a copy than interpreting. If you really want something that has all the oddity & specificity the thing to do would be to arrange to take scans of the objects ( the brass or the paper result) & then commission a designer to undertake a conversion into digital type.
