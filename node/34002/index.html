Carolbee If you are thinking of setting up an eBay account, note that there are sellers' fees. Both eBay and PayPal. U&lc issues don't go for much (somewhat of a hard sell). But if you decide to partake, best word of advice: don't close an auction on a full moon. . . or during holidays. Or for that matter Fridays or Saturdays—web traffic drops dramatically. And if you don't believe that, monitor the daily usage statistics of Typophile (ain't nothing ever going on here on the weekends—not sure what that reveals about type designers/typographers). Gerald
