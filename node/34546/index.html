2007-06-18 15:55:15 • Grot Esqué

Do you mean a [[single story]] ‘a’? There are hundreds of typefaces with this variant. Here are some of the better:

FF Super Grotesk Drescher Grotesk Bryant FF Advert Sassoon Garage Gothic Geometric 415 (AKA Metro) FF QType FF Schulbuch Berlinsans Proxima Nova (alt)

It is also often found in the italics of most sans serifs.
