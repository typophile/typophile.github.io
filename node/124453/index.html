|PROTOCOL| is some form of condensed Didot/Bodoni. Haven’t found a match yet.

|Ltd| (or at least |Lt|) appears to be Rage Italic. Either the |d| was edited or brought in from some other typeface (haven’t found a match to it): http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/linotype/rage-italic/

The rest appears to be garden-variety Helvetica: http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/linotype/helvetica/
