According to Martin Kotulla, SoftMaker is finally extending their 2 versions of "ITC Avant Garde Gothic" and "ITC Lubalin Graph" to include 10 styles each. SoftMaker's versions of those 2 font names being cloned under license from Brendel - "A850 Geometrical" (ITC Avant Garde Gothic clone) and "L850 Slab" (ITC Lubalin Graph clone) - are both finally coming in all 10 styles each for the "regular" parts, and the small-caps parts each have approx. 2 styles. SoftMaker's versions of those 2 font names being cloned under license from URW++ - "Avignon" (ITC Avant Garde Gothic clone) and "Square Serif" (ITC Lubalin Graph clone) - are both finally coming in all 10 styles each for the regular, alternates #1, alternates #2, and old-style figured parts. The "condensed" width parts of each font comes in 4 styles (Book, Medium, Demi Bold & Bold), and the small-caps parts each come in 2 styles (Book & Demi Bold). These will appear on two of SoftMaker's premium-quality typeface collections - "MegaFont XXL 3.0" (for home users) and "infiniType 4" (for designers) - both being released in May 27, 2014.
