I have consulted Hitchcock’s Benton Types. What do you want to know about it? Patricia Cost is the authority on Benton’s life and will be publishing a book on her research. She interviewed his daughters and has a lot of personal information about him. My research was into his type designs, not his life. I wanted to know why he has remained relatively obscure and what made him tick as a designer. The ATypeI presentation was necessarily condensed from the dissertation I did at Reading, but it included some research done post-Reading. If you’d like more information you can contact me offline.
