Find a magazine based near you, and phone the production manager to find out where it is printed. Then phone the printers and ask what sizes they can handle. Most places have a range of sizes, depending on press and configuration.

At the same time you will be able to get some ideas of print runs and (though you will have to talk to a salesperson for this) costs with different types of papers. You will need to know these things as you work out your business plan.
