_ Set text with News Gothic in Photoshop. _ Turn off kerning _ Overlay with subtle texture or noise filter _ Flatten layers _ Gaussian blur (more blur for rounder corners) _ Increase contrast until type edges are sharp _ Adjust brightness to increase or decrease the weight
