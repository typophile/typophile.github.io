Have any fonts/typefaces influenced your handwriting?

August 27, 2010 • Pylon

I’m just wondering, as I’ve noticed I’ve paid more attention to the form of my handwriting ever since I developed an appreciation for various typefaces. For example, I started to add a tail onto my Gs due to the influences of Helvetica and occasionally try to write my Rs like the Helvetica Rs. Also, for a time I tried to make my question marks similar to that of Futura and tried adding a flag to my 1s, again influenced by Futura.

So have any of you changed your handwriting due to the influences of a particular typeface?

Not yet, but I’m reading a book set in Tekton, which is based on the author’s handwriting, and it’s got me thinking about trying to improve my handwriting a little bit. Though my handwriting is a horrible.

2010-08-30 21:07:21 • Nick Job

I overhauled my signature in high school, and my redesign was heavily influenced by Tolkien’s Fëanorian Tengwar, with a lot of sharp descenders and a couple swashes.

Likewise, I redesigned my messy handwriting in high school, shifting to a printed Roman drawn with different strokes many of which simulate serifs (on the B, D, I, J, P, R, and S), which greatly improved the legibility and readability of my printing. I didn’t have a specific serif face in mind when I did the redesign, so it was probably Times New Roman that influenced it the most; still, clarity is one of TNR’s virtues, so I could have chosen a worse model.

No. I used the cursive style I had learned at school by age 10. It became slicker by the time I was an adult, but had the same basic construction. Then around age 30 I studied calligraphy, and subsequently adapted my casual handwriting to use the proper joining rules of the chancery script.

The only typeface I’ve made from my handwriting is Panoptica Script, but it’s not my everyday writing. Handsome, a script face, was “written” in Fontographer with a Wacom stylus, but the effect is too stiff and formal to bear much relationship to my “real” handwriting. One of these days I might make a typeface based on my writing.
