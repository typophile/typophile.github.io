What is the best OT feature to add many ampersands in a font?

September 10, 2010 • paulow

Hi, wise-people. I am developing a script font with many alternates and sets. In your oppinion, what is the best OT feature to add many ampersands in a font?
If you’re doing something that is supposed to feel randomized/varied (as in handwriting), the correct feature would be contextual alternates. If not, consider stylistic sets or stylistic alternates (maybe doubled in titling alternates depending on the intended application support).
