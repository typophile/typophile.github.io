2007-09-08 20:10:30 • ColbyZ

It’s most likely a profile problem, yes.

Just check your color settings in Illustrator and Photoshop (Edit -> Color Settings). They should be set correctly for your workflow, and both be the same. Also make sure you set the Color Management Policies right, probably set to Preserve and check all tickboxes (if the embedded profile is different, it will ask you what to do with it) You should probably do Adobe RGB (1998) for RGB, and whatever CMYK profile is default where you are at (I use Euroscale Coated/Uncoated v2 as default). It’s not waterproof colormanagement, but at least it will be consistent.

I am always amazed when I check the settings at other designers computers, to see they have it set completely wrong. Or not at all. :)
