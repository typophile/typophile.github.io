Saved By The Bell Similar Font

September 30, 2008 • dvann

I assume the logo is probably a custom type. Trying to find a font similar to the Saved By The Bell logo.

I doubt if any fonts were used to make that logo, and at least two styles were used: sans and serif. The fact that the two L’s in BELL are so different supports the idea that these were drawn by hand. Maybe [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/letraset/boink/|Letraset Boink]] could be used for BELL, with some tweaking. [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/pizzadude/el-vistereo/|El Vistereo]] might be a starting point for SAVED, and [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/blhd/soylent-blu-bh/|Soylent Blu]] for BY THe. You could probably find freebie fonts that would also have some of the same feel as these three lettering styles.
