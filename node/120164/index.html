Q: “why it’s NOT Helvetica.” A: At least five letters, G with no spur, R with straight leg, and C, D and O with a vertical section in the curved strokes. The letter S could pass for Helvetica, Arial etc. The letters have some similarities to DIN, except for the S. Might not be a standard font. Don
