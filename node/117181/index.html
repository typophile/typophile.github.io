Matamoros NF is based on a similar but different font – note for example the lazy s counters in O and Q, similar to Inland Foundry’s Inland – from circa 1900 called Jason Wood by Dan X. Solo and sold by him in digital form with one of the Dover Books. Used for the title of the Solotype catalog. I don’t know the designer, original name or foundry. Don
