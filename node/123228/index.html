Chesterfield was designed by Alan Meeks for Letraset in 1977. The glyphs in the digital Chesterfield, including _T_ and _F_, seem to match the original, as shown in my Letraset product catalog from 1985. Chesterfield was also digitizatized by Uwe Borchert as Mops Antiqua (Pug Serif) http://luc.devroye.org/fonts-56498.html It does not have the modified _T_ and _F_. So I agree with Fivos that the letters in_FOUNDATION_ are customized. Don
