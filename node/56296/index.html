OK, been concepting this for a little bit and I feel I'm close, but still not quite there. Starting to resemble broccoli to me. Been looking at it for too long and need some feedback. appreciate any I can get. Thanks.[img:sites/default/files/old-images/landmanagment_4347.jpg]
What exactly is this? Is it a landscaping service? I think the concept is promising either way. You need to give the tree a little more detail. Add branches where the trunk ends and the leaves start and maybe don't have the tree all blacked out. That forces you to notice the outer shape that resembles broccoli. Have a few 'holes' in between the leaves here and there. Also, add length to the trunk...that will help too. Have you tried anything else besides a wrench? I think it works to a degree, but I get more of a 'handyman' feel from it. JonE.
