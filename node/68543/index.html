Annoying Larabie Fonts

March 19, 2010 • Ray Larabie

I started making Larabie Fonts typefaces about 14 years ago. Some of the old versions were shit and I’m sure lots of designers had technical headaches working with them . . . or convincing clients to use a proper font. But I’ve been improving them over the years. Currently MyFonts is the only place that carries the very latest versions. My point is: if you have the misfortune of having problems with an early Larabie Font, please try the latest version. If it’s still screwed, contact me and I’ll fix it.
