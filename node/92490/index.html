Celebrating 3 years of our small digital foundry

April 25, 2012 • tourdeforce

Dear Typophiles, On 7th May, we'll make small internal celebration for our 3rd birthday since we published our first font family under our label – Tour de Force Font Foundry. We would like you to suggest one our font family that you'd like to see with discount 50% off! The list of font families can be found here: [[http://www.tourdefonts.com/font-catalog/]] Place your vote on Twitter ([[https://twitter.com/#!/tourdefonts]]) until 4th May, we'll count them all and choose the one most voted and make it available for the 7th May only. We'd like to thank all nice people we met (mostly online), experiences they shared, things we learned and all our old and new customers. Thanks all. - Dusan * Discount will be only available trough MyFonts, since their easy step-by-step promotion assistance.
