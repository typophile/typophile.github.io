As I announced in the "OK. TypeCon's over for now. Who's going to ATypI?" thread, I (we?) will be organizing a Typophile Meet'n'Greet in Brighton. This as a reaction to the recurring complaints after every conference that fellow Typophiles didn't get the chance to meet, often because they even didn't know they were there. I propose that the gathering takes place on Friday night after the ATypI Auction which starts at 18h45. I don't know yet where we'll have it, especially since I haven't got a clue how many will want to join us. Maybe it would be an idea to have some registration. What does everyone think?
