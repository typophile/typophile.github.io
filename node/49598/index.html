Museo Sans release

September 18, 2008 • Jos Buivenga

[[http://www.josbuivenga.demon.nl/museosans.html|Museo Sans]] has been released. It comes in 10 styles: five weights with each an italic. Two styles (500 & 500 italic) are free. [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/exljbris/museo-sans/|Available at MyFonts]].

Nice.

Looking at it I keep thinking ‘the other Avenir’ (‘other’ meaning nice alternative).
