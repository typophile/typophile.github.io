Behind every beautiful typeface there is a person.
Mark van Bronkhurst, who designed ITC Conduit in 1997,
depends on the sales of his work (the types he designs)
for an income. I don't think he'd appreciate it if the
fruit of his labour (and believe me: it takes a LOT of
time, work and dedication to design a succesful typeface)
was given away for free, just as you wouldn't like
it if the logos you design were not paid for.

I don't want to sound preachy, but this is the third
request for font piracy in a week on "our" board,
and I don't think I really like this evolution. It's
like going to a jewelers' convention and ask where
you could steal some jewelry. Get the picture?
