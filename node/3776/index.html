2004-03-29 19:35:38 • wolaka
> volume would be best expressed with point size.

I agree, but that's outside of the font itself. You could say that Amplitude is proportional to apparent size, and that depends on point size, yes, but also weight and x-height propotion - like how ITC Garamond is much "louder" than let's say Simoncini Garamond (at the same point size).

Inflection/stress: Tough one.
Italics might seem natural, but I would caution against it, since:
1) Slant introduces Speed.
2) Cursiveness introduces informality/friendliness.

I think Stress might be best marked with stroke contrast.
