Hi, This is my first post as I just found this website. :-) I'm working on redesigning a magazine that's about helicopters. I think the current set of fonts looks a bit dated (most body text in Dutch 801, headlines in Futura). I'm looking at a lot of helicopter trade magazines and noticing most of them use sans-serif for the body text. Could anyone make any font suggestions? I'm looking for something that's easy to read but with a little bit of style to it. Does the helicopter subject suggest anything to anyone? I could also just change to a different serif for the body text but just noticed most of the trade mags are in a sans. I'll be the first to admit I'm not great at picking out fonts. That's why I'm posting. :-) I have the Adobe folio (so could use any of those) though I'd also consider investing in something new. Any thoughts? Thanks, phyllis
