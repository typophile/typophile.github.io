  for drawing lettershapes on, prior to scanning is Clearprint: <BR><A HREF="http://www.clearprintpaper.com/media/vellums.html"
  TARGET="_top">http://www.clearprintpaper.com/media/vellums.html</A> <BR> <BR>They
  might have changed their numbering system since I last bought some, but I used to
  use the 1000HP with 10x10 grid. This has a tenth of an inch grid which to me is
  much more humane than working in millimeters. Also it is a vellum, which in Clearprint
  terms means the paper is made of cotton and stands up to a lot of erasing and ink
  pools. It is also see through for tracing purposes.'
