Logicalnot, these Forums are run by people from the type community. i think it would be quite illogical for us to point people to solutions that undermine the work of our very own people. You might not realize that, but Pill Gothic didn't just magically appear on a computer somewhere far far away and miraculously made its way to the Veer website. It was carefully designed, digitized, kerned and whatnot by a type designer, a living, breathing person who toiled away for endless hours crafting a beautiful typeface which performs impeccably in graphic and lay-out programs. This living, breathing person has rent or mortgages to pay, eat and drink, maybe support a family, enjoy some recreation once in a while. Ask yourself, how would you feel if someone asked us to provide whatever you do for a living for free, so you wouldn't be able to earn a decent living anymore? Need I go on?
