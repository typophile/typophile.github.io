Novecento (the family)

September 24, 2011 • Synthview

Dear typophiles, I’d like to present here the revised and improved version of Novecento, an uppercase-only font family inspired on european typographic tendencies between the second half of 19th century and first half of the 20th. Initially offered for free as Novecento Wide, my family has now a condensed, a narrow and a regular versions. In addition, extra light and extra bold weights were added. I’ve also added more OT features (12 now) and extra glyphs. Please discover Novecento font family at [[http://typography.synthview.com]]
Congrats! :-)
