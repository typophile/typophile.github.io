Hi,

I have five fonts to ID, all from a magazine from Hungary, from the 1980s - though these fonts are probably much older. Perhaps neither of them are digitized, but knowing the name or some info on them would be great.

The first one resembles Metro but it is definitely not that.

The second looks like a French design or a take on Antique Olive - but more varied than it.

The third looks like a metal engraver font - and I bet that it has more weights, but I have only found this weight in the magazine.

The fourth and fifth are most probably some kind of old DIN (Deutsche Industrie Norme) standard faces - they were widely used in Hungary in print and on signs. The two are different faces (just note the differences in the uppercase J and the question mark). The fifth also has a bold weight.

Any help would be much appreciated! Thank you, Gabor.Recska
