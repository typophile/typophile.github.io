Doesn't anyone want to help the kid? It's a Windows to Mac conversion problem. A quick Google search finds (from this page): "Another problem is the Euro currency symbol. In most of the new Macintosh fonts the general currency symbol has been replaced by the Euro symbol and in most of the new Windows fonts a formerly unused position got the Euro symbol. There is even a Unicode position defined for the Euro symbol: 20AC hexadecimal, which is the same as 8364 decimal."
