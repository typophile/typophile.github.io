Answering on ‘2PACALYPSE’ piece (thought like Don it could have been ‘FEAR OF A BLACK PLANET’ which probably set with [[http://www.myfonts.com/search/antique+olive|Antique Olive]] BTW). For the rest, at this size, the 2 /A look different to me so I’d say “not-a-font/hand-done”. Even if that sounds cliché, I’d probably have a look to some fonts tagged at MyFonts ‘[[http://www.myfonts.com/tags/graffiti|graffiti]]’, ‘[[http://www.myfonts.com/tags/tag|tag]]’ or ‘[[http://www.myfonts.com/tags/hip+hop|hip hop]]’. DaFont [[http://www.dafont.com/theme.php?cat=606|Graffiti section]] is also worth a look. This lettering has also a ‘[[http://www.myfonts.com/search/comic|comic]]’ feeling so maybe having a look to [[http://www.myfonts.com/foundry/Blambot|Blambot]], [[http://www.myfonts.com/foundry/Comicraft|Comicraft]], [[http://www.myfonts.com/foundry/Canada_Type|Canada Type]] and (My)Fonts tagged ‘[[http://www.myfonts.com/search/tag%3Acomic|comic]]’ would be useful too. Finally, I would also browse [[http://www.myfonts.com/foundry/Typodermic|Typodermic]] and [[http://www.myfonts.com/foundry/PizzaDude.dk|Pizzadude]] collections. Following these tracks and without too much effort, I’ve found [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/pizzadude/show-no-shame|Show No Shame]], a Pizzadude font which looks not so far with its arced /A “à-la-[[http://www.myfonts.com/search/hobo|Hobo]]”.
