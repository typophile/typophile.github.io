TC07: Design is a fight...

August 2, 2007 • Joe Pemberton

Design is a fight against ugly. -- Massimo Vignelli describing his modernist design philosophy in the Helvetica film. Great juxtaposition of characters versus David Carson, purveyor of ugly.
Ugly, like beauty, is often in the eye of the beholder. But if you can make a career out of ugly, then that would seem to be a reflection on our society. Perhaps people become so desensatized to the marketing of 'beauty' that 'ugly' catches them off-guard. Then one can only wonder what happens when they become desensatized to ugly. I submit that the result would be Ultimate Fighting, or "human cockfighting" as it is sometimes referred to. Believe it or not, some people see a beauty in that. Go figure...
