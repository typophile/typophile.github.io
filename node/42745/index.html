2008-03-03 19:21:18 • Alessandro Segalini

Warren Chappell’s [[http://www.amazon.com/Short-History-Printed-Word/dp/0881791547/ref=sr_1_1?ie=UTF8&s=books&qid=1204733673&sr=8-1|A Short History of the Printed Word]] is really good but it covers only the printing period. It was updated by Robert Bringhurst.

On a much more “calligraphic” or writing approach, George Jean’s [[http://www.amazon.com/Lecriture-Memoire-Archeologie-Georges-Jean/dp/207053040X/ref=sr_1_1?ie=UTF8&s=books&qid=1204733772&sr=1-1|L’Écriture mémoire des hommes]] is really good, if you understand French. It was republished by Gallimard in 2007.
