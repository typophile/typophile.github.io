Hey Elle, I'm a recently graduated student as-well. I found the best way to seek out good printers was to simply ask my tutors in college, they are probably from your area and will already have a personal relationship built up with a particular printer. When it comes to your printing specs just ask the printer! On my first real job I talked to a good 5 printers and each time I grilled them for information. Not just about my upcoming project but anything that came to mind. They should be more then happy to help and answer all your questions.
