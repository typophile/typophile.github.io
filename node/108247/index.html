Traditionally Hebrew does not use this kind of decorative approach.

Have a look at http://www.typophile.com/node/35367 if you must! Also at David, http://typophile.com/node/90718 especially the inclined forms.

To compound your problems, Hebrew does not naturally incline to the right. Modern Hebrew fonts that do incline to the right are offering almost a strong maybe aggressive ‘in your face’ kind of emphasis quite unlike traditional italian italics. Mike

(Stay away from Gill. He did not understand Hebrew.)
