  • Color: Black and white only

The nibbed flat pen is well suited to writing this way. It is much harder to stay vertical with your arm/hand movement. Also, spacing is very predictable with almost uniwidth letters with parallel sides. In centuries past, many books were written with this hand and are still marveled at today. Buy a flat pen and try it! It is actually kind of fun.
