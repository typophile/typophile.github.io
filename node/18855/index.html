Just like any software product, pricing will vary according to the vendor.

You’re looking at essentially the same font, available from different foundries. The Creative Alliance version was licensed from ITC by Monotype and they chose to sell it at a different price. Now that Monotype owns the ITC collection it seems strange that they still offer it through both foundries, I know.

The OpenType version is sometimes priced higher for no other reason than it offers simplicity (1 file) and cross-platformability (Mac and Windows). For some folks, this convenience is worth a few extra bucks.

I understand a bit of confusion in seeing the same font data for different fees, but I have to ask: you’re spending hours to save $15? That’s not too grand a price break.
