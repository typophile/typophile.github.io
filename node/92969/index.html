Cordia New is a Thai font that happens to include Arial characters in it to support Latin. The more likely answer is that it's Arial.
2012-05-13 01:17:11 • Mark Simonson
You are right Mark, it's more likely an Arial ;) The only difference I can see is that: a. Cordia New's Latin characters look much thinner than Arial at the same weight (e.g. Normal) and b. you need to specify a point-size of 20 to Cordia New to match the height of a 14 pts Arial
