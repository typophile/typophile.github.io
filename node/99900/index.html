Lots of variations in the picture; does anyone recognize this?

The letter “h” is pretty distinctive, as are the ampersands “&” and the way the tail of the lowercase “y” bends, and the way the bottom tip of lowercase “t” sweeps up at severe angle rather than a curve.

It must be [[http://www.findmyfont.com/index.php/fonts/font-preview?fset=FontShop&ffam=Scala%20Sans%20-%20Bold&fstyle=&fsize=60&fid=db8c0f622c0071be30ae331517cee3d5&wrap=2&text=Users%20love%20it.%20empoyees%20%26%20engaging|FF Scala Sans]] by Martin Majoor (1990, FontFont)
