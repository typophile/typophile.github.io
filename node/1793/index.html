A fine old Victoria printer, Morriss Printing Ltd., is making some of its letterpress-related equipment and supplies available for purchase. Included are metal fonts (both lead and brass), proof presses, cutters, storage, and etc. Although a "garage sale" is planned for November 1 and 2 at 1745 Blanshard Street, you can sneak an early look by phoning Keijo (pronounced kay-oh) Isomaa at 250 656 2807 and arranging a mutually convenient time. There is a lot of certified "cool" stuff.
