Glyphs that Gall

January 15, 2007 • pdxuser

Does anyone else get annoyed with certain letter shapes in fonts? For example, I can’t stand the foot of the R in Gill Sans stretching out well beyond its bowl above. It’s as though it’s taunting, “Don’t even think about auto-kern.” The cockeyed serifs of Garamond’s T also niggle me. I want to nudge them like I would an oil painting hung askew on a wall. In fact, I don’t know if I’ve ever used Garamond or Gill Sans in any project, probably because I see them as flawed. Are there others who get vexed by a P or peeved by an X?

I hate the counters of the R and P in Palatino; that little angle in the curve becomes visible at around 13 points and irritates the hell out of me.

Of course, at 10 points it’s a gorgeous book face, so I can live with it.
