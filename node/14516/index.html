2005-08-16 17:49:03 • caboume
I don't get the point of the pill. Before you introduced it, you were on your way to a nicely abstract logo. The pill makes it so much more figurative that a) you lose the European feel and b) you risk stirring associations you might not want. If by European you mean highly simplified and sober, I'd go back to the first image. Using the pill makes me think much more of American than European logo's. Something else: have you tried scaling the image slight up? I think the subtly round edges should aline with the round tops of the o and e. Optically round forms need to extend slightly over the top and bottom of the x-height to create the illusion of eveness.
