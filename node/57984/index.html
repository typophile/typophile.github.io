2009-05-14 04:45:07 • Frode Bo Helland

Two problems:

1: Your writing implement metaphor is inconsistent. Your ‘C’ looks like a brush stroke, while your ‘h’ looks like a broad nip pen stroke.

2: Your ductus is inconsistent. Let’s say you decide on a broad nip pen metaphor, then how do you hold it, in what angle do you write (stress axis), with which speed and with what amount of contrast modulation?
