I don’t think this was based on a pre-existing font. The p’s are just inverted d’s. The line weight and end strokes are inconsistent. The G is just horribly outlined, and really bumpy. I think the l was flipped for some reason. The stroke on the o is as thick as the G.

If it was based on a font, it was then horribly mangled into what we see today.

I would believe that it was hand done and then scanned digitally.
