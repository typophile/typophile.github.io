There are so many. I could recommend one of mine. But I think the best thing you could do is go to a bookstore and look through a few magazines, and find ones whose body text you like and that you think would be suitable for your layout, client and readers. You don’t have to copy the fonts or setting exactly, but it will help focus your thinking.
