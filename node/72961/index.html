2010-08-06 15:54:54 • bowfinpw
For your capital R sketch, it's hard to decide how close to your sketch you want the font to be. There is a whole spectrum of variations from very simple to rather ornate that could resemble that general shape. You could check various parts of my Script Font ID Guide, especially parts 7, 8 and 13, but almost any part could have a few fonts with similar R shapes. I picked three different styles just as examples: Rob Leuschke's Oh Ley for a fun, casual interpretation; the handwriting font Miss Le Gatees, from Sudtipos, and the wildly elaborate display script Affair, also from Sudtipos, which has many alternates. - Mike Yanega
