Skeletal Feet poster feedback.

July 15, 2008 • Eli Jager

This is a poster I made at the Rhode Island School of Design pre-college summer program that I’m currently attending, it was made for a basic design class. The project was to make a painting inspired by an assigned sin, I was assigned the sin of sloth. I very much wanted to make a super graphic, beautiful, and jumpy representation of such a brown, slow, and tired sin. I first made it in illustrator then had it printed out in black and white, cut that out into a stencil then traced it onto a sheet of BFK paper, I then painted it using red gauche. As always, your feedback is more than welcome.
Looks cool. Composition is solid. Definitely an interesting approach to Sloth – took me a second – but yeah – I like it. Hard to tell at this resolution – but the edges seem a little muddy. I could be wrong.
