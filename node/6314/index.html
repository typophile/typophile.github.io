Hello again Just came back from holidays and a lot of work is waiting. One of them it is about a freelancer whose subject is "space sculpting". Actually she is making the work of an interior designer but she also works for public places as squares and monuments. So she is arranging the space in an artistic and functional way, let's say. As you see I have two solutions. In Aii the idea is to use the 5 colours you see and each time to apply one of them as a background colour of the media. The sequence of the colours remains the same but the "colour-bar" moves as one of the colours becomes the same with background. I am looking forward to read your comments. christos
