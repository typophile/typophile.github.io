2007-09-21 23:58:38 • fontplayer
I would map the characters onto a 3d model in wireframe Illustrator CS or Dimensions, simple enough with a regular shape like a balloon, copy the characters into a new layer – still requires adjustment though. Another thing you might try is to create a rectangle to represent the face of the balloon, place your characters within it and envelope distort both elements (have the balloon shape visible in the background to guide you), this should reduce the effect of having/not having ascenders and descenders.
