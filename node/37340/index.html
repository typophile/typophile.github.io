2007-09-21 17:30:10 • Stefan Seifert

Alignment Zones: Font Info -> Hinting Settings -> Primary Zones (right panel).

For example, if you want/need Alignment Zones of 15 units, you add two Primary Zones, one from -15 to 0, one from 1000 to 1015. I don’t know the technical usefulness of it, but I believe they are mainly as space for overshooting round and/or pointed shapes below and above the x-hight. And I guess that helps hinting.
