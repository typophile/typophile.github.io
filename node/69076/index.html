mixing typefaces: frutiger – caecilia?

April 3, 2010 • jdpicc

I’m working on a booklet, and was wondering if these two typefaces, Frutiger (sans serif) and Caecilia (slab serif) would work well together. Frutiger would be used in headings/subheads, while Caecilia would be applied to body text.

Anyone have experience with these two typefaces?

In capable hands, there is no reason why PMN Caecilia cannot work with Frutiger. Better still, Frutiger Next.

For the opposite query, where Caecilia is the display face in need of a text companion, Cf. http://typophile.com/node/50705
