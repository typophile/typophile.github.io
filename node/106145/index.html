As stated in the title, it’s a logo(work). Don’t know any font that could match it. Doesn’t look to hard to reproduce though. You could start with any generic sans of your choice, add an outline stroke, vectorize lettering and remove all terminal bars to recreate this “gas pipe” treatment. [[http://www.myfonts.com/search/Avenir|Avenir]] or even the ubiquitous [[http://www.myfonts.com/search/Helvetica|Helvetica]] (Neue) would do the job quite nicely.
