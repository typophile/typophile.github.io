Step 1: Choose your best work and break it down into categories. Don’t limit how much work you choose. Step 2: Photograph all your physical pieces at once, using the same lighting and camera. Retouch all images to have similar color balance. Step 3: Collect screen shots of your digital work. Step 4: Sort all the images into categorized folders so that you can easily reuse them for multiple online and offline portfolios. Step 5: Write descriptive copy for everything. Step 6: Set up a huge indesign file of your portfolio. Put in everything. Don’t include page numbers! Step 7: Generate a custom PDF featuring the most appropriate work for every application!
