I’ve been looking over various versions of [[News Gothic]] and it appears that Monotype’s version is more suited for text use and smaller sizes (which is what I’m looking for). Can anyone else confirm this? Are there any other versions that any of you prefer more?

Thanks, Addison

i cant add anything really helpful, but maybe benton sans instead of the original news gothic?

In 1903 faced with the welter of sans offered by ATF, Morris Fuller Benton designed News Gothic, a 20th century standard. In 1995 Tobias Frere-Jones studied drawings in the Smithsonian and started a redesign. Cyrus Highsmith reviewed News Gothic, and with the Font Bureau studio expanded it into Benton Sans.
