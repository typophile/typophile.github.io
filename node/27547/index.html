Should be: Veronique Vial, fashion and celebrity photographer, grew up in Paris where her family runs a publishing house. Living in Los Angeles, she has eleven photobooks behind her, e.g. Cirque de Soleil, Hollywood Splash and Men before 10AM

The paper types I like for my photos, they are matt or semi matt and really white.
