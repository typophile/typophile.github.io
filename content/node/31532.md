body: "During the 3rd International Conference on Typography and Visual Communication
  (Thessaloniki, Greece, this June) an exhibition will be held with the aim of capturing
  current trends in the design of Greek typographic alphabets. The event, co-organised
  by the 3rd International Conference on Typography and Visual Communication and backpacker.gr,
  invite typeface designers to participate in a typographic exhibition centred on
  the \"Greek alphabet\" (\u03B5\u03B1). The participants are asked to submit Greek
  characters sufficient to set the following sentence:\r\n\u03AD\u03BD\u03B1 \u03B1\u03BD\u03B1\u03BC\u03C6\u03B9\u03C3\u03B2\u03AE\u03C4\u03B7\u03C4\u03B1
  \u03BE\u03B5\u03C7\u03C9\u03C1\u03B9\u03C3\u03C4\u03CC \u03B3\u03B5\u03B3\u03BF\u03BD\u03CC\u03C2\r\nThe
  selected entries will be exhibited during the Conference, and will be included in
  the exhibition catalog. After the conference the exhibition might travel to other
  selected venues in order to be presented to a wider audience. \r\nMore details can
  be found at http://ictvc.org/3/97/&pid=115\r\n\r\n(on behalf of http://www.ictvc.org
  , where updates will be posted) "
