This is an archetypal Tuscan style produced by various Victorian foundries, sometimes with with relatively minor variations. I know the quality of Michael Hagemann’s version [Buckhorn based on an old font called Broadside] is first class. If your examination of Big Top shows that it is of good enough quality for your purposes, you can follow Ryuk’s suggestion. If you feel Big Top is not quite as high a quality as you would like, I suggest you follow Kevin’s suggestion of Buckhorn and edit as required. Don
