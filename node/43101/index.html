How do I shake the blues? (in FontLab)

March 12, 2008 • dezcom

While I go merrily along kerning using class-based kerning in FontLab, I would like a way to turn the blue color off of the non-key glyphs in the Metrics window. It would be great if I could just hit the tilde key for a second while I judge the proximity of a kern pair but I would settle for something clumsier if that were all that were possible. I have tried clicking on the “Preview” mode but that does not seem to do anything at all. I get all characters in black only while in metrics mode but this does not help me judge a kerning pair. Is there some hidden magic I have overlooked or is this just another known bug that has not been fixed?

ChrisL

Is there some hidden magic I have overlooked […] ?

Indeed: Preferences > Metrics Window > Metrics window colors
