Suzee, Coco and Signalist are interesting as being close on m and n but more angular than the other lower case letters in Tim Hortons. All of the suggestions are useful for their insights as to how a Tim Hortons font could be constructed. An interesting possible alternative to the endless assortment of bulgy irregular doodlings and creamy packaging letters that pass for modern thick scripts. Don
