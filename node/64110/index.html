“Crescendo” appears to be set in Apple Capitals. If this is the case, the last letter is an “O”, but I can’t stop reading “Crescend-Zero”. :(

Well, you’re absolutely right. As a matter of fact, that “O” is very narrow, especially compared to the “C” it is supposed to counterbalance. That’s a trait of the typeface itself that the logo’s designer didn’t bothered to compensate. In fact, had they used an actual “0”, it would have appeared slightly better.

That said, what’s wrong with terminal 0s? ;-)
