Annie Up is interesting. A blend of the old design for Concave Tuscan, e.g. http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/intellecta/concave-tuscan/ with the mini serifs of a font such as Memo: http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/mti/memo/pro-medium/ Hope someone finds a match for you. It would be a real challenge to make a font that could look like Pistol Annies. This would require special ligatures for most of the letter combinations. So it’s probably lettering. Don
