As Birdseeding rightly surmised, this poor typeface has been abused to make it resemble the original hand-drawn logo. It has been artificially compressed (horizontally) and straightened closer to vertical (by about 15 degrees, I’d say).

Snapper Script, by Art and Sign: http://artandsignstudio.com/snapper.html
