2005-06-30 21:11:28 • Dan Weaver

You need to look for:

  1. Die Brücke (the bridge)
  2. Der Blaue Reiter (the blue rider)

To see samples of “fonts” (or lettering) look for the woodcuts by Erich Heckel; and also see the work by George Grosz, Käthe Kollwitz ( i just love her work!!!).

Fonts? maybe look for the advertisement designed by John Heartfield; around that time you’ll find more about the Bauhaus.
