It is indeed custom. Drawn by [[http://covers.fwis.com/everything_is_illuminated|Jon Gray]] and digitized by FontShop I believe. The actual name is HP PSG. It exists as a full typeface and also has numerous alternate vector drawings of single letters.

The text that reads “The Computer is Personal Again” is actually an official logo lockup so it is was made separately from the typeface.
