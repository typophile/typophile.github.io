A lot of sign makers (at least in Germany, Switzerland …) have this typeface. It’s sort of a DIN rounded. DIN17 SB by Scangraphic is too wide, though. Cornel Windlin made a font, explicitly based on these name plates. It’s called Gravur Condensed and comes very close, imho. If you don’t need a digital font, just ask your local sign makers, probably that’ll do.
