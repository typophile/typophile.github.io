Chocolate pilcrow

December 5, 2009 • 72pt

[img:sites/default/files/old-images/capitulum_header_5545.jpg] I wanted to share my latest typography-related project with you. Every year on the 5th of December we celebrate Sinterklaas in the Netherlands. One of the traditional gifts is the chocolate letter, you will probably get the first letter of your name or maybe an S (or M). For a while I had been thinking about making my own chocolate letter, or more specific, a pilcrow. I will not go into detail, but this week I finished the first batch and I thought I'd share it with you. [img:sites/default/files/old-images/pilcrow_04_chocolade_09_4549.jpg] You can read about it (in Dutch) here, or pictures of the process can be found here. I enjoyed doing this project and I'd like to hear what you think :)
