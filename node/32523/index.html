Letterforms in the mind

March 25, 2007 • michelemiller

Spring cleaning–I’ve been going through my papers and found some printed samples of 1966 typography studies from students in Basel. Always amazing.

A friend emailed some PNGs of a face she’s working on–very fresh and multi-dimensional. So I’m sitting here at the kitchen table with a large “A” on my laptop and in the distance, in my field of vision is Tom Geismar’s “Z” poster.

So I’m wondering, what is it about letterforms, that psychological something, that’s so evocative?

You love them.
