An example for the “hallowed” style might be ITC Tiffany: http://new.myfonts.com/fonts/itc/tiffany/

However most fonts that have a “hallowed” apex to the “A” have a simple pointed vertex in the “V”, as in most versions of Caslon for instance. Be mindful that such details aren’t always “mirrorable”, or so straightforward in type.

BTW, ITC Benguiat also has the “full serif”.
