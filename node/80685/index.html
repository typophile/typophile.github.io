After some research: the florin isn’t used in any programming language (at least the most popular 50 according to TIOBE), but Mac users use it for “folder”. I’ve tried a few alternatives for the f and the nicest one was B with a one-pixel descender.

The & looks a little squished now, but it doesn’t disturb the surrounding letters anymore.
