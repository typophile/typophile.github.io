In France this type of script was known as Calligraphics and a version was produced by at least one French foundry, Deberny & Peignot. Closer to the lettering is Graphic Script Bold by Bauer, 1934: The lettering is more upright and is heavier with lower contrast. It is possible that this adaptation has been produced as a digital font, but I have not seen it. Don
