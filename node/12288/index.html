May 9, 2005 • Jason Smith

[[Indices]] : [[Type Foundries]] : Fontsmith [[Fontsmith]] is a leading London based type design studio founded in 1999. The studio consists of a skilled team of creatives, lead by [[Jason Smith]] and [[Phil Garnham]]. Fontsmith is dedicated to designing and developing high quality typefaces for both independent release as well as bespoke fonts for international clients.
