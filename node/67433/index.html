I’ve scoured Myfonts for this face, but can’t find it – though I’ve found many like it. Does anyone have an ideas?

Well, if you know this is from the 60’s that should tell you why finding similar fonts could be hard. Many pre-digital fonts never made it into digital form.

The most similar font I could think of is called [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/berthold/arbiter-bq/|Arbiter]], by Aldo Novarese.

I have scoured the Solotype Catalog, Rookledge’s Typefinder and some of my Photo-Lettering books. It has a familiar look, so it’s driving me nuts. I think Mark Simonson has a pretty good collection of older type books, and might recognize this.

I am still looking.
