Western Style Font in Logo

October 18, 2012 • mmp-designer

Client is looking to have their logo recreated in Vector. All they have is an old raster. It has come down to either finding the font or hand-retracing the text, which I have little desire to do.

Perhaps someone out there knows what this is, or at least something close enough.

This looks like it was hand-drawn to look sort of like a Western font, but I can’t find anything with its same features in one font. Font Mesa has the best collection of these Western styles, and I would suggest a blend of [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/fontmesa/miss-scarlett/|Miss Scarlett Plain]] and [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/fontmesa/cowboy-western/|Cowboy Western]] would give you a more professional looking Western font with most of these features.
