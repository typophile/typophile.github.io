"Ristorante" is very, very similar to the font, Freshscript - but you will have to do a slight modification to the letter "t" to match it. "pizzeria" I believe is ITC Eras Book - with a lot of letter spacing. "GNA" is Zapf Chancery - but you will have to chop off the tail of the "G" to make it match. But as for "West Reading, PA" - no idea, but you may want to try looking in the Brush family.
