Given the brief ("other fonts similar or even dissimilar that create the same look and feel" & "a good fit for the cover title"), our chef recommends for your consideration Linotype Le Griffe (designer: Andre-Michel Lubac; publisher: Linotype), P22 Afton (designer: Ted Staunton; publisher: Sherwood Type), Medici Script (designer: Hermann Zapf; publishers: Adobe and Linotype), or even Feather Pen (designer: John Nahmias; publisher: JonahFonts). Hope this helps.
