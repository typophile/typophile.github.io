Akzidenz-Grotesk was released by Berthold in 1896 as a metal type – digital came in the 1980s. Also available as metal type from Linotype and Intertype. Your book is definitely pre-digital so ignore the digital release dates. Digital versions are not necessarily a perfect match to the metal type. Don
