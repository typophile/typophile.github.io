body: "<strong>[[Indices]] : [[Designers]] : Doyald Young</strong>\r\n\r\nDoyald Young
  is a designer of typefaces, custom lettering, logotypes, and has been involved in
  the field since 1955. Not only is Young a designer, he is also a teacher, and has
  been teaching for many years at the [[Art Center College of Design]] in California.
  In 2001 he was honored for his contributions by being named an Inaugural Master
  of the School. To date he has also worked on custom lettering for a number of high
  profile clients such as the General Electric, Art Directors Guild, Prudential Financial,
  and the Grammy Awards. To date Young has designed one commercially available script
  typeface called Young Baroque, but has designed many other custom types for use
  in corporate identities such as Home Run, and Prudential Roman.\r\n\r\nThroughout
  his career he has published five books:\r\n\r\n[[Logotypes and Letterforms]]\r\nHandlettered
  Logotypes\r\nTypographic Considerations\r\n[[Fonts and Logos]]\r\nThe Art of the
  Letter\r\nDangerous Curves\r\n\r\nLogotypes & Letterforms and Fonts & Logos are
  currently available through <a href=\"http://www.delphipress.com\">Delphi Press</a>,
  Young's own imprint."
