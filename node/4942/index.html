May 26, 2004 • A. Scott Britton

If the first "press" established in the Americas was in use in Mexico City in 1539, what are the chances that a monumental text by a Jesuit sometime near 1599–in what was then a remote region of Mexico–utilized a typeface designed in the Americas rather than Europe? I guess my question is, which is more likely under these circumstances, a Mexican or European typeface?
My money's on European.
