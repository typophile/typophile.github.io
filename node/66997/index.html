I realise this may be a long shot as the type is quite small. Unfortunately I couldn’t find any bigger versions of this cover. Maybe the one-storey “a”, peculiar “y”, and swash on the capital “G” (the descending “F” may very well be a customisation) rings a bell with someone who knows what this one is.

I am probably very-very off track here but tome it looks like someone took y and G from a Goudy Modern Italic and straightened them.

But I am probably totally wrong.
