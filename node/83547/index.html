Pretty! I think most of the bottom text (not the «Workers Club») is Knockout, which is available in a wide range of widths and references old wood type: http://www.typography.com/fonts/font_overview.php?productLineID=100013&path=head

Though in the bottom there’s something else too (for the times: 4PM–LATE, 12NOON–1AM and also the 1AM LICENSE bit is a different typeface (note the raised vertex of the “M” for instance, the cuts of the “S”, and Knockout has a different “1” with no beak – actually maybe that’s why they substituted something else here.)
