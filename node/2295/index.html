This is the font used by the publisher of AD&D, TSR, for their 1st edition releases in the Dark Sun product line. I have found similar fonts, but none that match it precisely.

I have chosen letters for the sample that are some of the most distinctive of the font as a whole. If this still is not enough for a positive ID, I can provide a whole page of scanned text.

Good luck…I don't think you can do it. (Just challenging you to excellence.)

OptiFonts are handled these days by Castcraft.

Their website is, to say the least, outdated and the only part that
functions usefully is the Designer Collections link. Since Opti
Packard is not in any of the four Designer Collections and the
Online Catalog images don't appear, you would need to contact
Castcraft.

Over the last few years, I've contacted them by e-mail 5 times and
received a reply once. Hopefully you'll fare better. I'm sure that they
could provide the font in the format of your choosing.

As to which font would be most accurate, it seems likely that the
OptiFont was used. Since Dieter Steffmann's version is free, you
can check it out for yourself. It doesn't fare the best on-screen, so
be sure to print a sample paragraph or two to see it on paper,
where it looks splendid.
