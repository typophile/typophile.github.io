What you posted isn’t a typeface.

In the era of moveable type (wood and metal), catchwords—common small words such as “and” and “the”—were complete units of type that could be plugged into layouts. They were often decorative and/or angled (like your sample) and were used in letterhead and headline design.

Searching MyFonts using the term “catchwords” will turn up dozens of digital typefaces that might include something similar.
