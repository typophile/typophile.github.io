In addition to what is written in the wiki, I might add another speculation about the origin of serifs, one that I heard in a documentary many years ago: the serifs were put at the extremes of the letters to reduce the possible erosion faced by outdoors writings, to make rain flow at the sides of the letter instead of stagnating and eroding at the bottom of the vertical parts.
