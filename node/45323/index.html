Base,

I would let the both “T” go together and form a “ligature”. Too much space between them both as it is now. Another option would be to shorten the inner part of both “T” if you want the letters to be separated. Tighten about 25-30%. Secondly I would also tighten up the kerning on the pay-off about 20-30% and som extra on the space between “brand” and value. It might just be the picture, but to me the vertical stroke seems too thin to my eyes. Good luck.
