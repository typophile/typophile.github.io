If you’re interested there’s a great paperback published by the Pro Helvetia, the Swiss Arts Council, called ‘Book Design in Switzerland’ (ISBN 3-908102-12-x) written by Jost Hochuli (with a profile of Lars Müller along with people like Max Caflisch and Hans Rudolf Bosshard). They were sending this out for free a couple of years ago. Might be worth writing to see if they have any left http://www.prohelvetia.ch/Contact.23.0.html?&L=4
