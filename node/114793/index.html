I GAVE A MAN! The scripts we have suggested so far lack the tone of the anguished and explosive cry for help of this lettering. Note the sharp corners. As a substitute I would use BiffBamBoom by Comicraft http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/comicraft/biff-bam-boom/ The lettering is very successful because it expresses the vigor shown in BiffBamBoom within the frame work of a less staccato treatment of the tops and bottoms of letters. A good project for a font maker. Somewha similar idea with Piekos FX http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/blambot/piekos-fx-bb/ Don
