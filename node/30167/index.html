Page sizes of magazines are affected by standard paper sizes and web widths. Typical point-of-purchase racks also follow suit. The user needs something portable fitting in a briefcase and storable on shelves and easily readable on a plane or subway car. The typical mag today seems about right for all this. For my tastes, most magazines could use some more white space, they just look too crammed to me.
