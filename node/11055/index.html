Lux Typographic Logo

March 8, 2002 • designalchemy

This is a typographic logo / flyer for Lux, an upscale coffeehouse in Phoenix, Arizona.
It was printed on a 5.25 x 5.25 cmyk / bw water and tear resistant matte laminated stock.

typeface used is slightly modified Beta by Joshua Distler (shiftype)

Hello Marcox. Well there is a story behind this.
Back in 1998 I created this logo in a horizontal and vertical format for the a cafe called Lux in
the hip part of Seattle called Belltown. There was a neon sign as well as vinyl window sign fabricated. This establishment was sold in 2000 to a couple who sold again a year later, but kept he logo and brought it back to Phoenix, Arizona when they sold the Seattle location. I reprinted the businesscards and this promo with the new address on it in december 2001. I have not ever been to Phoenix or seen the new location, so I find it interesting that someone in the typophile community drives by there all the time. thanks for the info. Ole
