Looks like a ransom note where letters from fonts of different widths but the same caps height, and a lowercase e expanded to the caps height, were carefully lined up to make words. Note that some letters such as A, M and R come in both wide and narrow widths. Possibly the capitals are from the same font and were severely compressed in some cases but not in others. Don
