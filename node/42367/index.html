Which is correct? I’ve found Italian sources that list the former and American sources that list the latter. Is Giambattista simply a nickname or an Americanized name? (No I’m not suggesting he was an American.)

I think it’s the same name — “Giambattista” being simply a shorter (i.e. contracted) form of the complete “Giovanni Battista” (“Jean-Baptiste” in French, “John the Baptist” in English).
