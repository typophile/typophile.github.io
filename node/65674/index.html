Behrens Antiqua was a Klngspor type. So it might be possible, that Schriften-Service Gerstenberg, the follow-up of D. Stempel in Frankfurt, Germany, still holds the matrizes. Its not in the current program. This means, officially its only possible to order 6 times 1/2 minimum. So if you would order a, lets say, 28 p (best choice to produce clean prints from) 1/2 minimum’s weight is at about 8 kg. 1 kg = 65 Euro. 8 x 520 Euro. 6 times 1/2 kg … lot of money. :( - may be, its possible to order just 3 times 1/2 minimum… I don’t know yet.
