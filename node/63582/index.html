I’m looking at Dafont now and I notice a font that could be used as a starting point to ‘tweak’ into this style. See [[http://www.dafont.com/tes.font?nb_ppp=50&text=WHEN+PLANETS+EXPLODE|Tes]], and flip the M to become the W. Convert the letters to outlines and start moving some points around to change the new W width, the E and H crossbar height and the endpoint of the N diagonal. The shapes of the other letters look almost dead on to me.
