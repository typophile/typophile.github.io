2008-02-19 02:20:22 • Hofweber

I still need to finish a lot more letters before I move on to spacing.

IMHO you have to space it closer to final to design it. Eg, is the ‘a’ too wide? I don’t know how you can answer that without doing some spacing, and putting it into some words. Also the o and e would normally relate more closely as to width. If you are going to violate that you’d better make sure it works. And to do that you need to put it into words, and spacing will matter. As Mario Feliciano said to me: you want beautiful words, not beautiful letters.

Good luck!
