DOES ANYONE KNOW A TYPEFACE SIMILAR TO THIS ONE? (STRIKED OUT/ CRACKED?) THNK YOU!
The effect is custom. Note how the angled cut rises with each of the ten letters in the word. So pick a heavy sans of your choice and apply a diminishing angled cut one letter after another to form the word you want to make with this effect. for example, ITC Franklin Std black: http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/itc/franklin/std-black/ Don
