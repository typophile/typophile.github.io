Hi I'm new here and hoping some one can answer my question. I have an existing font family I've taken and modified some of the characters and created a series of custom glyphs and ligitures for a new brand for a client my question is does my client need to now buy and license the original font in order to use the new modified font? Thanks for any advice
Which font is it? Most commercial font licenses don’t even allow such modifications. It’s best to discuss this with the foundry and negotiate a deal for this new custom font. In general and assuming a modification would be allowed in the EULA: Yes, the client also needs to buy a license.

In case you live and work in the northern Americas this is how you will avoid any legal hassle: 1. Print the glyphs you need for the logo. 2. Scan. 3. Trace them in Illustrator or any other vector based app. 4. Do what you want. 5. Sell to client. In the rest of the world the issue is somewhat clouded. Contact the typedesigner and talk about it.
