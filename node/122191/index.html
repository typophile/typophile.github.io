Good find for seeing _G_ and _C_ as being from Campignon Alt Swash modified to an upright version. These letters are found as alts in the otf version of Campignon. The other letters are also in the otf version of Campignon. Consider: [img:sites/default/files/old-images/CampignonModified1_4176.jpg] I sloped all letters 36 degrees left except for the _C_ which I sloped 24 degrees. The _r_ and _k_ are alts. Some minor tinkering and there would be an exact match. BTW this font is a good exemplar of the fine work that Claude Pelletier does in creating digital fonts. Don
