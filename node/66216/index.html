I just remembered that Seria has a recently-made Arabic companion: http://www.behance.net/Gallery/FF-Seria-Arabic-First-Arabic-Speaking-FontFont/242682 Although it’s not what I consider ideal, it’s still quite good.

  • You could set the Regular large and the Bold small to end up with a visual balance.
