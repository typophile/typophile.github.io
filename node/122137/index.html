closest I can find is Dennis Ortiz-Lopez’s OL Egyptian Extra Bold https://www.myfonts.com/fonts/ortizlopez/olegyptian/ All the serifs are in the right places, except for bottom rhs on h and m. General match for all letters, including the terminal on r. Minor adjustments in weight and width and tinker with some details, such as the bowl on a, for an even closer match. Don
