Hi all…

Tiffany–I got all your emails. That was just my automatic “vacation” message you were getting.

Gent was wonderful and Yves is very lucky to live there. He gave us a great evening walking tour of the downtown area. I don’t think we actually talked about type very much. I tried to stump him by getting him to identify a somewhat obscure font on a sign (fat chance), but that was about it. Mostly we talked about Gent and differences between living there and the U.S., the design business and stuff.

Oh, and I finally got that beer he promised me three years ago. :-)

Earlier in the day, before we met up with Yves, we visited the Plantin-Moretus Museum in Antwerp and got to see some of the few remaining Garamond punches:

I’ll try to post some stuff about the museum this week on my blog.

Thanks again, Yves, for the tour and everything.
