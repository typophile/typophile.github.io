Sam, I have to disagree with Mike on the age of this sign. The Braille appears to have been put up at the same time as the numerals, which would date it after the Americans With Disabilities Act was passed in 1990 (this is assuming the sign is in the US). The disproportionate spacing between 1 and 4 also places it in the digital age; it indicates this was set on a keyboard, where all numerals have a one-en set width.
