actually, Benguiat is similar in construction – but I guess that’s not quite what you’re looking for. :-)

As the hand-made letterforms really are very diverse (just look at the 1st and the 2nd ‘K’), I don’t think there’s a 100%-match. What do you like about it, the quirky feel? The Venetian ‘e’? The figures?

For now, here are some wild suggestions that I like: Anselm Serif, Koch-Antiqua or Post-Antiqua, while all being very idiosyncratic, share a certain crankiness.

Hiroshige or Poor Richard are yet another two – completely different – trails …
