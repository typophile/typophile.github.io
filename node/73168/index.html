So far it’s been pretty easy. Now I suggest you do the ultra bold. That will be quite hard (most people who do this kind of face don’t get heavier than a medium-weight bold), but it will resolve a lot of issues, ultimately save you a lot of hassle, and set the face apart from the crowd.
