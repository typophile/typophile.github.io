I think you can make the slant in either direction using your graphics software. I think you will be unlikely to find a font that matches the style and has the slants too.

The closest font I found so far was Nick Curtis’s [[http://new.myfonts.com/fonts/nicksfonts/route-66-nf/|Route 66]]. Other fonts had bigger problems with the ‘1’, or the 6 or 9 not being closed the same way.
