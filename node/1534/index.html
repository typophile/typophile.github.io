Sabon (JT's)

October 6, 2003 • hrant

Motivated by the discussion about Sabon in the "Trapping" thread, I tracked down the issue of the Penrose Annual that Rodolfo mentioned.

Sabon had two ranges: 6-12 and 14-48. The former range:
1) Was for all three systems: Monotype, Linotype, foundry.
2) Has short descenders, but apparently longer alternates were provided.
3) Has optical scaling, but not very pronounced in the 6.
4) Has wide "a"s.

While the latter:
1) Was for foundry setting only.
2) Has long descenders.
3) Has nice optical scaling.
4) Has narrow "a"s.

I'm starting to think that JT opted for the wide "a" in the smaller range to accomodate the smallest sizes better.

BTW, I think I've found the worse glyph ever: Sabon's italic "f".
