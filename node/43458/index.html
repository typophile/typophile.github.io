Why is the diagonal in the sans-serif 4 drawn noticeably narrower than the verticals and horizontals?
I think this is come from the calligraphic technic. Make a test, use a large ink pen and work at an angle of 45 degrees, by drawing figure 4 you will have a narrower line for the diagonal than the vertical or horizontal one.
