http://i1158.photobucket.com/albums/p601/MeshyPet/fontsample_zps18129937.png

This is the font that came off of a custom IMVU Profile job for myself. It was described as “dark wood”. Where it would be easy to contact Ash, She is almost never on anymore. The font is unlike those I’ve seen before. The tops or sometimes the bottom of the letters will split off in what kind of reminds me like broom bristles. I don’t know how to really describe it other than that. Thank You for any help You can give me.

Somewhat similar to Flash ND http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/neufville/flash-nd/, a digitization by Fabrizio Schivai for Neufville Digital, of a font produced by Enric Crous-Vidal for Fonderie Typographique Française in 1953. Aka Flashes, a version by Ralph M. Unger for Pro Fonts http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/profonts/flashes/. Don
