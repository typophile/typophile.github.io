Help! I love this font, and I can’t figure out what it is. I’ve used a few font identifier programs and none of them can properly identify what it is. You can find it on jcrew’s website and in their catalog. Thanks for your help!

That P bowl is very high. Add to that the abrupt curve in J, the long middle stroke in E, the high crossbar in A, angular cut endings in K and an out-of-style apostrophe and the sum makes me think this might not be a professional font.

You might want to check out the thinnest weights of [[http://www.fontshop.com/search/?q=futura%20light%20urw%20ot|URW Futura]] for a somewhat similar feeling. Also see [[http://www.linotype.com/90672/avenirnext-family.html?subviewmode=FONTS&samplestr=JENNA%92S+PICKS|Avenir Next]].
