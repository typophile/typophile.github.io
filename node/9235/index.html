You also asked about other ultra bold fonts and what happens to some of the letters. The super heavy weights of the Gill Sans family are probably good examples.

See Gill Sans Ultra Bold and Gill Kayo Condensed to see some interesting alterations to the lower case (and upper case) at those weights.
