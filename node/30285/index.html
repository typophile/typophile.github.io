Coliseum Books, in NYC, is closing

January 2, 2007 • Ricardo Cordoba

This time it’s for good, alas: Coliseum Books, which had recently relocated to just across the street from Bryant Park in New York City, will close its doors for good this Sunday, January 7. I happened to pass by last weekend, and they have some pretty big discounts on everything. Plenty of design and typography titles still left, folks (along with fiction, non-fiction, etc.). Get ‘em while you can.
