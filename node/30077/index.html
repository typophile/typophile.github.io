I don’t know what happened afterwards. I collected cigarette packages in the UK in the early 1970s, but then moved to Canada, quit that hobby (and the habit), and haven’t paid attention to cigarette package design since. What got me interested in the first place was the addition of the warning, which spoiled the designs, and drew my attention to the nice old packages, which I had occasionally come across in second-hand markets. There was also a sub-culture of collectors. The evolution of the Cadillac tail-fin would also make a good animation. And I suppose the same thing could be done for typefaces – sort of an animated Multiple Master along the time axis.
