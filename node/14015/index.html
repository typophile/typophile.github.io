[[Indices]]: [[Designers]]: Neville Brody Neville Brody (b. 1957) is a London born graphic designer. Studied at the [[London College of Printing]]. Primarily known for his work as Art Director on The Face magazine and for his studio's work on the late 1990s [[Macromedia]] identity. In 1990 he and [[Erik Spiekermann]] started [[FontFont]] and [[Fuse]]. Currently he operates his [[London]] studio, Research Associates. Some of his early design work was for British bands. Fetish, Hanibal, 23 Skiddo, and Cabaret Voltaire all have album covers designed by Brody. See a collection of his designs for the Dutch postal service [[http://www.katranpress.com/stamps_brody_1_1.html|here]].
