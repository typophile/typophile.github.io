Lookie See, It starts with S

February 4, 2009 • azucena

Below is a sketchbook assignment given by my graphic design teacher. We were required to chose a letter, find 20 differnt versions of the letter, then find 20 images that started with the letter and paste it into our sketchbook anyway we choose.

I chose the letter S and tried to find images that started with that letter that served multiple purposes, for example the legs have Stockings, Shoes and Stripes and the Fork image has Silverware and Spaghetti.

I like your composition. I swear over half the people in my class used ‘S’.
