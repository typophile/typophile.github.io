Kris Sowersby

October 12, 2005 • kris

[[Indices]] : [[Designers]] : Kris Sowersby Kris Sowersby is an independent type designer, typographer and graphic designer. Based in Wellington, the capital of New Zealand, he runs the [[Klim]] Type Foundry. Three of his typefaces have been awarded [[TDC]]2 awards. National in 2007, Hardys and Serrano in 2008. Kris has designed or collaborated on the following typefaces: Retail
  • Feijoa (2007, a serif family for text, Village).
  • National (2007–2009 a sans serif family, Village).
  • FF Meta Serif (2007, Serif counterpart to FF Meta, designed with [[Christian Schwartz]] and [[Erik Spiekermann]], microsite).
  • Newzald (2008, a serif family for text, Village ).
  • Galaxie Copernicus (2009, a reconsideration of [[Plantin]], designed with [[Chester Jenkins]], Village ).
Custom
  • Eliza (2003).
  • Rewards (2006).
  • NZ Rugby Chisel (2006, The All Blacks Typeface).
  • Hokotohu (2007, A typeface for the Moriori).
  • Victoria Sans and Serif (2007, Custom typefaces for Victoria University).
  • Hardys (2008, a typeface for Constellation Wines Australia).
  • Methven Flow (2008, a typeface for Methven).
  • Serrano (2008, a typeface for The Bank of New Zealand).
