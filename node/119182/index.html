Font from older Sherlock Holmes film

September 3, 2014 • gorillapictures

Hello,

I am restoring text from a lost feature film version of Sherlock Holmes (1983). I cannot seem to find the font anywhere, and no automated services are even coming close. Any ideas as to what font to look for? Even something close while keeping a similar look (especially with the ABFGQVWYabfghjkqtvwy characters on this image.

I created this image by going through the film, tracing the characters, cleaning them up as vectors in Illustrator, and arranging them on the following image.

Any advice is incredibly helpful! Thanks!

[img:sites/default/files/old-images/SherlockFont_4819.png]

Parsons, by Will Ranson (American Type Foundry, 1918): http://luc.devroye.org/fonts-71338.html

Digital versions include: http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/nicksfonts/parsnip-nf/ http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/barnhartbrother/parsons/ [font appears to have been removed]

I won’t post links to the several cheap knock-offs on the free font websites….
