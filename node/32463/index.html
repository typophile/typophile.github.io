How bad is $14 an hour for a FT design job?

March 22, 2007

I've been freelancing so long it seems horrible. I've been offered a FT job with full benefits for $14. an hour - that's under 30k. The work is not hard, and not interesting at all. It's production work for a small publication - who still uses Quark 4 and PageMaker. Is the pay really bad? opinions?
Calculate how much the benefits are worth over the month and add that into the pay -- if they're going to pay full medical and dental (and depending on your situation -- you need some serious work done on your teeth or have a high prescription bill), it might be worth it.
