It's Maxima allright... Brings back memories! By the way: 17M is not DM, but East Mark. Back then, there were 10 East Marks in a Deutsche Mark, if I remember well. So the cassette was about 60 dollarcents in -let's say- 1988. (Orwo also made roll films for photography. They were dead cheap too, but could only be processed in the German Democratic Republic and especially the slides had a very weird hue to them.) BTW: all their printing was done with raised type on polymer plates, not with offset!
