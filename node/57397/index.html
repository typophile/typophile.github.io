The only one with a funky k and y similar to these was LSC Condensed, which was an old ITC face (digitized in old Image Club collections). Other sort of similar high contrast faces that have digital versions: Arsis, Big Figgins, Didoni, Falstaff and Modern Heavy Condensed. Most of these had the similar y, but not the k.
