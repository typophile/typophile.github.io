2007-12-10 00:32:51 • Tonie Blaze

Arrow was a Visual Graphics Corp (VGC) typeface in the Film type era, and it was in the pre-acquisition versions of the Image Club Letterpress CD (one weight only). The typeface also shows up as a single weight in the Phil’s Photo “Homage to the Alphabet” book from 1985. Joe Treacy’s Arrow family was originally a Headliners family from 1992, before he took over that line of fonts. One book I have says Arrow was a type design by Walter Diethelm, a Swiss designer who died in 1986. There was no picture of it, so I’m not sure it’s the same typeface.
