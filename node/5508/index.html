Jim, Thank you for your kind and encouraging words. Funny how you smile and shake my hand in person then insult me and my company when sitting alone behind a keyboard and the comfortable warm glow of a computer monitor. To clarify a few things: 1. The shop stocks shirts in S to XXL…we just keep mediums on display because it looks better when they all hang at the same length. All you had to do is ask. 2. There are six different styles of jeans, available in 30 through 38 waist. There is no inseam selection, but we'll nip around the corner and have them hemmed. 3. There are also sweats as well as men's and women's dress shirts in five different colors and two different styles. I guess you didn't notice the custom Lewis Leathers Jackets or the George Cox Brothel Creepers in four different colors. We also brought the last two of the limited edition walnut Neutra chairs, which are on the shop floor. 4. Pricing: Yep, it's expensive. The fabric for the t-shirts and sweats is custom dyed and then the garments are custom sewn to our specifications. Likewise for the jeans. Both of those are made in limited runs in Portugal, not in a far east sweat shop. The dress shirts, shoes, and Lewis Leathers are all made in England. Combine that with Soho rent, VAT, custom-printed wallpaper, custom cut stainless steel signs and then tell me how expensive our store is. By the way, we sell the fonts in the store for the direct conversion from dollars to pounds. Anyway, thanks for stopping by. Rich
