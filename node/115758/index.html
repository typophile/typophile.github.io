April 14, 2014 • Émilie Rigaud

Hello everyone,

Just to let you know that since last october, the ANRT (Atelier National de Recherche Typographique) has re-opened in France. The call for applications is opened until the end of may, we select 6 students-researchers each year. For more informations, you can check [[http://www.anrt-nancy.fr/en/how-it-works|our website]].

We also organize on May 6th and 7th a symposium about “Automatic Type Design”, [[http://www.anrt-nancy.fr/en/how-it-works|more infos here]].

Hope to meet some of you soon.
