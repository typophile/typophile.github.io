2014-12-23 08:37:12 • donshottype
Similar feeling of oppressive futurism in a font based on propaganda displays at the Communist Red Square parade in 1937 by Rian Hughes with the name Korolev Compressed Heavy http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/device/korolev/ It has an S with a straight spine like the movie’s unusual S. The other letters could be clipped and tinkered with to make a reasonable match. BTW note the movie title’s strange G which looks like C except for the sharp corner on the lower rhs. Don
