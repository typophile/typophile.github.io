Serif font with similar feel to Verlag?

August 10, 2010 • lhype

Hi, I’m a graphic design student doing a freelance assignment designing a logo for a community orchestra. I used Verlag at first, but it is too similar to what another music group in town uses, so now I’m looking for a serif font with a similar feel. I’d like something cool, contemporary, and clean. Any suggestions? Thanks in advance!

Verlag is yet another design derivative of an older Typeface called Erbar (1927 Jacob Erbar-Germany). So is the dutch Nobel and other designs.

Maybe google for typefaces from around that time period, or maybe from the Bauer Type Company, which published many of those typefaces back then. Like Bauer Bodony, Weiss, Beton (slab), Candida (slab) etc.

Depending on your taste, I would say Weiss is a closer Serif to the sans cut and proportions you’re looking for. It’s also rather beautiful.

good luck, A
