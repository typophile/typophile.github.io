Three words:

Gabriel Martinez Meave.

Lagarto - http://www.kimera.com.mx/mac/tipogalerialagarto.html

Arcana - http://www.kimera.com.mx/mac/tipogaleriaarcana.html

When I decided in college (in Mexico City) that for my thesis I wanted to design a typeface all my teachers were completely baffled. There was only one teacher who had taken type design courses in Switzerland and she was the dean. I didn't get much help from Academia.

Tiypo, I think, is still going. I recently e-mailed Paco and he talks about it like it's still alive.

The magazine Matiz (you can read about it in Step and Comm Arts) was one of the first and greatest forums for showcasing mexican typography and it kicked absolute . Too bad it's gone now. Nacho Peon (http://www.igloodesign.net) and his sister Monica Peon did some great typefaces for that magazine and for personal use too.

Right now, I have no clue what is going on, since I'm very removed from the professional designers back home. It seems like they are a little late to the party
