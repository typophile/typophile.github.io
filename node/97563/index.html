2012-10-26 21:52:47 • hrant

Another Optima alternative that a really like is [[http://www.fontshop.com/fonts/downloads/monotype/mentor_sans_pro_volume/|Mentor Sans]], particularly the bold. Where Optima has a wonderful classical stateliness and elegance, Mentor Sans has a briskness and vigor and strength that is wonderful in its own distinctive way. By contrast, to me most Optima variants look like they’re trying to be Optima and failing.

By the way I like both the old and new versions of Optima by Zapf, except for the condensed, which to me is a betrayal of the concept, with the wide, elegant classical arches.
