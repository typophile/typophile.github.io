Is SchoolText a freely-available font?
Unlikely. It's apparently been bundled with other products such as ClarisWorks and (as such fonts often do) has made its way into the archives of some mass font repositories. I don't know of any legitimate source to download it, but it is part of the School Font Collection CD ([[http://www.halfhollowhills.k12.ny.us/uploaded/PDFs/Technology/sf.pdf|PDF link]]) which is available from several online vendors. http://goo.gl/zrghu
