Hi Mario-

There are A LOT of handwriting fonts about, some free and some commercial, some good and lots bad.

The handwriting in the sample you’ve attached is just that - handwriting, not a font. Personally I prefer not to use handwriting-style type as I believe the result will never be as idiosyncratic, authentic or satisfying as using your own. However, I appreciate that that’s not always possible or convenient. My recommendation is that if you CAN use your own hand, DO. Failing that, here’s a point towards some interesting handwritten fonts.

http://new.myfonts.com/fonts/sparkytype/panhandler/ http://new.myfonts.com/fonts/typefetish/reverend-jim/ http://new.myfonts.com/fonts/typadelic/mayfield/ http://new.myfonts.com/fonts/paratype/federico/

Have a look through the Handwritten section on DaFont as well.

http://www.dafont.com/theme.php?cat=603

-NJ
