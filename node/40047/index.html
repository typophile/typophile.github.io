Hi Tom,

Legible from a distance? yep

Even colour? N, Y and W too heavy, A slightly heavy, M functional but lacks stylistic integration

Any strokes look too fat or thin compared to the rest? N verticals and joints, joints at baseline of W

Do any curves look strange? nothing overtly strange, but “wierd is good” in the sense that a lot more can be done with the curves you’ve got. There’s a tameness and repetition that leaves a feeling you are holding back something more interesting. For example, the asymmetric theme used on E, F, L could be applied to the curves of C, G, O, Q, P, R, S—a faint wiff of Revue.

Any letterforms ’out of place’? N structure/style and heaviness, same for W

What characters don’t sit next to each other well? need to see more combinations, set words. N and W fail to integrate with most others.
