Nicholas Felton (AKA Feltron) is known for his personal annual reports, as well as his contribution to Penguin’s We Tell Stories series, Hard Times. I just recently discovered another of his projects: Daytum.

Daytum is essentially a data diary system. It allows you to log and track your personal day-to-day activity and then visualize the collected information in a variety of ways. The site is clean and attractive and looks like a lot of fun:
