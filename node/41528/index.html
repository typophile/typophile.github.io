As for the names of the cast members, I vote Century Gothic. The titles are here: http://vimeo.com/5848690 If you look at the name Robert Sean Leonard in that video, the crossbars of the E and of the A are at very close heights, whereas in Futura (and, for that matter, in Tw Cen MT), the crossbar of A is much lower. Also the base of the L seems to be just as wide as the base of the E, whereas in Futura, the base of the L is shorter than that of the E. Forgive me if I got any of those typographic terms wrong -- I'm but a newbie in this whole world!
