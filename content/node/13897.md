body: "<strong>[[Indices]] : [[Terminology]] : Text Figures</strong>\r\n\r\nAlso called
  lowercase figures, or oldstyle figures, these number forms are designed to be used
  in running text. Text figures feature numerals that come to the [[x-height]]: 0,1,2;
  figures with [[descender|descenders]]: 3,4,5,7,9; and [[ascender|ascending]] figures:
  6, 8. Some varations of text figures may not fit this same scheme, but this is the
  norm.\r\n\r\nSee also [[Lining Figures]].\r\n\r\n<strong>Discussion:</strong>\r\n<a
