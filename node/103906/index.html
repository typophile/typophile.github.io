This fancy letter would have been drawn by a monk illuminating a medieval manuscript. The sample image is small but the F seems to include Celtic knots, a common theme in the initial letters used in famous manuscripts such as the Irish Book of Kells. Too fancy for a font, but a vector version of this F may be available. A Google search for vector Celtic illuminated manuscripts will lead you to similar letters, and with some perseverance you might find a vector of the initial F used on the book cover. A good assortment of old fancy initial capitals, but not including this particular F, can be found at Aridi initial capitals. Don
