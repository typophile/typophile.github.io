Hi. For a book we want to recreate the attached stamp. However I can’t figure what typeface was used - the two ‘2’s are different for one etc .. anyone any ideas?
Unless this English photographer’s stamp is recent it probably was not created directly from a font. To get something out of the box that would work you could use different fonts for the letters and numbers. The scale of the stamp is small so there is some ambiguity in the letters. For the lowercase letterforms you could use an English font like Eric Gill’s Infant http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/mti/gill-sans/std-infant/ or a less quirky alternative in Nicholas Garner’s Friendstar https://www.myfonts.com/fonts/aviation/fiendstar/ But you might get a closer match with a new font, Olivier Gourvat’s Sofia. You have a choice of regular and condensed letters https://www.myfonts.com/fonts/mostardesign/sofia-pro/ and https://www.myfonts.com/fonts/mostardesign/sofia-pro-condensed/ Other possibilities include AG Schoolbook http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/berthold/ag-schoolbook-bq/ and Alex Haigh’s Hiruko https://www.myfonts.com/fonts/thinkdust/hiruko-pro/ Don
