April 30, 2009 • Chris Dean

A little bird told me that tomorrow, Friday, 01 May, 2009, is [[http://www.fontbureau.com/people/MikeParker|Mike "the May King" Parker's]] 80th birthday. Happy birthday Mr Parker. Sincerely, Christopher Dean Typographer
I adore Mike Parker and hope that there are many, many more birthdays to come. Every year at TypeCon, his stories (which often run into the wee hours of the morning) are one of the highlights of the entire event. Best Wishes Mike, and Happy Birthday!
