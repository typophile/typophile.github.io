Happy Birthday Peter Bruhn!

May 19, 2006 • Stephen Coles

It’s now past midnight in Malmö, Sweden and time to celebrate the birth of the founder of Fountain and my personal type design idol, Peter Bruhn. Here’s to another year of experimentation and inspiration. Looks like there’s some good stuff ’round the bend.

Wish the man luck today as he travels to speak at TYPOBerlin. A guy with a humble heart, Peter is always nervous giving speeches, but the audience is none the wiser as his work makes him smile and shine on stage.
