When looking at the font specifics on the Microsoft Typography site, I noticed something interesting with the ClearType fonts. Calibri, Cambria and Consolas all were “modified” in 2012. Constantia however, was not modified that year.

Scroll down to “Modified Date”: Calibri: http://www.microsoft.com/typography/fonts/font.aspx?FMID=1914 Cambria: http://www.microsoft.com/typography/fonts/font.aspx?FMID=2112 Constantia: http://www.microsoft.com/typography/fonts/font.aspx?FMID=2053

What does this mean? Was Constantia already ‘perfect’ in 2009, so it didn’t need modification in 2012?
