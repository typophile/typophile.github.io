Read up on the symbol sets for Lintotype’s Vialog typeface. Linotype.com has some information, and published a great brochure that I’m sure they’ll send out. At least two good articles have been published about Vialog’s symbols in the last six years (In Metropolis and PRINT). And while you’re at the Linotype site, buy their catalog—a quick look through it would have been a great start to your research. It’s a steal even with the cost of shipping it to the states.
