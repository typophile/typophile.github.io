Just an FYI: If I worked for a type foundry - I would not put my faces out in PDF form - (unless they were photoshop - and no fonts attached). I've been watching the "black market" -- kids out there love to get PDF files so they can extract the font. They don't care about kerning. They don't care about stealing. They just don't care. I belong to several different type boards - and some are definitely easier than others. Some you learn their personality - and it works. One feature that makes a type board easiest - is a strong SEARCH feature.
