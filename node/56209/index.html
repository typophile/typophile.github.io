Microsoft: Windows 7 to support Capital Sharp S

March 24, 2009 • Andreas Stötzner

Some of the core fonts of Windows 7 will feature the Capital Sharp S at its new codepoint 1E9E. This has been confirmed to me yesterday by Microsoft and Ascender Corp. representatives. An article in German and an image can be found at http://www.signographie.de/cms/front_content.php?idcat=130
Just to be clear I don’t think the inclusion of the character in popular fonts equals a Microsoft endorsement of it. We looked at the Unicode 5.1 extensions and added only characters most likely to be used.
