Thanks!!! ---- The problem & the solution: "Both the "preview problem" and the"lack of pixel squares" are the result of one bug in BitFonter's export. To repair FontLab Studio, open the Preferences > Glyph Window > Dimensions page and change Visual ascender and descender fields to defaults: 100 and -40. Then you will be able to use the Metrics window and others. But if you export from BitFonter to FontLab Studio again then BitFonter will change FontLab's preferences back to invalid values. Both problems will be fixed in BitFonter 3.0.1 release." ---------
