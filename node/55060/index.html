I find it interesting that not a single person (besides me) has indicated that he or she liked the solution used in the newspaper. I console myself that those who like it probably aren’t going to respond with a “Yes, I like it too.” comment.

I agree that it looks like a D, but the number 1 in the list looks like a lower-case l – most people can tell the difference by the context.

And yes, there may be a more optimal solution (after all this was created for a daily newspaper, probably under some tight time constraints), but none of the possible solutions suggested so far seem any better – they just have different problems.
