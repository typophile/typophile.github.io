2010-01-22 20:57:26 • Beauclair

The name of the typfaces is Bradford. If you download the Patrick Frey pdf here www.swips.ch/downloads/Vorschauen/patrick_frey_09.pdf and open it in acrobat it tells you that the name of the fonts embedded. Also its nice if you want to get a closer look at the typeface.

To me it neither looks very alike Palantin or Galaxie Copernicus. But it feels a bit like dtl Fleischman, with its very smooth outlines (if you take a look at the vectors in the pdf you can see that most corners ar rounded) which gives the typfeace a nice soft texture. Since I haven’t been able to find any information about it on the net, I also guess that its an unreleased typeface. I personaly doubt that it is designed by Elektrosmog, since it has a rather different look from the typefaces they usualy make. I think it looks more like a revival of a baroque typface which is based on prints, so the lettershapes are smoother and rounder. But thats just a guess.

Anyway thanks for your help, as always typophile members have been very supporting
