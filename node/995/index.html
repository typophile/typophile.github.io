2003-08-21 19:35:24 • fonthausen
If you're looking for something that emulates hand printing your choices are slim. Most are too formal (Petrarch), too cute (Comic Sans), or too mechanical to pass for handwriting. Here are a few that emulate real, legible hand scripts: Fineprint, Zemke Hand, Longhand, Erazure, Petescript, Grove Script, Felt Tip Roman (Mark Simonson's best-seller), and the hugely popular Marydale from the Type Quarry which sells a few other good scripts, including some nice historical reconstructions.
