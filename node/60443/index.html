Also, If I use a typeface for inspiration, what kind of copyright laws apply?

Well, speaking very broadly, I think that most type designers, at one point or another, get inspired by other typefaces. Copying a font’s outlines would cross the line, however. It’s one thing to be inspired or be influenced, but there wouldn’t be much point in doing a slavish imitation of something that already exists.

Having said that, we all have to start somewhere, right? At first you will likely be influenced by work you admire, but if you keep at it you will find your own voice.
