Forced Small Caps in InDesign CS2

September 25, 2007 • Ricardo Cordoba

At my current freelance gig, we are having some issues with forced small caps in Adobe InDesign CS2. (It may be a conflict with Extensis Suitcase X1, too.)

What happens is this: onscreen, the small caps look fine, but sometimes they print as gibberish text, or boxes with X’s… My solution so far has been to quit and then restart InDesign, which does the trick.

Has anybody else seen this happen? Any solutions, other than buying fonts with true small caps? (It’s not my call to do that here.)

Thanks in advance, R.

I can’t say I have seen the problem, but then I would rather loose my left arm than use faux small caps in something I felt was important. I would look at this problem as an opportunity. Tell whoever makes such decisions that due to this technical problem, if they want small caps, they should buy a proper font with designed small caps.
