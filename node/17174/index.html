2006-01-10 21:42:05 • Dan Weaver
mde, I got my hands on other logos of this Krono group. It seems that it really is a modified face after all, as the letters in other logos look +/- the same, but small differences (presence/absence of round corners and such) give away that they've been made by different people at various times to match the logo of the mother company more or less. So I decided to modify the funny Futura to match the letters. As for Dan's comment on the stroking of the Pol part of the logo - this is what usually happens when you just hit "stroke" in the Illustrator without other tinkerings with the result. The person who did it probably was on a hurry or didn't give it any thought at all.. I wonder how Czechs have managed to have their beautiful Storm foundry, when Eastern Europe overall is a huge territory of typographical ignorance...
