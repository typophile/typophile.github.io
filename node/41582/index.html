2008-02-05 13:45:14 • baden03

This one is still haunting me too.

I searched Veer (Serif) and FontPool (Slab), and so far the ONLY font I found that even had a similar A and K was [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/typodermic/cinecav-x/|Cinecav X]] by Ray Larabie (Typodermic). The sans versions are the most similar, but I really don’t believe your font was made by adding serifs to this font. The serif brackets and the curving of some of the strokes add much more personality to your sample than almost any font I looked at that was supposed to be in this category. The one that felt closest in general style, though not structure, was Bob Alonso’s [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/bagraphics/wonka/|Wonka]]. If you widened it the resemblance would be stronger.

I was hoping we’d get some help from a few of the folks who’ve spent a lot of time scouring the more obscure sources, but apparently no one else recognized this either. Maybe it is time for both of us to move on.
