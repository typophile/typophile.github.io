body: "Organisers of the International Design Festival Design Attack invite designers
  to submit their proposals for the exhibition \"Material of the Future\", a part
  of the first edition of Design Attack Festival, which will be held in Krakow on
  6-30 November 2010. \r\n\r\nThe subject of the exhibition Material of the Future
  will revolve around the evolution of materials considered to be the future - from
  plastic (first bakelites, tomofon, the first biodegradable materials, laminates
  AbetPrint) towards ecology, nanotechnologies, and hybrid composites. The exhibition
  will present works and projects of Polish and foreign designers interested in the
  issue of using new materials in 3d design.\r\n\r\nThe call addresses designers specializing
  in design using innovative materials, as well as those who, for the purpose of the
  exhibition, will create experimental projects using new technologies and materials.\r\n\r\nThe
  exhibition will present completed 3d objects, prototypes, concept sketches, photographs,
  advertising materials, and educational boards. \r\n\r\nThe exhibition will be on
  display in the \"Solvay\" Center for Contemporary Art in Cracow during 1st international
  festival Design Attack. CCA \"Solvay\" is a cultural institution whose mission is
  to promote contemporary art, including interdisciplinary projects  combining visual
  arts, music and performance. The works and their authors will be also presented
  in the Design Attack Festival Catalogue and on the festival website. \r\n \r\nCall
  for submission is open for all participants. Deadline for submissions is August
  31st, 2010. Submissions can only be made online at www.designattack.pl, and send
  to the Organiser.  \r\n\r\nSubmissions will be judged by a professional Jury. All
  submitting artists will be notified via email by September 10th, 2010.\r\n\r\nFor
  more information go to: www.designattack.pl \r\n[img:sites/default/files/old-images/DA_logo
  kolor_6658.jpg]"
