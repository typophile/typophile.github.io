It is obviously advantageous to have a diverse library in itself, but making choices will be easier if you have a better idea of what typefaces you will need for your projects. So you may consider setting aside a typeface budget, so that when a project comes along for which you need something other than what you have in your current library, you can choose to buy one tailored for that project. You can build up your library organically that way.
