I understand your point about extended humanist sans serifs. At a certain width, it’s difficult to make a something with a rotund structure work without getting awkward. Better to keep fairly square and/or monolinear. In that vein:

Stainless Extended Relay Wide Vafle Extended

Not so small an x-height: FF Clan Extended Rhode Wide Titling Gothic Wide and Extended FF Cube Extra Expanded Trade Gothic Extended Eurostile Extended
