I’d say Avenir has too small of an x-height to work as text, but then again, I’ve never tried setting text in Avenir.

Anyway, how about something like the bold weight of Iridium also by Adrian Frutiger?

In the sans-serif category, Mostra by Mark Simonson or the Titling weight of Neutraface might work, depending on how bold your Avenir headings are.

It’s ok to use Avenir for longer text matter, I think. At least, I have done that a few times ; ). Mind that you use a fair amount of leading and that you check the basic kerning (the lighter faces need more than is built–in…).

BTW You can’t go wrong if you combine it with a Frutigerdesigned serif. There is something very consistent in Mr F’s designs. That may have to do with his exeptional drawing skills and his great knowledge of signs (he wrote about signs — a fascinating book: Der Mensch und seine Zeichen).
