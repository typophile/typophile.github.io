2007-04-05 05:32:43 • Darrin
There are a lot of differences - note the straight sides on the 'O' (and 'D' and 'C' and 'G' etc.) in Alternate Gothic, whereas they are curved in Koehler. The letterforms in Koehler are a little more crudely drawn (intentionally so) while the forms in Alternate Gothic are more regular. Koehler seems to have a little bit of contrast, but Alternate Gothic has none. The terminals of Koehler are cut at a greater angle than those of Alternate Gothic... - Lex
