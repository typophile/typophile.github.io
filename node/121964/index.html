The font really can’t be that old. I has to be recent because some people use it a lot. The bottoms of the Caps have arks in them and notice the C how it has two serifs. The A has two bumps on the top as it angels down to the left. The W in the middle crosses over. The T’s have slanted serifs that are raised above the letter. Notice the G, how round it is at the bottom as it has a smooth curve as it goes up from the right side.![](/images/old-images/Screen Shot 2014-12-10 at 1 21 18 PM_5580.png)
