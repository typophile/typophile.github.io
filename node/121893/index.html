1971 is near the apex of the photolettering era when fonts were commonly squeezed, stretched and otherwise distorted on demand. so the nameplate _THE BLACK PANTHER_ may be somewhat different from the font used to derive it. In no particular order, here are some fonts based on narrow linears of the era. Permanent Headline URW [img:sites/default/files/old-images/PermanentHeadlineURW-AsBLACKPANTHER_5780.gif] http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/urw/permanent/ Plakette TS Light [img:sites/default/files/old-images/PlaketteTSLight-AsBLACKPANTHER_5068.gif] http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/ef-typeshop/plakette/ British Inserat MN Cond[img:sites/default/files/old-images/BritishInseratMNCond-AsBLACKPANTHER_5343.gif] https://www.myfonts.com/fonts/itfmecanorma/british-inserat/ Don
