2008-04-04 07:58:42 • Tommy32
This will be harsh: this is a terrible mixture of three fonts. I count logotype and tagline; you have one too many fonts. Also, I don't have anything against free fonts (I used Coolvetica by Ray Larabie recently with a satisfactory result), but I'm getting the impression that you found three free fonts that you thought were cool and then gave them a role in your blog's header. Find a font that strongly expresses the content of your blog and then design a logotype using that. If you have to pay for a font, then bite the bullet and shell out the cash. This is your brand you're developing here. Actually, if you're investing the money, you may consider hiring a professional... --- Nick Hladek
