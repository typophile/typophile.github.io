I made a barn dance invitation about a year ago and ended up using Briem Akademi Std (slightly modified), from Adobe's FontFolio. It has nice strait edges (I believe it was originally created to not have any rounded curves). It looks nice and clean, and barns are usually not constructed with elaborate rounded curves, etc... It's very plain and simple, without getting tacky (in my opinion). [img:sites/default/files/old-images/barndance_4604.jpg]
