This is the logo from 1961 to 1972. http://logos.wikia.com/wiki/Sprite The treatment of the lettering in_Sprite_ is very late 1950s early 1960s when "tipsy" or "bouncy" serifs were the height of fashion. The Font Diner fonts already suggested are good options for substitutes with the tipsy/bouncy style of the era. Here's a few fonts from Filmotype in the same style: Orlando: http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/filmotype/orlando/ Panama: http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/filmotype/panama/ Parade: http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/filmotype/parade/ Also Fanfare http://marketplace.veer.com/font/Fanfare-JBT0000066 Also Periwinkle http://marketplace.veer.com/font/Periwinkle-JBT0000088 Don
