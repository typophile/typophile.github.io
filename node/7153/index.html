Step outside, Hubert. The typefaces you named are very common--you'll find examples everywhere you turn!

That said, here are a few examples off the top of my head:

Caslon: Reader's Digest uses Big Caslon for headlines

Optima: Used for the engraved soldier's names at the Vietnam Veterans Memorial, Washington, D.C.
http://www.nps.gov/vive/home.htm

Futura: Volkswagen uses a custom version of this face for all of their brand communications

Bodoni: Influential designer Massimo Vignelli is a big fan of Bodoni -- commissioned his own version, in fact.
http://www.vignelli.com/
http://www.fontshop.com/index.cfm?fuseaction=catalog.fonts&searchID=902&searchby=designer
