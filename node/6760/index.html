2004-09-22 12:35:56 • geraintf
Take the innards out of 6 cheap ballpoint pens. Sand the edges of the tips for closer spacing on your lines. Tape them in a row with a splint if need be. Draw to your hearts content. This was the method pioneered by Charles Dikkens, the well know dutch composer.
Time Estimate: 5 minutes
Cost Estimate: 50 cents

One issue for your pizza cutter is getting good ink coverage.

It would be much simpler to cut your own punch, or if you're interested in an argument use a Benton Pantograph, make a matrix, cast some lead staves, set in a comp stick to the right length, fire it up and letterpress that badboy… use vegetable based inks. They're more organic…
Time Estimate: 5 months
Cost Estimate: Plenty

Come to a bunch typophiles, what do you expect :-)
Good luck.
