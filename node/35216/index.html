Who wants to learn about copyrighting fonts?

July 18, 2007 • Miss Tiffany

[[Frank Martinez]], an expert in copyright law, and one of the only lawyers qualified to serve the font industry, is teaching a workshop and a breakout session at TypeCon this year. I am excited to be able to sit down and hear about these processes directly from the horses mouth.
