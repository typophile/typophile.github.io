The Sterling typeface, designed by Joshua Darden, was created exclusively for Tiffany by H&FJ (now H&Co); it is not commercially available:

http://designarchives.aiga.org/#/entries/%2Bid%3A640/_/detail/relevance/asc/0/7/640/sterling-type-family/1

Because it’s not based on any one historical source, you won’t find a match in other digital fonts.

And Pentagram evidently just did a refresh of the main logo, also custom:
