February 21, 2010 • mbcohen

This shows up on a ton of paperbacks from Bantam in this era. It is not quite Profil/Decorated 035–it lacks that oblique touch.

http://img90.imageshack.us/img90/6513/thebarbarouscoastax0.jpg

Any ideas?

That time period probably means a film type was used. It’s a slab/Egyptian style, similar to [[http://new.myfonts.com/fonts/linotype/glypha/|Glypha]], but a bit condensed. It’s rather hard to find these with middle serifs on the E, and that’s why I picked Glypha as a similar font.
