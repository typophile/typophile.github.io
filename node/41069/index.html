How should I go about being able to use these fonts for logos without getting into trouble? Unless they come with a EULA when you download them which prohibits usage in a logo you are in the clear. Also, will the company be able to Copywrite the logo I gave them If I used that font? No. What do the other companies do when they make logos? Some companies you fonts out of the box. Some take those fonts and tweak them to suit. Some hand-letter their own. Generally speaking there are very few fonts which you cannot use in logos and most of those are dingbat or picture fonts.
