It is not uncommon for full typefaces to be derived from custom lettering in logos. I have no idea whether such has been done in this case, or whether or not it would be publicly available had such a font been made. Sometimes they are freely given by the designer, sometimes they are held by the trademark owner under exclusive license, or anything in between.

Best of luck in your search for something appropriately similar for your project.
