ninth letter literary journal + ninthletter.com ::
exceptional design, compelling cultural content

Ninth Letter is pleased to announce the launch of its newly re-designed web site. Ninthletter.com combines exceptional design with compelling cultural content. Our goal is to explore and extend the intersection of literature and visual culture.

Ninth Letter is intended for an audience that desires both beauty and logic, entertainment and thought, feeling and analysis. Our intent is to incite multiple activities, but to also provide some order and organization to the rapidly expanding dataspace.

With literary tradition as our jumping off point, ninthletter.com seeks to explore how art interfaces with the electronic terrain. Our goal is to find and feature examples of the best electronic art currently being produced by web artist/authors, focusing explicitly on work that is not supported by, and cannot be backward engineered into older cultural forms. We introduce new artists, and seek to engage them and our audience in lively conversations about contemporary art, culture, and technology.
