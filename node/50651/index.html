Hi Eric,

some similar square tech sans faces (most of them of pretty rough & unpolished ‘charme’):

  • Baksheesh by [[Stuart Brown]]
  • Blender and Regular by [[Nik Thoenen]] via Binnenland
  • BDR Mono by [[Lopetz]]
  • AutoScape and Lutz Headline by [[Cornel Windlin]] (also Alpha Headline, available again in 2012)
  • Arame by [[Dimitre Lima]]
  • Screener by [[Patrick Griffin]]
  • Super Duty by [[James Grieshaber]]
  • T26 Carbon and T26 OCRX by [[Anuthin Wongsunkakon]]
  • United Sans by [[House Industries]]
  • some of the Neubau fonts.
Also have a look at License plate typography. F
