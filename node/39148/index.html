My favorite underused classic serifs:

ITC Mendoza FF Atma FF Clifford MVB Verdigris ITC Galliard CC Plantin Miller Arnhem Freight Text Feijoa Rongel Lexicon Renard Le Monde Columbus Mercury Farnham Dante Paperback Eason Hollander Eldorado FF Maiola FF Quadraat Figural Spira Hightower Albertina DTL Documenta DTL Fell DTL Fleischmann Adobe Jenson Laurentian Whitman

As you make your selection, remember to print PDFs and examine catalogs (ask the font seller for a PDF if there is none is on the site). You can’t truly test a text face on screen.
