How is Cataneo BT for a spiritual book title?

Hi everyone,

I have been looking at various typefaces to see which ones would be a good fit for the cover title. I find Cataneo BT quite appealing.

http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/bitstream/cataneo/cataneo/

Given the brief (“other fonts similar or even dissimilar that create the same look and feel” & “a good fit for the cover title”), our chef recommends for your consideration Linotype Le Griffe (designer: Andre-Michel Lubac; publisher: Linotype), P22 Afton (designer: Ted Staunton; publisher: Sherwood Type), Medici Script (designer: Hermann Zapf; publishers: Adobe and Linotype), or even Feather Pen (designer: John Nahmias; publisher: JonahFonts).

Hope this helps.
