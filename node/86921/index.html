Hand-Carved Deutsche Schule Signage

October 30, 2011 • dejongal

This signage for the Deutsche Schule in Richmond UK was carved into limestone by Tom Perkins, a skilled craftsman who specializes in stone letter carving, so I believe the letterforms are probably unique. Nonetheless, I’d appreciate any suggestions from my fellow typophiles for any fonts or designs I could explore that bear some resemblance to the proportions and serifs of these letters, especially the ‘S’, ‘C’ and ‘D’- I’m really taken with those curves! Many thanks in advance.
