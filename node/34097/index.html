To me, “tough” and “stealth” seem mutually exclusive… I could see “stealth in a VERY light weight sans serif, but “tough” usually tends toward somwthing like Goudy Oldstyle extrabold; you might compromise on something like Glaser Stencil (http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/ef/glaser-stencil/), there’s a “rough” version here too.
