Typophile Calendar

November 30, 2006 • Miss Tiffany

Need a reason to celebrate? Subscribe to the Typophile Calendar and get access to historical dates and birthdates associated with the Typophile community and greater world of Typography.

The calendar is available in XML, ICS (iCal), and HTML formats.

iCal users

Step 1: Copy this link. Step 2: Within iCal, under the CALENDAR pull-down menu, select SUBSCRIBE and paste the link. It will now create a new calendar. Step 3: Enjoy! It is now a calendar which you can set to automatically or manually update. If you’re aware of any historical dates not already in the calendar, post them below as comments and one of the Typophile moderators will update it.

Google calendar users

Step 1: Directly underneath “Other Calendars [+]” type the word “Typophile” Step 2: Subscribe to the second one listed.
