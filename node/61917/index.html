2009-09-18 16:18:14 • agisaak

Thanks for uploading another sample. I really like the interwoven letters.

I asked about the double acute since it is typically made up of two normal acute accents, which could not possibly work with your wide acute. It seems to be made up of two narrow acutes instead. My solution would probably be to use a shape similar to your current narrow acute on both wide and narrow letters, double it to make a double acute, and use the same/opposite angle for the grave, circumflex and haček, and make the tilde and breve taller as well to match the other diacritics. You are clearly much more creative than I am, so I am sure you will be able to come up with a less conventional solution if you want to use different accent shapes/widths on different letters.

The comma has the same shape as the apostrophe, as one would expect. In my eyes, this shape looks a bit too square, spiky and ‘unfriendly’ compared to the generous curves elsewhere. Perhaps something more like your d' apostrophe might work just as well for commas, apostrophes, double quotes etc.?

I am looking forward to seeing the final result.
