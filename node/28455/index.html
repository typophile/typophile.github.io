IRISH WHISKEY LABEL

September 27, 2006 • byngo

TIA

I was surprised to find how few fonts I could find that have curved legs on both the K and the R. One was Grouch, but the E and W were not similar, among many other differences. In fact, the digital version does not show the forms of the K and R that are shown in the old Phil’s Photo ‘Homage to the Alphabet’.

I tried using my Serif Font ID Guide and couldn’t get anything that was very close, either. This one therefore becomes tempting to label as custom, so I will be curious to see if anyone can spot it.
