While I agree that any graphic designer should be able to take a crack at it, that kind of highly-dramatic 3D lettering is not something that’s needed very often, so some designers wouldn’t have much experience with it. So I’d look carefully at their portfolios to see if they have nice examples showing similar techniques. Many designers would subcontract an illustration like that out to a commercial illustrator who had a lot of experience with that type of lettering.
