Hello everybody. I have created a simple new typography site at This Day in Type.

This Day in Type is a collaborative venue for typography that uses the day’s date as its subject. Guests may submit artwork for a future date in hopes that it will be selected for publication.

It’s in preLaunch right now, but you may submit art to it for June 1st onward at any time. And please email me at horton[at]thisdayintype{d}com if you find any bugs or turkeys. Enjoy!

2007-05-17 01:33:18 • ebensorkin

Interesting question. Typophile is much more “referential” and “insider” than I intend for This Day in Type. I want to create a venue for great typography in action in a way that the general web public can appreciate. Its about type expression.

The BBS on the site is really there to discuss the artwork for This Day in Type itself. It does allow for some overlap in type discussion, but I am skirting outright war :).

Typophile is the dairy commission; This Day in Type is a farm real estate agent. (One of my crazier metaphors.)
