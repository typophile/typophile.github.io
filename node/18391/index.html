2006-03-08 15:47:02 • oldnick

Eric Gill’s work; he started as a stone cutter. Most of his typefaces derive from the alphabets he learned from stonecarving teachers.

Perpetua, Joanna, Aries, Golden Cockerel

Michael Harvey also has done a lot of carved lettering, as well as a bunch of very nice lapidary typefaces. One of Michael’s other major influences is his prolific work as a letterer for book covers, so his typefaces reflect this as much as they reflect his stonecarving work.

Strayhorn, Mezz, Ellington, Andreas
