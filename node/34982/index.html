These [[http://www.kokuyo.co.jp/hiramekitoys/toypography/|clever blocks]] in a word can spell the word in English and Japanese and form a picture.

(found via [[http://www.boingboing.net/2007/07/08/toypography_blocks_s.html|BoingBoing]])

This is brilliant.

Some of the roman characters that come out of these, while perfectly legible, have some irregularities in form (at the joins, cusps, etc.) I wonder how pure (strictly, hair-splittingly speaking) the characters come out on the Japanese end.
