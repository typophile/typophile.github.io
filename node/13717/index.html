I prefer 4 and 5, but the type (particularly the 'clinton' and 'guild') is flat and dead next to the mark. Try a serif with more humanist features and softening elements; more quirk! Never show a logo you don't want them to choose (if you have a choice). I usually never show more than 3 major variations (sometimes I include sub-variations). If I can, I show 1.

When I think 'ceramics shop' I think of the truly handmade crap you see in every semi-artsy tourist town.
