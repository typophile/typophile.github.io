March 16, 2007 • timfm

Does anyone have suggestions for how to reconcile the fact that my address is changing frequenty and that I’d prefer to not have a PO Box as regards to how to economically design one’s corporate idenity materials?

Thanks, T

You could approach it with only contact information that pertains to phone and email. You could use a web address instead of a physical one. You can list the city, state without a street address. A lot of options.

Another would be, to take the opportunity to create an identity that reflects your transition, and then when you’re established again, create a new identity. You’re a designer, you’re quite capible of doing any of the above or nothing at all.
