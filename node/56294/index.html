2009-03-26 14:32:56 • Dav

It’s very unlikely for someone to make such minor alterations to Lubalin Graph Extra Light and then call it a font. My guess is it’s either a version of Lubalin Graph with alternate characters, or the designer who set the headline just played with Lubalin Graph, got rid of some serifs and added some.

I went to soniacheadle.com (the source of this headline) and in the adress/body copy they’re using Lubalin Graph so even though in the headline/logo the N and the H are slightly wider, the A is missing its “egyptian” apex serif and the E is suffering an extra serif, I doubt that the designer used a different typeface that is sooo similar to the one used in body copy to create this headline. I suspect they altered Lubalin Graph Extra Light.
