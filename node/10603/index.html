I think it's at least customized, mostly because the 'whiskers' (or spikes, sounds more rugged) all point outward on the outer letters, regardless of case. This seems too fortuitous just to be part of the font design.

The lettering style has many Fraktur features, particularly the dagger-like 'p'. but you'll notice there are two different 'h' shapes. This argues even more for completely custom lettering, based on possibly a variety of Germanic Blackletter styles.
