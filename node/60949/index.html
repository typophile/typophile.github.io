St Atmos released

August 13, 2009 • Sascha Timplan

I finally released my first professional Typeface. St Atmos, a bold Headline Typeface with some nice Ink Traps. It’s available right now on MyFonts.com.

[[http://new.myfonts.com/fonts/stereotypes/st-atmos/|St Atmos on MyFonts.com]]

THE CARACTERS ARE NOT REALLY SHARP, MAYBE NOT A GOOD SCAN. LOOK AT SYNTAX FOR GOOD RELATIONSHIPS TO ‘T’ ‘A’ KERNING, MEIER IS NUMBER 2 FONT DESIGNER KNOWS WHAT HES DOING WITH THE HANDWRITING FONT EH?

GENERALLY NEEDS MORE WORK, ALTERNATE ‘R’ PENIS LOOK IS NOT NEEDED.

THERE ARE SO MANY SANS SERIFS THIS DAYS, A NEW ONE HAS TO BE SOMETHING VERY SPECIAL.

SAD. BUT I’M SURE YOU COME UP WITH GOOD IDEA IN THE FUTURE. BEAUTIFUL DESIGNS, GOOD ON YOU MATE.

HOFE.
