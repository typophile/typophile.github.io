Hi, I started this off in the Script section the other day:

(see image 1 below)

It was supposed to have a renaissance feel, but I did some more reading, and came to the conclusion that it was too scripty, since a large part of renaissance type/printing was to move away from the handwritten process. The very beginning though, seemed to be an imitation of handwriting. For this font, I want that feeling to remain, but still look like a jump in the direction towards printing. So, I decided to add serifs to my calligraphy based letters. While it may look like Jenson or Centaur, I'm hoping it has an even more calligraphic feel than both (though slight).
