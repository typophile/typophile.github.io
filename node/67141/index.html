Yes it’s possible - whether it’s economically feasible or even whether you’ll get decent enough results is a different question. This is going to take a lot of time and you’ll just have to trace out all the shapes by hand.

Or you could use something like vectormagic (http://vectormagic.com/home) and see how well automated vectorization algorithms handle it. But you’ll probably need to do quite a bit of clean-up afterwards.

Frankly I’d tell the client that this is too complex to turn into vector artwork without major simplification.
