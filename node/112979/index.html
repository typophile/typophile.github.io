2014-02-15 23:05:42 • Special-K
Some fonts do not age well. Technology changes. LCD was great for a 1980s movie to show the cutting edge of technology. Today it is beginning to look increasingly quaint. Same for OCR shaped fonts – so 70s. For a movie maker who wants a film to continue to communicate with people for many years into the future tried and tested works well. Consider Windsor in Woody Allen’s movies, or Bernhard Modern in Downton Abby.
