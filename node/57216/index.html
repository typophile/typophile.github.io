My first sans serif font

April 21, 2009 • Charles_borges_de_oliveira

I was inspired by a photo of some old letters on a building. So I decided to make my first sans serif typeface. These are some promo pieces I made. The font is called Gloria and will be released at Letterhead Fonts on April 27th.

I had alot of fun making it. Quite a different process than making a script face, thats for sure :)

-Charles Borges de Oliveira
