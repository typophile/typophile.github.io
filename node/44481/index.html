For example, to me, Palatino really looks like an old, somewhat distinct, calm and collected man, someone who has lived a life and has lots of experience but doesn’t flaunt it at all. Galliard is also someone with experience, but he can be a lot cooler and edgier than Palatino – which I see in the italic –, but a kind of an educated coolness that only years of experience can bring.
