Bob’s find of Yorkville is as close as I have seen. But, based on a reasonably thorough search, my opinion is that this probably does not exist as a publicly available font. In particular I have not spotted the combination of a vertical leg on the R like Helvetica but without the little kick at the bottom, G with a rounded lower rhs and no spur like a compressed Futura, W with a vertex that is at only about two-thirds of the letter height, and an S with extended terminals similar to Helvetica. Plus the the base of A is as wide as O. Futurvetica anyone?
