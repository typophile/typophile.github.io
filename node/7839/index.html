Custom, but not particulary well done, The designer must have started with an existing font, (over)slanted it and added flourishes.
Look at the way the flourish on the D is attached – it looks like a cut up O, clumsily glued on. The A is really bad; far too heavy in relation to the rest.
Thus must have been a well paid job and it makes one wonder about the ethical side of doing these kind of jobs…
