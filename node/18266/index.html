TypeCon Auction: yay or nay?

March 1, 2006 • Nick Shinn

A longstanding feature, the auction was dropped from TypeCon last year. It involved serious logistics to organize, and despite the efforts of volunteers, the large overheads involved meant that it didn't always pay its way. To be a success, it was dependent on getting good donations, and spirited bidders. TypeCon attendees -- did you miss it? Are your bookshelves overflowing with typographic exotica you'd like to donate? Would you rather acquire such stuff from the antiquarian bookseller at the conference, or does an auction's excitement, serendipity, and the prospect of an amazing bargain hold more allure? Should we have an auction at Boston? --Nick Shinn, Board Member, SOTA (TypeCon organizing body)
