Hi Jeremy! I live in nearby Pawtucket, Rhode Island, with my partner of twelve years and his elderly pug... :) As ChrisL suggests, RISD would be a good place to meet fellow typophiles too. There isn't much for bookstores, but in Downtown Providence there is a nice "closeout / remainder" store called Symposium Books with a nice selection of art books.
