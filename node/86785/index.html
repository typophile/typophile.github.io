I have done a lot of designs for high school and college sports teams–a vestige of being a parent–and some of fonts that I use a lot are Chevin, Eras, Morgan Avec and Morgan Sans, Soho and Soho Gothic and Vitesse in various weights and widths. They are solid and read well when screened and/or sublimated. They are also a little more current in appearance than the ‘old school’ look of Princetown and this appeals to athletes as well as the parents, coaches, staff or school paying for it.
