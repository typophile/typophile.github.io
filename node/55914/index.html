Hello all… Does anyone know what this font is? For a bonus point can anyone suggest a more appropriate font?? Thanks in advance!!

I think “Angel Delight” looks custom, although it may be a custom font. [The small lettering is FF Cocon, but not ‘fluffy’ and ‘light’, which seems to be a third style, similar to Frankfurter].

As for alternatives, if you want the halo effect in the lower bowl of the g from Angel, how about Canada Type’s [[http://new.myfonts.com/fonts/canadatype/orotund/|Orotund]]. It’s a better contrast with the other two typefaces used in the ad, as now they seem too similar to each other.
