Given that your sample is painted on glass, it's unlikely that it is a typeface. Sign painters learned, or developed their own versions of, block lettering and other styles. Agency Black Extended is similar, and it wouldn't take much work to flatten the spine of the S and close the gap in the R: https://store.typenetwork.com/fonts/?name=Agency
