2005-03-06 14:29:54 • bowfinpw
Ok, here is what I came up with. I used a book weight of Avant Garde and thickened the stroke. I used the Black weight of Quay Sans and thinned the stroke, and rotated the S back a bit (these were the only weights I had). That was pretty close, but in order to make a better match I ultimately had to move the leg of the R (after converting it to outlines) and use a triangle to fill in the point where the bowl almost touches the stem of the R.

So it wasn't as easy as I thought, but then I am not very skilled at outline manipulation of letters. Bottom line, I still think it's custom lettering, but what I did might be a way to re-create it.
