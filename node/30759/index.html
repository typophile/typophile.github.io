FF Typestar

January 21, 2007 • Stephen Coles

[[Indices]] : [[Typefaces]] : FF Typestar [[Steffen Sauerteig]], 1998 FF Typestar™ is a collection of five unaffected fonts for the working world -- the classic typewriter meeting the demands of modern communication. The four basic weights offer everything necessary for office communication and the OCR variation is a monospaced alternative for more mechanical moments.
