There is something fabricated about these letters. Look at the stems of the B and E. The S looks tipped back to the left, or inverted; the K looks compressed by comparison. The M could be an upside down W – in fact your choice of Eagle would actually get you VERY close if you start thinking 'modify', 'thicken', 'squeeze', and 'flip'. The W, for example, would make this M perfectly. The A, C and O are already exactly the right shapes, and maybe need stroke thickening. The J has been altered.

My vote is crudely customized Eagle Bold – just where you started.
