[[http://www.houseind.com/index.php?page=showfont&id=536&subpage=viewfonts/|Luxury Text]] reveals a lot of Novarese influence and can make a nice, crisp alternative.

The upright caps in Novarese Italic are a nod to the earliest italic styles in type, such as those by Arrighi, ca. early 1500s, which utilized roman capitals.
