Curiosity: The Swedish newspaper "Svenska Dagbladet" was re-designed (about 2004?) by Örjan Nordlings and the crew at Pangea Design with a little typographic help from Stefan Hattenbach (Mac Rhino) and Mark Winelid (Typecraft). The newspaper uses a font called SvD grotesk (with inspiration from Griffith and Bell) and a customized version of Poynter that's called Poynter SvD Os Display light and regular. You can read more about this at Pangea Design's site (Swedish only).
