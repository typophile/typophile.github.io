Some version of Didot compressed? Note the extreme thick & thin contrast and hairline squared serifs. But I don’t recognize this version offhand. Perhaps proprietary.

Fairly close is Typofonderie’s AmbroiseStdFirminLight: [img:sites/default/files/old-images/AmbroiseStdFirminLight_5040.jpg]

Some Didots, like this Ambroise and one from Canada Type have ball terminals similar to your image, while other Didots have a gradual flow into the hairline stroke. [img:sites/default/files/old-images/DidotDisplayDemiCT_5659.jpg]
