To me, the 2 best styles are Helvetica Neue Light and Helvetica Neue Bold which I believe are also considered by many as the most iconic and well designed styles of Helvetica Neue. [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/adobe/helvetica-neue|Adobe version]] is cheaper too. You could also have a look to Helvetica clones: URW++ [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/urw/nimbus-sans|Nimbus Sans]], Paratype [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/paratype/pragmatica|Pragmatica]], Scangraphic [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/efscangraphic/europa-grotesk-no-2-sh|Europa Grotesk]], Bitstream [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/bitstream/swiss-721|Swiss 721]] And finally, if you couldn’t afford any of them, you could fall to the lure of the Dark Side of the Force and go with Arial (Bold or Black) which comes free with Windows, mainly reworking terminals and making them horizontal.
