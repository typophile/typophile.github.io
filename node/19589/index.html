Suggestions for Corporate Newspaper Redesign

April 28, 2006 • thierry blancpain

hello typophiles

at work we are redesigning the corporate newspaper of a very big global company, active in the medical sector.

at the moment, they use the official CD-fonts, namely Minion for text and minion condensed for headlines and Berthold Imago for subheads, and captions and infoboxes.

needed:

  • two typefaces that get then used for headline and body copy, infoboxes and captions. normal, bold and italic, the fonts need char-sets for spanish, german, french and english.
  • a sans and a serif that work together
  • typefaces that dont use too much space (the layout has 5 columns, the papersize is only A3)
  • style: classic but modern, timeless, not trendy and not too playful (no vista, for example :))

do you have any ideas or thoughts? any help would be greatly appreciated!

I’m not the man, but I find it odd no one has jumped on this.

To start, classic and timeless things can be found at Storm Type.
