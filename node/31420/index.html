Because identical letters are not identical it points to a hand-drawn logo, or something hand-customized.

Similar fonts could be Mason Serif (or Sans) from Emigre.

Another called ‘Blackfriar’ actually has horns on the ‘g’.

For some reason Percolator seems to show up in a lot of devil-ish contexts, maybe because it ‘perverts’ normal letters?

Another font with somewhat arcane letterforms is Font Bureau’s Saber.

There are many others that just need some imagination and creepy shading to look sinister.
