Well, I made a determined effort to locate this: I searched all Handwritten fonts at MyFonts, I searched two categories from my Script Font ID Guide, and I searched Dafonts Handwritten fonts. I could not find an identical match.

Close-but-no-cigars were: MVB Calliope, Sweet Pea, Caliban, About Face and With Ink (all at MyFonts. You’ll have to pardon my failure to add links for them.)

I think your sample shows some professionalism in the way the letters are formed and kerned, so I believe it is a font, but I cannot find it (yet), and I don’t know if further searching is justified. Hand printed lettering is just too common.
