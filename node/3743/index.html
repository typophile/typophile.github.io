OK, but short ones.

Notan: the relationship/unity/balance of Black and White. Raw reading information.

Bouma: the visual-syntactic atom of immersive reading; almost always the blurry notan of a string of letters; usually the blurry outline of a single word in the parafovea.

Chirography: the effect of the arm/hand/pen system on letterforms.
