Details…

August 20, 2003

Due to recent email self-replicating virus floods all over the world, the lab has decided to reconsider its email exposure policy. As this happened in the middle of a major web site update, I am using this circumstance to take apostrophiclab.com offline for a while, until the new site is ready. The site will hopefully be back around the middle of September. A new format will be introduced, including the following:

- Periodical alphabet drawing courses, in both Fontlab and Illustrator.
- Periodical type history and font critique articles.
- Focus on text fonts.
- Possible affiliations with open source entities.
- An email newsletter, and possibly a print periodical.
- A font troubleshooting / typography consultation section.

All the Apostrophe/Fredrick Nader fonts that existed at the old lab site will change their licensing status from freeware to public domain. From that point on, all fonts designed and published on this site will be public domain. Naturally this will mean that many of the fonts that were on the old lab site will not be here anymore, but I believe that over the past three years there has been enough dissemination of this lab's work that everything can still be found somewhere on the internet.

All the best,

Fredrick Nader
