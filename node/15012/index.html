2005-09-09 16:50:46 • gln
[[Image Club]] licensed and digitized Stymie Hairline from ITC back in 1989. That particular version of the face was removed from the Image Club library for quality reasons during our tenure in the [[Adobe Type|House of Adobe]] around 1997. You can still find Stymie Hairline on used Image Club LetterPress CDs, which pop up once in a while. I may still have a couple buried in the basement somewhere… Just be warned, this digitization is a bit rough around the edges.
