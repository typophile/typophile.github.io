Could any of you tell me which are these fonts the Duden 6 dictionary of pronunciation uses:

http://www.amazon.de/Duden-Bde-Bd-6-Aussprachew%C3%B6rterbuch-Ausspracheworterbuch/dp/341120916X#reader_341120916X

?

Duden dictionaries are typeset using a modified (“forged”) version of Kepler.

Look at Roman “a” and Italic “p” to see such modifications (“forgeries”).

The headwords in the dictionaries are typeset using Frutiger Bold Narrow.
