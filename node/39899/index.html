You can go through the [[http://www.unicode.org/charts/|code charts at Unicode.org]], but even analyzing all those PDFs for the Latin characters alone can be overwhelming.

The [[http://diacritics.typo.cz/|Diacritics project]] explains the usage of many accents in several languages. It is very informative, but it still has some holes to be filled.
