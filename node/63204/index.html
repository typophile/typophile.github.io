what is your designer quirks ? (how do you nerd out)

October 17, 2009 • jwchen

I’m curious about your everyday designer quirks

my:

  • Will tryout a drink with an interesting label
  • Prejudging a public transport system base on their font
  • Purchasing a whole magazine because it have 1 inspirational picture/graphic/layout
  • Prejudice against bars with a san-serif logo

if you have twitter account you can #designerquirks and share

I will never draw or sketch anything in pencil.

I cannot stand the paleness and lack of contrast that pencils make. (all hardnesses)

If you are too afraid to rely on the finality and permanency of an ink pen (cheap and high quality), then you are too afraid to make a mark and back your hand, eye and mind.

Also, in design studios I have worked in the past I have loved companies that have had pens laying around everywhere. (& not to mention computers)

My 2 cents worth…

(Oh, and a coffee or a tea while on the computer makes the world a better place!)
