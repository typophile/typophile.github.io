If you like that kind of thing, try to get your hands on a copy of Harry Nilsson’s Son of Schmilsson album, the original 1972 vinyl edition. The whole album jacket, [[http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-2oYGA95F13A/Tvyonxe68xI/AAAAAAAAIv8/5dXygX8j1M4/s1600/front.jpg|front]] and [[http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-Qa5_FLDYuqY/TvyooDDJbLI/AAAAAAAAIwI/84FbUDQIXl8/s1600/back.jpg|back]], and a complete lyric sheet inside, were all lettered by hand (by Peter Agapiou) in a glorious sanguine style.

The music is pretty good, too.
