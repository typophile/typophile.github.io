ID Klondike Goldrush 1898 Job or Letterpress Type samples

April 8, 2011 • Janice Cliff

Hello everyone. I am hoping someone can help identify the names of the type found on posters and playbills in Dawson City Yukon during the goldrush…1898-1904 roughly. There were 2 newspaper printing presses here, and I believe many print jobs would have been done in Seattle. The distance and freight charges to get here would have made metal or lead type very expensive to stock. I am hoping to identify the name, the foundry, and an equivalent font available today. The only one I think I have identified is #6 “Shoeffer Old Style”.. but I have been unsuccessful finding a replica font on the market today. Any help would be most appreciated…we are trying to develop a toolkit for businesses and individuals to mimic and replicate the poster styles of the past, with as much authenticity as possible.
