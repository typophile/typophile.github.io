2005-01-22 13:41:58 • bowfinpw
Stuart Sandler (Font Diner) has a Dr. Seuss-inspired font called Jumping Bean (part of the "Doggie Bag" collection).

There was also a font created for the Grinch movie a few years ago, but I don't think it has ever been made available to the public. It had a Seussian feel to it, but was much more restrained.

I've done this style of lettering before, and I think it is very difficult to truly capture the irregular quality of Dr. Seuss' lettering style in a font. Type just doesn't work that way.
