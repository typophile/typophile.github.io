Title says it all. This is from a device made in 1965 in the UK. It was labled using dry transfer lettering. Any ideas?
That’s Cartoon C by Howard Allen Trafton, 1936. It was the lighter version of a font seen often in modern days, Cartoon Bold C. I know of no digital version of the lighter variant you seek.
