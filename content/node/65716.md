body: "Zara and I were talking recently about how much handier it would be to be able
  to spontaneously blog to Typophile when we find great content on other sites. \r\n\r\nIt's
  taken some trial and error but I'm happy to say that these work! Drag these links
  to your browser bar and click them whenever you want to share on Typophile. They've
  been tested on Firefox (Mac) and Safari (Mac) so let me know if you get unexpected
  results in another browser.\r\n\r\n(Note: You must be logged in at Typophile for
  these links to work.)\r\n\r\n<a href=\"javascript:q=''+(window.getSelection?window.getSelection():document.getSelection?document.getSelection():document.selection.createRange().text);location.href='http://www.typophile.com/node/add/blog?edit[body_filter][body]='+encodeURIComponent(location.href)+'&edit[title]='+encodeURIComponent(document.title)+q;\">Blog
  this on Typophile</a>\r\n\r\n<a href=\"javascript:q=''+(window.getSelection?window.getSelection():document.getSelection?document.getSelection():document.selection.createRange().text);location.href='http://www.typophile.com/node/add/forum?edit[body_filter][body]='+encodeURIComponent(location.href)+'&edit[title]='+encodeURIComponent(document.title)+q;\">Post
  to Typophile</a>\r\n\r\n"
