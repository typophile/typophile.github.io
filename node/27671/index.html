Two suggestions for the critique forum...better description + sticky posting guidelines

August 15, 2006 • aluminum

On the main forum page, the ‘critique’ forum mentions that it’s a place to critique typefaces. It’d be nice if that description was a bit detailed and included the fact that logos and layouts are also critiqued in there.

Once in there, it’d be nice to have a reminder for folks how to best label their thread. Here’s a quick sample of many of the current logo thread titles:

  • Logo Design Advice/Critique Please
  • Need advice on word mark
  • Advice for logo
  • Personal Logo… and Type
  • need opinion please
  • Help
  • Personal Logo
  • Need Feedback on “logo”

It’d be nice to remind people to use a descriptive title so we can follow the individual discussions a bit better.

Thank you for that post.

I was actually just thinking about that issue when I titled my post “Logo Design Advice/Critique Please.” My two concerns were:

  1. Judging by the way other posts are named, perhaps it is not good to put the name of the company in the label. In retrospect, that does not really make much sense.

  2. At times it can be difficult to keep a label concise and still desccriptive.

On the other hand. This very issue created a problem for me recently because I read a posting one day and then I could not find it again a few days later.

Again, thanks for bring up this concern. I for one will make sure to label my future posts more carefully.
