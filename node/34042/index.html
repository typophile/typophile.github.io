Hi, I know this could very well be hand drawn considering that it is from the early 70s, but could anyone help in possibly identifying this typeface, with or without the block shadow? Thanks in advance.

[img:sites/default/files/old-images/b79c_1_5710.JPG.jpg]

I looked through several books of Photo-Lettering and other film types, which were widely used in the 70’s. The open typeface which has the closest ‘G’ I could find was called Henrion Weight (In Photo-Lettering Catalog Vol 2). I don’t know of any digitizations of this.

For something similar in digital form, I think Folio has some weights that have this approximate shape and a somewhat similar G. I little shape manipulation could make the resemblance stronger, and Folio was designed in the late 50’s - early 60’s, so it fits the era.
