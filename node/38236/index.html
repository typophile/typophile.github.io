Palatino is similar to Fairfield and is a font most people have access to; in fact, I had a project where I needed a Fairfield substitute, and Palatino is exactly what I used–it worked swell. Microsoft’s True Type imitation of Palatino is Book Antiqua. I think you should stick to Venetians if you want to get the same look and feel as Fairfield.

Parisine–wouldn’t any nice humanist sans work too? Frutiger immediately comes to mind as an accessible but less sterile alternative to Officina Sans; if you have FF Meta, that would be an ideal alternative for obvious reasons.
