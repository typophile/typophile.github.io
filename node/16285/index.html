The original ligatures in the text were hardcoded, not automatically assembled by referencing their component letters. They might also have used expert set characters or a non-standard encoding (just speculating there).

You’ll need to search and replace them with their component characters. It may be easiest to do this in Word before bringing the text over to ID.
