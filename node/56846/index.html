the problem with the e is on the right side of the top bowl. it's too pointy at the corner and too flat at the 1-2 o-clock part of the curve. the curved terminals are cute. they would work better if they were consistent in their direction and application. try writing the name on paper a few times with a brush or a marker or something then pay attention how the terminals look. if you don't do anything else, at least add your curvey things to the crossbar and tail of the t. if you want to have this thing spaced so tightly, consider getting rid of the left side of the t's crossbar. lastly, that's a bad place for the registered mark. you want it out of the way, somewhere I'm not going to notice it.
