Luc Devroy is a fountain of type-wealth on this case, too: Here his list of Italien type designers.

One of the most famous is Aldo Novarese. Have a look at his Recta for a mid 20th century sans serif …

AFAIK, Akzidenz Grotesk was drawn by Günter Gerhard Lange and wasn’t redrawn/digitized by any Italian designer.
