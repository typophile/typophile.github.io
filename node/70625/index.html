How big is a "line" in a speciment book, i.e 2-line grotesque, 7-line grotesque?
2010-05-30 10:26:07 • hashiama
“#-line” designations started out with a reference to exactly what size was being multiplied — e.g., Four Lines English, Two Lines Great Primer, etc. Later, I believe that conventions converged on Pica as the foremost basic measure; and 2-line, 7-line, etc., thus refer to multiples of Pica.
