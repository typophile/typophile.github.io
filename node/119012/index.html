A little help on this sign shot from bus in Spain
I didn’t spot a font that matched these Spanish wine shop letters. Here is a somewhat similar font of about the right stroke thickness based on lettering found on buildings constructed in Spain’s Andalucian region in the 1930’s and 1940’s. John Chippindale’s Avenida http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/letraset/avenida/ Many other Art Deco fonts, but none that I saw get much closer. Don
