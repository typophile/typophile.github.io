Yes, Velvet looks quite accurate; MacBeth is more ornate.

An outline version of Othello was created by Dan Solo and included only in the Dover Book/CD “24 Outline Fonts”. It doesn’t seem to ever be sold separately.

A solid version, as well as a matching fill version, was recently (2009) produced by ‘Character’ and is available [[http://abfonts.freehostia.com/|here]]. They are probably back-creations from the Solo font.
