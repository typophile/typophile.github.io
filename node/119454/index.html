Over the last years we were relatively happy with our logo:

Nevertheless I was never really satisfied with how it had turned out and tried dozens of redesigns and spent hundreds of hours, working on concepts and ideas that all turned out to be mediocre at best.

Some days ago there was this idea of trying to set the word in a unicase type in the hope of ending up with a nicer and simpler overall appearance. I tried some different fonts and stumbled upon Designal from Félix Rufín.

I reworked the “L” to represent a lowercase “l” based on the underlying theme of DIN and worked on the overall rhythm by choosing a somewhat looser tracking. Finally I worked on the kerning and ended up with this:

It would be really great to get your feedback on the concept, choice of font, and especially the tracking and/or kerning.

Thank you very much :)

As could be expected, I think |L| is the weak spot. Unfortunately there is not much choice between a big white gap or sitting uncomfortably on the baseline…
