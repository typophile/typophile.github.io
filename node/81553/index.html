20thC Blackletter/Script hybrid on Balkan coffee packaging

April 25, 2011 • Birdseeding

Looking for blackletters on food packaging for the thread over in General Discussions, I saw this coffee brand over in the immigrant-item grocery store here in Kista, which appears to be from the Balkans somewhere, possibly Croatian as far as I can tell:

I really like the script at the bottom, that says “mljevena” and “pcžena u zcnu”. It seems to nicely synthesise Baseball-style scripts with contemporary blackletters like [[http://new.myfonts.com/fonts/underware/fakir/|Fakir]] and [[http://www.houseind.com/fonts/blaktur|Blaktur]], or possibly something in the Grecian vein, like the italic of [[http://www.typography.com/fonts/font_overview.php?productLineID=100000|Acropolis]].

Any idea what it could be? Custom? Or a typeface?

I don’t think I know of exact match, but Cabarga Cursiva is fairly similar.
