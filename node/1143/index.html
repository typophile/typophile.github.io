Some of you hit the nail on the head. That picture is a screen capture of Bitstream's proprietary outline format (DFO). Displaying the points' radii was optional and was really useless except for marketing purposes. Potential customers would be wow'd when they saw that display, and it made for good printed matter. The original display was the old green line on black, whatever that was called, which made it even more attractive. BTW, we still use the DFO format occasionally, but bezier is our format of choice now. Jim L.
