Despite propaganda to the contrary, there's nothing inherently wrong with style linking. You just have to make sure that the fonts given to the output provider include the bold (which may mean the whole family, depending on format and how they're built).

As long as you do that, you're fine. Any statement to the contrary is ignorant superstition (though there's plenty of it around).
