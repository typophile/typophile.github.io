2007-01-17 14:31:39 • timd

On the logo: Futura Caps are stronger than the lower case, so I think having the name of your company in all caps will look better. This may require the rest to be smaller, but that I doubt that that will be a problem.

On the lines: I agree with Franz that the rules don’t work well here. They don’t seem to have a functional purpose, and they also don’t add to the visual balance, but detract from it. If you want rules in the design, I think another layout will be needed.

I would also think about a design that balances the weight of the black and white flag in the type layout.

Oh I just checked some of the designs of Tschichold. Your assymetric clean sans layout is of the spirit of Tschichold in his modernist phase. There he didn’t use a rule like this, and perhaps it is out of place in this style of design. He does have designs with a thick rule to the right of a right ragged column of information.

You could experiment with that here by killing the rule at the top and putting a strong rule to the right of the information column, in the business card. Here the idea would be to balance the flag at the top left assymetrically with the rule at the bottom right…

[edit: just read Tim’s comment. That is another way to go. in any case, I think this needs further experimentation to really get it balanced.]
