In the late 1940s Jan Tschichold redesigned Penguin Books and developed the “Penguin Composition Rules”.

For the Penguin Classics, Tschichold would mix various weights of Monotype Bembo and Monotype Centaur Titling. For the the King Penguin Series quite a few: Pastonchi, Walbaum, Lutetia (by Jan van Krimpen). Bell for many of the Penguin Poets and Caslon Old Face for the Penguin Musical Scores.
