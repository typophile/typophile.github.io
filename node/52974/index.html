It is certainly a modified version of Stop by Aldo Novarese (also known as a Custom Stop!). Here is an instance of the M in Stop modified in a very similar way. The U and C are from an entirely different font, likely to be Futura as Stephen suggested; you can also tell they don’t fit by the fact that they aren’t overshooting properly and their weight is not quite right compared to the other shapes.

Yes, that’s definitely a possibility although I haven’t tracked down any instance of it so far. Stop is so easy to customise that I wouldn’t be surprised to find hacked copies of it in rogue type foundries’ catalogues. Yesterday for example I spent some time browsing dafont.com and found this Galant which is just one giant Custom Stop. I am also not aware of any alternate versions by Aldo himself.
