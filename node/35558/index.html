2007-07-30 22:51:47 • tilboy
Hi tilboy, Font-Choice: I think there are many rounded well executed fonts which are far better than the ancient VAG Rounded used in your draft. I can recommend Gotham Rounded (http://www.typography.com/catalog/gothamrounded/index.html) or Chevin (http://www.veer.com/products/typedetail.aspx?image=GTT0000003). Logo-Appearance: The first thing that came into my mind, is that the rainbowish forms look like a tunnel/cave or is picturing sending out a signal. Also I think that the square form with rounded corners is more Caravan like. If it should be the rainbow form, you can maybe use the C or c to build the caravan rainbow. The tracking for the word Caravan is to loose and the tracking for center to tight. Also I would correct the space on the right and left side of the v. Also the space between r and a is to loose. Are there other ideas/variants/drafts? Derya p.s. I like the second draft, but the word center looks bigger than the word caravan.
