Not exactly the same but…

[[http://cg.scs.carleton.ca/~luc/hutchings/gracia-medium.jpg|Arno Willy Schumann]] Born in 1891 in Leipzig-Gohlis, d. 1970, Leipzig. Designer at Schriftguss in 1927 of the flowing script font Butterfly. This was “aped” to some extent by the Amsterdam Foundry in its Gracia. At the same foundry, he designed the script faces Troubadour Magere and Halbfette in 1927, as well as Butterfly Halbfette in 1928.
