Boldoni - A Caricature of Modern Typefaces

October 3, 2007 • fortetype

Dear friends,

Boldoni is like a caricature: it brings with itself exaggerated elements of the modern typefaces, that has as main names Giambattista Bodoni and François-Ambroise Didot. Boldoni takes to the egde its width and serifs, causing a ultrafat type feeling, and its styles causes a monochromatic effect that remember the colors Black, Gray and White. Some of uses are titles, initials, drop caps, lettering, posters and vertical writing.
