If I remember my Caligraphy correctly (it has been 42 years), the Dwiggins sample you posted looks like a variant on Simplehand Roman. The proportions are different though. Simplehand was a true Roman proportioned writing. The Dwiggins above is much more monoproportioned. The "o" and "e" are quite narrow for Roman proportion but strangely the "g" is wider.
