Tag der Schrift

May 21, 2005 • spatium

[[Indicies]] : [[Conferences]] : Tag der Schrift »Tag der Schrift« (Day for Writing/Type/etc.) is an annual one-day conference organized by the Swiss media union [[comedia]] and the the School of Design in Zurich. The 2005 Conference In 2005, the conference was held on June 18. It included lectures by [[Gerard Unger]], André Baldinger (Paris), Wang Chao Ying (China) and Volker Heim. More information: http://www.typo-online.ch/tds/2005/ A report by [[Peter Reichard]], with images, can be found on [[TypeOff]] at http://www.typeoff.de/?p=30 The 2006 Conference Plans for the 2006 Conference have not yet been announced.
