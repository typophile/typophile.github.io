MAM, short for Merdeka Ataoe Mati: http://merdekaataoemati.com/works/dec08/entry02/01.swf http://merdekaataoemati.com/works/dec08/entry02/08.swf http://merdekaataoemati.com/works/dec08/entry02/10.swf

MEW: http://merdekaataoemati.com/works/jan09/entry04/01.swf

PS: You can zoom, rotate or mirror them if you view them on [[http://merdekaataoemati.com|my site]].

Well, I don’t want to be harsh but the only ambigrams here are the DIK and MEW. Ambigrams are words that after rotating them 180 degrees look the same… None of MAM symbols does that, neither jimmy (even though I like it most). And besides - it probably wasn’t very hard to make a MEW ambigram, huh? ;)

If I’m wrong - let me know. I’m not 100% sure…
