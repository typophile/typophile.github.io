Summer Workshops 2009 at the Academy of Art and Design Basel

January 30, 2009 • Oliver Wehn

The [[http://www.fhnw.ch/hgk/ivk|Visual Communication Institute]] of the Academy of Art and Design UAS Northwestern Switzerland in Basel is offering workshops for students, graphic designers and educators. The workshops afford insight into topical themes in Visual Communication in a study program reflecting the rich tradition of the Basel School of Design.

Practical exercises with a high level of professionalism form the core of the workshops’ focus. Reflection as well as contextual knowledge will be conveyed by way of input sessions, allowing participants’ work to be judged within a contemporary, future-oriented, context relevant to professional practice.

Choose a workshop on one of the topics Poster Design (with [[http://www.leandereisenmann.ch|Leander Eisenmann]]), Type Design (with Philipp Stamm, Co-Author of «[[http://www.amazon.com/Adrian-Frutiger-Typefaces-Complete-Works/dp/3764385812/ref=sr_1_1?ie=UTF8&s=books&qid=1233357434&sr=8-1&tag=visualattitud-21|Adrian Frutiger Typefaces. The Complete Works]]») or Inquiry By Design (with Michael Renner, head of the Visual Communication Institute).

Get more information [[http://www.fhnw.ch/hgk/ivk/summer-workshops|here]].
