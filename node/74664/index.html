2010-09-28 14:59:30 • oldnick

Akira Kobayashi’s FF Acanthus has some good neoclassical ornamentation.

But surely the point of the Didone (modern) style was a spartan classicism. The only ornament really necessary when working in this style is the bulging rule; IIRC H&FJ have a few of those.

Various Didone glyphs have been used decoratively, such as the asterisk.

I included a few dingbats (not really ornaments) in Scotch Modern.
