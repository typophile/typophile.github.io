Just a note.

I don’t want to be responsible for handling credit card information

For this you don’t need to use “hosted payment pages” (which is what you are effectively asking for), all you need is a hook-up to a “payment gateway”. It allows you to keep customers entirely on your site, but forward CC authorization to a third party. And if you do not store CC numbers anywhere on your server, you are in the clear in terms of PCI compliance (which is the “being responsible for handling cc information” part).

By processing the cards yourself (and compared to hosted option) you’ll be saving about 1% of commission and 10c or more in fixed fees per transaction. Something to think about I guess.
