Heavy metal bands are popular among Americans born after WWII, and I suspect that they chose these fonts because of Fraktur’s associations with Nazi Germany*. In other words, it symbolized something powerful and dangerous that Boomers' parents had fought against. What better symbol of adolescent rebellion could you ask for?

In 1941 Martin Bormann banned Fraktur in a Schrifterlass (edict on script) because it was “Schwabacher Judenlettern” —start quote— “For general attention, on behalf of the Führer, I make the following announcement: It is wrong to regard or to describe the so-called Gothic script as a German script. In reality, the so-called Gothic script consists of Schwabach Jew letters. Just as they later took control of the newspapers, upon the introduction of printing the Jews residing in Germany took control of the printing presses and thus in Germany the Schwabach Jew letters were forcefully introduced. Today the Führer, talking with Herr Reichsleiter Amann and Herr Book Publisher Adolf Müller, has decided that in the future the Antiqua script is to be described as normal script. All printed materials are to be gradually converted to this normal script. As soon as is feasible in terms of textbooks, only the normal script will be taught in village and state schools. The use of the Schwabach Jew letters by officials will in future cease; appointment certifications for functionaries, street signs, and so forth will in future be produced only in normal script. On behalf of the Führer, Herr Reichsleiter Amann will in future convert those newspapers and periodicals that already have foreign distribution, or whose foreign distribution is desired, to normal script”. —end quote— Moreover, Herr Hitler, wannnabe classical architect, seems to have always preferred the purity of text based on the Roman Trajan Letters. The nations of the east, the north and the west will, to communicate with us, learn our language. The prerequisite for this: The script called Gothic is replaced by the script we have called Latin so far … —end quote– I pulled these quotes from a wiki: http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Antiqua%E2%80%93Fraktur_dispute Anyway the love of blackletter by rock rebels and bikers seems to have missed the mark.
