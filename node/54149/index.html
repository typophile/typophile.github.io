2009-02-03 06:25:16 • AtoZ
With regard to your original question: I think you don’t have to worry too much about being too obvious as long as you do the obvious well – as you have on your site. To use a cross-cultural example, think of way that Handel set music to the words in his Messiah. You can’t get much more literal than he did there: when the text says “shake,” he shakes the music; when it says “exalted,” he exalts the music; when it say “low,” the notes are about as low as a singer can sing. And it’s all great, because it done well.            ~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~          When going from A to Z,          I often end up At Oz.
