2004-01-05 18:15:21 • minipod
I think it's fairly clear that this was a custom lettering job.
The letterforms have typical modern characteristics, but I
doubt you will find an exact match among existing fonts.

The distortions you're seeing have to do with the fact that it
was drawn as an italic, not simply slanted from upright
artwork. When you slant ovals, you get an unnatural looking
south-west to north-east diagonal stress. Correctly drawn
italics are designed to compensate for this distortion. (You
can see the compenstaion if you back-slant them to
upright.) In this case, there appears to be a bit of over-
compensation.
