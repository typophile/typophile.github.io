Do you have an idea as to the vintage of the cheque? Something like this would: A. have been engraved or lithographed for the bank, or B. if the bank was small it might purchase a special cut from a type foundry which would include everything shown in the image. These cuts were sometimes called billheads, or more recently C. a lettering artist would do the design and this would be photographed for printing. There might be a font with the interesting treatment of the e, but I don’t recall it offhand. Don
