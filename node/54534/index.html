Assuming Wikipedia has it right (http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Continental_Airlines), Continental’s logo of the period (ca. 1944-67) did not use the same font. It is in a fairly similar style, but the letterforms are not the same. Note in particular the “R” is radically different.

Also, if Alaska’s logo was used in the late 40s to mid 50s, it wouldn’t be the same as a typeface from the late 50s or early 60s (unless the typeface was based on the logo lettering, as with ITC Avant Garde).
