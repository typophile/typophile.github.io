2004-08-19 16:14:53 • alyce
Red Rooster has a number of condensed fonts with oblong O's like this. Schindler comes in 5 weights.
Raleigh Gothic Condensed comes in 3 weights, but is more squared perhaps than you want.
Pall Mall is another in 2 styles & 2 weights.
Keyboard comes in 3 weights.
Helium comes in 4 weights.
Grand Canyon is all upper case but comes in 8 flavors.
Europa Grotesque comes in 4 weights.
Coliseum has 3 weights.
Chamfer Gothic has angled corners, 3 weights and 2 styles (other is Small Caps Expert set).
And last but not least is Block Gothic, which comes in Condensed & Extra condensed, with 5 weights of each.

I just happen to have the Red Rooster fonts and I use these a lot when I want condensed styles.
