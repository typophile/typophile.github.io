An urgent request, apparently! :) I see this popping up quite often these days.

Jan (and all your other replies) are right, it really must be a version of Futura. Have you looked at how many versions MyFonts lists alone? http://new.myfonts.com/fonts/adobe/futura/ http://new.myfonts.com/fonts/neufville/futura-nd/ http://new.myfonts.com/fonts/berthold/futura-bq/ http://new.myfonts.com/fonts/bitstream/futura/ http://new.myfonts.com/fonts/ef/futura/ http://new.myfonts.com/fonts/urw/futura/ (there are more, don’t forget about Futura clones.)

At the time Futura was released, other foundries came up with similar fonts, to ride on the “Futura Wave”; one of them being Neuzeit Grotesk: [[http://new.myfonts.com/search/(neuzeit+grotesk)/fonts/]] Maybe that is a good direction for you?

A general problem with your request is that your sample images are really low-res, so it is difficult to make up any conspicuous features of the typeface. Also the all-caps / small-caps settings doesn’t help … :)
