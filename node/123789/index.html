Classic Ferrari serial number plates

March 12, 2015 • Pitch

I have been asked to re-create these three metal serial number plates to be used on a restored classic Ferrari. Hopefully the good people of the forum can help me with a few pointers as to the fonts used.

I’m counting 7 different fonts being used - not including the old style Ferrari logo typography (a vector of which is also proving difficult at the moment)

Thanks.

|motore contiene| is Eurostile: http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/linotype/eurostile/

|SHELL SUPER MOTOR OIL| could be replicated using Tablet Gothic (substitute a lowercase |l| for the |I|): http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/type-together/tablet-gothic/

The center panel appears to be a mix of Univers (numbers) and Helvetica (letters): http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/linotype/univers/ http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/linotype/helvetica/

|FERRARI 365 GTB/4-A| appears to be News Gothic Bold: http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/linotype/news-gothic/

|MOTORE ENGINE|, |AUTOTELAIO CHASSIS|, and |MADE IN ITALY| appear to be Futura: http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/linotype/futura/

|365 GTB/4-A| appears to be Reforma: http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/paratype/reforma-grotesk-bold-e/
