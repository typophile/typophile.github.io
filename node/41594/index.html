Addendum: I looked it up in [[Adrian Frutiger|Frutiger]]’s Der Mensch und seine Zeichen [Signs & Symbols]. Re Ondine: The typefaces he used for the layering chart are not Frutiger’s own designs, but the ‘most read’, i.e.:
  • Garamond
  • Baskerville
  • Bodoni
  • Excelsior
  • Times
  • Palatino
  • Optima
  • Helvetica/Univers [sic!]
And Frutiger doesn’t claim that this chart would reveal his Univers as the quintessence, at least not ad verbatim. Btw: He did the same thing for ‘e’ and ‘n’.
