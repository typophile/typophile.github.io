  href='wiki/Yves Peters' class='wiki'>Yves Peters</a>\r\n\r\n<strong >Why doesn't
  this page list freeware font websites?</strong>\r\nMost of the sites below are run
  by type designers or foundries, and they tend to offer a few dozen fonts at most.
  Why doesn't this page list the huge freeware websites? Take a quick look at those
  sites and you'll see that the fonts there are of variable quality, with not many
  that are good for professional design work. But even more important, many of the
  type designs are clearly copied from other people's work. To be fair, mixed in with
  the knockoffs are thousands of original fonts made by amateurs who just love type.
  But the lack of oversight\u2014anyone can upload\u2014and the confusing way that
  those sites mix the bad with the good, makes it hard for us to recommend them. (In
  the end, those sites need no help; a Google search quickly turns them up.) Below,
