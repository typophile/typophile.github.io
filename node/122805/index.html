Closely resembles an old wood typeface called Antique Six: http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/woodentypefonts/antique-six/

Haven’t found any digital matches to the |M| with the pointed center vertex shy of the baseline and the |B| with the stepped back upper bowl. Giza Five Seven by Font Bureau, Material Headline by Optimo, or Egiziano Classic by Dennis Ortiz-Lopez might be decent contemporary substitutes: http://www.fontbureau.com/fonts/Giza/styles/ http://www.optimo.ch/typefaces_Material_Headline.html http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/ortizlopez/olegiziano-classic/
