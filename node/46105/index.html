It seems to match the old Image Club [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/imageclub/arquitectura/|“Arquitectura”]], which is very close to the [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/redrooster/casablanca-rr/casablanca-rrcond-alt-light/|Casablanca Condensed Alternate]]. The main differences are the top of the A, and the extensions to the left of the stem, which are angled, like your sample.
