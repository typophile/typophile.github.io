2004-01-15 21:38:33 • Ignacio
The clock question has lots of answers, and no definitive one. Google "clocks IIII IV" and you'll see that a lot has been written on the subject. The most favored is that the IIII visually balances the VIII opposite it. Another has to do with the supposed practicality of casting an even quantity of each glyph. Others venture into urban legend territory, such as the idea that "IV" was considered too close to blaspheming Jupiter (Ivpiter). An important thing to keep in mind is that subtractive notation was never used consistently by the Romans.
