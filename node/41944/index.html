Have you seen this thread? Where can one learn to become a professional type designer? Here’s a summary of all places that offer type design (strictly):

  • MA Typeface Design (1-year postgraduate course) at the Department of Typography & Graphic Communication, [[University of Reading]], UK Teachers (past & present): [[Gerry Leonidas]], [[Gerard Unger]], [[Victor Gaultney]], [[Thomas Phinney]], [[Sumner Stone]], [[Peter Biľak|Peter Bilak]], [[František Štorm]], [[Albert-Jan Pool]], [[Fred Smeijers]], [[John Downer]] Language: English
  • Type & Media (1-year postgraduate course) at [[Royal Academy of Art|KABK]], Den Haag, The Netherlands Teachers: [[Jan Willem Stas]], [[Erik van Blokland]], [[Peter Verheul]], [[Frank Blokland]], [[Petr van Blokland]], [[Françoise Berserik]], [[Peter Biľak|Peter Bilak]], [[Robin Kinross]], [[Paul van der Laan]], [[Frans van Mourik]], [[Christoph Noordzij]], [[Just van Rossum]] Language: English
  • ‘DSAA Création Typographique’ (2-year program) at [[École Supérieure Estienne des Arts et Industries Graphiques|Ecole Estienne]], Paris, France Language: French
  • 1-year ‘Meisterschüler’ program at [[Hochschule fuer Grafik und Buchkunst|Hochschule für Grafik und Buchkunst]]/Academy of Visual Arts in Leipzig, Germany Supervised by [[Fred Smeijers]] Language: German (English?)
  • Master of Advanced Studies (MAS) at Hochschule der Künste, Zürich, Switzerland Teachers: [[Hans-Jürg Hunziker]], [[André Baldinger]], [[Hans Eduard Meier]], [[Robin Kinross]], [[Fred Smeijers]], [[Peter Rosenfeld]], [[Bruno Maag]], [[Luc(as) de Groot]], [[Ole Schäfer]], [[Peter Biľak|Peter Bilak]]
    • Language: German (French)
  • Master’s degree at Centro de estudios Gestalt, Veracruz, Mexico Language: Spanish (English?)
  • Non-academic: [[Font Bureau]] aka ‘The School of [[David Berlow|Berlow]] in Boston, USA, as suggested by Paul
