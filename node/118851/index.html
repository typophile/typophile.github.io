“Do you have a HD scan of this specimen?” For this you would need to access a printed copy of the ATF 1923 book or order photocopies from a library that has it and is willing to do photocopies. You have the page numbers. Please note that the old specimen books did not usually include a large scale character set. Rather they offered a presentation of phrases in various sizes. The 1923 ATF book was one of the earliest to offer a character set, but only at a smaller scale.
