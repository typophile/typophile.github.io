Tiff nails it on the head. This is not an isolated incident, but the culmination of several weeks and months of repeated incidents. I believe what we have witnessed here is a perfect example of group dynamism at work. Like it or not, Typophile is first and foremost a community, made up of living and breathing individuals. One of the characteristics of such a community is that it is governed by certain unwritten rules. For example a community will always be in flux, but at the same time try to reach a state of equilibrum. Every individual joining that community will disrupt that balance, whereafter the community will attempt to regain a new state of equilibrum. If the balance is too severely disrupted, the rest of the community will try to adapt. When it fails to achieve that in the usual manner, it is to be expected that other members will re-evaluate the community they belong to and their place in it. Some will leave, as we have unfortunately witnessed a little while ago when a most valued member decided to terminate his membership. Others will react and try to rectify the situation in more drastic ways by going head-to-head with the perceived disruptive element. As such, this thread is the result of something that has been building up for the last few weeks and months. We have to be realistic and accept that not everybody fits in just any community. That is the nature of the beast. Again, nobody was censored nor forced out. Joining and leaving Typophile is a personal decision.
