2007-07-20 17:31:14 • Lex Kominek
Those old small office copieers with the sliding top are fun. hang a bit of your original out the edge of the imaging bed and stop it at points as the top tracks across. You'll get fun stretchy copies that way You might even manage to get that stretchy effect on modern desktop scanners (though you'd have to supply motion in the original yourself), but you won't get the grittyness of a copier that way.
