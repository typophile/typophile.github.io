How Much To Charge For Font For Logo Usage

February 20, 2010 • coreyw

I’m sure this has been asked a million times, but I couldn’t find anything on Google, and the search here wasn’t working.

I have a font that is available to download for free for non-commercial work at dafont.com. Someone just asked if they could use the font for a logo for a restaurant. I really don’t know how much I should charge. Being that it will be in a logo makes me think it should be relatively higher priced than if it was being used for a t-shirt or something. Any ideas on an average price to ask?

Something to consider: maybe he’s asking for this font because, since it’s free for non-commercial use, he’s expecting you to charge less than a commercial font would cost. So I wouldn’t charge too much, or else you might lose the business when he decides that if he’s going to have to pay that much anyway then he should use a different font.
