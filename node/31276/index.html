Can’t think of anyone specific in Seattle, but you should visit the School of Visual Concepts. They have letterpress classes and other kinds of design programs, but they also have cool workshops, etc. SVC is going to be involved in Typecon, so that’s a good resource to look into.

If you don’t mind Canada, Jim Rimmer in Vancouver, BC has open invitations for people to come visit him in his shop. I haven’t had a chance yet, but it’s something I have on my list of things to do.

If this is something that Thomas Phinney would be up to, I think it would be great to be able to visit Adobe also. Sign me up for that one.

If you can’t find anything specific, I’m not a type designer, but I’m always down for grabbing a pint!
