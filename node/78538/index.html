> What is PBS?

Oh, sorry; being US-centric there. PBS stands for “Public Broadcasting Service” = American non-commercial broadcast television — hence the somewhat annoying fund drives throughout the year asking for financial contributions from the viewing public. During these periods, they often haul out good ol’ audience favorites for re-broadcast, interrupted with frequent intermissions to make the pitches for pledges.

Victor Borge is one of those perennials. He’s got several classic and really hilarious routines (if you don’t enjoy classical music, however, then many of the inside jokes might pass you by).
