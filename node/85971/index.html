I’m surprised how few orders there seem to be. I had a bit of time on my hands so I went through my sales reports and chose two orders roughly 24 hours apart and worked out from the order numbers how many orders MyFonts takes in a day, divided it by 24 then 60 and found they get about 1.5 orders (paid or free) per minute. I had way too much time on my hands so I did the same thing with longer periods of time between orders - surprisingly the order counts remain pretty consistent - the daily average number of orders is about 2200.

Looking at the map I guess about half of them are Museo ;)
