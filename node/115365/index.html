According to the copyright note in the font, this is “Sans Poster Bold JL” reworked with a starry effect. My initial impression is that “Sans Poster Bold JL” is not a rip of another font-maker’s work. So here is a link to “Starlight Sans JL” http://www.fonts2u.com/starlight-sans-jl.font Note that Jeff Levine’s website, “Jeff’s Fonts & Dingbats” has expired. Don
