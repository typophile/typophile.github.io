subject: contract template for commission?

July 9, 2014 • joachim

dear all,

are there any templates for contracts for type design commissions?

a new client has commisioned me to create a custom type family for them, and asked me to submit a contract for their review, since the matter is rather specialized and they have no similar documents they could use.

so, i’ve been wondering: are there any standard type design contract forms available covering commissioned work, that can be downloaded and customized? i’m happy to pay a reasonable fee if required.

if available, i’m specifically looking for inclusion of wording or clauses regulating: – client company has complete usage rights in print and digital media as long as company exists in its present form, but no rights to resell, while: – designer retains all rights to resell typeface to other companies or via retail.

thanks much in advance for any information! joachim

The Graphic Artists Guild (https://www.graphicartistsguild.org/) publishes The Graphic Artists Guild Handbook: Pricing & Ethical Guidelines, which includes a standard contract for Lettering and Typeface Design. You can order it through their website, or find it in any well-stocked bookstore.

Also, the website has a checklist for writing your own contract, under the resources link.
