You can get this kind of book printing from an online service such as [[http://www.lulu.com/|Lulu.com]]. [[http://www.acmebook.com/|Acme Bookbinding]] near Boston will give more personalized service. Both can ship internationally, but to a book printer, anything under 1000 copies is a very short run. If you plan to print several hundred copies, you may prefer to print them in Europe to avoid a very slow and/or expensive delivery. [[http://www.integratedbook.com/index1.html|IBT Global]] has a facility in London.
