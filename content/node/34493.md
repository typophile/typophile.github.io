    also testing it afterwards. It was published in the book <em>Letters of Credit:
    A View of Type Design.</em> Emil Ruder also used a similar method, published in
    the book <em>Typography, a Textbook of Design.</em>\r\nThey are both also in <em>Designing
    Type</em> by Karen Cheng. Very useful for testing especially horizontal spacing.\r\nMiguel
    Sousa made  <a href=\"http://www.adhesiontext.com/\">Adhesiontext</a>, which generates
    dummy text with a given string of characters. Can also be of use."
