Fonts for Peas has free handwritten fonts. They’re from handwriting samples submitted by regular people.

http://kevinandamanda.com/fonts/fontsforpeas/

Or send in your own handwriting to The Font Garden. They’ll turn it into a font for $40. They also have free and inexpensive handwritten fonts.

http://www.fontgarden.com/

If you want to give a “personal touch” with originality use your own handwriting. The question is: Is your handwriting legible? Here it is some excellent Type Foundries specialized in handwriting/lettering:

  • Sudtipos

  • Borges Lettering

  • DStype has the great Ventura (Dino is not a specialist in handwriting but Ventura is worthwhile to see)
