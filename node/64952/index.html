Letters to put on doors

December 3, 2009 • ebensorkin

I would like to label some doors with letters. The idea I have is to have red circles with white sans serifs capitals in them. But that is not a must, I just want them to be well made and clear. I have looked for something that is pre-made like this but with not much luck so far. Maybe this is always a bespoke product. Still, I thought maybe someone here knows a good vendor that has nice pre made ones. Thanks!
How many of these will you need? You process can be pretty labor-intensive if you’re doing a half-dozen, not if you’re doing a hundred. Will they be indoors or outdoors? What material are the doors made of (wood/metal/glass)? Are they going in a public space where they need to be vandal- and theft-proof? How long do they need to last? Affects your choice of materials, inks/paints, and method of attaching them to the doors.
