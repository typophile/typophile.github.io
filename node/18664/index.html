2006-03-20 16:17:48 • Bald Condensed

The ‘ff’ lig clearly identifies this as a metal Linotype version. That device couldn’t kern (in the classical sense of overhangs projecting beyond the body of a type), so they had to distort the letterforms to compensate. The ‘f’ almost always took the biggest hit.

So, I’m pretty sure this is metal Linotype Times, and have no reason to disagree with david that it’s a semibold italic.
