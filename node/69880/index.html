Font Aid IV update

April 27, 2010 • MichellePerham

So far, “Coming Together”, the Font Aid IV font, has raised approximately $12,000. All of the money raised is being donated to Doctors Without Borders to help with their relief efforts in Haiti. Thanks everyone for your support!

More information on the project can be found here: http://www.typesociety.org/fontaid.html

Book sales have been slow, but continue to climb. The book can be purchased online through Lulu: http://www.lulu.com/product/paperback/coming-together-font-aid-iv/6553234

Please continue to help us spread the word about this project! We really appreciate the enthusiasm and support we’ve gotten from the entire type community.

Michelle & the SOTA board
