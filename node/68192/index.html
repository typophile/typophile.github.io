Why not use the corporate font? It should be suitably understated. Scripts are OK for personal expressions of grief, but kitsch and phony for a corporate expression of sentiment. After all, corporations aren’t concerned with clients' feelings, they are legal entities predicated on profit. Note that you said the company sends out the card, not an insurance agent. If an agent is involved, their handwritten note on an ID-compliant card would make a nice contrast.
