Hi Jake –

I studied on the MA at CSM. There is a letterpress dept. there, which, if you can get in with Nick (the technician) can be very beneficial. You also have the added bonus of Phil Baines and Catherine Dixon as tutors there. I recall a few years ago when LCC was LCP they were getting rid of a lot of letterpress, not sure where it went.

If you wanted to learn letterpress at BA, you would have to probably take it up as a personal endeavour. A friend of mine did a lot of it on her BA at CSM, regardless of this, you’ll have to also study every other area of the graphic arts like every other BA.

Perhaps take a look outside of London. I know that The Cambridge School of Arts have letterpress.

You will probably find presses all over, but your best bet, and probably your best resource is London.
