I inherited this font that I believe is called “Booker” but I cant find any license information for it on the web — does anyone know where it might have come from/who the designer might be?

If nothing else, I would love to find a similar font that is available for commercial use.

The copyright of a Booker font says: “1995 Solotech Design. All Rights Reserved.” According to Luc Devroye info on Solotech: http://luc.devroye.org/fonts-29677.html

"[...] some of their fonts (if not all) are exact unaltered copies of other people's fonts."
Therefore I don't think the original font name was "Booker".

This is one of these fonts copied/sold by many forgery companies under different names and although I doubt any of them is the original design, I’ll list them here to help discovering the original: Booker - 1995 Solotech Design. All Rights Reserved. Dishrow - Converted to PC format for distribution by Reaso Eliza - Generated by Fontographer 3.5 Enterprise - (C) 1992-1993. ATTITUDE, INC. All Rights Reserved.
