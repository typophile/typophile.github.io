This part of a sketch book assignment that my Graphic Design teacher assigned. She asked us to cut out 20 examples of a letter, and 20 items that began with that letter. I only scanned half of mine for the blog. I obviously chose the letter ‘M’, and arranged them by color. I started with cool colors on the half you can’t see and ended with warmer colors.
