Hi Kris. Plantin & Foco have a very similar line colour which was the primary reason for the pairing. As this is primarily an academic text (although by nature of the subject it will no doubt become a coffee table book), I need to pair two fonts that would occupy the same negative space so the change in type face doesn’t interupt the reader.

Plantin is favourite of mine, it was a selfish choice.

Foco possess some very nice subtlities in the tails and feet. To me, and that is not to say this is fact, it seemed sympathetic to Maori line. It’s a bit of a stretch but it just seem to work.

To be fair post-rationalising is for clients. There’s something to be said when things ‘just work’ together. So I apologise if you were after a deeper meaning.

B.
