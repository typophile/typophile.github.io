Meta Everywhere

February 16, 2007 • AndrewSipe

I'm slowly becoming convinced that Meta is taking over Helvetica. I wouldn't be surprised to see a Meta Documentary to come in the near future following the success of the Helvetica one. Watching television, I see commercial after commercial with Meta used (mostly phone numbers). TBS uses it in some of their promos. I also saw is on the side of a Herman Miller Delivery truck this morning, probably 7000pt high. I don't know if this topic has any merit, but I love Meta and I'm glad it's giving Helvetica a run for it's money. Competition is good!
If you like Meta, go visit your local United Way branch.
