Looking for OTF Grotesques with SC/OsF

June 14, 2006 • NiceTry

I have been looking for a sufficiently cold-looking OT grotesque sans in a Swiss vein with all the goodies needed for proper typography, so far without success. There are OpenType versions of Berthold Akzidenz-Grotesk and Linotype Helvetica Neue, but neither of these have small caps or text figures, except the A-G which only has the light weight. I suspect the Helvetica Neue OT is a straight translation from the PS version and has no new typographic features. I emailed Berthold, but they didn’t mention anything about producing other weights with small caps and text figures, and instead pointed me towards Imago Pro, which doesn’t really do the trick.

Does anyone know if such a font as I have described exists? I would think that Font Shop would have something like this, but most of their typographic sans are humanist, and the ones that come close look more like Gotham. I seek something with a cold, blank, scientific stare that functions like a Swiss army knife.
