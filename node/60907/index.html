Jansen? I assume you mean Janson Text, right? The OpenType versions of Janson Text from Linotype do have oldstyle figures (osf), also in the Italics. There’s no extra tabular osf, but the default osf all share the same width, thus they virtually are tabular and do align verti­cally. F
