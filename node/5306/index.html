I have been three times at La Scala,
but only after its rennovation and transferring
to the new Teatro degli Arcimboldi,
http://www.teatroallascala.org/eng/arcimboldi/index.htm
The 3D typography ("Teatro degli Arcimboldi") out of the
building is designed (I think) with Bruce Rogers' Centaur.

> How do you cellebrate there?
A friend from Poland, Dorota, sent me a DVD,
"Czesc Tereska" ("Hi Teresa").
http://www.iofilm.co.uk/fm/h/hi_tereska_2001.shtml
http://www.rottentomatoes.com/vine/showthread.php?t=329449
I have seen it in Polish, with English subtitles,
I have seen it at my sister`s, while my sister
and her family took a walk somewhere on the hills.
I was fussing, that movie it is romantic but hopeless,
it is one of the best film I have ever seen.
I had my birthday cake with all my family,
a cake with strawberries and slices of banana,
it was really good.

Today, in the afternoon, I took the bike and went downtown,
just to buy my favorite tobacco, "Van Nelle."
There, one the way back, I met my good friend Alberto,
and it was really nice to talk with him.
We planned a bike trip (we both ride racing bikes,
but this year, nor me or him had the time to train).

Here is my grazie, set in Ernesto Hemingway,
the typeface family I designed for my MA thesis.
