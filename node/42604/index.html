Roman Figures (MMVIII) via opentype? Any possibility?

February 28, 2008 • Sebastian Nagel

I'm wondering whether and in which extent it is possible to make Roman figures an applicable opentype feature (Stylistic Set). for example:
  • converting "1234" to "MCCXXXIV" (there are javascripts who can do that, but they are rather complex compared to the possibilities of opentype)
  • kind of a ligature-feature, converting MCCXXXIV to "MCCX_X_XI_V" in an "intelligent" way (not creating groups like "MCCX_XX_IV" instead).
  • any other ideas?
Has anybody thought about this already? Or is it just impossible? Sebastian
