2006-08-26 20:18:08 • bowfinpw

Linotext’s name has NOT been changed. We have two typefaces based off of Wedding Text, Mariage and Linotext.

Here is the link to Linotext: http://www.linotype.com/948/linotext-family.html

Linotext is a slightly lighter font than Mariage, which feels like a slightly darker digitization to me. I recommeded Mariage in this case only because its slightly greater dense-ness seems closer to the Syndey Morning Herald’s nameplate than Linotext does.
