You may find it interesting that both the Euro symbol and the Estimated sign were not to be tampered with, but to have precise forms in all use. This was then immediately ignored by type designers in the case of the Euro. Tradition has normally had currency symbols styled to match the faces they are a part of.

> and what typefaces carry this You'll find the [[estimated]] symbol in all Adobe's (alphabetic) fonts. Some of these fonts even carry "alternates" of it, like the small cap variation in Minion Pro, and the alternate form in Garamond Premier Pro.
