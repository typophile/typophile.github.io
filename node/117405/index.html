This is a 19th century font patented by Herman Ihlenburg on October 6, 1891 and sold by MacKellar, Smiths Jordan, and other foundries as _Columbian_. Digital versions, compiled by the _Solopedia_, include P22 Victorian Swash http://www.fonts.com/font/p22/p22-victorian/swash and http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/p22/victorian-gothic/ Glorietta (Solotype), Trinigan FG (Fontgrube), Goodfellow (Scriptorium), Bluegum Forest (Tris Nguyen, corrected by MEG).
