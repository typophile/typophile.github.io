Font fitting for illustrations?

February 14, 2011 • buzzu

Hi everyone,

I’m currently working on an illustrated biography for children. The book is basically pencil drawing collages interwoven with a narrative and various letters (see pictures below for examples) and I don’t really know what font to pic for the text. I was thinking of a slightly geometric serif-font, something angular since that is also how I draw.

Any suggestions would be very welcome, thank you.

As for a fairly natural ductus in your drawing something classical like Caslon might do well. A didone face, on the other hand, may somehow reflect the prominent role of black-and-white contrast in the pictures. Anyway, I would be very careful with trying out sketchy fonts, which may turn out mocking the drawing rather than sensibly complementing it.

Nice drawings, b.t.w.
