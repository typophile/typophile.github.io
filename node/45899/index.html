Outside Vendors and Fonts

May 30, 2008 • lmariop

My company has hired an outside agency to do some work on some ads and signage. Can I provide them the fonts we use for our brand? Should they sign an agreement? Is there usage documentation anyone can provide me concerning this?

Thank you!

All depends on the arrangement you have with the foundry that supplied the branding fonts.

If you acquired them under a standard license, then probably not, as you’d be limited to a set number of workstations on your company premises, and you won’t be allowed to share them with suppliers.
