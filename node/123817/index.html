Hand drawn, with a basis in Roundhand Script.

This particular style—with a tail originating from the final letter that serves as an underline—is sometimes referred to as baseball script, and has been undergoing a resurgence lately.

Some digital typefaces that mimic the look: http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/baselinefonts/licious-script/ http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/wiescherdesign/grocers-script/ http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/nicksfonts/moonshine-script-nf/ http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/fontmesa/draft-beer-classic/ http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/fontmesa/draft-beer/ http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/edy-type/bambola/ http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/fontmesa/brewmaster/
