[[http://briem.net/|Briem’s online tutorial]] is IMHO the best place to start. Letters of Credit by Walter Tracy is extremely helpful, as is Cabaraga’s Learn FontLab fast. I learned a lot from Fonts & Logos by Doyald Young. Karen Cheng’s book is not a must have, though it has helpful comparisons of different fonts. The most important things are to study other fonts you admire in a font drawing program, see how they work on paper and screen, and draw, draw, draw.
