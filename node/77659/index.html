2011-01-02 13:33:26 • bojev

At least he isn’t looking for [[http://new.myfonts.com/search/Papyrus/fonts/|Papyrus]].

I’ve seen [[http://new.myfonts.com/search/Davida/fonts/|Davida]], which appears to be available from a number of foundries, used before where a Mexican-American atmosphere is being suggested.

Something between Blackletter and Davida is a tall order, but perhaps, ironically enough, the face from Letraset called [[http://new.myfonts.com/fonts/letraset/victorian/|Victorian]] might fill the bill.

The querent is searching for information, not aesthetic judgment.

Of course, there’s also [[http://new.myfonts.com/fonts/dtptypes/pretorian-dt/|Pretorian]] and even [[http://new.myfonts.com/search/Hobo/fonts/|Hobo]] ([[http://new.myfonts.com/fonts/profonts/roberta/|Roberta]], which also appears on that results page, might be of interest) that he might consider.

Besides Roberta, for something new, that isn’t overused, there’s [[http://new.myfonts.com/fonts/nicksfonts/durham-abbey-nf/|Durham Abbey]]; while that is pretty clearly Victorian, given the right context it could still work for the querent’s purpose.

Of course, authenticity isn’t a bad thing, either. I’m not sure how much authenticity there is in these suggestions from MyFonts:

[[http://new.myfonts.com/fonts/scriptorium/quetzal/|Quetzal]], [[http://new.myfonts.com/fonts/jonahfonts/yacqui/|Yacqui]], or [[http://new.myfonts.com/fonts/larabie/xolto/|Xolto]]…

Here are a few more ideas:

[[http://new.myfonts.com/fonts/nicksfonts/dime-box-wbw/|Dime Box NF]], [[http://new.myfonts.com/fonts/larabie/kirsty/|Kirsty]], or…

No, neither [[http://new.myfonts.com/fonts/jnlevine/supper-club/|Supper Club JNL]] nor [[http://new.myfonts.com/fonts/paratype/unovis/|Unovis]] is what I’m thinking of, but there might be something similar but less extreme that would do.

Ah, it took a while to find an example; [[http://new.myfonts.com/fonts/spiecegraphics/iron-type-sg/|Iron Type SG]] was what I was thinking of. There was also [[http://new.myfonts.com/fonts/northernblock/mekon/|Mekon]], but while it had the right weight, it didn’t seem to have the right spirit.

Ah, I finally persuaded MyFonts to show me what I was looking for: [[http://new.myfonts.com/fonts/fontbureau/eagle/|Eagle]].

Maybe [[http://new.myfonts.com/fonts/outras/alegria/|Alegria]]?

Also, [[http://new.myfonts.com/fonts/fontbureau/bremen/|Bremen]] might do in a pinch.
