[[Indices]] : [[Designers]] : Norbert Florendo Norbert Florendo (b. 19--) is a [[Graphic Designer|graphic designer]] and [[Typographer|typographer]]. He served as Art Director, Ad Manager, Creative Director, Manager of Multimedia at Compugraphic and [[Agfa]], and [[Typographer in Residence]] during the years between [[Allan Haley]] and [[Cynthia Hollandsworth]]. Contributed to the addition of over 400 new and licensed typefaces to the [[Agfa-Compugraphic]] library. Principally involved with the acquisition and marketing of [[Otl Aicher|Otl Aicher's]] [[Rotis]] Typeface System for Agfa. Past Director-At-Large, [[Type Directors Club]] Past TDC Traveling Exhibit Chairperson Former member of [[ATypI]] Graduate of [[Cooper Union|The Cooper Union]] and a calligraphy student of [[Paul Standard]].
