[[http://font.ubuntu.com/|Ubuntu]] has just gained a monospaced set, too.

No idea if [[http://cm-unicode.sourceforge.net/|CMU Typewriter]] is to your palate.

There’s also [[https://fedorahosted.org/liberation-fonts/|Liberation]] Mono and its big sib, [[http://www.omgubuntu.co.uk/2010/09/download-and-use-the-new-chrome-os-fonts-in-ubuntu/|ChromeOS Cousine]].

Hunting through the fixed-width/typewriter sections on DaFont and FontSquirrel will likely yield groans and grins.

Then there’s [[http://www.f25.cc/|F25 Digital Typeface Design]], which has a few monospaced faces available. Might be too specialised, though.
