A lot of people on here are slightly bitter about Helvetica for some reason they think they are above using it. That’s ironic haha. [[http://www.spiekermann.com/mten/2008/04/helvetica_can_be_nice.html | Its what you can do with the design that will make the difference. ]]

There are a lot of good alternatives (in the link above) and some of them could work quite well. But none of them are Helvetica, and none of them really could replace it (but yes they are alternatives), as you will get a different feel.

Univers & Helvetica are quite different, and you will get a different feel with both of them. Helvetica is warmer than Univers and has more character.

Best Helvetica [[http://www.linotype.com/2048/neuehelveticapro.html | Helvetica Pro]]

The spacing is slightly better than others, as well as on-screen rendering (in Indesign anyway).

Akkurat is a good modern alternative, but is quite different again.
