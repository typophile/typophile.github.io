Things to read about: • Optical trickery • Vertical rythm and the baseline grid • What a display face needs vs. what a text face needs • What paper does to a typeface • What printing does to a typeface • Misregistrations and coloured type • Pairing typefaces & superfamilies • Hierarchy and navigation • Even colour and optical sizes • The grid • Proper use of text figures and lining figures, punctuation and other nitty gritty stuff • White space • The physical restrictions of the magazine format • The mental restrictions of the human brain • Leading and measure • Widows and orphans • Balance and tension etc.
