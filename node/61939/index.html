Hello there, I've just been through a thread here on typophile and saw a post from charles_e. The thread is [[http://typophile.com/node/55212|http://typophile.com/node/55212]]. He got some line counting going on left to his textarea. Anyone got an idea how to activate that feature, or is it self made (not an autoation by ID)? Best, Tim
It's just a text frame on the master page. With a numbered list, all you have to do is hit Return until the entire text frame is filled. Put the frame onto a separate layer, so you can switch it off for a final version. The leading should, of course, be equal to the plain text leading in the real text frame (I think Charles uses Fit to baseline Grid). The side frame will not pick up any leading changes in the accompanying frame.
