body: "<strong>[[Indices]]: [[Designers]]: Jonathan Barnbrook</strong>\r\n\r\n<strong>Jonathan
  Barnbrook</strong> (b. 1966) is a British graphic designer, type designer and typographer.
  He studied graphic design at [[Central Saint Martins College of Art and Design]]
  and the [[Royal College of Art]] from 1985-1992, before starting his career as an
  independent graphic designer. Formerly of [[Emigre]], he now runs his own independent
  foundry [[Virus]] in London. Work includes the Roppongi Hills logo and collaborations
  with British artist Damien Hirst. Emigre fonts include [[Exocet]] and [[Mason]].
