Has your hand been bitten by the Type? Or: Type and Handwriting.

November 18, 2009 • James Deux

I found myself today doing something both nerdy and fun: I made an ffi ligature with my handwriting. Have any of you taken from typography when you write? What types of things do you try to mimic, and has anything crashed and burned terribly?
In design school I spent about six months integrating my hand with the handwriting of sixteenth century writing master Jodocus Hondius. Of course that only made it less legible, but whatever.
