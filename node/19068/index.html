Fontplayer - Photoshop is not a "design" software per se - it's used for images and image manipulation. For design, especially something like a magazine, you should be using Quark or Indesign, not photoshop. You should also familiarize yourself with guides so you don't have to be eyeballing anything. This applies to p-shop and Illustrator as well as Quark and Indy.
