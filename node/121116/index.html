The book being published by Gestalten, I would have expected they used one of their typefaces, or, at least, would have show a colophon for it somewhere. On both fronts, my searches were fruitless. In case it’s a reprint, we could be looking for a non yet digitised typeface. I haven’t found any digital equivalent which shares all the characteristics of the sample. I thought it could be some version of Akzidenz-Grotesk (a typeface Rams himself is known to have used extensively), but while there are lot of variations (especially in letters like |s| and |R|), I cannot find any with an |M| like the one in the sample.
