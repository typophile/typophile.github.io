Thanks, Mark, for correcting my “senior moment.” Hmmmm… Blippo, Pump, ITC Bauhaus, which came first, that is, after the prototype created by Herbert Bayer of the Dessau Bauhaus in 1925?

Looks like Blippo by Fotostar in 1970 (a “Linotype Original”) then ITC Bauhaus by Ed Benguiat and Victor Caruso in 1975 and almost simutaneously Pump (ITC) by Phillip Kelly in late ‘75/early ‘76.

It’s a good thing we weren’t given only the lowercase “a, b” and “d” to ID from :-)
