The birth of something new...

June 8, 2005 • zang

I launched hypeface.com today. Some of you may know me as the creator/operator of Zang-O-Fonts. It's a new site dedicated to, and created for the font community; designers, fans and lurkers alike. It offers news, events, commentary and as a celebration of it's birth, there's a contest to win some fonts from Zang-O-Fonts. In future months we plan on bringing you bigger and better things, as well as commentary from some of the bigger names in the community. This site is meant as a supplement to other community sites like typophile.com and typographica. I love these sites myself, and wouldn't want to encroach on what they offer, but wanted to give back in some way. Go, visit the site. Enjoy it and use it. That's what it's there for.
