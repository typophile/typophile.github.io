Long gone are the days of slow and static filled dial-up service. Today�s options are geared towards high end users who want and need speed and reliability.

DSL services can provide you with both reliable connection and high speeds. Using your traditional telephone lines for communication, it offers upload and download speeds up to 40 Mbps. It is an affordable choice for smaller business without large numbers of employees all cruising the net at the same time. DSL connection does not require the purchase or rental of large amounts of hardware and is the most affordable choice for small businesses.

Cable connections uses regular television cable lines which are often pre-existing in both commercial and residential buildings. Cable has slightly higher download speeds of 50 Mbps+. Since cable combines data transfer and voice calls over the same lines without a drop in productivity it is a great choice for any company that will have numerous employees using the internet and phone lines at the same time.

A fiber Optic connection offers the highest bandwidth with download speeds that can exceed 150 Mbps, even with multiple users using data transfer, voice calls, and video services. Fiber Optic is more expensive than DSL or Cable due to the need for specialized hardware and installation. It makes the most sense for larger companies who require a lot of IT support.
