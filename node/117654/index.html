Jamie Oliver Magazine Serif

July 1, 2014 • olliemills92

![](/images/old-images/Screen Shot 2014-07-01 at 12_4250.32.16.png)

“jamie” appears to be a modified version of Caslon Graphique. All the letterforms are a match except for the lowercase a, which has been modified to be rounder at the top and to have a more teardrop terminal.

“magazine” appears to be Fedra Serif A Book, though I can’t be 100% sure given the small size of the sample image.
