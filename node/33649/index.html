is the regular apostrophe the correct usage in this case? Yes! What you've got in your sample is a single left quote mark, not an apostrophe. An apostrophe is the same as a single right quote mark. Unfortunately, we see this mistake frequently due to the simplemindedness of desktop publishing software, which can't tell the difference between the beginning of a quoted passage and a contraction. So much for "smart" quotes.
