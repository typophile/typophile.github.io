ARTHUR – the new Art Deco fonts released

February 25, 2013 • Andreas Stötzner

.

I hereby take the pleasure to announce the release of my new ARTHUR font series.

It was obviously inspired by the elegance and sophistication of the Art Deco era …

… the classical age of travel and of the dawn of modern globalization.

There are two seperate sets of fonts: [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/andreas-stotzner/arthur-sans/|Arthur Sans]] (in 5 weights) and [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/andreas-stotzner/arthur-cabinet/|Arthur Cabinet.]]

Additionally, a seperate font, [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/andreas-stotzner/arthur-ornaments/|Arthur Ornaments,]] is available:

… offering about 70 glyphs with florals, borderpieces, travel, sports and lifestyle pics.

Thank you all typophiles who favourably [[http://typophile.com/node/98908|commented on the drafts]] during the last two months.

Finally, a glimpse on some preceding sketches. I hope you enjoy it.
