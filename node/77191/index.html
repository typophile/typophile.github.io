Type@Cooper Condensed

December 14, 2010 • cdiedwardo

Type@Cooper has just announced the new summer intensive program 2011. Sumner Stone, Sara Soskolne and Andy Clymer will teach an integrated and rigorous 5 week program from June 20–July 21, 2011 at Cooper Union in Manhattan. Applications will be accepted in February. 15 students will be accepted. For more information visit http://coopertype.org/
