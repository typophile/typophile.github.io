Hi, I am trying to figure out what the difference is, between NEO SANS and NEO SANS PRO?

FORMIK

Hi Hector & welcome to Typophile. According to [[http://www.fontshop.com/education/pdf/typographers_glossary.pdf|The Typographer’s Glossary]] ‘OpenType Pro fonts share the same technical specifications as OpenType Standard (Std, or simply OT) fonts, but support a broader range of languages. Standard OT fonts contain support for Western languages, while Pro fonts include Central European and often Latin Extended, and often Greek and/or Cyrillic and Extended Cyrillic.’
