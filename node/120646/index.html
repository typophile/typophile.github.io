I don’t know that it had a name originally. It was a drawn alphabet by Peter Schnorr - about 1900.

It can be found in the Treasury of Authentic Art Nouveau Alphabets, Decorative Initials, Monograms, Frames and Ornaments (how’s that for a title?) on plate 12A.
