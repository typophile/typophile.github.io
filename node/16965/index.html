Mixing Bodonis

December 26, 2005 • otterhound

I’m designing a business card and want to use a Bodoni. I love the Bauer Bodoni sc&osf, but I don’t like the Bauer Bodoni roman. I do like the Monotype Bodoni roman.

Do you think it would be in bad taste to pair the Monotype Bodoni roman with the Bauer Bodoni osf?

No doubt about it Using so many Bodoni ‘s on one visiting card is always dangerous because the text tend to become heavy & difficult to read. Regards
2005-12-28 15:58:19 • amey
Depending upon the pt size of the type, the serifs on the Bauer Bodoni – and eventually if too small on the Monotype Bodoni – will disappear. You might consider using ITC Bodoni, incorporating the various sizes. This would, I would guess, answer your needs and give you the options without having to mix and match from two different families.
2005-12-28 16:47:41 • Miss Tiffany
Berthold Bodoni is probably your best bet. It has sufficient heft for small text, and includes SC&OSF in the family. Filosofia is another Bodoni revival worth considering.
