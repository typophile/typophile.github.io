I think a way of better guessing if its a font versus handlettering is to first look at connected letters as possible ligatures. If the connections are smooth, as in the sample, it could still be ligatures or handlettering. When you see unconnected letters along side these smooth ligatures that seem slightly more awkward as in the mp, that’s a clue that makes me think its likely a font. Even having many random versions of alternates will have a tendency to look like a font if there isn’t some sense that the text flows like a person might write.
