The German expressionist font used in your poster is called Habsburg. It was designed by Heinrich (Heinz) Keune at J.G. Schelter & Giesecke, Leipzig, in 1903. He also designed a narrower version called Wittelsbach. Here are two fonts as shown in a S&G Schriften und Initialen type specimen book from the era [img:sites/default/files/old-images/HabsburgSM_6529.jpg][img:sites/default/files/old-images/WittelsbachSM_6650.jpg] Adapted from photos by Ben Mitchell on Flickr https://www.flickr.com/photos/ohbendy/13911086473/in/photostream/ AFAIK neither font has been digitized. However, as previously suggested, Moritz is almost identical and would work as a digital substitute. Don
