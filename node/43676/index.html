I can't quite figure out what this is. It's from the 2006 David R. Godine reissue of the classic children's book, How Tom Beat Captain Najork and His Hired Sportsmen—altogether a lovely production. I am restraining myself from including scans of the art by Quentin Blake. Anyway, thanks! Peter [img:sites/default/files/old-images/1_3644.jpg] [img:sites/default/files/old-images/2_5901.jpg]
Oh, wait, it's Miller! Sorry for the excitement. If this font were available in OpenType, and I had a copy of my own, I might not keep admiring it in different contexts and wondering what it is! It's so unmistakably a Matthew Carter font, with those even, rolling italics (a lot like Yale's) and pseudo-old-style figures that are basically more beautiful than the real thing. Anyway, sorry to blank out, there!
