EDITED: Ben Kocinski seems to have used more letters in the same style as _WHISKEY_ in his rebrand of the Loyal Stricklin Aviator Mug [img:sites/default/files/old-images/LoyalStricklinAviatorMugByBenKocinski_6051.png] If you want to complete the upper case alphabet you could draw on the design details of the tiny serif fonts mentioned by Ryuk that have some similarity to _WHISKEY_. There are quite a few more but none is a close match I note that _WHISKEY_ also has some flare-serif features -- notably in the cross-bar of _E_. Compare to Albertus [img:sites/default/files/old-images/AlbertusAsWHISKEY_4631.gif] [img:sites/default/files/old-images/AlbertusL_5037.gif]http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/mti/albertus/ For more ideas it might be worth going through the collections at places like Creative Market that focus on hand drawn fonts. Ben Kocinski's script _Flask_ looks very similar to some of the hand drawn fonts at Creative Market Don
