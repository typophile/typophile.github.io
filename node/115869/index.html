I saw this font in a 2014 wedding invitation. I was told the name the font is CRM, but this font doesn’t seem from the CRM family at all. Image search for CRM give the expected CRM-related results. [[http://www.mygatsby.com/scene7/image/fontstyle/id/1658]]

Most of their fonts are commercial fonts and they did not change the names. I was going to suggest [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/typadelic/sunnydale/?refby=bowfin|Sunnydale]] as a similar font, but I see that is also one of their other fonts at MyGatsby.com.

I could not find this at any freebie sites, which I looked at because the font seemed amateurish to me. It’s possible it is something they made themselves, as that kind of font can be made very cheaply.
