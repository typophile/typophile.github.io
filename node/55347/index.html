My favorite designer is Erik Spiekermann. He is a German typographer who created fonts for Nokia, Lexus, and Audi. He created FontShop and created Meta which is one of my favorite fonts. Erik has a [[http://www.spiekermann.com/en|blog]] and a [[http://www.fontshop.com/fonts/designer/erik_spiekermann/|FontShop]] page.

He’s the real deal. Have you seen him in the Helvetica movie? He saved it.
