So yes, I am a Harry Potter nerd. I have read them all at least twice, but I digress-That is not the point of this entry. Anyway, I thought that the cover designers really did a great job with this last book. This pictures show both the American book cover (above) and the English book cover (below). I think the American one really fits the book better just because it looks more unrealistic. I like the way they've echoed Harry's lightning bolt scar in the font too. I think the colors work really well together and really catch someone's eye as they walk by. The English one just doesn't remind me of Harry Potter. I guess it just looks more serious or something to me...I mean, it's well designed, I'm just not sure it fits the book in question. What do you think?
