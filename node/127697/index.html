June 3, 2018 • Textwrapper

Browsing early printed material on Ebay, I found this colophon. Does anyone know what the ligature (looks like a “q” and a “p”) on the last line might be?

You’ve already identified it—it’s a ligature of qp (though the ligature for cp looks very similar): https://bookmongre.wordpress.com/tag/ligatures/

My Latin is rusty, but I believe it stands for quantum placet (meaning “as much as you please” or “to the extent that”, depending on the context)
