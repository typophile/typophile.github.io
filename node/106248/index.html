Released in 1948 in the era of great show-card lettering. Compare your first image with this style sample for lettering artists: There are quite a few digitized brush script fonts but none is a perfect match. The closest I was able to find to the tone and general feel of your first image is Signalist by Mike Melvas: http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/mika-melvas/signalist/ There are also two fairly close fonts from Filmotype: Filmotype Lakeside: http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/filmotype/lakeside/ and Filmotype Lasalle: http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/filmotype/lasalle/ Lasalle is also available from Scangraphic and Elsner+Flake. BTW the lettering artists for Unfaithfully Yours were busy. They also produced a third version of the movie title: Don
