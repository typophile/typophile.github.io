Reminds me of F. H. Ernst Schneidler's "Schneidler-Mediaeval mit Initialen" aka Schneidler Initials released in 1936. But manually widened and with some editing cvhanges: clipping the tip on the bottom rhs of _N_, replacing the leg on _R_ and widening the bowl, clipping the stem on the bottom rhs of _U_. Compare the image to the Schneidler Initials digitized by Jason Castle as Shàngó [img:sites/default/files/old-images/ShangoAsCONSTRUCTIONS_3860.gif] https://www.myfonts.com/fonts/castletype/shango/ or as Shàngó Gothic [img:sites/default/files/old-images/ShangoGothicAsCONSTRUCTIONS_4611.gif]Gothic https://www.myfonts.com/fonts/castletype/shango-gothic/ Don
