It's not clear if you're specifically looking for vintage-style pointing fists, or if you're referring to the typographic fist itself as a vintage relic. [[http://www.fontshop.com/fonts/downloads/fontfont/ff_scala_hands/|FF Scala Hands]] is a great collection of variations. David Jonathan Ross included some pointing fists in his recently released [[http://www.fontbureau.com/fonts/Trilby/charset|Trilby]] from Font Bureau. Jonathan Hoefler's Fell Type Roman, from the [[http://www.typography.com/fonts/font_inside.php?wipID=16&productLineID=100012|Historical Allsorts]] set, has a couple vintage pointing fists (but I don't see any way of inspecting them via their website).
