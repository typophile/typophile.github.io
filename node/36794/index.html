Hi T Bones, yeah, the Typophile search is kinda crappy. Reading this thread will surely help.

So, make yourself a bookmark with the following as the address: javascript:void(q=prompt(‘Search%20Typophile:’,''));if(q)void(location.href=‘http://www.google.com/search?q=site:typophile.com'+'%20'+escape(q)) [Kudos to Grant Hutchinson]

And in case you don’t like Google, feel free to replace its url with that of a competitor …
