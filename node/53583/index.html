Letterpress Shops In US Using Interactive Websites?

January 16, 2009 • Bleisetzer

Hello, a german typographist visited me in my magazine: www.bleisetzer.de She told me about “Letterpress Shops” in San Francisco where she is working. She said there are around a dozend of these shops only in SF.

What I understood is: All these shops seem to be in a shopping marl. The people join the shop, and they produce a e.g. greeting card together with the typographist live on the screen. Of course they hold some dozends of standards on their Mac. But its possible to change logos and, of course, an individual text.

My question is: Does someone know any of these letterpress shops in US or wherever, using a website to choose the artwork (out of a database of standards)?

Would be a great help…

Regards, Georg

Custom, handmade, one-of-a-kind…it’s kinda the whole point of using letterpress.
