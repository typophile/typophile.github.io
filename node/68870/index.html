I’m designing a logo for a co. that is associated with newer commercial jet airplanes and am looking for fonts that communicate technical and mechanical innovation (but not NASA looking).

Suggestions welcome.

If you want a modern looking Sans without being ‘techno’ looking, how about [[http://www.fontshop.com/fontlist/super_families/fp_dancer/?&sample_text=Whisperjet%20WHISPERJET&update=0|FP Dancer]], or [[http://www.fontshop.com/fontlist/super_families/sansa/?&sample_text=Whisperjet%20WHISPERJET&update=0|Sansa]]? Admittedly, this is a random sampling, but I think these look fresh without being the clichéd ‘techno’ look we see too much of. Maybe some added slanting would be OK too with these.
