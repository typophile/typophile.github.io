  calligraphy, typefounding, typography, papermaking, bookbinding, illustration, and
  publishing. APHA is especially, but by no means exclusively, interested in American
  printing history.\r\n\r\nAPHA publishes an journal called [[Printing History]] which
  is only available to members.\r\n\r\nhttp://www.printinghistory.org/"
