Interpolate Node

August 29, 2013 • Jeremy Dooley

Recently, I have had the misfortune of using Illustrator for a large project. I am really dependent on Interpolate node in fontlab, and I am wondering, does such a thing exist in Illustrator CC either natively or as a plugin? Jdooley
I've played around with it, and will say you can use Object > Blend to achieve interpolation. You can also specify the number of steps you want so you will end up being able to choose which one you like the best. The downside? Unless you are super-attentive to making sure your two masters are node compatible, it will probably only work one character at a time without making a huge mess of things. I never tried it with multiple characters. It just occurred to me that I should have tried it with three in-line masters; that might be interesting.
