2014-12-05 19:26:15 • Special-K

It looks pretty, but I think you might need to compromise on that a little to make it easier to read. I think it would get tiring on the eyes to read a whole story in it. I would make the gaps smaller, perhaps even just touching, almost imperceptibly so that they still look like gaps. I would also make the x-height a bit bigger compared with the cap/ascender height.

The stroke thicknesses look a bit inconsistent to me, although I’m not sure if that’s just a rasterising effect in the bitmap: the fact that two lower-case l’s look different make me think that it’s that. Although the serifs do need to be more consistent… compare the top of the l with the top of the b. The exclamation point and question mark are more stylised than the letters, which may have been deliberate and look good in context, but to me, in this context, it looks a little jarring.

Don’t get me wrong, I think it’s a really nice font. And with a few more tweaks, I think it will be more than just nice.
