Removal of Banksy artwork

October 29, 2007 • Nick Cooke

I read today that Hackney council are going to remove all of Banksy’s Graffiti in the borough. Considering that his ‘work’ goes for up to a million nowadays, (the price that Brangelina supposedly paid for one of his pieces), it would be a shame. The council should be encouraging people to go on a ‘Banksy trail’ walking tour, not obliterating these works of art. It adds interest to an otherwise not very interesting area.
