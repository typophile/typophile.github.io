As long as we're "out there" . . . Typography and booze remind me of [[http://typophile.com/node/62789|this thread]], and of the following anecdote I posted therein: Matthew Carter tells the story of a time he was flying from the UK to the US and stopped in the duty free shop at Heathrow. He spied a bottle of Scotch whose label was printed using one of his types, so he bought it. As he exited the shop, a woman holding a clipboard asked him to take a market-research survey. When she asked him why he chose that particular brand of Scotch, he answered, "Because I designed the type on the label," and watched as the researcher puzzled over her questionnaire for a check box that corresponded to that answer.
