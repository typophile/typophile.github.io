J.Crew offers some nice copy in their advertisements and overall style. Does anybody knows which font the have used for the Liquor Store? Thanks in advance!
Don’t think “LIQUOR STORE” is a commercial font, looks “handdrawn” to me. Letters looks like to come from different typefaces. I guess they have retraced it from the former signboard, as it could be seen on [[http://www.jcrew.com/AST/Navigation/tribeca/ls1.jsp#/history|pictures]]. Some loosely similars for “LIQUOR”: [[http://new.myfonts.com/fonts/typeotones/wilma/|Wilma]], [[http://www.johannesbreyer.com/projects/font-heureka/|Heureka]], [[http://www.carvalho-bernau.com/jlg/|Jean-Luc Godard]], [[http://new.myfonts.com/fonts/fontbureau/titling-gothic-fb/cond-medium/|Titling Gothic Condensed]], [[http://new.myfonts.com/fonts/hubertjocham/motora-sans/|Motora Sans]] (not condensed), [[http://new.myfonts.com/fonts/t26/antitled/|Antitled]], [[http://new.myfonts.com/fonts/fontbureau/relay/|Relay]], [[http://new.myfonts.com/fonts/device/korolev/|Korolev]], [[http://www.smeltery.net/fonts/audimat|Audimat]], [[http://www.fontshop.com/fonts/downloads/bold_monday/panno_sign_ot/|Panno Sign]], [[http://new.myfonts.com/fonts/fontbureau/stainless/|Stainless (/R excepted]], [[http://www.houseind.com/fonts/neutraface|Neutraface Condensed]] (mainly for the /R), [[http://new.myfonts.com/fonts/redrooster/megaphone/|Megaphone]], [[http://new.myfonts.com/fonts/marksimonson/mostra-nuova/|Mostra Nuova]], [[http://new.myfonts.com/fonts/t26/mechanic-gothic/|Mechanic Gothic]]… For “STORE”? /S looks like upside down, /TORE could be easily reproduce with some [[http://new.myfonts.com/search/engravers+gothic/fonts/|Engravers Gothic]], [[http://new.myfonts.com/fonts/marksimonson/proxima-nova/|Proxima Nova]] or [[http://www.typography.com/fonts/font_overview.php?productLineID=100008|Gotham]]
