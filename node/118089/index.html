The H is handlettered. It is a Tuscan style letter with “mustache terminals.” Among fonts in this category are Hessian http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/madtype/hessian/ and Lynchburg http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/fontmesa/lynchburg/ You will have to add the special “demi-mustache” crossbar. Suggestion: take an I clip off half the mustache and rotate 90 degrees and use it as the basis for you crossbar. Do some more editing and you are there. Don
