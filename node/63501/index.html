That is not Helvetica. In Helvetica, the terminals on the C are horizontal lines whereas in Arial, they are slightly slanted. In addition the lowercase “a” has a rounded intersection between the lower, circular part of the a and the vertical line on the right side of the a. That might have been a bad description, but I know that what is posted at the top of this post is absolutely 100% NOT Helvetica. Also Marvelous Madness posted the word Arial in Helvetica. Look at the two if you want to see comparisons and you will see that the word Helvetica at the top of this post is Arial.
