Feature comparison of font tools

January 25, 2008 • cuttlefish

I am wondering of anyone has done a truly comprehensive feature comparison of various font editing and manipulation software from all available sources. [[http://www.fontlab.com/bc/compare/|The one on FontLab’s site]] basically only serves to show how other FontLab products are crippled compared to FontLab Studio. I’m sure they don’t mean it that way, but one can interpret it as driving an upsell to the higher end product rather than demonstrating how the lower priced programs are adequate for most needs. Of course, there is no mention of competing products, and the Third Party Font Software page on the FontLab site is horribly incomplete and outdated. Not even VOLT or AFDKO are listed.

Try http://www.font.org/software/
