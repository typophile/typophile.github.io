2004-09-05 12:35:51 • mikeb7
Be careful about trying to do too many things here. You've got a swash R, a pink flower, an unusual E, and two unusual S's. They all want your attention. Focus on one or two methinks. Swash letters are really hard to draw on a computer. Too often, the swashes just look unnatural. Your R is suffering that a bit plus it's creating a spacing problem for you. Print it out large and work over the shape with a pencil to get better flow. Maybe try to use the swash to tie the Princess to the word Flowers which is floating now. The C + - = E is an ok idea, but note that the cross bar should be thin for proper modulation. Like mike, the SS bugs. It's too Klingon for a princess IMHO. I like the flower in 2B because it reminds me of a Tiara. Maybe try pushing that connection a bit (but lean toward more floral and less representational tiara). Have fun! R
