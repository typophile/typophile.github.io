> 1.Does anyone regularly buy fonts?

I have a nasty font licensing habit that I'm doing my best to curb right now.

> 2.How does anyone feel about the font prices?

The foundries from which I have licensed have fair pricing: Emigre, Font Bureau, FontShop, Hoefler&Frere-Jones, Underware, House Industries .. all reasonable for the quality and customer service offered. I also use MyFonts which has again, I think, fair pricing for those foundries whom they represent.

> 3.How many times did you need accent characters/special and they where > not there? or made due

I've become spoiled by full character sets. I really think you limit yourself by not including as many as you can.

> 4.How interested are you in non traditional but nice looking font > design? ie the font "enabler"

I'm not sure I follow this question.

> 5.Anyone have experience selling fonts through a font house or sell > them yourself? if so how was it

I don't, but I think it will come down to how much time do you have to do your own marketing?
