Looking for fresh handwritten fonts like the ones in Domino mag

June 12, 2007 • knitaby

I know there are tons of threads about handwritten fonts and I’ve looked at them.

So far the handwritten fonts I like are:

Felt Tip Roman FF Hands MVB Calliope Marydale Professor

These feel like Fresh, young contemporary handwritten fonts. Like the ones Domino mag uses! (Do you know what handwritten fonts they use?)

What I’m not looking for:

FF Providence is too childlike. Brisa is good, but not sophisticated enough. (I feel that way about FF Hands too slightly) Handsome Pro is too cute. Voluta Script Pro is way too scripty and not natural, fresh, or real looking. P22 Pop art comic is cool, but not what I’m looking for.

Any other fresh, young, contemporary handwritten fonts out there? I have already looked at the “Contemporary Handwriting Fonts” newsletter from that one font place (as you can tell by my list).

Check out Mike Y’s Script ID Guide, especially section 11.
