[[Indices]] : [[Foundries]] : Carter & Cone Type, Inc. Carter & Cone Type Inc., formed in 1991, combines the talents of [[Matthew Carter]] and [[Cherie Cone]] to produce and market the designs of Matthew Carter. They license his typefaces to end users, and do custom work for corporations, newspapers, magazines, and university presses. Carter & Cone have produced types on commission for Apple, Microsoft (the screen fonts [[Verdana]] and [[Georgia]]), Time, Newsweek, Wired, U.S. News & World Report, Sports Illustrated, The Washington Post, and the Walker Art Center. www.carterandcone.com
