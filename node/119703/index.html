This may have been custom-created for Vogue by one of lettering artists of the time (Wes Wilson, Victor Moscoso, Rick Griffin). I’m not finding a digital match. And the style is actually based in Art Nouveau/Secessionist-era hand-drawn type, of the kind created by Alfred Roller.
