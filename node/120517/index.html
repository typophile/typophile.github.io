Definitely lettering. Kevin's suggested substitutes should work fine. As for Jean Luc Godard's movies themselves, they also used lettering rather than fonts as such. Kai Bernau from Atelier Carvalho Bernau created a JeanLuc Typeface in hommage to Jean Luc Godard based on the lettering in some of his movies: http://www.carvalho-bernau.com/jlg/ The web page includes an interesting discussion of the lettering used in the movies. It might also give you some insight into the lettering used in this poster for Godard's sixth movie, Le mépris of 1963, released in English as contempt http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Contempt_%28film%29 Don
