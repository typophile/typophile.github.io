There aren’t many Brazilian text (serif) typefaces; I don’t think there’s any with the characteristics you’re looking for (“taut and beziery, with M-formula-type curves”).

Yomar Augusto’s Den Dekker and Eduardo Berliner’s Pollen [PDF] are two nice typefaces with a strong calligraphic influence on letterforms.

Rodolfo Capeto’s Houaiss is a custom and exclusive typeface for the Houaiss dictionary.
