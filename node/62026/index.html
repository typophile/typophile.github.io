NOAA Biennial Conference Logo

September 16, 2009 • CorpusMilti

My graphic design class had a representative from NOAA come in and she asked us to create a logo for their Conference in the Tampa Bay area. She was looking for a logo that represented “old Florida” and it needed to have a dolphin. I created the typeface to add to the Art Deco theme. This is what I submitted— I would like to maybe keep workin on it. I’m still unsure if I should have made an ‘O’ based off the ‘A’. What about the placement of the dolphin? Any comments would be appreciated.

It’s hard to be sure at that size, but it looks like the apostrophe is backwards in ‘11. As it will probably be in this post of mine unless the smart-quotes routine is very clever. :)

With that much text, I don’t think the generous letterspacing works so well.

Other than that, it’s not bad.
