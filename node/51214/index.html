What you’re asking about are called “leader dots.” The setting is in the Tabs palette: right next to the field that shows the tab position, you’ll find a field for “Leader:”. Fill that with a period, or period space, or centered period, or whatever else you would want to have repeated as a tab leader.

– K.

A possible problem with dot leaders is that these sometimes don’t look very nice when too close to the glyph before them. I recently saw an alternative way of constructing them: Make a characterstyle with a dotted underline that shifts up. Apply that to the space.
