2015-01-12 16:54:09 • donshottype
Duoline is one of several digital ripoffs of the photolettering face, Skin and Bones - which, with its original alternates, would seem to be the closest match (dollar sign modified for s?). It makes sense that this would have been the inspiration, as it was created by Douglas F. Jones in the 1970’s. I can’t give an exact date, though, so it’s possible Skin and Bones postdates Mr. DeSpigna’s design.
