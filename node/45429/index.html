Maybe they can help you by refining their definition of ‘mature’, or better yet, maybe they can try and convey a period in time that would define them, so for example if they were a Ragtime band ‘Art Nouveau’ influenced fonts would be the right direction. It might sound like they are being pigeonholed, but its worth a try as a starting point. The (possibly) easier way is to ask them what types of fonts from other bands they like, so it gives you some idea. I know it’s always hard to figure out what someone wants simply based on a single word when it turns out you sometimes find that word they used bore no relation to what they end up being happy with.
