according to the OED Leam has several meanings:

Leam n.1 Light, flame; a flash, ray, or gleam of light; brightness, gleam. Also fig.

Leam n.2 A drain or watercourse in fen districts.

Leam n.3 The husk of a nut.

Leam v.1 intr. To shine, gleam; to light up.

Leam v.2 a. trans. To free nuts from their husks b. intr. Of nuts: To separate easily from the husk.

There is also: Leamer1 “One that flashes or radiates light.” Leamer2 “A nut fully ripe. Chiefly in brown leamer, a nut with a brown husk.”

and so on and so forth.
