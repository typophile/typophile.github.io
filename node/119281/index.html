Eau Cologne and Saigon-Hanoi are not the same design. Note for example the different N. For a striped font with the N in Eau Cologne, you can use Archive Copperplate Head [img:sites/default/files/old-images/ArchiveCopperplateHeadInUse_5607.gif] http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/archivetype/archive-copperplate-head/ which is an accurate, but indifferent quality, digital rendition of Hermann Ihlenburg’s Copperplate from the 19th century. Change the convex inner curve on the terminal serifs for E, L, C, and G to a concave inner curve by blanking out the unwanted part, and you have a fair match to Eau Cologne . Don
