The mystery has been solved after an email to the company. They sent my message to the designer who was kind enough to send the following feedback.

———- snip ———— We are very happy to learn that you like the typeface of our client’s logo. Unfortunately the typeface which you are interested in is currently not available, as it is a corporate typeface created for the branding of Temsamani Couture. But the typeface might get released through http://www.ydt-fonts.com in the near future.

All the best, Yvonne Diedrich ———- snip ————

After some research I discovered Yvonne also designed:

– Eplica (Published by Letraset in 2001. It was among the winners of the (2001 Type Directors Club Type Design Competition)

– ITC Dyadis (Published by ITC and Linotype in 1998)
