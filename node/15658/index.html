Looking for a Geometric Sans

October 15, 2005 • whoisdan

I’m starting an identity for a software design company and am looking for a nice geometric sans, maybe something along the lines of Futura or Gotham, just not their current Century Gothic.

Things to note: Circular lc “o”, shouldn’t be techie-looking, should be totally cool. I’m stuck.

Any ideas?

I’ve highlighted a few in Newstoday’s QBN Typecase, including Bryant and Relay. Also underused but lovely is FF Super Grotesk, a newer take on Futura. And I’ve always loved Nobel, both Font Bureau’s version and DTL’s.
