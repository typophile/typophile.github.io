Hi,

I am making a trip to South India in November and was wondering if there are any interesting places or people to try to get in touch with to share type experiences or meet.

Everything type related is very interesting – sign painters, movie poster designers, type designers, calligraphers, schools etc.

I am a swedish type designer and type developer. You can find more information/contact info on my website www.autodidakt.se or contact me through Typophile if you are interested in meeting up in India.

Thanks! Göran

Hi Goran, I was in India this May, but in the north. I went to collect newspapers. I loved walking through parks and busy areas of cities. In their own ways, each had a panoply of lettering to look at. Most fascinating is how scripts are mixed everywhere. I assume that everything “official” in the south is written in at least three languages: the local language, Hindi, and English… even though many southerners may not be able to read/speak the northern Hindi… together with English, it is an “official” language. The southern writing systems are quite beautiful. Take lots of photos!
