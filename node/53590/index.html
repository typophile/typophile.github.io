Tokyo Weekender Cover..

January 17, 2009 • Graphik-Movements

Hi, I’m currently working the latest cover for the Tokyo Weekender Magazine and even though this is still a work in progress, I just wanted to see what some of you think of it so far …. The Feature will be about the restoring of Tokyo Station.

P.S. the Tools spell out Tokyo Eki (Station) in Kanji .

Good start. Things I would work on: •How about some texture instead of just stark white? Or will this be printed on a nice Japanese stock? • Remove the shadows and put in one consistent shadow when you finish the photo-comp. • I don’t think that the dot around “Saving Tokyo Station” is the best way to separate the text from the image. If you really want to use it find a better way to play the two shapes against each other.
