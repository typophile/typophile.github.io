Nick, German spelling has for a long time tended to be more regular and phonetically reliable than English spelling, in part because it has not inherited the same complexity of etymological sources. In German ‘Gutenberg’ and ‘Gutenburg’ are not only spelled differently but pronounced differently. They are different names: as different as Goodhill and Goodcastle.
