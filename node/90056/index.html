I contacted the nice folks at [[http://www.woodproductsigns.com/|Wood Product Signs]] because they had this photo posted of a newer sign they made with that older type: They told me that it was routered out using CAD software, and that the “font” (CAD letter outlines) they use is called “Old Time Round Gothic.” That’s not really a font, but maybe it can help you in your search?

None of these are exact matches, but have have a similar retro feel (to me):

  • [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/szardesign/zar-brush-gothic/|Zar Brush Gothic]]
  • [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/comicraft/dash-decent/|Dash Decent]]
  • [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/parkinson/wigwag/|Wigwag]]
  • [[http://www.myfonts.com/search/magnesium/fonts/|Magnesium]]
  • [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/dtptypes/delargo-dt-rounded/|Delargo Rounded]]

Search terms like “monoline,” “rounded,” “speedball,” “showcard,” retro may get you closer…
