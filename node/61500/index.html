> Its a Journal, rigth? I mean is public domain, no?

No, not really. Being public is different than being in the public domain. The article would still be under copyright and should not be reproduced in toto publicly (digitally or otherwise) without the author’s permission.

Looks like many back issues of the Printing History journal, including the one you seek, are still in print and available for purchase directly from the APHA. Info here:

http://www.printinghistory.org/publications/printing-history/back-issues.php

Doesn’t look like an order form is available yet, however, so you may have to contact someone directly.
