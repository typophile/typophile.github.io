Museo at Sonsbeek 2008

July 3, 2008 • Jos Buivenga

At the [[http://www.sonsbeek2008.nl/read/en/home|10th Sonsbeek International Sculpture Exhibition]] (sonsbeek 2008: grandeur) [[http://www.sonsbeek2008.nl/read/en/exhibition/artists/tavares|Ana Maria Tavares]] used Museo for her work “Five Secrets of the Waters (For Mnemosyne)”. There are five stones in Sonsbeek, each at a junction of an underground stream.

Museo is also used for a little booklet/folder.
