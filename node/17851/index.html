Sans Serif Search

February 10, 2006 • stuttle

I have a client that claims to have seen a typeface in which the Caps and Lowercase were interchangeable. Meaning the overall character height is the same. Of course changing type size is an option but she recalls the weight of the letterforms was the same. ie. an uppercase T was equal in size and weight to the lowercase e.

Does anyone know of any examples of such a face?

This is generally known as unicase:

Emigre has Filosofia Grand Unicase and Cholla Sans Unicase Shinntype has Panoptica

There are many many more.
