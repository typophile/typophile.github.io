I don’t need Lucky Luke, just the credits. These brush capitals are very similar to the Sin City logo, originally designed by Frank Miller’s brother. This could be a fan font, but I hope it’s a quality design.

The brush is Sin City based on the comic book logo (I’d recognize that ‘S’ anywhere).
