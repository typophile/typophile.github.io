Remember that economy isn’t just about width. It also involves less-obvious aspects like counters, aperture, and x-height. Caecilia can be set smaller than other faces. It’s still quite readable at 7-9 pt.

how does thesis serif compare to ceacilia? it looks pretty similar to me.

Both are excellent. The extra variations in TheSerif are helpful, but I prefer Caecilia’s larger counters and more upright italic.
