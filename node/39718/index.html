Hi,

As a project for my BA degree in graphic design we have been set to take on one of this years d&ad briefs. The one I have chosen is set by Research Studios and is to relauch Fuse Magazine and its 20th issue.

The brief Re-launch FUSE magazine by designing its 20th issue. Mandatory requirements FUSE was seen as a revolution in both typography and the format it chose for publication. This re-launch should further this FUSE heritage. Throughout your concept and design you must feature a new, original font created specially for this issue. Your final presentation must include: • Format and examples of layout • Complete font display • Promotional poster for the re-launch • Anything else you think is relevant

It can be read in full here: http://www.dandad.org/studentawards08/briefs/pdf/GRAPHIC_RESEARCHSTUDIOS.pdf

Research for this project has been fairly hard as I have not yet been able to even view a copy of Fuse Magazine.

Does anyone know where I can get hold of on? I am located in the United Kingdom. If not, any additional information would be appreciated. Or if someone has a copy at home a few scans would be great.
