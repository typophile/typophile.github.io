A font similar to Giorgio

November 6, 2008 • akira1975

A Japanese designer has released a font, called ‘[[http://www.flopdesign.com/font3/readymade.html|Readymade]]’. It is similar to [[http://www.christianschwartz.com/giorgio.shtml|Giorgio]] by [[Christian Schwartz]]. [img:sites/default/files/old-images/Readymade_4110.jpg] Of course, the x-height of Readymade is shorter than the one of Giorgio, and the characters are not the very same as characters of Giorgio. But Readymade seems to be inspired by Giorgio and Corvinus. The designer is selling Readymade for commercial use, but it is downloadable freely only in this November for personal use.
Maybe that's why it's called Readymade? The similarities are definitely too uncanny to be overlooked though.

It is funny to see these type published nowadays. The first type I ever designed (with FontStudio 1.0!!!) was a copy (read "inspired") of a typeface that the magazine Premiere was using for their titles around 1991-92. I rely liked that typeface, and i wanted to use it, but I had no idea how to get it, so I decided to make my own version of that font as an exercise to learn how to design a typeface. [img:sites/default/files/old-images/criniera_5921.gif] For everybody amusement you can see the results. Don't laugh too hard please. Only many years later I learned that the original type was called Corvinus Versailles and it was designed by Dennis Ortiz Lopez. My congratulations to both Christian and Flop Design for their more professional work.
