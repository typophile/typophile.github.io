That’s understandable, because many techno fonts use an A like your sample, but I would not be surprised if many of them are just flipped V’s. The reason I am not fond of “Techno” as a style is that there are so many fonts that look like one another. If most designers think ‘techno’ means reducing letterforms to simple lines and curves, it stands to reason that many of the alphabets they produce will be mind-numbingly similar, and they are.

Considering the number of fonts that could be called Techno, I think the percentage that show any real design artistry is quite small. Just my opinion.
