2007-07-14 16:46:42 • fontplayer
  • PTL Skopex Gothic by Andrea Tinnes (5 weights & italics and small caps each — ‘contemporary, vertical’)
  • FF Karbid by Verena Gerlach (3 weights & Display cuts, small caps etc. — ‘Berlinesque, readable’)
  • Linotype Authentic Sans by Karin Huschka (4 weights & italics each — ‘post-modern’)
  • Ambigue by Carla Schweyer (7 weights — ‘low x-height’)
  • Ronnia by Veronika Burian (5 weights & italics each — ‘humanist’)
I’ll leave it to you to judge whether they’re chunky or not …
