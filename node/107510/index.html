I suspect the sign is lettering not a font as such. But I would be happy to learn of a matching font. The lettering looks like something like John Hancock – Keystone Type Foundry, circa 1903, and condensed version by Lanston Monotype, circa 1917, digitized by Claude Pelletier http://www.dafont.com/john-hancock-cp.font – meets early 20th century lettering for the _ g_. Here is one variation by Speedball with a bottom heavy g. What is the relationship between the sign and Harper’s Weekly? Another bottom heavy g, from a 1921 Motor Tours book: Don
