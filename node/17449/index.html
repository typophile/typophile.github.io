Is "Weserve" what you were going for? I would tend to want to take this even closer to a pixel grid, and make all the counters the same optical width as the strokes. I might try to bring more diagonals in too, since the one in the V seems a little lonely. That said I think what you've got already functions admirably, and if the client likes it that's a strong argument to leave everything the way it is!
