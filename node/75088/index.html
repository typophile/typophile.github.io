2010-10-11 16:15:18 • Jackson
The Intellecta foundry has produced some revivals of traditional "line-drawing illustrations of letters made from pieces of wood" typefaces. I think that people expect "Real Wood" fonts to be similarly naturalistic or representational. But your typeface, to its credit, is an original concept and a new stylization. You do it a disservice to describe it in the same manner as Intellecta's unimaginative and unoriginal history-mining. I would say "Wood Never Looked Like This Before!" or possibly, if you're feeling on top of the world, "Wood Never Looked So Good!"
