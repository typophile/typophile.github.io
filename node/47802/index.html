Patagonica rearranged

July 28, 2008 • Marcelo Soler

Time ago I posted the [[http://typophile.com/node/47627|newborn font Patagonica]] for critizising. Now I go again, after some basis changes on the original design, with a new set of characters slightly different at the point I solved to rename it Patagonica 2.0 for such reasons. [[http://marcelosoler.com/mcs/patagonia2.php | Here]] you can find [[http://marcelosoler.com/mcs/patagonia2.php|a sample PDF]] for Patagonica text (the font is, as supposed, embedded in the file). It is inteded to be a body text and captions family (I'm working on bold/italic/light weights and alternate styles), highly readable and Spanish readers oriented —it includes special treatment for Spaniard kerning and special chars like ñ, á, é, í, ó, ú...—, thought it must have a good behavior in other languages. Here it goes. Thank you for your contribution.
