  body: "\"Regular Italic\" sounds confusing. The word \"Regular\" is normally omitted
    on the Regular weights. I'd name this way:\r\n\r\nMyTypeface\r\n-- Light\r\n--
    Light Italic\r\n-- Light Inline\r\n-- Light Italic Inline\r\n+\r\n-- Regular\r\n--
    Italic\r\n-- Inline\r\n-- Inline Italic\r\n+\r\n-- Bold\r\n-- Bold Italic\r\n--
    Bold Inline\r\n-- Bold Italic Inline"
