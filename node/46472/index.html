This time I tried “legible sans” at MyFonts using ‘stage flaky’ as the sample text. It gave me 752 sans families to look at (Note to Stewf: Once again I plead with you to have FontShop keep the sample text the same as each page of search results is displayed. PLEASE!).

I didn’t find a match (I am convinced this must be from a small, obscure font source), but these were the most similar in terms of the specific key letter attributes and/or general appearance:

[[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/brassfonts/solo-sans/|Solo Sans]];

[[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/agfa/generica-condensed/|Generica Condensed]];

[[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/bitstream/antagometrica/|Antagometrica]];

[[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/wiescherdesign/yang/|Yang]];

and [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/paragraph/galette/|Galette]]

I would probably say that Yang, or Antagometrica, would give the most similar look, but really none of these choices is especially close. This style is a bit hard to categorize, because it almost has a hand-drawn lettering feel.

I’m really curious if anyone else has any ideas. I think I have done as much as I can think to find it. I even looked for Israeli designers, but most of their fonts were Hebrew.
