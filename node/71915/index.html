Not in the City now, but I am from Vienna. There is a lot of Jugendstil. Generally walking through not so fast and up and coming parts of the city you can spot shop displays which are several decades old, some true gems. I remember there was an exhibition once on that. A lot of Metro Stations are designed by Otto Wagner, where you have some Jugendstil writing in the Stations. But I really am not an expert. The main university library holds books dating back 400 years or more. I really don't know where you could get specimen. Maybe you contact the art universities about your inquire.
