New Font Release - Graffix - Your Verdict?

September 21, 2012 • studio-k

The name should give you a clue. A would-be modern classic in the mould of Helvetica or Futura, but with just a hint of Art Deco flavouring thrown in. Crisp, bold, geometric, ideal for headlining an ad or a magazine article, yet versatile enough to be used as a text face. What do you think? Just released from my foundry – Studio K – at myfonts.com, Graffix is available in Regular, Italic, Bold and Bold Italic in OTF and TTF and as a web font.
