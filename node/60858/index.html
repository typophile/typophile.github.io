2009-08-21 21:21:12 • cfig

Green is looking good.

On the PMS matching: Always pick a PMS that you like first. Then,I usually ask for a a match print from clients printer, on the paper you are going to use. this print contains one only the Pantone, and one full CMYK with a few variations. Out of these variations one can pick the closest to the Pantone print.

Also, for final colors, its really hard to match a PMS on the screen:)
