I like it. The way you alternate contrasting fonts, sizes, and shapes creates a nice rhythm that puts the emphasis in the right places.

Anything with so much variety needs a unifying motif to hold it all together, and you’ve got one: Everything is very symmetrical. So when you click through to the questionnaire pages, the small instruction paragraphs (“You give permission . . .” etc.) look wrong because they’re aligned on the left. I suggest you center each line of those paragraphs.
