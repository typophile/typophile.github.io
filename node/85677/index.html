It was interesting to read one of the comments saying that it looks quite Dutch. I wonder if anyone else here has a shared opinion of this? I welcome all types of discussion on this matter!
Woe betide the Dutch type designer who creates a geometric sans :-) That would be De Roos, Crouwel, Kisman, Pool, Verkaart, &c., &c… It would be more accurate to speak of a certain kind of Dutch type design, informed by the theories and teaching of Gerrit Noordzij (author of The Stroke).
