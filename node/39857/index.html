[[Indices]] : [[Terminology]] : Typeface Series

A typeface series is a set of typefaces that form a [[family]], a [[superfamily]] or even have a looser connection, for example are designed by the same designer with a common thought in mind. So, any family or superfamily is a series, but also [[Verdana]], [[Tahoma]], [[Nina]] and [[Meiryo]] form a series, though not a family nor a superfamily. Another example of a series are Arepo, Stone Print, SFPL, and Cycles by [[Sumner Stone]].
