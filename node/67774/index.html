Type for a statement of faith

February 24, 2010 • Tomufied

This might be an odd request, but for one of my classes I want to typeset the United Church of Christ’s [[http://www.ucc.org/beliefs/statement-of-faith.html#DOX|Statement of Faith]] and am having trouble deciding on which font to use.

I am presently leaning toward Scala Sans Light. To my (amateur) eye it’s fresh, non-authoritarian, and has a sense play - all things I would like to convey.

Does anybody have any other suggestions?

Scala Sans does seem to be a good choice given your intent, although it seems to me to be a bit too plain. Clearly, you don’t want, say, Wedding Text, since that would produce the opposite of the effect you are pursuing.

Murray Hill and Park Avenue aren’t right either; but I would be inclined to suggest some sort of light, playful face that still makes a nod to the past by being calligraphic. But I can’t find what I’m thinking of, although Tiffany, Bauhaus, or Korinna might (or might not) work for you.
