You might want to post a slightly smaller image, but with a uniform white margin running around it. Would make it easier to view.

The way you’ve done the ‘A’ bothers a bit. Somehow it seems horizontally flipped. I think the curving of the apex should be on the left-hand side. You’re probably trying to mirror the lines of the ‘R’ on SURF, but it doesn’t work too well.

Also the weight of the first ‘A’ does not match with that of the other characters in the word AUSTRALIA. Have you created it by scaling up the smaller ‘A’?

Likewise, the R also may look better if redrawn. Being a curved form, it typically should go a little over the cap-height so that it visually aligns with the more angular characters.
