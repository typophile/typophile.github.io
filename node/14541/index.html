I have few skills in design and type (but a lot of interest), and I'm trying to design an attractive resume for an out-of-college full time job in the ibanking arena. Can someone suggest a hot template (I know that's a taboo word) or maybe a font that would look good at a size below 12? Would you suggest serif or sans for body?
I would suggest discussing your content first. You have to remember culling down the vast number of résumés a potential employer recieves, means its a quick read and not a lot of detail. Its about organizing the information. Once you make the cut, then the interview process is the game maker or breaker. This is not a project that you need a lot of design, but a lot of layout.
