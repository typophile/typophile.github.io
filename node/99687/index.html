I would go with the Bodoni; ﬁne-tuning the relationship between the weight of the headline’s “hairlines” and the subhead’s linearity will provide a constraint you can design against, an issue that, when resolved, will create harmony in your type hierarchy. Bear in mind that you can adjust the weight of the headline type (especially the thickness of its hairlines) by stroking the type outline in your layout app. You can also introduce line rules into your layout which play against this quality.
