It’s my first time here, so I’ll quickly introduce myself: I’m Carel Fransen, graphic designer from the Netherlands, and currently working on my graduation projects.

For my project, I’m looking for a quality condensed font with a large x-height that does great at small corps-sizes (approx. 6-7 pt).

I have got one font that works very well: [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/fontbureau/bureau-grot/light/|Bureau Grot Light]]. However, I’d like to have some options. Unfortunately, due to the “advanced search”-mechanisms on big font-sites not being that advanced, it’s nearly impossible to search for something this specific.

I hope that some of you might be able to give me some recommendations. Preferably, I’d like a font with some Opentype-features like different kinds of figures, but most importantly it should be a bit condensed, readable at small sizes, and kerned nicely.

Thank you in advance!

Kind regards,

Carel
