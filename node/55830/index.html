I did what I suggested and found a few that were very similar. Closest in shape was [[http://www.dafont.com/typesource-extol.font?nb_ppp=50&text=Super+Auto+Care|Typesource Extol]] by Aenigma. It looks like a heavier version of a very close font to your sample. Square off the u on the left side, and you’re there, if this was a lighter weight. Maybe another of their fonts (perhaps not a freebie) would be a better match.

The other font I thought was similar was [[http://www.dafont.com/black-wolf.font?nb_ppp=50&text=Super+Auto+Care|Black Wolf]] from GreyWolf Webworks.

These might give you a place to start.
