Hello urfan.alimov,

The logotype “iddaa” looks to be [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/linotype/helvetica/|Helvetica Bold]] or a variant of it such [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/linotype/neue-helvetica/|Helvetica Neue Bold]]. It’s not perfectly printed, but that’s the most probable typeface chosen.

The body text is a tough one. I ran it through [[http://www.identifont.com/similar?2LA|Identifont]] and that is the best result from my findings on there. It’s a proportional, san-serif typeface typical of a receipt that’s all I can see from it something similar to this imitation called [[http://www.fontspace.com/cumberland-fontworks/merchant-copy|Merchant Copy]]. I have also done some good, old Googling and encounter this [[http://graphicdesign.stackexchange.com/questions/20027/identify-these-fonts-used-on-receipts|thread]] on the graphics forum of the Stack Exchange. Have a read, it appears to suggest a font called [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/green-type/hypermarket/|Hypermarket]], which looks close to your receipt.
