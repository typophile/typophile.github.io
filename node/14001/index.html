Frutiger condensed italic

July 20, 2005 • allenhanford

Does this font exist from any foundry? I’m a production manager at an agency where naughty young designers have specified Frutiger condensed as part of a corporate identity, and now we’re fudging when we need Frutiger condensed italic. Is there another font that would pass for Frutiger cond ital?

Give Myriad Pro Condensed Italic a try. Myriad is also called the better Frutiger. - Frutiger itself dont loved the idea of a true italic, but the market demanded, so Linotype made it. Have a look to Linotypes Frutiger Next. They used the italic model of Myriad for their version, but not for the condensed ones.
