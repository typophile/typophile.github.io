I would say that the version you are looking for was digitized from a larger point size than the Bitstream version. FYI, metal type was cut differently for different sizes. Digital foundries don't necessarily digitize from the same original size, which might account for what you're seeing. It's also possible that the designer of this logo made some changes, such as moving the i dot down.
