2007-04-25 20:41:21 • AndrewSipe
MetaDesign's strategy for the system was born of the idea of mnemonics. Due to the acquisition of Macromedia by Adobe their product portfolio went from roughly seven products to around sixteen. A system similar to the periodic table was proposed. The characters chosen to represent the product are for memorability purposes; sometimes this leverages the already familiar. I should note, Meta proposed this strategy along with an entire brand evolution (having been the agency responsible for the design of CS1 and CS2 packaging and icons), they were not responsible for the execution of anything CS3.
