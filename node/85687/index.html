I’m using Ostrich Sans and Ostrich Sans Black ( [[http://www.theleagueofmoveabletype.com/fonts/18-ostrich-sans]] ) for headlines / titles and I’m looking for a body text font that would be a nice complement. Any ideas? I’m having a hard time finding a serif font that seems to go well with it and I’m almost tempted to use another sans-serif font, but i know that’s generally not advisable. Any suggestions would be appreciated.

Something squarish: Hermann Zapf’s [[http://www.linotype.com/1222/melior-family.html|Melior]] or Curus Highsmith’s [[http://www.fontbureau.com/fonts/IbisText/|Ibis]].

Something top heavy (see the S in Ostrich): Sindre Bremnes’ [[http://typophile.com/node/81129|Satyr]] or David Jonathan Ross’ [[http://www.fontbureau.com/fonts/Trilby/|Trilby]].
