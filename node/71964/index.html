TypeTalks Brno write-ups online

July 5, 2010 • Florian Hardwig

TypeTalks was a one-day symposium about type and typography in Brno, Czech Republic (announcement on Typophile). The event that took place two weeks ago in the Brno House of Arts was organized by David Březina and some of his students.

For those who couldn’t make it to Brno, there are several write-ups online now: Dan Reynolds @ typeoff.de [English] Michael Hochleitner @ typejockeys.com [English] Florian Hardwig @ MyFonts.de, part 1 and part 2 [German, but with lots of pictures. You can try Google Translate to get the basic idea: part 1, part 2]

Furthermore, there are a few photos up on Flickr, tagged with ‘TypeTalks’.
