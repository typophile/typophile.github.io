The old text face was simply called Economist or Economist 101 and was designed, I believe, by Briem. You can see a sample here http://www.fonts.com/findfonts/detail.asp?pid=243431
or in the Precision Type Reference Guide (5.0) if you have that at your disposal. The redesigned face is called EcoType; it's clearly a derivative of Economist, but is a little lighter and slightly more condensed in comparison.

But James is right: Erik is the source.
