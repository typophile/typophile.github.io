I’m trying to find this typeface from a movie cover my client would like me to use. I know Futura is a close option, but I would love to know this specific face, for my client and to scratch my curiosity itch. Any ideas?

Thank you!

Lots of very similar typefaces, but I couldn’t find a spot-on match. Best recommendation to recreate the look is Neutra Face No. 2 Text Bold (blunt the top of the |A| and the bottoms of the |V|s, and alter the angle of the stroke ends on the arms of the |s|): http://www.houseind.com/fonts/neutraface2

Also very close: Verlag Black by Hoefler and Co., Transat Black by Typetanic, Radikal by Nootype, and OL Eagle by Dennis Ortiz Lopez.
