Not that pirates need a good rationalization, but… I must admit that I overlooked the Turkish character set for many years, until Hasbro Toys commissioned a special version of Boyz R Gross NF, which included Turkish and Greek. Needless to say, the Turkish characters were a breeze, compared to my first foray into Greek characters.
