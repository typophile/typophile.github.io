Bold vs. Extra Bold vs. Black?

October 9, 2008 • ShepherdJim

Okay, I get the difference between a Bold face and an Extra Bold, but where exactly does a “Black” fit into the grand scheme of a font? Every time I think I have sussed out what the definable difference might be I run into a font that breaks “the rule.”

Apologies to all who’ll say that I should just choose what works/looks best. But, I’m the picky type who likes to understand the small stuff.

This is the sequence that appears in the first Font Info panel of FontLab, so it represents something of a standard, as this is what a lot of type designers and font developers go by.

However, it should be pointed out that when one of these is selected, Fontlab assigns a numerical weight value from 100 to 1000 (in increments of 100) to the font, so several of the weights share the same number.
