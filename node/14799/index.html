August 24, 2005 • timfm

Hi -- Just setting up overprinting options in Illustrator CS2 and viewing them with Overprint Preview. Is there a way to export these so I can post them to Typophile and still retain the overprint effects? -t
What you need to do is make a copy, then 'flatten transparency'. When you do, uncheck the 'preserve overprint' option. This will give you a CMYK conversion of what Illustrator thinks the overprint will look like. One word of caution: If you are actually going to be overprinting solid inks, you might not want to take the 'illustrator' preview of that to seriously. Its very hard to simulate something the mixing of the inks. Although, it can serve many other purposes.
