Joos typeface

March 3, 2011 • Jonathan Fabreguettes

Hello,

just a word to tell you that the Joos typeface, made by Laurent Bourcellier in 2009 and awarded at the TDC in 2010, is now freely available in Demo Version through email request at the foundry typographies.fr.

It is with a limited character set and only for non-commercial use, but we hope it will answer the testing needs of publishers and graphic designers.

Furthermore, for the hundredth request, instead of a Demo Version, we will send a Pro Version as a gift.

Yours sincerely.
