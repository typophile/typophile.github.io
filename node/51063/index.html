October 29, 2008 • Frode Bo Helland

The publication is a small fanzine, directed towards youth/young adults. A "karabin" is a gun. I'm not really sure about the a, neither the K. Please give your opinions on the masthead, but bare in mind I'm new with letters.
We're writing about creativity and life, and there's a great deal of music in there as well. It's a small project, only to be read by friends and family, but I'd still like to do it properly.

2008-10-30 15:16:07 • bemerx25
good start frode The K feels too plainly geometric in relation to the other letters, and the waist is rather low slung. You might play with the upper arm perhaps. It might seem too heavy if you move the waist up. The squared-off 'a' is kinda cool, but it doesn't seem to mesh/match 100 percent with the r-b-n. maybe for this zine, that's a good thing. the spacing needs some slight work. maybe the a-r and the a-b might need ever-so-slight widening and the b-i slight reduction. (just a hair) the r-a pair feels okay small, but larger feels like it needs more air. good luck.
