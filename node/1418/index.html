Which Bodoni? Typophiles Recommend...

September 26, 2003 • Jon Whipple

This is the result of another conversation. The objective is to assemble a list of 'recommended' typefaces from the Typophile forums. I chose Bodoni as a test case because it's a large part of my world. What I want to know is: Is this kind of summary useful? Are there things that would make it more useful? * When asked "Which Bodoni should I buy?", Typophiles said: ITC Bodoni ITC Bodoni: 7 endorsements Berthold Bodoni Antiqua and Expert: 1 endorsement Berthold Bodoni Oldface and Expert: 1 endorsement Bauer Bodoni: 2 (1 of which is specifically in reference to metal) Linotype Bodoni Classico: 1 endorsement Alternatives suggested: HTF Didot, Linotype Didot, Zuzana Licko's Filosofia Source Thread: http://www.typophile.com/forums/messages/30/13805.html and http://www.typophile.com/forums/messages/30/13806.html Notes: http://www.typophile.com/forums/messages/72/1526.html Fellow typophile names baby after Bodoni My relatively broad search on the phrase Which Bodoni yeilded 95 threads and hundreds of entries. I read as much of a thread as I felt I needed to decide if it was directly relevant to a question, and I did a search for the word Bodoni in the page to determine its context. Other candidates are: Which Didot? Which Futura? Which Garamond? And I am sure there are more. So let me know what you think. Jon
