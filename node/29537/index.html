Looks very good! The style difference between the R & G doesn’t really bother me, but the uppercase E does need a little work (it reads like a C now)… Maybe you could smoothen the style difference between the R & G by blending with the E, making the top part angular & the bottom part curved (as in some ampersands)? As for the lowercase s, maybe making a small straight vertical line at the top & the bottom before curving could help…
