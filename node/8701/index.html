Here is Wrigley and Wrigley Italic …
sorry for the poor scan, but I didn't
want to crack the spine too much.

The nameplate has evolved over the years;
if you look at the evolution, you can see
that each time it's a custom job. Impact
Wide is probably the closest thing going,
nice spot Ryan.

The nameplate is not set in Wrigley, but
Wrigley is one of the headline families, along
with Carter's Georgia.

Carter's Fenway is the SI text face.
