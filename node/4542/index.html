Weird/Unproven Personal Theories

April 29, 2004 • kris

I find that when I am absorbed laying out type
or making glyphs in FL I come up with rough
theories/ideas/saying pertaining to typography.
None of which are proven, just a sudden thought.
I was wondering if anyone has little snippets
of ideas they would share. They don't have to
be right, just more or less your own!

I'll start. "The perfect glyph only ever exists
within its own family (context), never outside"
The idea "singularities" that are most memorable to me seem to be the ones most paradoxical. For example that "a text font has to have a certain ugliness about it", or that "you can never show a concept, you can only explain it".
