May 10, 2005 • James Grieshaber

[[Indices]] : [[Designers]] : James Grieshaber James Grieshaber attended [[RIT|Rochester Institute of Technology]] and earned a BFA in [[Graphic_Design|Graphic Design]]. Grieshaber was a staff designer at [[P22|P22 Type Foundry]] and helped establish [[IHOF|International House of Fonts]]. He has been honored with awards of Excellence in Type Design from [[ATypI|Association Typographique International (ATypI)]] for [[Gothic_Gothic|Gothic Gothic]] and from [[TypeArt'05|TypeArt'05]] for his [[Operina|Operina]] Cyrillic type designs. He has been involved with the [[SOTA|Society of Typographic Aficionados]] and served as Chairman of SOTA from 2008—2010. Designer and Co-editor of the [[Indie Fonts]] book series, Grieshaber has taught typography at RIT and now runs [[Typeco]], his own type design studio, in Chicago, IL. Type designs by James Grieshaber include: Cezanne Pro, Operina Pro, Segoe Print, Reenie Beanie, Black Ops, Short Stack, Atomic Age, Supermercado, Metamorphous, Sarina, Posada, Woodtype, Art Deco Chic, Arts & Crafts Hunter, Arts & Crafts Tall, Glyphic Neue, Chunkfeeder, Trapper, Super Duty, Cypher, Gothic Gothic, Cusp, Numismatic, Yule, and Zaftig.
