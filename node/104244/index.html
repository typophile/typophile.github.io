Harlequin romance novel cover typeface

June 29, 2013 • Thermidor

I’ve been wracking my brain and searching my type books trying to identify the typeface regularly used on the cover of vintage Harlequin romance novels, but so far I’ve had no luck.

The good news is I have an extensive sample character set: https://dl.dropbox.com/u/17582217/FileChute/harlequin_sample.png

It’s not Tiffany, though some of the letters are clearly close.

Does anyone have any guesses? This is driving me crazy!

Thank you in advance for any help!

Your effort to post an extensive set of letters is appreciated. I suspect this is a font the company uses for its brand image and would not wish to see it sold or elsewhere. Copyright laws, at least in USA, suggest that if you did get a copy of the digital data for the font it would probably be unwise to use it. As for the base font, i.e. before the swashes, one of our typophiles may identify a spot on match. So far I have not found a full match with the spurless C & S, outbowed legs on K & R, left cusp at the apex of A, and splayed M. Your letters have some resemblance to Tiempos Headline Bold. Don
