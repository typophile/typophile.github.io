Today the E that looks like a C with a crossbar reminds us of the Euro sign, but in the 1940s there were a few fonts with this feature, such as Lucian Bernhard’s Fashion and Gothic, which had it as an alternate character. It is revived in a font by Nick Curtis, Samosata NF http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/nicksfonts/samosata-nf/ Don
