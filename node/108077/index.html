UPM other than 1000

January 8, 2014 • Toby

Is it totally ok for a otf-font to have a different UPM than 1000, if the ascender and descender adds up to that other value?
Yes. I would highly recommend reading through, [[http://kltf.de/downloads/FontMetrics-kltf.pdf|Karsten Luecke’s guide to font metrics]] (fair warning, depending on your browser, that link will trigger an automatic download of a PDF). There are also numerous discussions elsewhere on Typophile, but the one about [[UPM and vertical font metrics: please help to clarify|UPM and Vertical Font Metrics]] should have the level of detail you need and cover most questions.
