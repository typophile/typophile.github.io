has anyone ever heard anything about a website called “whohub.net”?

i received an email from them asking for an interview, and i was wondering if they were legit.

Look at the whois:

" Registrant: F.S.O. 195 St Miami , Florida 33179 United States

Administrative Contact: F.S.O. 195 St Miami , Florida 33179 United States fran@jerni.com +01 0000000000

Technical Contact: F.S.O. 195 St Miami , Florida 33179 United States fran@jerni.com +01 0000000000

Record expires on 2009/08/23 UTC Record created on 2008/08/23 UTC "

Strike 1: it’s in Florida - scam central. Strike 2: “195 St” isn’t a valid address. Strike 3: jerni.com doesn’t come up, and its own whois is suspicious.

And to top it off: the creation date is two days in the future…
