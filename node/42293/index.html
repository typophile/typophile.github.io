Faber & Faber's W.B. Yeats

February 20, 2008 • SMangan1980

I have a beautifully typeset collection of poetry by Yeats, selected by Seamus Heaney (London: Faber & Faber, 2000). The typeface appears different from some of the other books in the series. Unfortunately, I only have a picture of the cover, but maybe some helpful characteristics include: the 3, 4, 5, 7, & 9 are lowered; the apostrophe is thin with a lot of arc. [img:sites/default/files/old-images/71ESFHAP46L__SS500__5249.jpg]
And the descending angle at the head of the lower-case ’t' is straight but does not run continuously into the left arm, there is a right-angle ‘notch’ cut into it.
