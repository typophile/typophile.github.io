Hi Joshua,

‘Moutard’ looks like Revenue; maybe a bit stretched. For ‘Champagne’, Engravers Roman is very similar, though no cigar. The script is an Anglaise, and might be custom – note the swashes. For alternatives, see Champion Script or Bickham Script; they both have tons of alternate glyphs and swashes to play around with. Novia is another fine copperplate script. Even more options in this list of Formal Calligraphic Scripts.
