I have enountered some professional cohorts who are hesitant to license fonts. They say that everyone uses traded/downloaded fonts, and they will do so unless they simply cannot get the font files any other way than by buying them.

I'm trying to educate these acquaintences about the foolishness and dishonesty of this path, but they do not believe there is any risk.

Does anyone have any anecdotes or news reports of design professionals getting in trouble for using unlicensed fonts?
