Hi (from an amateur typographer, but take it as you wish). So here it goes. Your font has a nice horizontal flowing feeling to it yet you should improve one or two things: - The lower stem+belly connection on the "b" and "d" should get fixed, as in a simplified curve from the base to the stem instead of a complex stem to curve intersection (specially the "b"). It would prevent it to be a "brake" in the horizontal flow of the line. On the other hand your font will get "rounder"... - The other thing is the horizontal bar on the "a", the "e" and specially the "3". There are to many curves. It gives your characters a blurry look. Probaly if you eliminate the top or the bottom curve joint of the inner intersection it'll lighten up without loosing the design. I think you can develop a very nice font if you keep it up. I specially like the "z". Keep it up! Cheers. By the way,
