Schreibwerkstatt Offenbach

July 6, 2005 • dan_reynolds

[[Indicies]] : [[Organizations]] : Schreibwerkstatt Offenbach Schreibwerkstatt Klingspor Offenbach e.V. für Kalligrafie, the Offenbach/Klingspor Calligraphy Workshop Founded by [[Karlgeorg Hoefer]] during the 1980s, this organization offers calligraphy courses to the public. They also organize lectures and exhibitions. Their website may be found at: www.schreibwerkstatt-klingspor.de/ They are loosely associated with the [[Klingspor Museum]].
