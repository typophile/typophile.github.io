Tough, thick and square

November 14, 2009 • Als02

Searching for a thick, bold and square font for a construction co that works with young people on major recreational projects. I quite like this http://www.sundogtours.com/ to impart a cool yet strong feel.

The SunDogs typeface seems a bit too techno. Soft rounded curves don’t say ‘tough’ to me.

Now Ironmonger is a symbol of toughness.

Another would be Force

If you don’t like the slab serifs you could try Tank

Search “construction” and “military” at MyFonts.com for a pile of options.
