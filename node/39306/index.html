In Reading is this year’s MA Typeface Design class at the University of Reading (the class of 2008, I mean). No public list of the students is posted anywhere online yet, but Paul Hunt—another Typophile moderator and regular—is in the group, too. One of last year’s (2007) graduates is still in town, and several more are scattered across the country. We don’t have an awful lot of contact with the local British typographic scene, though (at least I don’t… this is what happens when you are only going to live in a place for a year, I guess :-( ).

The Department of Typography and Graphic Communication here is worth a day trip if you’ll be in London for awhile. I’m sure we could round up a good group to go for a pint. Feel free to contact me over my Typophile profile anytme.
