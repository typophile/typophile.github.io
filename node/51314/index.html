Sardines extra light

November 5, 2008 • fonthausen

Sardines, an original fat monospaced and square typeface. After being used for private and exclusive purposes only, Sardines has now a full characterset (ASE) and will soon be made available on Vette Letters

'… an amusing parade of heavyweight characters crammed into squares.' (Jan Middendorp, Dutch Type, 2004)

Used as title face in the first restyled issue (2004) of the dutch design magazine Items.

// Jacques
