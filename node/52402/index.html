Peppermint OpenFace

December 5, 2008 • phrostbyte64

A fun typeface for light-hearted projects. I’ve used it primarily for t-shirt designs and posters for children’s activities. I know it isn’t the usual fare for folks around here. Available at Myfonts.com for $10 - includes the solid and the openface forms.

[[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/fontry-west/wild2-peppermint/]]

Thank You for not brandishing pitchforks and lighted torches….

Not something you’d use everyday, but I think it looks really cool :D The odd angles really make each character stand out, so that the whole word seems to be alive. I can see it being used for a coffee shop advertising a peppermint latte or something.

Peace, JT
