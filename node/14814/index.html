For a similar but slightly less “scripty” face, try Mercurius especially the Italics, or alternatively Maiandra again the Italic cuts being a closer sister.

If you have loads and loads of money (and I mean loads!) then you could try Trinité by Enschedé Font Foundry! This one may not seem like such a match at first but look deeper into the weight set, in my mind it is one of the most beautiful typefaces ever cut.

Happy hunting…
