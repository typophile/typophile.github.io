There actually is something funny about the this shirt - not about pedophilia, Joe, but about ignorance. As in, "What the hell is a typophile? Isn't that someone who likes little kids?" The fact that the suffix phile (or the sound "ophile") most immediately elicits a correlation with the most depraved philia is more an indictment of our literacy as a culture than an endoresment of NAMBLA. I think we all know many more people who enjoy a good book, their iPod, and a decent Merlot than the company of a young child, but how often are the terms biblio-, audio-, or enophile uttered in mainstream media (or in conversation)?
