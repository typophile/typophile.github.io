I just stumbled across this old thread. "URW Garamond No. 8" is a digitization of the old Compugraphic rip-off font "Garamond Antiqua", later also called "CG Garamond Antiqua" and much later sold as "CG Garamond" by Monotype and also supplied by HP with its Laserjet printers. For source see "THE type BOOK" by cg Compugraphic Corp., Wilmington 1988 This huge law book http://delegibus.com/2008,2.pdf is typeset using URW Garamond No. 8
