There are a lot of things you can do to maximise FontLab's autohinting without getting into manual tweaking of hints. See [[http://scripts.sil.org/NotesOnHintingWithFL|Notes on Hinting with FontLab]]. Adam Twardoch wrote up a much more detailed follow-on: [[http://www.fontsurgery.com/fonts/font-dev/hinting/autohinting-with-fontlab-studio-5/|Autohinting with FontLab Studio 5]]. You may also want to try avoiding FL's hinting altogether with [[http://www.freetype.org/ttfautohint/|ttfautohint]].
