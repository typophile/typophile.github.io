Definitely not a font. It’s quite lovely, though.

There don’t seem to be a great deal of script fonts around that approximate this “texture” of handwriting, if you like. Blambot’s After Dark BB (http://new.myfonts.com/fonts/blambot/after-dark-bb/) has big caps and a small, delicate lowercase, but is quite scrappy and not as elegant as your example. There’s always something like Johann Sparkling (http://new.myfonts.com/fonts/itc/johann-sparkling/), which is quite open but more oblique and swooshy than After Dark.

Here are a few more suggestions:

http://new.myfonts.com/fonts/ef/petras-script/ http://new.myfonts.com/fonts/scholtz/arabesque/ http://new.myfonts.com/fonts/sudtipos/sudestada-basic/

Alternatively, get a pen and start writing!
