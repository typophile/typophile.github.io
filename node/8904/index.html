Most of the characters in the large italic sample match the shapes of Bellevue, except for being a bit heavier overall--but that is easily done with a stroke or just a result of overinking. The characters that don't match--H, L, and v--would have been easy enough to modify. Certain characters--the a, e, t, and especially the j--match so closely to Bellevue (except for the heaviness) that I have to think that this is actually the case.

I think the reversed text is definitely Utopia, though.
