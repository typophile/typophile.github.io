Jim, my font prucahses are pretty modest as I rely on my employer for most everything but I was thinking to myself just the other day that there seems to be a lot of homogeniety in the type vendor web space.

I was just thinking that it would be very useful to have an on-screen comparison tool, where I could look at details in a font and compare them with 3(?) others all in the same screen. That way I can make a choice between, or find contrasting or complementary types quickly and easily. I imagine a screen divided into quadrants kind of like a Photoshop 'Save for web…' dialogue box.

Another idea is to provide users with a bookmarking or specimin utility. I am thinking of the light box concept at istockphoto where as a user I can collect photos and refer to them quickly and easily at a later date (a page with thumbnails that point to the actual catalogue entry). Or at least come
back and pine for the day I need them or can afford them.

I'd love features like these and would head there first if I needed to find something.
