Yes! There is no way of just “bolding” a typeface. You just have to design the bold version. You should understand the specific needs of your own typeface. Turning bold is not just pressing a button, that’s why designing type takes so much time.

However, once you understand how the “Bold” and other transformations work, it is possible to design your face with these in mind, to make “bolding” easier.

Certain kinds of typeface are more amenable to “push button” bolding, for instance light, monoline faces.

This happens when the outlines are in the wrong direction. Reverse the contours to counterclockwise (Type 1) direction.
