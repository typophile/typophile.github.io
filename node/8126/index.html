HE'BREW, the chosen beer

December 11, 2004 • dan_reynolds

I heard about this microbrewery today on NPR's Weekend Edition. I must say that this is the funniest website that I've seen in a good two weeks or so.

Additionally, they've made use of some sort of faux-latin lettering for their beer bottle labels, which is no surprise. Certainly quite different from the beer labels I see every day
He'brew combines bad taste and stupidity, as I suppose befits a beer. Some of this stuff is in bad taste too - with the requisite faux-Hebrew letters - but most is pretty funny, which is a saving grace, if I may use the expression!
