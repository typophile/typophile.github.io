The Foundry of Ralf Herrmann

Ralf Herrmann studies Communication Design at Bauhaus-University Weimar. He is the webmaster of www.typografie.info, the well-known independent web site dedicated to typography and fonts in Germany. Herrmann is founder of a web design agency in Germany, writes books about fonts and typography and is advisor of FontShop Berlin.
