I think too many people these days assume that screen generated design has limits and so everything should follow the same patterns.

A DVD menu can be what ever you want it to be, as can everthing else. Why cant a DVD menu be similar to that of a menu or contents found in a book or that of a restaurant menu.

My advice it just follow the elementry rules when designing something. What is it for, who it is aimed at, choose an appropriate typeface etc etc.

Forget waht existing menus look like. If it were me I would be thinking of creating a piece f design that works for the topic. The only rule you may have it the format, but even that can be broken.
