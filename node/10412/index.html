Hrant Papazian at City College SF - May 2, 2005

April 19, 2005 • abecedarienne

Yay! A typographic event in San Francisco!

Our very own Hrant Papazian will be speaking on "Nature and Nurture in Typeface Design" at City College of San Francisco on Monday, May 2, 2005.

It's on the Ocean/Phelan campus, in the Rosenberg Library, room 305 at 6:30pm.

It's free, exciting and all kinds of accessible, so come on down!

I’ve been meaning to write up my (pretty extensive) notes from the presentation for Typomancy, but like most of my writing projects, it’s gotten stalled on the completion of some real-life projects. In brief, it was a lot of fun, Hrant spoke well and was an engaging speaker, and the questions were indeed the best part! It’s interesting seeing graphic design students wrap their heads around the special challenges posed by typeface design. I was left with the strong regret that Art Center is too far away for me to take Hrant’s class.
