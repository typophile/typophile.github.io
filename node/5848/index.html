2004-07-31 01:07:41 • pablohoney77
Metadox. I can answer your question about the creator of the
Marker Felt fonts. That was Pat Snyder (who correctly shows
up in the copyright of the font you linked to in your last post).
I registered my copies of various of his fonts long ago.

Mr. Snyder used to have a Web page, but that disappeared
years ago. All efforts of mine to find and contact him again
a few months ago failed.

The fonts were originally (no surprise) for the Mac and can
be found here, complete with documentation. Mac folk: See
his nifty SnyderSpeed font as well. Marker Felt Wide and
Marker Fine Point for Windows may be found here (file
names begin with mf) with docs.

This Apple web page gives credit to Pat Snyder. I hope that
they gave him some cash too.
