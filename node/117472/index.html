Angled terminals like your _V_ are found in the lowercase of Jenson Oldstyle designed by J. W. Phinney of the Dickinson Type Foundry in 1893, [img:sites/default/files/old-images/JensonOSLC_5304.jpg] but not in the uppercase. Now available as LTC Jenson http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/lanston/ltc-jenson/ The terminal style was popular circa 1900 and it was used in the uppercase of some lettering designs. Some digging might turn up a digital font with the _V_. don
