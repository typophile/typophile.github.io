Bertham Pro - a new Goudy revival from Ascender

August 6, 2009 • billdavis

Ascender is pleased to announce the release of [[http://www.ascenderfonts.com/font/bertham-pro-family-4-fonts.aspx|Bertham Pro]], another Goudy revival from the Ascender team.

This classic face has been digitally remastered and brought back to life in all its OpenType glory by Steve Matteson. The Bertham Pro family includes 4 fonts: the original roman weight designed by Frederic W. Goudy’s Bertham and three new styles (bold, italic and openface) created by Steve Matteson.

The Bertham Pro fonts include a variety of OpenType features including swash capitals, small capitals and old style figures. Bertham Pro has a distinctive quality that is unmistakably American in appearance recalling a day of quality craftsmanship and hard work.

View the fonts at [[http://www.ascenderfonts.com/font/bertham-pro-family-4-fonts.aspx|www.AscenderFonts.com]]
