2012-04-04 17:19:27 • JamesT
I once gave a talk in Thessaloniki about the hidden benefits of ligation, including aiding readability. At least one person though thinks that talk was crap. :-) But he's (still) a friend. I haven't thought it all through, but I think the best basis would be to go through the lexicon of a given language and for word pairs (or more generally, sets) that have convergent boumas use ligation to diverge them. But it has to be consistent. For example in English, for "guest" and "quest" the latter might get an "st" ligature but the former not. hhp
