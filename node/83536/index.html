2011-07-07 10:09:31 • Vaatika

The exclamation mark indicates a “sticky” thread – meaning one that always appears at the top of a forum list (usually this is a Read Me type of thread).

If you have posted in a thread, then it will appear in the Track tab of your user account page. (Many people will post a dummy comment in order to make a thread appear there.)

Alternately, you can bookmark a thread by clicking on the faint “bookmark” icon in the upper right of the initial post in a thread. When you do, the icon will turn solid and red.

To access the list of your marked threads, click on “My marks” under your account name in the upper right sidebar.
