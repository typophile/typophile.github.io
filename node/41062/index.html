Please help me choose a typeface!

January 18, 2008 • Kate_K

I need to design a t-shirt with two words on it and am having trouble finding an appropriate typeface. The t-shirts are aimed primarily at 30-/40-somethings and are not ‘comedy’ but will probably raise a wry smile from those who get the reference, which is to a pop song from the eighties.

I can’t reveal the text yet (sorry), but it’s along the lines of “Like a Yo-Yo” - i.e. lyrics from part of a song. If there is to be a whiff of retro about it, then it should be subtle.

I would be very grateful for your suggestions! Thank you, Kate

The original CHOOSE LIFE T-shirts designed by Katharine Hamnett were set in Folio Bold Condensed (or something similar).

Folio is generic-looking enough that it doesn’t look overtly “retro” by any means, but people who get the reference will recognize the face from the original T-shirt, consciously or otherwise.
