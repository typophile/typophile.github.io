2006-09-18 07:59:33 • alexfjelldal

Padding, margin, typographic options etc are all available in InDesign’s tables, without a plug-in. Nearly anything you can do with a regular text box, you can do with a table cell.

As for styles, that I don’t think is available. You can adjust any cell, row, or column just by selecting it and changing options either in the table palette or dialog box. I assume you have done this, but wish to adjust the same column on multiple tables at once. I see your dilemma.
