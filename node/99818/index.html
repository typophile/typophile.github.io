Although recently I've found very similar [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/moretype/rehn/|one]], but it was already too late to quit. I intended this for a paragraph-length copy. Quotes, openings etc. Originally it was an upright italic, but everything changed except /e slanted crossbar. 2 Masters, (prob.) 5 weights, Multilatin, Expanded Cyrillic. My first font being made with Glyphs. It's awesome. Won't be going back to FontLab. Neris is a name of a river that goes through my city.
I like this more than I thought I would. :-) BTW if you never make an Italic for it, don't feel bad, since Italics are over-rated, especially in a typeface with finely gradated weights. Related: http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/stone/silica/ hhp
