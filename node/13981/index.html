Hi Joffre,

yes, it was one of Ed Benguiat’s many designs that he kept in his famous drawer of sketches to develop at some point. I selected this one for Ed to expand so we could present to the 1992 Barcelona Summer Olympic Games design committee.

It was to become the “official” typeface for that Olympiad, and it’s working name was Catalana. Once Kodak found out that Agfa was involved, they strong-armed the committee to shut us out based on their sponsorship of being the ONLY film based company associated with the 1992 Games.

All I know is that Ed put it back in his drawer and haven’t found out if it was ever finished and released under another name.
