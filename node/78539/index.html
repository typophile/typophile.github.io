Hi guys, I am working now on my new Emila font and i have encountered some difficulties regarding the listing of the fonts.

Is there any way you can arrange the different weights the way you prefer? Because i would like my Pattern and Frames, to be on the bottom of the list, logically the weights such as Thin, Medium Bold etc. come first…

You can see on the picture what i mean exactly. Please anyone who knows i would be very thankful.

You can ship around by using an other font family name like Emila Extras - Patern. This is the best solution!

The hard one: Try to change the table OS/2 weight(class) and width(class) values. If you have no plans of expanding Emila with a condensed, compressed or extended weights declare the pattern & frames to be condensed - or extended weights. This should lead to an grouped sorting at the end or at the top of your style list. But you have to understand the weight and width classes first.

2011-01-30 18:02:19 • andreas
Right. In FontLab Info panel one, you could change the Heavy name to Extra Bold (numerically 800), and then make the Weight of Pattern and Frames, as Black (900) and Fat (1000). Applications that sort menu styles by number will then place them in the order you want. That might work.

2011-01-31 23:42:13 • Arno Enslin

You can actually do increments of weightclass by as little as five (but not as little as one, as I recall).

So if you only have one weight each of patterns and frames, you could do:

Bold: 700 Heavy: 800 Black: 850 Ultra: 900 Patterns: 910 Frames: 920

Or something like that.
