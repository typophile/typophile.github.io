Back in the day, there was a magazine called Factsheet Five that served as a reviewer/chronicler/informal clearinghouse for hundreds of zines. It was founded by Mike Gunderloy and ran for several years before foundering in the 90s after a couple of changes in owner/editorship. (The publication's ghost site is still afloat at http://www.factsheet5.com)
