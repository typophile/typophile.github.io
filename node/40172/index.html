It’s a fabulous book about engaging the senses with design, largely via anticipation. Hara is one of Japan’s most eminent designers with a western audience in mind. He discusses his ideas about stimulating touch, doing really deep design, and the color white, among other things. Intellectually, it’s at least on par with Alan Fletcher, but written from the point of a Japanese ascetic who happens to be an intellectual giant.
