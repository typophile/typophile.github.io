Right font, wrong cut. This is Berthold's "Standard" version vs. your sample: [img:sites/default/files/old-images/banodep_4136.png] The "difference" in the a--as well as the overall weight and sharpness--can be attributed to ink gain, either on the press or from linecast repros. Unless your purpose is out-and-out forgery, I doubt that you'll get any closer with digital type.
