I’m buying Akzidenz-Grotesk Collection for my client who is a medical science company; I was wondering if someone knows if the new OT-Pro version contains Greek mathematical glyphs like delta, pi or mu?

Thank you KTKM

These are part of the basic character set, so even the standard Akzidenz-Grotesk BQ has those characters. What the Pro version adds is Eastern European, Turkish, and Baltic character sets, as well as small caps and figure alts (oldstyle/lining, fractions) rolled into one font per style.
