Have you ever heard about Purup Prepress AS, Denmark? They were market leader for technical design of labels and packaging in the 1990ies.

Before I hooked up there as a region sales manager (I am a newspaper specialist, coming from Atex) I had no idea how difficult and experienced packaging printing is. Purup gave me a chance to learn a lot. Purup did a good job for education and documentation of their technology. I know e.g. there is a movie for free download on Lars Kähler’s Global Type: http://www.global-type.org/Film-Purup.543.0.html (157 MB - 50 minutes) - so a lot of download-time. But the film is great.
