From John Downer:

I don’t know of any single standard term for exactly what you’re asking, but any of the following terms would be understood by American sign painters:

  • swash underline
  • swash underscore
  • swash tail

The word “flourish” (or the word “decorative”) can be substituted for “swash” without degrading the meaning.
