Inkjet printer font needed

May 14, 2011 • Claire Bibio

hello. i’m interested in a modern font preferably serif for long text setting that will look good and sharp when printed with inkjet printer on A4 80 gram paper. any recommendations. thank you much.

I’m not being funny but have you considered times? I know it isn’t modern, but it was created for poor ink and paper. You have the advantage of better paper and ink so it will look good. You will be hard pressed to get a font that looks sharp with and inkjet on 80gsm, as by its nature the ink bleeds. Do you have access to a laser printer? you may want to try a better grade paper, this should combat the bleeding and give the fonts a sharper appearance. A nice modern serifed font? Bodoni http://new.myfonts.com/fonts/bitstream/atf-bodoni/

Hope that helps.
