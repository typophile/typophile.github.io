Probably not a publicly available font. Rather, it could be a font built into the printing machine. Some general similarity of the numbers to AG Book http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/berthold/ag-book-pro/ The large bowl of 5 is found on relatively few fonts. For example Scout https://www.myfonts.com/fonts/fontbureau/scout/ As for the letters, they are a rather generic condensed Gothic/grotesque with some unusual features such as the convex leg on R and the straignt section on the diagonal of S Don
