The intended use of the broadsheet will impinge on the can of worms that is legibility and readability (even if there was a consensus on what improves/reduces either). Questions of your audience for example, what age are they, how do you perceive them reading it (ie is it at arms length, stuck on a wall, will they read it in one “sitting” or will they return to the subject etc, etc). Once you have a clear concept of that, and remembering that your views will not chime with every member of the audience so you will need to consider extremes also, you could show it to unbiased readers. It sounds to me that you are trying to create something that introduces conflicts to the detriment of the content. Tim
