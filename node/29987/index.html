FF Clan by Łukasz Dziedzic

December 8, 2006 • twardoch

Of course, the problem with the term “clan” now is that FontFont just released the large FF Clan family by my good friend Łukasz Dziedzic. It’s the first Polish type design released through FontFont, and hopefully not Łukasz’s last.

Overview of the FontShop release 39: http://www.fontshop.de/news/NewFontFonts/Overview%20FF39.pdf

FF Clan sample: http://www.rzeczy.net/data_img/2006-10-11/FFClan.pdf

Łukasz Dziedzic’s website featuring some of his other typefaces: http://www.alfabety.pl/portfolio/

An interview with Łukasz (in English) on rzeczy.net: http://rzeczy.net/en_czytaj_167.php
