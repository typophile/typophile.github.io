David Lu is a visual designer, interaction designer, illustrator, and software developer. He studied design at Interaction Design Institute Ivrea and has had the great privilege of working with studios such as Pentagram Design, Futurefarmers, IDEO, and Frog Design. David’s collaborations with Futurefarmers have appeared at DEAF, Yerba Buena Center for the Arts, and Whitney Artport. His commercial projects have included dynamic signage and information display projects for Dallas Museum of Art, NY Botanical Garden, and Walker Art Center. He holds BA degrees in Computer Science, Economics, and Psychology from Rutgers College.
