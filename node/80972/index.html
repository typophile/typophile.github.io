According to the Michael Freimuth, who did the design work and identity for Glass Shop, it is a (unintended) customized version of ‘Empire’. It is unreleased.

Michael noted, “I’d had a box of vintage pamphlets floating around my office a while back, and one in particular contained a face I thought would be a fit for Glass Shop – I scanned it and thought I’d found a beautifully random old face to re-build and work with – I redrew it from hand, and made some alternate weights and an italic version. It was quickly pointed out to me by an associate that I’d basically just made Morris Fuller Benton’s 1937 ‘Empire’ and when I looked more closely I believe that’s exactly what it was.

So I essentially just re-built his original and all credit and apologies goes to him, though technically I believe some of the characters vary in weight and exact structure. But to be fair, let’s just call it Empire.”

Perhaps the previous requests for a match that Riccard pointed out are something else entirely, or other customizations of Empire (or Hastile as done for Nebiolo)?
