Heidelberg Palatina Collection

June 10, 2009 • Uli

In my hometown Heidelberg, the gigantic Palatina Collection comprising more than a quarter of a million pages of handwritten manuscripts featuring medieval scripts of the pre-Gutenberg era is freely accessible to the public as of this week.

see http://palatina-digital.uni-hd.de

All scans are zoomable to extremely high precision, and all collections are also downloadable as pdf files.

Link for English speakers:

http://www.ub.uni-heidelberg.de/Englisch/helios/digi/palatina-digital.html
