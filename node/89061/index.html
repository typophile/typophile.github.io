My First Sans ... Just say it !!

January 13, 2012 • mmoustafa

This is my first attempt at a sans serif. I’ve just started and made a few glyphs and kerning to preview it. kindly tell me what do you think.

Before you invest in any kerning make sure you get the balance and the curves right! Look how the horizontal parts of c are thicker than the round part (same goes for a which is only copied from the c – or vice versa, and e and f, and so on … Examine how the stroke is leaving the stem of b: much too thick! There are also big breaks where a curve is ending into a horizontal. There is no smoothness. The endings of c and e will turn out to be much too long when preceding other characters. Above all this is way too constructed. Study typefaces like Bauhaus, for instance, which have similar design principles and see how they work.
