Logo for School Gardens Non-profit

February 26, 2009 • SilasLevi

Hello All, Its my first time posting here after watching for a long time. In my Graphic Design class we created a non-profit organization based on a cause we were interested in and created a name for it. I’m concerned about school nutrition and eco-literacy so my non-profit is one that helps develop school gardens for learning and to provide the schools with fresh foods. The gardens would be run by the students of the schools.

I have created three different logos. I want to exude a playful look without being childish because the company has to get across its mission to the school officials and parents not just the students.

Two are more of symbol like logos and I am still working on them and finding the proper type to go with them. Please let me know your suggestions and otherwise. For the typographic marks I used Plak by Paul Renner, Clarendon, Glypha, and Gill Sans.

I prefer the fourth one (picture 8).

Have you thought about taking the S formed in the logo and creating a typeface (or just the letters needed) based around it for the word Sprout? It would create a stronger relationship between the logo and type.
