Excelsis is similar but not quite identical to Rubens. I remember seeing Rubens on one of the Solotype CD's, probably the Victorian one. Castcraft/Optifont also sells a version. There is a "freeware" version of dubious origin under the name Ruben. I worked on a new digitization of a condensed Rubens-style typeface, based on typography from Disney's Haunted Mansion attractions. It's called Ravenscroft and is freeware. Treehouse also has a Haunted Mansion inspired font called Victorian Mansion.
