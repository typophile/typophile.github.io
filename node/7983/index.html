The obvious pairing would seem to be a Jannon Garamond - look at that "g". In fact the numerals in Stanyan seem to be derived directly from ATF Garamond, with the idiosyncratic slight vertical extensions.

A much more interesting choice however would be a Fleischman, like the fresh-off-the-grill Farnham:
