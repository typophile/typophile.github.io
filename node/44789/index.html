On rags and writing styles

April 29, 2008 • blank

When deciding whether to rag or justify text, how many of you take writing style into account? I’ve found that ragged text can be great for work without a lot of long words, but writing that features longer words can be a real beast to rag well, even with the occasional hyphen. Conversely I find that anything with a lot of proper nouns is a pain to justify because they cannot (usually) be hyphenated. Am I on the right track here?
Makes sense. But in text with short words, the opportunity might exist for achieving well-spaced, barely hyphenated justified blocks of text, which when done well can be things of beauty.
