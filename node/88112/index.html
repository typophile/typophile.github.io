Logos for "The Flight of the Condor" and "Reaching for the Skies"...

December 7, 2011 • Cosmonaut X

Hi, all

I’m trying to replicate the logos for two ’80s TV series, but I’m having trouble identifying the font used.

The first is the font used for the VHS release of “The Flight of the Condor”. It’s quite a distinctive serif, with odd finials etc. but I just can’t place it. The series aired in 1982 on the BBC, and the VHS release this cover is from was - I think - released in the mid-to-late 1980s.

The second is the font used in the title card for “Reaching for the Skies”. At first I thought this was Futura, but the “S” is wrong, and a few other details don’t match. The distortion of the text doesn’t help of course! This was from the original airing of the program on the BBC in the late 1980s.

Any help would be much appreciated!
