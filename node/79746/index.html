Help a ’merican understand: why a ‘ch’ ligature in “Schrift” and “zeichnen” but not “Schönheit”? And why a ‘ch’ lig at all?
2011-07-26 22:18:30 • Stephen Coles

Schönheit and Charakter are letterspaced = bold. Presumably Saskia has no Bold, in the manner of old fraktur, where this convention for emphasis originated. Allegro makes a good bold for this: http://www.identifont.com/show?21W

c is only ever followed by h or k in German, so making these ligatures was an efficiency.
