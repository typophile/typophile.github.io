Have not found an exact match yet but can give you some info about the style. Dynamo Bold Condensed, and similar fonts shown in the link have the same end of stoke wedge style. Dynamo was designed by K. Sommer and released by Ludwig & Mayer in 1930. In your image the style is applied to a square sans. It could easily be recreated.
