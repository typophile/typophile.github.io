Dez Squeeze Pro 7 width family released.

August 26, 2013 • dezcom

Dez Squeeze Pro is a display family in seven bold widths. Choose the width that fits the space available for your headline. Dez Squeeze Pro is a very bold display face with multiple language support, nearly 600 glyphs, stylistic sets, Unicase, and many alternates. Dez Squeeze Pro is Bold enough for knock-out photographs, so go ahead, knock yourself out. Currently on MyFonts but other places soon http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/dezcom/dez-squeeze-pro/
