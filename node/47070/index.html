Outras Fontes releases Jana Thork Bold

July 4, 2008 • outrasfontes

Hi there,

In November 2007 we released a type family called Jana Thork. Since that time that fonts received a very good exposition all over the world, being selected for Tipos Latinos 2008 — the most important type design contest in Latin America.

Now we release Jana Thork Bold & Jana Thok Pro Bold — a complement to this font family that can be used in graphic designs that need more complex hierarchic relations or greater visual impact.

The Bold packages are already avaliable for immediate download after purchase at MyFonts.

Cheers,

– Ricardo Esteves Gomes http://www.outrasfontes.com
