I wrote an e-mail to Radim Pesko and he graciously sent back this reply and image.

“The font is called ‘Larish’ and was indeed drawn specially for Secession identity which was developed together with Willi Schmid, in 2007-2008. Larish will be completed with italic released in Spring 2009.”
