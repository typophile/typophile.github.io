Hi Ryuk, good find. About all I can offer is that this is a creation of Mecanorma during its photolettering years, based most probably on Art Nouveau letters dating to about 1900.But I have drawn a blank on who did the original letters and whether it was ever a pre photolettering font. Nothing in Solopedia, Luc Devroye etc. For more fonts with this flavor I suggest Eckmann, Display Art One, Two & Three [Gerald Gallo] , Metropolitaines , Arnold Böcklin, & Absinthe. Don
