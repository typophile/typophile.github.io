2004-03-17 20:47:31 • John Nolan
No disrespect to John, but you should figure this out: the Art Deco period was mainly during the 1920s and 30s (with a revival c.1970), and characterized by geometric shapes. So categorize the typefaces you have accordingly, and you will come up with a hardcore, authentic choice.

You can find out the dates of the typefaces at the Adobe.com/type website.
