Suggestions for a bitmap typeface (others then Screen Matrix, Knit Map and Folk Art)

April 26, 2007 • Maria de Lurdes

Hello! I’m looking for a bitmap typeface with a construction similar to these ones: Screen Matrix, Knit map and Folk art. I would like it to be more neutral then folk art but more gentle and delicate then Screen Matrix. Do you have any suggestions that might help me? Thanks a lot in advance.
Gretel and FF Nelio might not be what you're looking for, but they are others in this elegant pixel genre. You might also like FF Screenstar which is a simple pixel sans, but a tad more style than FF Screen Matrix. Nick Shinn's Panoptica is also very interesting and Unibody is without doubt the finest free pixel family.
