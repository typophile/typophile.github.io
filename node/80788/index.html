I am not sure what you are looking for. Schoolhouse Fonts offers educational handwriting fonts in the American Cursive style (Zaner-Bloser and D’Nealian). There are variants with guidelines and instructive (not decorative) arrows, indicating order and direction.

A famous penmanship typeface, on the other hand, would be something like P22 Zaner Pro or Compendium.
