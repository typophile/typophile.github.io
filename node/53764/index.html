While I’m not an expert, I have worked in a letterpress shop. AFAIK, there’s nothing specific to design for when designing a font for letterpress anymore than designing a font for any ink-based printing system.

As James mentions, modern letterpress isn’t really the same as the traditional craft.

I do think we need a new term…perhaps lettersmooshed? Or pressmashed? Or more seriously, perhaps we should just call it ‘Embossing with ink’

I admit, I like it. Nothing funner than smashing ink into beer-coaster-weight paper. But yea, it’s not printing in the traditional sense.
