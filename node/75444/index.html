2010-10-20 08:28:37 • Andreas Stötzner

Look for a face with short descenders. It’s also important to have tabular figures that are the same width in different weights. Some would say a foot serif on the figure one is necessary.

Amplitude is a good agate face. Not only does it have the right proportions, but its ink traps keep the image sharp.

I would be remiss not to mention my own Sense, which is presently used for the stock tables in The Globe and Mail.
