'Masthead' Typefaces Released

June 22, 2011 • Greater Albion Typefounders

Our two new ‘Masthead’ typefaces-especially designed to simplify the production of logos, mastheads and signage with character-have now released on Myfonts.com.

[[http://new.myfonts.com/fonts/gatf/bettendorff/ | Bettendorff]] is one of two new ‘Masthead’ typefaces from Greater Albion. This 1900′s inspired face makes the construction of ribbon or cartouche banners and mastheads the simple work of a few moments. Bettendorff is also particularly designed to compliment our Spargo and Mexborough families.

[[http://new.myfonts.com/fonts/gatf/wolvercote/ | Wolvercote]] is one of two new ‘Masthead’ typefaces from Greater Albion. This Victorian inspired face makes the construction of ribbon or cartouche banners and mastheads the simple work of a few moments. Wolvercote is also particularly designed to compliment our Wolverhampton and Mexborough families.
