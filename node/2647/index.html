The font is based on the Avant Garde magazine (hence the name) logotype designed by Herb Lubalin, hence its inferiority to Futura (in my unsolicited opinion). So, it's technically on the cover of Avant Garde, in that its inspiration is there, but they aren't all the same letterforms. For example, the Vs in the logotype have a vertical left extender, rather than an angled one. This would be a fun/ relevant example, at least, if not a strict example.
