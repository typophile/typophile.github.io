John,

TypeTool 2 and ScanFont 4 for Mac from Fontlab Ltd. (http://www.fontlab.com ) should do the trick for you. ScanFont 4 will open an EPS/AI file and automatically separate the letters into separate glyphs, and export it to TypeTool 2, which you can use to build the TrueType font. There's a ScanFont+TypeTool pack for Mac for $199:
http://www.fontlab.com/Font-utilities/FontLab-Quickshop/
Of course, you can choose to get FontLab 4.6 instead of TypeTool 2, but that would be much more expensive
