The Association of Graphic Arts and The Design Studio of Vassiliy Shishkin produces a new international contest-exhibition “The Type”. The Exhibition will take place in St. Petersburg from 21 to 26 of June 2005 at the Artist`s Union of Russia Exhibition Center (B. Morskaja, 38). Works of the Russian and foreign participants, typefaces and type compositions will be presented there.

We would like to invite you to take part in the work of the Exhibition.

All materials presented at “The Type” will be published in The Catalogue of The Exhibition.

Here we attach technical requirements for files, which are to be printed in The Catalogue.

Technical Requirements A participant sends by e-mail a files created in a vector format using one of those programs: Adobe Illustrator, Free Hand, Corel Draw for a press in a catalogue. The participant receives a turn. The format of the catalogue is 240x315 (VERTICAL), black-and-white, the turn is 480x315. On the turn both a font and the letter, some letters or some fonts can be placed. The file in Word format with the brief description of fonts and short information about participant are also required. Works should be send in black-and-white. Texts should be in curves, raster images linked, 4 mm from a cut line. Participants receive one catalogue free.

The Terms. All materials for the catalogue should be given not later then the 10 of June, 2005.

We hope that our offer is of considerable interest to you. Please do not hesitate to write us if you require further information.

Thanking you in advance for the cooperation, Yours sincerely, Vassiliy Shishkin
