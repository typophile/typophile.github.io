I don’t want to start an ideological or sociological debate, but I am afraid this issue will NOT be over until the remote day when drugs become legal. However, although this is very hard in some places (mostly northern cities like Tijuana, Ciudad Juárez or Culiacán), I was born and live in Mexico City and I can tell you that here we don’t have this kind of problem (yet?), so it’s not an issue to be worried during the congress, if that is your concern. Actually, downtown, in which Typ09 will take place, is one of the safest and most beautiful neighborhoods in the city (the so-called Historic Center).

In addition, you may visit some other incredible places near from here: I strongly recommend Puebla and, a little further, Oaxaca (southern city).
