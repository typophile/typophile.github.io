September 25, 2005 • Paul Cutler

I recently received a style guide. This was the opening salvo: The new Sprint logo expresses the joy of choice and flexibility that is the embodiment of our core positioning. The symbol reflects a sense of dynamism, fluidity and forward motion. The proud. gestural form of the symbol also evokes qualities of confidence and optimism. The overall shape of the symbol connotes an arrow head or a bird in flight, a dramatic flourish or a flag waving at the finish line. The symbol reflects convergence and a broad array of customer offerings. The new logo also builds upon the heritage of our company. The symbol is reminiscent of the Sprint "pin drop" and the use of Nextel yellow and black is bold, differentiating and ownable within our category. Here's what they're talking about.
