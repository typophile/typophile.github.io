body: "<strong>[[Indices]] : [[Font Retailers]]/[[Foundries]] : Village</strong>\r\n\r\nVillage
  is based in the East Village of New York City. It is owned and run by [[Chester
  Jenkins|Chester]] and [[Tracy Jenkins]] who are type and graphic designers, respectively.
  The first typeface published under the Village label was [[Galaxie Polaris]], a
  Modernist sans serif family. Village is the 'house brand' and founding member of
  the type collective, vllg.com.\r\n\r\nVillage is a type co-op; the union of eleven
  young type foundries who have decided to go it alone, together. vllg.com is the
  information channel and boutique storefront for this group of equals. The members
  of the co-op hail from [[Canada]], [[Finland]], [[Germany]], [[Greece]], [[Italy]],
  The [[Netherlands]], [[New Zealand]], [[Portugal]], the [[United Kingdom]], and
  the [[United States]].\r\n\r\n<strong>[[Foundries]] and [[Designers]] represented
