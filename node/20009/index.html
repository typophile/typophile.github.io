Hello I am trying to explore what attractive fonts I could use as I design a website for a large commercial printing company. Big facility, lots of beautiful print. They are starting to ramp up their web-to-print abilities, but work conventional commercial printing with great success. I am looking for inspiration of a font in the navigation and body at this point. Love to hear any feedback! And, thanks for your patience. wnf
If its a HTML based website you are / may be pretty limited in choosing typefaces for the body copy. You have to rely on those that come pre installed on most computers. To try and compare the most common types for web, you may want to check the fabulous typetester.maratz.com. As for the navigation / menus, you may want to consider types that stay legible and 'clean', even when used / presented very small, and on screen. ( Like one of those greyscale bitmap / pixel fonts. ) Dav
