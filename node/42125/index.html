Bifur was created by famous Art Deco designer A. M. Cassandre in 1929 for Deberny & Peignot. Cassandre was the pseudonym of Adolphe Mouron 1901-1968. Several versions have been created besides the P22 Bifur (which has a lowercase), Haroldsfonts has one with several color options.
