June 3, 2009 • George Coltart

Hi there.

Ive basically convinced myself this logo is finished, but then again Ive only shown it to a few people so would appreciate any feedback you have.

Its for a group-travel website/app. The “Stormer” in the title is reffering to “brainstorming” however I avoided using imagery that relates to brainstorming as its usually pretty cliche.

So have gone literal on it and used the tornado type of storm which Im pretty happy with.

The typeface is National.

thanks for your time and feedback obviously welcome. George
