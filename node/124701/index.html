There are typos in every part of calt that is meant to sub a ligature with its alternate. In b_s, v_s, and w_s, the underscore is only missing from the ignore line. o_s is the same but uses the full word “substitute”, which I thought was a valid form, but it must be affecting it somehow. From ograve_s onward, the underscore is missing from both lines, which would change “òs” to “òòs” if liga hadn’t gotten to it first. From oacute_s onward, the ignore line is also missing the _s entirely. And the last three are also missing a space before “by”.

Now, you could fix all these lines, OR you could REMOVE them and execute calt before liga, because you have a whole redundant system in place.
