Based on the numbers from one of the posters, it appears to be a modified version of PF DIN Mono: http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/parachute/pf-din-mono/

If you’re only looking to replicate the logotype, Dylan or Dylan Condensed (with a little artificial condensing or expanding), come close: http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/wiescherdesign/dylan/ http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/wiescherdesign/dylan-condensed/

Other similars: http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/mti/felbridge/ http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/linotype/sassoon-infant/
