I recently picked up the entire Kepler Opticals set, and I was wondering what people's opinions of it were. With 168 different fonts, I assume it is safe to refer to this as a Super-Sized package? What kind of uses have you found for it? What kind of uses would you possibly consider it for? My initial impression of it is that it is "classy" like a Modern face, but with a wicked edge. My favorite so far is the Light Extended Display. [img:sites/default/files/old-images/KeplerLED_5226.gif] Any thoughts?
If you have or plan to use Utopia, it makes a surprisingly ideal display companion to that. Kepler has size variants already, but I don't always like all that flavor in the text sizes. Newspaper or magazine layouts would be nice with Utopia as text and Kepler as display.
