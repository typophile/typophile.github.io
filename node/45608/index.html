Almost no design practices have a large or wide enough customer base for their replies to be relevant. Design firms are small and tend to stay local. If you want meaningful numbers, you need to get them from someone who communicates with a lot of printers and can sift through the data on a large scale. To get real answers you need to do some real research and not just ask people for answers online.
