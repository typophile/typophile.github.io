2010-09-12 11:43:15 • cdavidson
Good overall feel, looks especially good at larger sizes. I like the m, o and a especially and the cuts on the m, n, etc. look great. Not entirely keen on the w; I think the right hand stem makes it look too much like an upside down m. The bowl of the uppercase R seems a tiny bit too tall - I'd like to see it maybe a little bit smaller to make it less identical to that of the P (looking at word 'APPROACHES' here). The f seems to interact somewhat awkwardly with the other letters ('fun', 'coffee', 'shuffle) but maybe some looser spacing would help this. Coming along nicely! - Claire
