Lestatic

February 8, 2013 • Choz Cunningham

I am happy to finally announce the release of “Lestatic”, my largest typeface project to date, and one with a slightly more serious take than usual from !Exclamachine. It is available exclusively through [[http://www.myfonts.com/search/Lestatic/fonts/|myfonts.com]]. The collection of 9 families and 27 styles includes a free mashup font to give a taste of the grungier sides of Lestatic. Also, for a limited time, Lestatic Lashed, a more stroked variant, is 75% off!
