There was just a similar sort of question about finding an equivalent serif font to a too familiar design (Times NR). I could give you the equivalent suggestion for Sans fonts -- use the [[http://www.bowfinprintworks.com/SansIDPage.html|Sans Serif Font ID Guide]] and pick some of the Key letters from Corisande and look up the (possibly) similar sans font families with similar structural/stylistic attributes. The Guide catalogs over 900 sans families. - Mike Yanega
