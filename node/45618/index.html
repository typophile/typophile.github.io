There is too many things happening in this logo. First off, go back to designing in black and white, color is one of the last things you deal with in a logo.

First off, you might want to consider picking a different font, especially if you’re “geared towards women”. Second, the weight change for “ress” isn’t necessary and adds little to the meaning, same with the illustrator brush stroke ‘v’.

The tagline also needs reworked, it’s much too small, and looks smashed up under the end of the logo like a wad of clay.

The icon, I assume is a calendar, doesn’t look like a calendar. Pull it out and rework it to resemble a calendar more. This could become the logomark and work side by side with your logotype.

Back to the sketch pad with this one.
