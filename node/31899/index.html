Eduardo Manso has done it again !!!

March 2, 2007 • SuperUltraFabulous

[[Eduardo Manso]] can't seem to make one flop yet. Bohemia (under the name Argot) won a TDC in 2003. Relato Sans and another in TDC award 2006. And now Lorena Serif yet another TDC award winner 2007!!! Here he has done it again with well a executed text system intended for newspapers. I particularly enjoy the efficient use of space and the sturdy lachrymal terminals. ('Georgia' for print???) MyFonts's Jan Middendorp interviewed the 'MAN' for the their February newsletter. Very well done. Check it out: http://www.myfonts.com/newsletters/rs/200702.html#typedesigner Enjoy, Mike Diaz PS 1... I wonder who the other TDC winners are. Any inside info??? PS 2... Is it just me? because the TDC website is ironically one of the ugliest websites ever! They gray on gray color scheme is dated, poor/low res sample showings, pain in the you-know-what navigation, and generally speaking the entire site does not really befit an organization who's aim is to highlight the best in typography.
I've actually long wondered about the TDC site's design. It seems purposefully odd, and I can't quite decide if I like it or not. Largely, I find it difficult to navigate. But maybe there's some history I'm not aware of.
