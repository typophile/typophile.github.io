The serifs are unbracketed. That is a Didot. I'm going to guess Didot from Hoefler & Frere-Jones because the roundness of the interior shapes on the n. It isn't the same shape in Linotype's Didot. Unless, of course, that is Mayeur from James (Montalbano). But I really don't think so as the serifs have no shape or further articulation.
