I am working on a project to catalog movie titles, and one of the things I want to do is include the typefaces that are used. I expect that there will be many that I can’t ID, so I am going to use this board as a resource. If this gets annoying, just ask me to cool it.

My first movie puzzler is Federico Fellini’s 8 1/2. The title itself is probably custom but based on a typeface, and the credits are some type of Clarendon/Egyptian/Italienne that I can’t find. Anyone know better?

I know it looks like it could be a typeface, but I think there is a good possibility that it was custom lettering. It doesn’t look like any typeface that I know, plus the “D” and “I” are noticeably different in the smaller and larger sizes. Notice that the serifs are about the same size, even though the letters aren’t. The two “F"s look different, too.
