I just checked the filmography of Catherine Rouvel (well known French actress) and I couldn’t find a movie that she appeared in with all the cast listed in your poster (not that the filmography is totally complete).

She did star along with Daneille Darc in the famous 1970 film Borsalino, and the 1974 sequel Borsalino and co.

My feeling is that the movie titles were constructed from actual neon signs, therefore not a font at all, but derived from actual neon tubing.

Is the poster in color? Are they actually neon lights? Lest not we forget that many typefaces were actually “inspired” by handpainted signs and letterforms, architecturally sculpted forms, and yes, bright lights and neon!

Throughout the ages, type designers have inspired sign crafters and vice versa… mutual love for letterforms and invention.
