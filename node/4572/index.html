Here are the Google search results for 'Latinskaya typeface'. A typeface that claims to be based on it (Quant Antiqua, from Paratype) is available, but that may be all you can find these days. It has a few resemblances, but many more differences. For example, the a, i, e and y in your sample are quite different in Quant Antiqua. The M is similar, but that is the only u.c. letter in your sample.
