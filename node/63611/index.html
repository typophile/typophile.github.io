Fabian Barral was kind enough to pass on the name of this quirky and confident, squarish display font with the oddly balanced counters seen above. At some point in time, he encountered it on dafont – and indeed it is priceless – but officially it’s free for non-commercial use.

We can find it now strutting about the sunny shores of its home, [[http://www.cape-arcona.com/|Cape Arcona]], where it responds promptly to the name [[http://www.cape-arcona.com/start.php?link=strongman&typo=2|CA Strongman]] by flexing and winking on cue.

Enjoy, and many thanks Mr. Barral!
