================================ NEW LATINOTYPE FONT RELEASE

MOTE, a black-a-licious type.

Mote is the first typeface from Latinotype, a small new foundry based on latinamerican culture & local vernacular lettering.

Mote, a black-a-licious type is a modern interpretation of latinamerican lettering founded in Santiago de Chile “Mote” drink store. The “Mote with Huesillos” is a traditional Chilean drink of sugary teaish water with a dried peach and bulgar wheat. We take this drink as a lovely chilean icon to represent our “Muy Delicious” lettering tradition in yummy tasty letters.

  • A unique & friendly black typeface
  • Up to 300 yummy glyph caps!
  • Opentype / Western European ready
  • Postcript flavoured curves

More information: http://www.latinotype.com

I’m going to spend the next month showing up for meetings just so I can try to get someone to spec this typeface for a job. Because, it’s, ya know, FAB!
