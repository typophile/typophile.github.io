[img:sites/default/files/old-images/3willows cover_3496.jpg] I also found this book, The Joy Luck Club. The title is so familiar, but I've never read it. I love the cover. It is simple and elegant and I like it, and even though stacking type is a type crime I love how the author's name makes the cover asymmetrical. [img:sites/default/files/old-images/joy luck_4577.jpg]
