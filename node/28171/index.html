Graphically, the mixture of OSF and LF in your example looks off. It should be consistent, either OSF or LF. Imagine if this person were talking to you, giving the same info that is pictured. They would begin talking in, say a Southern accent, telling you their name, title and "516." Then all of a sudden, mid-sentence, they begin to speak with a heavy "Nu Yawk" accent. Sure, they're still speaking the same language, but you'd still be looking at them thinking, "What the?!"
