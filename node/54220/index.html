Helvetica Ligatures

January 31, 2009 • grntwlkr

I was curius what helvetica would look like with some ligatures, and couldn’t find any examples, so I decided to make my own. I made two versions, one is more linear based on the forms of the t and the second is more curvy based on the top of the s. I understand that this is purely ornamental, but I wanted to do some experimenting.

http://www.typeandglyph.com/2009/01/deservedly-rare/

Has anyone else seen any good examples of helvetica ligatures.

I think the first version is very interesting but the second one is keeping with the typeface better. Cool stuff.
