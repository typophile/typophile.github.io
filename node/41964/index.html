Well, yes, that could be done by someone good with outline manipulating. I’m not advocating it, but I think you see my point about how much this resembles Perpetua. There is another Gill face called [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/grouptype/aries/|Aries]] that is also similar, but less so.

In truth, having some idea of the artists available to Tiffany’s, I wouldn’t be surprised if someone working for them drew the word by hand, to make a more elegant version of those Perpetua letters. It’s easy to forget how many skilled lettering artists are still around, although some of them are regulars here.

Along these lines I was hoping to find that Dennis Ortiz-Lopez had done a Light Perpetua, but I could not find that among the types shown on [[http://www.ortiz-lopez.com/|his site]]. He has done this for several other classic typefaces, but I didn’t see this one.
