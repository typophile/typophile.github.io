I have jumped into fontographer and managed the basics, a tip – when transferring the glyphs from illustrator to fontographer is to have a box of the same size around each character (probably based on cap M with ascender and descender heights and slightly larger), otherwise fontographer will scale each character, remember to delete the boxes after importing. Good luck, I’m sure that more qualified users will be able to help with most problems, especially in the Build forum.

If the initial item going to press doesn’t require a lot of text using this font (a couple of short lines, maybe), you could just place the Illustrator images of the necessary letters in your layout and kern by shifting their boxes. Of course you’ll still want to make a proper font of it, but this might be a quickie method to meet your press deadline for the moment.
