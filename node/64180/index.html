Karmaform — a voluptuous typeface design.

November 12, 2009 • dsgnco

Karmaform is a series of experimental, robust type designs by Jeremy Smith. It started with a couple of years of simple studies, random sketches and a lot of patience. It is my first attempt to design a display typeface. This Friday 13th (TOMORROW), I will close the availability to download {kf} I really need to let the dust settle on this experiment, as I have many more things planned in the upcoming months involving type! So on Friday at noon, I will open up this site for everyone to comment, love it or hate, I am sticking my neck out to hear what you have to say about Karmaform. Until then, feel free to download the font if you havent already. Karmaform will only be available for a few more days! Visit http://karmaform.com to download now!
