By Wayne J. Stettler, 1965 for VGC. There is a font out there called GT-Stetler (yes, one t) that is only the caps of Stettler. Not much help, but that is the only digitization I’m aware of.
2014-06-16 13:09:32 • Mike F
If you are looking for substitutes you could use Identifont for Optima http://www.identifont.com/find?font=optima&q=Go and go through the similar fonts. Intersting ones include Cyrus Highsmith’s Amira, Thierry Puyfoulhoux’s Alinea Incise, José Mendoza y Almeida’s Pascal, Gábor Kóthay’s Minerva Modern, and Hermann Zapf’s Optima aka Classico or Zapf Humanist 601.
