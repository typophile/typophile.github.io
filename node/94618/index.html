Further reading and links: Behance http://bit.ly/kabehance MyFonts http://bit.ly/kacallista http://www.kateliev.com/images/TYPOGaytan.png Gaytan Sans & Serif Short Description: Gaytan (Bulgarian for braid) is a fresh new insight on archaic letter forms. A family of two unicase typefaces – a modern looking sans and more classic looking serif, equipped with many alternates, so they can suit any typographic taste.
