More "Caslonish" to me (adding an outline stroke and reworked to make it very contrasted)... Very close to [[http://typography.com/fonts/font_overview.php?productLineID=100020|Requiem (Fine Roman)]] by Hoefler & Frere Jones. Here are also some "similars": - [[http://www.fontshop.com/fonts/singles/monotype/perpetua_titling_roman|MT Perpetua Titling]] by Eric Gill - [[http://www.fontshop.com/fonts/singles/carter_cone/big_caslon_cc|Big Caslon]] by Matthew Carter (Carter & Cone) - [[http://www.dardenstudio.com/typefaces/freight_big|Freight Big]] by Joshua Darden - [[http://typography.com/fonts/font_overview.php?productLineID=100031|Chronicle (Display Extra-Light]] by Hoefler & Frere Jones
