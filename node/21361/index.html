I would probably start with indesign in this case. First get all the text in there and then play around with the general layout. Make a couple of different layouts and compare them. after you are happy with the text layout you can get ps in there (or illustrator or flash..whatever you use) to add navigational items. But I think it’s most important to have a clear view of the plain text first before being distracted by images and things. dav.
