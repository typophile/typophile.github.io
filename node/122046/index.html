The closest to Face Photosetting’s Stack is the font by Bright Ideas called Staxx, which was found by Ryuk. Like Stack, Staxx has three layers, a shaded effect, and sharp corners. Stroke thickness and spacing between stacks are similar. Letter-forms for the capital letters are generally similar but the numbers are quite different. Major differences in the capital letters include the lack of a spur on G, angled instead of horizontal terminals on S, the curved stroke and terminal angles on C and G, and the bottom stroke on J. Comparison was made to the Stack specimen in my Letraset Product Manual. See the scan I posted in http://typophile.com/node/107360 Unfortunately, Staxx is unusable for commercial purposes because it is an orphan font by a defunct company, and there does not seem to be any legal owner of the intellectual property in its digital fonts.
