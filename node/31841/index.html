2007-02-28 16:46:42 • aluminum
I’ve always thought of it as the “miser” 3. Long ago and far away (as the story goes), there was a merchant who had to travel a great deal. One night he stayed in an inn, and while the food was mediocre, there was a beautiful painting on the wall. When he got home and mused about the journey, he thought of that beautiful painting. “Why,” he thought “I’d pay 70 colonmonetaries for it.” He decided to write the innkeeper and make an offer to buy the painting. As he wrote, he was halfway through writing the “7” (they wrote slower long ago & far away), he thought “maybe he’d take half that,” so he changed the “7” into a “3”.
