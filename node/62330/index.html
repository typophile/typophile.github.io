2009-09-24 14:32:10 • Solt

ballpoints glide over the surface nearly without friction.

That depends heavily on the quality of the ballpoint. The kind of cheap disposable pens that school children are often given to use do not glide. Rather, the glutinous ink makes the ball sticky such that it doesn’t roll where directed by the writer and, instead, rolls where the friction of the ink directs it.
