Angelos Vertetios?

July 26, 2009 • nstockallen

I’ve lost my source that names calligrapher Angelos Vertetios as the inspiration for Estienne when he designed the Grec du roi circa 1540…Garamond later cut the punches. If you are aware of Vertetios (or his proper name) and a source can you let me know? Thanks.
Angelos Vergetios or Vergikios, depending on the source, of which there are several. Proctor (1900, I think) and Scholderer (1927) are fairly standard. Your university library should have them. You could also trust your eyes: there are very good images of the grec-du-roi online, and there are anthologies of Greek scribal hands in print (I can’t recall off the top of my head; I’m in a different country from my library).
