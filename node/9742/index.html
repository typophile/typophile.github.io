Hi all,
I am looking for the font used for "regret" in the bottom right-hand corner here: http://www.mediacampaign.org/mg/print/ad_present.html

There are many of these Anti-Drug ads so I imagine that it is actually a font and not hand-drawn.
I seem to recall that we may have had this question once before, but I hunted anyway. I searched all of Part 11 and Part 12 of the Script Font ID Guide. I found a number of very similar styles, but didn't see an exact match in over 500 samples.

Now it's time for my 'standard' reply about such questions: basically, it is so simple and cheap to make a font like this, that any agency that wants a unique look, just needs a pen, a scanner or fax machine and a computer. For $9 you can send a sample of your printing and have a font made.

This means that looking to identically match fonts like this is generally a waste of time. You are better off to look through samples at any of the sites that have hand-printed fonts (mine are samples only - no font downloads - you look up the source in my Reference List and get them from the supplier), and find something similar to the one you are trying to match.
