Stephen -- Clarification: The going rate for FB fonts is $40 per weight for singles and through resellers. Font Bureau's own direct Retail site gives quantity discounts. So at 3–5 styles the per font cost goes down to $35 (thus the $175 rate quoted above), and 6 or more styles drop to $30 per. Further discounts above 16 styles. These are the single workstation rates, and styles may be mixed across families. (More info at http://www.fontbureau.com/help/quickpricingchart) -- K.
