Greater Albion Typefounders

December 30, 2008 • Rasendyll

Greater Albion Typefounders' biography on MyFonts.com says: "Whither elegance in this modern world?" Greater Albion Typefounders has been established to do a little bit to overcome one of the many crises facing the world today. We (The Typefounders) can't make a dent in global warming; we don't know where to start with the Global Financial Crisis, but we thought we'd ‘have a bash at our own little bit of the crisis in design. To us, modern design all seems so bland. Sure, it works, sometimes it even works well. But there’s seldom a sense of style, seldom a hint of elegance unless you call severe blandness ’[elegant simplicity', which isn't a line of thinking that ever impressed us. So we decided to do something about it- we decided to bring you new Typefaces that honor the spirit and style of the past. Want to be right up to date with a brand new typeface, but right in tune with the spirit of designers trained in classical ascetic values? Then why not try us and see what we have to offer. What we will offer is new designs, replete with Edwardian Fun, Victorian distinction, or any other piece of elegance we can manage. Come along for the ride!" Hopefully this captures the spirit of this foundry, newly established in 2008, by Paul J Lloyd, previously known as a prolific designer of freely released fonts. As at December 2008 they are offering the following families of faces: - Absinette - Adantine - Anavio - Arkwright - Bamberforth - Bertoni - Bonaventura - BonaVia - Bonning - Bonnington - Braxia - Bromwich - Bronzino - Brossard - Brosse - ButtonFaces - Chapter Initials - Cherritt - Chiara Script - Chipperly - Chipping - Christmas Fleurons - Cirflex - Clementhorpe - Clunic - Coronard - Corton - Corvone - Crewekerne - Crewekerne Magna - Crewekerne Magister - Crucis Ornaments - Cullion - Daub - DoodleBirds - Easter Fleurons - Eldridge - Endymion - Federal Streamliner - Fleete - Gildersleeve - Goldbarre - Granville - Great Bromwich - Halloween Fleurons - Haymer - Helenium - Howlett - Jonquin - Keynsia - Larchmont - Lowndes - Luscombe - Magdalene Sans - Mellin - Merry Fleurons - Merry Snowmen - Mexborough - Morover - Movella - Oxonia Roman - Pardon Me Boy! - Paveline - Plebia - Sabinard - Sabio - Svengali Roman - Slatterine - Stannard - Syondola - Tectura - Terazza Tiling - Tumbletype - Valentine's Fleurons - Vectis - Veneribe - Vertrina - Wellingborough - Whitehaven - Woodruff - Zenia Greater Albion Typefounders currently maintains a blog [[http://gatf.wordpress.com|here]] where new releases and special offers are announced.
