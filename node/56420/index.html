Scarlett , I designed that cover and honestly I don’t recall all the fonts used. I’ll give it a try: Balthazar is the logotype and it is based on Akzident Grotesque bold extended Keith Mc Nelly is AT Sackers Clarkson Potter is FF Bodoni Chancery Cookbook I think is Geometric Bold Condensed (not sure) by is definitely Bickham Script Some of the ornaments are Arabesque Ornaments (I think next to Balthazar) and next to the is FF Bodoni Ornaments The other ornaments are from the Dover book of Charles Derriey “Borders, Frames and Decorative Motifs from the 1862 Derriey Typographic Catalog”.
