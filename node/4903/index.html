It's very Melior-like, but it's not Melior. Renault is also similar. I suspect they were custom designed for the ratings board when they were introduced in the late 1960s. This poster, which shows all of them together, seems to support this. The ratings that were added later--NC-17 and the 13 in PG-13--don't match the style. In the early years of the system, there was also X, and, for a short time, M and GP.
