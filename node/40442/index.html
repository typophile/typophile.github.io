1970s german newspaper font...

December 30, 2007 • krautland

gotta love xmas visits home… the family found an old local newspaper in the isolation of their home while renovating and thought they’d keep it for me. I posted the pictures below to my flickr account and hope the links will work.

anyone recognize the headline font? I find the “ß” and “f” in here quite beatiful. here is a closeup.

and just for fun - check out this W. is that an infinity symbol in there?

(couple more shots in my flickr stream, if you like)

The ß looks like Bodoni Antiqua from Berthold (see attached sample from the Guide). However the ‘f’ doesn’t match. Most Bodonis have a flat-topped ’t', but your newspaper doesn’t – also like Bodoni Antiqua. It’s hard to see if the same type was used for the text, as well as the headlines, but you might be able to use the Serif Font ID Guide if you have a good look at the letters.
