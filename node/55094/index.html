70s or 80s Display Face

February 22, 2009 • tylor.reimer

In the area where I live there is a store that has a sign with a typeface I can’t for the life of me figure out. I’m pretty sure the sign was created in the 70s or 80s. The closest typeface I can find is Stop (http://new.myfonts.com/fonts/linotype/stop/) but it doesn’t have lowercase letters. Maybe a non-digital version did? I’m not sure.

I always just noticed yesterday it was used on the cover of the Atari game Space Jockey.

It’s an old Letraset face called Motter Tektura, designed by Othmar Motter around 1974. The only digital version appears to be Motter Tektura Cyrilic. The digitization is poor, but it’s free. Motter Tektura was also the face used for the original Apple logo (not counting that drawing of Newton they used briefly).
