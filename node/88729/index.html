BBC Radio series on the writing

January 1, 2012 • John Hudson

Starting Monday 2 January, BBC Radio 4 is running a week long special edition of In Our Time devoted to the history of writing. Sure to be fascinating. http://www.bbc.co.uk/programmes/b018wfsc
Thanks for the heads up. I'm a huge fan of In Our Time, which for the uninitiated is a radio programme which invites experts to discuss topics chosen from virtually any field of knowledge from culture and history to philosophy and science. They've improved access to their [[http://www.bbc.co.uk/radio4/features/in-our-time/archive/|huge archive on the website]] so that you can listen to any of the past broadcasts on such topics as the Library at Nineveh, John Wyclif, Antarctica, and Language and the mind. I recommend checking them out.
