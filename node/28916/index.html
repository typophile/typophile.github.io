2006-10-19 09:19:44 • brampitoyo
Nice shapes! However: - the V is too fat; - the thin stems of A, N and Δ are slightly too heavy; - the pointed below of the N extends a bit too far below the base line; - try to straighten the inner contour of the thick stem in the bowls of B, P and R (i.e. no backeted serif at the right); this doesn't need to be done elsewhere (e.g. in D), because there is enough white space around; - the same goes for the inner serifs of W; - make the bowl of G bigger so that the upper serif optically matches the width of the character in the lower half; - compensate for ink squash / blackness in the joint of the Q tail with the bowl; - the curled, tilde shaped cross bar of E and F is somwhat disturbing; if you want to keep that feature, make sure this matches the cross bars in Ð, Θ and ł.
