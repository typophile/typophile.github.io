Many fonts include glyphs for the basic ASCII characters in addition to the characters of the writing system they are primarily intended to support.

So whether you see characters in Sinhalese script or Latin characters depends on what text you type to be displayed in the font.

Sinhala MN, I see, is one of the standard fonts included with OS X 10.6 and greater.
