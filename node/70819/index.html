2010-05-31 11:21:10 • dberlow

Motion Graphic Design and Fine Art Animation: Principles and Practice & Motion Graphic Design: Applied History and Aesthetics

Motion graphics design as a whole doesn’t delve into type motion specifically. The same key techniques used in creating the aesthetics of any object in motion is applied to text, as single objects. There should be a sub discipline dedicated to type in motion, but the readability of the text is left to the judgment of the designer. It can’t be too fast, too blurry, or too small. But those rules depend on the context of the project. There are a few specific books out there, but they just reiterate motion design principles and not specifically outline special techniques for text.
