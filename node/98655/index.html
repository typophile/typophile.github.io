Typographica.org has a good review on the Calibre typeface that should be helpful in your search… it would be useful to frame questions not only with “what is similar”… but also with: “the design intentions to fulfill”. As an example, Calibre is in a similar genre as “signage” types such as FF Din and Interstate…
