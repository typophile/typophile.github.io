Anyway this is what he said…

Dear Hubert,

Thank you for your kind note.

This image is a random image taken from a collaborative project with artists from both Switzerland and Egypt, realised in Cairo in 2005. It is a visual essay about the reality of the egyptian capital, a megacity of the third world, which resulted in a small but substantial photographic book with more than 400 pages, called “Do you cake?”. The cryptic title refers to the equally cryptic question of a hotel maid asked at breakfast one morning in a Cairo hotel, when she was about to serve the usual tea and cake.

It is a little-known project that I am quite fond of. I attach a jpg of the front cover. The book is available on request through Pro Helvetia in Cairo, in case you would like to obtain a copy:

Pro Helvetia Cairo Swiss Arts Council Hebba Sherif Director c/o Embassy of Switzerland 10, Abdel Khaleq Tharwat Street, EG-Cairo, Egypt Tel. +202 257 74 100, Fax +202 257 92 267 E-mail: cairo@prohelvetia.org.eg

Hope this answers your question! Best regards,

Cornel Windlin
