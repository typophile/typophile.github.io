2008-02-04 22:56:44 • cerulean

Yeah, the recognizable part of a typeface (glyphs in this case) is the upper part. There is a classic test, where they cut off either the bottom half of a line of type or the upper half. ANd then let people read these lines. Most of the recognition aspects are in the upper parts.

Personal opinion: cut off at baseline does it for me.
