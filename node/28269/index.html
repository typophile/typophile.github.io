I see the yellow part first. It really was never obvious enough to me that those were a 2 and a 7. I just now realised that the two are a mirror flip of each other. I am not sure what that square is for. I would like to see something joining the 2 and 7 together more, like a square wrapping around them together rather than inside them. The 2006 is okay.

Overall it just feels too busy. It’s like you got three great ideas and combined them into one logo. Try to pick the best one and maximise it, whether that be the s-7 symmetry, the cut off letters, or the color, or the square thing, whatever that is.

-sam
