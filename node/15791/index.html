Is that a standing corporation? As in stationary? It should be stationery, as in: Middle English staciouner, a bookseller, from Medieval Latin statinrius, shopkeeper (as against a peddler), probably from Latin stati, statin-, place of business. (http://www.bartleby.com/61/92/S0719200.html) A frequent mistake, almost as frequent as spelling seperate, rather than separate. Latin separare, to divide (no source, just my memory).
