I second the DIN suggestion, but would refine it to DIN Neuzeit Grotesk (for the middle and right): http://www.fontshop.com/fonts/downloads/linotype/din_30640_neuzeit_grotesk_std_light/ot_ps/specimen/tester/

Bold condensed of the same font for the label on the left.

The original version of this typeface was designed in 1928 by Wilhelm Pischner for the D. Stempel Foundry, and was a staple of the moveable type and photo-lettering eras.
