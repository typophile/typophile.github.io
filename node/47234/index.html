So Here is the Brief:

  • We need a nice text for something close to 500 characters for an invitation.
  • The style of the text we need is similar to: this one: http://www.veer.com/products/typedetail.aspx?image=PTT0000041#specimen or this one: http://www.veer.com/products/typedetail.aspx?image=UMT0000104

If you want to make it an effective selection process, what I’ll need will be:

  • How much will you charge for this work
  • 2 samples of a phrase. One with the style we are looking for, and other in case you have a style that fit’s this project and you feel more comfortable about.

the sample phrase is: “Jackdaws love my big sphinx of quartz.”
