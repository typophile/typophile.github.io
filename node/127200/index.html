The Beatles, “She Loves You” 7-inch cover – what fonts are used?

November 21, 2017 • thomaskyhn

Does anyone recognize any of the three fonts that are used on the cover of The Beatles’ “She Loves You” 7-inch (SWAN S-4152)? I’ve tried various font identification websites, but none of their matches come close.

THE BEATLES is Filmotype Washington, which sadly has not had a proper digital revival: http://luc.devroye.org/fonts-27506.html [search the page for a sample]

OPTI-Castcraft had a bootleg version of it, but use with extreme caution: http://opti.netii.net/fonts-w/index.htm

Dave Bailey did a much better revival, but it doesn’t appear to have been released yet: https://dave-bailey.squarespace.com/typeface-design/#/filmotype-washington/
