2010-04-13 16:02:31 • Tim Ahrens

If you want to use the fonts, unmodified, for serious bookwork, Charter is the one to use. Charis, though, has fantastic potential, with its extensive character set. And modification, esp. for one’s own use, is allowed by the license.

I picked Charis to use for a recent book because Charter was designed when the print workflow was repro-negative-plate, and to my eye, comes out a bit light when printed CTP (computer to plate) on a high-resolution offset press. Charis likely will print better.

I did do a fair bit or re-working of the fonts, including old-style numbers, setwidth changes (esp. in italic), and kerning. I haven’t seen the printed book yet. If you’re interested, re-post in a month or so & I can let you know how it did.
