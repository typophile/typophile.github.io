For 1 I’d search under “stencil” on myfonts.com. The “MARKETING” could be Larabie’s [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/larabie/gunplay/|Gunplay]].

2 will most likely have been hand drawn, & the original ink work would probably look like twigs & branches. You might find something similar in a Dover Books Victorian collection.

3 looks like it is based on the Coca Cola logo. I’m sure various people have done freeware fonts based on the latter but they would probaly need some tidying up if used as a logo.
