2006-10-25 11:58:46 • kjeld

The more you print the less limited it becomes. If you think you have something which collector’s will want then you should print fewer and spend more on the production of it.

One example of this is the book about Gudrun Zapf von Hesse. Her book was printed in 3 varying ways, three different quanitities and three different prices. Price for each was aimed at a different kind of collector or bibliophile.
