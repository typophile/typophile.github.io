Hi Reimund-

My first instinct was Gothic by Red Rooster (http://new.myfonts.com/fonts/redrooster/gothic-rr/), but it’s too wide, and the K and S are both wrong. You mentioned Hoefler’s Knockout, which it’s not, but Champion Featherweight (also by Hoefler, http://typography.com/fonts/font_overview.php?productLineID=100002) is better, the K, S and " are very close indeed.

-NJ
