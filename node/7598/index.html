Wow.

And I was just thinking about how long it would take for a program to search each and every document on my computer for font tags.

I think Suitcase, Font Reserve, ATM Deluxe, or some like program is probably a better choice. You can de-activate fonts and then have them auto-activate whenever you open a document where it's required.

Plus, you get a better way to organize and scroll through your typefaces. Just because you're not using a font now doesn't mean you won't ever use it.

It also saves you from moving typefaces out of your Fonts folder and then having to put them back whenever their service is once again required.

I'm not sure which font organizer is best. Perhaps others in this forum will be able to inform you better about the pros and cons of your choices.
