In fact FontFont IS embedding-friendly for apps, documents, and even devices.

§ 2.2 of FF’s EULA requires a subsetted font used in a secure format for permitted non-editable embedding. As Sii already mentioned, no format is absolutely secure, but as a foundry you have to define your understanding of security. So ‘secure’ means here: encrypted which may be related to the font file and/or the document containing the embedded font file. Formats with encryption/protection of the whole document are allowed as the fonts are protected within the document. Flash is such a format.
