Hi Nick

Having a guess at what is happen here, it could be that the ‘swsh’ feature may be happening before ‘smcp’ and/or ‘c2sc’. You probably want all the standard replacements to happen first - then have the swash ligatures lookups act on what’s there after. Also if changing text with swash ligs into small caps, you may need to decompose the swash ligs back to standard glyphs in a separate lookup before applying smcp lookups.
