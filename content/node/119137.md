body: "Tom Carnase and Ronne Bonder designed a very simplified blackletter called
  ITC Honda in 1970.\r\nA modified version of the font is used in two books, published
  in 1977 and 1980.  I spotted differences in _f_, _t_, and _s_. There are probably
  others.\r\n[img:sites/default/files/old-images/ITCHondaModified_f_t_s_1sm_5875.jpg][img:sites/default/files/old-images/ITCHondaModified_f_t_s_2sm_3858.jpg]\r\nSquadron/Signal
  Publications Aircraft No. 30 1977 ISBN: 089747029X\r\nSquadron/Signal Publications
  Aircraft No. 44 1980 ISBN: 0897471067\r\nThe changes could perhaps be the result
  of  editing by the cover designer,  but I would be very interested to learn if there
  was an alternate version of ITC Honda, perhaps under another name.\r\nLarger scale
  images are attached.\r\nThanks.\r\nDon"
