There's always some type on eBay, but:
1) The keywords vary, especially among people who are selling stuff they don't understand. Some people for example use the term "hot stamping" to mean lead type. :-/
2) Be careful of crappy ones. Like there's this guy who sells them heaped in plastic bags...

Here's something decent:
http://cgi.ebay.com/ws/eBayISAPI.dll?ViewItem&category=46735&item=3849892871&rd=1&ssPageName=WDVW

Also: besides avoiding Helvetica outright, I would recommend staying away from very small sizes. Not because they're useless, but because they might sap your passion for typesetting. I know I personally hate fidgeting with the really small stuff.
