2010-01-03 13:45:19 • leonard eisenhut
Hi Noël, it certainly is not Eagle Black. This is very likely lettered, i.e. not a font. Compare the two ‘E’ in the top and bottom spread: not the same. The ‘L’, ‘T’, ‘S’ – how would they go together in one typeface?. This is inspired by 1930s constructivist/futurist lettering. A digitally available typeface that shares some characteristics is, in addition to the aforementioned Mostra Nueva, Eagle – see its ‘C’ and ‘N’ in particular. F
