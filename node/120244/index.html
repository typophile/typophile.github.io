Reminds me of F. H. Ernst Schneidler’s “Schneidler-Mediaeval mit Initialen” aka Schneidler Initials released in 1936. But manually widened and with some editing cvhanges: clipping the tip on the bottom rhs of N, replacing the leg on R and widening the bowl, clipping the stem on the bottom rhs of U. Compare the image to the Schneidler Initials digitized by Jason Castle as Shàngó https://www.myfonts.com/fonts/castletype/shango/ or as Shàngó Gothic Gothic https://www.myfonts.com/fonts/castletype/shango-gothic/ Don
