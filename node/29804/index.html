Sudtipos redesigned website

November 29, 2006 • Ale Paul

SUDTIPOS //// NEWS

We are pleased to announce the launch of a new, fully redesigned Sudtipos web site, with many features and new additions.

The new site was built to accommodate the showing and interactivity with the many fonts we have developed over the past three years. The reconfigured interface now includes a rollover list that quickly previews each font in the Sudtipos collection.

Another important addition to the site is the Sudtipos Probador™, our fully interactive type tester that allows users of the site to try any Sudtipos font on screen before licensing.

Two new sections were added, one to showcase the use of Sudtipos fonts by the general public, and one to preview upcoming fonts and projects in development.

We hope you enjoy the new Sudtipos web experience. Please direct any feedback to sudtipos@sudtipos.com
