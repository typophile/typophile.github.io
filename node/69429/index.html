Thick & Thin, experimental font in progress

April 14, 2010 • intermalte

Hello together!

I would like to get some crits on my new “Thick & Thin”-Font. It is relatively experimental and readability seems to be limited. I think the construction rules are quite obvious: Each letter consists of one “thick” simple shape and one thin line. The font is not yet finished, some symbols are missing and I’m not happy with some letters.

I like it. Quirky and a little bit retro.
