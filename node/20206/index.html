FogLamp 2 Released by Fontlab

May 25, 2006 • tharrson

Fontlab Ltd. announced the release of the second version of FogLamp, its conversion tool for Fontographer .fog files. The big feature of the new version is the ability to convert .fog files directly to OpenType font files - either .ttf or .otf. In addition a number of bugs have been fixed. There is no change in the price of the product (US$79), and users of version 1 can upgrade for US$19.95. Further information at http://fontlab.com/Font-tools/FogLamp/
