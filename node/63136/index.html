Typeradio asks: What would you like to ask Adrian Frutiger & Buro Destruct?

October 15, 2009 • Zara Evens

On behalf of the fine folks at [[Typeradio]] (Akiem, Liza and Donald), we would like to pose these questions to the community: What would you like to ask [[Adrian Frutiger]]? What would you like to ask Büro Destruct? Please use this thread to compile your questions. *Late edit. See Fontblog.de for more info and questions. I believe the interview takes place Friday Oct 16 in Europe, so any questions posted after the fact may not be usable.
To Frutiger: 1. How is your approach to a sans different from to a serif? Do different standards apply? You said you are a 'sans mensch', in contrast to Hermann Zapf. What does a 'sans mensch' have to particularly sensitive to? 2. In your interview posted on the Linotype site, you gave standards for ideal weight of strokes for a text face, and ideal letter spacing. Do you have any second thoughts about these? Have your views on these changed over time? Generally, what have you changed your mind about as regards letter design during your career? 3. You said that "activating the whites" is important in your approach to design. Could you explain what you are looking for when you look at the whites, and how this makes a difference to a design? Could you give some examples of glyph or designs that you think are hurt by lack of attention to whites? Ones that are particularly good? 4. Finally what is your view of the many designs that have been influenced by Frutiger, the typeface?
