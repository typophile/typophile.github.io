Looking to ID the main title’s font, doesn’t look custom to me at least. Thanks.![](/images/old-images/Screen Shot 2014-11-09 at 5_6588.33.42 PM.png)

Tallington Modern by Letterhead Fonts appears to be a decent match for THE PROFESSIONAL: http://www.letterheadfonts.com/fonts/tallington.php

LÉON is not the same typeface, however. House Gothic Condensed Black seems to work: http://www.houseind.com/search/?search=House%20Gothic%20Black

The movie came out in 1994. House Gothic has been around since 1993, but Tallington has a copyright date of 2005. If the title sequence is from the the original release of the film, it must share an original typeface source with Tallington….
