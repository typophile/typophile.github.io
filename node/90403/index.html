I have several versions of Highway and Roadgeek, but it doesnt seem to be either one. It looks really similar though. Does anyone know what it might be. My new customer is redoing unit numbers on his entire fleet of vehicles and these were the only three digits that he had left. Any help is much appreciated.

it looks like a cut of Blue Highway Bold by Ray Larabie (Typodermic Fonts)

You can download the current version at: http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/larabie/blue-highway-2/ http://typodermicfonts.com/blue-highway-50

There was some (older?) more wide version which matches 100% your 3 letters sample, but I think you may also achieve a good match by expanding a bit the current version.
