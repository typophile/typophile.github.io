2009-05-18 07:13:44 • Ray Larabie

Let me predicate this by saying that I love H&FJ types, and I’ve given them more money than I can afford as a freelancer. But…

There’s an elephant in the Sentinel’s room. Is anyone else disappointed that small caps are not included? We know how well a Clarendon can set real small caps by the few cuts out there that do include them.

I’m not saying I know better; H&FJ are total experts (and I’m sure they weighed the pros and cons of including small caps). I’m just saying if you’re going to add yet another version of Clarendon (even if it is one of the best) and go all out with italics and all, it should contain small caps, too.

Maybe they’ll be released at a later date (but I don’t believe updated releases are their style. Not sure).

All that said, I happen to think Sentinel is gorgeous and I still adore H&FJ.
