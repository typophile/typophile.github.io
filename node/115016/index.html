Agree with Don on customization, not on base. Can't be Franklin Gothic to me, mainly because of /G and /O external (left) outline which looks like a straight line on your sample and curved on FG. To me, that's either [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/linotype/trade-gothic|Trade Gothic]] (Condensed) Bold or [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/linotype/alternate-gothic-lt|Alternate Gothic]] #3.
