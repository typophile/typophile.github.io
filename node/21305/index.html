For a few years now, type designer extraordinare Christian Schwartz has been allowing Pinball Publishing to use his fonts, often pre-commercial release, for their literary journal eye~rhyme. Any of the volumes are a nice addition to your typographic library, as well as being well-written compendiums of short fiction and poetry. The latest one utilizes Schwartz’s Luxury for the headlines. Take a gander here: http://pinballpublishing.com/
