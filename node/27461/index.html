I like the outlined better. I just wonder if there’s any logic to the breaking of the letters? First I wanted to make some sort of autogram. I decided to take the whole name instead, and hook the letters onto each other. But then some characteristics would disappear: let say, the 't' for example, didn't function as a 't', could be an 'l' as well. By deleting some pieces, that problem was resolved. Anyway, I wanted to keep the autogram idea, it must be a fluent integration of letters, open and friendly. And how are you going to print it? Well, yeah, I still have to find out. ;-)
