Well, what do you mean off my a few MM? Is the page size you set up the same as the page size you are having printed? The software shouldn't be off by anything. It sounds perhaps more like a communication issue between you and the printer. As for page creep, when you saddle stitch a publication, due to the thickness of the paper, the insise pages 'creep' out away frm the spine more so than the outside/cover pages. This is called the creep, and is trimmed off at the end. If you don't account for it, you will notice your page margins shrinking as you get to the center of the magazine/book. Technically, the signature in the center is going to be narrower than the signature used for the cover.
