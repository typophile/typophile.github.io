2005-01-12 06:09:20 • James Gareth
Not that I want discourage anyone from considering Changeling, but…

There was a VGC display font from the 1970s called China. I don't know of any authorized digitizations, but there is a version called Camp Caps which seems to be the one that most people are using when you see this font. It's not a very good digitization (some of the characters are badly distorted). It's easy to identify by the squashed 2 and some other earmarks.

Changeling is my redesign/remix/expansion of China.
