Once upon a time.. Just kiddin

I made a Kids magazine time ago. I can give you these tips. 1.- use a one-column layout. kids gets losed in multiple columns… 2.- use a geometric font (i like Futura, have a lot of weights). why geometric?, becouse the ‘a’ and ‘g’ glyps. Some kids can’t recognize or get confused with the shapes of different fonts. 3.- Use font in large size: 12 and up… 4.- Most important: BE CAREFULL, even more than other designs. Kids believe everything you publish, so take a time to check everything. 5.- EVEN most important: HAVE FUN. (i miss a lot that magazine)

Hope it helps
