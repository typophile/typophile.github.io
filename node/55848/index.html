March 14, 2009 • phrostbyte64

I managed to delete my first post offering a “free” font. I was trying to fix a spelling error. Okay, The font is not free. It is a $0.00 priced license (I hope we have that clarified). It is not a watered down version of the font. Freemium? Nope. Actually, I doubt anyone here will have much use for it. It is a turn of the century (1900’s) sign painters letter style. But, please accept it in the spirit in which it is offered.

Okay, boys and girls, guys and gals, the Fontry has a freebie offer “for a limited time only.” Mr. Adkins in his infinite wisdom and generosity is giving away FHA Eccentric French. This font is from the suffering genius of Mr. Adkins and Mr. Smith (I kid you not) based on the font published by Mr. Atkinson himself. Please, use it and enjoy. If you come up with any really cool designs with Eccentric, drop me an email. I’d love to see what you come up with.

Leave comments as you see fit.

The Terms… This font (FHA Eccentric French) is free for personal or commercial use. It is not free for distribution without permission. Do not modify or reverse engineer or whatever. That is the extent of my EULA.
