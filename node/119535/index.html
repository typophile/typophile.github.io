I got no hits on this from any of the major search databases, which leads me to believe it may be custom.

You can recreate the look using a number of typefaces. The B is a 3 with a vertical added to the left, you can lower the crossbar of the A, the C can be rotated, a slash can be added to the D, and the R is actually a P with the upper half of its vertical removed and an added diagonal line:

http://www.myfonts.com/search/Transat/fonts/ http://www.typography.com/fonts/verlag/overview/
