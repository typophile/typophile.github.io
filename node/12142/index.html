[[Indices]] : [[Designers]] : Herbert Thannhaeuser

BirthDate: 2 of December 1898 BirthPlace: Berlin DeathDate: 12 of April 1963 DeathPlace: Kleinmachnow (near Berlin) Activities: Artdirektor, Artist, Typedesigner, Poet

Thannhaeuser was the first art director of the well kown East German foundry [[Typoart]].

In the pre WWII time he designed typefaces for the German foundries [[D. Stempel AG]], Frankfurt a. Main; [[Schriftguss AG]], Dresden and mainly for [[Schelter und Giesecke AG]], Leipzig.
