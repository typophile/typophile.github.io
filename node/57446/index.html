You Blow Me Away

April 28, 2009 • Pin

Ok, so obviously I haven’t posted in a while but I just found this amazing piece called “You Blow Me Away” by Jason Tozer. He printed the words “you blow me away” on 20 7mm sheets of glass & photographed how the glass broke when hitting it with objects like a pool ball. The lettering on the broken glass while it’s flying through the air are just breathtaking!
