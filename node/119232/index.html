Or you could skip the work and follow Dick’s suggestion of Serif Gothic Bold. Your image is a cameo or negative, light letters on a dark background. Letters in this form can look optically identical to a lighter weight. Nice explanation in the book on Frutiger’s life But your negative letters look heavier than Serif Gothic Bold. I conclude from this that your letters are a further emboldened version of Serif Gothic Bold. Or are a modified version of Serif Gothic Heavy. Don
