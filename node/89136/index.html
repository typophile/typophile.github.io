As Jan said, the first approach is ITC Franklin Gothic LT Demi for “CARERS” and ITC Franklin Gothic Book for “RIGHTS DAY” http://new.myfonts.com/fonts/itc/franklin-gothic/ It’s an almost perfect match except of the little inclination on capital /C ends.

If you want a better match for /C you can try Moby Dick 10 Bold for “CARERS” and Moby Dick 12 for “RIGHTS DAY” (Cambridge Fontworks)

Edit: I see there is at least one cut of Franklin Gothic having the correct /C, so there is no need to look for a different font.
