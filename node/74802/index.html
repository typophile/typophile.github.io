Design Week / LA 2010

October 1, 2010 • Spencer Cross

Hey gang. I’m on the coordinating committee for this and I thought some of you might find it of interest

Design Week / LA is an annual celebration of design that inspires and brings together Los Angeles' abundant and vital design communities by promoting a wider awareness of design through initiatives and programming that highlight the richness and diversity of design activities happening throughout the communities of LA. This year’s Design Week / LA will be taking place October 9 - October 17.

[[http://designweekla.org/]] [[http://twitter.com/DesignWeekLA|DWkLA on Twitter]] [[http://www.facebook.com/pages/DesignWeekLA/121595634559184?v=info&ref=ts|DWkLA on Facebook]]

If you’re organizing or promoting a design-related event during Design Week, we’d love to feature it on the site. Please visit our “Add an Event” page to submit your event info:

[[http://designweekla.org/add-event/]]

Or, if you’re in the LA area and want to organize an event but need support, this would be a great time to check out AIGA Los Angeles' new micro-programming initiative (and you don’t need to be an AIGA member to take advantage):

[[http://aigalosangeles.org/features/2010/07/micro-programming-faqs.php]]

Hope to see some of you out and about!

– Spencer Cross http://tokyofarm.com http://kernspiracy.com http://designweekla.org

Oooh, also, forgot to mention that one of our Supporting Partners is Pasadena Museum of California Art and, as part of Design Week, they’re offering FREE admission to the museum for the California Design Biennial from Oct. 9th to Oct. 17th. You just have to download and print out a pass from the Design Week site:

[[http://designweekla.org/2010/09/california-design-biennial-2010-actionreaction/]]

Cheers!
