Personally I don't think 'Dine' is the right word to use. When I read out Game Time Dine it sounds pretty awkward. I would also connect the word 'dine' to a cheap restaurant not a café. Do you have other options or is the client set on 'Dine'? Agreed that the type for 'Dine' doesn't work. In the second version it's very crowded as well. The Connection between 'e' and 'T' seems awkward. I think I would separate 'Game' and 'Time' and shorten the part of the e that sticks out. Otherwise I like the typeface. I get a baseball feel from it which is suitable. The arch idea sound interesting that might work very well.
