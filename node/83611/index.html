2011-07-09 16:45:40 • Frode Bo Helland
There are millions of logos in use, so it’s likely that similar ones exist somewhere. If you’re doing a logo for a big company, you’d want to do a thorough search, but since you’re going for bargain-basement logo design I presume this is just for small business, so I’d just go to Google Images and search for competitors' logos. For example, if your business is “Sam’s Deli” I’d search Google Images for “logo deli” and “logo sam”.
