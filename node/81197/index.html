Get hold of some Punch magazines from the era. The editorial style is Victorian, but the ads will give you a good idea of the traditional conservative aesthetic of the era (less continental, less American than other trends of the era, therefore more British). Most of them are set in Monotype faces. Plantin would be a good choice for the text face.
