Have you generated/installed your font once as OpenType-TrueType and once as OpenType-PostScript (or the other way round)? Then you should try this: Search your system partition for "fnt" -- if you use Windows, make sure your search includes hidden files! You will find files named after a pattern like "AdobeFnt06.lst" or "AdobeFnt10.lst". Can be quite many files. Delete them. (Just to be sure, restart.) But I am not sure *if this* is your problem because you say that upon moving, glyphs do become visible ... Have you tested your font in other applications than Adobe's?
