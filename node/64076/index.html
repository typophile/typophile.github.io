Band-Logo

November 10, 2009 • Simplicious

The band's name is Big And Loaded and the concept is pretty self-explanatory: The 'Big' is big and the 'Loaded' is loaded. The music they are playing is some kind of 'Pop-Punk', close to Bands like Blink 182 or New Found Glory. [img:sites/default/files/old-images/bal_4891.jpg] Since this is for some friends of mine and there is no budget, I decided to make a Slab-Serif on my own. It's based on Akzidenz-Grotesk and very much inspired by Memphis. I'm a little concerned about the 'and' and that it might get lost at smaller sizes. The 'loaded' feels a little off right now, but I want to check your opinions on this before I move on. I am especially doubting the distance between 'Big And' and 'Loaded'. Ohh ... and the center of 'I' is the perfect center of 'BIG' -- reminds me of the Simplease-Logo. ;)
What exactly do you mean by the loaded is loaded? Anyways, I think LOADED needs to be darker...and the face used for that isn't very interesting. There does seem to be a problem with the space between the lines. It looks larger below the B, smaller below the G. Perhaps if you remade the G entirely so that there was no cut into the bottom of it, and that stroke just sort of went directly into the point at the bottom right. But I dunno, maybe that'll just look weird...
