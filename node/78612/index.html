Typographic pub name for new drinking hole!

February 1, 2011 • Double Elephant

Hi, My local pub (near my letterpress printing studio) has decided to expand to a new (second) pub that'll be just round the corner from a couple of big publishing companies. All the houses in the area were originally built for printers, so it would only seem right to have a printing related name for the new pub. The landlord's current pub (my local) is called the Rusty Bicycle and they'd like a similar name (quirky adjective followed by printing term) for their new place, without the printing name being too obscure (preferably, the non-designer public will know what it means). Current favourites: The Wonky Press The Inky Fist etc Any suggestions? Thanks
