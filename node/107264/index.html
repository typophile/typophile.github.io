Now that I look at it, and checking the font notes by Adobe, I do recall this awkward and rather ugly creation from the days when the secretaries used the IBM Selectric typewriter. I tried to avoid having it used whenever possible. Thankfully I did not have a lot of tables typed out. But this creature is perfect for the Sopranos opening.
