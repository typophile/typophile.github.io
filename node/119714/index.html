Edited: Perhaps not a font. At least I don’t recall seeing a font that looks quite like this. The sign could be lettering based on a free interpretation of some late Victorian era designs. The arrowhead terminals on the swan neck S and other letters, were a common design solution. The boxy high waist design was fairly popular. The swirl decorations were sometimes applied to letters. Very little of this is found in digital fonts. Mural, suggested by Kevin has boxy high waist design. Another is Yolanda countess by Rian Hughes.
