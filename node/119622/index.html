Dan X. Solo’s Boul Mich was included with the Dover Book 24 Art Deco Alphabets. Wordshape’s digital version by Ian Lynam is at http://www.youworkforthem.com/font/T3153/boul-mich?affid=143174 Unless you can buy a copy of the Dover book, the only legitimate way to get Boul Mich is to purchase Ian Lynam’s version. The Solo version is more artistically pleasing for some glyphs, such as the 8, but the Lynam version is more precisely engineered. Both versions could be improved from a technical point of view. Your image could be from the Solo version or from a predigital source. Don
