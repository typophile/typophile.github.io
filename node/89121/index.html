Jean François Porchez Lecture in Seattle at the University of Washington

January 16, 2012 • kcheng

The type designer Jean François Porchez will be giving a free public lecture on his work at the University of Washington in Seattle on Wednesday, February 15th.

Jean François Porchez’s biography is online here: http://www.typofonderie.com/profile/profilejfp

The lecture will be on the UW campus in Kane Hall. Directions and parking information here: http://www.css.washington.edu/KNE_Directions

Here’s a Facebook page for the lecture: http://www.facebook.com/events/321905661175081/

There is also simple web page here (in case you are not on Facebook): http://depts.washington.edu/uwch/projects/colloquia-and-conferences/letters-from-france

The event is free to the general public. This event is sponsored by UW Simpson Center for the Humanities and the UW Division of Design.

Radical! I’ll have to see if I can make it over to Seattle, but this should be quite a treat.
