The general typestyle is known as Mansard (the serifs look like mansard roof-lines). The "E" is likely to have been introduced. These are not exact matches, but are similar and typify the form: [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/woodentypefonts/mansard/Jordan Davies' Mansard Extra Bold]] (he has nothing thinner). [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/fontmesa/red-dog-saloon/|Red Dog Saloon family(FontMesa)]] [[http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/baselinefonts/57-rodeo/|57 Rodeo (Baseline Fonts)]] [[http://www.houseoftype.com/prod_display.asp?group_id=HelvinTR|Helvin Black (Type Revivals)]] Mansard-Normal (NovelFonts/Photolettering). No internal attribution. By the way, [[http://sohorep.org/wp-content/uploads/2011/10/f_unclevanya_940x470-01.jpg|this image]], without the 'extended' overprint, was pretty easy to find. - Herb VB
